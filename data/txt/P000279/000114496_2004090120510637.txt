Excellency:
We refer to the above-mentioned Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) dated 
December 22, 1989, as amended to date between Burkina Faso (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association (the Association). Further to discussions held 
between representatives of the Borrower and the Association during the Mid'Term 
Review of the Project in January 1994, we are pleased to inform you that the 
Association hereby agrees to amend the Agreement as follows:
The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Agrrrement is deleted and 
replaced with the new table set forth in the Annex attached hereto.
This amendment shall become effective as of the date of this letter.
