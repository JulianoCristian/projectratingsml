                 Re: Loan No. 2978 AL
                     (Irrigation Engineering Project)
                     Modification to the Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Your Excellency:
     Please refer to the Loan Agreement for the above-mentioned Project, dated January 
13, 1989, entered into by and between the Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria 
(the Borrower) and the IBRD (the Bank).
     I am pleased to inform you that, pursuant to your request of September 10, 1990, 
subsequent letter from the Ministry of Equipment of April 21, 1991, and telex from 
your Ministry of August 27, 1991, the Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement to (a) 
reflect the creation of the Ministry of Equipment and Housing which has taken over the 
functions of the former Ministry of Hydraulics and Forestry, (b) replace the Ministry 
of Finance by the Ministry of Economy as the Borrower's representative to the Bank, 
and (c) define the new allocations of the Loan amount required at mid-term of the 
Project implementation period.
     The amendments are the following:
     1 - Section 1.02 (a) is deleted and replaced by the following:
          "(a) "MEL" means Ministere de l'Equipement et du Logement, the Borrower's 
Ministry of Equipment and Housing;"
     2 - In section 3.01, the term "MHF is deleted and replaced by the term "MEL;"
     3 - Section 6.01 is deleted and replaced by the following:
         "Section 6.01. The Minister of the Borrower in charge of Economy is 
designated as representative of the Borrower for the purposes of section 11.03 
of the General Conditions."
     4 - In section 6.02, the designation "Ministry of Finances" is deleted and 
replaced by "Ministry of Economy";
     5 - Paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is deleted and replaced by the attached text (see 
Attachment to this letter);
     6 - Parts B (1) and B (2) of Schedule 2 are deleted and replaced by the 
following:
         " (1) Preparation of feasibility studies for the development of large scale 
irrigation on about ten areas covering about 120, 000 hectares.
         (2) Preparation of detailed design and tender documents for selected 
schemes identified on the basis of work carried out under part B (1) of the 
Project.
         (3) Modification of the existing detailed design for a scheme covering 
about 4,000 hectares.
     7 - Part C ( 1) of Schedule 2 is deleted and replaced by the following:
          "(1) Provision to MEL, to AGID, to Agence Nationale tea Ressources 
Hydrauliques and to Institut National de Cartographie of technical and 
scientific materials and equipment needed to carry out parts A and B of the 
project."
