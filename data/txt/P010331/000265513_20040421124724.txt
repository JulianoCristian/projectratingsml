NOTICE OF REGISTRATION 
Dear  Sirs: 
 
Re: Request for Inspection
 
-
 
Nepal:  Arun  III Hydroelectric Project
  
 
 
 
The Inspection Panel (the “Panel”) has received a Request for Inspection  (the “Request”) dated October 
24, 1994 from citizens of Nepal (the “Requesters”) who claim that their rights/interests have been adversely affected 
by acts and omissions of the International Development Association (“IDA”) during the design and appraisal of the 
above mentioned project.  Two of the Requesters claim that they and their families have been directly and adversely 
affected by the design and implementation of the resettlement programs related to the proposed project.  All of the 
Requesters claim to be affected by alleged violations of provisions of,
 inter-alia
, the following IDA policies and 
procedures: 
\01\02
Operational Policy/Bank Procedure 10.04: 
Economic Evaluation of Investment Operations
 
\01\02
The World Bank Policy on Disclosure of Information
, March 1994; Bank Procedures 17.50 and 10.00 
Annex A 
\01\02
Operational Directive 4.01: 
Environmental Assessment
 
\01\02
Operational Directive 4.30: 
Involuntary Resettlement
 
\01\02
Operational Directive 4.20: 
Indigenous Peoples 
 
 
In accordance with paragraph 18 of  the Panel’s Operating Procedures  (the “Operating Procedures”),  I 
am notifying  you that I have on November 3, 1994, which is also the date of the dispatch of this notice, registered in 
The Inspection Panel Register the Request, together with the attachments and other supporting documentation 
received subsequently by the Panel on November 2, 1994. 
 
Two of the Requesters who wish to remain anonymous are represented by Requesters Messrs. Gopal 
Siwakoti and Ganesh Kumar Ghimire of Kathmandu.  All communications  to the Requesters in connection with this 
Request will be sent to the addresses below, unless another address is indicated to the Panel. 
 
In accordance with paragraph 18 of IDA Resolution No. 93-6 and paragraph 18(d) of the Operating 
Procedures,  Management of IDA is hereby notified that it must provide the Panel no later than December 6, 1994 
with written evidence that it has complied, or intends to comply with the relevant policies and procedures in the 
design and appraisal of the above mentioned project.  The essential criteria for the response to the Request are set 
out in paragraph 33 of the Operating Procedures. 
 
The Request has been assigned  IPN Request Number RQ94/1.

yours,
