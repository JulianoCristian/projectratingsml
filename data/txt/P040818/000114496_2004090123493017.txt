Re: Credit 3068 ALB (Durres Port Project) 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement 
 
Dear Mr. Angjeli: 
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the above-referenced Project, dated 
June 9, 1998, as amended, between Albania and the International Development Association (the 
Association). 
 
We also refer to your fax dated June 6, 2000, requesting the Development Credit 
Agreement be amended. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association hereby agrees to your request.  
Accordingly, the Development Credit Agreement is hereby amended as set forth in the 
Attachment hereto.  
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by countersigning both copies of this 
amendment letter and returning one of the countersigned copies to us.  Upon receipt by the 
Association of the copy of this amendment letter countersigned by you, this amendment letter 
will become effective as of November 1, 1999.  We will notify you of the receipt of your 
countersigned letter.

4. 
Schedule 3, Section I, Part C: Other Procurement Procedures
(i) Part C.2 is modified as follows: 
 
“2. Procurement of Small Works
The total amount of contracts that may be procured under this procedure is increased 
from US$200,000 to US$300,000; and 
 
(ii) A new paragraph 3. is added that reads as follows: 
 
“3. 
National Competitive Bidding
Items of works under Part A of the Project, estimated to cost not more than US$300,000 
per contract, up to an aggregate amount not to exceed the equivalent of US$600,000 may be 
procured on the basis of national competitive bidding procedures in accordance with the 
provisions of paragraphs 3.3 and 3.4 of the Guidelines.” 
 
5. 
Schedule 3 – Section II, Part C: Other Procedures for the Selection of Consultants
(i) 
A new paragraph 3. is added, which reads as follows:

and 5.3 of the Consultant Guidelines.”
