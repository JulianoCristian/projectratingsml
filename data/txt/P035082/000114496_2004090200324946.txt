Municipal Finance Project - Loan 42080/1-POL – Amendment to the 
Loan Agreement
We refer to the Loan Agreement for the Municipal Finance Project, dated 
September 5, 1997 (the Loan Agreement), as amended,  between the Bank for 
Socio-Economic Initiatives S.A (BISE) and the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the letters from BISE dated 
October 9, and 26, 2000, requesting certain amendments to the Loan Agreement.  
We hereby confirm the Bank’s agreement to the following amendments to 
Schedule 4 of the Loan Agreement.
Paragraph 3(b):  the minimum Subloan size in paragraph 3(b) of Schedule 
4 is amended to read “less than the equivalent of $150,000.”
Paragraph 5(c):  the date in paragraph 5(c) of Schedule 4 is amended to 
read “June 30, 2001.”
Please confirm your agreement with the forgoing amendments by 
countersigning, dating and returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter. 
This amendment will become effective as of October 9, 2000, the date of your 
initial request, upon receipt by the Bank of the countersigned copy of this 
letter.
