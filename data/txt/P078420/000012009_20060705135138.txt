2. 
Short list comprising entirely of national consultants
:
Short list of consultants for 
services, estimated to cost less than $ 200,000.00 equivalent per contract, may comprise 
entirely of national consultants in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 2.7 of the 
Consultant Guidelines. 
 
3.  Any Other Special Selection Arrangements: 
Eligibility of State Owned Institutions. 
Short lists for assignments to be procured under the provisions of Part B.2 of this Section 
may, with the prior agreement of the Bank, include, or be entirely composed of, suitable 
State owned entities, up to an aggregate amount not to exceed $1,500,000 and subject to 
the proviso that no entity may appear on a shortlist for any assignment where it is the 
direct beneficiary of the outputs of the assignment. [including advance procurement and 
retroactive financing, if applicable]
