                         Implementation Status Overview
                         As the MDTF-SS PSD project comes to an end, despite the challenges of a nascent and fragile country (government and economy), the overall progress of the project remains
                         satisfactory with significant accomplishments made. The Trade Policy was developed and submitted to the Economic Cluster of Council of Ministers for further deliberations. The

                         mining bill was submitted to the parliament. Additionally, four bills (insurance, microfinance, competition, counterfeit goods) were developed and submitted to the Ministry of Legal
                         Affairs for legislative procedures. The Microfinance Policy was developed and delivered to the Central Bank of South Sudan as the parent institution. The microfinance Apex
                         Institution, the South Sudan Microfinance Development Facility (SSMDF) has been successfully established and operational at in the Ministry of Commerce, Industry, and Investment;
                         and after the departure of the Frankfurt School, it is now completing its staffing. The Sector Growth Strategies for medium term industrial competitiveness were developed and
