Third Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Minister Lavagna: 
 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated May 6, 1998 entered into between the 
Argentine Republic (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), as amended. 
 
In response to the Borrower’s August 1, 2002 request to, 
inter alia
:
(a) extend the 
Closing Date from June 30, 2003 to June 30, 2004; (b) add a new Project activity to strengthen 
the operational capacity of Social Program for Agriculture, through the provision of technical 
assistance to said program, to assist the Borrower, through SAGPyA to carry out Parts A.1, A.2 
and A.3 of the Project; (c) reallocate a portion of the Loan proceeds amongst the disbursement 
categories set forth in Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement to, 
inter alia
,
permit the carrying out of 
the new Project activity referred to in (b) herein; and (d) increase the disbursement percentage for 
certain disbursement categories, and further discussions on said subjects during the November 
25-26, 2002 portfolio review meeting between the representatives of the Borrower and the Bank, 
the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows: 
 
I. 
Loan Agreement
1. 
Section 2.03 is hereby amended in its entirety to read as follows: 
 
“Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be June 30, 2005 or such later date as the 
Bank shall establish. The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower of such later date.”. 
 
2. 
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended in its entirety to read as set 
forth in the annex to this Amendment Letter. 
 
3. 
In Schedule 2, a new Project activity is added after Part A.3 which shall read as follows: 
 
Page  2
2
“4. Strengthening the operational capacity of the Social Program for Agriculture, through 
the provision of technical assistance, for purposes of assisting SAGPyA in the carrying out of 
Parts A.1, A.2 and A.3 of the Project.”. 
 
II. 
Effectiveness
:
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall be an 
original. The provisions of this Amendment Letter will become effective as of the date first above 
written upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this Amendment Letter. Please 
confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating the two counterparts 
of this Amendment Letter.
