Re: DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC OF CONGO
Third Additional Financing For Health Sector Rehabilitation Support Project
Grant No. H750
Amendment to the Financing Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Financing Agreement (the "Agreement") dated July 30, 2012 for
the above-referenced project (the "Project") between the Democratic Republic of Congo
(the "Recipient") and the International Development Association (the "Association"), and
to your request dated May 6, 2014, to extend the Closing Date and add a reference to the
new co-financings from the Multi-Donor Trust Fund for Health Results Innovation
(HRITF).
We are pleased to inform you that the Association hereby agrees to your request
and proposes to amend the Agreement as follows:
1.    Paragraph 2 of Section IV to the Agreement is amended to read as follows:
"2.   The Closing Date is December 31, 2014."
2.    Section V of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is amended by adding a new
paragraph 5 to read as follows:
"5.   The Recipient shall ensure that by no later than June 30, 2014, the HRITF
Grant Agreement be executed for the Co-financing in a manner satisfactory to the
Association."
3.    The Appendix to the Agreement is amended by adding the following new
definitions:
35. "Co-financing" means, for purposes of paragraph 11 of the Appendix to the
General Conditions, an amount of ten million Dollars (US$10,000,000), to be


﻿provided by the Association acting as administrator of the Multi-Donor Trust
Fund for Health Results Innovation, to assist in financing the Project.
36. "HRITF Grant Agreement" means the agreement to be entered into between the
Recipient and the Association acting as administrator of the Multi-Donor Trust
Fund for Health Results Innovation providing part of the Co-financing."
All other provisions of the Agreement, except as amended, shall remain in force and
effect.
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by countersigning,
dating and returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter, while keeping one original for
your records.  The amendment will become effective as of the date of the
countersignature.
