                         Implementation Status Overview
                         On November 20, 2013, Level-II Restructuring of the Project was approved in response to the Government of Georgia's requests dated of February 26, 2013 and December 5, 2012.
                         The project closing date is extended from November 30, 2013 to August 30, 2015 and another road - Sasadilo-Sioni (17 km) which feeds the Vaziani-Gombori-Telavi circuit road has
                         been added the rehabilitation under this project.

                         The first component of improvement of the Vaziani Gombori Telavi road has been completed and is under defect liability. The Roads Department (RD) continues monitoring a
                         landslide prone area on the Gombori-Telavi section. The procurement process for rehabilitation of the Sasadilo-Sioni road (with 5 bridges) which was added under this component
