                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The first component of improvement of the road Vaziani Gombori Telavi road has been completed and is under defect liability. The Roads Department (RD) continues monitoring a
                         landslide prone area on the Gombori-Telavi section.

                         The second component (road safety component and institutional strengthening) is still under implementation with delays compared to the initial project plan. The RD has contracted
                         out priority road safety measures identified in the Road Safety Management Plan for the Vaziani-Telavi-Bakhurstikhe Sagarejo-Vaziani ring road is now under implementation.
