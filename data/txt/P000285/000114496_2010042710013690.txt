Re:
BURKINA FASO:  Agricultural Services Project
(Credit No. 1979 BUR)
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
Excellency,
We refer to the above-mentioned Agreement, dated January 28, 1989 (the 
Agreement), between Burkina Faso (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association), and to your letter, dated April 3, 1993, wherein you 
have requested the Association to amend the Agreement in order to have the outstanding 
balance due under the Project Preparation Advance for a proposed
Livestock Project (PPF P304-BUR) refinanced under the above-mentioned Credit.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association accedes to your request and 
agrees, accordingly, to amend Section 1.02 (x) of the Agreement to read as follows:
(x)
"Project Preparation Advance" means the project preparation 
advances granted 
by the Association to the Borrower pursuant to a first exchange of 
letters, dated May 14 and  September 14, 1984, and to a further exchange of letters, 
dated May 7 and June 15, 1987, 
between the Borrower and the Association;
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment, on behalf of the 
Borrower, by signing and dating the confirmation form on the enclosed copy of this 
letter and returning it to us for our records.
Sincerely yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
