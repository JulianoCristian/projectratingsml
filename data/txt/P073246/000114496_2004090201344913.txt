Re: 
 
NICARAGUA: GEF-PPG TF050821 (Grant for the Preparation of the 
Off-Grid Rural Electrification Development Project) 
 
Amendment to Letter Agreement
Dear Sir: 
 
Please refer to the Letter Agreement  between the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank) acting as an Implementing Agency of the GEF and the Republic of 
Nicaragua (the Recipient) dated April 10, 2002, with regard to the referenced Project. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to your request, the Association agrees to: (a) 
increase the financing percentage: (i) for international consultants, hired for six months or less, from 
85% to 100%; and (ii) local consultants and, international consultants hired for longer than six 
months, from 85% to 91%; (b) to make both increases retroactive as follows: (a) (i) to December 
17, 2002 and (a) (ii) to August 21, 2002, respectively; and (c) to extend the Closing Date until April 
30, 2003. 
 
Therefore, the table under paragraph 4.2 of the Letter Agreement is amended as reflected in 
the Annex to this Letter. 
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating this letter 
in the spaces provided below.  This Letter of Amendment will be executed in two counterparts, each 
of which shall be an original. 
 
Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed original to us.  The provisions of 
this Letter of Amendment will become effective as of the date of this letter upon receipt by the 
Bank of one fully executed original of this Letter. 
 
Very truly yours,
