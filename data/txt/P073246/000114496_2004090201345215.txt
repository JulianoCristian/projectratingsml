Re: NICARAGUA: Global Environment Facility Trust Fund Grant Agreement  TF051960 
(Off Grid Rural Electrification Project 
–
PERZA)- Amendment to the GEF Grant 
Agreement 
 
Excellency: 
 
Please refer to the GEF Trust Fund Grant Agreement (the GEF Grant Agreement) for the 
above-referenced Project between the Republic of Nicaragua (the Recipient) and the International 
Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), acting as an Implementing Agency of the 
Global Environment Facility, dated May 27, 2003. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to our recent discussions on the matter, the 
Association agrees to increase the disbursement percentages in the table in paragraph 1 of 
Schedule 1 of the GEF Grant Agreement. Consequently, the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 of 
the GEF Grant Agreement will be amended as set forth in the Annex to this Amendment letter. 
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating each 
copy of this Letter of Amendment in the spaces provided below. This Letter of Amendment shall 
be executed in two counterparts, each of which shall be an original. Upon your confirmation, 
please return one fully executed original to us. The provisions of this Letter Amendment shall 
enter into effect on the date of this letter, provided that the Bank receives one signed original. 
 
Sincerely,
