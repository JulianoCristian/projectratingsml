GUARANTEE AGREEMENT
     AGREEMENT, dated January 8, 1991 between DEMOCRATIC AND POPULAR REPUBLIC OF 
ALGERIA (the Guarantor) and INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (the 
Bank).
     WHEREAS (A) the Guarantor and the Entreprise Nationale de Produits  Metalliques 
Utilitaires (the Borrower), having been satisfied as to the  feasibility and priority 
of the Project described in Schedule 2 to the Loan  Agreement of even date herewith 
between the Bank and the Borrower (the Loan Agreement), have requested the Bank to 
assist in the financing of the Project; and
     (B)  by the Loan Agreement, the Bank has agreed to extend to the Borrower a loan 
in various currencies equivalent to twelve million seven hundred thousand dollars 
($12,700,000), on the terms and conditions set forth in the  Loan Agreement, but only 
on condition that the Guarantor agree to guarantee the obligations of the Borrower in 
respect of such loan as provided in this Agreement; and
     WHEREAS the Guarantor, in consideration of the Bank's entering into the Loan 
Agreement with the Borrower, has agreed so to guarantee such obligations of the 
Borrower;
Page  2
     NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
General Conditions; Definitions
     Section 1.01. The "General Conditions Applicable to Loan and Guarantee 
Agreements" of the Bank, dated January 1, 1985, with the last sentence of  Section 
3.02 deleted (the General Conditions) constitute an integral part of this Agreement.
     Section 1.02. Unless the context otherwise requires, the several terms  defined 
in the General Conditions and and Section 1.02 of the Loan Agreement  have the 
respective meanings therein set forth.
ARTICLE II
Guarantee
     Section 2.01. Without limitation or restriction upon any of its other obligations 
under the Guarantee Agreement, the Guarantor hereby unconditionally: (i) guarantees, 
as primary obligor and not as surety merely, the due and punctual payment of the 
principal of, and interest and other charges on, the Loan, and the premium, if any, on 
the prepayment of the Loan; and (ii) undertakes not to take or permit to be taken any 
action which would interfere, directly or indirectly, with the Borrower's 
administration and management of its operations, and of its performance of its 
obligations as set forth in the Loan Agreement.
ARTICLE III
Representative of the Guarantor; Addresses
     Section 3.01. The Minister of the Guarantor in charge of Economy is designated as 
representative of the Guarantor for the purposes of Section 11.03 of the General 
Conditions.
