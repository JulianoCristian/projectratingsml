                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project aims at improving access of rural households and entrepreneurs to goods, social services and income generating opportunities through reduced transport
                         costs and better rural transport infrastructure and through piloting, in 15 provinces, specific incentives aiming at promoting greater complementarities and bundling of
                         rural infrastructure interventions at the provincial level.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         As of January 2013, the following goals have been fulfilled: 95 percent (3193km) of rural roads have been rehabilitated, 86 percent have received periodic maintenance, 87 percent
                         (2365km) of footpaths have been rehabilitated, 98 percent of provincial plans (191 provinces) have been prepared and 93 percent of the project provinces (14 provinces) have
                         developed the infrastructure plans. It was noted that the development of tourist footpaths and institutional strengthening didn't show significant progress, therefore, the Implementing

                         Agency was asked to make stronger efforts in those tasks.
                         The project works with 857 rural road maintenance microenterprises that are operating in 143 provinces, 752 districts in 908 road sections with an overall coverage of 16,867 km.
                         The Local Development Window has been implemented in 30 provinces, of which work in 24 has concluded by financing 43 projects. These projects have been implemented as
                         follows: 29 at the district level, 13 at provincial level and one at the regional level. Additionally, 52 business plans for??? have been financed through the project.
