SCHEDULE 2
Description of Project
The Project is the construction of generating and trans-
mission facilities for the 120 MW Matucana hydroelectric
power station. The Project includes:
(a) an intake and sand trap on the Rimac River at
Tamboraque,
(b) a, free flow lunnel about 20 km long and 3 nieters in
diameter from  the intake to the forebay,
(c) a steel penstock about 1700 meters long to convey the
water from the forebay to the power station,
(d) a power station in which two 60 MW    turbine-gen-
erator units operating under a head of 960 meters
are installed,
(e) a 220 k  double circuit steel tower transimissioi line
about 80 km long from the power station to Lima,
and associated substations,
(f) (list ribution system expansion.
It is expected that the Project will be conpleted about
December 31, 1971.
