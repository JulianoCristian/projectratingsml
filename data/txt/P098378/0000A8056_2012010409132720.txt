                         Implementation Status Overview
                         In general, the Project is performing well toward achieving its development objective. The last supervision mission, which took place in October 2011, noted that all performance
                         indicators and triggers have already been met, 13 months before the closing date, November 2012.
                          The annual physical average rate of execution as of October 2011 noted by the mission is 88.2%, and the financial disbursement rate is 70.65 %; through training, procurements

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         As was noted during the last two supervision missions, current procurement guidelines continue to be too complex for rural communes. Although these communes have been


                                                                                                                                                                                                                       Page 4 of 5
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                     Report No: ISR5360



                         demonstrating greater understanding of procurement procedures, thanks to training and capacity building activities, it remains critical for authorities to help simplify procurement
                         guidelines that take into account the capacity constraints at the commune level. As the issue of procurement became a national constraint in programs and projects implementation
                         the government has organized in November 2011 a national forum on procurement ; attendees were all stakeholders in procurement processes and decisions were taken to review in
                         2012 the national official documents of procurement processes and particular attention will be paid to the case rural communes' case and other decentralized institutions.
