                         Implementation Status Overview
                         he development and project objectives accurately reflect the needs of the Afghan Government and the project beneficiaries, as wellas the priorities of donors as articulated at the
                         London and Kabul Conferences. The implementation of the project has slowed down since the November elections and the Mid-Term-Review. The biggest achievements have been
                         made in organizing training for judges, establishing legal libraries and dissemination of laws. Delays can be attributed to the post election personnel changes in the SC, MOJand the
                         AGO and the focus of the institutions on the post election political processes and policy discussions, inlcuding Kabul Conference. Project management capacity (the capacity of a
