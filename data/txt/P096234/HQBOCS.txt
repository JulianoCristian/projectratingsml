Re: IRAQ - Third Emergency Education Project (Credit No. 4128-IQ)
Third Amendment to the Financing Aereement
Excellency:
We refer to the Financing Agreement between the Republic of Iraq (the "Recipient") and the
International Development Association (the "Association") dated April 2, 2007, as amended (the
"Financing Agreement"). We also refer to your letter dated January 20, 2013 requesting the Association
to amend the Financing Agreement.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association has acceded to your request. Accordingly, we
propose to amend the Financing Agreement as follows:
a. The Closing Date in the second paragraph of Section IV.B of Schedule 2 to the Financing
Agreement is amended to read June 30, 2014.
b.The performance indicators under B. Part I of Schedule 4 to the Financing Agreement are
amended to read as follows:
"B. Intermediate Results Indicators:
Part 1: Educational Infrastructure:
1. Development of four prototype designs for school buildings.
2. MOE and DOE engineers who have completed training in procurement and contract
management.
3. Number of schools built.
4. Number of six-classroom block extensions (including toilets) built.
5. Number of additional classrooms built or rehabilitated at the primary level resulting
from project intervention.
6. Number of additional classrooms built or rehabilitated at the secondary level
resulting from project intervention.
c.The table in paragraph 2 of Section IV. A of Schedule 2 to the Financing Agreement is revised
as follows:
