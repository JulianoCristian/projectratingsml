Re:  Loan Number 4330-IND
(Kecamatan Development Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Kecamatan Development Project) 
between the Republic of Indonesia (the Borrower) and the International 
Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), dated June 26, 
1998.  We also refer to the Ministry of Finance’s letter dated 
November 30, 2000, requesting amendments to the Loan Agreement.
The Bank is pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, 
it has agreed to your request to amend the Loan Agreement as set forth 
below.
1.
Section 1.02 (b) of the Loan Agreement is amended to read:
"(b)
"BAPPEDA Propinsi" means the Provincial Development Planning 
Agency established and operating within a province of the Borrower;"
2.
Section 1.02 (g) of the Loan Agreement is amended to read:
"(g)
"Fiscal Year" means the Borrower’s fiscal year commencing 
April 1, 2000 and ending December 31, 2000, and, thereafter, 
commencing January 1 and ending December 31;"
3.
The Bank has established December 31, 2002, as the later date for 
purposes of Section 2.03 of the Loan Agreement.
4.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is 
amended as follows:
5.
The completion date set forth in Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement 
is amended to read "December 31, 2002."
6.
Part A of Section I of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is amended 
to read:
1.
Goods and works shall be procured in accordance with (a) the 
provisions of Section I of the "Guidelines for Procurement under 
Page  2
IBRD Loans and IDA Credits" published by the Bank in January 1995 
and revised in January and August 1996, September 1997 and 
January 1999 (the Guidelines) and the provisions of the following 
Parts of this Section I.
2.
In paragraphs 1.6 and 1.8 of the Guidelines, the references 
to "Bank member countries" and "member country" shall be deemed 
to be references, respectively, to "Participating Countries" and 
"Participating Country"."
7.
Part A of Section II of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is 
amended to read:
"1.
Consultants’ services shall be procured in accordance with 
(a) the provisions of the Introduction and Section IV of the 
"Guidelines:  Selection and Employment of Consultants by World 
Bank Borrowers" published by the Association in January 1997 and 
revised in September 1997 and January 1999, subject to the 
modifications thereto set forth in paragraph 2 of this Part A 
(the Consultant Guidelines), and (b) the provisions of the 
following Parts of this Section II.
2.
In paragraph 1.10 of the Consultant Guidelines, the 
references to "Bank member countries" and "member country" shall 
be deemed to be references, respectively, to "Participating 
Countries" and "Participating Country"."
8.
Paragraph 3 of Schedule 5 to the Loan Agreement is amended as 
follows:
“3.
The Borrower shall ensure that:
(a)
each BAPPEDA Propinsi shall, in consultation with the 
relevant Bupati, annually select proposed Project Kecamatans from 
a list provided by BAPPENAS through the PMU of Kecamatans meeting 
the criteria set forth in paragraph 1 of this Schedule;
(b)
each BAPPEDA Propinsi shall furnish the annual 
consolidated list of proposed Project Kecamatans to  the PIU and 
the Bank for approval, by not later than December 15, 1998, for 
Fiscal Year 1999/2000, December 15, 1999, for Fiscal Year 2000, 
December 15, 2000, for Fiscal Year 2001, and, thereafter, 
September 15 of each year for the following Fiscal Year, 
commencing September 15, 2001, and until completion of the 
Project; and
(c)
not later than March 31, 1999, for Fiscal Year 
1999/2000, March 31, 2000, for Fiscal Year 2000, March 31, 2001, 
for Fiscal Year 2001, and, thereafter, December 31 of each year 
for the following Fiscal Year, commencing December 31, 2001, and 
until completion of the Project, the approved annual consolidated 
list of Project Kecamatans is made freely available by the PIU."
9.
Paragraph 4 of Schedule 5 to the Loan Agreement is amended as 
Page  3
follows:
"4.
The Borrower shall ensure that the relevant Bupatis and 
Camats shall:
(a)
issue to the Project Kecamatans, by not later than 
January 31, 1999, for Fiscal Year 1999/2000, January 31, 2000, 
for Fiscal Year 2000, January 31, 2001, for Fiscal Year 2001, 
and, thereafter, October 31 of each year for the following Fiscal 
Year, commencing October 31, 2001, and until completion of the 
Project, public information aimed at disclosing to villagers and 
their representatives, all administrative, financial, 
environmental, procedural and technical aspects pertaining to the 
selection, design, preparation and implementation of 
Sub-projects; and 
(b)
make such information, including the Project Manual, 
freely available for consultation at the Kecamatan office."
10.
Schedule 6 to the Loan Agreement is amended as follows:
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing and 
dating the duplicate of this letter in the space provided.
The above amendment will become effective upon your confirmation 
and upon the effectiveness of the Development Credit Agreement between 
the Borrower and the International Development Association of even 
date herewith for the Kecamatan Development Project.
