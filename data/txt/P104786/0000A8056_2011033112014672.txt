                         Implementation Status Overview
                           Overall, the project has recorded good progress towards achievement of the PDO. The project has reached 131,100 farmers, which is higher than the target set of 120,000 farmers
                         for the first phase, which ends in March 31, 2011. Thirty Seven (37) percent of are women. The first phase was designed to establish a foundation for service delivery, to start the
                         process of rebuilding capacity foragricultural research, to train researchers and service providers in both the public and private sectors, to reconstruct and re-equip local offices, and
