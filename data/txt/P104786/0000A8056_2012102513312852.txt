                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Progress towards achievement of the PDO is still moderately satisfactory as in the last reporting. The Project was successfully restructured and the closing date extended to
                         December 31, 2012 to enable completion of works contracts that had earlier been delayed. As at this reporting all planned physical infrastructure has been completed and most
                         handed over to government. The project has thus far supported 97,294 direct beneficiaries (an increase of about 16,300 farmers over and above those supported as at the last
                         reporting)to increase production and productivity of staple food crops. Average yields of major staples per feddan have not yet been computed but anecdotal evidence points to an
                         increase over the three year average that was attained by 2011. A total of 625 staff from Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry, Cooperatives and Rural Development, State Ministries of
