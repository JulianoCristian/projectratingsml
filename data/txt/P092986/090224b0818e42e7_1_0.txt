                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to support the Recipient to perform its role as a facilitator for economic development through: (i) implementation by MDAs of a reform
                         initiative for improved public sector management and service delivery; and (ii) strengthening of the governance and competitiveness of the financial sector

                         The ISR reports on an implementation support mission of August 27-31 (with a primary focus on strengthening M&E reporting), and subsequent actions to secure a five month project
                         extension (until end May 2013) to enable the utilization of unspent PSR component funds. Almost all project activities are either complete, or underway. As at the end of the
