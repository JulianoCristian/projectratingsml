                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The last implementation supervision for the project was carried out from July 11-15, 2011, and the team again noted the consistently high project performance. All procurement
                         activities under this project have been completed with 99.7% of the loan amount disbursed, and actual disbursements consistently above the estimates during the implementation
                         period. POWERGRID would be able to claim full disbursement within the four-month period after the loan closing date. The project closed on July 31, 2011.

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The last supervision was carried out from July 11-15, 2011, and POWERGRID was commended for an efficient and diligent project implementation. Based on project performance,
                         the Development Objective rating of PSDP III is rated Highly Satisfactory. No significant shortcomings have been recorded in the project's ability to meet its development objectives,
                         which remain highly relevant, especially in the context of the perennial power shortages India has to deal with. The project closed on July 31, 2011.
