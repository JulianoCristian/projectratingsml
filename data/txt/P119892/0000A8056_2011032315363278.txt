                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Gambia Emergency Agriculture Production Project (EAPP) was approved on December 29, 2009 and became effective on March 01,2010. Overall the project has achieved more
                         than the indicators targets such as quantity of seeds delivered, area in hectares cultivated, number of beneficiaries and targeted production output. All mitigation measures agreed
                         have been implemented. The Project unit has conducted the required visibility activities in close collaboration with local medias and full involvement of the EC delegation in Banjul.
