Re:
Loan No. 4016 UA (Coal Pilot Project)
Amendment to Loan Agreement and 
Extension of the Closing Date
Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Coal Pilot Project), dated July 11, 1996, 
between Ukraine (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), as amended (the Loan Agreement), and to letters of Mr. 
Makatsariya to the Bank of June 30, 1999 and July 19, 1999, requesting that the Loan 
Agreement be amended to maximize the utilization of mine closure cost savings achieved 
under the Pilot Loan for expansion of the social mitigation and job creation programs 
to all mines transferred to UDKR pursuant to the Government of Ukraine’s coal sector 
restructuring Program, and also requesting that the Closing Date of the Loan be 
extended in order to enable implementation of the increased social mitigation 
programs.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank accedes to your request. Accordingly, 
the Bank has established December 31, 2000, as the later date for purposes of Section 
2.03 of the Loan Agreement. The Loan Agreement is also hereby amended as follows:
1.
Section 1.02 is hereby amended as follows:
(a)
definition (m), “Participating Cities” is amended by adding at the end of 
the definition the words “and other cities in the Donetsk Oblast that are affected by 
the closure of Program Mines.”; and
(b)
a new definition (x) is added as follows:
“(x)
“Program Mines” means coal mines that are transferred to UDKR for 
closure 
pursuant to Decisions of the Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine and 
subsequent orders by the 
Ministry of Coal Industry.”
2.
Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement, as amended, is hereby further amended as 
follows:
(a)
The table in Schedule 1 is amended as set out in the Attachment to this 
letter; and
(b)
Paragraph (b) of the amendment dated May 6, 1998 is deleted.
3.
Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
(a)
Part B.1 is amended by adding, at the end of the sentence, the words “and 
each of the Program Mmines.”;
(b)
Part B.2 is amended by replacing the words “Project Mines” with the words 
“Program Mines”;
(c)
Part B.3 is amended by adding, at the end of the sentence, the words “and 
areas in the Donetsk Oblast affected by the closure of Program Mines.”; and
(d)
Part E.3 is amended to read:
“3.
Financing of: (i) audits required under the Project, (ii) 
Page  2
technical 
assistance; and (iii) office equipment required by the MOF 
in order to strengthen its 
capacity to administer social mitigation and 
credit line activities for the Program Mines.”
4.
Schedule 5 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended  as follows:
(a)
Part B.1 is amended by inserting the words “and each of the Program 
Mines” after the words “Pravda Mines”;
(b)
Parts B.3 (a) and (b) are amended by  replacing the words “Donetsk 
Oblast” with the words “the Fiscal Agent”; and
(c)
Part B.4 (i) is amended by inserting the words “and areas in the Donetsk 
Oblast affected by the closure of Program Mines” after the words “Project Mines”.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing, dating and 
returning to us the copy of this letter. This amendment shall come into effect on the 
date upon which the Bank dispatches to the Borrower the notice of its receipt of the 
copy of this letter countersigned by you.
