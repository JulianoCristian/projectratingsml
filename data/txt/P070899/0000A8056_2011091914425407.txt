                         Project Development Objectives
                         Laguna de Bay Institutional Strengthening and Community Participation/LISCOP (P070899)
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)
                         The overarching objective of the proposed project is to assist the Laguna Lake Development Authority (LLDA), Local Government Units(LGUs) and other
                         stakeholders to improve the environmental quality of the Laguna De Bay watershed.
                         This will be achieved through: (i) behavioral changes and activities undertaken by watershed users to improve the environmental quality o f the lake and its
                         watershed; and, (ii) improved planning, regulatory instruments and incentives, and participation in the environmental management o f the Laguna de Bay
                         watershed.
