We would like to refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) dated 
December 21, 1990, and its amendment signed on August 16, 1996, for the Agricultural 
Diversification Project (ADP) between the International Development Association (the 
Association) and the Republic of Ghana.  We also refer to your letters dated July 24, 
1998, and August 28, 1998, asking for an extension of the credit closing date and an 
amendment to the DCA for the above Project.
The purpose of this letter therefore is to inform you that IDA has agreed to 
extend the Closing Date of the project by only one year to December 31, 1999 and to 
cancel the surplus amount of SDR 1.4 million which the project will be unable to 
utilize during the one year extension period.  It is therefore proposed that the

Attachment II
SCHEDULE 5
Implementation Program
Eligibility Criteria for Subprojects
The Borrower shall ensure that, unless the Association shall otherwise agree, no 
Subproject shall be eligible for financing out of the proceeds of the Credit unless 
JRB and ADB shall have determined on the basis of an appraisal conducted in accordance 
with the guidelines set forth in the respective Operations Manual, that the Subproject 
satisfies the eligibility criteria specified in more detail in such Operational 
Manual, which shall include:
(a)
the Subprojects shall be for palm oil production, coffee propagation or 
pineapple production and export;
(b)
the Subprojects shall be initiated by farmers or a group of farmers or 
cooperatives;
(c)
the Subprojects shall be targeted at lower income group beneficiaries;
(d)
the Subprojects shall be designed in such a way that their operation and 
maintenance shall be sustainable; and
(e)
the Subprojects shall be in compliance with the standards set forth in 
the applicable laws of the Borrower relating to health, safety and environmental 
protections as well as the Association’s applicable guidelines.
Page  7
Terms and Conditions of Subprojects Financing
In financing Subprojects, JRB and ADB shall enter into a Financing Agreement 
with the farmers or cooperatives, under terms and conditions which shall include the 
following:
(a)
financing to be on a loan basis, under terms and conditions specified in 
the respective Subsidiary Loan Agreement;
(b)
the obligation to carry out the Subproject in accordance with the 
Operational Manual, with due diligence and efficiency and in accordance with sound 
technical, agricultural, financial, environmental and managerial standards and to 
maintain adequate records to reflect, in accordance with sound accounting practices, 
the operations, resources and expenditures in respect of the Subproject;
(c)
the requirement that: (i) the goods, works and services to be financed 
from the proceeds of the Credit shall be procured in accordance with the procedures 
set forth in Schedule 3 to the Development Credit Agreement; and (ii) such goods, 
works and services shall be used exclusively in the carrying out of the Subproject;
(d)
the right of JRB and ADB to inspect by itself, or jointly with the 
Association, if the Association shall so request, the goods, works, sites, plants and 
construction included in the Subproject, the operations thereof and any relevant 
records and documents;
(e)
the right of JRB and ADB to obtain all information as JRB and ADB or the 
Association shall reasonably request regarding the administration, operation and 
financial conditions of the Subproject; and
(f)
the right of JRB and ADB to suspend or terminate the right of any farmer 
or cooperative to use the proceeds of the Credit for the Subproject upon the failure 
by the farmer or cooperative to perform any of its obligations under the Subproject 
Agreement.
