                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project's main development objective is to broaden and deepen the access of Turkish small and medium enterprises to medium- and long-term finance, with a view
                         to ultimately contributing to an expansion of productive activities and job creation.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The overall implementation of SME II, on balance, continues to be strong. As of June 2013, 76.7 percent of the loan (US $383.6 million) has been disbursed to beneficiary SMEs ,


                                                                                                                                                                                                          Page 1 of 7
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                     Report No: ISR11925



                         with Ziraat accounting for US $120.7 million; Vakif for US $162.9 million; and Kalkinma (through its intermediaries, Deniz Bank, Garanti Bank and Garanti Leasing) for US $100
                         million. Kalkinma Bank had completed disbursement of its portion (US $ 100 million) by September 2012. 518 SMEs have been financed under the project, with most companies
                         coming from the manufacturing sector. Maturities vary from 24 to 120 months, with an average of 51 months. Sub-loans are being extended both in foreign currency and Turkish Lira.
                         The proceeds for a large proportion of the sub-loans (close to 67 percent) are being used for working capital purposes. Disbursements to underserved areas are still relatively low,

                         with about 14.3 percent of total sub-loans going to those areas (compared to a project requirement of 25 percent).
