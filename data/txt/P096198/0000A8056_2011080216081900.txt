                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project Development Objective (PDO) of the Multi-sector Demographic Program (PRODEM) is to strengthen Government's capacity toaddress its
                         demographic challenges through: (i) enabling the Ministry of Population to design and implement a Nationwidemulti-sector population program; and (ii)
                         increasing general awarness on population and reproductive health issues.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Following its restructuring, the project has achieved substantial progress in the areas of communication activities, Reproductive Health (RH) services provision, and capacity building.
                         The major Key Performance Indicators have also progressed well, in particular the exclusive breastfeeding for children aged 0-5 months and the contraceptive prevalence rate (for
                         modern methods). However, the attribution of these results to project activities will require additional data as well as a strengthened Monitoring and Evaluation system. A new
