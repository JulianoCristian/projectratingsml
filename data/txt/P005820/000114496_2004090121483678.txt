           Re:  Credit No. 1983 YAR
                (Eastern Region Agricultural Development Project)
                Amendment to the Credit Agreement
Excellency:
          Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement, dated
May 24, 1989, between the International Development Association and
the Yemen Arab Republic (the "Credit Agreement").  Pursuant to the
telex dated September 19, 1991, from Dr. Jaffer Hamed, we hereby
agree to amend the Credit Agreement as follows:
1.        The name of the Borrower, "Yemen Arab Republic", set
forth on the Cover Page, in the first paragraph of page 1, in the
address on page 9 and on the signature line on page 10 of the Credit
Agreement is changed to the "Republic of Yemen".
2.        The definition of "ARA" set forth in paragraph (a) of
Section 1.02 is changed to read as follows:
          "(a) 'AREA' means the Agricultural Research and
          Extension Authority established and operating under the
          Borrower's Law No. 32 of 1983, and any successor
          thereto;"
and all references to ARA in the Credit Agreement are amended to
refer to AREA.
3.        The definition of "MAF" set forth in paragraph (f) of
Section 1.02 is changed to read as follows:
          "(b) 'MAWR' means the Borrower's Ministry of Agriculture
          and Water Resources, and any successor thereto;"
and all references to MAF in the Credit Agreement are amended to
refer to MAWR.
4.        The date set forth in Section 3.05 is changed from
December 31, 1989 to December 31, 1992.
5.        In Section 3.06, subsection (i) in paragraphs (a) and
(c) are deleted.
6.        The person designated in Section 6.01 as representative
of the Borrower is changed from the "Minister of Development and
Chairman of the Central Planning Organization" to the "Minister of
Planning and Development" and the address for notices designated in
Section 6.02 is changed from the "Central Planning Organization" to
the "Ministry of Planning and Development".

8.        Schedule 2 is hereby amended as follows:
     (a) Part A (1) is amended to read as follows:
     "1.  Construction of about ten housing units and provision of
     furniture, vehicles and equipment for ERADA headquarters in
     Marib."
     (b) Part A (4) is deleted, and new Parts A (4) and A (5) are
added:
     "4.       Construction of about 12.5 kilometers of gravel
     feeder roads, and rehabilitation of about 40 kilometers of
     existing gravel roads in the Project Area."
     "5.  Stabilization of sand dunes in the Project Area to limit
     encroachment of sand on agricultural land and villages."
     (c) Part B (1) is amended to read as follows:
     "1.  Construction of buildings, including approximately seven
     extension centers and one main center in Marib, and provision
     of furniture, vehicles and equipment."
Page  3
     (d) Part C (3) is amended to read as follows:
     "3.  Construction of approximately 12.5 kilometers of
     secondary canals; design of the tertiary canal distribution
     system for irrigation of approximately 1,750 hectares; and
     construction of pilot tertiary canal blocks, irrigation pipe
     systems and land levelling for demonstration purposes."
9.        Schedule 4 is amended as follows:
     (a)  The first sentence of Paragraph 1 (a) of Schedule 4 is
amended to read as follows:
     "(a) The Borrower shall maintain the permanent Water Advisory
     Group for the purposes of ensuring the proper operation of the
     Marib Scheme and the distribution of water according to an
     agreed irrigation plan.  The Water Advisory Group shall
     consist of representatives of ERADA, the Governor of Marib and
     the local population."
     (b)  Paragraph 2 of Schedule 4 is deleted.
     (c)  The date set forth in Paragraph 5 of Schedule 4 is
changed from June 30, 1989 to December 31, 1992.
     (d)  Paragraph 6 of Schedule 4 is amended to read as follows:
     "The Borrower shall conduct an evaluation of the progress in
     implementing the Project with terms of reference satisfactory
     to the Association, and shall submit not later than June 30,
     1994 a report of such scope and detail as shall be acceptable
     to the Association containing, inter alia, the findings and
     recommendations, if any, on measures required to be taken to
     ensure the achievement of the objectives of the Project."
     (e) New Paragraphs 7, 8, 9, 10, 11 and 12 are added to
Schedule 4 as follows:
     "7.  Prior to issuance of a request for bids to construct the
     secondary canals under Part C (3) of the Project, the Borrower
     shall cause all such action to be taken as shall be necessary
     to acquire all such land and rights in respect of land as
     shall be required for construction of such secondary canals."
     "8.  Prior to issuance of a request for bids for the
     installation of wells for sand dune stabilization under Part
     A (5) of the Project, the Borrower shall cause all such action
     to be taken as shall be necessary to acquire all such land and
     rights in respect of land and water as shall be required for
     the installation of such wells.  In order to ensure the proper
     stabilization of the sand dunes in the Project Area, the
     Borrower shall prevent trespassing on shelter belts and
     unauthorized use of water from wells installed under the sand
     dune stabilization program."
     "9.  The Borrower shall, by June 30, 1992, employ a
     consultant for sand dune stabilization in accordance with the
     provisions of Section II of Schedule 3 to this Agreement to
     assist in carrying out Part A (5) of the Project."
     "10. The Borrower shall cause ERADA to select individuals
     with qualifications and experience satisfactory to the
     Association to fill the positions of internal auditor and
     director of finance and shall appoint the individuals selected
     to the respective positions not later than December 31, 1992."
     "11. The Borrower shall cause ERADA to select individuals
     with qualifications and experience satisfactory to the
     Association to fill the positions of financial advisor and
Page  4
     monitoring and evaluation specialist and shall appoint the
     individuals selected to the respective positions not later
     than December 31, 1992."
     "12. The Borrower shall cause AREA to select an individual
     with qualifications and experience satisfactory to the
     Association to fill the position of research advisor and shall
     appoint such individual not later than December 31, 1992."
          Please confirm your agreement to the amendment by
signing and dating the form of confirmation on the enclosed copy of
this letter and returning said copy to us.
