                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has now completed about 160 small-scale infrastructure subprojects providing more than 112,000 beneficiaries with access to improved education, health care, water
                         supply, and other priority rural services. The project will eventually reach more than 300,000 rural beneficiaries (more than half the national population). With only five months
                         remaining before project close, nearly all subproject funds have been transferred to beneficiary communities and the the pace of subproject completion is increasing. Targeted
                         support to assist communities in completing their subprojects is being deployed. Satisfaction with the services received and their targeting of community needs is very high. Nearly
