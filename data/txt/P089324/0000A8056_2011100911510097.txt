                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Guyana's Education For All Fast Track Initiative (EFA-FTI) continues at a steady rate of implementation. The Continuous Professional Development for Trained Teacher's Program
                         (CPD) has developed 24 courses that will be delivered this summer.

                         School feeding and School Improvement Plans continue to perform well. The Community-based School Feeding Program (SFP) benefits 92 out of 138 hinterland schools which
