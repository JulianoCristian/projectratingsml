Third Amendment to the Loan Agreement 
Dear Sir: 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement, dated December 23, 1997, between the International 
Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the State of Paraná (the Borrower) for 
the above-referenced Project, as amended (the Loan Agreement), and to your letter, dated August 
18, 2003, requesting an amendment to the Loan Agreement.  We agree with your request and 
propose to amend the Loan Agreement as follows :  
1. A new Section 3.08
is added to Article III to read as follows: 
“Section 3.08.  The Borrower shall, not later than September 30, 2004, provide 
evidence, satisfactory to the Bank, that it has carried out, with the Borrower’s own 
funds, rehabilitation of at least 190 km of the Borrower’s paved road network under 
Part A.3 (b) of the Project.” 
2. The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1
is amended to read as set forth in the Annex to 
this letter. 
3. In Schedule 2
,
Part A.3 (b) of the Project
is amended to read as follows in its entirety: 
“(b) rehabilitation of approximately: (i) 6,000 kilometers of rural feeder roads, within 
the beneficiary microcatchment areas, aimed at reducing water run-off; and (ii) 638 
kilometers of the Borrower's paved roads in poor areas of the Borrower's territory.”
