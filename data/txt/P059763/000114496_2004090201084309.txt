Re:  Pilot Cultural Heritage Project  (Credit No. 3269 BOS) 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated July 5, 1999, and the Project Agreement 
dated July 5, 1999 between the International Development Association (the Association) and Bosnia 
and Herzegovina (the Borrower) for the Pilot Cultural Heritage Project (the Development Credit 
Agreement) and your letter dated April 6, 2002, requesting the Association to amend Schedule 1 of 
the Development Credit Agreement. 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association hereby has agreed to amend Development 
Credit Agreement as follows:
1.
The Closing Date set forth in Section 2.03 is extended to “December 31, 2003”.
2.
Based on your request Categories (1) (a), (b) and (c) set forth in the table of paragraph 1 of 
Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement is revised as set forth in the Annex attached to 
this amendment letter.
3.
The Project Completion Date set forth in Schedule 2 of the Development Credit Agreement is 
extended to “September 30, 2003”.
Please confirm your agreement with this amendment by countersigning, dating and returning 
to us the enclosed copy of this letter. This amendment shall become effective as of the date of 
countersignature upon receipt by the Association of the countersigned copy of this letter.
Sincerely yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
