                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has been consistently achieving and in some cases exceeding the targets. It is highly likely that the project will achieve its development objectives by the end of the


                                                                                                                                                                                                         Page 1 of 7
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                     Report No: ISR4659



                         project, i.e., March 31, 2012. The implementation progress is highly satisfactory. The project is successfully reversing the water scarcity to surplus in the project GPs, with an
                         increase of the average discharge rate from 5-8 LPM to about 18 LPM, providing renewable water for irrigation and domestic use. Such reversed trend is contributing visibly to new
                         the livelihood opportunities in the project areas.
