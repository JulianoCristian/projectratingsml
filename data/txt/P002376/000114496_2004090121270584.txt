Re:
Credit No. 2759-SE (Private  Sector Capacity Building 
Project)
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
Dear Sir:
We refer to the above-captioned Development Credit Agreement, (the Agreement), 
dated August 8, 1995, between Republic of Senegal (the Borrower) and International 
Development Association (the Association). Further to discussions held between 
representatives of the Borrower and the Association, we are pleased to inform you that 
the Association agrees to amend the percentage of expenditures to be financed by the 
Association in Category 7 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement to read:  “100% until 
November 30, 2000, and 85% thereafter.”
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing by countersigning and dating 
the form of confirmation on the two original copies of this letter and returning one 
countersigned copy to us.
Sincerely,
Mahmood A. Ayub 
Country Director, Senegal
