﻿NICARAGUA - CREDIT NUMBER 966-NI
AMENDING AGREEMENT
Agreement, dated   6kii      r   , 1980, between Republic of
Nicaragua (hereinafter called the Borrower), International Bank
for Reconstruction and Development (hereinafter called the Bank)
and International Development Association (hereinafter called the
Association).
WHEREAS by a Development Credit Agreement (hereinafter called
the Credit Agreement) dated January 4, 1980, between the Borrower
and the Association, the Association has made a credit to the
Borrower in an amount in various currencies equivalent to ten
million United States dollars ($10,000,000), on the terms and
conditions set forth in the Credit Agreement;
WHEREAS by a Loan Agreement (hereinafter called the Loan
Agreement) dated January 4, 1980, between the Borrower and the
Bank, the Bank has agreed to make available to the Borrower an
amount in various currencies equivalent to twenty million United
States dollars ($20,000,000), on the terms and conditions set
forth in the Loan Agreement;
WHEREAS by a Project Agreement (hereinafter called the
Project Agreement) dated January 4, 1980, between the Associa-
tion and Banco Nacional de Desarrollo (hereinafter called BND),
BND undertook to carry out the Project through Fondo Especial de
Desarrollo (hereinafter called Fondo);
WHEREAS Decree No. 463 of the Borrower, dated-*July 2, 1980,
abrogated Decree No. 43 of the Borrower, dated August 15, 1979,
entrusting to Corporacion Financiera de Nicaragua "CORFIN" (here-
inafter called CORFIN) the operation of Fondo;
WHEREAS the Borrower, the Bank and the Association have
agreed to certain changes in the Credit Agreement and in the Loan
Agreement, and, for such purpose, have agreed to enter into this
Agreement (hereinafter called this Agreement), upon the terms and
conditions hereinafter set forth;
NOW THEREFORE, it is hereby agreed as follows:


﻿-2-
ARTICLE I
Section 1.01. The Credit Agreement is amended as follows:
(a) Fifth and Seventh WHEREAS clauses, Sections 1.02 (a),
1.02 (ff), 4.01 (a) and Schedule 2, Parts C (1) and C (2). Delete
"BND" and substitute therefor "CORFIN";
(b) Section 1.02 (cc). Delete in its entirety and substitute
therefor the following:
"(cc) "Decree No. 463 means Decree No. 463 of the
Borrower dated July 2, 1980 creating CORFIN, and under which
the operation of Fondo was entrusted to CORFIN, as such
Decree may be amended from time to time;"
(c) Section 1.02 (dd). Delete in its entirety, renumbering
the subsequent paragraphs accordingly;
(d) Section 1.02 (ee). Delete in its entirety and substitute
therefor the following:
"(ee) Whenever reference in this Agreement shall be
made to action by CORFIN or Fondo, it shall be deemed to mean
action by CORFIN taken pursuant to authority of CORFIN vested
in it by Decree No. 463;"
(e) Section 4.01 (e). Delete in its entirety and substitute
therefor the following:
"(e) a change shall have been made in the Law or in
Decree No. 463 which would materially and adversely affect
the operations or the financial condition of Fondo with
respect to the carrying out of the Project.";
(f) Section 1.02. Insert new paragraph (gg), which shall
read:
"(gg) "BND" means Banco Nacional de Desarrollo, as created
by Decree No. 22 of the Borrower dated July 25, 1979."
ARTICLE II
Section 2.01. The Loan Agreement is amended as follows:
"Section 2.03. Delete "Banco Nacional de Desarollo" and
substitute therefor "Corporacion Financiera de Nicaragua (COR-
FIN)".


﻿-3-
ARTICLE III
Section 3.01. This Agreement shall become effective upon
receipt by the Association and the Bank of an opinion or opinions
satisfactory to the Association and the Bank of Counsel acceptable
to the Association and the Bank showing that:
(a) the person or persons executing and delivering this
Agreement are duly authorized to act on behalf of the Borrower for
such purpose;
(b) the persons executing and delivering the Amending
Agreement for the Project Agreement are duly authorized to act on
behalf of CORFIN and on behalf of BND, respectively, for such
purpose; and
(c) this Agreement and the Amending Agreement for the
Project Agreement have been duly authorized or ratified by, and
are legally binding upon, the Borrower, CORFIN and BND, in accord-
ance with their terms.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto, acting through
their respective representatives thereunto duly authorized have
caused this Agreement to be signed in their respective names, and
delivered in the District of Columbia, United States of America,
as of the day and year above written.
