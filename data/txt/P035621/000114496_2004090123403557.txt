Ministry of Economy, Finance and Planning 
Dakar, Senegal
Re:
Credit No. 2873-SE (Female Literacy Project)
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
Dear Sir:
We refer to the above Development Credit Agreement, (the Agreement), dated 
June 17, 1996, between Republic of Senegal (the Borrower) and International 
Development Association (the Association). Further to discussions held between 
representatives of the Borrower and the Association, we are pleased to inform you that 
the Association agrees to the following amendment to Schedule 1 to the Agreement 
(a)
The amount in Category 3 will be reduced from 750,000 SDR to 350,000 SDR.
(b)
Category 8 will be created as AGETIP Management Fees for an amount 
equivalent to 450,000 SDR.
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing by countersigning and dating 
the form of confirmation on the two original copies of this letter and returning one 
countersigned copy to us.
Sincerely,
By:
Mahmood A. Ayub 
Country Director, Senegal
