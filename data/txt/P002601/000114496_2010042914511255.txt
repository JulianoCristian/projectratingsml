      Re: Credit No. 1961 SU (Southern Kassala Agricultural Project)
          AMENDMENTS  TO DEVELOPMENT CREDIT AGREEMENT
Dear Sir:
      We refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated April 26,
1989 between the Republic of Sudan (the Borrower) and the
International Development Association (the Association), and to your
telex dated May 8, 1991 requesting amendments to the Development
Credit Agreement in view of the inability of the United Nation's
High Commission for Refugees to meet its commitments under the
Project.  The Association agrees to your request and consequently
proposes to hereby make the following amendments to the Development
Credit Agreement:
      1. Paragraph (B) of the Preamble is amended to read as
follows:
      "(B) the Borrower has received from the United Nations High
Commission for Refugees a grant in an amount equivalent to four
hundred and fifty nine thousand dollars (the Grant) to assist
in@financing part of the project on the terms and conditions set
forth in an agreement of even date hereto entered into between the
Borrower and the Association as Administrator of the Grant funds;"
      2. Article V is hereby deleted and the subsequent Articles and
Sections therein renumbered accordingly.
      3. The table  in Paragraph 1 of  Schedule  1  is modified to
read  in
its  entirety as  follows:
