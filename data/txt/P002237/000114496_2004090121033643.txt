     Credit No. 2272-RW (Population Project)
                Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
     We refer to the above-captioned Development Credit Agreement, (the
Agreement) between Rwandese Republic (the Borrower) and International
Development Association (the Association), dated July 3, 1991. We also
refer to your letter, dated November 20, 1995, requesting the Associa-
tion to amend the Agreement.
     This letter is to inform you that the Association has agreed to
the request and proposes the following amendments:

B.   Schedule 2
     Part B.1 of Schedule 2 to the Agreement shall be amended to read
as follows:
     "Strengthening existing and establishing new family planning posts
     including acquisition of equipment, contraceptives, drugs and
     medical supplies; rehabilitation of health centres in the regions
     of Gikongoro and Cyangugu; and training of staff of such posts."
C.   Schedule 3
1.   The words "and works" shall be added after the word "goods" on the
first line of Part A.1.
2.   The words "and works" shall be added after the word "goods" on the
first line of Part B.
3.   Part C.1 shall be amended to read as follows:
     "1. Works estimated to cost the equivalent of less than $100,000
     per contract and goods estimated to cost the equivalent of less
     than $300,000 per contract, up to an aggregate amount equivalent
     to $1,000,000, may be procured under contracts awarded on the
     basis of competitive bidding, advertised locally, in accordance
     with procedures satisfactory to the Association."
4.   The following new paragraph C.3 is added:
     "Drugs may be procured from the UNICEF Procurement and Assembly
     Center (UNIPAC), in accordance with procedures acceptable to the
     Association."
D.   Schedule 4
1.   Paragraph 1 (a) shall be amended to read as follows:
     "(a) The term "eligible Categories" means Categories (1) to (4)
     and (6) set forth in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to
     this Agreement."
2.   The words "cost of goods and services" on line 2 of paragraph 1
(b) shall be amended to read "cost of goods, works and services".
     All other provisions of the Agreement shall remain in full force
     and effect. These amendments shall become effective upon the date
     of countersignature by your authorized representative.
     Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing by countersigning
and dating the form of confirmation and returning one original as
countersigned to us.
