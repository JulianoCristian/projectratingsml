Dear Mr. Minister:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated May 31. 1989, between the Federative 
Republic of Brazil (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank).
Following discussions between Companhia de Desenvolvimento do Vale do Sao 
Francisco (CODEVASF) and the Bank, and to avoid delays in the start-up of civil works 
construction under the
project, the Bank is pleased to inform you of its agreement to amend Category (i) of 
Schedule 1 of the Loan Agreement as follows:
                        Amount of the
