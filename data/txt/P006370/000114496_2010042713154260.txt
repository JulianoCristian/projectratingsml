Re:
Brazil - Loan 3013-BR (Jaiba Irrigation
Project) Second Amendment to the Loan Agreement
 Dear Madam Minister:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated May 31, 1989, between the Federative 
Republic of Brazil (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), as
amended.
Following discussions between Companhia de Desenvolvimento do Vale do Sao 
Francisco (CODEVASF) and the Bank, and to avoid further delays with the construction 
of civil works under the project, the Bank is pleased to inform you of its agreement 
to amend Category (1) of the table in paragraph a of Schedule 1 of the Loan Agreement 
as follows:
                        Amount of the
                        Loan Allocated             % of
                        (Expressed in           Expenditures
   Category             Dollars Equivalent)     to be Financed
(1) civil works         24,000.000              100% of Foreign
                                                Expenditures and
                                                35% of Local
                                                Expenditures
If you are in agreement with the foregoing amendment please sign and return the 
enclosed confirmation copy of this letter.
Because of their interest in this matter, I am sending copies of this letter to 
Dr. Hilberto Mascarenhas Alves da Silva, General Secretary, Secretaria Nacional de 
Irrigacao (SEMIR); Dr. Erasmo Jose de Almeida, President, Companhia de Desenvolvimento 
do Vale do Sao Francisco (CODEVASF); Presidente, Dr. Ibrahim Eris, Banco Central do 
Brasil (BACEN); and Dr. Clodoaldo Huguenty Filho,
Director, Departamento de Assuntos Internacionais (DEAIN).
