Your Excellency:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement entered into between the Argentine Republic 
(the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the 
Bank) on August 13, 1991, for the above Project.  Pursuant to the request submitted to 
the Bank by Mr. Felipe C. Sola, Secretary of Agriculture, Livestock and Fisheries, the 
Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
