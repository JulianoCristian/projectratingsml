Dear Mrs. Greciani,
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Social Investment Fund Project) dated March 
17, 1999, between the Republic of Moldova and the International Development Association (the 
Association). We also refer to your letter dated June 10, 2002, requesting certain amendments to the 
Development Credit Agreement.
We hereby confirm the Association’s agreement to the following amendment:

to read as set forth in the Attachment to this letter.
Please confirm your agreement to this amendment by countersigning, dating and returning to us 
the enclosed copy of this letter. This amendment will become effective as of the date of countersignature 
upon receipt by the Bank of the countersigned copy of this letter.
