Pursuant to your letter dated October 12,  2003, the Association hereby agrees to 
amend the Development Credit Agreement and Grant Agreement, to:  a) extend the 
Closing Date of both Agreements; b) reallocate part of the proceeds of the Credit and 
Grant Agreements; c) increase the Authorized Allocation of the Grant Agreement for the 
FPS Swedish Special Account and; d) create a new Category 5 in the Grant Agreement.  
The use of this new Category will be retroactive up to 12 months prior to the date of 
effectiveness of this amendment.
