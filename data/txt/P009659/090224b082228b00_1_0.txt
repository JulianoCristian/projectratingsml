﻿AN AGREEMENT made this 24th day of November 1972 between THE
GOVERNOR OF STATE OF MAHARASHTRA (hereinafter called "the Governor")
and INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (hereinafter called "the
Association");
WHEREAS by a Development Credit Agreement dated March 29, 1972 and
made between India, acting by its President, (hereinafter called "the Borrower")
and the Association (hereinafter called "the Development Credit Agreement") the
Association has agreed to make available to the Borrower an amount in various
currencies equivalent to thirty million dollars ($30,000,000), on the terms and
conditions set forth in the Development Credit Agreement, but only on condition
inter alia that the State of Maharashtra (hereinafter called "Maharashtra") agrees
to undertake such obligations toward the Association as therein set forth;
AND WHEREAS by a Project Agreement of same date made between the
Association, of the one part, and Agricultural Refinance Corporation and
Maharashtra State Co-operative Land Development Bank Limited, of the other part,
the Agricultural Refinance Corporation and Maharashtra State Co-operative Land
Development Bank Limited, respectively, have agreed to undertake certain
obligations in respect of the carrying out of the Maharashtra Agricultural Credit
Project described in Schedule 2 to the Development Credit Agreement (hereinafter
called "the Project");
AND WHEREAS by further Agreement dated March 29, 1972 and expressed
to be made between the State of Maharashtra of the one part and the Association
of the other part (hereinafter called "the Maharashtra Agreement") in consideration
of the Association entering into the Development Credit Agreement with the
Borrower, the State of Maharashtra agreed to undertake the obligations therein
set forth;
AND WHEREAS the Maharashtra Agreement has been executed for and on
behalf of the State of Maharashtra and the parties thereto have already prior hereto
acted thereupon;
AND WHEREAS doubts have now arisen about the legality of the form
in which the Maharashtra Agreement has been executed;
AND WHEREAS since March 29, 1972 the parties to the Maharashtra
Agreement have already been acting upon the Maharashtra Agreement and it has
now been agreed between the parties to execute an Agreement in the proper form
being in fact these presents;


﻿4
NOW, THEREFORE, THE PARTIES HERETO HEREBY AGREE that in
consideration of the Association entering into the Development Credit Agreement
with the Borrower, the Governor doth hereby agree that the State of Maharashtra
doth undertake all the obligations set forth in the Maharashtra Agreement as if
the provisions contained in the various Articles of the Maharashtra Agreement had
been specifically set out herein.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto, acting through their
representatives thereunto duly authorised, have caused this Agreement to be signed
in their respective names and delivered in the District of Columbia, United States
of America, as of the day and year first above written.
For and on behalf of the Governor
of the State of Maharashtra
