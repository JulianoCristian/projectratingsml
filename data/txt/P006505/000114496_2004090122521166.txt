Re.:  Mato Grosso Natural Resources Management Project
(Loan Number 3492-BR) 
Amendment to the Loan and Project Agreements
Dear Sirs:
We refer to the Loan Agreement between the Federative Republic of 
Brazil (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), and to the Project Agreement between the Bank and 
the State of Mato Grosso (the State), both dated September 16, 1992, for 
the above referenced Project.  We are pleased to inform you of our 
agreement to amend the Loan Agreement and the Project Agreement as follows:
General
1.
Wherever it appears in the Loan and Project Agreements, the term 
“SDR-PR” is replaced with the term “MPO-SEPRE”.
