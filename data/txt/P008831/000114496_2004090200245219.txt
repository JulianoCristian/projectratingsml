Re: 
Loan No. 4035-RU (Legal Reform Project) 
 
Amendments to the Loan Agreement 
 
Dear Mr. Kolotoukhin: 
 
We refer to the Loan Agreement dated June 21, 1996 for the Legal Reform Project (the 
Project) between the Russian Federation (the Borrower) and the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), and your letter Reference No. 27-00-09/1402 dated 
November 27, 2000 to the Bank requesting certain amendments to the Loan Agreement. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank accedes to your request and hereby amends 
the Loan Agreement as follows: 
 
1. 
In Section 2.03
,
the date is changed to “June 30, 2002”. 
 
2. Schedule 
1
(Withdrawals of the Proceeds of the Loan) is hereby amended. The revised 
Schedule 1 is attached hereto as Annex 1. 
 
3. 
Schedule 2, Part D
(Description of the Project) is hereby amended to read as follows: 
 
“Part D
:
Court Reform and Alternative Dispute Resolution
1. 
Strengthening of the functional capacity of the Judicial Department needed for the design 
and establishment of the new court administration system and provision of technical assistance, 
training and computer equipment needed therefor. 
 
2. 
Assistance in the establishment of the Academy of Justice with a view to build capacities 
needed for the design of the future judicial training programs which will include innovative 
teaching techniques and methodologies on the basis of improvement of the Academy of Justice’s 
technological capacities. 
 
3. 
Expanding alternative dispute mechanisms through training, provision of advisory 
services, materials and equipment.

2. 
For the purposes of this Schedule: 
 
(a) 
the term “foreign expenditures” means expenditures in the currency of any 
country other than that of the Borrower for goods or services supplied from the territory of any 
country other than that of the Borrower;   
 
(b) 
the term “local expenditures” means expenditures in the currency of the 
Borrower or for goods or services supplied from the territory of the Borrower; 
 
(c) 
 the term “Incremental Operating Costs” means Project related operating costs 
and expenses, including incremental staffing costs, rental, communications, utilities, office 
equipment and supplies, travel and subsistence allowance, interpretation and translation, and such 
other  incremental office operational costs and expenses as may be agreed by the Bank; and 
 
(d) 
the term “training programs” means Project related costs and expenses related to 
study tours, seminars, workshops and other training activities which may be carried out through a 
consultant services contract or by other means; 
 
3. 
Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph 1 above, no withdrawals shall be made in 
respect of payments made for: (a) expenditures prior to the date of this Agreement; and (b) 
expenditures to be financed out of the proceeds of the Loan allocated to any Disbursement 
Category of this Schedule for training programs carried out by other means except through a 
consultants services contract, until the Bank shall have been furnished with the information, 
acceptable to the Bank, relating to the objective, content and selection of the beneficiaries of the 
training activities concerned, and the proposed budget therefor, as required pursuant to the 
provisions of paragraph 1 of Section VIII of Schedule 2 of the Project Agreement supplemental to 
this Agreement.   
 
4. 
The Bank may require withdrawals from the Loan Account to be made on the basis of 
statements of expenditure for expenditures for: (a) goods under contracts costing less than 
$100,000 equivalent; and (b) services under contracts, in case of firms, costing less than $100,000 
equivalent and, in case of individual, costing less than $50,000 equivalent, under such terms and 
conditions as the Bank shall specify by notice to the Borrower.
