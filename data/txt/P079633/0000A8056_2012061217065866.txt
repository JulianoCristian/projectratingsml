                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The development objectives are to: (a) accelerate, in a commercially viable manner, the use of electricity for economic growth and social services and thus improve the
                         quality of life in un-served and underserved areas (peri-urban and rural); (b) improve governance and ensure the financial sustainability of the sector, through the
                         involvement of the private sector in partnership with the public sector; (c) reduce deforestation and increase access and diversify choice to renewable and cleaner fuels
                         to the household and SME sectors; and (d) promote regional cooperation while protecting the environment in a sustainable manner.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Implementation progress is steady and disbursement is more than 80%. Procurement and financial management are making satisfactory progress. No special safeguard problem to
                         report so far. The implementation of the biomass component is improving and the impact on communities is positive.The financial situation of the distribution utility improved as a
                         result of the project. The Project Development Objectives remains achievable.
