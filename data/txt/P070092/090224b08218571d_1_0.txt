                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Objectives of the Project are to assist the Recipient in: (a) protecting residents, economic activities and infrastructure from destructive effects of seasonal flooding
                         in Taiz, Hadramout and AlMahra; (b) restoring access to critical road infrastructure damaged by the floods; and (c) strenhgthening the capacity of local governments and
                         support decentralization.
