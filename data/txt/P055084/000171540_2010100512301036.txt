Re: 
Guatemala:  Japan Social Development Fund Grant for Capacity Building 
for Indigenous People and Local NGOs in the Western Altiplano.  
 
First Amendment to the JSDF Grant No. TF055809
Dear Minister Balsells and Secretary Slowing,  
 
Please refer to the JSDF Grant Agreement dated October 19, 2006 (the “Agreement”) 
entered into between the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the “Bank”), 
and the Republic of Guatemala (the “Recipient”), for the Project referred to above. Terms not 
otherwise defined herein shall have the meanings assigned to them in the Agreement. 
 
Pursuant to the request contained in your letter dated April 22, 2010, requesting the Bank 
to increase the disbursement ceiling, we are pleased to inform you that the Bank agrees to amend 
the terms of the Agreement as follows:  
 
1.  Paragraph 1(c) of Attachment II to the Grant Agreement is hereby amended to read as 
follows: 
 
“(c)  
the term “Authorized Allocation” means the amount of two hundred 
thousand United State Dollars (US$200,000) to be withdrawn from the Grant 
account and deposited into the Special Account pursuant to paragraph 3(a) of this 
Attachment.” 
 
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and dating this 
letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter shall be executed in two counterparts, 
each of which shall be an original.  The provisions of this Amendment will become effective as of
