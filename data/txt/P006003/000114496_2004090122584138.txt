Re:
Loan No. 3611-AR (Road Maintenance and Rehabilitation Sector 
Project) Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank) and the Argentine Republic (the Borrower) dated October 1, 
1993 (the Loan Agreement), as amended, entered into with respect to the 
above-captioned Project.  We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to the 
Borrower's request and our recent discussions on the subject, the Bank agrees to amend 
said Loan Agreement as follows:
Section 1.02
1.
Paragraph (j) of this Section is hereby amended by inserting the following words 
immediately prior to the semi-colon at the end thereof:  "and as such program may be 
reflected in REMA contracts (as such term is defined in paragraph 2 (e) of Schedule 1 
to this Agreement)".

3.
Paragraph 2 of this Schedule is hereby amended by deleting the word "and" at the 
end of subparagraph (c) thereof, replacing the period at the end of subparagraph (d) 
thereof with the word "; and" and adding a new subparagraph (e) to read as follows in 
its entirety:  "the term "REMA contract" means a multi-year contract entered into by 
DNV with a private sector contractor, which contract shall obligate the private sector 
contractor to undertake all phases of road rehabilitation and maintenance work as a 
single package, from design and programming of works, through the execution of such 
works.".
Schedule 4
4.
Section I, Part C, paragraph 2 of this Schedule is hereby amended by inserting 
the following immediately prior to the period at the end thereof:  ", but only if such 
works are financed in part by proceeds allocated to disbursement Category (2) in the 
table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement".
5.
Section I, Part D, paragraph 1 (a) of this Schedule is hereby amended by 
replacing the words "and (ii) the first three contracts" with the words "(ii) each 
REMA contract (as such term is defined in paragraph 2 (e) of Schedule 1 to this 
Agreement); and (iii) the first three contracts (other than REMA contracts)".
Schedule 5
6.
Paragraph 1 (a) of this Schedule is hereby amended by replacing the words 
"Categories 1, 2, 3 and 4" with the words "Categories 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5".
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter shall be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us.  The provisions of this amendment letter 
will become effective upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this 
amendment letter.  Because of his interest in this matter, a copy of this amendment 
letter is being sent to the Administrador General of the Direccion Nacional de 
Vialidad.
