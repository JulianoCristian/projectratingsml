                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Yemen Emergency Crisis Recovery Project (ECRP) was signed on February 27, 2013, became effective on May 8, 2013, and is scheduled to close on June 30, 2014. The
                         objective of the ECRP is to assist the Government of Yemen in mitigating the impact of the 2011 crisis by providing cash benefits to eligible poor households (about 400,000 poor
                         households). It is providing cash assistance to about 400,000 households which have been added to the Social Welfare Fund (SWF) program in 2011 using the Proxy Means
                         Targeting (PMT). To date, US$ 85 m of the total US$100 m has been disbursed.
