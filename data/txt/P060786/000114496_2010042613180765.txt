Your Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Arrangement dated May 24, 1995 as amended, between the Netherlands 
Ministry for Development Cooperation (the Donor) and the International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (“IBRD”) and the International Development Association (“IDA”) (collectively 
referred to as “the Bank”) to support the preparation of the Public Sector Reform Project (now 
referred to as the Public Sector Modernization Project)-Grant Number TF 050298, NL Ref 
AM004403.   We also refer to the correspondence from Mr. Taco Stoppels, Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs, dated July 15, 2003, approving the extension of the Arrangement’s termination date 
(attached). We are pleased to inform you that the Bank has amended the Arrangement as follows: 
 
1. 
Paragraph 4 of the Amendment is amended to read as follows: 
 
“The support will be provided for the period from September 1, 2001 through December 
31, 2004”. 
 
2. 
The first sentence of  paragraph 5 of the Arrangement is amended to read as follows: 
 
“It is expected that the Grant funds will be fully utilized in accordance with the provision 
of the Arrangement by Dec 31, 2004.” 
 
All the terms and conditions of the Arrangement, as amended hereby, shall remain in full 
force and effect.
