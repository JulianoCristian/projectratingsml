                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Under the new Acting CEO, for first registrations of buildings and apartments from the pilot Building Cadastre Construction (BCC) field surveys were carried out in 2006 and 2007,
                         which is a welcome breakthrough and a positive step to meeting the PDOs.
                         The Business Services Integration Component has been successfully progressed with launching of 8 pilot Municipal Business Centers (MBCs) having progress in inspection and
                         licensing review, and advancement in procurement activities. .
