Dear Excellency, 
 
Amendment to Development Grant Agreement for the Emergency Living Conditions 
Improvement Project (Grant H164)
We refer to the Financing Agreement for the Emergency Living Conditions Improvement 
Project (the “Financing Agreement”) between International Development Association (IDA), and 
Democratic Republic of Congo (the “Recipient”) dated June 28, 2005. We also refer to the 
understandings reached between the Recipient and the Association to amend certain provisions of the 
Agreement. 
Accordingly, the Agreement is hereby amended as follows: 
1. 
Section 1.02 (y) and (z) of the Financing Agreement are added as follows: 
“(y) “Emergency Economic and Social Reunification Project“ or “PUSPRES” means 
Project carried out under the Development Financing Agreement between the 
Recipient and the Association, dated September 22, 2003, as amended to the date of 
this Agreement (Grant  No H064- DRC and Credit No 3824-DRC); and 
(z) PUSPRES RAP” means the 
plan, in form and substance satisfactory to the 
Association, describing measures for the mitigation of the
 PUSPRES’s impacts on 
Displaced Persons, prepared on August 3, 2010, by the Recipient pursuant to the 
Resettlement Policy Framework prepared under the PUSPRES January 1, 2006. 
2. 
The Closing Date referred to in Section 2.03 of the Financing Agreement is extended 
to February 29, 2012 for Parts A, B and E of the Project.
