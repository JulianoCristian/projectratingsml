                         Implementation Status Overview
                           The Project continues to make significant progress in implementing the corrective action plan for clearing the backlog of conditional cash transfer (CCT) payments. The backlog of
                         CCT payments has now been cleared for the January-December 2012 time period, and CCT backlog payments for the period January 2013 onwards are ongoing, and are expected
                         to be cleared by July 2013. The Project has now officially appointed a new Director. Disbursement of funds has accelerated and financial management and implementation have
