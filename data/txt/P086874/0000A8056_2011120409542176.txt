                         Implementation Status Overview
                         By end October 2011, FSRDC had selected and approved 196 sub-projects, or about 70 percent of all the sub-projects that will be financed under the First Additional Financing


                                                                                                                                                                                                         Page 1 of 7
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                                 Report No: ISR5222



                         (AF1) that covers all of DRC's eleven provinces. The approved sub-projects include 55 schools, 47 markets, 41 water and 34 road projects and 12 health centers. By end October,
                         80 out of the approved sub-projects were under execution and two sub-projects had been completed.

                         The Second Additional Financing (AF2) of PASU that will benefit local communities in the Seke Banza Territory in the Bas Congo province was declared effective on October 19,
Public Disclosure Copy




                         2011. FSRDC is now in the process of selecting and approving sub-projects that will be financed under this AF. This process is expected to be completed by mid-January 2012.

                         Concerning the Labor Intensive Public Works Project in Katanga, by end October 2011, a total of 26 LIPW sub-projects have been completed and eight sub-projects are under
                         implementation. To date, more than 222,000 person days of employment have been created benefiting 2341 men and 1032 women.
