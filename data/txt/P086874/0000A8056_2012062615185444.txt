                         Implementation Status Overview
                         FSRDC is working with 662 communities in all 11 provinces. The approved sub-projects include 370 schools, 44 markets, 72 water and sanitation, 29 transport and 83 health


                                                                                                                                                                                                         Page 1 of 8
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                       Report No: ISR7091



                         centers. On average enrolment rates in schools built through the project have increased 65 % and utilization rates in health centers have increased 72%. Over 1 million people have
                         benefited from the project. Under the Labor Intensive Public Works (LIPW) component more than 222,000 person days of employment have been created.

                         Currently, under the first additional financing, out of the 340 approved sub-projects, 17 have been completed and 158 are ongoing. For the second additional financing, out of the 60

                         approved sub-projects, 11 are ongoing. Under the LIPW project, to date about 220,000 person days of work have been created which has benefited more than 3500 women and
                         men.

                         The problem of transferring FSRD's project accounts to the BCC, which significantly slowed down project implementation, was recently resolved.
