Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement for the above-referenced Project, dated June 
29,1995, as amended, between the Republic of Bulgaria (the Borrower) and the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank). We also refer to 
your letter of June 10, 1999, requesting the Bank to amend the said Loan Agreement in 
order to fund the procurement and installation of  heat regulators under Part C of the 
Project by (i) using expected cost savings under Part C of the Project in the amount 
of US$4.5 million equivalent, and (ii) reallocating US$50,000 and DM 2.6 million, 
currently allocated to fund civil works under the said Part C, for this purpose.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank acceeds to your request. Accordingly, 
the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
1. 
Part C of Schedule 2 of the Loan Agreement will be modified to read:
“Part C: District Heating Pilot
Procuring and installing heat meters and regulator sets for buildings in 
the fifteen 
heating systems of the Borrower included in the Project.”\02\0d
