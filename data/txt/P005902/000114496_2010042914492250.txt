Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement  
Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Southern Governorates Rural 
Development Project) between the Republic of Yemen (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association (the Association) dated November 5, 1997, as 
amended (the Credit Agreement) and to your letter of December 15, 1999, requesting the 
amendment of the Credit Agreement to include the carrying out of a clean up operation 
of pesticide contamination at Surdud farm in the Tehama Region.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association, after due consideration, has 
acceded to the above request.  Accordingly, the Credit Agreement is hereby amended as 
follows:
1.
