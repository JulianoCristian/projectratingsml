Credit No. 2566-CV - Public Sector Reform & Capacity Building
Project Amendment to the Development Credit
Reallocation of Credit Proceeds
We refer to the above-mentioned Development Credit Agreement, dated February 16, 
1994, (the Agreement), between the Republic of Cape Verde (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association (the Association).  Further to the discussions 
held between representatives of the Borrower and the Association during the mid-term 
review of the project in December 1996, and further to your letter dated December 20, 
1996, we are pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to modify the SCHEDULE 
I, paragraph 1, regarding the reallocation of proceeds of the Credit, as shown in the 
attachment.  The amended Schedule I, shall come into force and effect as of the date 
of this letter.
Sincerely,
By /s/ Mahmood A. Ayub
