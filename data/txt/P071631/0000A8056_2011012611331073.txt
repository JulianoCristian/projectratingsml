                         Implementation Status Overview
                           The project is advancing well and has all the elements in place to achieve its development objectives. For components one and two (Social mobilization and public sector
                         interventions), the project outcome indicator surpasses the target of 90 percent and stands at 99 percent for the percentage of men and women 15-49 who received an HIV test and
                         came back for their test results. Components 3 and 4 strengthening capacity building activities and coordination, management monitoring and evaluation are also progressing well.
