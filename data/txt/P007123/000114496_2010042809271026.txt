            Re:   Loan No. 3285-EC (Municipal Development and Urban
                  Infrastructure Project) Amendment to Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
      Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the International Bank for
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the Republic of Ecuador (the
Borrower) dated February 15, 1991.  We are pleased to inform you that,
pursuant to our recent discussions on the subject, the Bank agrees to amend
the Loan Agreement as follows so as to modify the percentage of expenditures
to be financed by the proceeds of the Loan, thus in effect changing into
parallel cofinancing for the Project what had originally been foreseen as
joint cofinancing with the Inter-American Development Bank:
1.    Schedule 1
