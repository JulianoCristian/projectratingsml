Mascara Emergency Reconstruction Project
Amendment to Loan Agreement                  
Excellency Harchaoui:
We refer to the Loan Agreement ("Agreement") dated December 20, 1994 entered 
into between the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the "Bank") 
and the Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria ("Algeria"), in respect of the 
Mascara Emergency Reconstruction Project ("Project"). We also refer to the recent 
project restructuring aide memoire and to the Algerian Government’s request to amend 
the Agreement to reflect the restructuring of the Project.
Therefore, by the present, the Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
(i)
Section 2.03 is revised to read: " The Closing Date shall be 
December 31, 1999, or such later date as the Bank shall establish. 
The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower of such later date."
(ii)
The table set forth in Paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is modified to
