Dear Mr. Minister, 
 
Re: Credit 3322 ALB (Water Supply Urgent Rehabilitation Project) 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the above-referenced Project, dated 
April 18, 2000, as amended, between Albania and the International Development Association (the 
Association). 
 
We also refer to your letter dated September 23, 2000, requesting the Development 
Credit Agreement be amended. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association hereby agrees to your request.  
Accordingly, the Development Credit Agreement is hereby amended as follows: 
 
1. 
The Closing Date for the Project under Section 2.03 has been extended from March 1, 
2003 to March 1, 2004. 
 
2. 
The new category for the “Management Services” is added as category (7).  Paragraph 1 
of Schedule 1 is modified as a result of reallocation and the proceeds of the Credit now read as set 
forth in the Annex to this letter. 
 
3. 
Paragraph 2 (d) is added to read: “the term ‘management services’ means management 
services under Part A (h) of the Project, comprising the entire management of the water utilities 
including but not limited to operation, maintenance, management of billing and collection 
systems and customer relations.” 
 
4. 
The threshold amount for aggregate under National Competitive Bidding method in 
paragraph 1 of Part C of Section I of Schedule 3 is changed to $1,500,000. 
 
5. 
The threshold amount for aggregate under procurement of small works in paragraph 4 of 
Part C of Section I of Schedule 3 is changed to $450,000.
