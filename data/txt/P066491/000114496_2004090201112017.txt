Re:
Credit 3322 ALB (Water Supply Urgent Rehabilitation 
Project)
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Mr. Angjeli:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the above-referenced 
Project, dated April 18, 2000, as amended, between Albania and the International 
Development Association (the Association).
We also refer to your fax dated November 23, 2000, requesting the 
Development Credit Agreement be amended.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association hereby agrees to your 
request.  Accordingly, the Development Credit Agreement is hereby amended as 
follows:
Paragraph 3(b) of Schedule 1 should read: "payments made for expenditures 
under Category (1) (a) for rehabilitation works, unless the contract for the 
private operator will be awarded by June 30, 2002.”
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by countersigning both 
copies of this amendment letter and returning one of the countersigned copies to 
us.  Upon receipt by the Association of the copy of this amendment letter 
countersigned by you, this amendment letter will become effective as of the date 
of your countersignature.  We will notify you of the receipt of your 
countersigned letter.
Sincerely,
