Re:
Loan No. 3514-VE (Judicial Infrastructure Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement between the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela (the 
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), 
dated December 30, 1993, as amended.
We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to your request dated May 2, 2001 the 
Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
Section 1.02:
(a)
Paragraphs (b) and (d) are deleted.
(b)
Paragraph (f) is deleted and replaced by:
"(f)  "Escuela" means the Borrower Judicial School, the Escuela Judicial 
operating under the Supreme Tribunal (as defined hereinafter)."
(c)
Paragraphs (n), (u) and (v), the words "by the Consejo" are deleted.
(d)
Two new definitions (dd) and (ee) are added to read as follows:
"(dd)  "DEM" means Dirección Ejecutiva de la Magistratura, the 
Borrower’s Executive Office of the Judiciary established within the Supreme 
Tribunal;"
"(ee)  "Supreme Tribunal" means Tribunal Supremo de Justicia, the 
Borrower’s Supreme Tribunal."
2.
All references to "Consejo" in: 
(a)
Paragraphs (k), (m) and (x) of Section 1.02 in Sections 3.01 through 
3.08, in paragraph (c) of Section 5.01, and Parts A and B.1 (c) (as redesignated in 
this Amendment) on Schedule 2 of the Loan Agreement are deleted and replaced by 
"Supreme Tribunal;" and
(b)
all other references to "Consejo" are deleted and replaced by "DEM."
 
3.
Schedule 1:
(a)
A new category (4) is added to read as follows:
"(4)
Works for Part B.2 
of the Project
600,000
80%"
(b)
the existing Unallocated Category (4) is designated as Category (5); and
(c)
the other Categories in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 are 
reallocated as per Attachment 1 to this letter.  Such table also reflects a 
cancellation.
4.
Schedule 2: 
Page  2
Part B is amended:
(a)
by redesignating the existing Part B as 1, and the subcomponents now 
designated (1) through (5), as (a) through (e); and 
(b)
by adding a new subcomponent (2) to read as follow:
"2. Rehabilitation of office space of the Selected Courts."
5.
Schedule 4:
Part C is amended by adding a new paragraph 3. to read as follows:
"3.
Procurement of Small Works
Works under Part B.2 of the Project may be procured under lump-sum, 
fixed-price contracts awarded on the basis of quotations obtained from three 
(3) qualified domestic contractors in response to a written invitation.  The 
invitation shall include a detailed description of the works, including basic 
specifications, the required completion date, a basic form of agreement 
acceptable to the Bank, and relevant drawings, where applicable. The award 
shall be made to the contractor who offers the lowest price quotation for the 
required work, and who has the experience and resources to complete the 
contract successfully."
If you agree with the proposed amendment, please sign the confirmation form on 
this letter.  The amendments set forth in this letter shall enter into effect upon 
receipt by the Bank of a duly executed counterpart of this letter.
