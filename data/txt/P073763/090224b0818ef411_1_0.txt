                         Project Development Objectives
                         Vietnam Water Supply Development Project (P073763)
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)
                         The project development objective is to assist the Borrower to improve water and household sanitation services in selected district towns and large urban centers in the
                         participating provinces, in ways that are financially and environmentally sustainable, thus enhancing the health and economic potential of the resident households.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Program?

                         12, 2012. Project implementation of Component A, apart of Component B (in Hai Phong and Quang Ninh) and Component C was completed. As a result, about 1.1 million peoples
                         having access to improved water supply service (original target was 1.6 million); 31,000 peoples having access to improved sanitation services (original target was 59,700 peoples)
                         and about 85,000m3 a day of water saved in Ho Chi Minh City. For the extended project components, good progress has been made in Ha Tinh under project component B as well
                         as in project components D and E implemented by MOC. However, delays were still observed in Ho Chi Minh City in regards to the construction of district metering areas (DMAs) and
