                         Project Development Objectives
                         Vietnam Water Supply Development Project (P073763)
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)
                         The project development objective is to assist the Borrower to improve water and household sanitation services in selected district towns and large urban centers in the
                         participating provinces, in ways that are financially and environmentally sustainable, thus enhancing the health and economic potential of the resident households.

                         The project is making progress towards meeting its PDO. On June 29, 2012, in response to the Government request, the Bank has agreed on (i) extension of the Credit closing date
                         for specific activities and components until June 30, 2013; (ii) partial cancellation of the Credit (from original US$ 112.6 million to revised US$ 98.02 million) and (iii) reallocation of the
                         Credit proceeds. In parallel, the project was restructured to reflect the changes during implementation with an amendment to the DCA has been sent to the Government for
                         countersignature.
Public Disclosure Copy




                         Project implementation of Component A, Hai Phong and Quang Ninh in Component B and Component C was completed. It is about 919,000 peoples having access to improved
                         water supply service, about 29,600 peoples having access to improved sanitation services and about 71,000m3 a day of water saved in Ho Chi Minh city resulting from project
                         activities.
