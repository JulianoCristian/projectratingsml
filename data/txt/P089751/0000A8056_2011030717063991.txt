                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The proposed IDP Economic Development Support Project (IDP-EDS) is a repeater project that replicates part of the Pilot Reconstruction Project (Cr.
                         31090/31091-AZ), and its objective, like that of its predecessor, is to help improve the living conditions of IDPs and enhance their economic opportunities and
                         prospects for social integration.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Although the Project Development Objective was revised for the IDP-EDS Additional Financing, the change is not yet reflected in the portal. The new PDO is to, "improve living
                         conditions for IDP, through demand-driven community based infrastructure and service micro-projects". Progress of the project in meeting this PDO continues to be satisfactory. The
                         project is contributing to the improvement of infrastructure and services for internally displaced persons through the funding of community micro-projects. To date 107 out of an
                         expected 250 micro-projects have been completed, reaching 56, 766 beneficiaries. Some of the outputs achieved through these micro-projects are: construction of 3.9km of new
