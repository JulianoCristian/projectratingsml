Amendment to Loan Agreement                                
Excellency Harchaoui"
We refer to the Loan Agreement as amended ("Agreement") dated June 16, 1994 
entered into between the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the 
"Bank") and the Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria ("Algeria"), in respect of 
the Water Supply and Sewerage Rehabilitation Project ("Project"). We also refer to the 
recent project restructuring aide memoire and to the Algerian Government’s request, 
dated September 28, 1998, to amend the Agreement to reflect the restructuring of the 
Project.
Therefore, by the present, the Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
(i)
Section 1.02 is amended by including the following two additional 
definitions:
"(g) 
"EPEAL" means the Borrower’s establishment for the 
production and management of water distribution in Algiers 
(Etablissement de Production, de Gestion et de Distribution d’Eau 
d’Alger), a public authority established pursuant to Decree No. 92-100 
of the Borrower, dated March 3, 1992, as the same may be amended from 
time to time;"
"(h)
"EPECO" means the Borrower’s establishment for the 
production and management of water distribution in Constantine 
(Etablissement de Production, de Gestion et de Distribution d’Eau de 
Constantine), a public authority established pursuant to Decree No. 
92-100 of the Borrower, dated March 3, 1992, as the same may be amended 
from time to time;"
(ii)
Section 1.02 (k) is deleted in its entirety and the term "New 
Public Authority" is removed from throughout the Agreement.
(iii)
Sub-sections (g) through (J) and Sub-sections (l) through (n) in 
said Section 1.02 are re-lettered (i) through (l) and (m) through 
(o) accordingly.
(iv)
The term "Concession Agreement" as defined in Section 1.02 (k), 
and wherever else it appears throughout the Agreement, is amended 
to read "Performance Agreement", and the term "Concession", 
wherever it appears throughout the Agreement, is amended to read 
"Performance".
(v)
The term "Water Authority" in Section 1.02 (n) is amended to 
further include "EPEAL", "EPECO", and "EPEA".
(vi)
Section 3.03 (a) is amended to read as follows" " The Borrower 
shall, not later than January 1, 1997, enter into, and thereafter 
maintain in effect, a Performance Agreement with each Water 
Authority pursuant to which the Water Authority shall pay a fee, 
all in accordance with terms and conditions satisfactory to the 
Bank, including, without limitation, those set forth in Schedule 7 
to this Agreement."
Page  2
(vii)
Section 3.04 is deleted in its entirety, and Section 3.05 is 
re-numbered as Section 3.04.
(viii)Section 3.04 (a) is amended to read as follows" "The Borrower 
shall select, from its investment program for the water and 
sewerage sector, sub-projects for the rehabilitation of sewerage 
systems, including sewerage treatment plants to be financed out of 
the proceeds of the Loan under Part C of the Project, on the basis 
of the criteria and in accordance with the procedures set forth in 
Schedule 8 to this Agreement."
(ix)
In Section 3.04 (b) and in Paragraph 3 (b) of Schedule 1 the term 
"sewage treatment plant" is amended to read "sewerage system", and 
in Sub-paragraphs 2 (a), 2 (c), 2 (d), 2 (f) and 2 (g) of Schedule 
8, the term "sewage treatment plant" is amended to read "sewerage 
system, including the sewage treatment plant".
(x)
In Section 4.01 (b) (ii) the term "six months" is amended to read 
"nine months".
(xi)
In Sections 4.02 (a), 4.02 (b) (iii) and 5.01, the terms "rent" 
and "Concession", wherever they appear throughout the Agreement, 
are amended to read "fee" and "Performance", respectively.
(xii)

(xiii)Paragraph 4 of Schedule 1 is amended to read as follows" "The Bank 
may require withdrawals from the Loan Account to be made on the 
basis of statements of expenditure for expenditures for" (a) goods 
and works under contracts not exceeding three hundred thousand 
dollars ($300,000) equivalent; (b) contracts for the employment of 
consulting firms not exceeding one hundred thousand dollars 
($100,000) equivalent; and (c) contracts for the employment of

individual consultants not exceeding fifty thousand dollars 
($50,000) equivalent, all under such terms and conditions as the 
Bank shall specify by notice to the Borrower."
(xiv)
Part A of Schedule 2 is revised to read as follows" 
"Rehabilitation of the water supply systems in the cities of 
Algiers, Annaba, Constantine and Oran through leak detection and 
repairs, meter installation, gauging, establishment of effective 
billing and collection procedures, and the preparation and 
periodical updating of a listing of water customers."
(xv)
The phrase "about twenty-four (24)" in Part C of Schedule 2 is 
amended to read "about twenty-two (22)".
(xvi)
Part B (e) of Schedule 5 is amended to read as follows" "describe, 
on the basis of monitoring indicators acceptable to the Bank, the 
performance of EPEA, EPEAL, EPECO and EPEOR under their respective 
Performance Agreement."
(xvii)In Part E (2) of Schedule 5, the phrase "Fiscal Year 1997 for all 
remaining Water Authorities", wherever it appears, is amended to 
read "Fiscal Year 1998 for EPEA and EPECO".
      (xviii)Part F of Schedule 5 is deleted in its entirety.
(xix)
An additional Paragraph (j) is added to Schedule 7 to the 
Agreement to read as follows" "such Performance Agreement shall be 
reviewed yearly jointly by the Bank and the Borrower, at such 
times as the Bank may reasonably request, and, with the prior 
agreement of the Bank, said Performance Agreement may be amended 
as required."
