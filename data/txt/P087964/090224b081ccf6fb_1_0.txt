                         Implementation Status Overview
                          The Project continues to make progress towards meeting its development objectives. Activities on rehabilitating flood control and drainage rehabilitation infrastructure are completed
                         on 36 schemes, while works on 6 others are ongoing. The works on developing minor irrigation schemes continue to lag behind schedule due to time lost in adopting the enabling
