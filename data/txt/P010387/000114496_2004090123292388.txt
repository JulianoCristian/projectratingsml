Dear Sir:
                  Re:   Maternal and Child Health Project
                        Amendment of the Japanese Grant Agreement
      We refer to the Japanese Grant Agreement dated June 5, 1991 between
India (the Recipient) and the International Development Association (the
Association) on its own behalf and as Administrator of certain grant funds
provided by Japan for the Maternal and Child Health Project. In accordance
with discussions between the Association's staff and concerned Government
officials regarding the utilization of funds provided under this Japanese
Grant Agreement for the preparation of projects in the maternal and child
health sector, we are pleased to inform you that the Association has agreed
to amend the Schedule to the Japanese Grant Agreement as follows:
      (1) The first paragraph in the Schedule is deleted and substituted
by the following:
      The objectives of the technical assistance are to assist the
      Recipient in respect of the formulation of a maternal and child
      health/child survival and safe motherhood project, as well as other
      maternal and child health projects in other states, for the purpose
      of supporting the Recipient's national immunization mission or
      universal immunization program (UIP), UIP Plus, and Safe
      Motherhood.
      (2) The following is added as a new sub-paragraph (8) of the
Schedule:
      (8) Preparation of externally assisted investment projects in the
maternal and child health sector.
      Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing and
dating the enclosed form of confirmation and returning it to us. Subject
to your confirmation as indicated above, this amendment will be effective
as of the date of this letter.
