Second Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Excellency: 
 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated October 29, 2004 (the Loan Agreement), 
entered into between the State of Tocantins (the Borrower) and the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) for the above captioned Project.  
 
We are pleased to let you know that pursuant to your letter of request dated September 9, 
2010, as confirmed by the Guarantor on November 4, 2010 through COFIEX letter 
(Recommendation #605 of October 13, 2010) dated November 4, 2010, the Bank hereby agrees 
to amend the Loan Agreement so as to extend the Project Closing Date and revise the Project 
allocation of funds, as set forth below: 
 
1. Section 
2.03.
Section 2.03 is hereby amended and replaced to read in its entirety as 
follows: 
 
“Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be October 31, 2011 or such later date as the Bank 
shall establish, after having received evidence that COFIEX has authorized the extension of the 
Closing Date. The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower and the Guarantor of such later date, 
which shall not be a date beyond the date authorized by COFIEX.” 
2.         Schedule 1.
The table in paragraph A.1 is hereby amended and replaced to read as follows 
in its entirety:
