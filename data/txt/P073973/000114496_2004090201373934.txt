Re:    Japanese Grant For the Preparation of 
 
the Community Investment Project (Grant No. TF026808) 
 
Amendment to the Grant Agreement                                   
Dear Mr. Abildaev: 
 
We refer to the Letter Agreement, dated January 8, 2002, between the Kyrgyz Republic (the 
Recipient) and the International Development Association (IDA) for the above-referenced Japanese Grant 
(the Agreement).  Pursuant to your request, IDA agrees to amend the Agreement as follows: 
 
1. 
In the reference line of the Agreement and in paragraph 1.1 of the Annex to the Agreement the 
name “Community Investment Project” is changed to the name “Village Investment Project”. 
 
2. 
The amount allocated for the Social and Environmental Assessment Component in paragraph 
1.1.(a) of the Annex to the Agreement is changed to the amount of US$111,338. 
 
 
3. 
The amount allocated for the Capacity Assessment of Rural and Community Institutions 
Component in paragraph 1.1(b) of the Annex to the Agreement is changed to the amount of US$28,505. 
 
4. 
The amount allocated for the Development of a Typology of Micro-Projects Component in 
paragraph 1.1(c) of the Annex to the Agreement is changed to the amount of US$85,193. 
 
5. 
The amount allocated for the Design of Project Implementation Framework Component in 
paragraph 1.1(d) of the Annex to the Agreement is changed to the amount of US$144,474. 
 
6. 
Paragraph 1.1(e) of the Annex to the Agreement is amended to read: 
 
“(e) 
Establishment of the Project Preparation Unit (PPU) Component 
 
Provision of technical assistance and operating costs to: (i) support the establishment of a PPU 
under the Office of the Prime Minister of the Recipient, including the design and establishment of

“(d) “operating costs” means expenditures incurred by the Recipient on account of office
