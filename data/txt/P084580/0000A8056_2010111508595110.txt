                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The proposed Sri Lanka Education Sector Development Grant (ESDG) will support the GOSL Education Sector Development Framework and Program (ESDFP)
                         2006-2010. Th                              e Project's overall objective is to: (a) promote equitable access to basic educ                                ation
                         (grades 1-9) and secondary education (grades 10-13);(b) improve the qualit                                y of education, (c) enhance the economic efficiency
                         and equity of resource alloc                              ation and distribution within the education system, and (d) strengthen education
                         governance and service delivery.
