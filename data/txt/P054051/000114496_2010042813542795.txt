Re: 
Solid Waste and Environmental Management Project - TF 024696
Amendment to the Trust Fund Credit Agreement
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Trust Fund Credit Agreement for the Solid Waste and 
Environmental Management Project (TF024696) between the Palestine Liberation 
Organization (for the benefit of the Palestinian Authority) (the “Borrower”) and the 
International Development Association, acting as administrator of the Trust Fund for Gaza 
and West Bank (the “Administrator”), dated November 14, 2000, as amended (the “Credit 
Agreement”). We also refer to the letter from H.E. Mr. Mohammed Nashashibi, the 
former Minister of Finance of the Palestinian Authority and authorized representative of 
the Borrower, dated June 1, 2002, requesting the Administrator to reallocate the proceeds 
of the Credit to assist in financing emergency damage repair and services support 
activities in the West Bank and Gaza. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Administrator has agreed to the above 
request. Accordingly, the Credit Agreement is hereby amended as follows: 
 
1. 
Section 1.02 (a) shall read as follows: ““Eligible Categories” means categories (1), 
(1) (a), (1) (b), (2) (a), (2) (b), (2) (c), (2) (d), (3) (a), (3) (b), (4) and (4) (a) set forth in the 
table in Part A.1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement;”. 
 
2. 
Section 1.02 (d) shall read as follows: ““EQA” means the Environmental Quality 
Authority, an authority established under the laws currently in force in the parts of the 
West Bank and Gaza Strip under the jurisdiction of the Palestinian Authority;” 
 
3. 
The Borrower, through the Palestinian Authority, shall cause EQA to assume all 
of MEnA’s rights and obligations under the Credit Agreement, and to carry out and fulfill 
all of the covenants, agreements and obligations of MEnA contained or referred to in the 
Credit Agreement. 
 
4. 
The table in Part A.1 of Schedule 1 to the Credit Agreement shall read as follows:

5. 
In paragraph B.1 of Schedule 1, the word “and” in the second line shall be deleted 
and shall be replaced by a comma, and the words “and Z (i)” shall be inserted between the 
words “C.3” and “of”; and the word “and” in the third line shall be deleted and the words 
“and Z (ii)” shall be inserted between the words “2” and “of”. 
 
6. 
In Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement, a new Part Z shall be added and shall read as 
follows:  
 
“Part Z
:
Damage Repair and Emergency Service Support Activities
Carrying out a program, through (i) JSC and (ii) EQA, consisting of the 
provision of goods and the execution of works, with a view to rehabilitating damaged 
physical and institutional infrastructure, and mitigating the decline in essential public 
service provision, including electricity, water, wastewater, solid waste and social 
services.” 
 
7. 
In Schedule 4 to the Credit Agreement, the following language shall be inserted after 
the full stop in the fourth line of paragraph 1: “JSC and EQA shall be jointly responsible 
for the carrying out of Part Z of the Project.” 
 
8. 
All the terms and conditions of the Credit Agreement, not amended hereby, shall 
remain in full force and effect.
