Fund Development Credit Agreement
Excellency,
We refer to the above-mentioned Interim Fund Development Credit Agreement (the 
Agreement), dated January 24, 1997, between Burkina Faso (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association as Administrator of the Interim Trust Fund (the 
Association) , and your letter, dated October 2, 1997, requesting the Association to 
extend the dates contained in paragraphs 6 (a) and (b) of Schedule 4.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association accedes to your request and, 
accordingly, agrees hereby to modify paragraphs 6 (a) and (b) of Schedule 4 of the 
Agreement to read respectively as follows:
“ (a) take all necessary measures to ensure that as of November 3, 1997, 
OCECS shall 
operate, on the basis of the Borrower’s Decret 96-111 PRES/PM/MESSRS 
dated April 25, 1996; 
and
(b) no later than February 5, 1998, conclude a technical assistance 
contract with an 
institution of pedagogical evaluation, acceptable to the 
Administration, to assist OCECS in 
evaluating and testing students under Part 
B(3) (a) of the Project.”
All provisions of the Agreement, except as amended herein, shall remain in full 
force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
the confirmation form on the enclosed copy of this letter and returning it to us for 
our records, while keeping one original for your records.
This amendment shall come into force and effect as of the date of confirmation 
of this letter.
Sincerely,
By /s/ Hasan  A. Tuluy 
Country Director for Burkina Faso
