                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The disbursement rate is now about 80% (as of February 28, 2011) and most the remaining funds are already committed to pay outstanding contractual activities , school grants/
                         Conditional Cash Transfer (CCT ) and Early Childhood Development (ECD) activities. School grants have been disbursed to about 2000 schools while all the states are now
                         preparing for the last grant roll out. The Teacher Professional Development (TPD) activities are progressing well and last round of training has commenced to make teaching methods
                         more child-centered. All major procurement activities have been completed, civil works for school rehabilitation and classroom construction is at 85% completion level, and all
