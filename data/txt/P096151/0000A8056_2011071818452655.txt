                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has achieved almost all of its development objectives having met most targets under all the three components, in targeted Local Government Authorities in the States of
                         Kaduna, Kano and Kwara: (a) Under the School Development Scheme, 1,974 schools received school development grants for improvement in physical environment and in the
                         quality of teaching/learning, with considerable community ownership, participation and contribution). (b) Quality Improvement in Basic Education has facilitated the provision of 3
                         million textbooks and guides to teachers; about 6,500 teachers were trained in-service under the Teacher Professional Development program; and 976 school facilities were
