                         Implementation Status Overview
                         With ASAP now entering its completion phase, most project activities have now been completed. Most of the activities under Component I and 2 have now been fully implemented.
                         The Land Parcel Information System (LPIS) is functional and the Farm Register operational and ready to be connected to the rest of the Integrated Administrative Control System
                         (IACS). The Agency for Financial Support of Agricultural and Rural Development (AFSARD) signed its first contracts under the EU IPARD grant program, and made a second call for
