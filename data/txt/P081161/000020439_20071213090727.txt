b) Flood Protection 
1. Nanchuan: 9.6 km flood control embankment, bund road 5.12km, 4 bridges, 
sewage pipeline 5.1 km. 
2. Rongchang: 10.2 km embankment, 8.9 km bund road for Laixi; 1.2km 
embankment, 800 m bund road for Chishui; 5 bridges 
3. Shizhu: 6km embankment 
c) Roads 
1. Qiangjiang: 8.5 km Class II urban road, four lanes with separate NMV lanes, 
one bridge, 1.2 km connection road. 
2. Shizhu: 5.3 km class II link road between Nanbin town and Chengdu-Shanghai 
expressway 
3. Tongnan: 11 km Class III road between Tongba and Chengdu-Chongqing 
Expressway 
4. Yubei: 14 km 2-lane Class II road linking Tongjing with Yu-Lin Expressway 
(incl. 8.5 km rehabilitation of existing road) 
d)
Environmental Sanitaton 
Public Toilet Rehabilitation: Upgrading and reconstruction of about 200 public 
toilets. 
e)  Institutional Strengthening and Training  
1. Design Review and Advisory (DRA) services, for design review and 
certification, during detailed design and bid document preparation (conforming to 
design and implementation requirements), to be incorporated the legal 
Page  3
CHONGQING SMALL CITIES INFRASTRUCTURE IMPROVEMENT PROJECT 
agreements. The DRA consultant will have an enhanced ‘sign-off’ (certification) 
responsibility for review of detailed designs, geotechnical and topographic 
investigations, and review bid documents prior to invitation. 
2. Construction supervision and contract management to FIDIC terms, for all 
project towns.  
3. Capacity building (institutional and financial) support for utility companies, 
and the district and county governments 
4. Carrying out studies on solid waste management, ground water, regional water 
supply price policy, etc. 
5. Support on management and operation of local governments. 
6. Organizing study tour and training.
