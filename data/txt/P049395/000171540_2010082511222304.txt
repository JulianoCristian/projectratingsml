Ethiopian Former Women Fuel Wood Carriers  
 
Amendment Letter - Reallocation and Extension of Closing Date  
 
We refer to the Grant Agreement dated August 12, 2005 (Agreement), between the 
Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia (the Recipient) and the International Development 
Association (the Bank), acting as the Administrator for the Japan Social Development Fund 
(JSDF) for the above-reference Grant, as amended.  We also refer to the letter from Ministry of 
Finance and Economic Development (dated April 30, 2010), requesting a reallocation of the 
Grant funds among various categories of expenditures, and a one-year extension of the closing 
date of the Agreement. 
 
I
am pleased to inform you that the Association concurs with the Recipient’s request, and 
hereby agrees to amend the Agreement as follows. 
 
1.  August 12, 2011 is established as the later date for purposes of Section 4.3(b) of the 
Annex to the Agreement.   
 
2.  The disbursement table in Section 4.2 of the Annex to the Agreement is deleted and 
replaced with the table set forth below.
