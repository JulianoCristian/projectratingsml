GUARANTEE AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated September 14, 1998, between UNITED MEXICAN STATES (the 
Guarantor) and INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (the Bank).
WHEREAS (A) the Guarantor and Banco Nacional de Obras y Servicios Públicos, 
S.N.C. (the Borrower), having been satisfied as to the feasibility and priority of the 
Project described in Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement, have requested the Bank to 
assist in the financing of the Project;
(B)
by the Loan Agreement of even date herewith between the Bank and the 
Borrower, the Bank has agreed to extend to the Borrower a loan in the amount of one 
hundred eighty million, two hundred thousand Dollars ($180,200,000), on the terms and 
conditions set forth in the Loan Agreement, but only on condition that the Guarantor 
agree to guarantee the obligations of the Borrower in respect of such loan and to 
undertake such other obligations as provided in this Agreement; and
WHEREAS the Guarantor, in consideration of the Bank’s entering into the Loan 
Agreement with the Borrower, has agreed so to guarantee such obligations of the 
Borrower and to undertake such other obligations as provided in this Agreement;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows: 
ARTICLE I
General Conditions; Definitions
Page  2
Section 1.01. The “General Conditions Applicable to Loan and Guarantee 
Agreements for Single Currency Loans” of the Bank, dated May 30, 1995, as amended 
through December 2, 1997 (the General Conditions), constitute an integral part of this 
Agreement.
Section 1.02. Unless the context otherwise requires, the several terms defined 
in the General Conditions and in the Preamble to and Section 1.02 of the Loan 
Agreement have the respective meanings therein set forth.
ARTICLE II
Guarantee; Provision of Funds
Section 2.01. Without limitation or restriction upon any of its other 
obligations under the Guarantee Agreement, the Guarantor hereby unconditionally 
guarantees, as primary obligor and not as surety merely, the due and punctual payment 
of the principal of, and interest and other charges on, the Loan, and the premium, if 
any, on the prepayment of the Loan, and the punctual performance of all the other 
obligations of the Borrower, all as set forth in the Loan Agreement.
Section 2.02. The Guarantor shall provide, or cause to be provided, in a timely 
manner as needed, the funds, facilities, services and other resources required for the 
Project.
ARTICLE III
Execution of the Project
Section 3.01. The Guarantor declares its commitment to the objectives of the 
Project as set forth in Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement, and, to this end, shall, 
with due diligence and efficiency and in conformity with appropriate educational, 
financial, technical and administrative standards and practices, as well as in 
accordance with the implementation schedule and targets set forth in the 
Implementation Letter:
(a)
cause Part A of the Project to be carried out by SOFES with the support 
of the Trust; and
(b)
cause Part B of the Project to be carried out by ICEES with the support 
of the Trust. 
Section 3.02. The Guarantor shall enter into, and comply with the terms of, the 
contractual arrangements referred to in Section 3.01 of the Loan Agreement and, except 
as the Bank may otherwise agree, shall not amend or fail to enforce any provision of 
such contractual arrangements.  The Guarantor shall exercise its rights under such 
contractual arrangements in such manner as to protect the interests of the Guarantor 
and the Bank and to accomplish the purposes of the Loan.
Section 3.03. Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree, procurement of the goods 
and consultants’ services required for the Project and to be financed out of the 
proceeds of the Loan shall be governed by the provisions of Schedule 5 to the Loan 
Agreement.
Section 3.04. (a) The Guarantor shall carry out or cause to be carried out the 
obligations set forth in Sections 9.04, 9.05, 9.06, 9.07, 9.08 and 9.09 of the General 
Conditions (relating respectively to insurance, use of goods and services, plans and 
schedules, records and reports, maintenance and land acquisition) with regard to the 
Project.

continued achievement of the Project’s objectives; and

SCHEDULE
Terms and Conditions of Intermediary Agreements,
Intermediary Loans and Student Loans; 
Eligibility Criteria for Eligible Students
1.
Eligible Students
To qualify as an Eligible Student, a student shall:
(a)
in the case of a student seeking to receive a Student Loan from SOFES: 
(i) have a grade point average of at least 7 (out of a maximum of 10) in the student’s 
course of study undertaken prior to the course of study to be supported by the Student 
Loan; and (ii) fall in any socio-economic category (other than the categories “high C” 
or “high B”) as determined through a socio-economic evaluation undertaken for SOFES as 
set forth in the SOFES Operational Manual; and
(b)
in the case of a student seeking to receive a Student Loan from ICEES: 
(i) be a native of the State of Sonora or a State of Sonora resident for at least 
three consecutive years immediately prior to receiving the Student Loan; (ii) fall in 
any socio-economic category (other than the categories “high C” or “high B”) as 
determined through a socio-economic evaluation undertaken for ICEES as set forth in 
the ICEES Operational Manual; (iii) have a guarantor for the Student Loan; and (iv) 
achieve a priority ranking according to a classification exercise undertaken by ICEES, 
which exercise considers the student’s financial need, the potential for Student Loan 
recovery, the student’s academic background and ICEES’ institutional targeting goals, 
all as set forth in the ICEES Operational Manual.
2.
Student Loans
(a)
A Student Loan made by SOFES shall be documented in a loan agreement 
entered into between SOFES and an Eligible Student prior to the first disbursement of 
the Student Loan and shall:
(i)
be denominated in UDIs and bear a variable positive real interest 
rate, as set 
forth in the SOFES Operational Manual;
(ii)
have an amortization and grace period as set forth in the SOFES 
Operational Manual;
Page  5
(iii)
be made to finance the Eligible Student’s registration, insurance 
and tuition 
fees incurred in pursuing a course of study at a university 
that is a SOFES 
shareholder (or, if the Bank shall so agree, to also 
finance the Eligible 
Student’s book, academic supply 
(including computers required by the 
university), room and 
board and transportation expenses incurred in pursuing 
such a course 
of study);
(iv)
be in an amount to be determined as a function of the Eligible 
Student’s 
economic need and proposed course of study, all as 
set forth in the SOFES 
Operational Manual; and
(v)
be accompanied by a scholarship from the relevant university that 
is a SOFES 
shareholder, again in an amount to be determined as a 
function of the Eligible 
Student’s economic need and proposed course of 
study, but only if and to the 
extent required by the SOFES Operational 
Manual.
(b) 
A Student Loan made by ICEES shall be documented in a loan agreement 
entered into between ICEES and an Eligible Student prior to the first disbursement of 
the Student Loan and shall:
(i)
be denominated in Mexican pesos and bear an annual fixed interest 
rate, as set 
forth in the ICEES Operational Manual;
(ii)
have an amortization and grace period as set forth in the ICEES 
Operational Manual;
(iii)
be made to finance the Eligible Student’s registration, insurance 
and tuition 
fees incurred in pursuing a course of study at a public or 
private university 
(or, if the Bank shall so agree, to also finance the 
Eligible Student’s book, 
academic supply (including computers required 
by the university), room and 
board and transportation expenses 
incurred in pursuing such a course of 
study); and
(iv)
be in an amount to be determined as set forth in the ICEES 
Operational Manual.
3.
Intermediary Loans
(a)
The Intermediary Loan to SOFES shall be documented in an Intermediary 
Loan Agreement entered into between SOFES and the Trustee prior to the first 
disbursement of the Intermediary Loan and shall:
(i)
be denominated in UDIs and bear an interest rate of four percent; 
and
(ii)
have an amortization period equal to fifteen years (including five 
years of 
grace within those fifteen).
(b)
The Intermediary Loan to ICEES shall be documented in an Intermediary 
Loan Agreement entered into between ICEES and the Trustee prior to the first 
disbursement of the Intermediary Loan and shall:
(i)
be denominated in UDIs and bear an interest rate of four percent; 
and
(ii)
have an amortization period equal to fifteen years (including five 
years of 
grace within those fifteen).
