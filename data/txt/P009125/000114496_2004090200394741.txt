Re:   Uzbekistan Health Project (Loan Number 4396 UZ)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Your Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement dated November 16, 1998, between the Republic of Uzbekistan 
(the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) regarding the 
above-captioned Project (the Agreement). Further to discussions held between the representatives of the 
Borrower and the Bank, and to your letter dated November 8, 2000,we are pleased to propose the 
following amendments:
1. 
Section 1.01(c) of the Agreement is amended to read as follows: 
 “(c)  “Participating Regions” means the Borrower's regions of Syr Darya, Navoi, Ferghana and, in 
relation to provision of drugs and medical supplies under Part A (2) of the Project, such other regions as 
may be agreed upon between the Borrower and the Bank from time to time;”.
2.
