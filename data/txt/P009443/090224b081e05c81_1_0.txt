DEVELOPMENT CREDIT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated         Q                            , 1984,
between PEOPLE'S REPUBLIC OF   AN' DESH (hereinafter called the
Borrower) and INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (hereinafter
called the Association).
WHEREAS (A) by Development Credit Agreements of various
dates between the Borrower and the Association (hereinafter
called the Principal Development Credit Agreements), the Associa-
tion extended credits to the Borrower (hereinafter called the
Principal Credits) for the purposes of the projects referred to
in Schedula 1 to this Development Credit Agreement and more fully
described in Schedules 2 to the Principal Development Credit
Agreements (hereinafter called the Projects);
(B) the Borrower has requested the Association to provide
supplemental assistance for the purposes of the Projects by
extending the Credit as hereinafter provided; and
(C) some of the Projects will be carried out by certain
separate entities referred to in the relevant Principal Develop-
ment Credit Agreements (hereinafter called the Project Executing
Agencies) with the Borrower's assistance, and, as part of such
assistance, the Borrower will make available to the Project
Executing Agencies part of the proceeds of the Credit as herein-
after provided; and
WHEREAS the Association has agreed, on the basis, inter
alia, of the foregoing, to extend the Credit to the Borrower upon
th( terms and conditions hereinafter set forth;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I

provisions of the General Conditions Applicable to Development

the same force and effect as if they were fully set forth herein,
subject, however, to the following modifications thereof (said
General Conditions Applicable to Development Credit Agreements of
the Association, as so modified, being hereinafter called the
General Conditions):

ARTICLE II
The Credit
Section  2.01.   The  Association  agrees  to  lend  to   the
Borrower, on the terms and conditions in the Development Credit
Agreement set forth or referred to, an amount in various curren-
cies equivalent to twenty-eight million seven hundred thousand
Special Drawing Rights (SDR 28,700,000).
Section 2,02. (a) The amounts of the Credit may be withdrawn
from the Credit Account in accordance with the provisions of
Schedule 2 to this Agreement, as such Schedule may be amended
from time to time by agreement between the Borrower and the
Association, for expenditures made (or, if the Association shall
so agree, to be made) in respect of the reasonable cost of goods
and services required for the Projects and to be financed out of
the proceeds of the Credit.
(b) The Borrower shall, for the purposes of the Projects,
open and thereafter maintain in taka Special Accounts in a
commercial bank on terms and conditions satisfactory to the
Association. Deposits into, and payments out of, the Special
Accounts shall be made in accordance with the provisions of
Schedule 3 to this Agreement.
Section 2.03. Except as the Association shall otherwise
.agree, procurement of the goods, services and civil works
required for the Projects and to be financed out of the proceeds


﻿ii                                  -4-
of the Credit shall be governed by the provisions of (a) Sched-
ule 3 to the Principal Development Credit Agreement referred to
in Section 1.02 (a) (i) of -this Agreement and (b) the Schedules
to the Principal Project Agreements.
Section 2.04. The Closing Date shall be June 30, 1986 or
ouch later date as the Association shall establish.     The Asso-
ciation shall promptly notify the Borrower of such later date.
Section 2.05. (a) The Borrower shall pay to the Association
a commitment charge at the rate of one-half of one per cent (1/2
of 1%) per annum on the principal amount of the Credit not with-
drawn from time to time. The commitment charge shall accrue from
a date sixty days after the date of the Development Credit Agree-
ment to the respective dates on which amounts shall be withdrawn
by the Borrower from the Credit Account or shall be cancelled.
(b) The commitment charge shall be paid: (i) at such places
as the Association shall reasonably request; (ii) without
restrictions of any kind imposed by, or in the territory of, the
Borrower; and (iii) in the currency specified in this Agreement
for the purposes of Section 4.02 of the General Conditions or in
such other eligible currency or currencies as may from time to
time be designated or selected pursuant to the provisions of that
Section.
Section 2.06. The Borrower shall pay to the Association a
service charge at the rate of three-fourths of one per cent (3/4
of 1%) per annum on the principal amount of the Credit withdrawn
and outstanding from time to time.
Section 2.07. Commitment charges and service charges on the
principal amounts of the Credit allocated to the Projects shall
be payable semiannually on the dates on which such principal
amounts shall be payable in accordance with the provisions of
Section 2.08 of this Agreement.
Section 2.08. The Borrower shall repay the principal amount
of the Credit allocated to each of the Projects in semiannual
installments payable as follows:
(a) with respect to the Muhuri Irrigation Project (Credit
725 BD-Supplemental, on each May 1 and November 1 commencing
November 1, 1987, and ending May 1, 2027, each installment to and
including the installment payable on May 1, 1997, to be one-half

installment thereafter to be one and one-half per cent (1-1/2%)
of such principal amount;
(b)  with respect to the Fertilizer Industry Rehabilitation
Project (Credit 1023 BD-Supplemental), on each April 15 and
October 15 commencing October 15, 1990, and ending April 15,
2030, each installment to and including the installment payable
on April 15, 2000, to be one-half of one per cent (1/2 of 1%) of
such principal amount, and each installment thereafter to be one
and one-half per cent (1-1/2%) of such principal amount;
(c) with respect to the Chittagong Port Project (Credit
1247 BD-Supplemental), on each April 1 and October 1 commencing
October 1, 1992, and ending April 1, 2032, each installment to
and including the installment payable on April 1, 2002, to be
one-half of one per cent (1/2 of 1%) of such principal amount,
and each installment thereafter to be one and one-half per cent
(1-1/2%) of such principal amount; and
(d) with respect to the Rural Electrification Project
(Credit 1262 BD-Supplemental), on each April 1 and October 1
commencing October 1, 1992, and ending April 1, 2032, each
installment to and including the installment payable on April 1,
2002, to be one-half of one per cent (1/2 of 1%) of such prin-
cipal amount, and each installment thereafter to be one and
one-half per cent (1-1/2%) of such principal amount.
Section 2.09. The currency of the United States of America
is hereby specified for the purposes of Section 4.02 of the
General Conditions.

limitation on the obligations assumed pursuant to the Principal
Development   Credit   Agreements   and  the   Principal   Project

to each of the Projects:

reflecting all expenditures on account of which withdrawals are

(b) retain, until one year after the Closing Date of the
Principal Credit or the Credit, as the case may be, all records

the Principal Credit Account and the Credit Account, respective-

the purpose for which they were provided; and
(e) furnish or cause to be furnished to the Association
such other information concerning said expenditures and records
' *and the audit thereof as the Association shall from time to time
reasonably request.

(a) for Credit 725 BD, June 30, 1986; and
(b) for Credit 1023 BD, June 30, 1986.

lopuenit projects. To this end, the Borrower shall:

recommendations of said review in a manner and within a time-
frame satisfactory to the Borrower and the Association.
