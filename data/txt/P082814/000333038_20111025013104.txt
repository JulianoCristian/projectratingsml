7a. Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
The development objectives of this project are (i) to improve the capacity of the MOH and HII to
effectively formulate and implement health policies and reforms in provider payments, monitoring and
quality assurance, (ii) to improve both the access to, and quality of, primary health care services, with an
emphasis on those in poor and under-serviced areas, and (iii) to improve governance and management in
the hospital sector.
