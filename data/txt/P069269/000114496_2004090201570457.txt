Re:
Paraguay: Japanese Grant for the Preparation of the
Community Development and Poverty Reduction
Amendment to the TF Grant Agreement No. 026317
Excellency:
Please refer to the Japanese Grant Agreement for the Preparation of the 
Community Development and Poverty Reduction dated August 21, 2000 between the Republic 
of Paraguay (the Recipient) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development, as administrator of grant funds provided by Japan (the Bank).
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to the request in your letter dated 
January 18, 2001, we agree to amend the TF Grant Agreement as follows:
Section 2.01 is hereby amended and replaced in its entirely to read as follows:
“The recipient, through its Social Action Secretariat (Secretaría de Acción 
Social) shall: (a) carry out the Activities with due diligence and efficiency and 
efficiency; (b) promptly provide the funds, facilities, services and other resources 
required for that purpose; (c) furnish all information covering the Activities and the 
use of the proceeds of the Grant as the Bank shall reasonably request; (d) from time 
to time exchange views with the Bank’s representatives on the progress and results of 
the Activities; and (e) take all necessary measures required to enable the Bank to 
visit the territory of the Recipient for purposes related to the Grant.  Without 
limitation on the forgoing, the Recipient shall, if the Bank shall so request, prepare 
and furnish to the Bank promptly upon completion of the Activities a report, in form 
and substance satisfactory to the Bank, on the results and impact of the Activities.
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing this 
letter in the space provided and returning it to us.  This amendment shall be executed 
in two counterparts, each of which shall constitute an original.  The amendment shall 
become effective upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this 
letter.
Very truly yours,
