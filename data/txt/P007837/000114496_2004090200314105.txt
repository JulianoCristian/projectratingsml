August 20, 1997
Dr. Guillermo Chapman 
Minister of Planning and Economic Policy 
Ministry of Planning and Economic Policy 
Panama 3, Panama
Re:
Loan No. 4191-PA (Social Investment Fund Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Minister:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the Republic of Panama and the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), dated August 7, 
1997.
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to your request, the Bank agrees to 
amend said Loan Agreement as follows:
1. Section 3.01 (e) is hereby amended by inserting the following phrase at the 
end “. The respective Loan proceeds for Part B.3 (a) of the Project may be 
administered through a fiduciary or other financial administration arrangement 
,satisfactory to the Bank, (the Financial Administration Agreement) between FES and 
the Banco Nacional de Panama; 
2. Section 3.01 (f) (I) is hereby amended by inserting “ and the Financial 
Administration Agreement” after “Participation Agreement”.
3. Section 3.01 (f) (iii) is hereby amended by inserting “or the Financial 
Administration Agreement” after “Participation Agreement”.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
this letter in the space provided below. This amendment letter shall be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original. Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us.  This amendment letter will become effective 
upon receipt by the Bank of a duly executed original of this letter.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION
  AND DEVELOPMENT
/s/ Donna Dowsett Coirolo
    Director
    Central America Department 
    Latin America and the Caribbean Region
