Re: 
Loan No. 4191-PAN (Social Investment Fund Project) 
Fourth Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency: 
 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the Republic of Panama (the Borrower) and 
the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) dated August 7, 1997 
with regard to the above-captioned Project, as amended (the Loan Agreement). 
 
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to your request and our recent discussions on 
the subject as reflected in the 
Ayuda Memoria
 dated October 4, 2002, the Bank hereby agrees to 
amend the Loan Agreement so as to increase the amount of the authorized allocation of the 
Special Account. 
 
Consequently the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows: 
 
Schedule 5, paragraph 1 (c) is hereby amended and replaced to read in its entirety as 
follows: 
 
“(c) 
the term “Authorized Allocation” means an amount equal to $2,800,000 to be 
withdrawn from the Loan Account and deposited into the Special Account pursuant to paragraph 
3
(a) of this Schedule.” 
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and dating this 
letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter shall be executed in two counterparts 
each of which shall be an original.
