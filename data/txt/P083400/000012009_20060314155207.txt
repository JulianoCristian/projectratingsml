earlier as necessary.

3.  Date of General Procurement Notice:  July 2004   
 
4. Period covered by this procurement plan
:
August 2005 to February 2007 
 
5. Credit related PPF: 
 
A
PPF (Project Preparation Fund) was approved and went into effect in mid-June 2004 to 
assist the Government to lay the preparatory work for the credit. The PPF for US$542,400.00  
consists of several sub-component:  (i) Upgrading of PIU set up and skills to match World Bank 
standards :  training in procurement, hiring of a procurement specialist; hiring of a consultant to 
help develop a Financial, Administrative and Procurement Manual of procedures;  design and 
install a Financial Management System (FMS) at the PIU (including the information system and 
necessary training); and operational costs of the PIU. (ii) In public finance management, in PRGF 
negotiations, studies on nomenclature harmonization, macro-economics and network system 
functions revision of financial manual and PRSP monitoring.  (iii) In the petroleum sector, staffing 
of the National Petroleum Agency and extension; repairs of buildings and data network 
installation;  and TA consultancies for architect consultancy, study on revision of exploration 
results and technical assistance in information system management.  
 
The PPF contains a stand alone procurement plan and the present procurement plan will not 
contain any PPF activities.    The PPF is slated to close February 15, 2004.
