                         Program Development Objectives
                         Program Development Objective (from Program Document)

                         HEDPO3 aims to strengthen governance, financing and quality of higher education by (i) improving the responsiveness of higher education and research and increase
                         the quantitative capacity of the system; (ii) enhancing fiscal transparency, sustainability and effectiveness of the higher education sector; and (iii) improving the quality
                         of Higher Education institutions.
                         Has the Program Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Program?
