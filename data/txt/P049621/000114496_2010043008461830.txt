Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement and the Loan Agreement (Bukhara and 
Samarkand Water Supply Project) between the Republic of Uzbekistan (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development respectively (hereinafter collectively referred to as the Bank), both dated April 12, 
2002.  We also refer to your letter dated November 14, 2003, requesting certain amendments to 
the Development Credit Agreement.  
 
We hereby confirm the Bank’s agreement to the amendment set forth below. 
 
1. 
In Category (1) (b) of the table set forth in paragraph A.1 of Schedule 1 to the 
Development Credit Agreement, the words “Goods under Part B.1 of the Project” are revised to 
read “Goods under Part A.1 of the Project”. 
 
2. 
In Category (1) (e) of the table set forth in paragraph A.1 of Schedule 1 to the 
Development Credit Agreement, the words “, including audit costs” are deleted. 
 
3. 
In Category (2) (e) of the table set forth in paragraph A.1 of Schedule 1 to the 
Development Credit Agreement, the words “, including audit costs” are deleted. 
 
4. 
Paragraph 2 (d) of Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement is revised to read as 
follows: 
 
“(d) 
the term “Incremental Operating Costs of the PCU” means operating expenditures 
incurred by the PCU on account of management of Project implementation for: (i) 
communications, utilities, printing and publications, office rent, maintenance and 
supplies; (ii) office security systems, office and computer equipment, office furniture, and 
vehicles, all of which shall be procured in accordance with the provisions of Section I of

Schedule 3 to this Agreement; (iii) vehicle operation and maintenance, transportation and 
field trip expenses; (iv) training of PCU staff for purposes of Project management, 
remuneration for staff, including qualified social charges but excluding salaries of civil 
servants; and (v) such other expenditures as may agreed upon by the Association.” 
 
5.  In Part C of Schedule 2 to the Development Credit Agreement, the words 
“, including audit services,” are deleted. 
 
Please confirm your agreement with the forgoing amendment by signing, dating 
and returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter.  Upon receipt by the Bank of the copy 
of this letter countersigned by you, this amendment will become effective as of the date 
of the countersignature.
