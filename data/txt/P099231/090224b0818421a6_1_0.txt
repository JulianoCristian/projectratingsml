                         Implementation Status Overview
                         An implementation support mission was held between Oct 29- Nov 2, 2012. The objectives of the mission were to carry out a comprehensive review of project implementation since


                                                                                                                                                                                                           Page 1 of 5
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                        Report No: ISR8886



                         the last implementation support mission in May 2012, as stated in the mission announcement letter sent on Oct 12, 2012.

                         The mission was pleased to make note of the sustained uptick in the pace of project implementation over the last year. It was noted that the committed expenditures, largely on
                         contracts currently underway, are USD 7.6 million bringing the total projected disbursement by closure to 100%. The mission consulted with various technical teams and the PAT on
Public Disclosure Copy




                         feasibility of each of the line items under the committed expenditures and was generally satisfied that implementation of these activities was on track.

                         The task team made note of the fact that the most of the ongoing work will be completed by the original project closing date of January 31, 2013, several important activities relating
                         to the drafting of the deposit insurance law, establishment of the commodity exchange, development of the national policy on insurance, designing of the public education materials
                         on leasing, public awareness programs for pensions etc. will benefit from an additional cushion of 5 months to ensure their completion.
