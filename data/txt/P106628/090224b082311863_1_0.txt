                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the proposed operation is to improve the transparency, coverage, operation and interoperability of key national financial management information
                         systems that will allow Government decision makers to link performance measures with planning, budgeting and other managerial actions.
