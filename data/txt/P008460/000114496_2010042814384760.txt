Dear Ms. Tarjan:
     Referring to the Guarantee Agreement, dated May 30, 1989,
between Republic of Hungary and International Bank for
Reconstruction and Development, and the amendment to the Loan
Agreement No. 3056 HU between International Bank for Reconstruction
and Development and the Hungarian Oil and Gas Company Ltd. MOL Rt.,
formerly National Oil and Gas Trust, dated August 13, 1993, we
propose to amend the above-mentioned Guarantee Agreement as follows:
     All references in the agreement to the "National Oil and Gas
Trust" are substituted by the "Hungarian Oil and Gas Company Ltd.
MOL Rt.".
     Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing,
dating and returning to us one original copy of this letter.
