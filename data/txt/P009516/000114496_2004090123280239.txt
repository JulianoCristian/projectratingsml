Dear Mr. Chaudhury:
Re:
Credit No. 2233-BD
(Agricultural Support Services Project)
Amendment of the Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated June 17, 1991 between the 
People's Republic of Bangladesh (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association) for the Agricultural Support Services Project.  We also 
refer to your letter dated November 12, 1991, requesting amendment of the Development 
Credit Agreement to revise the dates set forth in the provisions of the Development 
Credit Agreement referred to below.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association has agreed to amend the 
Development Credit Agreement as follows in accordance with your above request:
(a)
the date December 31, 1991 in paragraph 1 of Schedule 4 to the 
Development Credit Agreement is deleted and replaced by the following date: May 31, 
1992;
(b)
the date October 31, 1991 in paragraph 2 of Schedule 4 to the Development 
Credit Agreement is deleted and replaced by the following date: March 31, 1992;
(c)
the date March 31, 1992 in paragraph 3(a) of Schedule 4 to the 
Development Credit Agreement is deleted and replaced by the following date: August 31, 
1992;
(d)
the date October 31, 1991 in paragraph 4(a) of Schedule 4 to the 
Development Credit Agreement is deleted and replaced by the following date: March 31, 
1992; and
(e)
the date October 31, 1991 in paragraph 4(e) of Schedule 4 to the 
Development Credit Agreement is deleted and replaced by the following date: March 31, 
1992.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the 
enclosed form of confirmation and returning it to us.  Subject to your confirmation as 
indicated above, this amendment will be effective as of the date of this letter.
Sincerely yours,
By /s/ Jochen Kraske
Director
Country Department 1
South Asia Region
CONFIRMED
PEOPLE'S REPUBLIC OF BANGLADESH
