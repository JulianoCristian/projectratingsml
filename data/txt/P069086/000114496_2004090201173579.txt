Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) for the Community 
Based Poverty Reduction Project, entered into between the Federal Republic of Nigeria 
(the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association) on 
April 20, 2001, and to your letter dated October 11, 2001, requesting an amendment to 
the above Agreement. 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association hereby agrees to the following 
amendment to the Agreement:
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is amended to read as set forth in the 
Annex to this letter.
Please indicate your agreement to these amendments by signing and dating the two  
original copies of this letter in the space provided therefor and by returning one 
duplicate original duly signed and confirmed to us. This amendment shall become 
effective upon receipt by the Association of the duplicate original duly signed and 
confirmed.
Sincerely,
By /s/ Mark D. Tomlinson
