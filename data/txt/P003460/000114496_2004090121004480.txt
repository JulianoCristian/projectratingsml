May 27, 1992
H.E. Wang Bingqian
Minister of Finance
Ministry of Finance,
Sanlihe, Beijing,
People's Republic of China
Re:
Development Credit No. 2219-CHA (Liaoning Urban
Infrastructure Project)
Amendment of the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Liaoning Urban Infrastructure 
Project) between the People's Republic of China (the Borrower) and the International 
Development Association (the Association) dated April 25, l99l, and to your Ministry's 
telex dated March 12, 1992 requesting the Association for an increase in the amount of 
the Authorized Allocation of the Special Account from $5,200,000 to $7,000,000 under 
the above Project.
The Association is pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, it has 
agreed to the above request. Therefore Paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 3 to the 
Development Credit Agreement is hereby amended to read as follows:
"(c) the term  "Authorized Allocation" means an amount equivalent to 
$7,000,000 to be 
withdrawn from the Credit Account and deposited in the 
Special Account pursuant to paragraph 3 
(a) of  this Schedule."
 
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
the enclosed form of confirmation and by returning it to us. This amendment shall 
become effective as of the date hereof upon receipt of your confirmation in this 
regard.
A copy of this letter is being forwarded to the Liaoning Provincial Government 
for their records.
Sincerely,
By /s/ Joseph R. Goldberg
Acting Director
China and Mongolia Department
East Asia and Pacific Region
