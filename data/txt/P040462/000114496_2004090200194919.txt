Re:
Loan 3912-ME (Essential Social Services Program) 
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Sirs: 
We refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank) and Nacional Financiera, S.N.C. (the Borrower) dated June 23, 
1995 (as amended), pertaining to the above-captioned Project.  We are pleased to 
inform you of our agreement to further amend said Loan Agreement by replacing the 
words “Civil works under part B.3 of the Project” in Category (1) of the table in 
paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to said Loan Agreement with the words “Civil works under 
Parts A.3 and B.3 of the Project”. 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
the three originals of this letter in the spaces provided below, and returning one to 
us.  This amendment shall enter into effect as of January 1, 1997 upon receipt by the 
Bank of one original of this letter signed by you.
Sincerely yours,
