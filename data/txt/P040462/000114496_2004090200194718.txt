            Re:     Essential Social Services Program - (Loan No. 3912-ME)
       Amendment to the Loan and Guarantee Agreements
Dear Sirs: 
We refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank) and Nacional Financiera, S.N.C. (the Borrower), and the 
Guarantee Agreement between United Mexican States (the Guarantor) and the Bank, both 
dated June 23, 1995, as amended.  We are pleased to inform you of our agreement to 
amend the Loan and Guarantee Agreements, as follows:
I.
Amendments to the Loan Agreement
1.
Section 2.03 of the Loan Agreement is hereby amended by replacing the 
date “June 30, 1997” with the date “June 30, 1998”.
2.
The last sentence in Schedule 2 is hereby amended by replacing the date 
“December 31, 1996” with the date “December 31, 1997”. 
II.
Amendments to the Guarantee Agreement
3.
Part C.1 of Section I of the Schedule is deliberately left blank.
4.
Part C.2 (d) of Section I of the Schedule is hereby amended to read as 
follows in its entirety:  “Goods (including, inter alia, medical equipment, 
pharmaceuticals and micronutrients) other than those procured as permitted elsewhere 
in Part C.2 of Section I of this Schedule, estimated to cost the equivalent of less 
than $350,000 per contract, up to an aggregate amount equivalent to $31,500,000; and”.
5.
Part C.3 (e) of Section I of the Schedule is hereby amended by deleting 
the word “and” at the end thereof.
6.
Part C.3 (f) of Section I of the Schedule is hereby amended to read as 
follows in its entirety:  “Printing and distribution of educational materials under 
Part A.2 (a) of the Project (as well as paper therefor if separately purchased from 
the printing), costing up to an aggregate amount equivalent to $79,000,000, may be 
procured under contracts awarded on the basis of national shopping procedures in 
accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 3.5 and 3.6 of the Guidelines if such 
contracts were entered into prior to January 1, 1996; and”.
7.
A new Part C.3 (g) of Section I of the Schedule is hereby added 
immediately after Part C.3 (f) of Section I of the Schedule, and shall read as follows 
in its entirety:  “Medical equipment, pharmaceuticals and micronutrients, estimated to 
cost $100,000 equivalent or less per contract, up to an aggregate amount equivalent to 
$10,500,000, may be procured under contracts awarded on the basis of national or 
international shopping procedures in accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 3.5 
and 3.6 of the Guidelines.”.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating

it shall apply retroactively from January 1, 1996 onward.
