         Mauritius: Private Sector Competitiveness Development Policy Loan (DPL)

                               SUMMARY OF DISCUSSION

                              Meeting of the Executive Directors

                                          March 27, 2012


       With regard to the Private Sector Competitiveness Development Policy Loan, Executive
Directors approved this DPL to the Republic of Mauritius in an amount of GBP 9.6 million (US$
15 million equivalent) on the payment terms and conditions set out in the President’s
Memorandum (R2012-0041).

        Directors commended the Authorities for the sound macroeconomic management that
provided the country with the resilience at the onset of the global economic crisis. They further
noted that reforms were necessary for Mauritius to maintain its growth potential. Directors also
commented on the role of IFC in support to the Development Bank of Mauritius. Executive
Directors emphasized the importance of addressing the challenge of economic and external risks
to the operation and encouraged the continued collaboration with other development partners.
