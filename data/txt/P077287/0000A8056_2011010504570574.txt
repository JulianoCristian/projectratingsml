                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The development objective is to improve water and household level sanitation services and their effective use in selected provinces in ways that are financially,
                         technically, and environmentally sustainable, thereby contributing to poverty alleviation in participating rural communities.
