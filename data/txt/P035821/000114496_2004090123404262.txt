AGREEMENT AMENDING PROJECT AGREEMENT 
 
AGREEMENT dated May 12, 2003 between 
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (the 
Association) and the STATES OF ASSAM, GUJARAT, 
HARYANA, HIMACHAL PRADESH, KARNATAKA, 
KERALA, MADHYA PRADESH, MAHARASHTRA, 
ORISSA, TAMIL NADU, UTTAR PRADESH AND 
CHHATTISGARH, each acting by their respective 
Governors, (hereinafter referred to individually as the 
“Project State” and collectively as the “Project States”);
 
WHEREAS (A) by the Development Credit Agreement dated July 15, 1996 
between the Borrower and the Association, as amended to date (the Development Credit 
Agreement), the Association has agreed to make available to the Borrower an amount in 
various currencies equivalent to two hundred ninety one million seven hundred thousand 
Special Drawing Rights (SDR 291,700,000) (the Credit) to assist in financing the Project 
described in Schedule 2 to the Development Credit Agreement; 
 
(B) 
by the Project Agreement dated July 15, 1996 between the Association and 
the states of Assam, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Karnataka, 
Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Orissa, Tamil Nadu and Uttar 
Pradesh, as
 
amended to date (the Project Agreement), the Project States 
agreed to undertake certain obligations with respect to the carrying out of 
the Project; 
 
(C) 
the Borrower has advised the Association through Gazette Notice No. 36 
dated August 25, 2000 of the enactment of the Madhya Pradesh Reorganization Act, 
Page  4
-2- 
2000 creating the new State of Chhattisgarh (Chhattisgarh), formerly a part of the State of 
Madhya Pradesh; 
 
(D) 
the Borrower has requested the Association to amend the Development 
Credit Agreement to take into account the creation of the State of Chhattisgarh and the 
obligation of the State of Chhattisgarh to carry out part of the Project. 
 
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree to amend the Project 
Agreement as follows: 
 
A. 
Effective Date; Termination, Cancellation and Suspension 
 
The following new Section is added after Section 4.03 in Article VI: 
 
“Section 4.04. The Agreement Amending the Project Agreement shall come into 
force and effect on the date upon which the Agreement Amending the 
Development Credit Agreement becomes effective.”  
 
B. Miscellaneous 
Provisions 
 
The following new addresses are added after the address for the State of Uttar 
Pradesh in Section 5.01:

Raipur 492 001

C.  
Procurement and Consultant Services 
 
Part E (2) (b) of Section 1 of Schedule 1 to the Project Agreement is amended to  
read as follows: 
 
“(b) the first five contracts to be procured by Gujarat, Himachal Pradesh, Madhya 
Pradesh, Chhatisgarh and Orissa, the procedures set forth in paragraphs 2 and 3 of 
Appendix 1 to the Guidelines shall apply.”
