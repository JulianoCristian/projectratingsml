           Republic of Tunisia - Employment Development Policy Loan

                           SUMMARY OF DISCUSSION*

                         Meeting of the Executive Directors 

                                    July 1, 2010 




       Executive Directors approved the Employment Development Policy Loan to the
Republic of Tunisia (R20 10-0153/1) in the amount of $50 million on the payment terms
and conditions.

        Executive Directors expressed strong support for the DPL and endorsed its
objectives. Executive Directors expressed general satisfaction with respect to the multi
sector approach adopted by the Bank to address employment issues in the country in line
with the country's request.
