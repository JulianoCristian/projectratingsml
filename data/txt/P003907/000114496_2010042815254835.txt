                  Re:   Loan 2930-IND (Forestry Institutions and
                         Conservation Project)
                         Amendment of the Loan Agreement
Dear Minister Sumarlin:
      We refer to the Loan Agreement (Forestry Institutions and
Conservation Project) between the Republic of Indonesia (the
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development (the Bank) dated April 20, 1988.  We also refer to your
Ministry's letter of July 27, 1989 requesting the Bank for an
increase in the authorized alloation of the Special Account under
the above project.
      The Bank is pleased to inform you that it agrees to the above
request.  Therefore, the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as
follows:
      1.    The amount stated in paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 6 to
the Loan Agreement is amended to read "$2,500,000".
      Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by
signing and dating the enclosed form of confirmation, and by
returning it to us.
                                      Sincerely yours,
                                       /s/ Amar Bhattacharya
                                       Acting Director
                                       Country Department V
                                       Asia Region

REPUBLIC OF INDONESIA
