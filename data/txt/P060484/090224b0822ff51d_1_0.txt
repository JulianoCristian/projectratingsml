                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation is progressing well. The Basic Municipal Services Project includes 22 sub-projects, out of which 9 are already finished, benefiting directly 68,000 people and
                         indirectly 370,000 people - which represent already 43 percent of the target number of expected beneficiaries - and 13 sub-projects still under construction, which would benefit
                         directly 222,000 people and 920,000 indirectly. Once all 22 sub-projects would be completed, about 1.5 million people would benefit directly and indirectly from the project, exceeding
                         the target of one million beneficiaries for the first Project outcome indicator. The project will also achieved second Project outcome indicators as 12 out of 22 of the sub-projects are

                         Sub-projects include improvement in water and sewerage services and wastewater treatment plant, construction of sewerage collectors, urban drainage and urban and rural roads.
