I. General
1. 
Project information
:
The Government of Malawi (GoM) is undertaking a Health Sector Wide Approach (SWAp) with 
contributions from various bilateral and multilateral development partners. The Ministry of 
Health has developed a 6-year Program of Work (PoW) outlining the priorities for 
implementation under the SWAp, based on the provision of an Essential Health Package (EHP) 
for all Malawians. 
 
The objective of the sector wide program is to improve the health status of the population while 
reducing the geographical, socio-economic and gender inequalities in health.  
 
The program will include the following components: 
 
1.  Human resources  
2.  Pharmaceuticals, Medicines and Laboratory services  
3.  Essential basic equipment  
4.  Infrastructure development  
5.  Routine operations  
6.  Policy and support systems
