                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The development objective of the RCDP is to improve the living conditions of project-supported rural communities in terms of (i) access to basic socio-economic
                         services, and (ii) a sustainable increase in incomes, while promoting improved natural resource management practices.
