                         Implementation Status Overview
                         PACR is leading towards its closing date on December 31, 2013 following its extension for 6 months to compensate for the suspension of activities implementation subsequent to the
                         coup d'Etat. Within this extension time frame all planned activities have been implemented as agreed on with the Government in the implementation plan. All the four PDO indicators
                         have been reached and outpaced in certain cases : about 478, 000 have benefited from the project against 350,000 planned); The percentage of professional organization to

                         improved natural resources management have been reached respectively attaining 70% against 50% and the percentage of rural accessing basic social services reached 87%
