                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation experienced some delays due to the renewal of field operators' contracts. Nevertheless the project is still making good progress toward PDO achievement: (i)
                         515 social investments (against an initial objective of 450) have been delivered tocommunities through local governments; on average, a commune has benefited of 9.5 social sub-
                         projects for an average investment of 110 million CFA (US$230,000), mainly in education (44%), water supply (22%) and health (17%); (ii) 765 productive sub-projects (for 900
