                         Implementation Status Overview
                         PACR is leading towards its closing date on December 31, 2013 following its extension for 6 months to compensate for the suspension of activities implementation after the coup
                         d'Etat. Within this extension time frame project activities have all been implemented as agreed on by the Government and the Bank. Project development objective indicators on their
                         way to be achieved of which some have been outpaced. The current disbursement rate stands at 98% and is expected to reach about 100% at the closing date. A Thorough impact
