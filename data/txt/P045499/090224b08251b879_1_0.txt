                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         1) Increase the efficiency of drainage of about 1 million feddan of irrigated land, (2) increase agricultural production and improve rural income, and (3) to identify and
                         oversee the resolution of environmental issues resulting from the discharge into the open drains of untreated industrial and domestic waste water in the project area.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The World Bank recently processed level-2 restructuring of its loan in response to the Government request to reallocate loan proceeds among different project categories, as well as
                         to increase IBRD's loan percentage share for works category from 65% to 85% to alleviate the Government's fiscal challenges.

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The World Bank processed level-2 restructuring of its loan in March 2014 in response to the Government request to reallocate loan proceeds among different project categories, as
                         well as to increase IBRD's loan percentage share for works category from 65% to 85% to alleviate the fiscal challenges the Government faced.
