                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project development objective (PDO) is to strengthen Uruguay's capacity to generate, transfer and adapt knowledgeandtechnology b y supporting: (i) the
                         institutional framework for science, technology, and innovation (STI); (ii) investments inhumancapital and h i gh-quality research teams; (iii) technology transfer and
                         private sector innovation; and (iv) cross-sectoralandinternational resear c h collaboration and mobility of researchers between public institutions and the productive
                         sector.

                         Bibliografías On-line), allowing access to Scientific Publications.Downloads in the period between January and December 2011 were 241,401 articles. Component 2 continued with

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         Stimulating Technological Demand Project ("Proyecto de Estímulo a la Demanda Tecnológica"), was launched to promote the use of technology and to facilitate the association
                         between public and private actors in order to enhance productive sector performance. This subproject aims to stimulate the development of technological networks, and was
                         approved by the ANII Directory in February 2012.
