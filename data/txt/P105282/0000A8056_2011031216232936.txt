                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has suffered long delays and difficulties in implementation in the beginning of the project. However, more recently, there has been a significant improvement in the
                         project implementation. Most of the pending activities have been addressed since the last mission in July 2010 or are currently being addressed: development of draft health
                         financing policy and strategic plan, middle level management training, installation of IT equipment for Health Management Information System, recruitment of health workers, and
                         rehabilitation of target referral hospitals have been completed or are near completion. Also, both nurse anesthetist and radiographer training programs at Tubman National Institute of
