Re:
Loan  Number 4124 – CHA (Second National Highway Project) 
Amendment to the 
Loan Agreement
We refer to the Loan Agreement between the People’s Republic of China (the 
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), 
dated August 29, 1997, for the above referenced Project (the Loan Agreement). Further 
reference is made to the letter from Mr. Wu Gang, Deputy Director-General, 
International Department, Ministry of Finance, People’s Republic of China, dated March 
10, 2000, requesting the Bank to raise the percentage of Bank financing of certain 
category expenditures under the Project from 34 percent to 48 percent.
The Bank is pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, it has agreed 
to your request to increase the financing percentage for civil works relating to the 
Guangdong Component of the Project from 34 percent to 48 percent and that section 1 of 
Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended as indicated in the Attachment to this 
Amendment Letter.
Further, the Bank is pleased to inform you that it has agreed to your request to 
reallocate the proceeds of the loan as follows:
(i)
in category 1(e) of Schedule 1, the unallocated amount of the loan 
proceeds is 
reduced from US$20,000,000 to US$2,162,000;
(ii)
in category 1(b) of Schedule 1, allocation relating to the supply 
and 
installation of electronic, electric and mechanical 
equipment is reduced from 
US$39,300,000 to US$20,240,000; and
(iii)
consequently, the allocation of the proceeds of the loan to 
category (a) of 
Schedule 1, is increased from US$129,700,000 to 
US$166,598,000.
Except as specifically amended by this amendment letter, all provisions of the 
Loan Agreement remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement to the amendment set out above by having the 
authorized representative of the Borrower sign the form of confirmation set forth 
hereinbelow, and returning one fully executed original of this amendment letter to us 
at your earliest convenience and retaining a copy of the letter for your records. This 
amendment letter will become effective as of the date first above written, once it has 
been fully executed by all parties thereto.
Yours sincerely,
