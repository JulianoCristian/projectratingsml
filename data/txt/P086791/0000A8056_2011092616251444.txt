                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Government of Bangladesh is implementing the Reaching Out-of-School Children (ROSC) project, with support from IDA, in 90 poorest upazilas (sub-districts) to bring
                         disadvantaged children back to school. To date, the project has provided second chance education to more than 750,000 out of school children in about 22,000 Learning Centers
                         (LCs). It is an important intervention of the Government for eligible children in upazilas with high rates of poverty and low enrollment rates to appear and pass the Grade 5 terminal

                         IDA's original Grant Financing of US$51 million since 2004 and Additional Credit Financing of US$35 million since 2010 support the provision of grants to LCs and allowances to
