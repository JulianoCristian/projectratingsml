Rwanda: Transport Sector Development Project
(Grant No. H331-RW & Credit Number 4968-RW)
Amendment to the Amended and Restated Financing Agreement
We refer to the Amended and Restated Financing Agreement (the Agreement) for the
above-mentioned Project, dated July 8, 2011, between the Republic of Rwanda (the Recipient) and
the International Development Association (the Association). We also refer to your letter dated
September 13, 2013 requesting extension of the Closing Date, reallocation of the proceeds of the
financing and changes in the project activities.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association concurs with your request and proposes
to amend the Agreement as follows-
1. Part 1 (b) of Schedule 1 to the Agreement is amended by deleting sub-section "(i)" in its
entirety and renumbering sub-sections "(ii)" and "(iii)" as "(i)" and "(ii)" respectively.
2. The table set forth in Section IV. A. 2 of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is deleted in its entirety
and replaced with the table set forth in the Annex to this letter.
3. The latter date for purposes of Section IV.B.2 of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is December 31,
2014.
All other provisions of the Agreement, except as herein amended, shall remain in full force
and effect.
