Third Amendment to Development Credit

Dear Sir/Madam:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement between 
the Republic of Bolivia (the Borrower) and the International 
Development Association (the Association) on the above-mentioned 
Project, dated September 20, 1991, as amended.
We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to your October 
18, 1994 and January 16, 1995 requests, the Association hereby 
agrees to amend the Development Credit Agreement as follows:
1.
Section 1.02 is hereby amended by: (a) deleting the word 
"and" at the end of paragraph (w); (b) deleting the period at 
the end of paragraph (x); (c) adding the word "and" at the end 
of paragraph (x); and (d) adding a new "paragraph (y)" which 
shall read as follows:
"(y) 'Eligible Municipalities' means those 
municipalities which have 
been selected by the Borrower 
and accepted by the Association.".
2.
Paragraph (b) of Section 3.01 is hereby amended as 
follows:
"(b) Without limitation to the provisions of 
paragraph (a) hereof, the 
Borrower shall provide, 
pursuant to the USAID Balance of Payments Agreement, 
funds for the Project in an amount equivalent to at least 
$6,000,000 (six 
million Dollars).".
3.
Section 3.04 is hereby amended to read as follows:
"Section 3.04. Without limitation to the provisions 
of Section 9.06 of 
the General Conditions, the Borrower, 
through the Project Technical Director, 
shall not later 
than February 28 and August 31 of each year prepare and 
furnish to the Association bi-annual progress reports, of 
such scope and in 
such detail as the Association shall 
reasonably request, concerning the 
execution of the 
Project.".
4.
Section 3.05 is hereby amended to read as follows:
"Section 3.05. The Bank shall: (a) for each year 
during the execution of 
the Project, prepare and furnish to 
the Association a PAO, by December 31 of 
each year 
with respect to the carrying out of the Project: and (b) refrain 
from entering into any new contract for purchase of 
goods or employment of 
consultants under any given PAO 
until such PAO has been approved by the 
Association.".

Section 3.06 is hereby deleted.
6.
Section 3.07 is hereby amended to read as follows:
"Section 3.07. The Borrower shall cause: (a) its 
ministries, the 
Eligible Public Entities and the 
Central Government Institutions to start to 
formulate 
their own budgets, as provided in Part A.1 of the Project, by 
October 31, 1995; and (b) the Regional Development 
Corporations to formulate 
their own budgets under Part A. 
1 of the Project, provided that the capital of 
such 
Regional Development Corporations is wholly owned by the 
Borrower or by 
any legal entity wholly owned by the 
Borrower.".
7.
Section 3.08 is hereby amended to read as follows:
"Section 3.08. The Borrower shall: (a) complete the 
organizational 
changes for cash management in TGN, 
under Part A.2 of the Project, not later 
than June 
30, 1995; (b) cause the payment system through the Banking 
Network, 
under Part A.2 of the Project, to be operative not 
later than June 30, 1995, 
including inter alia, the 
issuance of adequate daily statements, computer 
controls, audits and sanctions for non-compliance with agreed 
banking 
services; and (c) furnish to the 
Association, not later than June 30, 1995, a 
timetable,

elimination,  under Part A.2 of the Project, of the function of 
the subtreasuries within TGN 
to make payments of funds 
within the public sector, such elimination to be 
completed not later than June 30, 1996, including, inter alia, 
the changes in 
the Banking Network referred to in paragraph 
(b) of this Section.".

follows:
"3. (a) (i) Development of financial management systems

Section 11 of Schedule 31 is hereby amended as follows:
"Section II: Employment of Consultants
1.
Consultants' services shall be procured under 
contracts awarded in 
accordance with the provisions 
of the "Guidelines for the Use of Consultants 
by the 
World Bank Borrowers and by the World Bank as Executing Agency" 
published by the Bank in August 1981 (the 
Consultants Guidelines). For 
complex, time-based 
assignments, such contracts shall be based on the standard 
form 
of contract for consultants services issued by the Bank, with 
such 
modifications thereto as shall have been agreed by 
the Association. Where no 
relevant standard contract 
documents have been issued by the Bank other 
standard 
forms aareed with the Association shall be used.
"2.
Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph I of 
this Section, the 
provisions of the Consultant Guidelins 
requiring prior Association review or 
approval of 
budgets, short lists, selection procedures, letters of 
invitation, 
proposals, evaluation reports and contracts, shall 
not apply to: (a) contracts 
for the employment of

consulting firms estimated to cost less than $60,000 
equivalent each; or (b) contracts for the employment of 
individuals estimated 
to cost less than $25,000 equivalent 
each. However, said exceptions to prior 
Association 
review shall not apply to: (a) the terms of reference for such 
contacts; (b) single-source selection of consulting 
firms; (c) assignments of  a critical nature, as reasonably

the employment of consulting firms raising the contract value

the employment of 
individual consultants raising the 
contract value to $25,000 equivalent or 
above.".

effective as of January 2, 1995 on the date on which the

amendment letter.

by signing and dating the two counterparts of this Amendment 
Letter. Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed 
original to us.
