﻿AMENDING AGREEMENT
WHEREAS the Republic of Honduras (hereinafter called the
Republic), Banco Centroamericano de Integraci6n Econ6mica (here-
inafter called BCIE), International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development (hereinafter called the Bank), International Develop-
ment Association (hereinafter called IDA) and Empresa Nacional de
Energia El5ctrica (hereinafter called the Borrower) have entered
into a Joint Financing Agreement dated March 27, 1980 (hereinafter
called the Joint Financing Agreement) which regulates matters
concerning the financing by the Republic, BCIE, the Bank, IDA and
the Borrower of certain parts of the El Caj6n Power Project
which is described in the Schedule to the Joint Financing Agree-
ment;
WHEREAS the Commonwealth Development Corporation (hereinafter
called CDC) has agreed to make to the Borrower a loan not exceed-
ing five million Pounds Sterling (hereinafter called the CDC Loan)
on the terms and conditions set forth in the Loan Agreement dated
November 21, 1980 between CDC and the Borrower (hereinafter called
the CDC Loan Agreement) concerning the financing of the same parts
of the El Caj6n Power Project being co-financed by the Bank, IDA
and BCIE;
WHEREAS the parties to the Joint Financing Agreement have
agreed to amend such Agreement in order to reflect the participa-
tion of CDC in the financing of the Project and to describe in
more detail the parts of the Project being jointly financed by the
parties to the Joint Financing Agreement and CDC;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
The Joint Financing Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
(a) by deleting Section 2.04 and substituting therefor:
"Section 2.04. Subject to the provisions of their
respective agreements, BCIE shall finance 32.4% of the
expenditures (excluding cement supply) incurred by the
Borrower in connection with the parts of the Project
described as the Powerhouse Package in Part II of the Sched-
ule to this Agreement and BANK/IDA shall finance 29.2% of


﻿-2-
such expenditures, it being the intention that the whole
amount of such expenditures shall be met at about the same
time by disbursements also of the CDC Loan and contributions
by the Borrower in approximately the following percentages of
such expenditures: the Borrower 27.8% and CDC 10.6%".
(b) By adding a new Section 3.08 to read:
"Section 3.08. BCIE hereby authorizes the Bank and IDA
to supply to CDC all pertinent information regarding the BCIE
Loan, disbursements made thereunder, BCIE's financing of the
Powerhouse Package, the service of the BCIE Loan, this
Agreement and any other information received from BCIE with
regard to the financing and execution of the Project, which
in the opinion of the Bank and IDA may be of interest to
CDC."
(c) By adding the following Part II to the Schedule to the
Joint Financing Agreement:
"PART II
Description of the Powerhouse Package
The civil works for the underground powerhouse (cavern),
access tunnel, control building, switchyard, inclined parts of the
pressure shafts, tailrace tunnels, access road to switchyard,
extension of substations, supply and installation of certain
electromechanical equipment and erection and installation of
other equipment and of hydraulic steel structures as the same are
included in the proposed contract documents for that part of the
Project works known for contracting purposes as Lot VII-l."
ARTICLE II
This Agreement shall come into force and effect upon its
signature by all the parties hereto.
