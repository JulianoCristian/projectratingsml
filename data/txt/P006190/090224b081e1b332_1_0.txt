PROJECT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated         _                  , 1994, between
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (the Association) and FONDO
NACIONAL DE DESARROLLO REGIONAL (FNDR).
WHEREAS (A) by the Development Credit Agreement of even date
herewith between Republic of Bolivia (the Borrower) and the
Association, the Association has agreed to make available to the
Borrower an amount in various currencies equivalent to thirty
million   one    hundred   thousand   Special    Drawing   Rights
(SDR 30,100,000), on the terms and conditions set forth in the
Development Credit Agreement, but only on condition that FNDR agree
to undertake such obligations toward the Association as are set
forth in this Agreement;
(B)   by a subsidiary loan agreement to be entered into
between the Borrower and FNDR, the proceeds of the Credit provided
for under the Development Credit Agreement necessary to carry out
Part A of the Project in respect of Subloans and Participating Loans
will be relent to FNDR on the terms and conditions set forth in said
Subsidiary Loan Agreement; and
WHEREAS FNDR, in consideration of the Association's entering
into the Development Credit Agreement with the Borrower, has agreed
to undertake the obligations set forth in this Agreement;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
Definitions
Section 1.01. Unless the context otherwise requires, the
several terms defined in the Development Credit Agreement and in the
General Conditions (as so defined) have the respective meanings
therein set forth.
ARTICLE II
Execution of the Project;
Management and Operations of FNDR
Section 2.01. (a) ENDR declares its commitment to the
objectives of the Project as set forth in Schedule 2 to the
Development Credit Agreement, and, to this end, shall carry out


﻿-2-
Part A of the Project and conduct its operations and affairs in
accordance with sound administrative, management, engineering,
environmental and financial standards and practices, with qualified
management and personnel, and in accordance with the FNDR Charter.
(b)   Without limitation upon the provisions of paragraph (a)
of this Section and except as the Association and FNDR shall
otherwise agree, FNDR shall carry out Part A of the Project in
accordance with performance and implementation indicators acceptable
to the Association.
Section 2.02. (a) FNDR undertakes that, unless the Association
shall otherwise agree, Subloans, Participating Loans, Investment
Grants and Matching Grants shall be made in accordance with the
procedures and on the terms and conditions set forth or referred to
in the Schedule to this Agreement and the Credit Regulations.
(b)   FNDR shall exercise its rights in relation to each
Investment Project and Preinvestment Project in such manner as to:
(i) protect the interests of the Borrower, the Association and
FNDR; (ii) comply with its obligations under this Agreement and the
Subsidiary Loan Agreement; and (iii) achieve the purposes of Part A
of the Project.
Section 2.03. FNDR shall carry out -the obligations set forth
in Sections 9.03, 9.04, 9.05, 9.06, 9.07 and 9.08 of the General
Conditions (relating to insurance, use of goods and services, plans
and  schedules,   records  and   reports,  maintenance  and   land
acquisition, respectively) in respect of Part A of the Project.
Section 2.04. Except as the Association shall otherwise agree,
procurement of the goods, works and consultants services required
for Part A of the Project and to be financed out of the proceeds of
the Credit shall be governed by the provisions of Schedule 3 to the
Development Credit Agreement.
Section 2.05. FNDR shall duly perform all its obligations
under the Subsidiary Loan Agreement. Except as the Association shall
otherwise agree, FNDR shall not take or concur in any action which
would have the effect of amending, abrogating, assigning or waiving
the Subsidiary Loan Agreement or any provision thereof.
Section 2.06. (a) FNDR shall, at the request of the
Association, exchange views with the Association with regard to the
progress of Part A of the Project, the performance of its


﻿- 3 -
obligations under this Agreement and under the Subsidiary Loan
Agreement and other matters relating to the purposes of the Credit.
(b)   FNDR shall promptly inform the Association of any
condition which interferes or threatens to interfere with the
progress of Part A of the Project, the accomplishment of the
purposes of the Credit, or the performance by FNDR of its
obligations under this Agreement and under the Subsidiary Loan
Agreement.
(c)   Without limitation to its obligations under paragraphs
(a) and (b) above, FNDR shall:
(i)   not later than March 31 each year furnish to the
Association progress reports on the Investment
Projects and Preinvestment Projects approved by
FNDR;
(ii)   not later than November 15 each year furnish to
the Association the commitments and disbursement
projections for Part A of the Project for the
following three years, including as a part
thereof the related Project implementation and
performance indicators, and specifying the extent
to which projections submitted the prior year,
were met during that year;
(iii)   not later than November 15 each year, review with
the   Association    its    progress   in    the
implementation of Part A of the Project;
(iv)   not later than two years after the Effective
Date, review with the Association, in addition to
the matter referred to in paragraph (iii) hereof:
the adequacy of the allocation of Credit proceeds
for the financing of Subloans, Participating
Loans, Treasury Investment Grants, and Matching
Grants and the need for any modifications
thereof; and
(v)   if the results of any of the reviews referred to
in paragraphs (iii) and (iv) hereof are not
satisfactory to the Association: (A) not later
than sixty days after the respective review,
furnish to the Association an action plan,


﻿-4-
satisfactory to the Association, with the actions
necessary to achieve the objectives of Part A of
the Project and to meet the performance and
implementation indicators referred to in Section
2.01  (b) of   this Agreement; and     (B) take
promptly, all actions included in such action
plan.
Section 2.07. FNDR shall enter into Participating Agreements
with each Participating RDC on terms and conditions which shall have
been approved by the Association which shall include, inter alia,
those set forth or referred to in Part D.1 of the Schedule to this
Agreement, and shall duly perform all its obligations under the
Participating Agreements. Except as the Association shall otherwise
agree, FNDR shall not take or concur in any action which would have
the effect of amending, abrogating, assigning or waiving the
Participating Agreements, or any provisions thereof.
ARTICLE III
Financial Covenants
Section 3.01. (a) FNDR shall maintain procedures and records
and separate accounts adequate to monitor and record the progress of
Part A of the Project and of each Investment Project and
Preinvestment Project (including its cost and the benefits to be
derived from it) and to reflect in accordance with consistently
maintained sound accounting practices the operations and financial
condition of FNDR.
(b)   FNDR shall:
(i)   have its records, separate accounts and financial
statements (balance sheets, statements of income
and expenses and related statements) for each
fiscal   year   audited,  in   accordance   with
appropriate  auditing   principles  consistently
applied, by independent auditors acceptable to
the Association;
(ii)   furnish to the Association as soon as available,
but in any case not later than six months after
the end of each such year: (A) certified copies
of its financial statements for such year as so
audited; and (B) the report of such audit by said


﻿- 5 -
auditors, of such scope and in such detail as the
Association shall have reasonably requested; and
(iii)   furnish to the Association such other information
concerning said records, accounts and financial
statements as well as the audit thereof, as the
Association shall from time to time reasonably
request.
Section 3.02. FNDR shall take such steps satisfactory to the
Association as shall be necessary to protect itself against risk of
loss resulting from changes in the rates of exchange between the
various currencies (including the currency of the Borrower) used in
its operations.
ARTICLE IV
Effective Date; Termination;
Cancellation and Suspension
Section 4.01. This Agreement shall come into force and effect
on the date upon which the Development Credit Agreement becomes
effective.
Section 4.02. (a) This Agreement and all obligations of the
Association and of FNDR thereunder shall terminate on the earlier of
the following two dates:
(i)   the  date   on  which  the  Development  Credit
Agreement shall terminate in accordance with its
terms; or
(ii)   the date   15 years after the date of this
Agreement.
(b)   If the Development Credit Agreement terminates in
accordance with its terms before the date specified in paragraph (a)
(ii) of this Section, the Association shall promptly notify FNDR of
this event.
Section 4.03. All the provisions of this Agreement shall
continue in full force and effect notwithstanding any cancellation
or suspension under the General Conditions.

SCHEDULE
Procedures for and Terms and Conditions
of Subloans, Participating Loans, Investment
Grants and Matching Grants
Part A:     Terms and Conditions of Subloans
1.    (a)   Amount: Each Subloan shall not exceed 95% of the cost of
the Investment Project.
(b)   Currency: Subloans shall be denominated in dollars,
disbursed in dollars or Bolivian pesos, and repaid in Bolivian pesos
in amounts calculated at the official exchange rate prevailing at
the time the disbursement or the repayment, as the case may be, is
made.
(c)   Repayment Period: The repayment period shall not exceed
15 years, including a grace period of a maximum of 4 years.
(d)   Interest Rate: The Beneficiary shall pay interest
semiannually on the principal amount of the Subloan withdrawn and
outstanding at a rate for each semester equal to at least the
Certificate of Deposit Rate.
(e)   The Beneficiary shall pay a commitment charge at a rate
of one percent (1%) per annum on the principal amount of the Subloan
not withdrawn from time to time.
2.    No expenditures for goods or services required for an
Investment Project or Preinvestment Project, shall be eligible for
financing out of the proceeds of the Credit unless:
(a)   the Subloan for such Investment Project or Preinvestment
Project shall have been approved by the Association and such expen-
ditures shall have been made not earlier than ninety days prior to
the date on which the Association shall have received the appli-
cation and information required under paragraph 3 (a) of Part A of
this Schedule in respect of the respective Subloan; or
(b)   the Subloan for such Investment Project or Preinvestment
Project shall have been a free-limit Subloan for which the
Association has authorized withdrawals from the Credit Account and
such expenditures shall have been made not earlier than ninety days
prior to the date on which the Association shall have received the


﻿- 9 -
request and information required under paragraph 3 (b) of Part A of
this Schedule in respect of such free-limit Subloan. For the
purposes of the Development Credit Agreement and this Agreement, a
free-limit Subloan shall be a Subloan, other than the first two
Subloans proposed to be financed out of the proceeds of the Credit,
for an Investment Project or Preinvestment Project, the total cost
of which shall not exceed: (i) $1,500,000 equivalent for an
Investment Project for water supply, sanitation and road construc-
tion; (ii) $1,000,000 equivalent for an Investment Project for other
infrastructure construction and public service facilities; and (iii)
$150,000 equivalent for cadastre and preinvestment studies.
3.    (a)   When presenting a Subloan (other than a free-limit
Subloan) to the Association for approval, FNDR shall furnish to the
Association an application, in form satisfactory to the Association,
together with: (i) a description of the Beneficiary, and an
appraisal of the Investment Project or Preinvestment Project,
including a description of the expenditures proposed to be financed
by the Subloan and other sources of financing including, if
applicable, any Matching Grant; (ii) the proposed terms and
conditions of the Subloan, including the schedule of amortization
therefor; and (iii) such other information as the Association shall
reasonably request.
(b)   Each  request by   FNDR  for  authorization  to make
withdrawals from the Credit Account in respect of a free-limit
Subloan shall contain: (i) a summary description of the Beneficiary
and the Investment Project or Preinvestment Project, including a
description of the expenditures proposed to be financed by the
Subloan and other sources of financing including, if applicable, any
Matching Grant; and (ii) the terms and conditions of the Subloan,
including the schedule of amortization therefor.
(c)   Applications  and   requests  made  pursuant  to   the
provisions of subparagraphs (a) and (b) of this paragraph shall be
presented to the Association on or before December 31, 1996.
4.    Subloans shall be made on terms whereby FNDR shall obtain by
written contract with the Beneficiary or by other appropriate legal
means, rights adequate to protect the interests of the Borrower, the
Association and FNDR, including the right to:
(a)   require the Beneficiary to carry out and operate the
Investment Project or Preinvestment Project with due diligence and
efficiency and in accordance with sound technical, financial,


﻿- 10 -
environmental and managerial standards and to maintain records and
accounts adequate to reflect the operations, resources and
expenditures of the Investment Project or Preinvestment Project;
(b)   require that: (i) the goods and services to be financed
out of the proceeds of the Credit shall be procured in accordance
with the provisions of Schedule 3 to the Development Credit
Agreement; and (ii) such goods and services shall be used
exclusively in the carrying out of the Investment Project or
Preinvestment Project;
(c)   inspect, by itself or jointly with representatives of
the Association if the Association shall so request, such goods and
the sites, works, plants and construction included in the Investment
Project or Preinvestment Project, the operation thereof, and any
relevant records and documents;
(d)   require that: (i) the Beneficiary shall take out and
maintain with responsible insurers such insurance, against such
risks and in such amounts, as shall be consistent with sound
business practice; and (ii) without any limitation upon the
foregoing, such insurance shall cover hazards incident to the
acquisition, transportation and delivery of goods financed out of
the proceeds of the Credit to the place of use or installation, any
indemnity thereunder to be made payable in a currency freely usable
by the Beneficiary to replace or repair such goods;
(e)   obtain all such information as the Association or FNDR
shall reasonably request relating to the foregoing and to the
administration,   operations  and   financial  condition  of   the
Beneficiary and to the benefits to be derived from the Investment
Project; and
(f)   suspend or terminate the right of the Beneficiary to the
use of the proceeds of the Credit upon failure by such Beneficiary
to perform its obligations under its contract with FNDR.
Part B:     Terms and Conditions of Investment Grants
1.    Each Investment Grant shall not exceed 95% of the cost of the
Investment Project.

3.    (a)   When presenting a Treasury Investment Grant to the

(b)   Each  request by   FNDR  for  authorization   to  make

Treasury Investment Grant shall contain a summary description of the
Beneficiary, and the Investment Project or Preinvestment Project,

(c)   Applications  and   requests  made  pursuant  to   the

to the Association on or before December 31, 1996.
4.    Investment Grants shall be made on terms whereby FNDR, in
respect of Treasury Investment Grants, and the Participating RDC, in
respect of RDC Investment Grants, shall obtain, by written contract
with the Beneficiary or by other appropriate legal means, rights

(a)   require the Beneficiary to carry out and operate the
Investment Project or Preinvestment Project with due diligence and
efficiency and in accordance with sound technical, financial,
environmental and managerial standards and to maintain records and
separate accounts adequate to reflect the operations, resources and
expenditures of the Investment Project or Preinvestment Project;
(b)   require that: (i) the goods, works and services to be
financed out of the proceeds of the Credit shall be procured in
accordance with the provisions of Schedule 3 to the Development
Credit Agreement; and (ii) such goods, works and services shall be
used exclusively in the carrying out of the Investment Project or
Preinvestment Project;
(c)   inspect, by itself or jointly with representatives of
the Association if the Association shall so request, such goods and
the sites, works, plants and construction included in the Investment
Project or Preinvestment Project, the operation thereof, and any
relevant records and documents;
(d)   require that: (i) the Beneficiary shall take out and
maintain with responsible insurers such insurance, against such
risks and in such amounts, as shall be consistent with sound
business practices; and (ii) without any limitation upon the
foregoing, such insurance shall cover hazards incident to the
acquisition, transportation and delivery of goods financed out of
the proceeds of the Credit or the Participating Loan to the place of
use or installation, any indemnity thereunder to be made payable in
a currency freely usable by the Beneficiary to replace or repair
such goods;

or the Participating RDC, as the case may be, shall reasonably
request relating to the foregoing and to the administration,

(f)   suspend or terminate the right of the Beneficiary to the

by such Beneficiary to perform its obligations under its contract
with FNDR or the Participating RDC, as the case may be.
Part C:     Terms and Conditions of Matching Grants
The amount of a Matching Grant shall not exceed: (a) 20% of
the cost of an Investment Project benefitting a low-income
population (as defined in the Credit Regulations) of a Municipality;
(b) 20% of the cost of an Investment Project for water supply and/or
sewerage, drainage or flood or land erosion control; (c) 20% of the
cost of an Investment Project for cadastre; and (d) 40% of the cost
of an Investment Project which meets the requirements referred to in
paragraphs (a) and (b) above.
