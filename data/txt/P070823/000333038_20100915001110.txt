7a.  Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
The project will improve education quality by improving the conditions and processes of teaching and 
learning at the school level, and the capacity for education service delivery across the education system by: 
(i) increasing the number of qualified teachers, (ii) improving the capacity and the quality of education 
service delivery by fostering community participation and by strengthening the management of human and 
financial resources at district and primary school levels, and (iii) improving learning outcomes at all levels 
by providing enhanced conditions for school effectiveness that will contribute to changes in the behaviors 
of
teachers and students.  This project is considered a bridging operation consolidating policies and 
building the foundation for a broader sector program.
