Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Technical Assistance Project) between the 
Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria (the "Borrower") and the International Bank 
for Reconstruction and Development (the "Bank"), dated September 26, 1990.  We also 
refer to the Borrower's request contained in a letter from the Directeur General des 
Relations Economiques Exterieures, Ministry of Economy, to the Bank dated July 28, 
1992, for reallocation of the proceeds of the Loan.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank, after due consideration, has acceded 
to the above request.  Accordingly, the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
1.
Paragraph (f) of Section 1.02 is amended to read as follows:
"(f) "CIEF" means the Engineering and Financial Expertise Center (Centre 
d'Ingenierie et d'Expertise Financiere) established pursuant to Decree No. 90-07 of 
the Borrower, dated January 1, 1990;"
2.
All references to CRD in the Loan Agreement shall be deemed to refer to CIEF.
3.
The word "and" appearing at the end of paragraph (h) is deleted, the existing 
paragraph (i) is re-lettered as "(j)" and a new paragraph (i) is inserted to read as 
follows:
"(i) "IGF" means the General Inspectorate of Finance (Inspection Generale des 
Finances) within the MOE".
4.
The word "and" is added at the end of the new paragraph (i).
5.
In Schedule 1, the table of paragraph 1 is replaced to read as follows:

6.
In Schedule 1, paragraph 3 is amended to read as follows:
"3. Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph 1 above, no withdrawals shall be 
made in respect of: (a) payments made for expenditures prior to the date of this 
Agreement; and (b) expenditures under Category 2 (c) of the table in paragraph 1 
above, unless the Borrower shall have complied with its obligations under Section 3.06 
of this Agreement."
7.
In Schedule 2, Part A (1) is amended to read as follows:
"(1) (i) Preparing and carrying out a training program for selected MOE 
personnel to strengthen their capabilities in, inter alia, the following areas: public 
finance, accounting and administrative, civil and commercial law and related 
procedures; (ii) auditing and accounting in supprt of IGF audits of Bank projects and 
public enterprises; and (iii) acquiring and installing technical equipment in 
connection with said training program."
8.
In Schedule 2, Part B (1) is amended to read as follows:
"(l) Providing technical assistance to the Central Bank for inter alia: (i) 
designing jointly a Central Bank computerization program and a financial sector 
information system; (ii) acquiring and installing computer equipment and related 
material; (iii) acquiring and installing money handling and control equipment, and 
acquiring money transport vehicles; and (iv) developing a debt management training 
program.:
9.
In Schedule 2, Part C (1) is amended to read as follows:
"(1) Preparing the following studies and statistical survey: (i) a study on the 
use of funds o insurance companies operating in the Borrower's territory; (ii) a study 
on the incidence of consumer subsidies in the Borrower's territory; (iii) a 
statisticall survey measuring living standards in the Borrower's territory."
10.
In Schedule 2, Part C (2) is deleted in its entirely.
11.
In Schedule 5, paragraph 1 (a) is amended to read as follows:
"(a) the term "Eligible Categories" means Categories (1), (2) and (4) set forth 
in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement."
All the terms and conditions of the Loan Agreement, as amended hereby, shall 
remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendments by signing and 
dating the confirmation on the enclosed copy of this letter and returning it to us.
