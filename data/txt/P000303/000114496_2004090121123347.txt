Re:
BURKINA FASO: Food Security and Nutrition Project
(Credit No. 2414-BUR)
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
Excellency,
We refer to the above-mentioned Agreement, dated October 6, 1992 (the 
Agreement), between Burkina Faso (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association), and to your letter, dated May 21, 1993, wherein you 
have requested the Association to have the Agreement amended in order to delete 
therein any reference to the recruitment of technical assistants for
purposes of the effectiveness conditions.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association accedes to your request and 
agrees, accordingly, to amend the Agreement by deleting in Section 7.01 (i) thereof 
the words "technical assistants and other".
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment, on behalf of the 
Borrower, by signing and dating the confirmation form on the enclosed copy of this 
letter and returning it to us for our records.
Sincerely yours,
