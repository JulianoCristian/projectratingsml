Excellency,
Re: Food Security and Nutrition Project (Cr. 2414-BUR)
    Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) for the Food Security and
Nutrition Project, dated October 6, 1992, between Burkina Faso (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association (the Association).  We also refer to your letter 
no 1554/MEF/SG/DG.COOP/DCM/OAJ-DHB dated October 27, 1997 requesting amendments to the Agreement.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association accedes to your request and agrees, 
accordingly, to amend the Agreement as follows (with changes indicated in bold):
1.
Section 3.01.(a) is deleted and replaced by the following:
“Section 3.01. (a) The Borrower declares its commitment to the objectives of the Project
2.
The table in paragraph one of Schedule 1 to the Agreement is deleted and replaced by the 
table set forth in the Attachment hereto. 
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and dating the 
confirmation form on the attached copy of this letter and returning it to us, while keeping 
the original for your records.
This amendment will become effective as of the date of confirmation of this letter.
