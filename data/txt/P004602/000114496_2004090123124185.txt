Re:  
Loan Number 4108-PH
(Third Elementary Education Project)
Amendments to the Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
I
refer to the Loan Agreement dated December 20, 1996, entered into between the Republic of 
the Philippines (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the 
Bank), for the above captioned Project, as amended (the Loan Agreement). I also refer to the letter dated 
September 5, 2001, from Mr. Roberto B. Tan, Assistant Secretary, on behalf of the Department of 
Finance, endorsing the request dated May 15, 2001, from Mr. Raul S. Roco, Secretary, on behalf of the 
Department of Education, Culture and Sports, for certain amendments to the Loan Agreement.
I
am pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, the Bank has agreed to your request and, 
therefore, the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
1.
WHEREAS clause (B) in the Preamble of the Loan Agreement is amended to read as 
follows:
“(B) the Borrower intends to contract from the Japan Bank for International Cooperation (JBIC) 
a
loan (the JBIC Loan) in an amount equivalent to one hundred twelve million dollars ($112,000,000) to 
assist in financing the Project on the terms and conditions set forth in an agreement (the JBIC Loan 
Agreement) to be entered into between the Borrower and JBIC; and”
2.
The word “OECF”, wherever it appears in the Loan Agreement, and except as otherwise 
replaced pursuant to this letter of amendment, is hereby replaced by the word “JBIC”.
3.
Section 1.02 (b) of the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows:
“(b) “DepEd” means the Department of Education (formerly known as the Department of 
Education, Culture and Sports), an agency of the Borrower, and any successor thereto.”
4.
The word “DECS”, wherever it appears in the Loan Agreement, and except as otherwise 
replaced pursuant to this letter of amendment, is hereby replaced by the word “DepEd”.
5.
Section 2.02 (b) of the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows:
Page  2
“(b) The Borrower may, for the purposes of the Project, open and maintain in dollars:
(i) a special deposit account for Parts A, B.1, B.2 (a), B.2 (b) and B.2 (d) of the Project 
and for the portion of Part B.2 (c) of the Project that is carried out by DepEd; and
(ii) a special deposit account for the portion of Part B.2 (c) of the Project that is carried 
out by DepEd through LGUs,
each in a commercial bank specifically authorized for this purpose by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas, on 
terms and conditions satisfactory to the Bank, including appropriate protection against set-off, seizure or 
attachment. Deposits into, and payments out of, the Special Accounts shall be made in accordance with 
the provisions of Schedule 7 to this Agreement.”
6.
Sub-paragraph (c) of Section 3.01 of the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows:
“(c) The Borrower shall: (i) in respect of each DEDP under Part B.2 of the Project, through the 
respective DepEd-DO enter into a memorandum of agreement satisfactory to the Bank with the 
respective provincial and municipal LGUs, parent representatives and other concerned parties, which 
shall set forth the obligations of each party to implement such DEDP in accordance with the TEEP 
Guidelines; and (ii) in respect of the carrying out of Part B.2 (c) of the Project, ensure that the school 
building program is in accordance with the TEEP Guidelines.”
7.
The Table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended as set forth in 
the Attachment I to this letter of amendment.
8.
Paragraph 2 (c) of Part B of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read as 
follows:
“(c) Expansion of student access to public elementary education in the Project Provinces 
through: (i) the construction of about 5,397 new classrooms; (ii) the repair and rehabilitation of about 
17,110 classrooms; (iii) the construction of other auxiliary facilities in selected schools; and (iv) the 
provision of furniture and equipment; and”
9.
Paragraph 2 of Part D of Section I of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read 
as follows:
“2.
Prior Review
The procedures set forth in paragraphs 2 and 3 of Appendix 1 to the Guidelines shall apply to:
(a)
each contract for civil works estimated to cost US$5,000,000 equivalent or more;
(b)
each contract for goods estimated to cost $1,000,000 equivalent or more; and
(c)
in respect of each Project Province, the first three contracts for works or goods irrespective of 
their estimated cost, including the first three contracts for works in respect of the portion of Part B.2 (c) 
of the Project that is carried out by DepEd to be procured after February 1, 2002.”
10.
Paragraph 10 of Part A of Schedule 5 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read as 
follows:
Page  3
“10.
The Borrower shall, by January 15, 2002, prepare and furnish to the Bank for its review 
and comment, a proposed strategy to address demand side issues in the provision of elementary 
education, including with respect to health and nutrition, and thereafter implement said strategy, taking 
into account the Bank’s comments thereon.”
11.
Schedule 6 to the Loan Agreement is amended as set forth in the Attachment II to this 
letter of amendment.
12.
Paragraph 1 (a) of Schedule 7 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows:
“(a) the term "eligible Categories" means for the DepEd Special Account Categories (1) (a), (1) 
(c), (2), (3), (4), (5) and (6) (collectively the DepEd Categories) and for the MDF Special Account 
Category  (1) (b) (the MDF Category), all as set forth in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to this 
Agreement;”
13.
Paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 7 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows:
“(c) the term “Authorized Allocation” means an amount equal to:
(i)
$3,000,000 for the DepEd Special Account; and
(ii)
$1,500,000 for the MDF Special Account;
to be withdrawn from the Loan Account and deposited in such Special Account pursuant to paragraph 3 
(a) of this Schedule, provided, however, that unless the Bank shall otherwise agree, the Authorized 
Allocation shall be limited to thean  amount equivalent to equal to $1,500,000 for the DepEd Special 
Account until the aggregate amount of withdrawals from the Loan Account plus the total amount of all 
outstanding special commitments entered into by the Bank pursuant to Section 5.02 of the General 
Conditions shall be equal to or exceed the equivalent of $20,000,000 for the DepEd Categories.”
Except as specifically amended by the provisions of this letter of amendment, all terms and 
conditions of the Loan Agreement otherwise remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm the Borrower’s agreement to the amendments set forth in paragraphs 1 through 13 
above by signing and dating the enclosed duplicate of this letter of amendment in the space provided, and 
by returning it to us.  This letter of amendment shall enter into effect as of the date of countersignature. 
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT
By /s/ Robert Vance Pulley
   Country Director, Philippines
