We refer to the Loan Agreement dated December 20, 1996 between the Republic of 
the Philippines (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), as amended on July 2, 1999, and December 13, 1999 
respectively, and to the letter from Mr. Joel Bafares, Undersecretary, Department of 
Finance dated March 24, 2000, requesting the Bank to raise the percentage of Bank 
financing, and to cancel twenty million, one hundred thousand United States Dollars 
(US$20,100,000)from the Loan.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank, after due consideration, has 
approved your request, and Schedule 1 of the Loan Agreement is amended as indicated in 
the Attachment to this Amendment Letter.
Except as specifically amended by this amendment letter, all provisions of the 
Loan Agreement remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement to the above amendment by signing the form of 
confirmation set forth below, returning to us one fully executed original of this 
amendment letter, and retaining one original for your records.
The effective date of this amendment letter is May 1, 2000, and it relates only 
to expenditures incurred after this date.
Sincerely,
