7a.  Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
The project Development Objective is to: expand access to and improve the quality of basic education 
Sierra Leone.  The grant components are to: (i) expand access to basic education by carrying out activities 
(a) to enhance the awareness of government fee-free and other education policies; (b) to increase 
enrollment in primary one; and (c) to construct, rehabilitate and provide basic schools with furniture; (ii) 
improve quality of teaching and increase access to learning materials by (a) carrying out primary and junior 
secondary teacher in-service and distance education training and (b) providing teaching and learning 
materials to basic schools; and (iii) enhance coordination and management of the education sector by (a) 
strengthening sector capacity to coordinate and monitor education interventions and (b) improving human 
resource and teacher payroll management.
