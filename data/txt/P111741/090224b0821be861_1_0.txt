                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project closing date was extended from April 2013 to August 2013 as per the request of the Palestinian Authority in order to accommodate a delayed start to several capacity
                         building packages, and some anticipated delays in a few civil works packages. The project officially closed on August 31, 2013.
                         Project disbursements from the World Bank fund and MDTF totaled US$ 34.35 million, representing 91% of the total US$ 37.74 million. Disbursement is expected to reach 100% by
