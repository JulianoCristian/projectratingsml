Re:
TURKEY - Loan. No. 4631 TU 
Agricultural Reform Implementation Project
Amendment to the Loan Agreement                     
Dear Mr. Ekren,
We refer to: (a) the Loan Agreement, dated July 12, 2001,  between the Republic of Turkey (the 
Borrower) and International Bank for Reconstruction and Development  (the Bank) for the above noted 
Project; and (b) the letter of October 3, 2001 from Dr. Salih Yilmaz, Acting Director General, The 
Undersecretariat of Treasury of the Borrower. The Borrower has requested the Bank to increase the 
existing ceiling in the Loan Agreement of retroactive financing for Category 6 of the table under Part A.1 
of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement, for job loss compensation payments under Part C of the Project, 
from $32,000,000 to $48,000,000. The Bank is pleased to accede to the Borrower’s request and hereby 
amends paragraph 3, Part A of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement as follows:  
In said paragraph, the amount “$32,000,000” is amended to read  “$48,000,000”. 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing, dating and returning to us the form of 
confirmation on the enclosed copy of this letter.
This amendment shall become effective upon receipt of the countersigned copy of this letter by 
the Bank.
Sincerely,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION
AND DEVELOPMENT
