Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Oporto : 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the Province of Buenos Aires (the Borrower), with Guarantee 
of the Argentine Republic (the Guarantor), and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the 
Bank) dated June 15, 2001, (the Loan Agreement).
Pursuant to your request dated December 6, 2001, we are pleased to inform you that the Bank hereby agrees 
to amend the Loan Agreement so as to increase the aggregate amount of National Competitive Bidding for works set 
forth in Section I, Part C.1 of Schedule 4 of the Loan Agreement.
  Consequently, the Loan Agreement is amended as follows:
1.
Section I, Part C.1 of Schedule 4 is hereby amended and replaced to read in its entirety as follows:
“1.
National Competitive Bidding
Works estimated to cost the equivalent of $350,000 or more per contract, up to an 
aggregate amount not to exceed $33,000,000 equivalent, and goods estimated to cost less than 
$350,000 equivalent but more than $100,000 equivalent, up to an aggregate amount not to exceed 
$4,000,000 equivalent, may be procured under contracts awarded in accordance with the 
provisions of paragraph 3.3 and 3.4 of the Guidelines.”
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating this letter in the spaces provided 
below. This amendment letter will be executed in three (3) counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  
Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed original to us and deliver one fully executed copy 
to the representative of  the Guarantor (the Minister of Economy). The provisions of this amendment letter will 
become effective as of the date of this letter upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this 
amendment letter.
