Re:
Third Agricultural Research Project (Loan 3130-BR)
Amendment
 Dear Mr. Minister:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement for the above-referenced Project between the 
Federative Republic of Brazil (the Borrower) and the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), dated January 10, 1990 (the Loan 
Agreement).
Following the request by the Executive Secretary of the Ministry of Agriculture 
and Agrarian Reform dated September 6, 1992, we are pleased to inform you that the 
Bank has agreed to delete the restriction on financing vehicles and computer equipment 
under the Loan and to amend Category (2)  of the table set forth in paragraph 1 of 
Schedule 1 of the Loan Agreement to read as follows:
"(2)
Goods:
100% of foreign
expenditures and 
15% of local
expenditures
(a)
Goods, other
   10,000,000
than vehicles
and electronic
or computer
equipment
(b)
vehicles and
    4,000,000"
electronic
or computer
equipment
If you are in agreement with the foregoing, please so indicate by signing and 
dating, the form of confirmation on the enclosed copy of this letter and returning it 
to us.  This amendment shall become effective on the date shown by your signature, but 
only after receipt by the Bank of the enclosed copy, duly countersigned.
Because of their interest in this matter, we are sending copies of this letter 
to H.E. Dr. Lazaro Ferreira Barbosa, Minister of Agriculture, Supply and Agrarian 
Reform; Dr. Murilo Xavier Flores, President of EMBRAPA; and Ambassador Jose Artur 
Denot Medeiros, Secretary, Secretariat of
International Affairs (SEAIN).
Sincerely,
