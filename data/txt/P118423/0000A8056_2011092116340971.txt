                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The grant became effective on March 23, 2011 and its implementation is on track. Several large procurement packages, including teacher training, developmental toys and visual
                         aids, are underway and contracts have already been signed with winning firms to provide furniture to classrooms. On the basis of current progress, the team expects the grant to
                         achieve its development objective in a timely manner.
