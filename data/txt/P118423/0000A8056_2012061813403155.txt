                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The grant is on track towards completion. The key performance indicators have been met. The training of primary school teachers in pedagogies has been completed. 3,200
                         teachers have participated in the training. 68 methodologists have been trained to serve as mentors and facilitators of pedagogical practice. This marks the completion of 100% of
                         the targeted teachers and pedagogical methodologists to be trained. The provision of furniture to all schools in four oblasts (Osh, Jalal-Abad, Talas and Issyk-kul) was completed.
                         The savings generated from the competitive procurement are used to provide furniture to schools in three additional oblasts. The provision of a standard package of teaching
                         materials, including games, reading books, visual aids and methodological materials was organized through a tender conducted in March, 2012. The delivery of these inputs enabled

                         the official launch of the 240 hour school preparation program country wide in early April, 2012. The significance of the launch means 2, 135 schools have opened at least one
                         preschool class offering the 240 hour program to the communities they serve. This is 2 schools more than the ordinal target of 2, 133 schools that the grant was designed to support.
                         The total number of children enrolled in the program amounted to 55,000. This provides good evidence on achieving the grant development objectives.
