                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The grant is closing at the end of December, 2012. The implementation is winding up with the final round of procurement of additional learning materials using the remaining funds.
                         All planned activities have been completed and the key performance indicators have been met. While the current disbursement shows a balance of US$1.4 million, the grant is
                         expected to be fully disbursed with the final payment for the package of additional furniture and children's learning materials in the next two weeks.

                         The latest data shows that the project provided inputs resulted in an expansion of the coverage of the 240 hour school preparation program from zero to over 50% of the cohort of

                         children of 6 years old. More specifically, this was achieved by the offering of 2440 pre-school classes in 2300 schools benefiting 55,267 children, equivalent to over 50% of the
                         children enrolled in Grade 1 in the 2012-2013 school year. This achievement puts the expansion in access to school preparation on a fast track. Supporting this expansion, the
                         project financed pedagogical training of 3,253 teachers and 68 methodologists. A standard package of teaching materials, including games, reading books, visual aids and
                         methodological guides was provided to all schools in delivering the 240 hour program. School furniture was provided to 7 oblasts, 3 more than the original target with the savings
