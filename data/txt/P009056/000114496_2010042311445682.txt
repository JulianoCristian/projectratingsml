               Re:  Loan No. 3077-TU (Agroindustry Project)
                    Amendment to Loan Agreement and
                    Supplemental Letter No. 4
Dear Mr. Egilmez:
     We refer to the Loan Agreement (Agroindustry Project) dated
June 28, 1989 between the Republic of Turkey (the Borrower) and the
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), as
amended (the Loan Agreement), and to Supplemental Letter No. 2 of
the same date.  We also refer to Mr. Selekler's letter of July 25,
1991, requesting certain amendments.  In response to your
Government's request, we are pleased to agree hereby to the
following amendments:
1.   Paragraph B1 of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is hereby
     deleted.
2.   In paragraph 2 of Supplemental Letter No. 2 to the Loan
     Agreement:
     "$ 5,000,000" is hereby replaced by "$10,000,000", and
     "$10,000,000" is hereby replaced by "$15,000,000".
     Please indicate your approval of the above amendments by
signing, dating and returning to us the attached copy of this
letter.
                                   Very truly yours,
                              /s/  Michael H. Wiehen
                                   Director
                                   Country Department I
                                   Europe, Middle East and
                                   North Africa Region
CONFIRMED by REPUBLIC OF TURKEY on 10/9/91
