7a.  Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
The project development objective (PDO) is to assist the Recipient in: (i) increasing the access to 
STI/HIV/AIDS treatment; (ii) mitigating the health and socio-economic impact of HIV/AIDS at the 
individual, household, and community level; and (iii) building strong and sustainable national capacity to 
respond to the HIV/AIDS epidemic.
