Amendment to the Development Grant Agreement 
 
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Development Grant Agreement (the Development Grant Agreement), as 
amended, for the above-mentioned Project, dated April 16, 2004, between the Democratic 
Republic of Congo (the Recipient) and the International Development Association (the 
Association).  We also refer to your letter, dated January 8, 2010, requesting specific amendments 
to the Development Grant Agreement. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to your request and proposes to 
amend the Development Grant Agreement as follows. A new Category (7) has been added in the 
disbursement table comprising Categories (1) to (5), this will facilitate disbursement at the end of 
the Project. We also take note of the addition of a Project Coordinator whose role is to be defined 
in the Project Implementation Manual.

SCHEDULE 6 
Performance Monitoring Indicators 
 
1. 
800 pregnant women living with HIV received antiretroviral to reduce the risk of Mother 
To Child Transmission (in areas targeted by MAP). 
2. 
20,000 adults and children with HIV received antiretroviral combination therapy
 
(in areas 
targeted by the MAP). 
3. 
300,000 persons aged 15 and older received counseling and testing for HIV and receive 
their test results. 
4. 
15,000 orphans and vulnerable children provided with schooling through the project. 
5. 
60% of sex workers targeted by the program report using condoms during their last 
sexual encounter. 
6. 
200,000 units of blood collected which are qualified according to national norms. 
7. 
25,000,000 condoms distributed. 
8. 
100,000 Sexually Transmitted Infection (STI) cases treated according to national norms. 
9. 
100% of ministries targeted by the project with an operational unit for HIV/AIDS control.
