NOTICE OF REGISTRATION 
Dear  Sirs/Madams: 
 
Re: Request for Inspection
 
- Rondonia Natural Resources Management Project (Loan 3444 BR)
 
 
 
The Inspection Panel (the “Panel”) received a Request for Inspection  (the “Request”) on June 16, 
1995 from Coordenação da União das Nações e Povos Indígenas de Rondônia, Organização dos 
Seringueiros de Rondônia, Associação do Povo Karitiana, Articulação Central das Associações Rurais para 
Ajuda Mútua, Movimento Sem-Terra, Associação dos Seringueiros de Machandinho, Associação dos 
Seringueiros e Soldados da Borracha de Pimenta Bueno, Associação dos Seringueiros de Guajará-Mirim, 
Associação dos Seringueiros do Vale do Guaporé, Associação dos Soldados da Borracha de Ariquemes, 
Associação Programa Amazõnia - Amigos da Terra Internacional, Comissão Pastoral da Terra, Conselho 
Indigenista Missionário, Associação de Defesa Etno-Ambiental, Associação de Presevação Ambiental e 
Recuperação de Áreas Indígenas, Projeto Pe. Ezequiel (Diocese de Ji-Paraná), Centro de Estudos e de 
Pastoral dos Migrantes, Ação Ecológica Vale do Guaporé, Comunidade BAHÁ’Í, Centro de Educaçãio e 
Alfabetização Popular, Sociedade Beneficente São Casimiro, Proteção Ambiental Cacoalense, Associação 
das Escolas Famílias Agricolas de Rondônia, Instituto de Pre-Historia, Antropologia e Ecologia (the 
“Requesters”) who claim that their rights/interests have been adversely affected by acts and omissions of 
the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (“the Bank”).  The Requesters claim that they 
have been directly and adversely affected by the Bank’s omissions and failures in the preparation and initial 
implementation of the above-referenced project.  The Requesters claim to be affected by alleged violations 
of  provisions of Bank policies and procedures set forth
 inter alia, 
in the following: 
 
Operational Directive 13.05:  
Project Supervision
 
Operational Policy 4.36: 
Forestry Policy
 
Operational Policy 11.02:  
Wildlands Policy 
Operational Directive 4.20:  
Indigenous People
 
Operational Directive 14.70:  
Involvement of NGOs in Bank-Supported Activities
 
Operational Directive 10.70:  
Project Monitoring and Evaluation
 
Bank Procedures 10.00: 
Investment Lending
:  
Identification to Board Presentation
 
Operational Directive 13.40:  
Suspension of Disbursements
 
 
 
In accordance with paragraph 18 of the Panel’s Operating Procedures (the “Operating 
Procedures”), I am notifying  you that I have on June 19, 1995, which is also the date of the dispatch of this 
notice, registered the Request in the Inspection Panel Register.

In accordance with paragraph 18 of Bank Resolution No. 93-10 and paragraph 18(d) of the 
Operating Procedures, Bank Management is hereby notified that it must provide the Panel no later than July 
19, 1995 with written evidence that it has complied, or intends to comply with the relevant policies and 
procedures in the implementation of the above-referenced project.  Allegations of non-compliance with OD 
11.00 on procurement matters need not to be addressed since such matters are outside the scope of the 
Panel’s mandate.  The essential criteria for the response to the Request are set out in paragraph 33 of the 
Operating Procedures. 
 
The Request has been assigned  IPN Request Number RQ95/3.
