                    Re: Loan 3395 AL
                        First Petroleum Project
                        Amendment to Schedule 1 to the Loan
                        Agreement
Gentleman:
     Please refer to the Loan Agreement (the "Loan Agreement")
entered into by and between the Societe Nationale pour la Recherche,
la Production, le Transport, la Transformation et la
Commercialisation des Hydrocarbures -- SONATRACH (the "Borrower" )
and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the
"Bank" ), and also to the Guarantee Agreement entered into by and
between the Bank and the Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria
(the "Guarantor" ), each dated August 02, 1991, in respect of the
above-referenced Project .
     The purpose of this letter is to amend the provisions of
Sub-paragraph 3 (ii) of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement .
Therefore, said Sub-paragraph 3 (ii) is hereby amended to read as
follows hereinbelow:
     (ii) no withdrawal shall be made under category (2) on
          account of expenditures in respect to Part C (4) of
          the Project, unless the Borrower has furnished to the
          Bank evidence, satisfactory to the Bank, showing that
          the Subsidiary Loan Agreement has been duly authorized
          or ratified by, and executed and delivered on behalf
          of, the Borrower and the Fund, and is legally binding
          upon said parties in accordance with its terms and
          conditions. "
     Kindly confirm your respective agreement, as the duly
authorized representatives of the Borrower and Guarantor,
respectively, to the foregoing amendment by signing and duly
dating the corresponding form of confirmation set forth below, and
returning this amendment letter to us at your earliest
convenience. Two signed duplicate originals of this amendment
letter are enclosed herewith for your respective records. This
amendment letter shall become effective, as of the date first
above-written, once it has been fully executed by both parties
thereto.
                              Very truly yours,
                                    INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR
                                      RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT
