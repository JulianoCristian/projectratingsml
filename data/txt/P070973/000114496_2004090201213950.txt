Re: Azerbaijan Republic: Financial Sector Technical Assistance Project 
(Credit No. 3518 AZ) 
 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister, 
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement between Azerbaijan Republic (the 
Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association) dated November 6, 
2001, for Financial Sector Technical Assistance Project and your letter dated October 8, 2002, 
requesting an amendment to the dates pertaining to the percentage of expenditures to be financed 
for incremental operating costs.  We are pleased to concur with your request. 
 
Consequently, the Association hereby changes the dates for percentages of expenditures 
to be financed under Category (3) 
–
Incremental Operating Costs – in paragraph A.1 of Schedule 
1
to the Development Credit Agreement as follows:  
 
“100% through June 30, 2003; and 0% thereafter .“ 
 
The revised Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement is attached as an 
Attachment to this letter of amendment.
