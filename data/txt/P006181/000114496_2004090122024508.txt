Dear Sir/Madam:
Re: Bolivia - Credit No. 2650-BO (Education Reform Project) Amen&nent
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement between the Republic of Bolivia 
(the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association) on the 
above-mentioned Project, dated November 22, 1994.
We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to your request in your letter dated 
April 7, 1995, the Association hereby agrees to amend the Development Credit Agreement 
as follows:
(a)
Section 5.01 paragraph (b):
(i)
the following words are added after the word "Agreement' ' in the 
first fine: "or the Third Swedish Grant Agreement or the KfW Grant 
Agreement";
(ii)
the following words are added in the third fine after the word 
"agree": "or the Dutch Grant Agreement shall have failed to become 
effective by December 31, 1996".
(b)
Schedule 1
(i)
The table in paragraph I is amended to read as per the attachment 
to this letter;
(ii)
Paragraph 3 is amended as follows:
(A) the word "and" is deleted at the end of subparagraph (b);
(B) the period at the end of subparagraph (c) is replaced by and"; 
and
(C) a new subparagraph (d) is added to read as follows:
"(d)
expenditures, under any part of the Project, that IDB 
has financed or agreed to finance."
(iii) Paragraph 4 is amended as follows:
(A)
the word "becomes" in the third line is deleted and replaced 
by the following words: ", the Third Swedish Grant Agreement 
and/or the Dutch Grant Agreement, as the case may be, become";
(B)
the words ", the Third Swedish Grant Agreement and/or the 
Dutch Grant Agreement, respectively" are added at the end of the 
paragraph.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing and dating the form of 
confirmation below and returning the same to the undersigned.
The provisions of this amendment letter shall become effective, upon receipt of 
the signed form of confirmation below, on the date the Development Credit Agreement 
becomes effective and on such date the requirement of Section 6.01 (a) thereof shall 
be deemed to have been waived by the Association.
