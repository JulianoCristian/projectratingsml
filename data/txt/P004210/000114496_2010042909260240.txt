Dear Sirs:
Re: Third Highway Improvement Project (Credit 2943 LA)
    (Section 3.01 of the Development Credit Agreement)
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement entered into between
the International Development Association (the "Association") and the Lao
People’s Democratic Republic (the "Borrower"), of even date herewith in
respect of the above-referenced Project.  We refer in particular to
Section 3.01 of the Development Credit Agreement.
In my capacity as the duly authorized representative of the Borrower,
I hereby represent and confirm that, without any limitation or restriction
upon any of the Borrower’s other obligations under the Development Credit
Agreement, the Borrower shall make available on terms and conditions
acceptable to the Association the following minimum budgetary counterpart
funds to assist in the financing of the Project: (i) during its Fiscal
Year 1997-1998, the equivalent of two million nine hundred fifty thousand
dollars ($2,950,000); (ii) during its Fiscal Year 1998-1999, the equivalent
of three million eight hundred forty thousand dollars ($3,840,000); (iii)
during its Fiscal Year 1999-2000, the equivalent of four million two hundred
ten thousand dollars ($4,210,000); (iv) during its Fiscal Year 2000-2001,
the equivalent of four million seventy thousand dollars ($4,070,000); and
(v) during its Fiscal Year 2001-2002, the equivalent of one hundred
thousand dollars ($100,000).
It is our mutual understanding that, in making the Credit and entering
into the Development Credit Agreement, the Association has relied on the
representation contained herein.
Very truly yours,
LAO PEOPLE’S DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC
By /s/ Hiem Phommachanh
       Authorized Representative
