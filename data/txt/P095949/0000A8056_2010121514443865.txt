                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall implementation of technical assistance has yielded positive results for municipalities despite some institutional instability experienced by some of them. This has resulted, for
                         example, in improved quality of the programming and implementation of infrastructure maintenance as well as in accelerated processing of the implementation of infrastructure works
                         under the direct responsibility of municipalities.

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The main challenge is in relation to the bulk of the works which are to be implemented under the delegated management contracting arrangements with NIGETIP. Despite some
                         improvements during the last 12 months, implementation progress is still very slow. There is a need to continue with targeted technical assistance activities as identified during the
                         June and September 2010 missions which provided a detailed action plan to boost project implementation. All these measures will be revisited during the project mid-term review
                         scheduled for May 2011.
