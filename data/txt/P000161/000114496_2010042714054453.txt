His Excellency
The Minister of Finance and Development Planning
Ministry of Finance and Development Planning
Gabarone
Republic of Botswana
Re:
Loan No. 3248 BOT
(Tuli Block Roads Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the above-mentioned Loan Agreement between Republic of Botswana (the 
Borrower) and International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), dated 
September 12, 1990, and to your request to amend such agreement in order to provide 
financing of consultants services under Part C of the Project.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank hereby accedes to your request and, 
consequently, proposes to hereby amend the first column of Category (2) in the table 
in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement to read as follows:
"(2) Consultants's services under Parts A and C of the Project".
If you agree to this proposal, please signal such agreement by countersigning 
and dating one of the duplicate originals of this letter in the space provided below 
and returning the countersigned original to us.  The amendment shall come into force 
on the date of countersignature by you.
Yours sincerely,
By /s/ David A. Cook
Acting Director
Southern Africa Department
Africa Region
