                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The overall project implementation status remains satisfactory. The disbursement‘s rate is at 67.78% and should reach 74% by the end-June 2012. Some progress has been made
                         since the last implementation support mission, especially with regards to the activities of components 1 (land transport) and 2 (maritime transport) that are lagging behind. For
