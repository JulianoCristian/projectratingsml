Amendment to the Grant Agreement
We refer to the Grant Agreement ("Agreement") for the PRIF Multi-Donor
Trust Fund Co-Financing of the Second Infrastructure Asset Management Project dated
February 1, 2012, between the Independent State of Samoa (the "Recipient") and the
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development and the International
Development Association (collectively, the "World Bank").
Pursuant to the discussions held between the Recipient and the World Bank
during the January 28 to February 7, 2013 mission and recorded in the aide memoire
dated February 11, 2013, we are pleased to inform you that, the Association hereby
agrees to amend Section 2.05 of the Annex to the Grant Agreement to read as follows:
"2.05. Project Monitoring, Reporting and Evaluation.
(a)    The Recipient shall monitor and evaluate the progress of the Project and
prepare Project Reports in accordance with the provisions of Section 2.06 of
the Standard Conditions and on the basis of indicators set forth below in the
attachment to this amendment letter. Each Project Report shall cover the period
of one (1) calendar quarter, and shall be furnished to the Association not later
than forty-five (45) days after the end of the period covered by such report.
(b)    The Recipient shall prepare the Completion Report in accordance with
the provisions of Section 2.06 of the Standard Conditions. The Completion
Report shall be furnished to the World Bank not later than six months after the
Closing Date."
All terms of the Agreement not hereby amended shall remain in full force and
effect.


﻿Please confirm the Recipient's agreement to the foregoing by having an
authorized official of the Recipient sign and date both enclosed copies of this
amendment letter, and returning one to the World Bank.
This amendment letter shall become effective on the date of the Recipient's
countersignature.
