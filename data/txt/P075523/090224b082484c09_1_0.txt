                         Implementation Status Overview
                         All physical works under the project have been completed, and the remaining activities related to training and road designs will be finished before closing. The project has helped to
                         improve the condition of roads and coastal protection infrastructure in Samoa, and strengthened the capacity to manage key assets, particularly for coastal communities. While gains
