                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objectives of the project are (i) to enhance the economic, environmental and social sustainability of transport and coastal infrastructure assets; and (ii) to manage
                         those assets and natural resources and respond to national emergencies, through an effective partnership with private sector stakeholders.
