Amendment of the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Mr. Chaudhury:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated June 18, 1990, between the 
People's Republic of Bangladesh (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association) for the Third Rural Electrification Project. We also 
refer to your letter dated November 2, 1991, requesting amendment of the Development 
Credit Agreement to change the currency of the Special Account established pursuant to 
Section 2.02 (b) of the Development Credit Agreement (Special Account) from dollars to 
Taka.
We are pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, the Association has 
agreed to the above request. Accordingly, the Development Credit Agreement is hereby 
amended as follows:
Section 2.02 (b) of the Development Credit Agreement is deleEed-and substituted 
by the following:
"Section 2.02 (b). The Borrower shall, for the purposes of the Project, 
open and maintain in Taka a special deposit accounit`in.a bank acceptable to 
the Association, on terms and conditions satisfactory to the Association, 
including appropriate protection against set-off, seizure or attachment. 
Deposits into, and payments out of, the Special Account shall be made in 
accordance with the provisions of Schedule 3 to this Agreement."
Terms and conditions under which the Special Account shall be operated pursuant 
to Section 2.02 (b) shall include, inter alia, provisions of the Circular dated 
September 2, 1991 of the Finance Division of the Ministry of Finance entitled 
"Procedure for operation of Convertible Taka Special Account (CONTASA) of IDA Aided 
Development Projects Under Government Departments/Autonomous/Semi-Autonomous Bodies", 
as may be amended in agreement with the Association.
Proceeds of the Credit converted into Taka for deposit to the Special Account 
shall be freely convertible and may be used to meet eligible expenditures in Taka as 
well as foreign currencies. 
Subject to your confirmation as indicated below, this amendment will Subject to 
your confirm be effective as of the date of this letter.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the 
enclosed form of confirmation and returning it to us.
