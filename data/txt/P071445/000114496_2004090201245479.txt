Annex
Demobilization and Reintegration Project
Key Performance Indicators
(Actual numbers in comparison to objectives et out in the Annual Work Plan and Budget 
for the reporting period)
1.
Number of soldiers removed from military records and payroll database, as 
verified by review of records by the independent monitoring and evaluation consultant.
2.
Number and value of cash and in-kind transitional support payments delivered to 
veterans at discharge, as verified by review of financial records interviews with 
veterans and direct observation.
3.
Number of veteran families who have received reintegration assistance packages, 
as verified by review of records.
4.
Type and value of reintegration assistance packages designed, selected and 
delivered on time to each veteran family, including assets, counseling  and training, 
and based on community and family needs assessment as provided in the Project 
Implementation Manual verified by review of case files and household surveys in the 
field.
