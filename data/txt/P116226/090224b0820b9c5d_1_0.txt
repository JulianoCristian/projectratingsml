                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project development objective is to (i) initially preserve and later expand the Popular Health Insurance's coverage of people without social security and (ii)
                         strengthen the capacity of the Commission (for social protection in health) and state health systems to effectively administer the entitlements of the Popular Health
                         Insurance.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project was approved on March 25, 2010, became effective on December 17, 2010 and will close on December 31, 2013.The loan amount (US$1,250.0 million) was fully
                         disbursed on June 25, 2013.

                         Progress towards the Project Development Objectives and Project implementation remain satisfactory. The National Commission of Social Protection in Health (CNPSS) has largely
                         achieved its objectives; it exceeded the end-of-project targets of three out of its four outcome indicators, including Popular Health Insurance (PHI) coverage and nearly achieved a

                         fourth outcome indicator (93%). As of June 30, 2013, 53.3 million people had been enrolled in the Popular Health Insurance (PHI), covering practically 100% of people without social
                         security. With this, universal health coverage has been reached.
