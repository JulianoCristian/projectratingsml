                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project development objective is to (i) initially preserve and later expand the Popular Health Insurance's coverage of people without social security and (ii)
                         strengthen the capacity of the Commission (for social protection in health) and state health systems to effectively administer the entitlements of the Popular
                         Health Insurance.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project became effective on December 17,2010. Progress towards the Project Development Objectives and Project implementation are rated satisfactorily. Most importantly, the
                         National Commission of Social Protection in Health exceeded the end-of-project target for Popular Health Insurance coverage (41.1 million or 85%). As of December 31, 2010, it
                         enrolled more than 43 million people and covered more than 91% of people without social security.
