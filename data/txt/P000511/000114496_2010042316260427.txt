Re:
Credits No. 1945-CD and A-37-CD
(Financial Rehabilitation Credit)
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
and to the African Facility Credit Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the above-mentioned Development Credit Agreement between the 
Republic of Chad (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the 
Association) and to the African Facility Credit Agreement between the Republic of Chad 
(the Borrower) and the International Development Association as Administrator of the 
Special Facility for Sub-Saharan Africa (the Administrator) both dated August 5, 1988.
Pursuant to discussions between representatives of the Borrower and the 
Association in order to lower from $10,000 to $5,000 the threshold for individual 
invoices acceptable for reimbursement, we are pleased to inform you that the 
Association accepts to amend the Development Credit Agreement and the African Facility 
Credit Agreement in the following manner:
In paragraph 2(d) of Schedule I to both Agreements, the amount
of "$10,000" is to read "$5,000".
This amendment shall be effective for all expenditures incurred after January 
1st, 1989. 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing and dating the 
enclosed copy of this letter and returning it to us. 
Sincerely,
