August 5, 1988
His Excellency
The Minister of Planning and Cooperation
Ministry of Planning and Cooperation
B.P. 286
N'Djamena
Republic of Chad
Re:
Credit No. 1945 CD
African Facility Credit No. A-37 CD
(Financial Rehabilitation Credit)
Amendments
Excellency:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement and the African Facility Credit 
Agreement of even date herewith between the Republic of Chad (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association acting in its own capacity and as Administrator 
of the Special Facility for Sub-Saharan Africa.
Pursuant to your telex dated August 1, 1988, informing us of the opening of the 
Special Accounts for the above Credits in Banque Tchadienne de Credit et de Depots, we 
hereby amend the Development Credit Agreement and the African Facility Credit 
Agreement as follows:
1.
Development Credit Agreement: In Section 2.02 the existing paragraph (b) is 
replaced by the following new paragraph (b):
"(b) The Borrower shall, for the purposes of the Program open and 
maintain in FCFA a 
special account in Banque Tchadienne de Credit et de Depots on 
terms and conditions 
satisfactory to the Association.  Deposits into, and 
payments out of, the Special Account 
shall be made in accordance with the 
provisions of Schedule 4 to this Agreement."
2.
African Facility Credit Agreement: In Section 2.02 the existing paragraph (c) is 
replaced by the following new paragraph (c):
"(c) The Borrower shall, for the purposes of the Program open and 
maintain in FCFA a 
special account in Banque Tchadienne de Credit et de Depots on 
terms and conditions 
satisfactory to the Administrator.  Deposits into, 
and payments out of, the Special Account 
shall be made in accordance with the 
provisions of Schedule 4 to this Agreement."
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
By /s/ Michael J. Gillette
       Acting Regional Vice President, Africa
