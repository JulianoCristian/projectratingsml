RE:
Loan 3529-CH (Environmental Institutions Development Project)
Amendment
Dear Mr. Aninat:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement (Environmental Institutions Development 
Project) between the Republic of Chile and the International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank) dated December 15, 1992, and to the letter from the Acting 
Minister of Finance, dated December 18, 1997, requesting some amendments to the Loan 
Agreement.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank agrees, with such request, and hereby 
proposes to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
Section 1.0o2 (o) is modified to read as follows:
“(o) ‘Dirección Ejecutiva’ means CONAMA’s Executive Director.
2.
The word “ST y A” is replaced by “Dirección Ejecutiva” wherever it appears in 
the Loan Agreement.
3.
In Section 2.03, the date “June 30, 1998” is replaced by “June 30, 1999”.
4.
Section 3.03 of the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows:
“Section 3.03. The Borrower shall: (a) submit, no later than July 30 of 
each year during 
the execution of the Project, of the Bank’s review and 
comments, the Dirección Ejecutiva’s 
program of Project activities for the 
next succeeding year, including the proposed allocation of 
fund therefor; and (b) 
afford the Bank a reasonable opportunity to comment, not later than 
November of each year, on program priorities included in the proposed CONAMA’s budget 
for the 
next succeeding year.”
5.
Section 3.06 of the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows:
“Section 3.06.  The Borrower shall: (a) not later than June 30, 1998 
submit to the Bank, 
for its review and comments, the proposed 
environmental standards and norms referred to in Parts 
A.9 and B.3 of the 
Project; and (b) take all measures required to implement such standards and 
norms, taking into account the Bank’s comments thereon.”
6.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 2 of the Loan Agreement is amended as set 
forth in the attachment to this letter.
7.
In Schedule 2, Part A of the Project is amended by adding a new paragraph 9 to 
read as follows:
“9.
Assistance in the implementation of programs for the establishment 
of environmental 
and emissions quality norms, and in the preparation of plans for 
the decontamination of polluted 
areas and the prevention of pollution, through: (a) 
the development of economic and technical 
studies; (b) purchase of 
equipment for the measurement of pollutants; and (c) public 
participation in the development of such decontamination plans.”
Page  2
8.
Last paragraph of Schedule 2 is amended to read as follows:
“The Project is expected to be completed by December 31, 1998.”
9.
Part C.2 of Section I of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is amended by 
replacing the amount of “$230,000” by “$500,000”.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing the two originals of 
this letter and returning one fully signed original to us.  Upon receipt by the Bank 
of such fully signed original, the amendments will become effective as of the date of 
this letter.
Sincerely,
