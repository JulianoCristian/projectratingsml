                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The purpose of the additional financing is to expand PNPM Generasi coverage from the original 164 pilot rural kecamatan (sub-districts) into a further 150 sub-districts in five existing
                         provinces in 2012 and 2013 (Gorontalo, West Nusa Tenggara, East Nusa Tenggara, Sulawesi Barat, and Maluku), and strengthen the capacity of the implementing agency to


                                                                                                                                                                                                             Page 1 of 3
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                         Report No: ISR14398



                         manage project scale-up. The project is meeting the outcome and intermediate results indicator targets for: immunization coverage (75%); 10% increase in pre-natal care visits; and
                         10% increase in deliveries assisted by trained professionals since 2010. The implementing agency is on track to expand project coverage to a total of 499 sub-districts. It mobilized
                         consultants and facilitators in 130 new sub-districts in three new provinces during the reporting period. The trust fund is on track to close on schedule in December 31, 2014. A related
                         project is supporting PNPM Generasi implementation through 2017.
