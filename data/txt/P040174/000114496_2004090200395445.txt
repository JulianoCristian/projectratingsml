Amendment to the Loan and Project Agreements
Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement between the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela (the 
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), 
and the Project Agreement between the Bank and Fundación para el Desarrollo de la 
Comunidad y Fomento Municipal, both dated December 16, 1998.
We are pleased to inform you that, as requested by a letter from your Ministry 
dated February 29, 2000, the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan and Project 
Agreements as follows:
