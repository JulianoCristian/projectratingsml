Second Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency: 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement (the Agreement) between the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela (the 
Borrower), dated December 16, 1998. 
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to your letter dated February 4, 2004 to the 
Bank in which you requested a partial Loan cancellation in the amount of $24,993,000, an 
extension of the Closing Date, an increase in the percentage of financing of Category (1) of the 
Loan, and a reallocation of Loan proceeds, the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan and Project 
Agreements as follows: 
I. Project 
Agreement
1. 
Second paragraph of the Preamble is hereby amended and replaced to read in its entirety 
as follows: 
“WHEREAS by the Loan Agreement of even date herewith between the Bolivarian 
Republic of Venezuela (the Borrower) and the Bank, the Bank has agreed to make a loan to the 
Borrower in the amount of thirty-five seven hundred thousand Dollars ($35,707,000), on the 
terms and conditions set forth in the Loan Agreement, but only on condition that 
FUNDACOMUN agree to undertake such obligations toward the Bank as are set forth in this 
Agreement;” 
II. Loan 
Agreement
2. 
Article II, Section 2.01 of the Loan Agreement is hereby amended and replaced to read in 
its entirety as follows: 
 
“The Bank agrees to lend to the Borrower, on the terms and conditions set forth or 
referred to in this Agreement, an amount equal to thirty-five seven hundred thousand Dollars 
($35,707,000).”
