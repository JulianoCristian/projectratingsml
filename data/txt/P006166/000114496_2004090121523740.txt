We are also pleased to inform you that, on an exceptional basis, we agree with 
your proposal to extend the Closing Date of the DCA by one year.  As a consequence, 
the Closing Date of Section 2.03 of the Grant Agreement is amended to read "December 
Page  2
31, 1997".
4.
Additionally, in order to conform with rules of the Association, the following 
additional language is hereby incorporated into Schedule 1 of the DCA:
"4.  The Association may require withdrawals from the Credit Account to 
be made on 
the basis of statements of expenditure for expenditures for:
 
(a)
services of consulting firms not exceeding the equivalent of 
$7,000;
(b)
services of individual consultants not exceeding the equivalent of 
$3,000, under such terms and conditions as the Association shall specify by 
notice to the Borrower."
5.
furthermore, in keeping with the above amendment, the paragraph in Schedule 5, 
Section II of the DCA shall heretofore be numbered 1, and the following paragraph 
shall be incorporated as paragraph 2, Section II, Schedule 5:
"2.  Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph 1 of this Section, the 
provisions of the Consultant Guidelines requiring prior Association review or 
approval of budgets, short lists, selection procedures, letters of invitation, 
proposals, evaluation reports and contracts, shall not apply to:  (a) 
contracts for the employment of consulting firms estimated to cost less than 
$7,000 equivalent each; or (b) contracts for the employment of individual 
consultants estimated to cost less than $3,000 equivalent each.  However, said 
exceptions to prior Association review shall not apply to:  (a) the terms of 
reference for such contracts; (b) single-source selection of consulting firms; 
(c) 
assignments of a critical nature, as reasonably determined by the 
Association; (d) amendments to contracts for the employment of consulting 
firms raising the contract value to $7,000 equivalent or above; or (e) 
amendments to contracts for the employment of individual consultants raising 
the contract value to $3,000 equivalent or above."
6.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating

of the duplicate originals to us at your earliest convenience.
