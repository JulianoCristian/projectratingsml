                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The implementation of the project is improving. The project has supported learning activities in more than 3,000 villages to help empower farmers, commercialize village production,


                                                                                                                                                                                                              Page 1 of 9
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                     Report No: ISR7308



                         strengthen interactions between key stakeholders, and support farmers' agricultural marketing and production initiatives. Nearly 600 new rural extension centers have been built and
                         200 existing ones renovated. A key focus now is to scale up Farmer Managed Activities to the district and provincial levels. Efforts are also underway to (a) strengthen linkages
                         between research, extension services, and farmers, (b) promote access to real-time supply chain and market information, and (c) disseminate innovative rice crop management tools.
