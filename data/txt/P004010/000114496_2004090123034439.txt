3.
Paragraph 2 (c) of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended as follows:
"(c)  the term "Project management and incremental operating costs" means expenditures 
for travel, per-diem, labor costs and office supplies to support Project coordination 
and supervision, dam inspection, and dam monitoring by the Project Management Unit, 
the Dam Safety Unit, and the Dam Monitoring Units at the central and provincial 
levels, but excluding salaries."
4.
Part B of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is amended by adding a new Part B (f) 
as follows:
"(f)
Strengthening the participation of communities in the carrying out of the dam 
safety programs for selected pilot dams, through the formation of dam safety community 
groups, and the carrying out of income generation activities by such community groups 
including the provision of related goods and technical assistance."
5.
Part C.4 of Section I of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is amended as follows:
The word "equipment" is replaced by the word "goods".
6.
Schedule 5 to the Loan Agreement is amended by adding a new paragraph 8 as 
follows:
"8.
In carrying out Part B (f) of the Project, the Borrower shall ensure that dam 
safety community groups that will carry out income generating activities enter into an 
agreement with the Borrower which shall specify: (a) the community group’s commitment 
to assist in ensuring the safety of the dam, and (b) the community group’s management 
of the income generating activity, including procurement procedures for goods."
Except as specifically amended hereby, all other provisions of the Loan Agreement 
remain in full force and effect. The above amendment shall enter into effect on the 
date of the Borrower’s countersignature of the letter.
