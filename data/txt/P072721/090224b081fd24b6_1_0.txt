                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The progress of the project towards the achievement of its development objective is rated moderately satisfactory, and so is the project implementation progress.

                         The project development objective was to be achieved through a "two-handed approach", combining improvements in the energy efficiency of residential buildings, and the gradual
                         implementation of the reform on heat pricing and billing (promoting consumption-based billing, and the modernization of heat supply systems to allow for consumption-based billing).

                         Two of the three project's outcome indicators are expected to be achieved by the end of October (current year building energy efficiency standard compliance and cumulative coal
