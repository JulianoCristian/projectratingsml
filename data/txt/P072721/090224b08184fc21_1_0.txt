                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The progress of the Project towards the achievement of its development objective, as determined by the Bank team during the last mission (October 22 to November 1, 2012) is
                         satisfactory, and so is the project implementation progress.
                         Project implementation has been improved significantly in 2012 and disbursement has increased from 38% to 55% (as of December 14, 2012). The project is being extended until
                         October 31, 2013 following a request from the Ministry of Finance.
