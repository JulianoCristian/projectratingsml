Re:
YEMEN: Credit No. 2570
Education Sector Investment Project
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement 
Your Excellency,
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement between the Republic of Yemen (the 
Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association) dated March 
23, 1994 (the Development Credit Agreement).  We also refer to your request contained 
in your letters dated December 3, 1996, and March 9, 1997, to amend the table set 
forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 (Withdrawal of the Proceeds of the Credit) to 
increase the percentage of expenditures to be financed under Categories (1) and (3) 
and to introduce a new Category to permit the financing of the operating costs of the 
Coordination Unit of the Project.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association, after due consideration, has 
acceded to the above request.  Accordingly, the Development Credit Agreement is hereby 
amended to read as follows:
1.
The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is modified to read as follows:
