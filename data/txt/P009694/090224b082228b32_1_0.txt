AMENDING AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT among INDIA, acting by its President (hereinafter
called the Borrower), INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
(hereinafter called the Association), THE STATE OF MADHYA PRADESH,
acting by its Governor (hereinafter called Madhya Pradesh) and
INDIAN DAIRY CORPORATION (hereinafter called IDC).
WHEREAS by a development credit agreement dated December 18,
1974 between the Borrower and the Association (hereinafter
referred to as the Development Credit Agreement), a Madhya Pradesh
agreement of the same date between the i'ssociation and Madhya
Pradesh (hereinafter referred to as the Madhya Pradesh Agreement),
and a project agreement of the same date between the Association
and Agricultural Refinance Corporation (hereinafter referred to
as the Project Agreement), the Association agreed to lend to the
Borrower an amount in various currencies equivalent to sixteen
million four hundred thousand dollars ($16,400,000), on the terms
and conditions set forth or referred to in the Development Credit
Agreement and on the basis, inter alia, that Madhya Pradesh and
Agricultural Refinance Corporation (hereinafter called ARDC)
agreed to undertake certain obligations in respect of the carry-
ing out of the Project described in Schedule 2 to the Development
Credit Agreement; and
WHEREAS the Borrower has proposed and the Association has
agreed that a part of the proceeds of the Credit provided for
under the Development Credit Agreement should be made available
to IDC for relending to MPDDC, Unions and Federations under the
Project on the terms and conditions hereinafter set forth;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
Definitions
Wherever used in this Agreement, unless the context shall
otherwise require, the several terms defined in the Development
Credit Agreement and in the General Conditions (as so defined)
have the respective meanings therein set forth.

ARTICLE IV
Execution of the Project
Section 4.01. IDC shall carry out its part of the Project
described in Schedule 2 to the Development Credit Agreement with


﻿-6-
due diligence and efficiency and in conformity with appropriate
administrative, agricultural, financial and engineering practices,
and shall provide, or cause to be provided, promptly as needed,
the funds, facilities, services and other resources required for
the purpose.
Section 4.02. Except as the Association shall otherwise
agree, procurement of the goods and civil works to be financed out
of the proceeds of the Credit, shall be governed by the provisions
of Schedule 1 to the Madhya Pradesh Agreement.
Section 4.03. Except as the Association may otherwise agree,
IDC shall cause all goods and services financed out of the pro-
ceeds of the Credit made available to it by the Burrower to be
used exclusively for the Project.
Section 4.04. (a) IDC shall furnish to the Association,
promptly upon their preparation, the plans, specifications,
reports, contracts documents and construction and procurement
schedules for its part of the Project, and any material modifi-
cations thereof or additions thereto, in such detail as the
Association shall reasonably request.
(b) IDC: (i) shall maintain records and procedures adequate
to record and monitor the progress of its part of the Project
(including its cost and impact), to identify the gooas and ser-
vices financed out of the proceeds of the Credit relent to it by
the Borrower, and to disclose their use in the Project; (ii) shall
enable the Association's accredited representatives to visit the
facilities and construction sites included in its part of the
Project and to examine the goods financed out of the proceeds of
the Credit and any relevant records and documents; and (iii) shall
furnish to the Association at regular intervals all such infor-
mation as the Association shall reasonably request concerning its
part of the Project, its annual work programs, budgets, and
quarterly progress reports, its cost and impact, the expenditure
of such proceeds and the goods and services financed out of such
proceeds.
(c) IDC shall enable the Association's representatives
to examine all plants, installations, sites, works, buildings,
property and equipment of IDC and any relevant records and
documents.
Section 4.05. IDC shall duly perform all its obligations
under the IDC Subsidiary Loan Agreement. Except as the Association

ARTICLE VI
Financial Covenants
Section 6.01. IDC shall maintain records adequate to reflect
in accordance with consistently maintained appropriate accounting
practices its operations and financial condition.
Section 6.02. IDC shall: (i) have its accounts and financial
statements (balance sheets, statements of income and expenses and
related statements) for each fiscal year audited, in accordance
with appropriate auditing principles consistently applied, by
independent auditors acceptable to the Association; (ii) furnish
to the Association as soon as available, but in any case not later
than six months after the end of each such year, (A) certified
copies of its financial statements for such year as so audited and
(B) the report of such audit by said auditors; and (iii) furnish
to the Association such other information concerning the said
accounts and financial statements and the audit thereof as the
Association shall from time to time reasonably request.
ARTICLE VII
Effective Date; Termination;
Cancellation and Suspension
Section 7.01. This Agreement shall come into force and
effect when:
(a) the IDC Subsidiary Loan Agreement referred to in Sec-
tion 3.01 (b) of the Development Credit Agreement, as amended, has
been duly executed on behalf of the Borrower and IDC and has been
duly authorized or ratified by all necessary governmental and
corporate action; and
(b) the Subsidiary Loan Agreement between the Borrower
and ARDC has been amended in a form satisfactory to the
Association.
Section 7.02. (a) This Agreement and all obligations of
the Association and of IDC hereunder shall terminate on the
earlier of the following two dates:


﻿7-9-
(i) the date on which the Development Credit Agreement
shall terminate in accordance with its terms;
or
(ii) a date on which the Madhya Pradesh Agreement and
the Project Agreement shall terminate in accordance
with their terms.
(b) If the Development Credit Agreement terminates in
accordance with its terms before the date specified in paragraph
(a) (ii) of this Section, the Association shall promptly notify
IDC of this event.
Section 7.03. All the provisions of this Agreement shall
continue in full force and effect notwithstanding any cancella-
tion or suspension under the Development Credit Agreement.
