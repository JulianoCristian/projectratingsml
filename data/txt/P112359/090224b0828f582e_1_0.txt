Project Development Objectives

 Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)
 The objective of the project is to provide additional transport capacity and reduce transport time between the less developed western region of
 southwest China and the relatively more developed Pearl River Delta region.


 Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project Objective?
