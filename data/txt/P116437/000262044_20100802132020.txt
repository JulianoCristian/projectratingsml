2. Project Objectives 
The EFA-FTI Catalytic Fund Grant aims to reduce class sizes by recruiting and retaining 
more teachers in the public education system, as well as deploying them in an equitable 
manner.   
 
3. Project Description 
(a) 
The project consists of one component.  The Year II CF Grant will be fully used 
to support the Government#s contract teacher program. It will contribute to the payment 
of the salaries of 37,200 primary public school contract teachers recruited during FY07, 
FY08, FY09 and FY10 during (a) the period from January 2010 through the date which is 
four months after the Effective Date, and (b) part of the remainder of the 2010 and 2011 
school year. The instrument will be a Specific Investment Loan (SIL), implemented via 
governmental institutions. The instrument and implementation arrangements provide 
efficient financing to a program that forms an integral part of the Government Education 
Sector Strategy.   
 
4. Project Location and salient physical characteristics relevant to the safeguard 
analysis 
The project will be implemented throughout the territory of Cameroon.
