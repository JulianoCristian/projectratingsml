GUARANTEE AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated September 14, 1998, between UNITED MEXICAN STATES (the 
Guarantor) and INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (the Bank).
WHEREAS (A) the Bank has received a letter dated May 13, 1998 from the Guarantor 
describing a program of actions, objectives and policies designed to achieve 
restructuring and development of the Guarantor's social insurance system (the 
Program), declaring the Guarantor's commitment to the execution of the Program, and 
requesting assistance from the Bank in support of the Program during the execution 
thereof;
(B) the Guarantor has carried out the measures and taken the actions described 
in the Schedule to this Agreement to the satisfaction of the Bank;
(C) on the basis, inter alia, of the foregoing, the Bank has decided, in support 
of the Program, to provide such assistance to the Guarantor by making a loan in an 
amount equal to four hundred million Dollars ($400,000,000) to Banco Nacional de Obras 
y Servicios Públicos, S.N.C. (the Borrower), on the terms and conditions set forth in 
the Loan Agreement, but only on condition that the Guarantor agree to guarantee the 
obligations of the Borrower in respect of such loan and to undertake other obligations 
as provided in this Agreement; and
WHEREAS the Guarantor, in consideration of the Bank's entering into the Loan 
Agreement with the Borrower, has agreed so to guarantee such obligations of the 
Page  2
Borrower and to undertake such other obligations as hereinafter provided;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
General Conditions; Definitions
Section 1.01. The "General Conditions Applicable to Loan and Guarantee 
Agreements for Single Currency Loans" of the Bank, dated May 30, 1995, as amended 
through December 2, 1997 and with the modifications set forth in Section 1.01 of the 
Loan Agreement (the General Conditions) constitute an integral part of this Agreement.
Section 1.02. Unless the context otherwise requires, the several terms defined 
in the General Conditions and Section 1.02 of the Loan Agreement have the respective 
meanings therein set forth and the following additional terms have the following 
meanings:
(a)
“AFOREs” means Administradores de Fondos para el Retiro, limited 
liability companies for the administration of SIEFOREs (as hereinafter defined);
(b)
“AFORE Maquiladora” means the activities performed by the AFOREs relating 
to record-keeping and the provision of information on behalf of Banco de México for 
workers who have not yet selected an AFORE;
(c)
“CNSF” means Comisión Nacional de Seguros y Fianzas, the Guarantor’s 
National Insurance and Bonding Commission;
(d)
“CONSAR” means Comisión Nacional del Sistema de Ahorro para el Retiro, 
the Guarantor’s National Commission of the Retirement Savings System;
(e)
“IMSS” means Instituto Mexicano del Seguro Social, the Guarantor’s Social 
Security Institute;
(f)
“ISSSTE” means Instituto de Seguridad y Servicios Sociales de los 
Trabajadores del Estado, the Guarantor’s Institute of Security and Social Services for 
Government Workers;
(g)
“SAR92” means the Sistema de Ahorro para el Retiro, the Guarantor’s 
Retirement Savings System introduced in 1992 pursuant to the Guarantor’s  Ley del 
Seguro Social (Social Security Law);
(h)
“SHCP” means Secretaría de Hacienda y Crédito Público, the Guarantor’s 
Secretariat of Finance and Public Credit; and 
(i)
“SIEFOREs” means Sociedades de Inversiones Especializadas de Fondos para 
el Retiro, specialized limited liability companies for the investment of retirement 
funds.
ARTICLE II
Guarantee; Transfer of Loan Proceeds
Section 2.01. Without limitation or restriction upon any of its other 
obligations under the Guarantee Agreement, the Guarantor hereby unconditionally 
guarantees, as primary obligor and not as surety merely, the due and punctual payment 
of the principal of, and interest and other charges on, the Loan, and the premium, if 
any, on the prepayment of the Loan, and the punctual performance of all the other 
obligations of the Borrower, all as set forth in the Loan Agreement.
Section 2.02. The Guarantor shall enter into the contractual arrangements with 
the Borrower referred to in Section 3.01 (a) of the Loan Agreement and, unless the 
Bank shall otherwise agree, shall not change or fail to enforce any provision of such 
contractual arrangements.
ARTICLE III
Page  3
Other Covenants
Section 3.01. (a) The Guarantor and the Bank shall from time to time, at the 
request of either party, exchange views on the progress achieved in carrying out the 
Program.
(b)
Prior to each such exchange of views, the Guarantor shall furnish to the 
Bank for its review and comment a report on the progress achieved in carrying out the 
Program, in such detail as the Bank shall reasonably request.
(c)
Without limitation upon the provisions of paragraph (a) of this Section, 
the Guarantor shall exchange views with the Bank on any proposed action to be taken 
after the disbursement of the Loan which would have the effect of materially reversing 
the objectives of the Program or any action taken under the Program, including any 
action specified in Section 5.01 (d), (e) and (f) of the Loan Agreement and any action 
specified in the Schedule to this Agreement.
