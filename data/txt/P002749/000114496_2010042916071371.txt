TANZANIA: Credit No. 1970-TA (National Agricultural and Livestock
          Research Project) Amendment to the Development Credit
          Agreement
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the above-
mentioned Project, dated February 3, 1989, between United Republic
of Tanzania (the Borrower) and International Development Association
(the Association), as amended to date. We also refer to your letter,
dated April 30, 1996, requesting the reallocation of funds, among
categories to ensure the completion of activities under the Project.
     The Association agrees to your request and accordingly amends
the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Development Credit
Agreement to read as in the Attachment to this letter.
     All provisions of the Development Credit Agreement, except as
amended herein, shall remain in full force and effect.
     These amendments shall be effective as of the date of this letter.
Sincerely,
James W. Adams
Director
Eastern Africa Department
Africa Region
