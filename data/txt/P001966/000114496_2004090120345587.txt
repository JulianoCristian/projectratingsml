               Re:  Credit No. 1880 NIR (Energy Project)
                    Amendment  to DANIDA Grant Agreement
Excellency,
     We refer to the above-mentioned Grant Agreement, dated March
18, 1988, between the Republic of Niger (the Borrower) and the
International Development Association (the Association), acting as
Administrator of Grant Funds provided by the Danish International
Development Agency (DANIDA), as subsequently amended by an amendment
letter, dated February 3, 1989, signed by the same parties.  We also
refer to your letter, dated August 29, 1989, requesting the
Association to increase the disbursement percentages indicated in
the Grant Agreement for all expenditures financed under that
Agreement.
     We are pleased to inform you that DANIDA has acceded to your
request and that, accordingly, the Association hereby agrees to
amend the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Grant Agreement
as follows:
