                 Re:   Loan No. 2921 UR
                       (Water Supply Rehabilitation Project)
                       Amendment to Loan Agreement and
                       Project Agreement
Dear Minister Ovalle and Mr. Bianchi:
      Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated April 14, 1988
between Republica Oriental del Uruguay  (the Borrower) and
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the
Bank) and the Project Agreement dated April 14, 1988 between
the Bank and Administracion de las Obras Sanitarias del
Estado (OSE).
      In response to the Borrowerþs request to reallocate a
portion of the Loan proceeds currently allocated under
Category (1), as set forth in the table in paragraph 1 of
Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement, to Categories (2) and (3)
therein due to the cost overruns of the activities to be
carried out under Parts A and B.9  of the Project,  we are
pleased to inform you that the Loan Agreement and Project
Agreement are hereby amended as follows:
      I.   Loan Agreement
           The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to
the Loan Agreement is amended to read as set forth in Annex 1
to this Amendment Letter.
      II.  Project Agreement
           Schedule 1 to the Project Agreement is amended to
read as set forth in Annex 2 to this Amendment Letter.
           This Amendment Letter shall be executed in three
counterparts each of which shall be an original, and shall
become effective on the date in which the Bank has received
one fully executed original of such Amendment Letter. Please
confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing
and dating the three counterparts of this Amendment Letter.
Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed
original to us.
Sincerely,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR
