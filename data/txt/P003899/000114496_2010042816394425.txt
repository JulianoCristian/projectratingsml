     Re:   Loan 2992-IND (Tree Crops Human Resource Development Project)
           Amendment of the Loan Agreement
Dear Minister Sumarlin:
      We refer to the Loan Agreement (Tree Crops Human Resource
Development Project) between the Republic of Indonesia (the
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development (the Bank) dated October 31, 1988.  We also refer to
your Ministry's telex dated June 2, 1990 requesting the Bank for an
increase in the authorized allocation of the Special Account under
the above project.
      The Bank is pleased to inform you that it agrees to the above
request.  Therefore, the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as
follows:
      1.    The amount stated in paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 6 to
the Loan Agreement is amended to read "$1,800,000".
      Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by
signing and dating the enclosed form of confirmation, and returning
it to us.
                                                Sincerely yours,
                                                /s/ Amar Bhattacharya
