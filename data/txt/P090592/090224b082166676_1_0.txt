                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has made good progress in 2013. The Outcome indicator targets are expected to be either fully or substantially achieved by the revised closing date for: new or upgraded
                         water supply coverage; percentage access to improved water supply and sanitation; new and rehabilitated piped water connections; and rural population with access to water supply.
                         Sustainability of water supply (measured through achievement of O&M cost recovery) will be reviewed in 2014.
