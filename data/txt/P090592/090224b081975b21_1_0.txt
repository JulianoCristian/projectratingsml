                         Implementation Status Overview
                         On project progress, implementation of new Water Supply schemes is progressing well (overall Project target is 3545 and achieved are 3105 villages (88%); under IDA, total
                         coverage target is 1545 villages and likely achievement is 1385 by end of Dec 12(90%). The Project is also making good efforts to take up more number of sewerage schemes in
                         villages (target is 100; 5 completed, 26 are being procured and another 56 are in pipeline) and optimistic of completing the targeted 200 villages under Performance Improvement
                         Schemes (Water).
