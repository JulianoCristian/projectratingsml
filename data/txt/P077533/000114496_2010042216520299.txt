Re:   
Grant No. TF051247 
 
(Japan Social Development Fund Grant for NGO Support Program)  
Amendment to Letter Agreement 
 
Excellency: 
 
We refer to
 the Letter Agreement (the “Letter Agreement”) relating to the Japan 
Social Development Fund Grant for NGO Support Program between the Transitional 
Islamic State of Afghanistan (the “Recipient”) and the International Development 
Association, acting as administrator of the grant funds provided by Japan (the 
“Association”), dated July 9, 2002. We also refer to the Recipient’s request, dated 
December 2, 2002, to increase the Authorized Allocation of the Special Account to four 
hundred thousand United States dollars (US$400,000).  
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association has acceded to the Recipient’s 
request. Accordingly, paragraph 1 (c) of Attachment II of the Letter Agreement is hereby 
amended as follows: 
 
“(c) 
the term "Authorized Allocation" means an amount equivalent to 
US$400,000 to be withdrawn from the Grant Account and deposited into the 
Special Account pursuant to paragraph 3 (a) of this Attachment.”
