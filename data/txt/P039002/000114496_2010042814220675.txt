          Re:  Loan 3070-YU
               (Slovene Coast Water Supply and Sewerage Project)
               Amendment to the Loan Agreement
          We refer to the Loan Agreement dated February 1, 1990 as
amended, between the International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development (the Bank) and Rizanski Vodovod Koper (the Borrower) and
your letter dated July 22, 1992 requesting changes to Schedules 1
and 2 of the Loan Agreement to permit the following:
     (1)  the financing of sewerage works for the Municipality of
          Koper; and
     (2)  the financing of a raw water pipeline from Rodik to the
          Rizana water treatment plant that will make it possible
          to purchase 130 lps of excess water from the Sezana
          Water Works.
     We hereby agree to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
     (1)  Paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 of the Loan Agreement is
          amended as set forth in the Annex to this letter;
     (2)  Paragraph (1), Part A of Schedule 2 is amended to read
          "530 1/sec" instead of "650 1/sec"; and
     (3)  Part B of Schedule 2 is amended to include the
          following new paragraph (6):
          "(6) Construction of a raw water pipeline from Rodik
               to the Rizana water treatment plant."
          Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by
signing and dating the form of confirmation below on the enclosed
copy of this letter and returning it to us.
                                      Very truly yours,
