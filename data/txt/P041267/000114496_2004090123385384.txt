Re:
Transport Rehabilitation Project (Credit No. 2819-YEM)
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement        
Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Transport Rehabilitation Project) 
between the Republic of Yemen (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association) dated March 6, 1996, as amended.  We also refer to the 
understanding between the Association and the Borrower that: (i) the responsibilities 
of the General Corporation for Roads and Bridges under the Project be assumed by the 
Borrower's Ministry of Construction, Housing and Urban Planning; (ii) the proceeds of 
the Credit be reallocated; and (iii) the description of the Project be modified.
Accordingly, the Development Credit Agreement is amended as follows:
1.
In Section 1.02:
(i)
Subparagraph (c) is deleted; and
(ii)
Subparagraphs (d) through (f) are relettered (c) through (e).
2.
Subparagraph (b) of Section 2.02 shall read as follows:
"The Borrower may, for the purposes of the Project, open and maintain in 
United 
States Dollars three separate special deposit accounts (the MOCHUP 
Special Account for Parts  A (1), A (2) and A (5) of the Project, the YRFB Special 
Account for Parts A (3) and A (4) of 
the Project, and the CAMA Special Account for 
Part B of the Project) in the Central Bank of  Yemen.  Deposits into, and payments out 
of, each respective Special Account shall be made in  accordance with the provisions of 
Schedule 4 to this Agreement."
3.
Section 3.01 shall read as follows:
"The Borrower declares its commitment to the objectives of the Project as 
set forth 
in Schedule 2 to this Agreement, and, to this end, shall carry out Parts 
A (1), A (2) and A 
(5) of the Project through MOCHUP, Parts A (3) and A (4) of the 
Project through the YRFB,  and Part B of the Project through CAMA, all with due 
diligence and efficiency and in 
conformity with appropriate aviation, 
engineering, financial and administrative practices,  and shall provide, promptly as 
needed, the funds, facilities, services and other resources 
required for the 
Project."
4.
The first line of Section 5.01 shall read as follows:
"The Borrower shall, and shall cause CAMA and the YRFB:"
5.
Section 5.04 shall read as follows:
"The Borrower shall, by December 31, 1996: (i) cause the YRFB to appoint 
the 
Consultants to assist the YRFB and the Road Maintenance Fund under Part A 
(3) of the 
Project, all in accordance with Section II of Schedule 3 to this 
Agreement.
6.

7.
Part A (1) of Schedule 2 shall read as follows:
"Rehabilitation and reconstruction of the Hodeidah-Salif Road."
8.
Part A (3) of Schedule 2 shall read as follows: 
"Provision of technical assistance for: (i) operation of the YRFB to 
manage the funds 
generated by Road User Surcharges to be deposited in the Road 
Maintenance Fund; (ii) 
establishment of a cost accounting system for the 
YRFB; and (iii) carryout a study of the 
institutional and managerial 
arrangements in the road sector."
9.
Part A (4) of Schedule 2 shall read as follows:
Page  3
“Strengthening the institutional capacity of the YRFB to manage the 
Borrower’s road 
network through: (i) provision of technical assistance to 
establish an appropriate planning 
system for maintenance of the road network; 
(ii) supply of survey and test equipment, 
computer systems and computer 
software to plan a road maintenance program; and (iii) 
provision of 
training and other technical assistance.”
10.
A new Part A (5) shall be added to Schedule 2 and shall read as follows: 
"Strengthen the capacity of MOCHUP to manage the Project through the provision of 
training, office equipment and supply, and technical assistance."
11.
Part B (1) of Schedule 2 shall read as follows:
"Reconstruction and rehabilitation of Aden International Airport Terminal 
and runway."
12.
In Schedule 4 Paragraph (a) of Section 1 shall read as follows: "the term 
“eligible Categories” means, in the case of the MOCHUP Special Account Categories 
1(a), 2(a), 2 (c) and 3(a) set forth in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to this 
Agreement; in the case of the YRFB Special Account Categories 2(b) and 3(b) set forth 
in the said table; and in the case of the CAMA Special Account Categories 1(b), 2(d) 
and 3(c)."
13.
In Schedule 4, Paragraph (c) of Section 1 shall read as follows: "the term 
“Authorized Allocation” means, with respect to the MOCHUP Special Account, an amount 
equivalent to $500,000, with respect to the YRFB Special Account, an amount equivalent 
to $100,000, and with respect to the CAMA Special Account, an amount equivalent to 
$500,000, to be withdrawn from the Credit Account and deposited into the respective 
Special Accounts pursuant to paragraph 3 (a) of this Schedule." 
14.
This amendment is effective as of September 1, 1998.
15.
All the terms and conditions of the Credit Agreement that have not been amended 
hereby shall remain unchanged and in full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the 
confirmation on the enclosed copy of this Letter Amendment and returning it to the 
Association.
