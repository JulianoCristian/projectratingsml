                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project has been extended till June 30th, 2012 and has since made further implementation progress. Additional functionalities of the MEH data center IT system are being

                         developed to display and query Environment Marine Information Overlays (E-MIOs). This the results of a collaborative effort by the Littoral States who provided samples of the static
                         data and the mobilization of relevant IT services through the assignment of an IT Adviser to Batam Management Office. The Littoral States have identified ships which will participate
                         in the sea trials as well as five stations that will provide dynamic data (wind, current and tide). The Littoral States are also discussing institutional sustainability of the Project beyond
                         the closing date of June 30th, 2012.
