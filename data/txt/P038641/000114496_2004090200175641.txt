                             PROJECT AGREEMENT
      AGREEMENT, dated April 19, 1995, between INTERNATIONAL
BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (the Bank) and
GUARANTEE ADMINISTRATION UNIT (the GAU).
      WHEREAS by the Loan Agreement of even date herewith
between the Republic of Moldova (the Borrower) and the Bank,
the Bank has agreed to make available to the Borrower an amount
in various currencies equivalent to thirty million dollars
($30,000,000), on the terms and conditions set forth in the
Loan Agreement, but only on condition that the GAU agree to
undertake such obligations toward the Bank as are set forth in
this Agreement; and
      WHEREAS the GAU, in consideration of the Bank's entering
into the Loan Agreement with the Borrower, has agreed to
undertake the obligations set forth in this Agreement;
      NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
                                 ARTICLE I
                                Definitions
Page  2
      Section 1.01. Unless the context otherwise requires, the
several terms defined in the Loan Agreement and the General
Conditions (as so defined) have the respective meanings therein
set forth.
                                ARTICLE II
                         Execution of the Project
      Section 2.01. (a) The GAU declares its commitment to the
objectives of the Project as set forth in Section 3.01 of the
Loan Agreement, and, to this end, shall carry out the Project
with due diligence and efficiency and in conformity with
appropriate administrative and financial practices, and shall
provide, or cause to be provided, promptly as needed, the
funds, facilities, services and other resources required for
the Project.
      (b)   For the purposes of the carrying out of the Project,
the GAU shall:
            (i)   enter into an agreement with the Agent Bank
                  (the Agency Agreement), whereby the GAU shall
                  delegate to the Agent Bank the issuance of
                  Letters of Guarantee and the making of Payments
                  under Guarantee Contracts, such agreement to
                  contain terms and conditions satisfactory to
                  the Bank, including (except as the Bank shall
                  otherwise agree) those set forth or referred to
                  in Schedule 1 to this Agreement;
           (ii)   enter into contracts with Guarantee Holders
                  (the Guarantee Contracts), such contracts to
                  contain terms and conditions substantially
                  similar to those of the Framework Guarantee
                  Contract; and
          (iii)   duly comply with all its obligations under the
                  Agency Agreement and the Guarantee Contracts.
      (c)   Without limitation upon the provisions of paragraphs
(a) and (b) of this Section and except as the Borrower, the
Bank and the GAU shall otherwise agree, the GAU shall carry out
the Project in accordance with the Implementation Program set
forth in Schedule 2 to this Agreement.
      Section 2.02. (a) The GAU shall, at the request of the
Bank, exchange views with the Bank with regard to progress of
the Project, the performance of its obligations under this
Agreement, the Agency Agreement and the Guarantee Contracts and
other matters relating to the purposes of the Loan.
      (b)   The GAU shall promptly inform the Bank of any
condition which interferes or threatens to interfere with the
progress of the Project, the accomplishment of the purposes of
the Loan, or the performance by the GAU of its obligations
under this Agreement, the Agency Agreement and the Guarantee
Contracts.
      Section 2.03. The GAU shall:
      (a)   on the first day following each six-month period
after the Effective Date, furnish to the Bank a report, in such
detail as the Bank shall request, on the Guarantee Contracts
issued and, if applicable, the Payments made during such
period; and
      (b)   promptly after completion of the Project, but in any
event not later than six months after the Closing Date or such
later date as may be agreed for this purpose between the Bank,
the Borrower and the GAU, prepare and furnish to the Bank a
report, of such scope and detail as the Bank shall reasonably
Page  3
request, on the execution and initial operation of the Project,
its cost and the benefits derived and to be derived from it,
the performance by the Bank, the Borrower and the GAU of their
respective obligations under the Loan Agreement and the Project
Agreement, respectively, and the accomplishment of the purposes
of the Loan.
                                ARTICLE III
                   Management and Operations of the GAU
      Section 3.01. (a) The GAU shall carry on its operations
and conduct its affairs in accordance with sound administrative
and financial practices and the provisions of the Operating
Manual, under the supervision of qualified and experienced
management assisted by competent staff in adequate numbers.
      (b)   Unless the Bank shall otherwise agree, the GAU shall
not amend or otherwise alter the Operating Manual or any
provision thereof.
                                ARTICLE IV
                            Financial Covenants
      Section 4.01. (a) The GAU shall maintain records and
accounts adequate to reflect in accordance with sound
accounting practices its operations and financial condition,
including records and separate accounts for the Project.
      (b)   The GAU shall:
            (i)   have the records referred to in Section 4.01 of
                  this Agreement, its accounts and financial
                  statements (balance sheets, statements of
                  income and expenses and related statements) and
                  records and accounts for the Guarantee Account
                  for each fiscal year audited, in accordance
                  with appropriate auditing principles
                  consistently applied, by independent auditors
                  acceptable to the Bank;
           (ii)   furnish to the Bank as soon as available, but
                  in any case not later than six months after the
                  end of each such year: (A) certified copies of
                  said financial statements for such year as so
                  audited, and (B) the report of such audit by
                  said auditors of such scope and in such detail
                  as the Bank shall have reasonably requested;
                  and
          (iii)   furnish to the Bank such other information
                  concerning said records, accounts and financial
                  statements and the audit thereof, as the Bank
                  shall from time to time reasonably request.
                                 ARTICLE V
                       Effective Date; Termination;
                        Cancellation and Suspension
      Section 5.01. This Agreement shall come into force and
effect on the date upon which the Loan Agreement becomes
effective.
      Section 5.02. This Agreement and all obligations of the
Bank and of the GAU hereunder shall terminate on the date on
which the Loan Agreement shall terminate in accordance with its
terms, and the Bank shall promptly notify the GAU thereof.
      Section 5.03. All the provisions of this Agreement shall
continue in full force and effect notwithstanding any
Page  4
cancellation or suspension under the General Conditions.

                                SCHEDULE 1
                             Agency Agreement
      Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree, the Agency
Agreement shall provide, in a manner satisfactory to the Bank,
that the Agent Bank shall have, inter alia, the following
duties and responsibilities:
      (a)   to issue a Letter of Guarantee upon issuance of a
Guarantee Contract by the GAU;
      (b)   to make Payments to the Guarantee Holders upon
presentation of documentary evidence in a form specified, that
the GAU has admitted liability in the amount of the Payment in
question or an arbitration award was rendered against the GAU
in the amount of the Payment in question; in making the
Payment, the Agent Bank will use either: (i) funds transferred
for this purpose into a specified account by the Borrower, or
(ii) if such funds are not deposited by the Borrower within 10
days of issuance of either the admission of liability by the
GAU or the arbitration award against the GAU, the amount
deposited for this purpose into the Guarantee Account by the
Bank;
      (c)   to ensure that the aggregate amount of Guarantee
Contracts outstanding at any time shall not exceed the amount
of the Loan available at that time; and
      (d)   to report to the Bank on a monthly basis on the
Letters of Guarantee issued during the preceding month.
