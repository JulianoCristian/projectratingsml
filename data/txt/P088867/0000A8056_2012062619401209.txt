                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         Within the overall ECSEE APL objectives/context, the project (ECSEE APL2-Serbia) would provide investment support and technical assistance for Serbia, and
                         complement the donor assistance for creation and institutional development of TSMO and the regulatory agency -- obligations Serbia has to fulfill under Athens
                         Memorandum. The objective of the project is to improve electricity market access for consumers and suppliers through increase in the quantity, quality, reliability,
                         safety and efficiency of the bulk power transmission system, and strengthen capacity of the institutions to participate in the regional electricity market.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?
