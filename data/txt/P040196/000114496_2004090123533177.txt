Re: Ln.4456/Cr.3189-IND (Sumatera Basic 
EducationProject)
Amendment of the Credit  Agreement
We refer to the Credit Agreement (Sumatera Basic 
Education Project) dated April 16, 1999 between the 
Republic of Indonesia and the International Development 
Association  (the Association), as amended.  We also refer 
to the Ministry of Finance’s letter dated November 3, 2000, 
requesting an amendment to the Credit Agreement.  We are 
pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, the 
Association has agreed to amend paragraph 1(c) of Schedule 
7  to the Credit Agreement to read as follows:
“(c) the term “Authorized Allocation” means an 
amount 
equivalent to US$ 5,650,000  to be 
withdrawn from the Credit Account 
or the Loan Account 
and deposited into the Special Account pursuant 
to 
paragraph 3 (a) of this Schedule.”
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by 
signing and dating the enclosed copy of this letter and 
returning it to us.  This amendment will be effective as of 
the date of your confirmation.
With best regards, 
Sincerely,
By /s/ Mark Baird
Country Director, Indonesia
