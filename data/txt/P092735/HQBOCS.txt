INDIA: National Agricultural Innovation Project (Cr 41620-IN)
Partial Cancellation of Credit Amount
I refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated July 24, 2006 (the
"Development Credit Agreement") between India (the "Recipient") and the International
Development Association (the "Association") for the National Agricultural Innovation
Project (the "Project").
I also refer to the letter dated December 3, 2013, from Department of Economic
Affairs, Ministry of Finance, requesting us to cancel US$ 10 million of the Credit amount
of the above referenced Credit.
I am pleased to confirm that the Association hereby cancels an amount of SDR
6,517,000, equivalent to US$ 10 million, from the Credit No. 41620-IN, effective as of
December 3, 2013, the date that the Bank received the letter request.
The withdrawal table set forth in part A Schedule 1 to the Development Credit
Agreement has been revised accordingly. The revised withdrawal table is in Attachment
I to this letter.
