Re:
BANGLADESH: Credit No. 2016-BD
(Power Distribution (16 Towns) Project)
Amendment of the Project Agreement
Dear Sir:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated June 7, 1989 between the 
People's Republic of Bangladesh (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association) and the Project Agreement of the same date between the 
Bangladesh Power Development Board (BPDB) and the Association for the Power 
Distribution (16 Towns) Project. We also refer to the letter dated August 16, 1992 
from M. Talat Mahmud Khan, Assistant Secretary, Economic Relations Division, Ministry 
of Finance, Government of Bangladesh, requesting amendment of the Project Agreement to 
procure 56 power line carrier terminals from a single source under the above credit in 
accordance with the Association's Guidelines on Procurement.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association has agreed to amend paragraph 
4 of Part C of Schedule 1 to the Project Agreement by deleting the following amount as 
presently set forth in that paragraph: "$800,000" and replacing it with the following 
amount: "$2,000,000".
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the 
enclosed form of confirmation and returning it to us.  Subject to your confirmation as 
indicated above, this amendment will be effective as of the date of this letter.
Yours sincerely,
By /s/ Jochen Kraske
Director
