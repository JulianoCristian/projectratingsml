Re: Credit No. 3085-NI (Second Road Rehabilitation and Maintenance Project) 
Third Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Your Excellency:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Third Road Rehabilitation and Maintenance 
Project) between the Republic of Nicaragua and the International Development Association (the 
Association) dated July 8, 1998 (the Development Credit Agreement), as amended on October 22, 1998 
and December 16, 1998. Whenever used in this letter, the several terms defined in the Development 
Credit Agreement have the respective meanings set forth therein.
Pursuant to the request in the letter from the Minister of Transport and Infrastructure dated 
March 14, 2002, the Association hereby agrees to amend the following provisions of the Development 
Credit Agreement as follows:
1
.
Schedule 1
:
The table in paragraph 1 is hereby amended and replaced by the one in the Annex to this 
Amendment Letter.
2. Schedule 
2
:
Part  A.1 of the Project
:
The reference to road sections (d) through (f) is deleted. Accordingly, Part 
A.1 of the Project shall read as follows: 
“1.
Rehabilitation and improvement of the following sections of the Managua to Muhan 
road: (a) Portezuelo – Managua Airport – San Benito; (2) San Benito – Santa Rosa; and (3) Santa 
Rosa – Muhan.”.
Part C.5 of the Project is amended to read as follows: 
“5.
the carrying out of studies for the improvement of road safety and reforestation in the 
area of the Atlantic Biological Corridor as defined in Grant Agreement Number 28361 (Atlantic 
Biological Corridor Project) entered into between the Bank acting as Implementing Agency for 
the Global Environment Facility and the Borrower on August 25, 1997
;”
