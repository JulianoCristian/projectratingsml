Re: 
EL SALVADOR; Loan No. 4082-ES 
 
(Public Sector Modernization Technical Assistance Project) 
 
Second Loan Amendment 
 
Excellency: 
 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated December 11, 1996 between the Republic of El 
Salvador (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) to 
finance the Public Sector Modernization Technical Assistance Project (the Project), as amended. 
 
Pursuant to the request in letter dated May 8, 20002 from Carmen Regina de Arevalo, 
Technical  
Coordinator of the Presidential Commission of Public Sector Modernization
,
the Bank agrees to modify 
the aggregate amounts for procurement of goods under International Shopping and National Shopping 
procedures.  Therefore,  the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows: 
 
Schedule 4 
Part C: Other Procurement Procedures 
 
2. International Shopping  
 
Shall read: 
 
“Goods costing less than the equivalent of $100,000 but $50,000 equivalent or more, per contract, up to 
an aggregate amount equivalent to $830,000, may be procured under contracts awarded on the basis of 
international shopping procedures, at the option of the Borrower, in accordance with the provisions of 
paragraphs 3.5 and 3.6 of the Guidelines.” 
 
3. National Shopping 
 
Shall read: 
 
Page  2
“Goods costing less than the equivalent of $50,000, per contract, up to an aggregate amount equivalent to 
$770,000,  may be procured under contracts awarded on the basis of national shopping procedures, at the 
option of the Borrower, in accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 3.5 and 3.6 of the Guidelines”. 
 
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing this letter in the space 
provided below and returning it to us.  This amendment shall be executed in two counterparts, each of 
which shall constitute an original.  The amendment shall become effective upon receipt by the Bank of 
one fully executed original of this letter.
