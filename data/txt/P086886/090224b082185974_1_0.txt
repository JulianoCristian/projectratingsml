                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This ISR update is based on the results of a World Bank implementation support mission of November 3-7, 2013, as well as subsequent exchanges of email and documents with the
                         Client. Activities listed in the next steps table of the aide memoire are on track, except for cost share contracts for the ice plants and the transfer of assets to Fisheries Authorities
                         (FAs) -- both ongoing but with slight delays. The draft of the new fisheries law will not be submitted to Cabinet pending outcome from the National Dialogue Conference (NDC) as it
