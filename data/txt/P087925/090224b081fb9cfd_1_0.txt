                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The development objective of the proposed project is to establish a decentralized beneficiary-driven land distribution mechanism that allows organized landless or poor
                         farmers to acquire suitable agricultural lands and implement investment subprojects which puts them on a sustainable, higher-income, livelihood path.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has continued to implement successfully. It has established a total of 237 Productive Associations (PAs), benefiting close to 2,900 families, mainly poor indigenous
                         families; and contributed to the incorporation of aproximately157,000 hectares into productive use by the PAs. A graduation strategy and technical assistance strategy for PAs is
                         under implementation to ensure the sustainability of results.
