     Re: NIGER - Credit No. 1890 NIR
                 (Small Rural Operations Project)
                 Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency,
     Reference is made to the Development Credit Agreement dated
September 14, 1988, for the Small Rural Operations Project between
Republic of Niger (the Borrower) and the International Development
Association (the Association).
     In order to create a separate disbursement category for
agricultural extension, the Association hereby proposes to amend
paragraphs 1 and 2 of Schedule 1 to the above-mentioned Development
Credit Agreement as follows:
     -  In paragraph 1., the description of items to be financed in
Categories (2), (4), (5) and (6) is completed with the following
words: "except for Part A.2 of the Project"
     -  Category (9) is deleted and replaced by the following:
"(9) Unallocated       -      2,400,000
(10) Extension costs1,140,000      -         100%"
      under Part A.2
      of the Project
               - Paragraph 2 is deleted and replaced with the
               following:
               "2.   For the purposes of this Schedule:
               (a)  the term "extension costs" means the
                    following expenditures for local extension
                    staff: acquisition, operation and
                    maintenance of vehicles and equipment,
                    training, travel allowances and salaries of
                    incremental contractual local extension
                    staff; and
               (b)  the term "operating costs" means the
                    following expenditures for local non-
                    extension staff: operation and maintenance
                    of vehicles and equipment, and salaries of
                    incremental contractual local non-extension
                    staff."
     Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing
and dating the form of confirmation below on the enclosed copy of
this letter and returning it to us.
                           Very truly yours,
                          Michael J. Gillette
                               Director
                          Sahelian Department
                             Africa Region
