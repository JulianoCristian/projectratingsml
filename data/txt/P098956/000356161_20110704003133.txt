Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
The Second Post Primary Education Projects (PEPP2) development objective is to support the GoBF
strategy to increase the number and quality of students graduating from secondary school at reduced costs
for parents, with increased equity of access by gender and by area (rural-urban).
