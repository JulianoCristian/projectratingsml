                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall, the project implementation status remains satisfactory. Progress in achieving project development objectives is also rated satisfactory. The third round of project impact
                         evaluation has revealed that all three key PDO indicators results have been achieved: (i) 85% of producers are satisfied with the advisory services contracted out of a target of 80%;
                         (ii) 72% (out of a target of 70%) of producers members of POs participating in PSAOP1 have adopted at least one innovation and have integrated it into their production system; and

                         procurement are rated moderately satisfactory.
