Amendment to the JSDF Grant Agreement
Excellency: 
 
Please refer to the Grant Agreement between the Republic of Ecuador (the Recipient) and 
the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) dated October 11, 2002 
with regard to the above-captioned Project (the Grant Agreement). 
 
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to your letter of request dated February 20, 
2003 and our recent discussions on the subject, the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Grant 
Agreement so as to: (a) modify the definition of “training” in paragraph 4.2 (a); (b) increase the 
financing percentage of Categories 1 and 4; and (c) reallocate funds among categories 
accordingly. 
 
Consequently Paragraph 4.2 is hereby amended and replaced to read in its entirety as 
follows:

For the purposes of this paragraph, the terms: 
(a) 
“training” means the cost of: (i) per diem and travel expenses of trainees; (ii) 
rental of training facilities and equipment; (iii) training materials; (iv) training fees; and (v) 
consultants’ services for training; and 
 
(b) 
“travel” means the cost of per diem and travel expenses of the staff of 
PROJUSTICIA required for the implementation of the Project.”
 
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and dating this 
letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter shall be executed in two counterparts 
each of which shall be an original. 
Upon confirmation please return one fully executed original to us. The provisions set 
forth in this amendment letter shall become effective as of October 11, 2002 upon receipt by the 
Bank of one fully executed original of this amendment letter.
