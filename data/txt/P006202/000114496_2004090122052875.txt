Credit No. 2772-BO (Rural Communities Development Project) -
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Please refer to the above mentioned Agreement.
We are pleased to advise you that pursuant to your request and our recent 
discussions on the subject the Association agrees to amend said Agreement so as to 
reflect the substitution of Secretaria Nacional de Desarrollo Rural by Secretaria 
Nacional de Participacion Popular, pursuant to Supreme Decree No. 24134 dated October 
2, 1995.
As a consequence:
1.
Section 1.02 (v) is deleted.
 
2.
All references to "SNDR" are replaced by references to "SNPP" and the 
corresponding sentences are adjusted accordingly.
3.
The first sentence in Section 3.01 (a) is amended to read as follows: "shall 
carry out Parts A, B, C.1, C.2, C.4 and C.5 (a) of the Project through SNPP".
4.
The words "SNDR and" in Category (5) of the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 
are deleted.
5.
The words "SNDR," in the first paragraph of Schedule 2 are deleted.
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below. This amendment letter shall be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original. Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us.
The provisions of this amendment letter will become effective as of the date: 
(a) the Credit Agreement becomes effective; and (ii) the Association receives one 
fully executed original of this amendment letter.
Sincerely,
Peter Ludwig
Acting Director
Country Department III
Latin America & the Caribbean Region
