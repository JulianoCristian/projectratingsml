                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project will facilitate the expansion of water and wastewater services in Ningbo City and Cixi City respectively in an economically efficient and
                         environmentally sustainable manner, thereby protecting public health, improving the environment, and supporting economic growth.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project closed on December 31, 2010, as originally scheduled, and the project development objective has been satisfactorily achieved:
                         (i) For the Water Supply Component, all civil works, including the water treatment plant, the ring main and transmission pipelines have been completed, and are supplying good
                         quality, reliable water. As a result, domestic water supply from high quality and reliable water sources has increased from 10 percent at baseline to 83 percent at closing, exceeding
                         the end of project target of 75 percent.
                         (ii) For the Cixi Wastewater Component, two wastewater treatment plants with a combined capacity of 150,000 m3 per day, were built and are being operated by the newly
