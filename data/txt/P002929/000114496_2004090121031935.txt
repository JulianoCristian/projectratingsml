AGREEMENT AMENDING PROJECT AGREEMENT
Amending AGREEMENT, dated February 22, 2000, between INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT 
ASSOCIATION (the Association) and UGANDA ELECTRICITY BOARD (UEB).
WHEREAS (A) by a Development Credit Agreement dated January 9, 1992 between The 
Republic of Uganda (the Borrower) and the Association, the Association agreed to make 
available to the Borrower an amount in various currencies equivalent to eighty-six 
million nine hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 86,900,000) on the terms and 
conditions set forth in the Development Credit Agreement, but only on condition that 
UEB agree to undertake such obligations toward the Association as are set forth in the 
Project Agreement (the Project Agreement) entered into on January 9, 1992 between the 
Association and UEB;
(B)    by a subsidiary loan agreement (the First Subsidiary Loan Agreement) 
entered into on April 29, 1992, between the Borrower and UEB, part of the proceeds of 
the credit provided for under the Development Credit Agreement were made available and 
relent to UEB on the terms and conditions set forth in 
the First Subsidiary Loan 
Agreement; and
(C)
under an Agreement Amending the Development Credit Agreement of even date 
herewith between the Borrower and the Association, the Borrower has agreed to make 
available a supplemental credit (the Additional Financing) in the amount of 
twenty-four million Special Drawing Rights (SDR 24,000,000) of which an amount of SDR 
20,900,000 shall be made available to UEB under a subsidiary loan agreement (the 
Page  2
Second Subsidiary Loan Agreement) to be entered into by the Borrower and UEB;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree to amend the Project Agreement as 
follows:
Section 1.01. All references to the Development Credit Agreement shall mean the 
Development Credit Agreement as amended by the Agreement Amending the Development 
Credit Agreement of even date herewith.
Section 2.01. All references to the Subsidiary Loan Agreement in the Project 
Agreement shall include the First Subsidiary Loan Agreement and the Second Subsidiary 
Loan Agreement.
Section 3.01. This Amending Agreement shall come into force on the date upon 
which the Agreement Amending the Development Credit Agreement becomes effective.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto, acting through their duly authorized 
representatives, have caused this Agreement to be signed in their respective names in 
the District of Columbia, United States of America, as of the day and year first above 
written.
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
