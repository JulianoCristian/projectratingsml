Dear Mr. Minister,
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement, dated June 29,1999, between Azerbaijan Republic (the Borrower) 
and International Development Association (the Association) for the above noted Project. In your letter of September 
25, 2001, you have requested for certain amendments to be made in the Development Credit Agreement. Except for 
the reallocation of $40,000 in Section II, Part C of Schedule 3, the Association is pleased to accedes to your request 
and hereby amends the Development Credit Agreement as follows:
A.
Schedule 1, Withdrawal of the Proceeds of the Credit
The table of allocation of the proceeds of the Credit under paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is substituted with the 
revised table in the Annex to this letter.
B.
Schedule 2, Description of the Project, Part C: Project Management, Monitoring and Evaluation, 
sub-paragraph (i)
A comma and word " works" is added between words "training" and "incremental" in the second line of the 
sub-paragraph. The amended sub-paragraph reads:
(i)
Strengthening the institutional capacity of the PIU to carry out the Project through 
provision of 
technical assistance, training, works, incremental operating costs and 
equipment.
C.
Schedule 3 Procurement and Consultants' Services
Section I
. Part B: Procurement Procedures
2.
International Shopping,
The aggregate amount of $830,000 in paragraph 2 is reduced to $630,000.
The following new paragraph 4 is added in Section I, Part B of the Schedule
4.
Direct Contracting
Page  2
Textbooks, teacher guides and workbooks and costing $150,000 equivalent or less in the aggregate, 
may, with the Association's prior agreement, be procured in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 3.7 
of the Guidelines.
Section II
. Part C
: Other Procedures for the Selection of Consultants
Paragraph 3. Individual Consultants
The aggregate amount of $ 275,000 in the paragraph is increased to $475,000."
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing, dating and returning to us the form of 
confirmation on the enclosed copy of this letter.
This amendment shall become effective upon receipt of the counter-signed copy of this letter by the 
Association.
