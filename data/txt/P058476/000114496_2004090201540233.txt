Dear Mr. Dombrovskis: 
 
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Liepaja Region Solid Waste Management Project) 
dated December 19, 2000 between the Republic of Latvia (the Borrower) and the International 
Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank).  We also refer to your letter dated April 7, 
2003 requesting that the disbursement percentages for Goods and Works be modified to reflect 
local expenditures. 
 
After due consideration, the Bank has acceded to your request and the table set forth in 
paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 
(Withdrawal of the Proceeds of the Loan
)
to the Loan Agreement is 
amended as a set forth in the Attachment hereto. 
 
All the terms and conditions of the Loan Agreement, as amended hereby, shall remain in 
full force and effect. 
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing, dating and 
returning to us the attached copy of this letter.  This amendment will become effective as of the 
date of this letter upon receipt by the Bank of the copy of this Amendment Letter countersigned 
by you. 
 
Very truly yours, 
 
/s/ :Roger W. Grawe
