7a. Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
The objective of the project is to significantly increase the use of banking system transactions for effecting
or receiving payments for labor, goods and services nationwide through the implementation of a national
inter-operable payments system that facilitates access to financial services and allows users access to their
bank account using any mobile phone operator and cashing through any banking agent.
