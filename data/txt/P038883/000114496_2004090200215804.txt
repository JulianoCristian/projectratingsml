Re:
Loan No. 3960-AR (Enterprise Export Development Project) 
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank) and the Argentine Republic (the Borrower), dated March 
26,1996, with respect to the above-captioned Project (the Loan Agreement).  
In response to your letter of  March 6, 1998 in which you informed the Bank that 
pursuant to Decree No. 219/98 of February 23, 1998 the Secretariat of Small and 
Medium-Size Enterprise will be the Borrower’s agency responsible to carry out the 
Project, we are pleased to inform you that the Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement 
as follows:
1.
Section 1.02 is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety:
"(u) "SEPYME" means Secretaría de la Pequeña y Mediana Empresa, the 
Borrower’s Secretariat of Small and Medium-Size Enterprise);".
2.
Section 2.08 is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety:
"Section 2.08. The Borrower’s Secretary of Small and Medium-Size 
Enterprise, or any other person designated in writing by said Secretary, is 
designated as representative of the Borrower for the purposes of taking any 
action required or permitted to be taken under the provisions of Section 2.02 
of this Agreement and Article V of the General Conditions."
3.
The term "SCI" set forth in Sections 3.01 (a) and (d)  and 3.03 is replaced with 
the term "SEPYME".
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter may be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us.  The provisions of this amendment letter 
will become effective as of the date first above written upon receipt by the Bank of 
one fully executed original of this amendment letter.
Very truly yours,
