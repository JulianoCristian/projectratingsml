               Re:  Loan No. 3543-ME (Transport Air Quality
                    Management Project for the Mexico City
                    Metropolitan Area) Amendment to the Loan and
                    Guarantee Agreements
Dear Sirs:
          Please refer to the Loan Agreement between
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the
Bank) and Nacional Financiera, S.N.C. (the Borrower) dated
December 18, 1992 (the Loan Agreement) and to the Guarantee
Agreement of even date therewith between the United Mexican
States (the Guarantor) and the Bank (the Guarantee Agreement),
each as amended by letter from the Bank dated May 13, 1994.
          We are pleased to inform you that, as a result of your
request and our recent discussions on the subject, the Bank
agrees to amend the Loan and Guarantee Agreements as follows:
LOAN AGREEMENT
Preamble
1.  WHEREAS clause (G) is hereby amended by inserting the word
"buses" immediately after the words "(with regard to".
Section 1.02
2.  Section 1.02 (h) is hereby amended by replacing the words
"with regard to taxis, minibuses and own account trucks" with
the words "with regard to buses, taxis, minibuses and own
account trucks".
Sections 4.01, 4.02 (a), 4.02 (b) and 4.03
3.  Each of these Sections is hereby amended by replacing the
words "(with regard to taxis, minibuses and own account
trucks)", wherever they appear therein, with the words "(with
regard to buses, taxis, minibuses and own account trucks)".
Section 4.08
4.  This Section is hereby amended by replacing the words
"(with regard to taxis, minibuses and own account trucks)" with
the words "(with regard to buses, taxis, minibuses and own
account trucks)".
Page  2
Schedule 1
5.  Paragraph 2 of this Schedule is hereby amended by replacing
the words "(with regard to taxis, minibuses and own account
trucks)" with the words "(with regard to buses, taxis,
minibuses and own account trucks)".
Schedule 5
6.  The title to this Schedule is hereby amended by replacing
the words "(with regard to taxis, minibuses and own account
trucks)" with the words "(with regard to buses, taxis,
minibuses and own account trucks)".
7.  Section II, paragraph 2 (c) of this Schedule is hereby
amended to read as follows in its entirety:  "Subloans shall be
repaid in monthly installments. Subloans shall have a maturity
of at least one year but no more than seven years, except for:
(i) taxis under Part A.4 (a) of the Project, in which case the
Subloans shall have a maturity of at least one year but no more
than three years (with a maximum of a one month grace period);
and (ii) buses and minibuses under Part A.4 (a) of the Project,
in which case the Subloans shall have a maturity of at least
one year but no more than four years (with a one month grace
period).".
8.  Section II, paragraph 3 (b) of this Schedule is hereby
amended by replacing the word "twenty" with the word "twenty-
four" and by replacing the words "(four for taxis, four for
minibuses, four for own account trucks, four for equipment
under Part A.5 of the Project and four for vehicle
modifications)" with the words "(four for buses, four for
taxis, four for minibuses, four for own account trucks, four
for equipment under Part A.5 of the Project and four for
vehicle modifications)".
GUARANTEE AGREEMENT
Section 3.02
9.  Paragraph (a) (i) (A) of this Section is hereby amended by
replacing the words "(with regard to taxis, minibuses and own
account trucks)" with the words "(with regard to buses, taxis,
minibuses and own account trucks)".
10. Paragraph (a) (i) (B) of this Section is hereby amended by
replacing the words "(with regard to taxis, minibuses and own
account trucks)" with the words "(with regard to buses, taxis,
minibuses and own account trucks)".
          Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing
amendments by signing and dating this letter in the spaces
provided below.  This amendment letter may be executed in three
counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your
confirmation, please return one fully executed original to us.
The provisions of this amendment letter will become effective
upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this
amendment letter.
