DEVELOPMENT GRANT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated September
12,
1991, between the REPUBLIC OF
ECUADOR
(hereinafter called the Borrower) and INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR
RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT, acting as Administrator on behalf of
the MINISTER FOR DEVELOPMENT COOPERATION OF THE NETHERLANDS
(hereinafter called the Bank).
 
WHEREAS the Borrower has requested the assistance of the
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development and of the
Government of the Netherlands in the financing of part of the Lower
Guayas Flood Control Project
(hereinafter called the Project)
described in Schedule
2
to the Loan Agreement, dated March
8,
1991,
between the Borrower and the International Bank for Reconstruction
and Development
(hereinafter called the IBRD Loan Agreement);
 
WHEREAS in consideration of the abovementioned requests, the
Minister for Development Cooperation of the Netherlands
(hereinafter
called the Minister) has, pursuant to the Arrangements for the
Page  2
Administration of certain funds dated December
11
,
1986
, between the
Minister and the Bank
(hereinafter called the Arrangements), agreed
to make available to the Borrower fourteen million Netherlands
Guilders
(DFL 14,000,000; hereinafter called the Grant) and has,
pursuant to such Arrangements, appointed the Bank, as Administrator,
for the purpose of providing assistance to the Borrower; and
WHEREAS, the International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development as Administrator as hereinbefore mentioned has agreed on
the basis, inter alia, of the foregoing to extend the Grant to the
Borrower upon the terms and conditions hereinafter set forth;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
 
ARTICLE I
General Conditions; Definitions
Section
1.01. The parties to this Agreement accept all the
provisions of the General Conditions Applicable to Loan Agreements
of the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development, dated
January
1,
1985, with the same force and effect as if they were
fully set forth herein, subject, however, to the following modifica-
tions thereof
(said General Conditions Applicable to Loan Agree-
ments, so modified, being hereinafter called the General Condi
-
tions):
 
(a)
the term
"Bank", whenever used in the General Condi-
tions, means the International Bank for Reconstruction and Develop-
ment acting as Administrator under the Arrangements for the
Administration of certain funds, dated December
11,
1986, between
the Minister for Development Cooperation of the Netherlands and the
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development;
(b)
the term
"Loan Agreement", whenever used in the General
Conditions, means this Development Grant Agreement;
(c)
the term
"Loan", whenever used in the General Condi-
tions, means the Grant hereunder;
(d)
the term
"Loan Account", whenever used in the General
Conditions, means the Grant Account, the account opened by the Bank
in its books in the name of the Borrower to which the amount of the
Grant is credited;
(e)
the term
"Project" 
means the project for which the Grant
is extended, as described in the IBRD Loan Agreement, dated March
8,
1991, and as the description thereof may be amended from time to
time by agreement between the Borrower and the Bank;
(f)
Sections
2.01 (5), (7), (12), (13), (14), (16), (17),
(19), 3.02, 3.03, 3.04, 3.05, 4.01, 4.02, 4.03, 4.04, 4.05, 4.06,
4.07, 4.08, 4.10, 6.05, 6.07, 7.01, 8.01 (a), 9.03 and 12.05 of the
General Conditions are deleted;
(g)
in Sections
2.01 (11) and 6.02 of the General Condi-
tions, the term
"Bank" shall include also the International Bank for
Reconstruction and Development acting in its own capacity;
(h)
the term
"CEDEGE" 
means the Borrower's Comision de
Estudios para el Desarrollo de la Cuenca del Rio Guayas, the entity
which shall carry out the Project as set forth in Section
3.01 (a)
of the IBRD Loan Agreement; and
(i)
the term
"DFL" 
means Netherlands Guilders, the currency
of the Netherlands.

ARTICLE II
The Grant
Section
2.01. The Bank agrees to extend to the Borrower, on
the terms and conditions herein set forth or referred to, a grant in
an amount of fourteen million Netherlands Guilders
(DFL
14,000,000).
 
Section
2.02. (a) The amount of the Grant may be withdrawn
from the Grant Account in accordance with the provisions of the
Schedule to this Agreement, as such Schedule may be amended from
time to time by agreement among the parties, for expenditures made
(or, if the Bank shall so agree, to be made) in respect of the
reasonable cost of goods and services required for the Project and
to be financed out of the proceeds of the Grant.
 
(b)
Withdrawals from the Grant Account shall be made in the
respective currencies in which the expenditures to be financed out
of the proceeds of the Grant have been paid or are payable, or at
the option of the Bank.
 
Section
2.03. Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree,
procurement of the goods, works and services required for the
Project and to be financed out of the proceeds of the Grant shall be
governed by the provisions of Section
3.02
of the IBRD Loan
Agreement.
Section
2.04. The Closing Date shall be June 30, 1999 or such
later date as the Bank shall establish. The Bank shall promptly
notify the Borrower of such later date.
 
Section
2.05. CEDEGE is designated as representative of the
Borrower for the purposes of taking any action required or permitted
to be taken under the provisions of Section
2.02
(a)
of this
Agreement and Article V of the General Conditions.
 
ARTICLE III
Execution of the Project
Section
3.01. Articles III, IV and V of the IBRD Loan
Agreement are hereby incorporated into this Agreement with the same
force and effect as if they were fully set forth herein, it being
understood that all references in such Articles III, IV and V to the
"Bank" shall be deemed to be references to the Bank, as
Administrator on behalf of the Minister for Development Cooperation
of the Netherlands under this Agreement, all references to the
"Loan
Agreement"
shall be deemed to be references to this Development
Grant Agreement and all references to the
"Loan"
shall be deemed to
be references to the Grant hereunder.
 
Section
3.02. The Borrower shall make the proceeds of the
Grant available to CEDEGE under a contractual arrangement to be
entered into between the Borrower and CEDEGE, under terms and
conditions which shall have been approved by the Bank.
 
ARTICLE IV
Effectiveness; Termination; Representation
Section
4.01. The following is specified as an additional
condition to the effectiveness of the Development Grant Agreement,
within the meaning of Section
12.01 (c) of the General Conditions,
namely that the contractual arrangement referred to in Section
3.02
of this Agreement has been duly executed and delivered on behalf of
the Borrower and CEDEGE.
 
Page  4
Section
4.02. The following is specified as an additional
matter, within the meaning of Section
12.02 (c) of the General
Conditions, to be included in the opinions to be furnished to the
Bank, namely that, on behalf of the Borrower and CEDEGE, the
contractual arrangement referred to in Section
3.02 of this
Agreement has been duly authorized or ratified by the Borrower and
CEDEGE and is legally binding upon the Borrower and CEDEGE in
accordance with its terms.
 
Section
4.03. The date December 12, 1991 is hereby specified
for the purposes of Section
12.04 of the General Conditions.
 
Section
4.04. This Agreement and all obligations of the
Borrower and the Bank hereunder shall terminate on the date on which
the IBRD Loan Agreement shall terminate in accordance with its
terms.
 
Section
4.05. Except as provided in Section 2.05 of this
Agreement, the Minister of Finance and Public Credit of the Borrower
is designated as the representative of the Borrower for the purposes
of Section
11.03 of the General Conditions.
 
Section
4.06. The addresses specified in Section 7.02 of the
IBRD Loan Agreement shall be the addresses specified for the
purposes of Section
11.01 of the General Conditions.
 
Section
4.07. All the provisions of this Agreement shall
continue in full force and effect notwithstanding any cancellation
or suspension under the IBRD Loan Agreement.
 
ARTICLE V
Transfer of Rights and Obligations
Section
5.01. In accordance with the Arrangements, the rights
and obligations of the Bank under this Agreement may be transferred
to the Minister of Development Cooperation of the Netherlands.
The
Borrower accepts and agrees that, upon notice by the Bank to the
Borrower to that effect, the Minister of Development Cooperation of
the Netherlands shall, ipso facto, from the date specified in such
notice, be substituted in all rights and obligations of the Bank
under this Agreement as if the Minister of Development Cooperation
of the Netherlands had been an original party to this Agreement,
without any further action or formality required on the part of any
party, and from such date the International Bank for Reconstruction
and Development shall cease to have any rights or obligations as a
party under this Agreement.

signed in their respective names in the District of Columbia, United
States of America, as of the day and year first above written.
