                         Implementation Status Overview

                         The project continues made significant progress towards meeting its development objectives in the four participating cities, especially in Ho Chi Minh city. To date, about 95 % of
                         project funds including additional financing have now been committed with signed contracts in place. Approximately all identified 200 low income neighborhoods have been upgraded,
                         providing good quality basic infrastructure services for over 2.5 million low-income residents. The related primary and secondary infrastructure improvements will benefit more than
                         3.5 million additional people. Progress to date includes 24,721 households with improved water connections and 409,000 households (1.5 inhabitants) with sewer connections and
Public Disclosure Copy




                         improved drainage (70,000 for Haiphong; 46,000 for Nam Dinh; 248,000 for HCMC; and 45,000 for CanTho) within the selected low income areas. The condition of primary and
                         secondary infrastructure has also significantly improved with 158 km of improved drains/sewers/roads and cleaned canals with embankments; and 5.2 ha of lakes cleaned and
                         improved. In addition, over 50,611 micro loans have been made in these neighborhoods for housing improvement and 43,859 micro loans for income generation. Overall,
                         communities and local authorities have expressed a high level of satisfaction with the progress and quality of implementation in all four cities.
