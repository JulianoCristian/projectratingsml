                         Implementation Status Overview


                                                                                                                                                                                                          Page 1 of 7
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                 Report No: ISR10287



                         The project has made significant progress towards meeting its development objectives in the four participating cities. To date, approximately 200 low income neighborhoods have
                         been upgraded benefiting over 2.5 million low-income residents. Related primary and secondary infrastructure improvements will benefit more than 3 million additional people.
                         Progress to date includes 24,721 households with improved water connections and 409,000 households with sewer connections and improved drainage (70,000 for Haiphong; 46,000
                         for Nam Dinh; 248,000 for HCMC; 45,000 for CanTho) within the selected low income areas. The condition of primary and secondary infrastructure has also significantly improved
Public Disclosure Copy




                         with 210 km of improved roads; 185 km of improved drains/sewers; 26 km of cleaned canals with embankments; and 5.2 ha of lakes cleaned and improved. In addition, over 46,613
                         micro loans have been made in these neighborhoods for housing improvement and 41,010 micro loans for income generation. Overall, communities and local authorities have
                         expressed a high level of satisfaction with the progress and quality of implementation in all four cities.
