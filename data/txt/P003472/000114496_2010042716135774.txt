December 27, 1989
His Excellency Chi Haibin
Vice Minister
Ministry of Finance
Sanlihe, Beijing
People's Republic of China
Re:
Loan No. 2968-CHA
(Fourth Railway Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Fourth Railway Project) between the People's 
Republic of China (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction ant 
Development (the Bank), dated January 25, 1989. We also refer to the discussions we 
hat in the field with the representatives of Ministry of Finance (MOF) and Ministry of 
Railways (MR) where it was pointed out that the Loan Agreement does not have a 
separate component for transfer of technology to Mr, though the financing for this 
component is
included in the Loan for the Project.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank has agreed to amend the Loan 
Agreement as discussed with you. Accordingly, a new part is added to Schedule 2 to tho 
Loan Agreement as follows:
Part E:
Assisting MR in improving its operation and efficiency through transfer 
of technology in 
areas such as permanent way maintenance machinery, 
signalling and communications equipment. "
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
the confirmation form on the enclosed copy of this letter and returning it to us for 
our files. A copy of this letter is enclosed for your files, and another copy is being 
sent to MR for information.
Yours Sincerely,
By /s/ Shahid Javed Burki
Director
China Department
Asia Region
