Re:
Credit No. 3509 LA
(Agriculture Development Project)
Participating Countries
Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the Agricultural Development 
Project of even date herewith (Development Credit Agreement) between Lao People’s 
Democratic Republic (the Borrower) and International Development Association (IDA).
Pursuant to Resolution No. IDA 194 of the Board of Governors of IDA, we wish to 
inform you that, for purposes of the Project, the following members of the Association 
are not Participating Countries within the meaning of Section 2.01(12) of the General 
Conditions referred to in Section 1.01 of the Development Credit Agreement:
Bahamas, Bahrain, Brunei Darussalam, Cyprus, Oman, Qatar, Singapore, and United 
Arab Emirates.
Sincerely yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION

Authorized Representative
