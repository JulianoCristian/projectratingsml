RE:
Irrigation Agriculture Intensification II Project (Loan No. 
4354-CHA)
Amendment to Loan Agreement
1.
I refer to the Loan Agreement between the People’s Republic of China (the 
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), 
dated July 31, 1998, as amended (the Loan Agreement).  I also refer to the letter 
dated December 22, 2000 from Ju Kuilin, Deputy Director General, International 
Department, Ministry of Finance, on behalf of the Borrower, requesting a change in the 
disbursement percentages for civil works under Category (1)(a) of the table set forth 
in Paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement and to reallocate the proceeds of 
the Loan among various Categories in said table.
2.
I am pleased to inform you that after due consideration the Bank hereby concurs 
with the Borrower’s proposal and agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as hereinafter 
provided.
3.
The table set forth in Paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement (the 
Disbursement Table) is amended by changing the percentage of expenditures to be 
financed under Category (1)(a) from 25% to 40%.
4.
The proceeds of the Loan as set out in the Disbursement Table are reallocated as 
follows:  (a) US$30,900,000 from Category (2) (Goods) to Category (1)(a) (Civil works 
for Part A of the Project excluding items referred to in Categories (1)(b) and 
(1)(c)); and (b) US$4,000,000 from Category (2) (Goods) and US$3,500,000 from Category 
(5) (Unallocated) to Category (1)(b) (Civil works for land preparation, soil 
improvement, earthworks, tree planting and roads under Part A of the Project).
5.
The Disbursement Table as revised by paragraphs 3 and 4 above is set forth in 
the Annex to this amendment letter.
6.
Please confirm the agreement of the Borrower to the amendments set forth in 
paragraphs 3 and 4 above by countersigning and dating the corresponding form of 
confirmation set forth below, and returning one fully signed original of this 
amendment letter to us.  One fully signed original should be retained by the Borrower.  
7.
Except as specifically amended hereby, all other provisions of the Loan 
Agreement remain in full force and effect.  Upon receipt by the Bank of the copy of 
this letter of amendment duly countersigned by the authorized representative of the 
Borrower, the above amendment shall become effective as of the date of 
countersignature of this letter of amendment.
Sincerely,
By /s/ Yukon Huang
Country Director, China
East Asia and Pacific Region
