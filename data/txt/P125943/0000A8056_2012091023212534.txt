                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project continues to achieve its objective to enhance the capacity of the Government to manage the impacts of natural disasters, with project implementation is progressing as
                         expected. The Government of the Philippines adopted its National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Plan on February 7, 2012 and continues to build its capacity to
Public Disclosure Copy




                         implement the plan. Guidelines to local governments on the use of the National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Fund have been issued by the Department of the Interior
                         and Local Government (DILG), in coordination with the National DRRM Council. Following Tropical Storm Sendong, the Office of Civil Defense trained government staff on the Post-
                         disaster Needs Assessment methodology and carried out a Post-disaster Needs Assessment in target affected areas.
