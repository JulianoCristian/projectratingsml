Re:
Credit No. 2119-BO (Eastern Lowlands: Natural Resource Management 
and Agricultural Production Project) Amendment
Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Credit Agreement between the Republic of Bolivia (the 
Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association) dated May 7, 
1990. We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to your request and our recent 
discussions on the subject, the Association agrees to amend the Credit Agreement as 
follows:
1.
Section 3.02
Section 3.02 is amended by inserting: "(i)" at the beginning of the 
paragraph and by adding the following new subsection at the end of the 
paragraph:
"; (ii)  The Borrower shall by January 31, 1991, have deposited into the 
Project Account referred to in Section 3.02 (i) (b) above, one hundred and 
eighty thousand dollars, in addition to the amount deposited in 
accordance 
with Section 7.01 (d)"
2.
Section 7.01
Paragraph (d) is deleted in its entirety.
Paragraphs (e) and (f) are hereby redesignated paragraphs (d) and (e).
The redesignated paragraph (e) is hereby amended to read as follows:
"the Borrower shall have, for purposes of the Project, allocated in its 
budget and deposited in the Project Account referred to in Section 3.02 (b) of 
this Agreement, an amount of at least $220,000 equivalent."
3.
Schedule 1
A new section (v) is added to paragraph 3 (c):
"the Central Bank's Board of Directors shall have adopted the lending 
policies and procedures referred to in Schedule 1 to the Project 
Agreement; and"
4.
Schedule 4
(a)
In Section I.1, the date "December 31, 1990" is replaced by the new date 
"September 30, 1991";
(b)
In Section 1.5, the date "September 30, 199011 is replaced by the new 
date "January 31, 1991";
(c)
In Section II, paragraph 7 (a) , the date "June 30, 199011 is replaced 
by the new date "February 15, 1991"; and
(d)
In each of paragraphs 7 (b) f 8 (a) , 8 (b) (i) , 9 and 11 (a) , in each 
case, the present date is replaced by a reference to the new date,, 
"January 31, 1991".
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
this letter in the space provided below whereupon this amendment shall become 
effective. Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed original to us.
