            Amendment: Agricultural Services Project (No. 2108)
Excellency:
      We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Agricultural
Services Project), dated April 24, 1990, between the Republic of
Senegal (the Borrower) and the International Development Association
(the Association).  Further to the discussions held with the
representatives of the Borrower, we are pleased to inform you that
the Association hereby agrees to the following amendment to the
above-referenced Development Credit Agreement:
      Schedule 1, Paragraph 2 is amended and restated to read in its
entirety as follows:
      "2.   For purposes of this schedule, the term "operating
      costs" means expenditures related to the operation and
      maintenance of vehicles and equipment, allowances of field
      staff, office supplies, and telephone, electricity, water and
      fascimilie services."
      This amendment shall apply to any expenditure incurred on or
after July 6, 1990.
      Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing by
countersigning and dating the form of confirmation on the two
original copies of this letter provided and returning one original,
as countersigned, to us. This amendment shall become effective as of
the date of receipt by the Association of this letter duly
countersigned by you.
                            Sincerely,
                        Katherine Marshall
