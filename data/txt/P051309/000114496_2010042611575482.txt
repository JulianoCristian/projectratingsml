Amendment to the Trust Fund Grant Agreement
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Grant Agreement dated October 16, 2001, between Albania (the 
Recipient) and the International Development Association (the Association). We also refer to 
your letter dated October 10, 2002, requesting that the Grant Agreement be amended.  We are 
pleased to inform you that the Association hereby agrees to the request.  Accordingly, the Grant 
Agreement is amended as follows:  
Paragraph 4.3(b) shall be amended to read: 
“No withdrawal shall be made from the Grant Account after February 15, 2003 or such 
later date that IDA shall establish by notice to the Recipient (the Closing Date).  
However, withdrawals may be made after the Closing Date for expenditures incurred 
prior to the Closing Date if the corresponding withdrawal application is received by IDA 
within six weeks after the Closing Date, after which time any amount of the Grant 
remaining unwithdrawn from the Grant Account shall be canceled; and” 
Page  2
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by countersigning both copies of this 
amendment letter and returning to us one of the countersigned copies. The amendment will 
become effective as of the date of your countersignature, upon receipt of the countersigned copy 
of this letter by us.
