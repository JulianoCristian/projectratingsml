AGREEMENT, dated July 19, 1999 between INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT 
ASSOCIATION (the Association) and ALBANIAN DEVELOPMENT FUND (ADF).
WHEREAS (A) Albania (the Borrower) and the Association have entered into a Development 
Credit Agreement (Community Works Project) dated February 1, 1999 (the Development Credit 
Agreement), for the purpose of providing support during the execution of the Project, as described in 
Schedule 2 of the Development Credit Agreement;
(B)
the Borrower has requested the Association to provide further additional assistance in the 
support of the Project by increasing the amount made available under the Development Credit Agreement 
by an amount in various currencies equivalent to three million seven hundred thousand  Special Drawing 
Rights (SDR 3,700,000); and
WHEREAS ADF in consideration of the Association entering into an Agreement Amending 
Development Credit Agreement with the Borrower, has agreed to undertake the obligations set forth in 
the Project Agreement dated February 1, 1999 and the following amendments to Schedule I as set forth 
herein:
1.
The aggregate amount of contracts for works under National Competitive Bidding 
Procedures is revised to read “$10,527,000”;
2.
The aggregate amount of contracts under International Shopping Procedures is revised to 
read “$450,000”;
3.
The aggregate amount of contracts under National Shopping Procedures is revised to 
read “$250,000”;
4.
Part B (1) of Section II is hereby revised and now reads:
“1.Selection Based on Consultants Qualifications
Consultants’ services for beneficiary assessments; training for local governments and 
communities in environmental issues, formation of user committees, and subproject 
maintenance; training abroad in engineering techniques and needs and capacity assessment, 
estimated to cost less than $344,000 equivalent in the aggregate, may be procured under 
contracts awarded in accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 3.1 and 3.7 of the 
Consultants' Guidelines”.
This Agreement shall come into force and effect on the date of the Agreement Amending 
Development Credit Agreement becomes effective.
