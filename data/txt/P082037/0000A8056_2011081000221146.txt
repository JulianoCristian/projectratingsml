                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Additional Financing Loan became effective in November. The new administration of MIDEPLAN has prepared a plan for implementation of the AF which although it makes
                         adjustments in details of the work program agreed at the time of negotiations, is consistent with the objectives and activities envisaged in the AF Project Paper. The revised
                         procurement plan has been approved by the. A project restructuring was also recently approved in order to extend the closing date for the project until December 31, 2012
