                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The newly established Ministry of Social Development (previously the Ministry of Planning) is making substantial progress in the implementation of the activities being supported by
                         the Additional Financing, particularly in the areas related to the reforms in the targeting instrument and in the design of the ingreso etico familiar. There are also plans to undertake
                         two studies related to the parental education program which is being delivered as part of Chile Crece Contigo.
