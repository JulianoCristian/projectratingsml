Amendment to Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated August 1, 1996, between the Republic of 
Ecuador (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development 
(the Bank) for the above-referenced Loan.
We are pleased to advise you that pursuant to CAAM’s request of today’s date the 
Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
Section 1.02:
(a)
Subparagraph (c):  The first three lines are deleted and replaced by the 
following:
“(c)
“CAAM” means Comisión Asesora Ambiental, the 
commission established pursuant to presidential decree number 1107 
of 
September 22, 1993 and operating under the Borrower’s Ministry 
of the 
Environment pursuant to presidential decree number 290 of 
November 8, 1996, or 
such other agency or entity”.
(b)
Subparagraph (h) “Environmental Legislation” is amended by adding the 
following words in the ninth line after the date “June 7, 1994”:
“Executive Decree Number 195-A published in the Registro 
Oficial Number 40 on October 4, 1996, Executive Decree Number 290 
published in 
the Registro Oficial Number 64 on November 8, 1996”.
2.
Section 2.08.  The words “, or any person designated by him or her in writing,” 
are added after the acronym “CAAM”; and 
3.
Section 3.06. Subparagraph (b).  The date “April 30” is replaced by “October 31” 
and the date “April 30, 1997” is replaced by “October 31, 1997”.
The provisions of this amendment letter will become effective as of the date the 
Bank receives one fully executed original of this amendment letter and will be applied 
retroactively to November 8, 1996. 
If you agree with the foregoing amendments, please sign and date this letter in 
the spaces provide below.  This amendment letter shall be executed in two 
counterparts, each of which shall be an  original.  Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us.
Sincerely,
