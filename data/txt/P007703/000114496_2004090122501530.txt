Dear Sirs:
Please refer to the Global Environment Trust Fund Grant Agreement 
among International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank; 
acting as Implementing Agency of the Global Environment Facility), the 
United Mexican States (the Guarantor) and Banco Nacional de Obras y 
Servicios Públicos, S.N.C. (the Recipient) dated April 28, 1992, as 
amended (the Grant Agreement), entered into with respect to the 
above-captioned Project.  We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to 
your request and our recent discussions on the subject, the Bank agrees 
to amend the Grant Agreement to read in its entirety as indicated in the 
Attachment hereto.
Please confirm your agreement to the aforementioned amendment by 
signing and dating this letter in the spaces provided below.  This 
amendment letter may be executed in three counterparts, each of which 
shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation, please return one fully 
executed original to us.  The provisions of this amendment letter will 
become effective upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original 
of this amendment letter.
