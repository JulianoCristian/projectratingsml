                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project implementation rating remains moderately unsatisfactory because of persisting implementation delays in all components. During the May 2012 mission, it was finally
                         agreed to proceed with project restructuring. The underlying rationale for it is the relevance of the activities to the National Employment Strategy (NES) as well as the feasibility of
