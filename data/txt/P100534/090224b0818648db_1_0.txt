                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         Realign policy formulation with Employment, Technical and Vocational Education and Training (E-TVET) operational mechanisms through the development of employer
                         community participation in (i) sector policy formulation, (ii) institutional development and reform, and (iii) skill development program design and delivery.

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The following key next steps were agreed during the September 2012 mission wrap-up meetings:
                         The DCU and the implementing entities will closely follow-up on the agreed action plan to timely address any arising issue in day-to-day operations, pertaining to the sectoral
                         performance assessment system, decision support system supporting the technical and vocational education and training (TVET) sector , and the piloting of the new business models
                         in three vocational training institutes.
                         The Bank Team will provide intensive supervision in the remaining 12 months of the project's life.
                         Next mission is scheduled for January 20 - 22, 2013.

                         Restructuring History
                         There has been no restructuring to date.
