                         Implementation Status Overview
                         While there is consensus among stakeholders that the original project development objective remains valid and that there is a need to restructure the project to be more focused on
                         what can realistically been achieved in the remaining time, there has been serious delays since October 2011 on formally proceeding on the restructuring process; including delays in
                         GOJ's request for such a restructuring. In the same time, the project level activities are being carried out; including the development and adoption of operational procedures for

                         sectors that would be used to develop sectoral policy directives. This is in addition to the on-going support to the restructuring of the Vocational Training Corporation (VTC), through
                         the project's second and largest component; whereby the draft business and governance model has been presented during a conference held in February 2012 and feedback is
