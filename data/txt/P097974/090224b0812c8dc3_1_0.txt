Project Development Objectives
Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)
The proposed project development objective (PDO) is to: (a) increase access to water supply services in peri-urban areas of Bujumbura; (b) increase the reliability and
quality of electricity services; (c) increase the quality and reliability of water supply services, with primary focus on Bujumbura; and (d) strengthen REGIDESO financial
sustainability.

-0   Implementation Status Overview
..   The project is a multi-sectoral water supply and electricity infrastructure operation that focuses on provision of electricity and water services to Bujumbura, the capital city of Burundi
and to improve the financial and operational capability of REGIDESO, the public utility in charge of production and distribution of water and electricity services in urban centers.
Implementation delays incurred during the early stages of the project have essentially been addressed. During the past six months, REGIDESO has successfully completed the

rehabilitation works; (v) procured and installed 10,000 water meters, (vi) completed pre-feasibility and launched the feasibility of small hydro plants (phase 1 and 2); (vii) contracted
out rehabilitation of water supply works and construction has begun. With the strong commitment from REGIDESO for timely completion of the planned infrastructure works, and
