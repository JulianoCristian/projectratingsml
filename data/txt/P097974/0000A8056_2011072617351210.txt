                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The proposed project development objective (PDO) is to: (a) increase access to water supply services in peri-urban areas of Bujumbura; (b) increase the
                         reliability and quality of electricity services; (c) increase the quality and reliability of water supply services, with primary focus on Bujumbura; and (d) strengthen
                         REGIDESO financial sustainability.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is a multi-sectoral water supply and electricity infrastructure operation that focuses on provision of electricity and water services to Bujumbura adn other urban areas and
                         to improve the financial and operational capability of the Regie de Productiond'Eau et d'Electricite (REGIDESO). Implementation delays during the early stages of the project have
                         essentially been addressed. During the past six months, REGIDESO has successfully completed the following major actions: (i) signed contract for the electricity rehabilitation of
