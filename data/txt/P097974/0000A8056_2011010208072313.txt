                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The proposed project development objective (PDO) is to: (a) increase access to water supply services in peri-urban areas of Bujumbura; (b) increase the
                         reliability and quality of electricity services; (c) increase the quality and reliability of water supply services, with primary focus on Bujumbura; and (d) strengthen
                         REGIDESO financial sustainability.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is a multi-sectoral water supply and electricity operation that focuses on the rehabilitation of transmission and distribution infrastructure and a capacity building program
                         for REGIDESO (the national water supply and electricity utility) and the Ministry of Water, Energy and Mines (MoWEM). Implementation delays during the early stage of the project
                         have essentially been addressed and during the past six months, the project has shown significant implementation progress. Tender for the main electricity rehabilitation works has
