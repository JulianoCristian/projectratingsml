                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project has two key objectives: to contribute on the one hand to the supply of basic services and facilities to the respective conflict-affected populations;
                         and to contribute to capacity building of the state government to enable it to more effectively execute its mandate, especially at the local (decentralized) level.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         All MDTF financed components (water, education, vocational training, rural household support) which were implemented by UNICEF and UNIDO were completed as
Public Disclosure Copy




                         scheduled. These activities were completed at a satisfactory quality.

                         The PCU has been up and running with an office in Kadugli, capital of South Kordofan and been adequately carrying out their activities.

                         Health, demining and the physical works of the capacity building components financed by GoNU have been completed at a satisfactory quality. The rural roads component is now
                         also progressing well and due to be completed by project closing (June 30, 2010).
