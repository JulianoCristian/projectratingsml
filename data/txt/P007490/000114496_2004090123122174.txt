Re:     Loan 4083-JM (Public Sector Modernization Project) 
Second Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Sirs:  
 
We refer to the Loan Agreement between Jamaica (the Borrower) and the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development  (the Bank), dated October 2, 
1996 (the Loan Agreement), with respect to the above-captioned Project, as amended.  
Terms not otherwise defined herein shall have the meanings assigned to them in the Loan 
Agreement.  
 
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to your letter dated December 24, 2002 
and our recent discussions on the subject, the Association agrees to increase the aggregate 
amounts for International and National Shopping procurement methods.  Therefore the 
DCA is hereby amended as follows: 
 
Schedule 4, Part C, paragraph 2 of the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to 
read as follows: 
 
“2.   International and National Shopping 
 
(a)  Goods estimated to cost $25,000 equivalent or 
more but less than $50,000 equivalent per contract, up to 
an aggregate amount equivalent to $663,424, may be 
procured under contracts awarded on the basis of 
international shopping procedures in accordance with the 
provisions of paragraphs 3.5 and 3.6 of the Guidelines. 
 
(b)  Goods estimated to cost less than $25,000 
equivalent per contract, up to an aggregate amount 
equivalent to $895,000, may be procured under contracts 
awarded on the basis of national shopping procedures in 
Page  2
accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 3.5 and 3.6 
of the Guidelines.” 
 
Please confirm your agreement to said amendment by signing and dating the two 
originals of this letter in the space provided below, and returning one to us.  This 
amendment shall enter into effect as of its date first above written, upon receipt by the Bank 
of one original of this letter signed by you.
 
Sincerely yours,
