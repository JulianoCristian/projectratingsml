Reallocation of Loan Proceeds and Third Amendment of the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Nemli, 
 
We refer to Loan Agreement between the Republic of Turkey (the Borrower) and the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) for the Social Risk Mitigation 
Project/Loan (the Loan Agreement), dated September 14, 2001, as amended.  We also refer to the 
September 29, 2003 letter to the Bank, signed by Ms. Seven Yucel, Acting Deputy Director 
General, the Undersecretariat of Treasury, requesting the Bank to reallocate the proceeds of the 
Loan and amend the Loan Agreement as described below.  We are pleased to inform you that the 
Bank has agreed to the Borrower’s request, and the proceeds of the Loan are reallocated and the 
Loan Agreement is amended as follows: 
 
1. 
The proceeds of the Loan as set forth in the table in paragraph 1 of Part A of 
Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement are hereby reallocated as follows: 
 
-
$2,300,000 from Category (1) (a) (Phase 1 of the Education Grants and 
Health/Nutrition Grants under Part B of the Project) to Category (5) (Incremental 
operating costs) 
 
-
$4,400,000 from Category (9) (Unallocated) to Category (4) (Consultants’ 
services, including training)   
 
The revised table in paragraph 1 of Part A of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement 
reflecting the above reallocation is attached to this letter. 
 
2. 
In paragraph 4 of Part B of the Annex to Schedule 5 to the Loan Agreement, the 
amount of $25,000 is replaced with the amount of $60,000, and the amount of $1,000 is replaced 
with the amount of $2,000.
