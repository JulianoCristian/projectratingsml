                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project development objective is to improve - through decentralization at the regional level - the prioritization, efficiency and effectiveness of regional transport
                         interventions and, hence, their contribution to regional development and poverty reduction inPeru.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Lately, most efforts have been concentrated in advancing ongoing works contracts to completion; advancing procurement processes for the contracting of consulting services to
                         support institutional strengthening; and final accounting to finalize payments for eligible expenses. The project closes on June 30th, 2014.

                         To date the following results have been achieved: (i)1,490 km of roads have been rehabilitated (84 percent of target); (ii) 3,334 km of roads have received periodic maintenance (150
