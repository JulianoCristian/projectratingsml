                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project objective is to increase production and market access of rural families living in the Chambo and Chanchan-Chimbo river basins within Chimborazo Province,
                         through investments in irrigation and roads improvement.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This ISR reports on the findings of a supervision mission performed in January and February 2014. Actual disbursements reach 83%, and committed funds bring disbursements up to
                         97%. Project will close end of June 2014 achieving objectives and using all available funds.
                         Subcomponent 1.1 Irrigation Improvements shows notable achievements, including the completion of the modernization of 46 irrigation systems (of a total of 54 systems). The Task
                         Team has confirmed on field the high level of objectives completion. During field visits, beneficiaries have expressed their satisfaction and requested extension of training services
                         performed by the project (operation and maintenance, irrigation and agro-ecological production, etc.).
