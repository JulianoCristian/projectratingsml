                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation progressed well during the 2014 construction season between April and June, but as expected slowed down during the monsoon season. The works at about
                         half the number of project schemes has now been completed, while the works at remaining schemes are at various level of completion. The project continues to support Madhya
