                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Bank has restructured the project and extended the Closing Date for the Emergency Power Sector Reform Capacity Reinforcement Project from December 31, 2010 to
                         December 31, 2012. At the time of this extension, the Project was rated by the Bank as Unsatisfactory in meeting its project development objectives (PDO), and an extension was
                         granted to allow for improved performance of the Project by December 31, 2012.

                         The Government now intends to allocate remaining funds in at least two of the following areas: a) support for the development of a roadmap for corporatization of EdL; b) further
                         study of Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) as a potential means to reform Lebanon's fuel supply and reduce the average cost of electricity; and, c) hire of individual specialists to support
