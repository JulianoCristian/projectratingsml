Excellency:
Credit No. 2731 BUR (Second Health and Population Project) 
Amendment to the 
Development Credit Agreement
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the “Agreement”) dated June 
12, 1995, between the Republic of Burundi (the Borrower) and the International 
Development Association (the Association) for the above referenced Credit and to your 
letters to the Association, dated November 19, 1997 and January 14, 1998, requesting 
the Association to increase certain disbursement percentages for local expenditures.
We are pleased to confirm, therefore, our consent to amend the table in Schedule 
1 to the Agreement as set forth in the attachment to this letter (with the changes 
indicated in bold).
The provisions of this amendment letter will become effective as of the date the 
Association receives one fully executed original of this amendment letter. 
Sincerely,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
