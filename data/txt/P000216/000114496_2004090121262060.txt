Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the “Agreement”) dated June 
12, 1995, as amended, between the Republic of Burundi (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association (the Association) for the above referenced 
Credit and to your recent letter dated April 22, 1999 to the Association, requesting 
the Association to increase the disbursement percentages for local expenditures 
related to incremental operating expenditures.
We are pleased to confirm, therefore, our consent to amend the table in Schedule 
1 to the Agreement as set forth in the attachment to this letter (with the change 
indicated in bold).
The provisions of this amendment letter will become effective as of the date the 
Association receives one fully executed original of this amendment letter.
