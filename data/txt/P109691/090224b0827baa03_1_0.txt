                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This report summarizes the results of the July 2014 mission and progress made thereafter.

                         During the July 2014 mission, the team reviewed the results and pace of implementation of the project. The team found that the project continues to produce positive results. The
                         team spent much of the mission discussing plans to ensure implementation and disbursement are complete prior to the project's close in December 2014. The team worked with the


                                                                                                                                                                                                          Page 1 of 9

                         counterparts to produce implementation and disbursement plans which were included as annexes to the aide memoire. The PIU also shared a timeline specifically for the commercial
                         code work, which is the largest activity pending completion. Approximately 88% of the loan has been disbursed and approximately US$1.4 million remains to be disbursed. It is
                         expected that these funds will be disbursed prior to the project's close.
