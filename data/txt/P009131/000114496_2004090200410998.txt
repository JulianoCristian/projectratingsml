Re:   Financial Institution Building Project (Loan No. 4479 UZ)
 
Amendment to the Loan Agreement                                             
Dear Mr. Minister: 
 
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Financial Institution Building Project) dated June 23, 
1999, between the Republic of Uzbekistan (the Borrower) and the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank). We also refer to the letter from Mr. Mirkamilov, 
Director, Financial Sector Development Agency, dated May 27, 2003, requesting that the audit 
costs for the Project be financed under the Consultants’ Services Category. 
 
We hereby confirm the Bank’s agreement to the amendment set forth below. 
 
1. 
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is revised as per Annex to 
this letter. 
 
2. 
In paragraph 2(c) of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement, the word “audit” is deleted. 
 
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by countersigning, dating 
and returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter. This amendment will become effective as of 
the date of countersignature upon receipt by the Bank of the countersigned copy of this letter. 
 
Sincerely,
