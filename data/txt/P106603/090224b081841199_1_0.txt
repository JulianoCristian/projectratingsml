                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project implementation is progressing smoothly and the Project Development Objective is likely to be achieved. All procurement activities under road asset management
                         component are fully completed and the road maintenance program which is the main activity of the project is expected to be completed on time.

                         Regarding the other components, the Asset Management Technical Capacity Development has been facing technical problems and the Road Data Collection Management Unit
