                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the project is to ensure continued effective use of the rehabilitated national and provincial road network in support of the economic development in
                         Cambodia. It will do so by improving the institutional and technical capacity of the Ministry of Public Works and Transport for road maintenance planning, budgeting
                         and operations, and by expanding and strengthening maintenance activities.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project was restructured on September 23, 2013 to re-allocate the remaining Priority Operation Cost (POC) to category 1 (civil works). Thus additional 10 km of rehabilitation
                         program and 6 km of flood emergency works will begin in December 2013 implemented byMarch 2014. Based upon the information gained during the site visits and the missionâ€™s
                         assessments, the project is being implemented smoothly. The key achievements include: (i) 460 km of national and provincial roads rehabilitated; (ii) 16 km including 6km of flood
                         emergency are under rehabilitation and will be completed by March 2014; (iii) the Road Data Collection Management Unit (RDCMU) is in full operation despite staff turnover, which is
