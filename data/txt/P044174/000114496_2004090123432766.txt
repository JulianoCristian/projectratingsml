Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to your request to amend the above captioned Development Credit 
Agreement dated March 27, 1997, between the Republic of Djibouti and the International 
Development Association (the Association), with a view to include the expenses related 
to the audit of the Project’s accounts in the operating expenses of the Project.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association, in order to facilitate the 
Project implementation, has acceded to the above request. Accordingly, the said 
Development Credit Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
Under Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement (Withdrawal of the Proceeds 
of the Credit), sub-paragraph 2 (c) shall be respectively modified to read as follows:
1.
Sub-paragraph 2 (c) of Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement:
“(c)
the term "operating costs" means the incremental operating costs 
incurred by the Borrower under the Project on account of: (i) travel costs and 
allowances, (ii) operation and maintenance of vehicles and equipment, (iii) 
office rent, furniture, supplies and utilities; (iv) salaries of local 
contractual staff; and (v) audit.”
All the terms and conditions of the Development Credit Agreement, as amended 
hereby, shall remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm your official agreement to the foregoing, by duly signing and 
dating the corresponding form of confirmation below and returning this amendment 
letter to the Association at your earliest convenience. A signed duplicate original of 
this amendment letter is enclosed herewith for the Republic of Djibouti’s official 
records.
Very truly yours,
