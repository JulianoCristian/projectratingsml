                         Implementation Status Overview
                         GOE has built 8,005 Kindergarten (KG) classrooms nationwide since the beginning of the project of which 3,987 classrooms (vs. 994 targeted) in the 165 project districts. This is in
                         addition to 405 classrooms completed to date under the Bank loan under the public KG classroom component out of 750 targeted and 414 Non-Government Organizations (NGO)
                         classrooms completed under the NGO component out of 500 targeted under the loan.

                         Standard-based curriculum has been generalized in all KGs both governmental and Project-financed NGO KGs. MOE has trained 15000 teachers and supervisors in addition to NGO
                         facilitators during summer of 2011 (July to September 2011). MOE covered the cost of training (L.E. 10 million). This school year 2011/12, MOE will train 8000 additional teachers,
                         facilitators and supervisors at a cost of L.E. 5 million from its own budget.
Public Disclosure Copy




                         Learning packages for 3600 KG classrooms have been purchased and distributed to the different KG classrooms of which 900 learning packages have been purchased under the
                         Bank Loan out of 2000 targeted under the loan. A new tender for supplying more packages has been prepared and is expected to be issued early CY 2012.

                         An agreement has been reached with the Canadian International Development Agency (CIDA), World Food Program (WFP), Ministry of Education (MOE) and Ministry of Planning
                         and International Cooperation (MOPIC) to finance the Evaluation/Impact Assessment of the Project. Data collection has already started in December 2011. PEMA of MOIC is the unit
                         in charge of the evaluation.
