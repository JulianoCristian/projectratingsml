                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The overall project objective is to assist the Borrower in improving the livelihood and increasing the incomne of people in the West Delta regions through: (i)
                         mitigating further environmental degradation caused by excessive drawndown of the groundwater resources; and (ii) establishing a framework for financial
                         sustainability of irrigation infrastructure in the use of water resources.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         One bid was received on 18 April 2011 for the Design, Build and Operate contract (the only contract under the project) . Two of the four earlier pre-qualified consortia withdrew from
                         the bidding process following the January 2011 revolution. The project is rated unsatisfactory because of the continued lack of progress. The project is due to close on 30 June 2011.
