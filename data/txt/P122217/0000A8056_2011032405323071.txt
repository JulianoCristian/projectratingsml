                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is designed to provide urgent assistance to REGIDESO, the state-owned water and power utility, in order to recover the capacity and reliability of electricity services in
                         Bujumbura. The project was approved by the World Bank's Board of Directors in September 2010 and was declared effective in February 2011.
