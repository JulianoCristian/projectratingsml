                         Implementation Status Overview
                         All contracted activities have been concluded or are on track for conclusion by the closing date of the loan on Dec 31, 2010. Disbursements are expected to reach 90% by the closing
                         date. Two small activities initially planned with an estimated 10% of the loan amount will not be implemented due to time limitation to complete the procurement process and the
                         outputs and/or a change in priorities.

                         Activities under Component 1 in support of policymaking and capacity strengthening at the National Housing Secretariat have all been implemented. Under Component 2, distance
