                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project's development objective is to: (i) improve the delivery of the Basic Package of Health Services (BPHS) in four states(Upper Nile, Jongeli, Central
                         Equatoria, and Eastern Equatoria); and (ii) strengthen key stewardship functions of the MOH,including monitoring.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The implementation of the project is progressing well; there is continuous joint supervision of the project which involves the MOH, the project team, the world bank task team, the
                         representatives of state ministry of health and Implementing lead agencies. We are expecting that the planned activities under the project will be completed by the closure of the
