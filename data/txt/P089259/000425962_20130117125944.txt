7a. Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
The three-pronged Project Development Objective contributes to the higher development objectives of
reducing poverty in rural areas and improving natural resources management. The project would enable
poor rural producers in rainfed areas to: (a) improve their production, processing and marketing systems;
(b) protect their assets: soil, water, rangeland, seeds and animals; and (c) get organized for the purpose of
(a) and (b) above.
