Re:
Loan 3841-PAN (Rural Health Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Minister:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the Republic of Panama (the Borrower) 
dated July 20, 1995, (the Loan Agreement). Pursuant to your request, we are pleased to 
confirm that the Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
A. Loan 
Agreement
1.
Section 1.02 is hereby amended by inserting the following new subparagraphs:
"(ae)
"JASPC" means a Junta Administradora de Subprojectos de Produccion 
Comunitaria, a community organization legally constituted in accordance with 
the rules and procedures of the Community Subproject Manual, and which 
community organization meets the criteria set forth in the Community 
Subproject Manual;
(af)
"Community Subproject" means an agricultural investment project 
designed to reduce the incidence of malnutrition among children aged less than 
5 years, breast-feeding and pregnant women and which investment project meets 
the 
criteria set forth in the Community Subproject Manual;
(ag)
"Community Subproject Agreement" means each of the agreements 
referred to in Section 3.02 (c) of this Agreement;
(ah)
"Community Subproject Manual" means the manual, approved by 
MDS and accepted by the Bank, setting forth the criteria, rules and procedures 
for the implementation of Part A.4 of the Project, as such manual may be 
amended from time to time by agreement between the Borrower and the Bank"; 
2.
Section 3.01 (b) is hereby amended by deleting "and" before "(iii) and deleting 
the last period and substituting therefor "; and (iv) the Community Subproject Manual.
3.
Section 3.02 is hereby amended  as follows:
(a)
Insert a new subparagraph (c) to read as follows:
"(c) The Borrower shall enter into agreements with JASPCs, containing 
terms and conditions satisfactory to the Bank, to make available the neccesary 
funds to implement the respective Community Subprojects; such agreements to 
include, inter alia,  provisions relating to the procument of goods under the 
respective Community Subprojects and the accounting and auditing of Project 
expenditures."
(b)
Delete subparagraph (c) and substitute therefor the following: 
"(d)
The Borrower shall exercise its rights under the FES Agreement, 
JAA Agreements and Community Subproject Agreements in such a manner as to 
protect the interests of the Borrower and the Bank, comply with its 
obligations under this Agreement and achieve the purposes of the Project. 
Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree, the Borrower shall not assign, 
amend, abrogate, waive or fail to enforce the FES Agreement, JAA Agreements or 
the 
Community Subproject Agreements or any provision thereof."
4.
Section 3.16 is hereby amended by deleting "June 30, 1996" and substituting

study  on the efficiency and impact of Community Subprojects".
6.
Section 3.21 (c) is hereby amended by deleting "August 15, 1997" and 
substituting therefor " July 31, 1998".
7.

Attachment 1 hereto.

MDS; and (ii) the respective Community Subproject Agreement has been duly executed." 
9.
Schedule 2 is hereby amended as follows:
(a)

"4.
Execution of about three hundred (300) Community Farm

Schedule 4 is amended as follows:
(a)
Subparagraph 2 (c) of Part C of Section 1 is hereby amended to read as 
follows:
(c)
(i) goods, other than Food Supplements, medical equipment and 
supplies and agricultural materials and supplies under Community 
Subprojects, 
estimated to cost the equivalent of less than $150,000 but more than $25,000 
per contract, up to an aggregate amount equivalent to $400,000, in the case of 
goods 
required for civil works to be carried out by force account, and 
$1,000,000 in the case of other goods; (ii) agricultural materials and 
supplies under Community Subprojects, estimated to cost the equivalent of less 
than 
$150,000 but more than $10,000 per contract.
(b)
Subparagraph 3 (d) of Part C.3 is hereby amended by inserting "and 
agricultural materials and supplies under Community Subprojects" after "supplies" in 
the first line.
(c)
A new subparagraph (e) is inserted in Part C.3 of Section 1 to read as 
follows:
"(e) Agricultural materials and supplies ùnder Community Subprojects 
estimated to cost the equivalent of $10,000 or less per contract, up to an 
aggregate amount equivalent to $300,000"
(d)
A new paragraph 6 is inserted in Part C of Section 1 to read as follows:
"6. Agricultural supplies under Community Subprojects estimated to cost 
the equivalent of $5,000 or less per contract, up to an aggregate amount 
equivalent to $200,000, may be procured under contracts awarded after direct 
negotiations with suppliers, in accordance with procedures acceptable to the 
Bank."
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below. This amendment letter is executed in three 
counterparts, each of which shall be an original. Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully-executed original to us. The provisions of this amendment letter will 
become effective on the date of this amendment letter, upon receipt by the Bank of one 
fully-executed original of this amendment letter.
