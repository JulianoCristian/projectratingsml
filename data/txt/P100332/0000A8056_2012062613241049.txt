                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project achievements to-date includes:

                         1. Overall progress of the road access component is satisfactory. All physical works are now completed (May 2012) well before the project completion date (early-Oct, 2012). RAP-II
                         has the following approximate targets: 65km new road construction and 24km road upgrading. The achievements are: 66.93km of new roads constructed and 23.34km of roads
                         upgraded. The project also constructed 15.2km of Otta-seal roads.

                         2. All planned activities under the human resources development and training sub-component are completed.
