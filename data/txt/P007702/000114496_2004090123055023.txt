Re:    Loan 3790-ME (Second Decentralization and Regional Development 
Project) 
Amendment to the Loan Agreement, Side Letter and 
Guarantee Agreement 
Dear Sirs: 
We refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank) and Nacional Financiera, S.N.C. (the Borrower) dated October 2, 
1994, as well as to the Guarantee Agreement and Side Letter (re:  Format of 
Semi-Annual Reports; Staffing of PCU and State PCUs) of even date therewith between 
the Bank and the United Mexican States (the Guarantor), all pertaining to the 
above-captioned Project.  We are pleased to inform you of our agreement to amend said 
Loan and Guarantee Agreements and Side Letter as follows: 
Loan Agreement
1.
Section 1.02 (g) is hereby amended by replacing the words “paragraphs 8 (a), 8 
(b), 10 (e) or 15 (c)” with the words “paragraphs 8 (a) or 8 (b)”.
2.
Section 1.02 (j) is hereby deliberately left blank.
3.
Section 1.02 (k) is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety:  
““Municipal Funds Program” means SEDESOL’s program for small municipal investments, 
defined in the Project Operational Manual as “Fondos de Desarrollo Social Municipal” 
(which includes, until December 31, 1997, SEDESOL’s program for school rehabilitation 
and maintenance called Escuela Digna);”.
4.
Sections 1.02 (q), (w) and (x) are hereby deliberately left blank.
5.
Section 1.02 (s) is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety:  ““Social 
Development Agreement” means the agreement (Convenio de Desarrollo Social) between the 
Guarantor, through SEDESOL, and each Project State, and/or any other written 
instrument, whereby each Project State commits to participate in the Project under 
terms and conditions which include compliance with the Project Operational Manual and 
timely provision of Project State counterpart funds for the Project;”.
6.
Section 5.01 is hereby amended by replacing the word “obligations” with the word 
"commitments”.
7.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended to read as set forth in 
Attachment A hereto.
8.
Paragraph 2 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended by replacing the words “PCU, the 
State PCUs and the MSU” with the words “PCU and the State PCUs”.
9.
Paragraph 3 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended by deleting the term “(2) (e),”.
10.
Parts A.2 and A.3 of Schedule 2 are hereby deliberately left blank.
11.
Paragraph 1(a) of Schedule 4 is hereby amended by replacing the words “through 
(4)” with the words “, (3) and (4)”.

Guarantee Agreement
12.
Section 3.01 (a) is hereby amended by inserting the following words immediately 
prior to the period at the end thereof:  “(or shall cause Project states to carry out 
their respective portions of Part A of the Project with said diligence and efficiency 
and in conformity with said standards and practices and with the terms of their Social 
Development Agreements)”.
13.
Section 3.01 (b) is hereby amended by inserting the following words immediately 
after “the Project”:  “, or cause the Project to be carried out,”.
14.
Section 3.04 (a) is hereby amended by replacing the words “Parts A.1 (b), A.2 
(b) and A.3 (c) of the Project to the Bank by January 31” with the words “Part A.1 (b) 
of the Project to the Bank by April 30”.
15.
Section 3.04 (c) is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety:  “grant 
the Bank access to the information available from the Monitoring System’s central data 
base at any time the Bank so requests, and provide the Bank with a copy of each Social 
Development Agreement promptly after each time such Social Development Agreement is 
formalized or amended;”.
16.
Section 3.04 (d) is hereby amended by replacing the date “July 31” with the date 
“September 30” and by replacing the date “January 31” with the date “March 31”.
17.
Section 3.04 (g) is hereby amended by replacing the dates “December 31, 1996”, 
“December 31, 1997” and “December 31, 1998” with the dates “June 30, 1997”, “June 30, 
1998” and “June 30, 1999”, respectively.
18.
Paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended by replacing the words “four program 
units” with the words “two program units” and by replacing the words “Parts A.1, A.2, 
A.3 and A.4 of the Project” with the words “Parts A.1 and A.4 of the Project”.
19.
Paragraph 3 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended by inserting the words “(or cause 
Project States to carry out)” immediately after the words “shall carry out”.
20.
Paragraph 4 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety:  
“In order to formalize the participation of the Project States in the execution of the 
Project on the basis of the Project Operational Manual, the Guarantor, through 
SEDESOL, shall cause each Project State to issue or enter into, and to comply with the 
terms of, a Social Development Agreement.”.
21.
Paragraphs 9 through 15 of Schedule 1 are hereby deliberately left blank.
22.
Section I, Part B.1 of Schedule 2 is hereby amended by replacing the figure 
“$666,000,000” with the figure “$820,000,000”, as well as by replacing the last 
sentence thereof with the words "No works under Part A.1 of the Project, costing more 
than $50,000 equivalent (excluding the value of community contributions), or costing 
more than $100,000 equivalent in the case of potable water works (also excluding the 
value of community contributions), shall be procured.  However, notwithstanding the 
above terms of this Part B.1, potable water works costing $100,000 equivalent or less 
may also be procured under contracts awarded on the basis of comparison of price 
quotations obtained from at least three suppliers eligible under the Guidelines, in 
accordance with procedures acceptable to the Bank.”.
23.
Section I, Part B.2 of Schedule 2 is hereby deliberately left blank.
24.
The first sentence of Section I, Part D.1 (a) of Schedule 2 is hereby amended to 
read as follows in its entirety:  “With respect to the first two contracts for goods 
or construction materials and the first two contracts for works in each of the Project 
States procured in accordance with the procedures set forth in Part A.1 of this 
Schedule, the procedures set forth in paragraphs 2 and 4 of Appendix 1 to the 
Guidelines shall apply.”.\0cSide Letter
