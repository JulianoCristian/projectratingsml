                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project activities have been implemented and credit is fully disbursed. A review of project development objectives also shows that project indicators have been met. The component
                         aimed at increasing access to electricity services in urban, peri-urban and rural areas has recorded significant progress with access to electricity to 107,178 households for SONABEL
                         (target achieved at 125%) and 19,600 for FDE (target achieved at 104%). For the wood fuels supply management and inter-fuel substitution initiative component (US$6.7 million),
                         progress is recorded with the management of 547,802 hectares under community-based wood energy management (target achieved at 124%). The subcomponent aimed at
