Re: Loan No. 4313-AR (Third Secondary Education Project)
Second Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank) and the Argentine Republic (the Borrower) dated November 16, 1998 
(the Loan Agreement), as amended, with regard to the above-captioned Loan.  
With reference to your request dated February 15, 2002 and our discussions during the 
past months in respect of changes required to adjust the Loan Agreement to the circumstances 
now prevailing, we are pleased to propose the following amendments to the Loan Agreement: 
1.
The table contained in Schedule 1, to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended and 
replaced in its entirely to read as per attachment I to this amendment letter. 
2.
Section I, Part C.2 of Schedule 4 of the loan Agreement is hereby amended to read in its 
entirely as follows:
“Shopping
Goods estimated to cost less than $100,000 equivalent per contract, up to an aggregate 
amount not to exceed $15,500,000 equivalent, may be procured under contracts awarded on the 
basis of shopping procedures acceptable to the Bank in accordance with the provisions of 
paragraphs 3.5 and 3.6 of the Guidelines.”
Please confirm your agreement of the foregoing by signing and dating this letter in the 
space provided below. This amendment letter may be executed in two counterparts, each of 
which shall be an original. Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed original to 
us. The provisions of this amendment Letter will become effective as of the date first above 
written, once the Bank has received one executed original of this amendment letter.
