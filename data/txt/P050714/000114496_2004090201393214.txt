Re: Loan No. 4313-AR (Third Secondary Education Project)
Third Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank) and the Province of Buenos Aires (the Borrower) dated November 
16, 1998 (the Loan Agreement), with regard to the above-captioned Loan.  
With reference to your request dated May 30, 2002 and our discussions during the past 
month in respect of changes required to adjust the Loan Agreement to the circumstances now 
prevailing and the lessons learned in the carrying out of the Project, we are pleased to propose the 
following amendments to the Loan Agreement: 
1.
Section 2.03 is hereby amended in its entirely to read as follows:
“The Closing Date shall be December 31, 2002 or such later date as the Bank shall 
establish. The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower and the Guarantor of such later date.”
2.
The table contained in Schedule 1, to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended and 
replaced in its entirely to read as per attachment I to this amendment letter. 
Please confirm your agreement of the foregoing by signing and dating this letter in the 
space provided below. This amendment letter may be executed in two counterparts, each of 
which shall be an original. Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed original to 
us. The provisions of this amendment Letter will become effective as of the date first above 
written, once the Bank has received one executed original of this amendment letter.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION
