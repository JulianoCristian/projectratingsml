Dear Sir: 
 
We refer to the Project Agreement (Social Investment Fund Project) dated January 21, 
1998, between the International Development Association (the Association) and Georgian Social 
Investment Fund, as amended by the letter to you from Nicolas Mathieu, dated June 5, 2000 and 
countersigned by you on June 8, 2000.  We also refer to: (i) the request by the Government of 
Georgia, the Borrower under the above-referenced Credit, that the Association provide additional 
assistance towards the financing of the Project, specifically on account of additional Project 
activities in connection with the rehabilitation of educational facilities damaged by the April 25, 
2002 earthquake in Tbilisi; (ii) the Agreement Amending Development Credit Agreement of even 
date herewith between the Borrower and the Association; and (iii) the need to introduce certain 
modifications to the procurement plan with respect to selection of consultants under the Project, 
as so amended. 
 
We hereby confirm the Association’s agreement to the following amendment to the 
Project Agreement: 
An additional paragraph 4 is added to Part C of Section II of Schedule 1 to the Project 
Agreement (Employment of Consultants) as follows: 
“4. 
Selection Based on Consultants’ Qualifications.  
Services for design and supervision under Part A of the Project estimated to cost less than 
$100,000 equivalent per contract, up to an aggregate amount not to exceed the equivalent 
of $250,000, may be procured under contracts awarded in accordance with the provisions 
of paragraphs 3.1 and 3.7 of the Consultant Guidelines.”
