ARTICLE II
The Loan
Section
2.01. The Bank agrees to lend to the Borrower, on the
terms and conditions set forth or referred to in the Loan Agreement,
various currencies that shall have an aggregate value equivalent to
the amount of sixty two million five hundred thousand dollars
($62,500,000), being the sum of withdrawals of the proceeds of the
Loan, with each withdrawal valued by the Bank as of the date of such
withdrawal.
 
Section
2.02. (a) The amount of the Loan may be withdrawn from
the Loan Account in accordance with the provisions of Schedule
1
to
this Agreement for expenditures made
(or, if the Bank shall so
agree, to be made) in respect of the reasonable cost of goods and
services required for the Project described in Schedule
2
to this
Agreement and to be financed out of the proceeds of the Loan.
 
(b)
The Borrower shall, for the purposes of the Project,
open and maintain in dollars a special deposit account in a bank
acceptable to the Bank on terms and conditions satisfactory to the
Page  4
Bank, including appropriate protection against set
-
off, seizure or
attachment. Deposits into, and payments out of, the Special Account
shall be made in accordance with the provisions of Schedule
6
to
this Agreement.
Section
2.03. The Closing Date shall be December 31, 1997 or
such later date as the Bank shall establish. The Bank shall promptly
notify the Borrower and the Guarantor of such later date.
 
Section
2.04. The Borrower shall pay to the Bank a commitment
charge at the rate of three-fourths of one percent (3/4 of 1%) per
annum on the principal amount of the Loan not withdrawn from time to
time.
 
Section
2.05. (a) The Borrower shall pay interest on the
principal amount of the Loan withdrawn and outstanding from time to
time, at a rate for each Interest Period equal to the Cost of
Qualified Borrowings determined in respect of the preceding
Semester, plus one-half of one percent (1/2 of 1%). On each of the
dates specified in Section
2.06 of this Agreement, the Borrower
shall pay interest accrued on the principal amount outstanding
during the preceding Interest Period, calculated at the rate
applicable during such Interest Period.
(b)
As soon as practicable after the end of each Semester,
the Bank shall notify the Borrower and the Guarantor of the Cost of
Qualified Borrowings determined in respect of such Semester.
 
(c)
For the purposes of this Section:
 
(i)   "Interest Period" 
means a six
-
month period ending
on the date immediately preceding each date
specified in Section
2.06 of this Agreement,
beginning with the Interest Period in which this
Agreement is signed.
 
(ii)   "Cost of Qualified Borrowings" 
means the cost, as
reasonably determined by the Bank and expressed
as a percentage per annum, of the outstanding
borrowings of the Bank drawn down after June
30,

the Bank's investments; and
(B) loans which may
be made by the Bank after July
1,
1989
bearing
interest rates determined otherwise than as
provided in paragraph
(a) of this Section.
 
(iii)   "Semester" 
means the first six months or the
second six months of a calendar year.
 
(d)
On such date as the Bank may specify by no less than six
months' notice to the Borrower, paragraphs
(a), (b) and (c) (iii) of
this Section shall be amended to read as follows:
 
"(a)
The Borrower shall pay interest on the principal
amount of the Loan withdrawn and outstanding from time to
time, at a rate for each Quarter equal to the Cost of
Qualified Borrowings determined in respect of the preceding
Quarter, plus one-half of one percent (1/2 of 1%).
On each of
the dates specified in Section
2.06
of this Agreement, the
Borrower shall pay interest accrued on the principal amount
outstanding during the preceding Interest Period, calculated
at the rates applicable during such Interest Period."
 
"(b)
As soon as practicable after the end of each

ARTICLE III
Execution of the Project
Section
3.01. (a) The Borrower declares its commitment to the
objectives of the Project as set forth in Schedule
2
to this
Agreement, and, to this end, shall carry out the Project with due
diligence and efficiency and in conformity with appropriate
administrative, financial, engineering, environmental and public
utility practices, and shall provide, promptly as needed, the funds,
facilities, services and other resources required for the Project.
 
(b)
In furtherance of the provisions of the preceding
paragraph and without limitation thereto, the Borrower shall ensure
at all times during Project execution: (i) the adequate operation,
under terms of reference satisfactory to the Bank, of a Project unit
vested with such powers as shall be convenient to enable it to take
all daily decisions related to the carrying out of the Project and
provided with adequate resources for the purposes;
(ii)
that the
coordination within the Borrower of all activities related to
Project execution is in charge of a Project Coordinator, with the
help of three Project Directors who will be responsible for
activities under each Part of the Project, all of them qualified and
experienced professionals;
(iii) that the said professionals are
assisted by the Project Unit and by the procurement, construction,
finance and engineering units of the Borrower, as required; and
(iv)
that neither the Project Coordinator nor any of the Project
Directors are replaced without affording the Bank a reasonable
opportunity to comment on the qualifications and experience of each
candidate to occupy the position in question.
 
Section
3.02. Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree,
procurement of the goods, works and consultants' services required
for the Project shall be governed by the provisions of Schedule
4
to
this Agreement.
Section
3.03. The Borrower shall carry out Part C of the
Project in accordance with objectives, tasks, investments and
technical assistance plans and terms of reference satisfactory to
the Bank.
ARTICLE IV
Management and Operations of the Borrower
Section
4.01. The Borrower shall carry on its operations and
conduct its affairs in accordance with the provisions of Schedule
5
to this Agreement and sound administrative, financial, engineer-ing,
environmental and public utility practices under the super- vision
of qualified and experienced management assisted by competent staff
in adequate numbers.
 
Section
4.02. The Borrower shall at all times operate and
maintain its plants, machinery, equipment and other property, and
from time to time, promptly as needed, make all necessary repairs
Page  6
and renewals thereof, all in accordance with sound engineering,
financial and public utility practices.
 
Section
4.03. The Borrower shall take out and maintain with
responsible insurers, or make other provision satisfactory to the
Bank for, insurance against such risks and in such amounts as shall
be consistent with appropriate practice.
 
Section
4.04. The Borrower shall at all times maintain its
existence and right to carry on its operations and shall, except as
the Bank shall otherwise agree, take all necessary steps that are
required to acquire, maintain and renew all rights, powers,
privileges and franchises which are necessary or useful in the
conduct of its business.
 
Section
4.05. The Borrower shall take all such action as shall
be convenient or necessary to increase the productivity of its staff
including abstaining to fill-in, except under exceptional circum-
stances which have been established to the satisfaction of the Bank,
at least
50% of the vacancies (due to resignation by, or retirement,
or death, or any other causes of the termination of the employment
of, the employee or worker in question) produced in staff positions
during each year or portion thereof, such abstentions to fill-in
vacancies to apply in respect of each year from
1990
through
1996,
inclusive.
Section
4.06. The Borrower undertakes: (i) to continue the
execution of the feasibility study it has initiated about August
15,
1989, in respect of the use of firewood in future power plants,
including an assessment of the environmental impact of such use,
under terms of reference reviewed and agreed with the Bank; and
(ii)
not to include any firewood thermal plant in its investment program
unless: (A) the aforesaid feasibility study warrants the construc-
tion of said plant in accordance with a least-cost expansion
program, satisfactory to the Guarantor and the Bank, and
(B) the
Guarantor, the Bank and the Borrower are in agreement with the
environmental protection measures to be taken in respect of the
operation of said plant before and during said operation.
 
Section
4.07. The Borrower shall, without limitation or
restriction to the provisions of Sections
3.01
and
4.01
of this
Agreement: (i)
not later than October
31,
1990, establish an
environmental unit that shall serve as a nucleus for the creation of
an adequate capability within the Borrower's organization aimed at
dealing with environmental matters related to the Borrower's
operations and to liaise with the Guarantor's environmental agencies
to help the Guarantor's implementation of its environmental policies
within the power sector; and
(ii)
promptly thereafter, furnish to
the Bank for comments the work program for the unit during the five-
year period ending December
31, 1995.

SCHEDULE
2
Description of the Project
The objectives of the Project are to improve: (a) the
Borrower's ability of meeting short-to-
medium
-term electricity
demand caused by droughts by installing new thermal generating
units; and
(b) the Guarantor's power sector efficiency by enhancing
the Borrower's managerial capability and reducing Power Losses.
The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such
modifications thereof as the Bank and the Borrower may agree upon
from time to time to achieve such objectives:
 
Part A:
Back-up Thermal Power Plant
 
Installation of a new thermal plant, including acquisition and
installation of two gas turbines of
113.5
Megawatts each,
construction and equipping of a control room, to be installed at the
same site as sub-station L in Montevideo, and of an associated step-
up sub-station and transmission line to interconnect said sub-
station to the existing transmission system, acquisition and
installation of fuel tanks and fuel pipe line to connect the tanks
to the plant, acquisition and installation of all additional
ancillary equipment, including the necessary spare parts for a five-
year period of plant operation, and ancillary additional civil works
for all of the above.
 
Part B:
Distribution Network Improvements
A program of actions aimed at increasing the reliability of
service to customers and reducing the actual level of Power Losses
by means of the rehabilitation of the medium and low voltage system
of the Montevideo power distribution network, including the
acquisition, installation and utilization of equipment and the
execution of minor civil works.
 
Part C:
Institutional Development Program
A program of institutional development of the Borrower
consisting of:
(1)
Development of adequate procedures, software and
computational systems to improve commercial and operational
practices, increase labor productivity, and improve administrative
systems particularly in the areas of procurement, inventories, and
information.
 
Page  14
(2)
Acquisition, installation, and utilization of new
computer equipment and facilities, including terminals to several
areas in the interior of the country, the related communications
facilities, and other necessary equipment and tools to support the
aforesaid.
(3)
A training program consisting of the training of about
50
of the Borrower's professional staff including, fellowships and
courses abroad, in-house seminars and in-job training in power
utilities abroad, as well as the rehabilitation of the Borrower's
training center with the necessary renewal of equipment and
facilities.
 
*   *   *   *
The Project is expected to be completed by June
30,
1997.
