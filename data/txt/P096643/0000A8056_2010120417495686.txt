                         Implementation Status Overview
                          The project is making progress towards project objectives both in the area of regulatory reform and of developing the quality infrastructure of Albania. Regarding the former, the
                         BERIS is assisting the Government in developing the framework for institutionalizing Regulatory Impact Assessment (RIA), in inspections reform and in establishing a registry of
                         business legislation, with the overall goal of facilitating business entry and operations. In the area of quality infrastructure, the project is assisting in enhancing the ability of the project
