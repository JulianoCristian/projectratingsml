Excellency,
We refer to the above-mentioned Development Credit Agreement between Burkina 
Faso (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association), 
dated February 18, 1992, discussions held between your Ministry and the Association 
during the last supervision mission of this Project and your letter dated February 9, 
1995, requesting a change of the disbursement conditions for the second and third 
tranches.
We are pleased to confirm the Association's approval to this request and, 
therefore, agree to amend the Development Credit Agreement as follows
