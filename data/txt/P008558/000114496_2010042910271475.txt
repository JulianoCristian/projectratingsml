Re:  Republic of Moldova: General Education Project (Loan No. 4151-MD)  
 
Amendment to the Loan Agreement                                                           
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Loan Agreement (General Education Project) dated June 2, 1997, 
between the Republic of Moldova and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), as amended. We also refer to your letter dated July 2, 2002, requesting 
certain amendments to the Loan Agreement. 
 
We hereby confirm the Bank’s agreement to the following amendments: 
 
1. 
The proceeds of the Loan as set out in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan 
Agreement are reallocated as follows: $24,300  from Category (2) to Category (3). Accordingly, 
the amounts now allocated to the different Categories are as shown in the Attachment to this 
letter.    
 
2. 
Paragraph 2 (c) of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is revised to read: 
 
“(c) 
the term “incremental operating costs” means operating costs which would not 
have been incurred absent the Project and include, inter alia, salaries of PMU staff, office 
supplies, communications, travel, postage, bank charges, and such other administrative and 
operating expenses incurred for the purposes of Project management and implementation, as may 
be agreed upon with the Bank.”   
 
3. 
The first part of paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 6 to the Loan Agreement is revised to read 
as follows: 
 
“(c) 
the term “Authorized Allocation” means an amount equivalent to $800,000 to be 
withdrawn from the Loan Account and deposited into the Special Account pursuant to paragraph 
3
(a) of this Schedule ………”.

Please confirm your agreement to this amendment by countersigning, dating and 
returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter. This amendment will become effective as of the 
date of countersignature upon receipt by the Bank of the countersigned copy of this letter.
