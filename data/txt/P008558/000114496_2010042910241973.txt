Re:
General Education Project (Credit No. 3003 MD) Amendment to the 
Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (General Education Project) dated 
November 17, 1997 (the Credit Agreement), between the Republic of Moldova (the 
Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association), as amended. 
We also refer to a  letter from the Director of the Project Management Unit, dated 
July 8, 1999 requesting that the Authorized Allocation for the Special Account be 
increased.
We hereby confirm the Association’s agreement to the following amendment to the 
Credit Agreement: Schedule 5
The first part of paragraph 1(c) of Schedule 5 to the Credit Agreement is 
amended to read as follows:
"(c)
the term "Authorized Allocation" means an amount equivalent to 
$450,000 to be withdrawn from the Credit Account and deposited into the 
Special Account pursuant to paragraph 3(a) of this Schedule, ....".
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing, dating 
and returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter.  This amendment shall become 
effective upon receipt of the countersigned copy of this letter by us.
