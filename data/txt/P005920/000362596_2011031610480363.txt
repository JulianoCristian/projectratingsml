Ozone Projects Trust Fund Grant Agreement (OTF Grant TF22013) 
Amendment to the Grant Agreement
Excellency, 
 
Please refer to the Ozone Projects Trust Fund Grant Agreement (the OTF Grant 
Agreement) between Argentine Republic and International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development acting as trustee of the Ozone Projects Trust Fund (the Bank) dated January 9, 
1997, supporting the Reduction of Ozone Depleting Substances Project (TF022013), as amended 
by letter agreement dated June 9, 2008.  
 
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to your letter of [October 1, 2009] the Bank 
(acting as Trustee of the Ozone Projects Trust Fund), has established December 30, 2014 as the 
later date for purposes of Section 2.03 of the Ozone Project Trust Fund Grant Agreement between 
Argentine Republic and the Bank, dated January 9, 1997. Also, the Bank hereby agrees to amend 
the Grant Agreement as follows: 
 
(i)

thousand Dollars ($28,914,000).” is replaced by the following: “thirty-
one million two hundred eighteen thousand six hundred seventy five
