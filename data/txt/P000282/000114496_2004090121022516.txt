Credit No. 2244 BUR (Fourth Education Project) 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the “Agreement”) dated July 5, 
1991, as amended, between Burkina Faso (the Borrower) and the International 
Development Association (the Association) for the above-referenced Credit and to your 
letter to the Association, dated February 24, 1998, requesting the Association to 
amend the Agreement in order to provide for community participation as one of the 
acceptable procurement procedures.
We are pleased to confirm, therefore, our consent to amend Part C (Other 
Procurement Procedures) of Schedule 4 to the Agreement by adding the following 
language at the end, as follows: 
“3.
Community Participation.  Goods and works required for Part C.3 of 
the Project 
shall be procured in accordance with procedures acceptable to the 
Association.”
If you agree with the foregoing amendments, please sign and date this letter in 
the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter shall be executed in two 
counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us.
The provisions of this amendment letter will become effective as of the date the 
Association receives one fully executed original of this amendment letter.
