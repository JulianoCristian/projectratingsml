Re: Fourth Education Project (Cr. 2244-BUR)
    Belgian Grant No. 20817 (Amendment to the Belgian Grant Agreement)
We refer to the Belgian Grant Agreement (the "Agreement") between Burkina Faso 
and the International Development Association as Administrator  of development  aid 
funds provided by the Royal Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Trade and Development 
Cooperation of the Kingdom of Belgium (the "Association"), dated July 24, 1996, and to 
your December 19, 1996 fax to the Association in which you informed us about your 
acceptance of an increase in the Belgian Grant fund amount.
We are pleased to confirm, therefore, our consent to amend the Agreement as 
follows:
1.
Section 2.01 is deleted and replaced by the following:
"The Administrator agrees to make available to the Grantee, on the terms and 
conditions set forth or referred to in this Agreement, an amount in various currencies 
equivalent to two hundred million Belgian Francs (BF 200,000,000) or such other amount 
as shall have been made available by Belgium to the Administrator for this purpose, 
pursuant to the Agreement."
2.
The table in Schedule 1 to the Agreement is deleted and replaced by the table 
set forth below:
