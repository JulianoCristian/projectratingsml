        FAMILY HEALTH PROJECT (CREDIT 1913-ET)
                AMENDMENT TO SCHEDULE 1
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Family Health
Project) dated July 14, 1988 and Amendment of Schedule I dated
December 17, 1991 between the People's Democratic Republic of
Ethiopia (the Borrower) and the International Development
Association (the Association).  We also refer to your letter of June
3, 1992 requesting a reallocation of proceeds of the above-mentioned
credit.
     We are pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to
your request, and that the Association has accordingly shifted SDR
1.78 million, equivalent to US$2.5 million from Civil Works to
Pharmaceuticals and Medical Supplies.  The attached amendment of
Schedule I reflects this reallocation.
     Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by
signing and returning the enclosed copy of this letter whereupon the
amendment shall constitute a binding agreement between the Borrower
and the Association.
                   Yours sincerely,
                   Francis X. Colaco
                       Director
               Eastern Africa Department
