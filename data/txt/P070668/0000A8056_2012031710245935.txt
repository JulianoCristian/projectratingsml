                         Implementation Status Overview
                         All CESSP activities were successfully completed by the project closing date of September 30, 2011 and the MoEYS is to be congratulated on its achievements, commitment and
                         dedication throughout the six years of project implementation. The project contributed significantly to Cambodia education development at all levels. Implementation of both soft and
                         hard components, including additional construction of schools affected by Typhoon Ketsana, were successfully completed, with some components achieving targets beyond the
                         appraisal estimates. The RUPP library expansion was completed within the extended closing date. This building is open to students from all universities and it is considered to be
