                         Implementation Status Overview
                         CESSP implementation continues to make considerable progress. Given the complexity of civil works activities, the shorter than average implementation period of five years and the
                         numerous quality improvement activities undertaken, the MoEYS is to be congratulated on its achievements, commitment and dedication. Implementation of the school construction
                         as a result of Typhoon Ketsana is moving forward in accordance with agreed timeline. All soft components activities under the project have been successfully completed, with some
