     Re:   Credit No. 2011 SU (Emergency Flood Reconstruction Project)
           Amendments to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Emergency Flood
Reconstruction Project), dated May 25, 1989, between The Republic of
the Sudan and International Development Association (the
Association).
     As discussed between representatives of your Government and
the Association, we propose to amend the Development Credit
Agreement by changing Schedules I and 2 to the Development Credit
Agreement as follows:
     1.   In the table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1, the
Categories related to Part C of the Project shall read as follows:
