                         Implementation Status Overview
                         State Chancellery continued to make good progress in implementing the activities included in CPAR implementation plan. Ongoing project activities continued as scheduled. Good
                         progress was also achieved in preparation of the new activities envisaged to start in 2011. The tender for procurement of the civil service register is continuing as planned.

                         In response to the Government request, the project closing date was extended for 2 years (till December 31st, 2012) to allow for implementation of civil service register and several
                         other activities critically important for the achievement of the Project Development Objective (PDO).

                         Based on the positive results achieved under the project, the Government has formulated the request for scaling up project activities aimed at strengthening Government policy
                         management capacity and has obtained the agreement from SIDA to provide an additional contribution to CPAR MDTF to finance the expanded scope of activities.
