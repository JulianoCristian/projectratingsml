                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The development objective of the MWRD program is to enhance regional integration among the riparian countries of the Senegal River Basin through OMVS for
                         multi-purpose water resources development to foster growth including improved community livelihoods.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The mission visit concluded that the overall implementation progresses satisfactory. This rating is based on the following: (i) confirmation that the Project remains on track towards
                         achieving the development objective;(ii) achievement of the milestones as set for December 2010; and (iii) a current disbursement level of 57%.
