Re: Poverty Alleviation Fund Project: Cr. 3248-PAK
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement and Project Agreement
Dear Mr. Akram:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (DCA) between the Islamic Republic 
of Pakistan (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the 
Association) and the Project Agreement (the Project Agreement) between the Association 
and the Pakistan Poverty Alleviation Fund, both dated July 7 1999, for the 
above-referenced Project.  We also refer to discussions held with the Borrower and to 
the letter dated January 22, 2001, from Mr. Bokhari, Economic Affairs Division (EAD), 
Ministry of Finance & Economic Affairs, requesting the Association to make certain 
changes to Schedule 1 of the DCA, and to the financing limit for micro-credit projects 
under Part A (i) of the Project.
The Association, after due consideration, has decided to accede to your request, 
and hereby amends the DCA and Project Agreement to read as follows:
Development Credit Agreement:
1.
Schedule 1 to the DCA is amended as set forth in Attachment I to this amendment 
letter.
Project Agreement:
2.
Section 2.03(a) of the Project Agreement is amended to read:
"(a) for purposes of financing MCPs under Part A (i) of the Project, no single PO 
shall receive a Sub-loan, which either by itself or together with any other 
outstanding Sub-loans received by such PO will exceed an aggregate amount equal to 
twenty five percent (25%) of the amount allocated from time to time to Category 1 
(Sub-loans) set forth in the table in Part A.1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement."
3.
Part B.2(a) ii of Schedule 2 to the Project Agreement is amended to read:
"Grants shall be made on a non-reimbursable basis, subject to the retention by the PO 
concerned of an amount not exceeding 10% of each Grant made to a Beneficiary for a 
SSIP, to finance the PO's supervision costs;"
Except as specifically amended by this amendment letter, all other provisions of 
the DCA and Project Agreement remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement to these amendments by signing the form of 
confirmation set forth below, and returning to us one fully executed original of this 
amendment letter at your earliest convenience, and retaining one original for each of 
your records.  
This amendment shall become effective as of the date of countersignature of this 
amendment letter.
Sincerely,
