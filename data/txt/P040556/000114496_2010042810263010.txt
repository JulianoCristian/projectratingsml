Dear Mr. Minister: 
 
Re:  
 
Cr 3357 GE (Georgia Roads Project) 
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement  
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Roads Project), dated July 26, 2000, 
between the Republic of Georgia (the Borrower) and the International Development Association 
(the Association) as amended.  We also refer to your letter dated December 18, 2003 and 
communication of January 8, 2004 to the Association requesting a reallocation and an updated 
Schedule 1 consistent with your request.  We are pleased to inform you, that the Association has 
agreed to your request and the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Development Credit 
Agreement is revised to read as set forth in the Attachment to this Letter.     
All the terms and conditions of the Development Credit Agreement, as amended hereby, 
shall remain in full force and effect. 
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing, dating and 
returning to us the attached copy of this letter. This amendment will become effective as of date 
of countersignature upon receipt by the Bank of the countersigned copy of this letter. 
 
Sincerely,
