Amendment to the

Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the Republic of Ecuador (the 
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), 
dated September 25, 1998 for the above-captioned Project (the Loan Agreement).  We are 
pleased to inform you that, as a result of the requests of the Government for 
additional assistance during the current economic crisis affecting the country, sent 
to the Bank on September 13 and 17, 1999, and our recent discussions on the subject, 
the Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is hereby 
replaced to read as shown in the table set forth in the Annex to this letter.
Please confirm your agreement  to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter may be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us. Upon receipt by the Bank of said fully 
executed original, the provisions of this amendment letter will become effective as of 
the date hereof.
Very truly yours,
By /s/ Andres Solimano 
Director 
Country Management Unit 
Colombia, Ecuador and Venezuela 
Latin America & the Caribbean Regional Office
