Excellency:
Credit No. 2864 CV (Capacity Building Project for Private Sector Development) 
Amendment to 
      Development Credit Agreement (Lifting of Disbursement 
Conditions)
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement), dated June 13, 
1996, for the above-mentioned Project between the Republic of Cape Verde (the 
Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association) and to your 
letter, dated March 6, 1998, in which you requested us to amend the Agreement in order 
to lift the two disbursement conditions relating to the training category in Schedule 
1.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association accedes to your request and 
agrees, accordingly, to amend Schedule 1 to the Agreement (Withdrawal of the Proceeds 
of the Credit) by deleting the disbursement conditions provided for in Section 3 (c) 
therein and by replacing “;and” at the end of Section 3 (b) with a full stop.
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and 
dating the confirmation form on the attached copy of this letter and returning it to 
us, while keeping the original for your records.
This amendment will become effective as of the date of confirmation of this 
letter.
Sincerely,
Mahmood Ayub
Country Director for Cape Verde
Africa Region
