               Re: Loan No. 3356-PR (Rural Finance Project)
                   Amendment of Schedule 1 to the Loan Aqreement
         We refer to the Loan Agreement (Rural Finance Project)
dated July 11, 1991 between International Bank for Reconstruction
and Development (the Bank) and Land Bank of the Philippines (the
Borrower).  we also refer to your letter dated November 22, 1991, in
which you request an amendment of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement.
         We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to the above
request, the Bank has agreed to amend the Loan Agreement as set out
in the attachment hereto.
         Would you please confirm the agreement, on behalf of the
Borrower, to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating the
confirmation form on the attached copy of this letter and returning
the signed copy to us for our records.
