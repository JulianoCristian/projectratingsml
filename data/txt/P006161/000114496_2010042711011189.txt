Re.:
Credit No. 2013-BO (Mining Sector Rehabilitation Project)
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement, for the above referenced 
Project, between the Republic of Bolivia (the Borrower), and the International 
Development Association (the Association) dated August 4, 1989, as amended.
I am pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to your request to amend 
such Agreement. Therefore, the Association proposes the following amendments thereto:
I.
Section 1.02:
Paragraph (w) is deleted and substituted with the following new paragraph:
"(w) DICISMIN means Directorio Central de Instituciones de Servicio a la 
Mineria, the Borrower's directorate which coordinates and directs the service 
institutes of the mining sector and which is to be established by the Borrower 
pursuant to the provisions of Section 3.05 (c) of this Agreement;" and
A new paragraph (bb) is added:
"(bb) "IIMM" means Instituto de Investigaciones Minero Metalurgicas, the 
Borrower's institute to provide services in the fields of mining and 
metallurgy."
II.
Section 3.01 (b), subparagraph (i)
IDM is substituted by DICISMIN.
III.
Section 3.05 (c) subparagraph (ii) is amended to read as follows: "(ii) 
establish DICISMIN and STCM in a form and with functions satisfactory to the 
Association."
IV.

V.
Paragraph 2 of Schedule I to the Credit Agreement is amended to read as follows:
"2.
For the purposes of this Schedule the term "foreign expenditures" means 
expenditures in the currency of any country other than that of the Borrower for goods 
or services supplied from the territory of any country other than that of the 
Borrower; the term "local expenditures, means expenditures in the currency of the 
Borrower or for goods or services supplied from the territory of the Borrower; and the 
term "operating costs" means Project-related incremental costs essential to the 
effective implementation of the Project such as utilities, transportation, maintenance 
of buildings, field camps and equipment, rentals, travel per diems and expenses, 
communication costs, insurance, preparation and printing of reports, transport and 
storage of equipment and material.
