Re:
Japanese Grant Agreement
(Environmental Technical Assistance Project)
Amendment of the Japanese Grant Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
We refer to the Japanese Grant Agreement (Environmental Technical Assistance 
Project) between the People's Republic of China (the Recipient) and the International 
Development Association acting as Administrator of Grant Funds provided by Japan (the 
Administrator), dated February 12, 1993. We refer also to the discussion.s held 
between the Administrator and the Recipient regarding extending the duration of the 
contract with the consultants in order to carry out the technical assistance for the
abovementioned Grant.
Accordingly, Section 1. 03 of the Grant is hereby amended to read as follows:
"Section 1.03. The Consultants are expected to commence their services on 
February 1, 1993 
and to provide a total of 175 staff-weeks of service between that 
time and June 30, 1994. "
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
the enclosed confirmation copy and by returning it to us for our records. Once 
confirmed by you, this amendment will take of this letter.
Sincerely,
By /s/ Shahid Javed Burki
Director
China and Mongolia Department
East Asia and Pacific Region
