Re:  Environmental Technical Assistance Project (CR. 2522 CHA)
Amendment to Loan Agreement
I refer to the Development Credit Agreement between People’s Republic of 
China (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the 
Association) dated July 16, 1993 for the above-mentioned Project, as amended by 
a March 24, 1998 letter countersigned on April 1, 1998 (the Credit Agreement).  
I also refer to the letter dated May 12, 2000, from Mr. Wu Gang, Deputy Director 
General, International Department, Ministry of Finance, on behalf of the 
Borrower, proposing the restructuring of the above-mentioned Project.  
I am pleased to inform you that the Association hereby concurs with the 
Borrower’s  proposal and agrees to amend the Credit Agreement as follows: 
1.
Section 1.02(g)
Section 1.02(g) is amended to read as follows:
“(g)  "Implementing Agency" means singularly the department or 
agency of the Borrower responsible for carrying out Project activities 
under Parts B.1, B.9, C.1 and C.2 of the Project under the direction of 
NEPA; and "Implementing Agencies" mean collectively the departments and 
agencies of the Borrower selected by NEPA, responsible for carrying out 
activities under such Parts of the Project;”
2.
Schedule 2, Part A, CAS Institutional Strengthening
Schedule 2 Part A is amended to read as follows after adding a new Part 
A.3:
“Provision of technical assistance, training and equipment to the 
Chinese Academy of Sciences to support:  
1.
the establishment and operation of the Chinese Ecosystem Research 
Network (CERN); 
2.
the establishment and operation of the Biodiversity Research and 
Information Management Program (BRIM); and 
3.
the development of environmental management capacity in selected 
institutes of the Academy of Sciences that are engaged in research 
relevant to environmental management and protection of the central and 
western regions of China.”
3.
Schedule 2, Part B, NEPA Institutional Strengthening
Schedule 2, Part B.1 is amended to read as follows:
“1.
the establishment and operation of regional, provincial and 
municipal environmental information and monitoring systems;”
4.
Schedule 4, Implementation Program
Page  2
Schedule 4 is amended  by adding a new paragraph 5 to read as follows:
“With respect to the implementation of Part B.1 of the Project, 
NEPA shall select the location of water quality monitoring stations in 
accordance with criteria acceptable to the Association.”
Additionally, the Association hereby re-allocates the proceeds of the 
Credit as set out in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Credit 
Agreement as follows: (i) an amount of SDR 106,000 from Category 1-B to Category 
1-A; (ii) an amount of SDR 1,207,000 from Category 2-B to Category 2-A; (iii) an 
amount of SDR 630,000 from Category 3-A to Category 2-A; and (iv) an amount of 
SDR 490,000 from Category 3-B to Category 2-A. The reallocated amounts are set 
forth in the Attachment to this letter
Please confirm the agreement of the Borrower to the amendments set out 
above by countersigning and dating the form of confirmation set forth below, and 
by returning one fully signed original to us for our files.  One fully signed 
original should be retained by the Borrower.
Except as specifically amended herein, all other provisions of the Credit 
Agreement remain in full force and effect.  Upon receipt of the copy of this 
letter of amendment duly countersigned by the representative of the Borrower, 
the above amendment shall become effective as of the date of countersignature of 
this letter of amendment.
Sincerely,
