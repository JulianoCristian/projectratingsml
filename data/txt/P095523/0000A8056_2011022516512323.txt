                         The Transport and Territorial Development Project (TTDP) aims at rehabilitating two transport corridors in the North and South-East departments, and fostering territorial
                         development along said corridors, based on a decentralized planning and implementation approach. This project has had so far a satisfactory implementation. Over the last 6 months,
Public Disclosure Copy




                         the two major road rehabilitation -- RN3, Barriere Battant / Carrefourt la Mort - and RD41, Thiotte / Anse a Pitre -- have progressed significantly. Twelve sub-projects resulting from
                         the territorial development approach were completed and thirteen are on-going.
