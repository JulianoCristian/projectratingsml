Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (the "Loan Agreement") relating to the Community 
Infrastructure Project (the "Project") between the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan (the 
“Borrower”) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (“IBRD”), 
dated August 27, 1997.  We also refer to the Borrower's request, dated November 11, 
1999 to amend the Loan Agreement to modify the Project description to allow for the 
financing of Pilot Sub-projects.  Accordingly, the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as 
follows:
1.
Section 1.02 is amended as follows:
(i)
the word "and" that follows definition (p) is deleted;
(ii)
the period "." at the end of definition (q) is replaced by a 
semicolon ";"; 
and 
(iii)
the following new definitions are added after definition (q): “(r)
“
Operations Manual for Pilot Sub-projects” means the 
Borrower's Manual, agreed 
with the Bank, for the carrying out of Part D 
of the Project, as the same may 
be amended from time to time with the 
agreement of the Bank;
(s)
“Sub-grant” means a grant made or proposed to be made by the 
Borrower, out of 
the proceeds of the Loan, to finance a Pilot Sub-project (as this 
term is hereinafter 
defined) under Part D of the Project; and 
(t)
“Pilot Sub-project” means a specific project to be carried out 
under Part D of 
the Project utilizing the proceeds of a Sub-grant.”
