                  Re:   Loan 3000-IND (Tree Crops Processing Project)
                        Amendment of the Loan Agreement
Dear Minister Sumarlin:
      We refer to the Loan Agreement (Tree Crops Processing Project)
between the Republic of Indonesia (the Borrower) and the
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank)
dated December 2, 1988.  We also refer to your Ministry's telex
dated June 2, 1990 requesting the Bank for an increase in the
authorized allocation of the Special Account under the above
project.
      The Bank is pleased to inform you that, after due
consideration, it has agreed to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
      1.    The amount stated in paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 5 to
the Loan Agreement is amended to read "$700,000".
      Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by
signing and dating the enclosed form of confirmation, and returning
it to us.
                               Sincerely yours,
                                /s/ Amar Bhattacharya
                                Acting Director
                                Country Department V
                                Asia Region
