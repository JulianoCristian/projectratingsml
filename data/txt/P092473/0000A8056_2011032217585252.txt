                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This ISR is a synthesis of 7 country project SPNs. Overall progress is satisfactory and the PDO will be achieved. Finalization of key activities have been ongoing at fast pace since
                         the second extension approved in June 2010. The Early Warning and Response (EWR) system is now fully functional in all countries as reflected by the country scorecards. The
                         Mauritania Center of locust control has demonstrated again (last successful demonstration was from October 2010 to February 2011) its capacity to control at a very early stage a
