Dear Sir:
Re: Bombay Sewage Disposal Project (Cr. 2763/Ln. 3923)
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement, the Loan Agreement,
the Maharashtra Agreement and the Project Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (DCA) between India (the 
Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association), the 
Loan Agreement between the  Borrower and the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), the Maharashtra Agreement between the 
Association, the Bank and the State of Maharashtra and the Project Agreement 
between the Association, the Bank and the Municipal Corporation of Greater 
Bombay (the Corporation), all dated December 28, 1995, as amended on January 18, 
1999.  We also refer to the request made through a letter dated May 25, 2000 
from Mr. Abhas K. Jha, Deputy Secretary, Department of Economic Affairs, 
Government of India, requesting the Association and the Bank to utilize two 
million, nine hundred sixty thousand United States Dollars (US$2,960,000) of the 
Project savings of twenty million United States Dollars (US$20,000,000) to 
finance some additional activities, and to cancel ten million United States 
Dollars (US$10,000,000) effective from June 13, 2000.
The Association and the Bank are pleased to inform you that, after due 
consideration, the DCA, the Loan Agreement, the Maharashtra Agreement and the 
Project Agreement are amended to read as follows:
1.
All references to the words “Bombay”, “Municipal Corporation of Greater 
Bombay”
and “MCGB” in the DCA, Loan Agreement, Maharashtra Agreement and the Project 
Agreement are hereby deleted and replaced with the following:
(i) “Bombay” is replaced with “Mumbai”.
(ii) “Municipal Corporation of Greater Bombay” is replaced with 
“Municipal 
Corporation of Brihan Mumbai”
(iii) “MCGB” and replaced with “MCBM”.
DCA
2.
Schedule 1 of the DCA is amended as provided in the Attachment to this 
amendment letter.
3.
Schedule 2 of the DCA is amended to read as follows:
Part A: Physical Components
(a) Part A (v) is deleted and replaced with the following:
“(v) The construction of Ghatkopar high level influent tunnel.”
Part B: Technical Assistance and Training
Page  2
(b) Part B (i) and (ii) are deleted and replaced with the following:
“(i) Design and supervision of construction of the works to be carried out 
under Part A (i) through (v), and (vii) through (viii).
(ii) Upgrading the operation and maintenance practices and facilities for 
Corporation’s water supply and sewerage system to acceptable levels.”
(c) Sub-paragraph (vi) is added to Part B and reads as follows:
“(vi) Training in all aspects of water supply and sewerage management.”
PROJECT AGREEMENT
4.
Schedule 1 of the Project Agreement is amended as follows:
(a)
Part B.2(a) of Schedule 1 to the Project Agreement is amended to 
read:
“(a) Prequalification
Bidders for works included in Part A (i), (ii), (iii) and (v) of 
the 
Project shall be prequalified in accordance with the provisions of 
paragraphs
2.9 and 2.10 of the Guidelines.”
(b)
Part C of Schedule 1 to the Project Agreement is deleted and 
replaced with the following:
“National Competitive Bidding
Contracts for works listed in Part A (iv), (vi), (vii) and (viii) of 
the Project may 
be procured under contracts awarded in accordance with the 
provisions of paragraphs 3.3 and 3.4 of the Guidelines.”
Except as specifically amended hereby, all other provisions of the DCA, 
the Loan Agreement, the Maharashtra Agreement and the Project Agreement remain 
in full force and effect.  Upon receipt of the original copy of this amendment 
letter duly countersigned by the representative of the Borrower and the 
Corporation, the above amendments shall become effective as of the date of 
countersignature of this amendment letter.
Please confirm the agreement of the Borrower and the Corporation to the 
amendments set out above by countersigning the form of confirmation set forth 
below and returning to the Association and the Bank one fully signed original of 
the amendment letter, and retaining one original for each of your records.
