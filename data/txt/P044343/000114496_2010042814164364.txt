Re:
HONDURAS (Biodiversity in Priority Areas Project)
GEF Trust Fund Grant No. TF028367
First
Amendment to the Global Environmental Facility
Trust Fund Grant Agreement - Amendment
Excellency:
Please refer to the Global Environmental Facility Trust Fund Grant Agreement 
dated December 19, 1997 entered into between the Republic of  Honduras (the Recipient) 
and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the 
United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) acting as Implementing Agencies of the 
Global Environmental Facility.
In order to permit the financing of: (a)  works under Parts A.1, B.2, B.3 and D 
of the Project; and (b) goods under Parts A.1, A.3 and D of the Project with part of 
the proceeds of the GEF Trust Fund Grant, the Bank and UNDP hereby agree to amend the 
Global Environmental Facility Trust Fund Grant Agreement, as follows:
1.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is amended to read as set forth in the 
Annex to this Amendment Letter.
2.
Part A.1 of the Project is hereby amended to read as follows:
“Part A:
Institutional Strengthening
1.
Strengthening of the capacity of DAPVS to manage the 
Protected Areas, through the rehabilitation of DAPVS’ facilities, 
the provision 
of technical assistance and training, the 
acquisition and 
utilization of goods and the hiring of personnel 
required therefor.”.
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in three counterparts each of which shall 
be an original.  Upon receipt by the Bank and UNDP of one fully executed original of 
this Amendment Letter, the amendments set forth herein will become effective as of the 
date above written.  Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by 
signing and dating the three counterparts of this Amendment Letter.
