7a. Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
In support of the implementation of the National Plan for Sustainable Rehabilitation and Reconstruction
(PRRES), the project will contribute to restore access to basic infrastructure for a portion of the affected
population in five target regions, and to strengthen the Government#s ability at the national, sectoral, and
municipal levels throughout the country to respond to future disasters.
