                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         In support of the implementation of the National Plan for Sustainable Rehabilitation and Reconstruction (PRRES), the project will contribute to restore access to basic
                         infrastructure for a portion of the affected population in five target regions, and to strengthen the Government#s ability at the national, sectoral, and municipal levels
                         throughout the country to respond to future disasters.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project was restructured on March 7, 2013 to extend the closing date from May 15, 2013 to September 30, 2013 in order to complete key activities including: completion of the
                         Alpacoma Watershed study and Oruro Land Planning (Component 1) and completion of civil works to reduce disaster vulnerability (Component 2).

                         At the time of preparation of this ISR the GoB: (i) has taken strategic actions to speed up the implementation of Component 1, including the contract for the Alpacoma Watershed
                         Study; and (ii) has fully implemented a portfolio of 212 subprojects, amounting to approximately US$6.84 million from the credit under Component 2, in the departments of Santa
