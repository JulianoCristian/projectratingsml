     Re:  Nicaragua: Credit No. 2556-NI (Health Sector Reform Project)
                     Fifth Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
     Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Health sector
Reform Project) dated December 17, 1993 between the Republic of
Nicaragua (the Borrower) and the International Development Association
(the Association) as amended on November 7, 1994, February 28, 1995,
August 7, 1995 and February 20, 1996.
     Pursuant to your request in letter dated September 9, 1996, we
hereby agree to amend: the Development Credit Agreement as follows:
     1.   Schedule 1:  The table in paragraph 1 is amended by modifying
the percentage of financing and allocations of the Credit and Norwegian
Grant specified for Category (4) Pharmaceuticals, and the amounts of
Category (3) Medical Equipment and Category (12) Unallocated
respectively as follows:
     "(3) Medical Equipment   551,000   1,731,650  75%  25%"
     "(4) Pharmaceuticals     854,600   4,844,400  50%  50%"
     "(12) Unallocated        613,800     564,400"
     2.   Schedule 5: paragraph 1 (a) by deleting the numeral "(9)" and
replacing it by "(11)".
     Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by
signing this letter in the space provided and returning it to us.
This amendment shall be executed in two counterparts, each of which
shall constitute an original.
     The amendment, in respect of eligible categories of the Special
Account shall be applied retroactively to February 20, 1996, and shall
become effective upon receipt by the Association of one fully executed
original of this letter.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
