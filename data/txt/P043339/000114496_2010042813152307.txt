Re:
Municipal Infrastructure Development Project - TF 026057
Amendment to the Trust Fund Credit Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Trust Fund Credit Agreement for the Municipal Infrastructure 
Development Project (TF026057) between the Palestine Liberation Organization (for the benefit 
of the Palestinian Authority) (the “Borrower”) and the International Development Association, 
acting as administrator of the Trust Fund for Gaza and West Bank (the “Administrator”), dated 
June 12, 1996, as amended (the “Credit Agreement”). We also refer to the letter from H.E. Mr. 
Mohammed Nashashibi, the former Minister of Finance of the Palestinian Authority and 
authorized representative of the Borrower, dated June 1, 2002, requesting the Administrator to 
reallocate the proceeds of the Credit to assist in financing emergency damage repair and services 
support activities in the West Bank and Gaza.
We are pleased to inform you that the Administrator has agreed to the above request. 
Accordingly, the Credit Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
1.
In Section 1.02 (g), the word “and” in the second line shall be deleted and shall be 
replaced by a comma, and the words “and Z” shall be inserted between the words “A.7” and “of”.
2.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Credit Agreement shall read as follows:

3.
In Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement, a new Part Z shall be added and shall read as 
follows: 
“Part Z
:
Damage Repair and Emergency Service Support Activities
Carrying out a program, consisting of the provision of goods and the execution of works, 
with a view to rehabilitating damaged physical and institutional infrastructure, and mitigating the 
decline in essential public service provision, including electricity, water, wastewater, solid waste 
and social services.”
4.
A new sentence shall be added at the end of paragraph 1 of Schedule 4 to the Credit 
Agreement and shall read as follows: “The Borrower shall cause the Palestinian Authority to vest 
the responsibility for implementation of Part Z of the Project in PECDAR.”.
5.
The definition of the expression “eligible Categories” in Schedule 5 to the Credit 
Agreement shall include Category (6) (a).
6.
All the terms and conditions of the Credit Agreement, not amended hereby, shall remain in 
full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing the confirmation on the 
copy of this Letter Amendment and returning it to our attention.
