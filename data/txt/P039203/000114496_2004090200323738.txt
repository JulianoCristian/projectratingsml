AFE PROJECT AGREEMENT 
AGREEMENT, dated July 1, 1997, between INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND 
DEVELOPMENT (the Bank) and ADMINISTRACION DE FERROCARRILES DEL ESTADO (AFE). 
WHEREAS (A) by the Loan Agreement of even date herewith between República 
Oriental del Uruguay (the Borrower) and the Bank, the Bank has agreed to make 
available to the Borrower an amount equal to seventy six million Dollars 
($76,000,000), on the terms and conditions set forth in the Loan Agreement, but only 
on condition that AFE agrees to undertake such obligations toward the Bank as are set 
forth in this Agreement; 
(B)
by a subsidiary loan agreement to be entered into between the Borrower 
and AFE, the proceeds of the Loan allocated to Parts A.2 and B.2 of the Project 
provided for under the Loan Agreement will be made available to AFE on the terms and 
conditions set forth in said AFE Subsidiary Loan Agreement; and 
WHEREAS AFE, in consideration of the Bank’s entering into the Loan Agreement 
with the Borrower, has agreed to undertake the obligations set forth in this 
Agreement;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows: 
ARTICLE I 
Definitions
Section 1.01. Unless the context otherwise requires, the several terms defined 
in the Loan Agreement and the General Conditions (as so defined) have the respective 
meanings therein set forth.
ARTICLE II 
Page  2
Execution of the Project 
Section 2.01. AFE declares its commitment to the objectives of the Project as 
set forth in Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement, and, to this end, shall carry out Parts 
A.2 and B.2 of the Project with due diligence and efficiency and in conformity with 
appropriate administrative, technical, financial, engineering and environmental 
practices, and in accordance with the Environmental Manual and the AFE Financial 
Targets, and shall provide, or cause to be provided, promptly as needed, the funds, 
facilities, services and other resources required for such Parts of the Project. 
Section 2.02. Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree, procurement of the 
goods, works and consultants’ services required for Parts A.2 and B.2 of the Project 
and to be financed out of the proceeds of the Loan shall be governed by the provisions 
of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement. 
Section 2.03. AFE shall carry out the obligations set forth in Sections 9.04, 
9.05, 9.06, 9.07, 9.08 and 9.09 of the General Conditions (relating to insurance, use 
of goods and services, plans and schedules, records and reports, maintenance and land 
acquisition, respectively) in respect of this Agreement and Parts A.2 and B.2 of the 
Project.
Section 2.04. AFE shall duly perform all its obligations under the AFE 
Subsidiary Loan Agreement. Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree, AFE shall not 
take or concur in any action which would have the effect of assigning, amending, 
abrogating, terminating, waiving or failing to enforce the AFE Subsidiary Loan 
Agreement or any provision thereof. 
Section 2.05. (a) AFE shall, at the request of the Bank, exchange views with the 
Bank with regard to the progress of Parts A.2 and B.2 of the Project, the performance 
of its obligations under this Agreement and under the AFE Subsidiary Loan Agreement, 
and other matters relating to the purposes of the Loan. 
(b)
AFE shall promptly inform the Bank of any condition which interferes or 
threatens to interfere with the progress of Parts A.2 and B.2 of  the Project, the 
accomplishment of the purposes of the Loan, or the performance by AFE of its 
obligations under this Agreement and under the AFE Subsidiary Loan Agreement. 
Section 2.06. AFE shall maintain policies and procedures adequate to enable it 
to monitor and evaluate on an ongoing basis, in accordance with the Performance 
Indicators, the carrying out of Parts A.2 and B.2 of the Project and the achievement 
of the objectives thereof.
Section 2.07.  Without limitation to the provisions of Section 2.05 of this 
Agreement, AFE shall participate in the reviews referred to in Section 3.12 of the 
Loan Agreement.
Section 2.08.  Without limitation to the provisions of Section 2.07 of this 
Agreement: 
(a)
AFE shall carry out AFE’s Implementation Plan in accordance with its 
terms; and 
(b)
within twelve months of the Effective Date, AFE shall review with the 
Borrower and the Bank AFE’s performance in carrying out said plan, particularly with 
respect to AFE’s promotion of private sector participation.
Section 2.09. (a) AFE shall establish, and thereafter operate and maintain 
during the implementation of the Project a Project implementation unit (the AFE PIU) 
with functions and responsibilities satisfactory to the Bank, including, inter alia, 
the responsibility of assisting AFE in the carrying out of Parts A.2 and B.2 of the 
Project.
(b)
AFE shall ensure that the AFE PIU is at all times headed by a coordinator  
and assisted by staff in adequate numbers, all with qualifications acceptable to the 
Bank. 
Section 2.10. AFE shall, if applicable, include in the bidding documents for the 
carrying out of each AFE Subproject the obligation of the bidder to take (prior to the 
commencement of any works under such AFE Subproject) the necessary actions to address 
any potential negative environmental impact identified in the environmental assessment 
carried out under Part B.1 (c) of the Project.

ARTICLE III 
Management and Operations of AFE 
Section 3.01. AFE shall carry on its operations and conduct its affairs in 
accordance with sound administrative, technical, financial, engineering and 
environmental practices under the supervision of qualified and experienced management 
assisted by competent staff in adequate numbers. 
Section 3.02. AFE shall at all times operate and maintain its plant, machinery, 
equipment and other property, and from time to time, promptly as needed, make all 
necessary repairs and renewals thereof, all in accordance with sound administrative, 
technical, financial, engineering and environmental practices.
Section 3.03. AFE shall take out and maintain with responsible insurers, or make 
other provisions satisfactory to the Bank for, insurance against such risks and in 
such amounts as shall be consistent with appropriate practice. ARTICLE IV Financial 
Covenants Section 4.01. (a) AFE shall maintain records and accounts adequate to 
reflect in accordance with sound accounting practices its operations and financial 
condition, including separate accounts for the railway operations and 
maintenance-of-way activities.
(b)
AFE shall: 
(i)
have its records, accounts and financial statements (balance 
sheets, statements of income and expenses and related statements) 
for each fiscal year audited, in accordance with appropriate 
auditing principles consistently applied, by independent auditors 
acceptable to the Bank;
(ii)
furnish to the Bank as soon as available, but in any case not 
later than six months after the end of each such year: (A) 
certified copies of its financial statements for such year as so 
audited; and (B) the report of such audit by said auditors of such 
scope and in such detail as the Bank shall have reasonably 
requested; and 
(iii)
furnish to the Bank such other information concerning said 
records, accounts and financial statements as well as the audit 
thereof, as the Bank shall from time to time reasonably request. 
ARTICLE V 
Effective Date; Termination; 
Cancellation and Suspension
Section 5.01. This Agreement shall come into force and effect on the date upon 
which the Loan Agreement becomes effective. 
Section 5.02. This Agreement and all obligations of the Bank and of AFE 
thereunder shall terminate on the date on which the Loan Agreement shall terminate in 
accordance with its terms, and the Bank shall promptly notify AFE thereof. 
Section 5.03. All these provisions of this Agreement shall continue in full 
force and effect notwithstanding any cancellation or suspension under the General 
Conditions.
