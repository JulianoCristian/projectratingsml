BRAZIL: Loan 3170-BR (National Irrigation Program -

Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Minister Malan:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the Federative Republic of Brazil and 
the International Bank For Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), dated April 19, 
1991, as amended, and to the letter, dated April 11, 2000, from the Ministry of 
National Integration proposing an expansion of the “Project Area” and a reallocation 
of the balance of the Loan proceeds to support the carrying out of the agricultural 
development component of the Project in the revised area, in accordance with an action 
plan approved by the Bank.
We agree with such request and propose to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
Section 1.02 (x) is amended to read as follows in its entirety:
“(x) 'Project Area' means the States of Minas Gerais, Bahia, Pernambuco, 
Alagoas, Rio  Grande do Norte, Ceará and Piauí.”
2.
In Section 3.01, a new paragraph (f) is added to read as follows:
"(f) Without limitation to the provisions of paragraph (a) of this 
Section, the 
Borrower shall complete the carrying out of Parts B.2 (a), B.2 (c) 
and C of the Project in 
accordance with the action plan submitted by the Borrower to 
the Bank in March 2000."
3.
