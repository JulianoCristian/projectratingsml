Re:   Credit No. 3035-AM (Agricultural Reform Support Project) 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement 
 
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (DCA) for the above-referenced Project, 
dated March 12, 1998, between the Republic of Armenia (the Borrower) and the International 
Development Association (the Association) as amended. We also refer to the letter of  
Mr. A. Margaryan, the Prime Minister, dated April 23, 2003, requesting the Association to use 
the Credit for the above mentioned Project to procure fertilizers for distribution to farmers 
affected by the frost damage. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association has agreed to the following 
amendments: 
 
1. 
A new paragraph 4 is added to Part C of Schedule 2 (Description of the Project) to the 
DCA and reads as follows: 
 
“4.
 
importing of agricultural inputs to support the rehabilitation of vineyards and 
orchards.” 
 
2.  
Paragraph C2 and C3 of Schedule 3 (Procurement and Consultants Services) to the DCA 
are amended to read as follows:  
 
(a)  
“2. 
International Shopping
Goods estimated to cost less than $1,300,000 equivalent per contract, up to an aggregate 
amount not exceeding $2,550,000 equivalent, may be procured under contracts awarded on the 
basis of international shopping procedures in accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 3.5 
and 3.6 of the Guidelines.” 
 
(b)  
“3. 
National Shopping
Goods estimated to cost less than $250,000 equivalent per contract, may be procured 
under contracts awarded on the basis of national shopping procedures in accordance with the 
provisions of paragraphs 3.5 and 3.6 of the Guidelines.”
