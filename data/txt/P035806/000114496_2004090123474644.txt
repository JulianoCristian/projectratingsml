Re:   
Cr. 3035-AM (Agricultural Reform Support Project) 
 
Amendment to Credit Agreement  
Excellency:
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Agricultural Reform  Support Project), 
dated March 12, 1998, between the Republic of Armenia (the Borrower) and the International 
Development Association (the Association) as amended. We also refer to your letter of June 26, 
2002 to the Association requesting extension of the closing date. We are pleased to inform you, 
that the Association agrees to the following amendments: 
 
1. 
The Association has established June 30, 2004, as the later date for purposes of Section 
2.03 of the Development Credit Agreement. 
 
2. 
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement is revised 
to read as set forth in the Attachment to this Letter. The financing percentages for ARC research 
contracts under category 4(b) and the incremental operating costs under category 5 in the table 
have been increased with retroactive effect from July 1, 2000.   
 
All the terms and conditions of the Development Credit Agreement, as amended 
hereby, shall remain in full force and effect. 
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing, dating and 
returning to us the attached copy of this letter. This amendment will become effective as of date 
of countersignature upon receipt by the Bank of the countersigned copy of this letter. 
 
Sincerely, 
By:   /s/  Donna Dowsett-Coirolo
