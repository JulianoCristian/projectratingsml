                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Electricity access is an important priority for the Borrower, who has remained committed and shown significant results to date, including: (a) the installation of about 9,200 solar home
Public Disclosure Copy




                         systems benefiting 45,000 people and 87 solar home systems in schools and clinics benefiting 30,000 people; (b) the provision of public lighting to 20,000 people; and (c) 7,700 new
                         rural electricity connections through the extension of the electricity grid contracts (densification).
