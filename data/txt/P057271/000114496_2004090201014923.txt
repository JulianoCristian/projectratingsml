       Credit No. 3139 GUA 
       (First Amendment of Development Credit Agreement)
Excellency:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated November 12, 1998 entered 
into between the Co-operative Republic of Guyana (the Borrower) and the International 
Development Association (the Association).
In response to the Borrower’s letter request of January 26, 1999, the 
Association hereby agrees to amend the Development Credit Agreement as follows:
1.
Section 2.09 is hereby deleted.
2.
Section 7.01 is hereby amended to read as follows:
“Section 7.01. The Minister of Finance of the Borrower is designated as 
representative of the Borrower for the purposes of Section 11.03 of the 
General Conditions.”.
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall 
be an original.  Upon receipt by the Association of one fully executed original of 
this Amendment Letter, these amendments will be effective as of the date first above 
written.  \0c Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and 
dating the two counterparts of this Amendment Letter.
