Excellency:
Please refer to the Ozone Project Trust Fund Grant Agreement, dated July 7, 1993, 
between the Republic of Chile and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development 
(the Trustee) as amended, for the Project in reference (the Grant Agreement).
Considering that the Executive Committee of the Multilateral Fund for the 
implementation of the Montreal Protocol on Substances that Deplete the Ozone Layer has 
approved an increase in the scope of the above referred Project, as previously discussed by us, we 
propose to amend the Grant Agreement as follows:
1.
 In Section 1. 0 1 (b)
, the following subparagraph (vi) is inserted to read as follows:
"(vi) the second sentence of Section 5.01 is modified to read:
'Except as the Recipient and the Trustee shall otherwise agree, no withdrawals 
shall be made: (a) on account of expenditures in the territories of any country which is not 
a
member of the Bank or for goods produced in, or services supplied from, such 
territories; or (b) for the purpose of any payment to persons or entities, or for any import 
of goods, if such payment or import, to the knowledge of the Bank, is prohibited by a 
decision of the United Nations Security Council taken under Chapter VII of the Charter of 
the United Nations.'."
2.
In Section 1.02 (c)
, the word "private" is deleted.
3.
In Section 1.02 (j), the words “in the refrigeration or foam industry sector” are deleted.
4.

, the following paragraphs (m) and (n)
 are inserted to read:

later date."
