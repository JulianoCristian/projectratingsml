Excellency:
Re:
Chile - Ozone Protection Policy and Institutional Strengthening Project 
(OTF Grant No. 21916-CH)
Amendment to the Ozone Project Trust Fund Grant Agreement
Please refer to the Ozone Project Trust Fund Grant Agreement, dated July 7, 1993, between the Republic of 
Chile (the Recipient) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development as Trustee of the Ozone 
Projects Trust Fund (the 
Trustee) for the captioned Project (the Grant Agreement), as amended on January 6, 1995 and July 25, 1996.
Pursuant to discussions between staff of the Recipient, and the Trustee, the Trustee agrees to further amend 
the Grant Agreement to enable financing of activities recently approved by the Montreal Protocol Multilateral Fund 
as follows:
1.
The text of paragraph (j) of Section 1.02 is deleted and the following text substituted therefor:
“(j)
“Subproject” means a project,  as shall be approved by the Trustee, to be 
carried out by a Beneficiary utilizing the proceeds of the OTF Grant, involving 
investments, including operating costs; demonstration and testing activities; training; 
provision of technical assistance; or other activities related to the reduction or 
elimination of ozone-depletion;”
2.
Schedule 3 to the Grant Agreement is amended as follows:
(a)
The text of paragraph 2, Section I, is amended by adding the following text after the word 
“Goods”: “and services, other than Consultant Services”; and
(b)
The text of paragraph 1, Section II, is amended by adding the following 
text after the words “In order to assist the Recipient in carrying out the Project,”: “or 
with regard to activities under Subprojects,”.
Kindly confirm the agreement of the Recipient to the terms of this letter by dating and signing the 
counterpart of this letter in the space indicated below. Upon receipt by the Trustee of a fully executed original of this 
letter, the above amendments to the Grant Agreement will be deemed effective as of the date of this letter.
Sincerely yours,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR
RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT
