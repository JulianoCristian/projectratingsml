TRUST FUND FOR EAST TIMOR

TRUST FUND FOR EAST TIMOR 
 SUPPLEMENTAL GRANT AGREEMENT 
 
AGREEMENT, dated July 23, 2002, between DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC OF 
EAST TIMOR (the Recipient) and the INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT 
ASSOCIATION (the Trustee) acting as trustee of the Trust Fund for East Timor.   
WHEREAS (A) the Recipient and the Trustee have entered into a Grant 
Agreement (Grant Number TF-027826, Economic Institutions Capacity Building 
Project), dated February 26, 2001 (the Grant Agreement), for the purpose of 
strengthening the capacity of the Recipient in evaluating economic options and designing 
and implementing economic policies through the training of relevant East Timorese staff; 
WHEREAS (B) the Recipient has requested the Trustee to provide further 
additional assistance for the Project in an amount of one hundred thousand Dollars 
($100,000); and 
WHEREAS (C) the Trustee has agreed, on the basis, inter alia, of the foregoing 
to provide such additional assistance to the Recipient on the terms and conditions set 
forth in this Supplemental Grant Agreement;  
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:  
 
ARTICLE I 
General Conditions; Definitions 
 
Section 1.01. 
The Introductory paragraph of the Grant Agreement is amended 
to read: 
“AGREEMENT, dated February 26, 2001, between DEMOCRATIC 
REPUBLIC OF EAST TIMOR (the Recipient) and the INTERNATIONAL 
DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (the Trustee) acting as trustee of the Trust 
Fund for East Timor.” 
 
 
Section 1.02. 
The Recital WHEREAS clause (B) of the Grant Agreement is 
deleted and WHEREAS clause (C) is renumbered (B).

Section 1.03. 
Section 1.01 of the Grant Agreement is amended by inserting in 
their proper alphabetical order the following new definition: 
“(d) 
“Supplemental Grant Agreement” means the agreement between 
the Recipient and the Trustee supplementing the Grant Agreement (Economic 
Institutions Capacity Building Project) between the Recipient and the Trustee, 
dated February 26, 2001.” 
Section 1.04. 
Section 2.01 of the Grant Agreement is amended to read as 
follows: 
“Section 2.01.  The Trustee agrees to make available to the Recipient, on 
the terms and conditions set forth or referred to in this Agreement, the Grant in 
an amount equal to six hundred thousand Dollars ($600,000), which includes: (i) 
an original amount equivalent to five hundred thousand Dollars ($500,000); and 
(ii) an additional amount equivalent to one hundred thousand Dollars ($100,000) 
(the Additional Financing).”   
 
Section 1.05. 
Section 6.01 of the Grant Agreement is amended to read: 
“Section 6.01.  The Minister of Planning and Finance is designated as
