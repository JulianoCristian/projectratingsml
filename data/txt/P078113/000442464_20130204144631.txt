7a. Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
The objective of the Project is to assist the Recipient to achieve primary education and completion
increased in the nineteen poorest districts in the six poorest provinces, quality improved, and capacities
strengthened to develop policies and strategies to monitor and manage primary education.
