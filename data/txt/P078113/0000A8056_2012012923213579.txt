                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to assist the Borrower to achieve universal completion of primary education by implementing education policies and reform
                         actions, including: (i) increasing access to, and completion of, primary schooling in the Project's target districts (Component A); (ii) improving the quality of
                         education (Component B); and (iii) building the policy development and management capacity in the Ministry of Education (Component C).
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation experienced a slow start but the pace of implementation is now improving. From January 2010 to November 2011, the project disbursed 4.9 million USD
                         against 15.5 million USD. Activities under Community-Based Contracting school construction and Community Grant components have started. Construction of 18 schools have been
                         completed.
