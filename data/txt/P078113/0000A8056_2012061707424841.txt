                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to assist the Borrower to achieve universal completion of primary education by implementing education policies and reform actions,
                         including: (i) increasing access to, and completion of, primary schooling in the Project's target districts (Component A); (ii) improving the quality of education
                         (Component B); and (iii) building the policy development and management capacity in the Ministry of Education (Component C).
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation is progressing well, with Community-Based Contracting for School Construction (CBC),making steady progress.One hundred and ten schools out of two
                         hundred ninety-two are now complete and fully in use. In addition, the second phase of CBC is well under way, Community Grants was delivered to the communities and teacher
                         upgrading training has started for the master trainers.
