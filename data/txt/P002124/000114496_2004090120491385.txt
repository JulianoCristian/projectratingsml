            Ref.: Third Multi State Agricultural Development Project
                  (Credit 2035-UNI)
                  Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
      Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Third Multi
State Agricultural Development Project) dated August 4, 1989 between
the Federal Republic of Nigeria (the Borrower) and the International
Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), as amended by
the letter of agreement between the Borrower and the Bank dated
February 21, 1991.  To facilitate the carrying out of the Project,
the Bank hereby proposes that the Development Credit Agreement for
the abovenamed Project be amended as follows:
      (A)  in Section 2.02 by deleting paragraph (b) thereof and
substituting therefor the following new paragraph:
      "(b) The borrower shall, for the purposes of the Project, open
      and maintain (or cause to be opened and maintained) in dollars
      special deposit accounts in a commercial bank or banks on
      terms and conditions satisfactory to the Association,
      including appropriate protection against set-off, seizure or
      attachment.  Deposits into, and payments out of, the Special
      Accounts shall be made in accordance with the provisions of
      Schedule 3 to this Agreement and Schedule 4 to the Project
      Agreements."; and
      (B) in Schedule 3 by deleting subparagraph (c) of paragraph 1
thereof and substituting therefor the following new subparagraph:
      "(c) the term "Authorized Allocation" means an amount
      equivalent to (i) $1,150,000 for FACU; (ii) $250,000 for PRSD;
      (iii) $100,000 for the Federal Department of Fisheries and
      Agricultural Cooperatives; and (iv) $500,000 for APMEU, to be
      withdrawn from the Credit Account and deposited in the
      respective Special Accounts pursuant to paragraph 3 (a) of
      this Schedule."
      Please indicate your agreement to the amendments proposed
above by signing the form of confirmation on the enclosed copy of
this letter and returning it to us.
                         Sincerely yours,
                           Edwin R. Lim
                             Director
                     Western Africa Department
