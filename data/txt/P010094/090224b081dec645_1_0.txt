PROJECT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated March 29, 1976, between INTERNATIONAL
DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (hereinafter called the Association) and
the PROVINCE OF SIND, acting by its Governor (hereinafter called
Sind).
WHEREAS by the Development Credit Agreement of even date here-
with between the Islamic Republic of Pakistan (hereinafter called
the Borrower) and the Association, the Association has agreed to
make available to the Borrower an amount in various currencies
equivalent to twenty-three million dollars ($23,000,000), on the
terms and conditions set forth in the Development Credit Agreement,
but only on condition that Sind agree to undertake such obligations
toward the Association as hereinafter set forth; and
WHEREAS Sind, in consideration of the Association's entering
into the Development Credit Agreement with the Borrower, has agreed
to undertake the obligations hereinafter set forth;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:


﻿-2-
ARTICLE I
Definitions
Section 1.01. Aherever used in this Agreement, unless the
context shall otherwise require, the several terms defined in the
Development Credit Agreement and in the General Conditions (as so
defined) have the respective meanings therein set forth.


﻿-3-
ARTICLE II
Execution of the Project
Section 2.01. Sind shall carry out and shall cause to be
carried out that part of the Project described in Schedule 2 to
the Development Credit Agreement for which Sind, its agencies or
the Sind Seed Corporation shall be responsible with due diligence
and efficiency and in conformity with appropriate agricultural,
engineering, economic, commercial and financial policies and prac-
tices, and shall provide, or cause to be provided, promptly as
needed, the funds, facilities, services and other resources re-
quired for the purpose.
Section 2.02. (a) Without limitation or restriction upon the
generality of the provisions of Section 2.01 of this Agreement,
Sind shall until such date as the Association shall establish,
provide the Sind Seed Corporation with: (i) equity capital required
to cover development costs, and (ii) monthly reimbursements for
subsidies involved in the marketing of seed; and in addition Sind
shall provide or ensure the provision of loans required for work-
ing capital.
(b) Funds to be provided to the Sind Seed Corporation as
referred to in paragraph (a) hereinabove, shall be made available
under procedures, terms and conditions which are acceptable to
the Association.


﻿-4-
Section 2.03. (a) Sind undertakes to insure, or make adequate
provision for the insurance of, the imported goods to be financed
out of the proceeds of the Credit made available to it by the
Borrower against hazards incident to the acquisition, transportation
and delivery thereof to the place of use or installation, and for
such insurance any indemnity shall be payable in a currency freely
usable by Sind to replace or repair such goods.
(b) Except as the Association may otherwise agree, Sind shall
cause all goods and services financed out of the proceeds of the
Credit made available to it by the Borrower to be used exclusively
:or the Project.
Section 2.04. (a) Sind shall furnish, or cause to be furnished
to the Association promptly upon their preparation, the plans,
specifications, reports, contract documents and construction
and procurement schedules for part of the Project referred to in
Section 2.01 of this Agreement, and any material modifications
thereof or additions thereto, in such detail as the Association
shall reasonably request.
(b) Sind: (i) shall maintain and shall cause its agencies and
the Sind Seed Corporation to maintain records adequate to record
the progress of the Project (including the cost thereof) and to
identify the goods and services financed out of the proceeds of
the Credit made available to it by the Borrower, and to disclose
the use thereof in the Project; (ii) shall enable the Association's
representatives to visit the facilities and construction sites
included in the Project, and to examine the goods financed out
of such proceeds and any relevant records and documents; and


﻿-5-
(iii) shall furnish and shall cause to be furnished to the Associa-
tion all such information as the Association shall reasonably re-
quest concerning the Project, the expenditure of the proceeds of
the Credit so made available to it and the goods and services
financed out of such proceeds.
Section 2.05. (a) Sind shall and shall cause its agencies and
the Sind Seed Corporation to, at the request of the Association,
exchange views with the Association with regard to the progress
of the Project, the performance of its obligations under this
Agreement, and other matters relating to the purposes of the Credit.
(b) Sind shall promptly inform the Association of any condi-
tion which interferes or threatens to interfere with, the progress
of the Project, the accomplishment of the purposes of the Credit,
or the performance by Sind of its obligations under this Agreement.
Section 2.06. Sind shall ensure the effective implementation
of the Cotton Ordinance, and notify and maintain until the complet-
ion of the Project, varietal zones and segregated areas in accord-
ance with said Cotton Ordinance.
Section 2.07. Sind shall ensure that the Sind Seed Corporation
shall be free to select its distributors of seed.


﻿-6-
ARTICLE III
Financial Covenants
Section 3.01. Sind shall, and shall cause its agencies and
the Sind Seed Corporation to maintain records adequate to reflect
in accordance with consistently maintained appropriate accounting
practices the operations and financial condition in respect of
activities related to the Project.
Section 3.02. Sind shall and shall cause its agencies and the
Sind Seed Corporation to, in respect of activities related to the
Project: (i) have the accounts and financial statements (balance
sheets, statements of income and expenses and related statements)
for each fiscal year audited, in accordance with sound auditing
principles consistently applied, by independent auditors acceptable
to the Association; (ii) furnish to the Association as soon as
available, but in any case not later than five months after the
end of each such year, (A) certified copies of the financial state-
ments for such year as so audited and (B) the report of such audit
by said auditors, of such scope and in such detail as the Associa-
tion shall have reasonably requested; and (iii) furnish to the
Association such other information concerning the accounts and
financial statements of Sind, its agencies and the Sind Seed
Corporation, and the audit thereof, together with their annual
budgets, quarterly progress reports, and draft annual accounts,
as the Association shall from time to time reasonably request.


﻿-7-
Section 3.03. Sind shall at all times cause the facilities,
plants, equipment and machinery required for that portion of the
Project located in Sind to be operated, maintained, renewed and
repaired in accordance with appropriate seed industry and manage-
ment practices.

ARTICLE IV
Effective Date; Termination;
Cancellation and Suspension
Section 4.01. This Agreement shall come into force and effect
on the date upon which the Development Credit Agreement becomes
effective.
Section 4.02. (a) This Agreement and all obligations of the
Association and of Sind thereunder shall terminate on the earlier
of the following two dates:
(i)       the date on which the Development Credit Agreement
shall terminate in accordance with its terms; or
(ii)      a date 20 years after the date of this Agreement.
(b) If the Development Credit Agreement terminates in accord-
ance with its terms before the date specified in paragraph (a)(ii)
of this Section, the Association shall promptly notify Sind of
this event.
Section 4.03. All the provisions of this Agreement shall con-
tinue in full force and effect notwithstanding any cancellation or
suspension under the Development Credit Agreement.


﻿ARTICLE V
Miscellaneous Provisions
Section 5.01. Any notice or request required or permitted to
be given or made under this Agreement and any agreement between
the parties contemplated by this Agreement shall be in writing.
Such notice or request shall be deemed to have been duly given or
made when it shall be delivered by hand or by mail, telegram, cable,
telex or radiogram to the party to which it is required or permitted
to be given or made at such party's address hereinafter specified
or at such other address as such party shall have designated by
notice to the party giving such notice or making such request.

Agreement on behalf of Sind may be taken or executed by its Addi-

sons as he shall designate in writin6.
Section 5.03. Sind shall furnish to the Association sufficient
