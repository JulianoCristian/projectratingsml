AGREEMENT AMENDING THE 
DEVELOPMENT CREDIT AGREEMENT 
 
AGREEMENT dated January 22, 2003, between INDIA, acting by its President 
(the Borrower) and INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (the 
Association); 
 
WHEREAS (A) by the Development Credit Agreement dated 
September 30, 1996, as amended to date, (the Development Credit Agreement) between 
the Borrower and the Association, the Association agreed to make available to the 
Borrower an amount in various currencies equivalent to nineteen million five hundred 
thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 19,500,000) (the Credit) to assist in financing the 
Project described in Schedule 2 to the Development Credit Agreement (the Project); 
 
(B) 
the Borrower has also obtained from the Global Environment Facility 
(GEF) a grant (the Grant) in an amount equivalent to thirteen million nine hundred 
thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 13,900,000) to assist in financing the Project on 
the terms and conditions set forth in the GEF Trust Fund Grant Agreement (the Grant 
Agreement) dated September 30, 1996, entered into between the Borrower (therein 
referred to as the Recipient) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development, acting as implementing agency of the GEF (the Bank); 
 
(C) 
by the Project Agreement (the Project Agreement) dated September 30, 
1996, among the Association, the Bank and the States of Bihar, Gujarat, Karnataka, 
Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan and West Benghal (the Project States), the Project 
States agreed to undertake certain obligations with respect to the carrying out of Parts A, 
B, C and D of the Project; 
 
(D) 
the Borrower has advised the Association and the Bank through Gazette 
Notice No. 38 dated August 25, 2000, of the enactment of the Bihar Reorganization Act 
(the Act), creating the new State of Jharkhand (Jharkhand), formerly a part of Bihar;  
 
(E) 
the Borrower has advised the Association and the Bank that the Project 
activities previously to be implemented in Bihar will now be carried out in Jharkhand, 
and that Jharkhand will be assum
ing all of Bihar’s obligations under the Project; and 
 
(F) 
the Borrower has further advised the Association and the Bank that 
effective November 15, 2000, the undisbursed balance of the proceeds of the Credit and 
the Grant previously allocated to Bihar for the Project under the Development Credit 
Agreement and the Grant Agreement, respectively, shall be disbursed to Jharkhand.

NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree to amend the Development 
Credit Agreement as follows: 
 
ARTICLE I 
 
General Conditions; Definitions 
 
(i) 
The following paragraphs of Section 1.02 are amended to read as follows:  
 
“(b) 
“Project States” means the States of Jharkhand, Gujarat, Karnataka, 
Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan and West Benghal. 
 
(h) 
“Protected Areas or PAs” means: (i) Palamau Tiger Reserve in 
Jharkhand; (ii) Gir National Park and Gir Sanctuary in Gujarat; (iii) Nagarhole (Rajiv 
Gandhi) National Park in Karnataka; (iv) Periyar Tiger Reserve in Kerala; (v) Pench 
Tiger Reserve in Madhya Pradesh; (vi) Ranthambhore Tiger Reserve in Rajasthan; and 
(vii) the Buxa Tiger Reserve in West Benghal, and PA means any of those PAs;” 
 
(ii) 
The term ‘and’ is deleted from Section 1.02 (h) and the following definitions 
added at the end of Section 1.02: 
 
“(j) “Agreement Amending the Development Credit Agreement” means the 
Agreement Amending the Development Credit Agreement dated January 22, 2003, 
between the Borrower and the Association for purposes of substituting Bihar with 
Jharkand, as one of the Project States implementing Parts A, B, C and D of the Project; 
and 
 
(k) “Assumption Agreement” means the Agreement dated January 22, 2003, 
among the Association, the Bank, Jharkhand and Bihar, through which Jharkand assumes 
all obligations previously undertaken by Bihar under the Project Agreement.”. 
 
ARTICLE VI 
 
Effective Date; Termination 
 
The following provisions are added to Article VI: 
 
“Section 6.03. The Agreement Amending the Development Credit Agreement 
shall not become effective until a legal opinion satisfactory to the Association showing 
that the Assumption Agreement has been duly authorized or ratified by, and executed and 
delivered on behalf of Jharkhand, and is legally binding upon Jharkhand in accordance 
with its terms, is received by the Association.

Section 6.04. If the Agreement Amending the Development Credit Agreement 
shall not have entered into effect within ninety (90) days of the date thereof, all 
obligations of the parties hereunder shall terminate, unless the Association after due 
consideration establishes a later date for purposes of this Section, and informs the 
Borrower of such later date.”
