ASSUMPTION AGREEMENT 
 
AGREEMENT dated January 22, 2003, among the INTERNATIONAL 
DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (the Association), the INTERNATIONAL BANK 
FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (the Bank), acting as implementing 
agency of the Global Environment Facility (GEF), the STATE OF JHARKHAND 
(Jharkhand) and the STATE OF BIHAR (Bihar); 
 
WHEREAS (A) by the Development Credit Agreement dated 
September 30, 1996, as amended to date, (the Development Credit Agreement) between 
India (the Borrower) and the Association, the Association agreed to make available to the 
Borrower an amount in various currencies equivalent to nineteen million five hundred 
thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 19,500,000) (the Credit) to assist in financing the 
Project described in Schedule 2 to the Development Credit Agreement (the Project); 
 
(B) 
the Borrower has also obtained from the Global Environment Facility 
(GEF) a grant (the Grant) in an amount equivalent to thirteen million nine hundred 
thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 13,900,000) to assist in financing the Project on 
the terms and conditions set forth in the Global Environmental Facility Trust Fund Grant 
Agreement (the Grant Agreement) dated September 30, 1996, entered into between the 
Borrower (therein referred to as the Recipient) and the Bank; 
 
(C) 
by the Project Agreement (the Project Agreement) dated 
September 30, 1996 among the Association, the Bank and the States of Bihar, Gujarat, 
Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan and West Benghal (the Project States), 
the Project States agreed to undertake certain obligations with respect to the carrying out 
of Parts A, B, C and D of the Project; 
 
(D) 
the Borrower has advised the Association and the Bank through Gazette 
Notice No. 38 dated August 25, 2000, of the enactment of the Bihar Reorganization Act 
(the Act), creating the new State of Jharkhand (Jharkhand), formerly a part of Bihar; 
 
(E) 
the Borrower has advised the Association and the Bank that the Project 
activities previously to be implemented in Bihar will now be carried out in Jharkhand, 
and that Jharkhand will 
be assuming all of Bihar’s obligations under the Project; and

(F) 
Jharkhand, in consideration of the provisions of the Development Credit 
Agreement, as amended, and the Grant Agreement has agreed to assume the obligations 
of Bihar arising under the Project Agreement; 
 
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows: 
 
ARTICLE I 
 
Assumption 
 
Section 1.01. 
Jharkhand hereby assumes, and agrees to carry out and fulfill all 
of the covenants, agreements, liabilities and obligations of Bihar arising as a result of 
actions taken or omitted to be taken by Bihar under the Project Agreement prior to the 
coming into effect of the Act on November 15, 2000.  The Association and the Bank 
hereby recognize the succession of Jharkhand to the rights and obligations of Bihar under 
the Project Agreement, and Bihar is hereby substituted with Jharkhand as one of the 
Project States for all purposes under the Project, the Development Credit Agreement and 
the Grant Agreement. 
 
Section 1.02. 
All actions taken, rights acquired or obligations incurred under 
the Project Agreement by Bihar shall be valid and binding on Jharkhand as though 
Jharkhand had been Bihar. 
 
Section 1.03. 
Notwithstanding the provisions of Sections 1.01. and 1.02 above, 
the coming into effect of this Assumption Agreement shall not affect the consequences of 
any action taken or omitted to be taken by the Association, the Bank or Bihar under the 
Project Agreement prior to the coming into effect of the Act on November 15, 2000. 
 
Section 1.04. 
Bihar agrees that Jharkhand shall now receive the undisbursed 
balance of the Credit and the Grant previously allocated to Bihar for the Project under the 
Development Credit Agreement and the Grant Agreement, respectively, effective 
November 15, 2000. 
 
ARTICLE II 
 
Effective Date; Termination 
 
Section 2.01. 
This Assumption Agreement shall come into force and effect on 
the date upon which the Agreement Amending the Development Credit Agreement 
becomes effective.
