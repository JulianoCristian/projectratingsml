                         Implementation Status Overview
                         NELSAP Regional Agricultural Trade and Productivity Project (RATP) is a regional technical assistance grant to the Nile Basin Initiative (NBI) Nile Equatorial Lakes Subsidiary Action
                         Program (NELSAP). The RATP has procured all major studies to be accomplished and the majority of them have been successfully completed.
