                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document): The project development objective is to minimize the threat HPAI infection and other
                         zoonoses posed to humans and the poultry industry in Tajikistan, and to prepare for, control and respond to influenza pandemics and other infectious disease
                         emergencies in humans.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project successfully completed all envisaged activities prior to the Closing Date of August 31, 2010, and the project succeeded in accomplishing the following major project
                         outcomes: (a) establishment of an intersectoral surveillance system for both animals and humans; (b) increased awareness of Avian Influenza risks and mitigation measures among

                                                                                                                                                                                                            Page 1 of 7

                         public; and (c) strengthened institutional capacity and disease surveillance diagnostic/containment capacity of the country's Veterinary and Health Sectors. The country's overall
                         preparedness in handling emergencies was particularly evident in the promptness with which Tajikistan managed addressing the polio outbreak in the summer of 2008 and the
