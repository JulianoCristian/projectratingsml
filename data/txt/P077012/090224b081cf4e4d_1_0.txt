                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project is very much on track towards achieving its overall development objectives as indicated by the status of implementation and progress made in terms of the key
                         performance indicators. The project continues to promote participatory local development planning. The project likewise sustained its effort to improve the engagement between the
                         community and the municipal local government unit (MLGU).
