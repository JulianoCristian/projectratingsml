Re:
Social Development and Public Works Project
(Credit No. 3225-DJI) - Amendment to Credit Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the credit agreement relating to the Social Development and Public 
Works Project (the Project) between the Republic of Djibouti (the "Borrower") and the 
International Development Association (the "Association"), dated June 10, 1999 (the 
"Credit Agreement"). We also refer to the understanding between the Borrower and the 
Association regarding the extension of the scope of the Project to include a health 
component.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association has acceded to the Borrower's 
request. Accordingly, the Credit Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
1.
Article 1 (General Conditions; Definitions)
Section 1.02 (q) shall read as follows:  “Project Preparation Advance” means the 
project preparation advance granted by the Association to the Borrower pursuant to 
exchanges of letters dated respectively June 2, 1993; February 21, 1994; October 24, 
1995; December 16, 1998 and January 2, 1999, between the Borrower and the 
Association”.
2.

3.
Schedule 2 (Description of the Project): A Part D is added to read as follows:
“Part D:
Health Sector Development:
Development of a national health plan and strengthening of the Borrower’s 
Ministry of 
Health through the provision of consultants’ services, training and 
equipment”.
4.
The refinancing date of the  Project Preparation Advance granted by the 
Association pursuant to the exchange of letters dated June 2, 1993; February 21, 1994; 
October 24, 1995, is hereby extended to January 31, 2000.
5.
All sections of the Credit Agreement that have not been amended hereby shall 
remain in full force and effect. This Letter Amendment is effective as of the day of 
its countersignature.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the 
confirmation on the enclosed copy of this Letter Amendment and returning it to the 
Association.
