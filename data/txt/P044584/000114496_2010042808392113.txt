Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Social Development and Public 
Works Project), between the Republic of Djibouti (the Borrower) and the International 
Development Association (the Association), dated June 10, 1999, as amended. We also 
refer to your request included in your letter dated November 20, 2000 aiming at 
increasing the authorized allocation for the Special Account and the understanding 
reached between the Borrower and the Association included in the conclusions of the 
Association’s Aide-Mémoire dated February 29, 2000 to facilitate Project 
implementation.
We are pleased to inform you that, to facilitate project implementation, the 
Association has acceded to the above request. Accordingly, the Development Credit 
Agreement is hereby amended as follows:

(ii)
Paragraph 2(c) is modified to read as follows:
"The term "Operating Costs" under Category (5) means the incremental 
operating costs incurred on account of Project implementation, including 
office supplies, communication costs and travel allowances and costs."
(iii)
Paragraph 2 will include the following additional point (d):
"The term "ADETIP’s management fees" means the remuneration received by 
ADETIP for the execution of the Project. These fees will amount to up to 10% 
of the expenditures effected by ADETIP for the execution of all the components 
under 
its direct responsibility."
(iv)
Paragraph 4 is modified to read as follows:
"The Association may require withdrawals from the Credit Account to be 
made on the basis of statements of expenditure for expenditures for: (i) goods 
under contracts costing less than $100,000 equivalent each; (ii) works under 
contracts costing less than $150,000 equivalent each; (iii) services of 
consulting firms under contracts not exceeding $50,000 equivalent; (iv) 
services of individual consultants under contracts not exceeding $10,000 
equivalent; and (v) for Community Subprojects, Operating Costs and training; 
all under such terms and conditions as the Association shall specify by notice 
to the 
Borrower."

(ii)
