          Re:  Credit No. 2149-TA (Integrated Roads Project).
               Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Sir:
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the
abovementioned Project dated September 28,1990, between the United
Republic of Tanzania and the International Development Association
and to Professor S. Mbilinyi's telex dated August 7, 1991,
requesting amendments to the Project to provide for the completion
of three sections of the TANZAM highway.
     This letter is to inform you of our agreement to your request
and to propose the following amendments to the Development Credit
Agreement:
     1.   The date in Section 3.06 is amended to read December 31,
1991.
     2.   The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is amended to
          read as appears in the Annex to this letter.
     3.   Part B.l. of Schedule 2 is amended by the addition of a
          new Part as follows:
          "(d) Rehabilitation of three sections of the TANZAM
          highway totalling about 222 km."
     4.   The date in Paragraph 7 of Schedule 4, is amended to
          read December 31, 1991.
     All other provisions of the Development Credit Agreement
remain in full force and effect.
     Your telex of August 7, 1991, and this letter shall constitute
an agreement between the Borrower and the Association and the
amendment shall become effective as of the date of this letter.
                              Yours sincerely,
                              Chaim Helman, Acting Director
                              Southern Africa Department
