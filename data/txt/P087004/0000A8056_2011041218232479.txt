                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The proposed Credit will enhance the Borrower's road sector Management. This will be achieved through: (i) institutional reform of the road sector and
                         improved functioning of related civil works markets; and (ii) better access to social and economic opportunities due to improved mobility for affected
                         populationss.
