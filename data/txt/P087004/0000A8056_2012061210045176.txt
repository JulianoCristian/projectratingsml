                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Implementation is progressing rapidly with all civil works, under the original and first AF, completed and implementation of institutional reforms well advanced.
                         A second additional credit of USD 10 million to support scaled up activities was signed on May 4, 2011, and became effective on July 20, 2011. The AF will finance the following
                         activities: (i) Rehabilitation of Praia-Cidade Velha road in the island of Santiago, (ii) rehabilitation of 7 km of rural roads in the island of Santiago, and (iii) Capacity strengthening of the
                         Road Agency (Institudo Estradas) in the form of Technical Assistance (TA). The upgrading works for Cidade Velha - Praia road (financed under the second AF) have started. The
