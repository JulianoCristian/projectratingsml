National Highways Rehabilitation and Maintenance Project 
(Loan No. 4295-AR) 
 
Second Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency: 
 
Please refer  to the Loan Agreement dated October 28, 1998 entered into between the Argentine 
Republic (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), as 
amended. 
 
In response to the Borrower’s May 13, 2002 request to: (a) extend the Closing Date from 
December 31, 2003 to December 31, 2004; (b) increase the percentage of expenditures to be financed 
under Category (2) set forth in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement; and (c) 
create a new disbursement category to finance in part the carrying out of Part A.3 of the Project, and 
further discussions on said subjects during the November 25-26, 2002 portfolio review meeting between 
the representatives of the Borrower and the Bank, the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement, 
as follows: 
 
I. 
Loan Agreement
:
1. 
Section 2.03 is hereby amended in its entirety to read as follows: 
 
“Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be December 31, 2004 or such later date as the 
Bank shall establish. The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower of such later date.”. 
 
2. 
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended in its entirety to read as set 
forth in annex 1 to this Amendment Letter. 
 
3. 
Part C of Section I of Schedule 4 is hereby amended by: (a) allowing certain maintenance 
works under Part A.3 of the Project to be procured under national competitive bidding 
procedures; and (b) adding a new procurement methodology immediately after Part C.2, all of 
which shall read as follows: 
 
“1. National Competitive Bidding
Page  2
(a) Goods estimated to cost less than $350,000 equivalent per contract or per bid 
package, up to an aggregate amount not to exceed $900,000 equivalent, may be procured under 
contracts awarded in accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 3.3. and 3.4 of the Guidelines. 
 
(b) Works under Part A.3 of the Project, up to an aggregate amount not to exceed 
$15,000,000 equivalent, may be procured under contracts awarded in accordance with the 
provisions of paragraphs 3.3. and 3.4 of the Guidelines. 
 
(c) In the procurement of goods and works in accordance with this Part, standard 
bidding documents acceptable to the Bank shall be used.  
 
3. Force Account
Works under Part A.3 of the Project which meet the requirements of paragraph 3.8 of 
the Guidelines, and costing $15,000,000 equivalent or less in the aggregate, may, with the Bank's 
prior agreement, be carried out by the Borrower’s provinces on behalf of DNV through force 
account based on unit price contracts in accordance with the provisions of said paragraph of the 
Guidelines.”. 
 
4. 
The reference to Part C.1 in paragraph 2 (a) (ii) under Part D of Section I of Schedule 4 is 
hereby replaced with the reference Part C.1 (a). 
 
5. 
Paragraph 1 (a) of Schedule 5 is hereby amended in its entirety to read as follows: 
 
“1. (a) the term ‘eligible Categories’ means Categories (1) through (5) set forth in 
the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement;”. 
 
II. 
Effectiveness
:
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall be an original. 
Upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this Amendment Letter, this amendment will 
become effective as of the date first above written.  Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing 
amendment by signing and dating the two counterparts of this Amendment Letter.
