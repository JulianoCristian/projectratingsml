Credit No. 2110-KE:  Fourth Population Project Amendment to Development Credit 
Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) for the Fourth 
Population Project, dated May 21, 1990, between the Republic of Kenya (the Borrower) 
and the International Development Association (the Association), and to the 
understandingagreement reached between the BorrowerMinistry of Health and the 
Association during February, 1998, the last supervision mission in February, 1998, 
regarding a minor adjustment of the disbursement percentage applicable to incremental 
operating costs underan amendment to the Agreement.
Pursuant to that understanding, we are pleased to inform you that the 
Association proposes to amend the Agreement by deleting the table in paragraph 1 of 
Schedule 1 thereto, and substituting therefor the table attached in the attachment to 
this letter.  This is to inform you of our agreement and to propose the following 
amendment to the Agreement: the table in the first paragraph of Schedule 1 is amended 
to read as in the Attachment to this letter.  This will adjust the percentage of 
expenditures eligible for financing under Category 6 (Incremental Operational Costs) 
from 100% to 95%.
Please confirm your agreement with the proposed amendment by signing, dating, 
and returning the enclosed copy of this letter.  The amendment shall be effective as 
of the date of this letter.
Sincerely,
Harold E. Wackman
Country Director for Kenya
World Bank, Kenya
