                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project's implementation pace and disbursement rate has improved. The closing date was extended until December 31, 2014. Among the important results in terms of
                         mainstreaming biodiversity in governmental sectors, to be highlighted is the adoption in 2012 of the National Policy on Agroecology and Organic Production and the related National
                         Plan to be officially launched on June 2013,which has as one of its objectives adding value to agro-biodiversity and socio-biodiversity products while stimulating local experiences in
