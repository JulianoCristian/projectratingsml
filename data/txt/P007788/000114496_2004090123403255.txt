Re:
Nicaragua:  Credit No. 2871-NI (Road Rehabilitation and 
Maintenance Project) 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Road Rehabilitation and 
Maintenance Project) dated July 19, 1996 between the Republic of Nicaragua (the 
Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association).
Pursuant to your request in letter dated June 9, 1998, we agree to amend the 
Development Credit Agreement as follows:
1.
Section 3.07 is amended by adding the words “community organizations and” before 
the word “Microenterprises”;
2.
Schedule 1:
(a)
the table in paragraph 1 is amended by reallocating: (i)  SDR460,000 from 
Category (5) to Subcategory (1) (b) Works under Part B of the Project; and (ii) SDR 
60,000 from Category (5) Unallocated to Subcategory 4 (b) Training under Part C.4 of 
the Project; and
(b)
paragraph 3 (b) the words “and community organizations” are added after 
the word “Microenterprises” in the third and fifth lines;
3.
Schedule 2 Part C.4 is amended by deleting the words “in routine” and replacing 
it by the words “and community organizations in”; and
4.
Schedule 3.  Part C.2.
(a)
the amount “$1,100,000” is deleted and replaced by $2,100,000; and
(b)
the words “and community organizations” are added after the word 
“Microenterprises”.
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing this 
letter in the space provided and returning it to us.  This amendment shall be executed 
in two counterparts, each of which shall constitute an original.  The amendment shall 
become effective upon receipt by the Association of one fully executed original of 
this letter and shall be applied retroactively as of May 15, 1998.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
Ian Bannon 
Acting Director 
Central America Country Management Unit
