                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project's development objective is to support improved performance in the planning, delivery and sustained provision of priority municipal services and
                         infrastructure by urban local governments.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is expected to be implemented in the current project time line. The project has disbursed about 100% of the original credit ($150 million), and about 126 million ($ 86%) of
                         the additional credit. The undisbursed balance is only US$20 million, and is expected to be disbursed before the end of June 2014. Most infrastructure works are completed, and
                         those remaining will be completed by the end of the fiscal year
