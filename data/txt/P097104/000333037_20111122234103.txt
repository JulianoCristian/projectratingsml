7a. Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
The development objective is to contribute to the improvement of the overall quality and performance of
teachers through enhancing teachers' knowledge of subject matter and pedagogical skills in the classroom.
Improvement of teacher quality and performance is to be measured by: (i) the increased number of teachers
meeting academic qualifications mandated by the Teacher Law; (ii) the increased number of primary and
junior secondary teachers in BERMUTU Districts using classroom instruction with specific subject and age
appropriate pedagogy; and (iii) the reduction in absenteeism of teachers in BERMUTU Districts.
