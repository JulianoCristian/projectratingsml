                                                             May 26, 1992
The Secretary to the Government
  of Pakistan
Economic Affairs Division
Islamabad, Pakistan
      Re:   Loan 2982-PAK (Private Sector Energy Development
            Project) Amendment of the Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
      We refer to the Loan Agreement (Private Sector Energy Development
Project) between the Islamic Republic of Pakistan (the Borrower) and the
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) dated
August 8, 1988, and to your Ministry's letter dated January 22,  1992
requesting the Bank for an amendment to the description of the Project in
order to enable the provision of financial advisory services to the Water
and Power Development Authority at Pakistan (WAPDA) to facilitate its
privatization efforts.
      The Bank is pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, it
has agreed to amend Part B (iv) of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement so
that it reads as follows:
      "(iv) to WAPDA, for: (aa) negotiating the purchase of power
      produced by Investment Projects, and training staff for this
      purpose, and (bb) supporting its privatization efforts."
      Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by
signing and dating the enclosed confirmation copy of this letter, and by
returning it to us for our records.  This amendment  will become
effective as of the date hereof upon receipt of your confirmation in this
regard.
                                Sincerely,
                               Paul Isenman
                                 Director
                          Country Department III
                             South Asia Region
