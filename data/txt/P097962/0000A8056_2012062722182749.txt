                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project development objective is to improve access to enhanced quality of education, alternate learning opportunities, development of life skills and basic
                         occupational skill training to primary school students, IDPs, demobilized soldiers, and other non-traditional learners.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         ERP has continued making significant implementation progress. The implementation of ERP is on track with most of the main project activities either completed or nearing
                         completion. The major activities completed include: Educational Management training of 317 head teachers and State Ministry of education officials and 40 Ministry of Education

                         head office staff and officials; an Intensive English Training of 917 teachers and education staff with Arabic Education background; a fast-track training of 1228 new teachers and a
                         pre-service training of 951 teachers; a pilot pastoralist education of 6416 youth and adults and training of 1000 Alternative Education teachers and 150 Local Education authorities. In
                         addition, the construction of 52 Primary Schools is completed, with 32 handed over, 18 undergoing repairs after final inspection and 2 schools being in defects liability period. The
                         construction of 14 County Education Centers is completed, with 11 handed over and 3 due for final inspection for final payment and hand over. The implementation of the following
