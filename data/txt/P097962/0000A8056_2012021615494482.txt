                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project development objective is to improve access to enhanced quality of education, alternate learning opportunities, development of life skills and basic
                         occupational skill training to primary school students, IDPs, demobilized soldiers, and other non-traditional learners.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         ERP has made substantial implementation progress since the last mission of mid-July 2011.This is reflected in the large increase in its cumulative disbursement. As of the end of
                         October, 2011, cumulative disbursement from MDTF reached $23.3 million, representing about 91.0% of the total MDTF allocation. Including Government contribution, the overall

                         In terms of the component performance, the Construction and AES Components have made most significant progress while the implementation progress under the teacher training
                         and Capacity Building components have been moderate.

                         With the remaining funds available because of savings, the Ministry will be undertaking the following additional activities, all of which will are in line with the Project Development
                         Objective: purchase of furniture for all schools built under the project and an expansion of the Intensive English Courses under the Alternative Education System component. These
                         activities will be completed by June 30, 2012, the project completion date.
