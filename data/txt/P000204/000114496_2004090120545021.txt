BURUNDI:  Transport Sector Project (Credit No. 2105 BU) 
   Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement), dated April 20, 
1990, as amended between the Government of Burundi (the Borrower) and International 
Development Association (the Association) for the above-mentioned Project, as well as 
your recent request for extension during the June 17-21, 1997 mission in Washington.  
I am pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to your request and has 
established October 31, 1997 as the later date for purposes of Section 2.03 of the 
Agreement.  A further extension may be granted in accordance with the attached letter 
from Mr. Tcheyan.
All other provisions of the Agreement shall remain in full force and effect.  
This amendment shall become effective on the date of countersignature by you or your 
authorized representative.  If you agree to the proposed amendment, kindly sign, date 
and return the enclosed copy of this letter to us.
Sincerely,
By /s/ Praful Patel
Acting Regional Vice President
