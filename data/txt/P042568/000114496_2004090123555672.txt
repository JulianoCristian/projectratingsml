Re:
Coastal Wetlands Protection and Development Project (CR 3292 - VN)
Amendments to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Governor Thuy,
I refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated February 24, 2000 between 
Socialist Republic of Vietnam (the Borrower) and International Development Association 
(the Association) for the Coastal Wetlands Protection and Development project (the 
Project) (the Credit Agreement).  I further refer to our letter dated July 11, 2001 
and a communication from State Bank of Vietnam dated September 7, 2001, both 
discussing the needs for certain amendments to the Credit Agreement to enable more 
effective implementation of the Project.
I am pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, the Association agrees 
to modify the Credit Agreement as follows:
1.
The date of March 31, 2000 set forth in Section 5.01 (a) of the Credit Agreement 
is changed to February 28, 2001.
2.
Paragraph 1 of Part C, Section II, Schedule 3 to the Credit Agreement is amended 
to read as follows:
"1.
Quality-based Selection
Services for independent monitoring of the RAP under Part D of the 
Project, and services for monitoring and evaluation under Part C.4 (ii) of the 
Project shall be procured under contracts awarded with the provisions of 
paragraphs 3.1 through 3.4 of the Consultant Guidelines."
3.
Paragraph 2 of Part C, Section II of said Schedule 3 is amended to read as 
follows:
"2.
Single Source
Services for: (a) forest inventory and forest planting and protection 
design work under Parts A.1 and A.2 of the Project; (b) carrying out adaptive 
research studies and development of technology packages under Part B.1 of the 
Project; (c) carrying out land and water use studies under Part C.1 of the 
Project; and (d) delivery of training, planning and implementation of the RAP 
under Part D of the Project; may, with the Association’s prior agreement, be 
procured under contracts awarded in accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 
3.8 through 3.11 of the Consultant Guidelines." 
4.
Two new paragraphs are added to Part C, Section II of said Schedule 3 as 
follows:
"4.
Least-cost Selection
Services for audits, engineering design of non-complex works, 
construction supervision and other assignments of routine nature, estimated to 
cost less than $100,000 equivalent per contract may be procured under contracts 
awarded in accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 3.1 and 3.6 of the 
Consultant Guidelines.
5.
Selection Based on Consultants’ Qualifications
Services for the design and construction supervision of small works, 
Page  2
estimated to cost less than $100,000 equivalent per contract, may be procured 
under contracts awarded in accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 3.1 and 
3.7 of the Consultant Guidelines." 
5.
The date of December 31, 2000 set forth in paragraph 9 of Schedule 4 to the 
Credit Agreement, Implementation Program, is changed to June 30, 2002.
6.
The date of December 31, 2000 set forth in paragraph 12 of said Schedule 4, is  
changed to June 30, 2002.
7.
The date of March 1, 2002 set forth in paragraph 13 (b) of said Schedule 4, is 
changed to December 31, 2002.
8.
The date of April 1, 2002 set forth in paragraph 13 (c) of said Schedule 4, is 
changed to January 31, 2003
Except as specifically amended hereby, all terms and provisions of the Credit 
Agreement otherwise remain in full force and effect.
Please indicate your agreement with the terms of this letter of amendment by 
countersigning and dating the form of confirmation below and returning one signed 
original copy of this letter for our files. Upon our receipt of a copy of this letter 
of amendment duly countersigned by an authorized representative of the Borrower this 
letter of amendment shall enter into effect as of the date of countersignature.
