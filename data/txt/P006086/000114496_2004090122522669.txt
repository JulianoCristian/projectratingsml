Re: 
BARBADOS: (Second Road Maintenance and Rehabilitation Project) Loan No. 
3493-BAR
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated July 17, 1992 entered into between 
Barbados (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development 
(the Bank), as amended.
In view of the fact that the Borrower has not been able to privatize the public 
transport services to the rural areas, the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan 
Agreement as follows:
1.
The definition of the term "Action Plan" set forth in paragraph (a) of 
Section 1.02 is hereby deleted.
2.
Section 3.07 is hereby deleted.
3.
Paragraph 3 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended by: (a) replacing the 
"semi-colon" and the word "and" at the end of paragraph (a) with a "period" ; and (b) 
deleting paragraph (b).
4.
The objective of the Project referred to in paragraph (e) of Schedule 2 
is hereby amended to read as follows:
"(e)
;transferring the provision of passenger services in urban areas 
to the private sector.".
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall 
be an original.  Upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this 
Amendment Letter, these amendments will be effective as of the date first above 
written.  Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and 
dating the two counterparts of this Amendment Letter.
