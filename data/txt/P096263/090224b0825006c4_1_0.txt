                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objectives of the Project are to: (i) improve the efficiency and effectiveness of land administration and management through enhanced tenure security and improved
                         urban planning; and (ii) rebuild physical assets and restore essential services in the flood affected areas.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This project closes on June 30, 2014. While the 2013 elections, change in government, and changes in IPRO staff slowed down progress in Component A, IPRO management
                         support has resulted in some positive developments over the last few months. Component B (Urban Land Management) and C (Municipal Investments) have been completed
                         satisfactorily. Component A (Security of Tenure and Registration of Immovable Property Rights) continues with the program of automation of offices. All 10 of the target of 10 offices
