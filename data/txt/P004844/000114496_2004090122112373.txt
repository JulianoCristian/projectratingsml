Amendment to the Credit Agreement
Dear Governor Thuy:
I refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated September 8, 1998, between the 
Socialist Republic of Vietnam (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association), for the above-captioned Project (the Credit Agreement).  
I also refer to the your letter dated June 20, 2001, requesting certain amendments to 
the Credit Agreement.
I am pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, the Association has 
agreed to your request and, therefore, the Credit Agreement is hereby amended as 
follows:
1.

2.
Sub-paragraph (c) of paragraph 2 of Schedule 1 to the Credit Agreement shall be 
amended to read as follows:
"(c) the term "field activities" means survey and mapping activities for land 
allocation, research field trials and crop and livestock demonstrations under Parts 
A.2, B and C of the Project; and"
3.
Sub-paragraph (d) of Part A.1 of Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement shall be 
amended to read as follows:
"(d) construction and rehabilitation of farm access tracks, to develop about 
40,000 hectares (ha) of new smallholder rubber plantations, each of about 1 to 10 ha 
of cultivable area, in the Central Project Provinces (except in Binh Dinh and Khanh 
Hoa provinces), and rehabilitation and improvement of about 17,600 ha of existing 
smallholder rubber plantations, each of about 1 to 10 ha of cultivable area, in the 
Central Project Provinces (except in Binh Dinh, Khanh Hoa and Phu Yen provinces)."
4.
Part A.2 of Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement shall be amended to read as 
follows:
"2.
Development and implementation of an adaptive research program to test 
and recommend agricultural technical practices for rubber plantations, annual and 
perennial crops and farming systems in the Central Project Provinces."
5.
Part B of Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement shall be amended to read as 
follows:
"Development and implementation of an agricultural land allocation program in 
the communes and villages in the Central Project Provinces, including survey and 
mapping of the land parcels, issuance of land use rights certificates to villagers, 
and provision of related equipment."
6.
Sub-paragraph (c) of Part C.1 of Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement shall be 
amended to read as follows:
"(c) technical support for livestock and poultry production, including field 
demonstrations;"
7.
Sub-paragraph (f) of Part C.2 of Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement shall be 
amended to read as follows:
"(f) vehicles, equipment and materials to develop a crop intensification and 
diversification program of annual and perennial crops in the Central Project 
Provinces." 
8.
Paragraph 4 of Part C of Section I of Schedule 3 to the Credit Agreement shall 
be amended to read as follows:
"4.
Force Account
Land surveying and mapping activities for land allocation under Part B of 
the Project, and research field trials and crop and livestock demonstrations under 
Parts A.2 and C of the Project, which meet the requirements of paragraph 3.8 of the 
Guidelines and costing $8,400,000 equivalent or less in the aggregate, may, with the 
Association’s prior agreement, be carried out by force account in accordance with the 
provisions of said paragraph of the Guidelines."
9.
Paragraph 5 of Schedule 4 to the Credit Agreement shall be amended to read as 
follows:
"5.
The Borrower shall cause RRIV and other qualified institutions, 
acceptable to the Association, to carry out the adaptive research program under Part 
A.2 of the Project in a manner and substance satisfactory to the Association."
Except as specifically amended by the provisions of this letter of amendment, 
all terms and conditions of the Credit Agreement otherwise remain in full force and 
Page  3
effect.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing on behalf of the Borrower, by 
countersigning, dating and returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter of 
amendment.  Upon receipt by the Association of the countersigned copy of this letter 
of amendment, this letter of amendment shall become effective as of the date of the 
countersignature.
