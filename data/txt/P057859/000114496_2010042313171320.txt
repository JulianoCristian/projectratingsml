Re:
HONDURAS (Grant for the Preparation of an Institutional 
Strengthening in Coastal and Marine Resources Management)
Trust Fund No. TF025317
Amendment to the Letter Agreement 
Excellency:
Please refer to the Letter Agreement from the International Development 
Association (the Association) to the Republic of Honduras (the Recipient) duly signed 
and countersigned by the Association and Recipient’s representatives on July 15, 1999.
In response to the Recipient’s request of June 25, 2001 to finance with the 
proceeds of the Grant the design of the financial management system for the Project 
and the preparation of a Project implementation manual, the Association hereby agrees 
to amend paragraph 1 of the Annex to the Letter Agreement by: (a) replacing the 
"period" at the end of subparagraph "(f)"  by a "semicolon" and the word "and"; and 
(b) adding two new subparagraphs after subparagraph "(f)" which shall read as follows:
"(g)
design of the financial management system for the Project; and
 (h)
preparation of the Project’s implementation manual.".
This Amendment Letter shall: (a) be executed in two counterparts each of which 
shall be an original; and (b) become effective on the date above written once the 
Association has received one fully executed original of this Amendment Letter. 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
the two counterparts of this Amendment Letter.
