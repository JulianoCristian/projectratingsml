                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The overall implementation progress of the project has been upgraded to Moderately Satisfactory and the project is likely to acheive its development objective.The mission noted
                         good progress on safety aspects, financial management, progress on removing encumbrances and environmental management of works. Howevere, mission was concerned to note
                         unsatisfactory contract/project management by NHAI; poor perfromance of one contract (5) and non-compliance to legal covenants.
