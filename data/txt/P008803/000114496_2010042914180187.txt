Dear Minister Kassianov:
Re:
Energy Efficiency Project (Loan No. 3876 RU)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
We refer to the Loan Agreement No.3876 RU for the Energy Efficiency Project 
between the Russian Federation and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), dated September 29, 1996, as amended (the Loan Agreement). We 
also refer to the letter from Minister M. Zadornov dated February 15, 1999, requesting 
certain amendments to the Loan Agreement.
We hereby confirm the Bank's agreement to amend the Loan Agreement as follows: 
1.
Section 1.02(b) is amended to read: “Executing Agency” means any system of 
district heating or combined heat and power plants, any oblast or city 
administrations, or other entities as may be agreed with the Bank, in the territory of 
the Borrower in accordance with the criteria set forth in Schedule 6 to this 
Agreement, which shall have been accepted for participation in accordance with Section 
3.02 of this Agreement.”
2.
Section 1.02 (d) is amended to read:
“Investenergoeffect” means a closed joint stock company, organized and 
established 
under the Federal Law on Joint Stock Companies (N 208-FZ), dated December 
26, 1995, and the 
Civil Code of the Russian Federation (N 51-FZ), dated November 30, 
1994, and operating on 
the basis of its charter registered on August 22, 1997 with 
the Registration Chamber of the 
Moscow Government, and such term shall include any 
successor or successors thereto.” The 
term “RESF” is hereby deleted and 
replaced with the term “Investenergoeffect” throughout the 
Loan Agreement.
3.
In Section 4.02 (b), the phrase “The Borrower shall cause Executing Agencies to” 
is amended to read: “The Borrower shall cause Executing Agencies, in connection with 
their respective Energy Efficiency Sub-projects, with the exception of any Energy 
Efficiency Sub-projects estimated to cost the equivalent of $5,000,000 or less, or any 
Energy Efficiency Sub-projects carried out by a city administration or oblast of the 
Borrower, to:”. 
4.
The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is deleted, and a new table is 
inserted as per Annex to this Letter of Amendment.
5.
A new sub-paragraph (c) is added to paragraph 2 of Schedule 1 to read:
“the term 'Incremental operating costs' means expenses incurred by 
Investenergoeffect 
on account of Project implementation for Investenergoeffect staff 
salaries and allowances, 
travel, office rent, office renovation and maintenance, 
transport, utilities and 
telecommunications, office equipment, annual 
audit costs and other related expenses, as 
agreed with the Bank.”
6.
A new paragraph (3) is added to Part B of Schedule 2 to read: “Financing of 
incremental operating costs in support of Project implementation.”
7.
Part B(1) of Schedule 5 is amended to read: “Interest rate of 2 percent above 
Bank's rate.” 8.
Schedule 6 is hereby deleted, and replaced with a new Schedule 6. 
“SCHEDULE 6 
Selection Criteria for Executing Agencies

1.
Executing Agencies shall be located in the following oblasts of the Borrower, or 
other oblasts and city administrations, as agreed between the Bank and the Borrower: 
(a)
Ryazan; (b)
Nizhny Novgorod; (c)
Arkhangelsk; and (d)
Kaliningrad.
2.
In order for Executing Agencies to become eligible for participation in the 
Project, the Borrower shall cause each Executing Agency, by no later than November 15, 
1999, to submit to the Bank: 
(a)
a final feasibility study for a proposed Energy Efficiency Sub-project, 
agreed upon with the Bank; and 
(b)
a signed Subsidiary Loan Agreement.” Please confirm your agreement with 
the foregoing amendment by signing, dating and returning to us the enclosed copy of 
this letter. This amendment will become effective as of the date of the 
countersignature, upon receipt by the Bank of the countersigned copy of this letter.
