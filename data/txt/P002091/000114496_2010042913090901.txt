Re:  Loan No. 3034 UNI
     (Imo Health and Population Project)
     Amendment of Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
     Please refer to the Loan Agreement (Imo Health and Population
Project) dated May 2, 1989 between the Federal Republic of Nigeria
and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development.  To
facilitate the carrying out of the Project, the Bank hereby proposes
that the Loan Agreement for the above-named Project be amended in
Schedule 1 as follows:
     (a)  by deleting in paragraph 1 of that Schedule Category (5)
and substituting for that Category the following new Category:

     (b)  by adding next after paragraph 1 the following new
paragraph and renumbering the existing paragraph 2 as paragraph 3:
     "2.  For the purposes of this Schedule:
     (a)  the term "foreign expenditures" means expenditures in
          the currency of any country other than that of the
          Borrower for goods or services supplied from the
          territory of any country other than that of the
          Borrower; and
     (b)  the term "local expenditures" means expenditures in the
          currency of the Borrower or for goods or services
          supplied from the territory of the Borrower."
     Kindly indicate your agreement to the amendments proposed
above by signing the form of confirmation on the enclosed copy of
this letter and returning it to us.
