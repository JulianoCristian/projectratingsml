                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         Improve the reliability of the Province's road sector assets and the efficiency of their management as a means to support the Province's competitiveness and economic
                         growth, by building up institutional capacity to encourage efficient planning policies and adequate resource allocation for the rehabilitation and maintenance of the core

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is almost complete with disbursements at 97 percent. The Bank approved a one-year extension of the closing date (original closing date October 31, 2012) to enable the
                         completion of all project activities (CREMA 3 and institutional strengthening component). This request for extension, which has been the first extension of the project, was also agreed
                         with the federal Government. The new closing date is now October 31 2013. The Institutional Strengthening activities are significantly advanced with use of remaining funds being
                         prioritized for the purchase of needed road safety equipment (alcohol breath analyzers and IT equipment related to road safety enforcement as well as support to the procurement of
