Re:
Romania: Bucharest Water Supply Project (Loan Number 4079 RO)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
We refer to the Loan Agreement dated August 2, 1996, for the above project, 
between the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and 
Romania (the Borrower), as amended on September 15, 2000, and your letters dated March 
30, 2001, where you propose an amendment to the same.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank agrees to make the requested 
amendment.  Accordingly, the Loan Agreement is proposed to be amended as set forth 
below:
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement, is amended to read 
as shown in Attachment 1 to this letter.
Please indicate your agreement to the above amendment to the Loan Agreement by 
signing and returning to us the attached copy of this letter.  This amendment will 
become effective upon receipt of a countersigned copy of this letter.
Sincerely,
