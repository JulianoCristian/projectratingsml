September 15, 1992
The Secretary
Economic Relations Division
Ministry of Finance
Government of the People's
 Republic of Bangladesh
Dhaka, Bangladesh
Re:
Bangladesh - Export Development Project (Cr 2000-BD)
 
Dear Sirs:
      Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated April 26, 1989 between 
the People's Republic of Bangladesh and the International Development Association 
(hereinafter the  "Association") for the above referenced project.  In response to 
your request and the discussions between our respective representatives, we are 
pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to amend the Development Credit 
Agreement for purposes of changing certain terms and conditions of Sub-loans as 
defined in such Agreement.
      Accordingly, the Association proposes hereby to amend the Development Credit 
Agreement as follows:
1.    Schedule 3 to the Development Credit Agreement is amended as follows:
      I.
The amount "$200,000" in paragraph A.5 is deleted and the following 
amount substituted therefore: "$500,000".
      II.
The amount "$500,000" in paragraph A.6 is deleted and the following 
amount substituted therefore: "$1,000,000".
      III.
Paragraph B.4 is deleted.
 
      Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment may be executed in two 
counterparts, each of which shall be an original. Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us. This amendment will be effective as of the 
date of this letter upon receipt by the Association of one fully executed original of 
this amendment.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
By /s/ Jochen Kraske
Director
Country Department I
South Asia Region
CONFIRMED:
PEOPLE'S REPUBLIC OF BANGLADESH
By /s/ Ayub Quadri
Authorized Representative
