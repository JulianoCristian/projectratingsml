Agreement contained, on the terms and conditions set forth
therein;

agree to the modification of some covenants, agreements and
obligations in the Loan Agreement and in the Project Agreement
contained and to enter into this agreement (hereinafter called
this Agreement) to reflect such modification; and
WHEREAS upon the basis, inter alia, of the foregoing the
Borrower, the Bank and INAN have agreed to enter into this
Agreement upon the terms and conditions hereinafter set forth;
NOW, THEREFORE, it is hereby agreed as follows:
