Cr. 3348-AM (Social Investment Fund II Project) 
 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement  
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Social Investment Fund II), dated May 19, 
2000 between the Republic of Armenia (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association). We also refer to your letters of November 11, 2002 and November 
12, 2002 to the Association requesting a reallocation of funds and changes to the procurement 
schedule to complete reconstruction works in the earthquake zone. We are pleased to inform you, 
that the Association has acceded to your request and agrees to the following amendments: 
 
1. 
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement is revised 
to read as set forth in the Attachment to this Letter.  
 
2. 
Paragraph 2 of Part C, Section 1 of Schedule 3 to the Development Credit Agreement is 
revised to reads as follows:  
 
“2. 
Procurement of Small Works  
Works estimated to cost less than $50,000 equivalent per contract, up to an aggregate 
amount not to exceed $2,940,000 equivalent, may be procured under lump-sum, fixed-
price contracts awarded on the basis of quotations obtained from three (3) qualified 
domestic contractors in response to a written invitation. The invitation shall include a 
detailed description of the works, including basic specifications, the required completion 
date, a basic form of agreement acceptable to the Association, and relevant drawings, 
where applicable. The award shall be made to the contractor who offers the lowest price 
quotation for the required work, and who has the experience and resources to complete 
the contract successfully.” 
3.  
Paragraph 2 (a) (i) of Part D, Section 1 of Schedule 3 to the Development Credit 
Agreement is revised to read as follows:

“2.

“4.

All the terms and conditions of the Development Credit Agreement, as amended hereby, 
shall remain in full force and effect. 
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing, dating and 
returning to us the attached copy of this letter. This amendment will become effective as of date 
of countersignature upon receipt by the Bank of the countersigned copy of this letter.
