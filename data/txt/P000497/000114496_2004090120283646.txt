Re:
Credit No. 1775-CD (Agricultural Rehabilitation Project)
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement No. 1775-CD (Agricultural 
Rehabilitation Project), between Chad (the "Borrower") and the International 
Development Association (the "Association"), dated July 15, 1987 (the "Development 
Credit Agreement").
We are pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to amend the Schedule 1 
of the Agreement to: (i) reallocate the funds between categories; and (ii) to increase 
the percentage of
expenditures to be financed by the Credit to 100% for Categories 4) and 5) (e). The 
table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement is hereby amended and 
restated entirely to be read as
follows:
