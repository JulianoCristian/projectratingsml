                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project closing date has been extended until 31 August 2013 to allow the complete installation of EDH remote metering system and remote meters for priority customers.
                         Management support to EDH provided by the Project helped designing and implementing EDH Commercial Action Plan 2012-2013 and EDH Financial Recovery Plan.
                         Activities under the Information Systems component have been completed and validated by EDH, the project is assisting EDH in the sustainable operational use of these modern
