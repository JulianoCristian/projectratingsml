                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         Results obtained in EDH Planning, Commercial, Technical and Financial sectors, thanks to management support to EDH provided by the project.
                         Activities under the Information Systems component have been implemented successfully.
                         Tendering process for procurement and installation of remote meters for priority industrial customers has been achieved successfully: awarded firm will install equipment within 9
                         months, from contract signing date. The Project Implementation Progress will be upgraded to the rating ‘Moderately Satisfactory’ once this contract is signed by both parties.
