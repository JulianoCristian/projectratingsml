ATTACHMENT II 
 
“SCHEDULE 2 
 
Project Description 
 
The objective of the Project is to assist in the transformation of the education system 
within the framework of the Borrower's 
Ley Federal de 
Educación
 by assisting Participating 
Schools in the development and implementation of Preschool Education, EGB I, EGB II, EGB III 
and Polymodal Education. 
 
The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the 
Borrower and the Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objective: 
 
Part A
:
Activities at the Level of the Participating Schools
1.  
 Improvement of Educational Resources
Improvement of the educational resources of Participating Schools by the provision and 
utilization of: 
 
(a)  
Multimedia 
Centers
:
construction or rehabilitation of school libraries so that 
students and teachers under EGB III and Polymodal Education will have access to various 
sources of information (books, documents, information networks, educational CD-ROM 
programs and videos, maps, etc.). 
 
(b) Educational 
Equipment
:
provision and use of computer equipment for teaching 
various disciplines of the curriculum of EGB III and Polymodal Education and provision and use 
of computers for teachers' offices for research, planning and design of teaching activities under 
said levels of education. 
 
(c) 
Natural Science and Technology Laboratories
:
provision of natural science and 
technology laboratories for carrying out experiments, simulations, analyses, and design and 
development of projects related to the defined curriculum of EGB III and Polymodal Education. 
 
(d) 
Basic School Supplies
:
Provision and distribution of basic school supplies 
(which supplies may consist of durable learning supplies, such as maps, rulers, sets for geometry, 
basic pocket calculators and pencil sharpeners) for the benefit of students at the Preschool 
Education, EGB I, EGBII, EGBIII and Polymodal Education levels.  
 
2. 
Improvement of School Infrastructure
Improvement of school infrastructure through the execution at the Participating Schools 
of civil works for: 
 
(a) Rehabilitation
:
renovation of appropriate classrooms and physical spaces for the 
use of new learning resources and implementation of EGB III and Polymodal Education. 
 
(b) Construction
:
building of appropriate classrooms and physical spaces for the use 
of new learning resources and implementation of EGB III and Polymodal Education. 
 
Page  7
3. 
Strengthening of Institutional and Teaching Capacity
Strengthening of institutional and teaching capacity at the Participating Schools of EGB 
III and Polymodal Education through: 
 
(a) 
Training of teachers, directors and supervisors to take full advantage of the new 
educational resources received under the Project. 
 
(b) 
Design and implementation of institutional projects that will promote the use of 
educational resources and innovative teaching practices. 
 
Part B
:
Project Coordination and Execution
1. 
Strengthening of the capacity of  UCP to coordinate, execute and supervise the 
implementation of the Project and to provide technical assistance to the Provinces. 
 
2. 
Strengthening of the capacity of the Provinces to monitor and supervise execution of 
projects in the Participating Schools. 
 
*
*
*
The Project is expected to be completed by December 31, 2002.”.
