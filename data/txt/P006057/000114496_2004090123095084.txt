ANNEX 3
SCHEDULE 2
Project Description
The objective of the Project is to assist in the transformation of the education system within the framework 
of the Borrower's 1'y Federal 
_de 
Educación 
by assisting Participating Schools in the development and 
implementation of EGB III and Polymodal Education.
The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the 
Bank may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objective:
Part A
: Activities at the Level of the Participating Schools
1. 
Improvement of Educational Resources
Improvement of the educational resources of Participating Schools by the provision and utilization of:
(a)
 Multimedia Centers: construction or rehabilitation of school libraries so that students and teachers 
will have access to various sources of information (books, documents, information networks, educational CD-ROM 
programs and videos, maps, etc.).
(b)
Educational Equipment: provision and use of computer equipment for teaching various disciplines 
of the curriculum and provision and use of computers for teachers' offices for research, planning and design of 
teaching activities.
(c)
Natural Science and Technology Laboratories: provision of natural science and technology 
laboratories for carrying out experiments, simulations, analyses, and design and development of projects related to 
the defined curriculum.
2.
Improvement of School Infrastructure
Improvement of school infrastructure through the execution at the Participating Schools of civil works for:
(a)
Rehabilitation: renovation of appropriate classrooms and physical spaces for the use of new 
learning resources and implementation of EGB III and Polymodal Education.
Page  7
(b)
Construction: building of appropriate classrooms and physical spaces for the use of new 
learning resources and implementation of EGB III and Polymodal Education.
3.
Strengthening of Institutional and Teaching 
Capacity
Strengthening of institutional and teaching capacity at the Participating Schools through:
(a)
Training of teachers, directors and supervisors to take full advantage of the new educational 
resources received under the Project.
(b)
Design and implementation of institutional projects that will promote the use of educational 
resources and innovative teaching 
practices.
Part B
: Project Coordination and Execution
1.
Strengthening of the capacity of NCUEFP to coordinate, execute and supervise the implementation 
of the Project and to provide technical assistance to the Provinces.
2.
Strengthening of the capacity of the Provinces to monitor and supervise execution of projects in the 
Participating Schools.
*   *   *
The Project is expected to be completed by December 31, 2000.
