KAZAKHSTAN:
Irrigation and Drainage Project (Loan 4041-KZ)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Esenbaev:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Irrigation and Drainage Project) between 
Republic of Kazakhstan and International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the 
Bank) dated June 25, 1996. We also refer to your request made on July 24, 2001 and the 
letter of Mr. Dunaev dated August 16, 2001, to amend the Loan Agreement. We are 
pleased to inform you that the Bank has agreed to your request and the Loan Agreement 
is amended as follows:
1.
In the table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1, Categories (3) and (5) are 
revised to read as follows:

2.
Sub-paragraph 1(c) of Schedule 6 is amended to read as follows:
"1(c)
the term "Authorized Allocation" means an amount equivalent to $1,500,000 
to be withdrawn from the Loan Account and deposited into the Special Account pursuant 
to paragraph 3 (a) of this Schedule, provided, however, that unless the Bank shall 
otherwise agree, the Authorized Allocation shall be limited to an amount equivalent to 
$750,000 until the aggregate amount of withdrawals from the Loan Account plus the 
total amount of all outstanding special commitments entered into by the Bank pursuant 
to Section 5.02 of the General Conditions shall be equal to or exceed the equivalent 
of $3,000,000."
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing, dating and 
returning to us the attached copy of this letter. This amendment will become effective 
as of the date of your countersignature, upon receipt of your countersigned copy by 
the Bank. A signed duplicate of the letter is enclosed for your records.
