                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This report follows the implementation support mission carried out on November 2013, which also included the supervision of the Rural Alliances Project II (separate report).

                         The Project has committed all its funds, executed 98% of its budget and its achievements are remarkable. It has financed about 770 alliances (14% over the target) and trained 6,640
                         producers in alliance management (64% over the target). The only achievements below expectations are in municipal subprojects (45% of target); however, this has not affected
                         overall results and the project is well on its way to achieve its development objective.
