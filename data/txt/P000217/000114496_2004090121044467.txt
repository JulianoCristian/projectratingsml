Re:
Credit No:  2288 BU (Water Supply Sector Project)
Amendments to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) dated August 7, 
1991 and entered into between the Republic of Burundi (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association (the Association) for the Project referenced 
above as amended to date.
Pursuant to discussions held between the representatives of the Borrower and the 
representatives of the Association and further to your request dated July 25, 1995, 
and our response dated August 18, 1995, regarding the lifting of suspension and 
restructuring of the Project, we are pleased to inform you that the Association 
accedes to your request and proposes to amend the Agreement between us as follows:
1.
Section 3.03 is amended in its entirety to read as follows:
 
"Section 3.03.  The Borrower shall ensure that the execution of the 
Project is 
coordinated through DGHER with the assistance of a manager and an 
accountant, all with 
qualifications and experience which shall be, at all 
times, satisfactory  to the 
Association."
 
2.
The language in Section 3.06 is deleted and replaced with the following new 
language:
"Section 3.06.  The Borrower shall, by December 31, 1996: (a)  (i) 
formulate the 
water supply masterplan; and  (ii) carry out the water 
tariffs study referred to under Part 
5 of the Project; and (b) promptly as soon as 
they become available, submit to the 
Association for its review and comments 
the draft water supply master plan and the results 
and recommendations of the water 
tariffs study; (c) finalize the draft water supply 
masterplan by taking into 
account the comments and recommendations made by the Association;  and (d) promptly 
implement the recommendations of the water tariffs study as agreed upon 
with 
the Association."
3.
Section 3.08 is hereby deleted.
 
4.
The table set forth in Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement is deleted 
and replaced with the new table set forth in the Annex  to this letter.
Please kindly confirm your agreement with these amendments by signing and dating 
the duplicate original of this letter in the space provided therefor and by returning 
to us one
duplicate original duly signed and confirmed.
These amendments shall become effective upon receipt by the Association of one 
duplicate original of this letter duly signed and confirmed.
Sincerely,
