Re:
Loan No. 3280-AR (Provincial Development Project) 
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank) and the Argentine Republic (the Borrower) dated February 
15, 1991 (the Loan Agreement), as previously amended. We are pleased to inform you 
that, pursuant to your request and our recent discussions on the subject, the Bank 
agrees to amend the Loan Agreement further as follows:
1.
Section 1.02 (v) is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety:  
"‘Rehabilitating Province’ means each of the Provinces of Buenos Aires, Chaco, 
Catamarca, Corrientes, Entre Ríos, Formosa, Jujuy, La Rioja, Misiones, Salta, Santa 
Fé, Santiago del Estero and Tucumán;".
2.
Section 1.02 (x) is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety:  
"‘Housing Sub-project’ means a specific investment project for reconstruction of a 
house damaged by the floods which occurred in May, June and July 1992 (or occurred or 
may occur during 1997 or the first six months of 1998) in a Rehabilitating Province or 
for the construction of a new house to replace a house destroyed by such floods, such 
Sub-project to be financed, by means of a grant to the owner or occupant of such 
house, in compliance with the eligibility criteria and other requirements set forth in 
the Annex to Schedule 3 to the Manual.".
3.
Section 3.03 (a) is hereby amended by replacing the words "Secretariat of 
Provincial Public Sector Reform" with the words "Secretariat of Financial Assistance 
to the Provinces".
4.
The table in Schedule 1, paragraph 1 is hereby amended to read as set forth in 
the Attachment to this letter.
5.
Schedule 1, paragraph 3 (h) is hereby amended to read as follows in its 
entirety:  "expenditures under an Eligible Emergency Sub-project or a Housing 
Sub-project incurred before June 16, 1992 (except that, in the case of Housing 
Sub-projects carried out as a result of the 1997 or 1998 floods referred to in Section 
1.02 (x) of this Agreement, such date is replaced by September 1, 1997); and".
6.
Schedule 1, paragraph 3 (i) is hereby amended to read as follows in its 
entirety:  "expenditures under an Eligible Emergency Sub-project or a Housing 
Sub-project incurred after September 30, 1994, or such later date as the Bank may 
establish by notice to the Borrower (except that, in the case of Housing Sub-projects 
carried out as a result of the 1997 or 1998 floods referred to in Section 1.02 (x) of 
this Agreement, such later date is already hereby specified as June 30, 1998).".
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter may be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us.  The provisions of this amendment letter 
will become effective as of the date first above written upon receipt by the Bank of 
one fully executed original of this amendment letter.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION
