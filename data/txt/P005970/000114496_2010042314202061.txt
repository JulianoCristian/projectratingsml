Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
     Please refer to the Loan Agreement (the Loan Agreement),  dated 
July 29, 1987, as amended to date, between the International Bank 
for Recon-struction and Development (the Bank) and the Argentine 
Republic (the Bor-rower) for the above-mentioned loan.  I am pleased 
to advise you that the Bank agrees to your recent request to amend 
the Loan Agreement in order to include provisions related to the 
parallel cofinancing loan, referred to in the Loan Agreement, by 
private commercial banks.
     Accordingly, the Loan Agreement'is hereby amended by adding, 
immedi-ately after Section 4.01 thereof, a new Section 4.02 which 
shall,read as follows:
     "Section 4.02. Pursuant to Section 7.01 (h) of the 
General Conditions, the following 
additional events are 
specified, namely that any of the events specified in 
paragraph (b) of Section 4.01 of this Agreement shall 
occur."
     Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing the 
enclosed duplicate original of this letter in the space below and 
returning that duplicate to us.
Very truly yours,
