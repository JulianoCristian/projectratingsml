Re:    Japanese Grant No. 29530 (Social Sector Reform Projects)
Amendment to the Letter of Agreement
Dear Sir: 
We refer to the Letter of Agreement between International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the United Mexican States (the 
Recipient), dated April 10, 1997, pertaining to the above-captioned Grant.  We are 
pleased to inform you of our agreement to amend paragraph 4 of the Annex to said 
Letter of Agreement by replacing the date “March 31, 1998” therein with the date 
“December 31, 1998”.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
the two originals of this letter in the spaces provided below, and returning one to 
us.  This amendment shall enter into effect as of its date first above written upon 
receipt by the Bank of one original of this letter signed by you.
Sincerely yours,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR 
   RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT
