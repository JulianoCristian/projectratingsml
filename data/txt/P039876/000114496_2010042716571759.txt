RE: Amendment to the Global Environment Facility Trust Fund Grant Agreement
(Biodiversity Resources Development Project)- GEF Trust Fund TF-028324
Dear Doctor Gámez: 
 
Please refer to the Global Environment Facility Trust Fund Grant Agreement (the Grant 
Agreement) for the Biodiversity Resources Development Project between the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), acting as an 
Implementing Agency of the Global Environment Facility and the Instituto Nacional de 
Biodiversidad – INBIO (the Recipient), dated March 6, 1998, as amended.  
 
Pursuant to your letter of June 24, 2002, and our recent discussions on the subject, we are 
pleased to inform you that the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Grant Agreement as set 
forth below. 
 
1.   
Paragraph “(j)” of Section 1.02 of the Grant Agreement is hereby amended in its 
entirety by expanding the definition of the term “Selected Taxonomic Groups”.  Said 
term to read as follows:
 
(j) "
Selected Taxonomic Groups" means hymenoptera, coleoptera, diptera, fungi,  
and Lepidoptera and any other additional or substitute group agreed between the 
Recipient and the Bank; and "Selected Taxonomic Group" means any of such 
groups;” 
 
2.  
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended in its entirety to read as 
set forth in the Annex  to this Amendment Letter.   
 
3.   
The header of Part A of the Project Conceptual Framework is hereby amended by 
adding “(Planning and Participation)” to said header. 
 
“Part A
:
Conceptual Framework (Planning and Participation)
”
4. 
Part D of the Project is hereby amended in its entirety so as to add Project audit 
costs said Part D to read as follows:  
 
Page  2
-2-  
 
“1. 
Strengthening of the institutional capabilities of the Recipient through the 
operation of the Project Coordinating Unit, technical assistance for Project 
supervision, construction and equipping of a fungi laboratory, and studies, that 
may be agreed between the Recipient and the Bank, related to Project objectives.” 
 
2. 
Carrying out of Project audits as referred to in Section 4.01 (b)(i) of this 
Agreement.” 
 
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall be an 
original and become effective as of the first date above written upon receipt by the Bank 
of one fully executed original of this Amendment Letter. Please confirm your agreement 
with the foregoing amendments by signing and dating the two counterparts of this 
Amendment Letter in the spaces provided below. Upon your confirmation, please return 
one executed original to us.
