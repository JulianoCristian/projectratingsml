GUATEMALA:  
Second Basic Education Project 
(Loan No. 3003-GU) 
Amendment to Loan Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated January 10, 1990 entered into between 
the Republic of Guatemala (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank), as amended.
In response to the Borrower’s March 19 and 26, 1998 requests to: (a) extend the 
Closing Date from March 31, 1998 to December 31, 1998; and (b) reallocate part of the 
Loan proceeds, the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
Section 2.03 is hereby amended to read as follows:
“Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be December 31, 1998 or such 
later date as the Bank shall establish.  The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower 
of such later date.”.
2.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is modified to read as set forth 
in the Annex to this Amendment Letter.
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall 
be an original.  Upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this 
Amendment Letter, these amendments will become effective as of the date first above 
written. Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and 
dating the two counterparts of this Amendment Letter.
Sincerely,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR
  RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT

Director
