GUATEMALA (Second Basic Education Project) Loan No. 3003 GU
       Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated January 10, 1990 entered into between 
the Republic of Guatemala (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank), as amended.
In response to the Borrower’s May 8, 1997 to: (a) extend the Closing Date from 
June 30,1997 to March 31, 1998; and (b) reallocate some of the Loan proceeds to 
continue financing the acquisition of school furniture for rural schools, the printing 
and distribution of texts for bilingual education and the distribution of basic school 
supplies to students in rural areas (as set forth in the annex to this Amendment 
Letter), the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1. Section 2.03 is amended to read as follows:
“Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be March 31, 1998 or such later date as the Bank 
shall establish. The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower of such later date.”.
2. The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is amended to read as set forth in the 
annex to this Amendment Letter.
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall 
be an original. Upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this 
Amendment Letter, these amendments will be effective as of the date first above 
written. Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and 
dating the two counterparts of this Amendment Letter.
