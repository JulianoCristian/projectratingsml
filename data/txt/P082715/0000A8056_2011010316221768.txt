                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to provide technical assistance to the Borrower to: (a) reinforce its transmission network and restore the financial sustainability of
                         the power sector; (b) strengthen its power sector institutions in charge of consumer protection, policy making and portfolio management; (c) increase the
                         supply of affordable energy to the poor; and (d) reduce potential negative environmental impacts.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?
