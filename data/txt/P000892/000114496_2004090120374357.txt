               Re: GHANA: Credit No. 1911 GH
                         (Financial Sector Adjustment Credit)
                          Amendment
Excellency:
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Financial Sector
Adjustment Credit), dated June 14, 1988, between the Republic of
Ghana and the International Development Association (the
Association).
     The Association proposes to amend the Development Credit
Agreement for the above-reference Project by changing the amount of
$50,000 referred to in paragraph 3 (d) of Schedule 1 to the
Development Credit Agreement to $10,000.
     Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing
the form of confirmation below on the enclosed copy and returning it
to us.  The amendment will become effective upon receipt of the
signed copy by the Association.
                   Sincerely yours,
                  M.G. Sri-ram Aiyer
                    Acting Director
