     Re:   GHANA - Credit No. 1911 GH
                   (Financial Sector Adjustment Credit)
                   Amendments to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Dr. Botchwey:
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Financial Sector
Adjustment Credit), dated June 14, 1988, between the Republic of
Ghana and the International Development Association (the
Association), as amended.
     Pursuant to your request of March 1, 1990, we propose to amend
the Development Credit Agreement as follows:
1.   Section 3.03 (a) of the Development Credit Agreement shall be
amended by adding:
     "(vii)    provide technical assistance for bank
               restructuring and to the Non-Performing Asset
               Recovery Trust."
2.   In the table set forth in paragraph (1) of Schedule 1 to the
Development Credit Agreement the amount allocated to Category (1)
shall be reduced from SDR 73,580,000 to SDR 71,660,000, the amount
allocated to Category (2) shall be increased from SDR 370,000 to SDR
760,000 and the amount allocated to Category (4) shall be increased
from SDR 1,980,000 to SDR 3,510,000.
     Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing
the form of confirmation below on the enclosed copy and returning it
to us.  This amendment shall become effective upon receipt of the
signed copy by the Association.
                   Sincerely yours,
                  Caio K. Koch-Weser
                       Director
               Western Africa Department
