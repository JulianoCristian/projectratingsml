Dear Mr. Paskaralingam:
          We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Emergency
Reconstruction and Rehabilitation Project) between the Democratic
Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka and the International Development
Association dated April 20, 1988.
          In response to your letter dated November 21, 1989, we are
pleased to inform you that the Association has agreed to amend Category
(1) of the table in Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement to
read as set forth in the Annex hereto.
          Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by
signing and dating the enclosed form of confirmation and by returning it
to us.
