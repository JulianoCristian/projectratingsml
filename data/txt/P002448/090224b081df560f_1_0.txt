﻿DEVELOPMENT CREDIT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated June 1, 1977, between SOMALI DEMOCRATIC
REPUBLIC (hereinafter called the Borrower) and INTERNATIONAL
DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (hereinafter called the Association).
WHEREAS (A) the Borrower has requested the Association to
assist in the financing of Parts A, B (ii), B (iv) and B (v) of
the Project described in Schedule 1 to this Agreement by extending
the Credit as hereinafter provided;
(B) the Project will be carried out by the Somali
Development Bank, a statutory body existing under the laws of the
Borrower (hereinafter called SDB) with the Borrower's assistance
and, as part of such assistance, the Borrower will make available
to SDB the proceeds of the Credit as hereinafter provided;
(C) the Borrower intends to contract from the Arab Fund for
Economic and Social Development (hereinafter called the Arab Fund)
a grant (hereinafter called the Arab Fund Grant) in an amount of
100,000 Kuwaiti Dinars equivalent to about $340,000 to be made
available to SDB to assist it in financing Parts B (i), B (iii)
and B (v) of the Project on the terms and conditions set forth
in an agreement (hereinafter called the Arab Fund Grant Agreement)
to be entered into between the Borrower and the Arab Fund; and

WHEREAS the Association has agreed, on the basis, inter alia,
of the foregoing, to extend the Credit to the Borrower upon the
terms and conditions hereinafter set forth and in the Project
Agreement of even date herewith between the Association and
SDB;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:

ARTICLE I
General Conditions; Definitions
Section 1.01. The parties to this Agreement accept all
the provisions of the General Conditions Applicable to Development
Credit Agreements of the Association, dated March 15, 1974,
with the same force and effect as if they were fully set forth
herein, subject, however, to the modifications thereof set forth
in Schedule 2 to this Agreement (said General Conditions
Applicable to Development Credit Agreements of the Association,
as so modified, being hereinafter called the General Conditions).
Section 1.02. Wherever used in this Agreement, unless the
context otherwise requires, the several terms defined in the
General Conditions have the respective meanings therein set
forth and the following additional terms have the following
meanings:
(a) "SDB" means the Somali Development Bank established
by Decree Law No. 2 of February 28, 1968, converted with
amendments into law by Law No. 15 of May 30, 1968, operating
pursuant and subject to Law No. 25 of April 30, 1970, of the
Borrower;
(b) "Project Agreement" means the agreement between the
Association and SDB of even date herewith, as the same may be

amended from time to time, and such term includes all agreements
supplemental to the Project Agreement;
(c) "Subsidiary Loan Agreement" means the agreement to
be entered into between the Borrower and SDB pursuant to Section
3.01 (a) of this Agreement, as the same may be amended from time
to time, and such term includes all schedules to the Subsidiary
Loan Agreement;
(d) "sub-loan" means a loan or credit made or proposed
to be made by SDB out of the proceeds of the Credit relent to
SDB under the Subsidiary Loan Agreement to an Investment
Enterprise for an Investment Project and "free-limit sub-loan"
means a sub-loan, as so defined, which qualifies as a free-limit
sub-loan pursuant to the provisions of Section 2.02 (b) of this
Agreement;
(e) "investment" means an investment other than a sub-loan
made or proposed to be made by SDB out of the proceeds of the
Credit relent to SDB under the Subsidiary Loan Agreement in
an Investment Enterprise for an Investment Project;
(f) "Investment Enterprise" means an enterprise to which
SDB proposes to make or has made a sub-loan or in which it
proposes to make or has made an investment;

ARTICLE II
The Credit
Section 2.01. The Association agrees to lend to the Borrower,
on the terms and conditions in the Development Credit Agreement
set forth or referred to, an amount in various currencies equivalent
to five million dollars ($5,000,000).
Section 2.02. (a) Subject to the provisions of other
paragraphs of this Section, SDB, on behalf of the Borrower
pursuant to Section 2.08 of this Agreement, shall be entitled to
withdraw from the Credit Account:
(i) in respect of the reasonable cost of goods and
services required for the carrying out of the
Investment Project in respect of which the
withdrawals requested and to be financed out of
the proceeds of a sub-loan or investment, up to
the equivalent of four million seven hundred
forty thousand dollars ($4,740,000):
(A) amounts expended (or, if the Association
shall so agree, such amounts as shall be
required to meet expenditures to be made)
in foreign currency for imported goods (except
goods described in paragraph (B) of this
Section) and services;


﻿-7-
(B) amounts expended in Somali Shillings on
account of not more than ninety-five per
cent (95%), or such other percentage as
shall be agreed between the Borrower and
the Association of the invoice price paid for
goods previously imported into the territories
of the Borrower through normal channels;
(C) amounts expended in Somali Shillings on
account of not more than eighty-five percent
(85%) (representing foreign exchange
expenditures) of the invoice price paid
for goods manufactured in the territories
of the Borrower;
(D) amounts expended in Somali Shillings on
account of not more than seventy-five
per cent (75%) (representing foreign exchange
expenditures) of the contract price paid

provided, however, that no amount shall be

Investment Project unless (1) the sub-loan or

been approved by the Association, or (2) the
sub-loan shall be a free-limit sub-loan for which
the Association shall have authorized withdrawals
frim the Credit Account; and
(ii) for Parts B (ii), B (iv) and B (v) of the Project,
up to an amount equivalent of two hundred sixty
thousand dollars ($260,000), the amounts expended

Association.

(c) Except as the Association and SDB shall otherwise
agree, no withdrawals shall be made on account of expenditures
made by an Investment Enterprise in respect of a sub-loan subject
to the Association's approval, or in respect of an investment, if
such expenditures shall have been made before the date of this
Agreement or more than ninety days prior to the date on which the

one Part will be insufficient to finance the agreed percentages

notification to the Borrower, reallocate to such Part to the
extent required to meet the estimated shortfall proceeds of the
Credit which are then allocated to the other Part and which are
not needed to meet other expenditures.

Association shall promptly notify the Borrower of such later

Section 2.04. The Borrower shall pay to the Association

(3/4 of 1%) per annum on the principal amount of the Credit
withdrawn and outstanding from time to time.
Section 2.05. Service charges shall be payable semi-annually
on June 1 and December 1 in each year.

Section 2.06. The Borrower shall repay the principal amount
of the Credit in semi-annual installments payable on each June 1

ARTICLE III
Use of Proceeds of the Credit
Section 3.01. (a) The Borrower shall relend the proceeds of
the Credit to SDB under a subsidiary loan agreement to be entered
into between the Borrower and SDB containing terms and conditions
which shall have been approved by the Association. Except as the
Association shall otherwise agree, the said agreement shall
provide, inter alia, that such proceeds will be made available to
SDB for a term of fifteen years, including five years of grace,
and for payment of interest by SDB at the rate of five per cent
(5%) per annum on amounts withdrawn and outstanding from time to
time.
(b) The Borrower shall exercise its rights under the
Subsidiary Loan Agreement in such manner as to protect the
interests of the Borrower and the Association and to accomplish
the purposes of the Credit, and except as the Association shall
otherwise agree, the Borrower shall not assign, amend, abrogate or
waive the Subsidiary Loan Agreement or any provision thereof.

ARTICLE IV
Additional Covenants
Section 4.01. The Borrower shall not take, or permit any
of its political subdivisions, or any of its agencies or
instrumentalities, or any agency or instrumentality of any
political subdivision, to take, any action which would prevent or
materially interfere with the performance by SDB of any of its
obligations under the Project Agreement or the Subsidiary Loan
Agreement, and shall take or cause to be taken all reasonable
action which shall be required on its part in order to enable
SDB to perform such obligations.

ARTICLE V
Remedies of the Association
Section 5.01. For the purposes of Section 6.02 of the General
Conditions the following additional events are specified:
(a) any part of the principal amount of any loan to SDB
having an original maturity of one year or more shall, in
accordance with its terms, have become due and payable in advance
of maturity as provided in the relative contractual instruments,
or any security for any such loan shall have become enforceable;
(b) SDB Law or By-laws shall have been amended, suspended,
abrogated, repealed or waived which will materially and adversely
affect the financial condition or operations of SDB;
(c) a resolution shall have been passed for the dissolution
or liquidation of SDB;
(d) a subsidiary or any other entity shall have been created
or acquired or taken over by the Borrower, if such creation,
acquisition or taking over would adversely affect the conduct
of SDB's business or the financial situation, or the efficiency
of SDB's management and personnel, or the carrying out of the
Project;
(e) SDB shall have failed to perform any covenant, agreement
or obligation of SDB under the Project Agreement, or the
Subsidiary Loan Agreement;


﻿- 15 -
(f) any proceeding shall have been taken by SDB or by others
whereby any of the property of SDB shall or may be distributed
among its creditors; and
(g) a change shall have been made in the Statement of Policy
without the Association's consent.
Section 5.02. For the purposes of Section 7.01 of the General
Conditions the following additional events are specified:
(a) the event specified in paragraph (a), or paragraph
(b), or paragraph (c), or paragraph (f), or paragraph (g) of
Section 5.01 shall occur; and
(b) the event specified in paragraph (d) or paragraph (e) of
Section 5.01 shall occur and shall continue for a period of sixty
days after notice thereof shall have been given by the Bank to the
Borrower and SDB.

SCHEDULE 1
Description of the Project
The Project consists of the following parts:
(A) The financing by SDB of specific development projects through
loans to and investments in productive enterprises in Somalia.
(B) Assistance to SDB in:
(i) financial planning and financial control through
the provision of services of a Financial Advisor;
(ii) establishing appropriate appraisal and follow-up
techniques through the provision of services of an
Investment Advisor;
(iii) the carrying out of technical appraisal of industrial
projects through the provision of services of an
Industrial Engineer;
(iv) appraising applications for financial assistance from
farmers and agricultural cooperatives and to assist
the Borrower in organizing cooperatives through the
provision of services of an Agricultural Advisor; and
(v) training abroad of SDB staff in financial management
(including audit) and project appraisal and follow-
up techniques.

the Credit Account, the Association may by notice to the
Borrower terminate the right of SDB to submit such
applications or requests or to make withdrawals from the
Credit Account on behalf of the Borrower, as the case may be,
