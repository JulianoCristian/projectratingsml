                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The proposed Project's development objective is to contribute to the Government of Haiti's response to the cholera outbreak and strengthen institutional
                         capacity to respond to disease outbreaks effectively.
