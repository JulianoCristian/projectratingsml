Third Amendment
Dear Dr. Cavallo: 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the Argentine Republic (the Borrower) 
and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) dated 
December 5, 1995, (the Loan Agreement). 
Pursuant to Mr. Gerardo Hita’s request dated November 3, 2000, we are pleased to 
inform you that the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement so as to modify 
Category 2 (a) and (b) of Schedule 1 of the Loan Agreement and the amount set forth in 
Section 3.01 (b) of the Loan Agreement.
Consequently, the Loan Agreement is amended as follows:
1.
Section 3.01 (b) is hereby amended and replaced to read in its entirety 
as follows:
"(b)
without limitation upon the provisions of paragraph (a) of 
this Section, shall make available not less than $105.800.000 
equivalent as counterpart funds over the period of the execution 
of the Project."
2. 
The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is 
hereby amended and replaced to read as per the attachment to this amendment letter. 
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating this 
letter in the spaces provided below. This amendment letter will be executed in two 
counterparts, each of which shall be an original.
Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed original to us. The 
provisions of this amendment letter will become effective as of the date of this 
letter upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this amendment 
letter.
Sincerely,
