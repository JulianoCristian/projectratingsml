            Transport Sector Adjustment/Investment Credit (No. 2266)
Excellency:
      We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Transport Sector
Adjustment/Investment Credit), dated July 22, 1991, between the
Republic of Senegal (the Borrower) and the International Development
Association (the Association).  Further to the discussions held with
the representative of the Borrower, we are pleased to inform you
that the Association hereby agrees to the following amendments to
the above-referenced Development Credit Agreement:
      1.    Section 3.05:  the date "October 31, 1991" is replaced
      by the date "July 31, 1992."
      2.    Schedule 6, paragraph 3: the date "December 31, 1991" is
      replaced by the date "May 1, 1992."
      3.    Schedule 6, paragraph 4: the date "October 31, 1991" is
      replaced by the date "May 1, 1992".
      Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing by
countersigning and dating the form of confirmation on the two
original copies of this letter provided and returning one original,
as countersigned, to us.  This amendment shall become effective as
of the date of receipt by the Association of this letter duly
countersigned by you.
                              Sincerely yours,
                              Director
                              Sahelian Department
                              Afric Region
CONFIRMED
REPUBLIC OF SENEGAL
By
      Authorized Representative
Date: February 27, 1992
