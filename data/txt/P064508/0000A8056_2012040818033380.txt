                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The rollout phase of the Federal Treasury Automation System (FTAS) was successfully completed in 83 regions on September 5, 2011, sooner than expected (end of 2011).
                         The Project Development Objectives (PDOs) for the improvement of budget execution have already been met including the introduction of a GFS compliant Chart of Accounts, setting
                         up of a Treasury Single Account, closure of Spending Unit Bank accounts and routing of all expenditure transactions through the Treasury system. Benefiting from the savings in
