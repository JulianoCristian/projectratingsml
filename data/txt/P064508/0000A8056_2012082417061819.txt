                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Treasury Development Project (TDP) was closed on June 30, 2012. The project activities resulted in a number of key achievements, which will be explained in detail within the
                         Implementation Completion Report (ICR). The Federal Treasury Automation System (FTAS) is fully operational in 83 regions since September 5, 2011.
                         Some of the important achievements of the project include the introduction of a GFS compliant Chart of Accounts, setting up of a Treasury Single Account to support all budget levels,
                         closure of Spending Unit Bank accounts and routing of all expenditure transactions through the Treasury system. Benefiting from the savings in procurement activities, the MoF/FT
