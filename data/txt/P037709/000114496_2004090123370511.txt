Re:  HONDURAS - Credit No. 2766-HO (Third Social Investment 
     Fund Project)
     Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated August 3, 1995 entered 
into between the Republic of Honduras (the Borrower) and the International  
Development Association (the Association), as amended.
In response to the Borrower’s March 14, 1997 request to: (a) transfer the 
responsibility to FHIS to carry out Part E of the Project; and (b) eliminate two of 
the conditions of disbursement referred to in paragraph 3 (c) and (d) of Schedule 1 to 
the Development Credit Agreement in order to permit the financing of expenditures 
under Parts A.2 and E of the Project , the Association hereby agrees to amend the 
Development Credit Agreement as follows:
1.
Section 3.01 is amended to read as follows:
“Section 3.01. The Borrower declares its commitment to the objectives 
of the 
Project as set forth in Schedule 2 to this Agreement, and, to 
this end: (a) shall 
carry out Part D of the Project; and (b) shall, 
under the FHIS Agreement, cause 
FHIS to carry out Parts A, B, C, E 
and F of the Project, all with due diligence 
and efficiency and in 
conformity with appropriate financial, administrative, 
health, 
educational, environmental, archeological and managerial 
practices, 
and according to the Project Implementation Guidelines, the 
Operational Manual, the Completed Subprojects Maintenance Plan and 
the 
Procurement Plan, and shall take or cause to be taken all action,  including the 
provision of funds, facilities, services and other 
resources, necessary or 
appropriate to carry out the Project.”.
2.
Section 3.02 is amended as follows:
“ Section 3.02 (a)  For the purposes of Section 3.01 (b) above, the 
Borrower shall enter into an agreement with FHIS, satisfactory to the 
Association, 
providing, inter alia, for:
(i)
the provision to FHIS of the funds, facilities, services and 
other resources required to enable FHIS to carry out Parts A, 
B, C, E and F 
of the Project, including the transfer of the 
proceeds of the Credit allocated 
to such Parts of the Project;
(ii)
the obligation of FHIS to carry out such Parts of the Project 
in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in this 
Agreement, 
including the obligation:
(A)
to maintain records and separate accounts pursuant to 
the provisions of Section 4.01 of this Agreement;
(B)
to carry out the obligations set forth in Sections 9.03, 
9.04, 9.05, 9.06 and 9.07 of the General Conditions 
(relating to insurance, use of goods and services, plans 
and 
schedules, records and reports, and maintenance, 
respectively), in respect of Parts A, B, C, E and F of 
the Project;
(C)
to review, jointly with the Borrower and the 
Association, not later than  November 30 and May 31 of 
each 
year, beginning in November 1995, the standard 
regionalized 
unit prices for goods and works established 
by FHIS for the purposes 
of contracts to be awarded 
pursuant to the provisions of 
Page  2
Parts C.3 (c) and C.4 (c) 
of Schedule 3 to this Agreement, and to change 
such 
prices, in form and substance satisfactory to the 
Association, if so recommended by each such review;
(D)
to prepare the reports referred to in Section 3.05 (a), 
3.06 and 3.08 (b) of this Agreement; and
(E)
to enter into arrangements, satisfactory to the 
Association, with the Borrower’s Ministries of Education 
and 
Health (Secretarías de Estado en los Despachos de 
Educación Pública y 
de Salud Pública y Asistencia 
Social), and the Selected 
Municipalities, establishing 
the criteria for their participation in 
the carrying out 
of Subprojects under Parts A and B of the Project.
(b)
The Borrower shall exercise its rights and comply with its obligations 
under the FHIS Agreement in such manner as to protect the interest of the Borrower and 
the Association, and to accomplish the purposes of the Credit, and except as the 
Association shall otherwise agree, the Borrower shall not amend, abrogate, waive or 
fail to enforce the FHIS Agreement or any provision thereof.”.
3.
Section 4.01 (a) is amended to read as follows:
“Section 4.01 (a) The Borrower shall maintain or cause to be 
maintained records and separate accounts adequate to reflect in 
accordance 
with sound accounting practices the operations, resources 
and expenditures of:
(i)
the departments or agencies of the Borrower responsible for 
carrying out Part D of the Project with respect to such Part 
of the 
Project; and
(ii)
FHIS with respect to Parts A, B, C, E and F of the Project.”.
 
4.
Paragraph 3 of Schedule 1 is amended to read as follows:
“Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph 1 above, no withdrawals shall be 
made in respect of:
(a)
payments made for expenditures prior to the date of this 
Agreement, except that withdrawals, in an aggregate amount not 
to exceed 
SDR1,910,000 may be made on account of payments made 
for:  (i) Subprojects 
under Categories (1), (2)(a) and (4) 
(a); and (ii) Categories (3) (c) 
and (4) (b) of the table in 
paragraph 1 of this Schedule before that date 
but after 
January 1,1995; and
(b)
payments made for Subprojects under Categories (1), (2) (a) 
and (4) (a) of the table in paragraph  1 of this Schedule, 
unless each 
Subproject has been approved in accordance with 
the provisions set forth 
in the Operational Manual.”.
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall 
be an original. Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing 
and dating the two counterparts of this Amendment Letter.  This Amendment Letter shall 
become effective as of the date first above written upon notification by the 
Association that it has received to its satisfaction: (a) one fully executed original 
of this Amendment Letter; and (b) evidence that the FHIS Agreement has been amended to 
incorporate the amendments reflected in this Amendment Letter.
