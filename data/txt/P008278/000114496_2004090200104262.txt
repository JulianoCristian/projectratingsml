April 7, 1995
Mr. Armen Yeghiazarian Minister of Economy Ministry of Economy
Yerevan
Republic of Armenia
Re:
Loan No. 3585 AM (Institution Building Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
We refer to the above-referenced Loan Agreement between the Republic of 
Armenia and International Bank for Reconstruction and Development dated April 7, 
1993, as amended on January 7, 1994, and to your letter dated March 10, 1995 
requesting to introduce certain additional amendments thereto.  We hereby agree 
to amend said Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
Schedule 1 is amended by adding a new paragraph 4:
4.
The Bank may require withdrawals from the Loan Account to be made 
on the basis of statements of expenditure for expenditures under: (i) 
contracts for goods  not exceeding $300,000 equivalent; (ii) 
consultants' contracts with firm not exceeding $150,000 equivalent; 
(iii) consultants' contracts with individuals and for expenditures for 
training and operating costs, each not exceeding $50,000 equivalent; 
under such terms and conditions as the Bank shall specify  by notice 
to the Borrower.
2.
Schedule 5, paragraph 1(a) is amended to read as follows:
(a)
the term "eligible Catecories" means Categories (1), (2) and (3) set 
forth in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing, dating and 
returning to us the form of confirmation on the enclosed copy of this letter.
This amendment shall become effective upon receipt of the countersigned 
copy of this letter by us.
Sincerely,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR
  RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT
