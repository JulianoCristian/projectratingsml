Dominican Republic – Provincial Health Services Project (L4272-DO) 
Amendment

Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the Dominican Republic (the Borrower) 
and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), dated March 
9, 1998 (the Loan Agreement) for the above-captioned Project, as amended.  We are 
pleased to inform you that, as a result of the request of the Government and our 
recent discussions on the subject, the Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as 
follows:
1.
The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is hereby 
replaced to read as shown in the table set forth in the Annex to this letter.
2.
Paragraph 1 (a) of Schedule 5 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as 
follows: 
“(a)
the term “eligible Categories” means Categories (1) through (6) 
and (8) set 
forth in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 of this 
Agreement;”
Please confirm your agreement  to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter may be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us. Upon receipt by the Bank of said fully 
executed original, the provisions of this amendment letter will be deemed 
retroactively effective as of October 13, 1998.
Very truly yours,
By /s/ Orsalia Kalantzopoulos 
Director 
Caribbean Country Management Unit 
Latin America & the Caribbean Regional Office
