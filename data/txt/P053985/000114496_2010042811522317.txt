Re: 
Bethlehem 2000 Project - TF 026046
Amendment to the Trust Fund Credit Agreement
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Trust Fund Credit Agreement for the Bethlehem 2000 Project 
(TF026046) between the Palestine Liberation Organization (for the benefit of the 
Palestinian Authority) (the “Borrower”) and the International Development Association, 
acting as administrator of the Trust Fund for Gaza and West Bank (the “Administrator”), 
dated November 2, 1998, as amended (the “Credit Agreement”). We also refer to the letter 
from H.E. Mr. Mohammed Nashashibi, the former Minister of Finance of the Palestinian 
Authority and authorized representative of the Borrower, dated June 1, 2002, requesting 
the Administrator to reallocate the proceeds of the Credit to assist in financing emergency 
damage repair and services support activities in the West Bank and Gaza. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Administrator has agreed to the above 
request. Accordingly, the Credit Agreement is hereby amended as follows: 
 
1. 
In Section 1.02 (i), the municipality of the city of Al-Khader shall be added to the 
definition of the term “Participating Municipalities”.

3. 
In Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement, a new Part Z shall be added and shall read as 
follows:  
 
“Part Z
:
Damage Repair and Emergency Service Support Activities
Carrying out a program, through (i) B-2000 Authority; and (ii) MLG and the 
Participating Municipalities, consisting of the provision of goods and the execution of 
works, with a view to rehabilitating damaged physical and institutional infrastructure, and 
mitigating the decline in essential public service provision, including electricity, water, 
wastewater, solid waste and social services.” 
 
4. 
In paragraph A.1 of Schedule 4 to the Credit Agreement, the following language 
shall be inserted after the full stop in the fifth line: “The Borrower shall cause the 
Palestinian Authority to vest the responsibility for the implementation of Part Z of the 
Project jointly in B-2000 Authority, MLG and the Participating Municipalities.”. 
 
5. 
In paragraph 1 (a) of Schedule 5, the definition of the term “eligible Categories” 
shall read as follows: “Categories (1), (2) (a), (3) (a) and (4) (a) in respect of Special 
Account A, Categories (1) (a), (2) (b), (3) (b) and (4) (b) in respect of Special Account B, 
and Categories (1) (b), (2) (c), (2) (d), (3) (c) and (4) (c) in respect of Special Account C, all 
as set forth in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement;”. 
 
6. 
The effectiveness date of this Letter Amendment is August 1, 2002. 
 
7. 
All the terms and conditions of the Credit Agreement, not amended hereby, shall 
remain in full force and effect.
