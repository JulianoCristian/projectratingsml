AMENDING PROJECT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated as of April 24, 1981, between INTERNATIONAL
BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (hereinafter called the
Bank) and KOREA HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND CONSTRUCTION COMPANY, LIMITED
(hereinafter called KHIC).
WHEREAS (A) KHIC was previously named Hyundai International
Incorporated (hereinafter called HII);
(B)  by the Loan Agreement dated June 30, 1977 (hereinafter
called the 1977 Loan Agreement) between the Bank and the Republic
of Korea (hereinafter called the Borrower) the Bank agreed to
make available to the Borrower an amount in various currencies
equivalent to eighty million dollars ($80,000,000) on condition,
inter alia, that (i) HII agree to undertake the obligations
toward the Bank set forth in the Project Agreement dated June 30,
1977 (hereinafter called the 1977 Project Agreement) between
the Bank and HII and (ii) certain shareholders of HII agree to
undertake the obligations towards the Bank set forth- in the
Shareholders' Agreement dated June 30, 1977 (hereinafter called
the 1977 Shareholders' Agreement) between such shareholders
and the Bank;
(C)   the Borrower has caused within the preceding twelve
month period an amount at least equal to three hundred twenty-
seven billion Won (Won 327,000,000,000) to be paid into KHIC as


﻿-2-
additional paid-in share capital to facilitate, inter alia, the
completion of the Project described in Schedule 2 of the 1977 Loan
Agreement; and
WHEREAS, on the basis of the foregoing, the Bank has agreed
to amend the 1977 Loan Agreement (such amendment as of even date
herewith hereinafter called the Amending Loan Agreement), the 1977
Project Agreement and the 1977 Shareholders' Agreement;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
Section 1.01. Wherever used in this Agreement, unless the
context shall otherwise require, the several terms defined in the
1977 Loan Agreement, the 1977 Project Agreement, the 1977 Share-
holders' Agreement, the Preamble to this Agreement and the
General Conditions (as so defined) have the respective meanings
therein set forth.
ARTICLE II.
Section 2.01. The date "July 1, 1983" contained in Section
3.04 of the 1977 Project Agreement is hereby amended to be
"July 1, 1987".
Section 2.02. The date "1983" contained in Section 4.04 of
the 1977 Project Agreement is hereby amended to be "1987".


﻿-3-
Section 2.03. All references in the 1977 Project Agreement to
Hyundai International Incorporated and HII are hereby amended to
be Korea Heavy Industries and Construction Company, Limited and
KHIC, respectively.
ARTICLE III
Section 3.01. KHIC hereby confirms all of its obligations
under the 1977 Project Agreement, as amended by the provisions of
Article II above.
ARTICLE IV
Section 4.01 This Agreement shall come into force and effect
on the date upon which the Amending Loan Agreement becomes
effective.
