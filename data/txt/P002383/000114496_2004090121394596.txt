Loan No. 3551 SEY (Environment and Transport Project)
Amendment to Loan Agreement and Project Agreement
We refer to the Loan Agreement (the Agreement), dated January 15, 1993, for the 
Environment and Transport Project (the Project) between the Republic of Seychelles 
(the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the 
Bank) and to the Project Agreement between the Bank and Public Utilities Corporation 
of even date therewith (the Project Agreement).  We also refer to your letter dated 
January 9, 1997 to the Bank requesting the Bank to agree to finance a water supply 
scheme in Baie Lazare as an additional component under the Project.
We are pleased to confirm, therefore, our consent to amend the Agreement as 
follows:
1.
Schedule 1 is amended to read as set forth in the Attachment to this letter.
2.
Paragraph 1. of Part A of the Schedule 2 to the Agreement will be changed to:
"1. Praslin Water Supply Phase II and Baie Lazare Water Works 
construction of two additional intakes near existing intakes at 
Fonds 
B’offay and Nouvelle Découverte in Praslin,  and, construction of  
treatment 
works, including a pumping station, to abstract water from 
Baie Lazare in Mahé.
Furthermore, we confirm our consent to amend Part B of Section I in Schedule 1 
to the Project Agreement as follows:
"2.
Civil works under Part A.1 of the Project, up to an aggregate amount equivalent 
to $600,000, may be carried out by force account.
3.
Civil works under Parts A.1of the Project, up to an aggregate amount equivalent 
to $500,000, may be carried out by national competitive bidding."
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and 
dating the confirmation form on the attached copy of this letter and returning it to 
us, while keeping the original for your records.
This amendment will become effective as of the date of confirmation of this 
letter.
Sincerely,
Michael N. Sarris
Country Director for the Republic of Seychelles
Africa Region
