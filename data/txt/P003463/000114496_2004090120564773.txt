Dear Mr. Minister:
Re:
Development Credit No. 2145-CHA
(National Afforestation Project)
Amendment of the Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (National Afforestation Project) 
dated June 20, 1990  between the People's Republic of China (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association (the Association). We also refer to your 
Ministry's telex dated September 25, 1990 requesting the Association to include the 
Province of Hainan under the Project.
The Association is pleased to inform you that, pursuant to the above request, it 
agrees to amend Section 1.02 (d) of the Development Credit Agreement to read as 
follows:
"(d)  "Project Provinces' means the Provinces of Anhui, Fujian, 
Guangdong, Guangxi, Guizhou, Hainan, Hebei, Henan, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, 
Liaoning, Shandong, Slchuan, Yunnan, and Zhejiang;"
This amendment, once confirmed, shall take effect retroactively to and including 
June 20, 1990.
Please confirm the agreement on behalf of the Borrower to the foregoing 
amendment by signing and dating the confirmation form on the enclosed copy of this 
letter and returning it to us for our records.  A copy of this letter is being sent to 
the Ministry of Forestry for their information.
Sincerely,
