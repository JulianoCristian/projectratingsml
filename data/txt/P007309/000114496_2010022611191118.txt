SCHEDULE
2
Description of the Project
The objectives of the Project are: (i) to assist in
financing such productive facilities and resources in the
territory of the Borrower as will contribute to the
economic and social development of such territory;
(ii)
support the Borrower's private industrial sector in
undertaking the adjustments required to become export
oriented and competitive;
(iii) assist the Borrower in
the carrying out of the Trade Policy Reform Program;
(iv)
assist in the further strengthening of BRH, FDI, and the
Financial Intermediaries; and
(v)
improve the regulatory
framework of the Borrower's industrial sector.
 
The Project consists of the following Parts, subject
to such modifications thereof as the Borrower and the
Association may agree upon from time to time to achieve
such objectives:
Part A:
 
1.
The financing of specific development projects
through loans to Restructuring Investment Enterprises.
Page  13
2.
The financing of specific development projects
through loans to Non-restructuring Investment
Enterprises.
 
Part B:
Technical Assistance
1.
Provision of technical assistance to FDI in carrying
out Part A of the Project and training of staff of FDI
and of Financial Intermediaries to improve their project
evaluation and supervision capabilities, and in achieving
the broadening of FDI's scope of operations and
promotional role.
 
2.
Provision of technical assistance to BRH to assist
BRHJ to improve its capabilities with respect to its
regulatory and supervisory role over the banking sector
of the Borrower.
3.
Provision of technical assistance to the Borrower in
the improvement of its customs administration through:
 
(a)
the setting up of a computerized export
verification system;
(b)
training of staff of the Borrower in the use of
said export verification system and in the use of the
trade data system known as Sydonia; and
(c)
acquisition and utilization of computer
equipment and training materials for the purposes of
paragraphs
(a) and (b) above.
 
4.
Provision of technical assistance to the Ministry of
Economy and Finance of the Borrower to assist in the
carrying out of studies:
 
(a)
of the Borrower's banking legislation and
regulations for the purpose of updating and adjusting the
same to the present day needs of the Borrower;
(b)
of the Borrower's development banking sector for
the purpose of assessing the necessity for restructuring
or consolidating existing institutions in such sector;
and
(c)
of the Borrower's non-banking financial sector
and informal credit sector for the purpose of: (i)
assessing the size and importance to the Borrower's
economy of such sectors;
(ii)
assessing the necessity for
protection of small saving accounts through regulation;
and
(iii)
assessing the resources available for the
financing of industrial development projects, and the
feasibility of establishing new financial markets there
for.
 
The project is expected to be completed by December
31, 1994.
