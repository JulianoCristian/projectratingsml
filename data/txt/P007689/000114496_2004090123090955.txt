Re:    Loan 3943-ME (Second Basic Health Care Project) Amendment to the 
Loan and 
Guarantee Agreements and to the Implementation Letter
Dear Sirs:
We refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank) and Nacional Financiera, S.N.C. (the Borrower), to the 
Guarantee Agreement between United Mexican States (the Guarantor) and the Bank and to 
the Implementation Letter, all dated October 8, 1995 and as subsequently amended, with 
respect to the above-captioned Project.  We are pleased to inform you of our agreement 
to amend said Loan Agreement, Guarantee Agreement and Implementation Letter as 
indicated below.
I.
Amendments to the Loan Agreement
1.
Section 1.02 (c) is hereby deliberately left blank.
2.
Section 2.03 is hereby amended by replacing the date “June 30, 2001” with the 
date “June 30, 2002”.
3.
Section 5.01 (c) is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety:  “that 
the PCU has been established and staffed as set forth in Section 3.11 (a) of the 
Guarantee Agreement;”.
4.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended to read in its entirety 
as indicated in Attachment A to this letter.
5.
Paragraph 2 (a) of Schedule 1 is hereby amended to read as follows in its 
entirety:  ““incremental staff and operating costs” means costs that would not have 
been incurred absent the Project and which are covered by the following SSA budget 
line-item classification codes (“clasificadores”) in effect as of August 1, 1995 (as 
the coverage of such codes’ scope is limited, if at all, by the parentheticals set 
forth below and by the last sentence of this paragraph):
1200 (salaries);
1300 (personnel non-salary benefits);
2100 (office supplies and other non-medical consumable materials);
2200 (only with respect to a nutritional supplement formula for babies under six 
years of age and for pregnant or lactating women, as part of Part A.1 (a) (ii) of the 
Project);
2300 (spare parts and small tools);
2400 (construction materials for community-built latrines);
2600 (fuel);
2700 (uniforms for field personnel);
Page  2
3100 (utilities and postage);
3200 (Project office space rental);
3400 (commercial, banking and customs services, excluding insurance services);
3500 (maintenance of equipment, vehicles and buildings obtained or constructed 
pursuant to the Project);
3600 (dissemination and communication services);
3700 (travel expenses only for TAPs and midwives selected for purposes of 
carrying out Part A of the Project);
3800 (expenses for conventions/congresses); and
4100 (fellowships only for said TAPs and midwives).
In no case may any of such costs include salaries or other staff remuneration or 
benefits other than with respect to:  (i) hardship post incentives for doctors, nurses 
and paramedics under Part A.2 (b) of the Project; (ii) SSA and SHA technical-level 
temporary fixed-term staff hired for purposes of carrying out Parts A and C of the 
Project; (iii) temporary fixed-term PCU and SPCU staff hired for purposes of the 
Project; and (iv) TAPs and midwives selected for purposes of carrying out Part A of 
the Project;”.
6.
Paragraphs 2 (b) and (c) of Schedule 1 are hereby deliberately left blank.
7.
The last sentence of Schedule 2 is hereby amended by replacing the date 
“December 31, 2000” with the date “December 31, 2001”.
II.
Amendments to the Guarantee Agreement
8.
Section 3.01 (d) is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety:  “have 
the PCU coordinate execution of the Project by the entities referred to in paragraphs 
(a) through (c) of this Section.”.
9.
Section 3.11 (a) is hereby amended by replacing the words “within SSA” with the 
words “within the SSA’s Dirección General de Extensión de Cobertura”.
10.
Section 3.11 (d) is hereby deliberately left blank.
11.
Schedule 1 is hereby amended to read in its entirety as indicated in Attachment 
B to this letter.
III.
Amendments to the Implementation Letter
12.
Attachments I through IV to the Implementation Letter are hereby amended to read 
in their entirety as indicated in Attachment C to this letter.
13.
Attachment V to the Implementation Letter is hereby deleted.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
the three originals of this letter in the space provided below, and returning one to 
us.  The terms of this letter shall enter into effect as of its date first above 
written, upon receipt by the Bank of one original of this letter signed by you.  
Consequently, the amendments reflected in the table set forth in Attachment A to this 
letter shall not apply to expenditures covered by Loan proceeds withdrawal requests 
received by the Bank prior to said date first above written; such amendments shall 
therefore only apply to additional expenditures covered by Loan proceeds withdrawal 
requests received by the Bank on or after said date first above written.
Sincerely yours,
