LOAN NUMBER 4370-CO
PROJECT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated December 4, 1998, between INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION 
AND DEVELOPMENT (the Bank) and INSTITUTO NACIONAL DE VIAS (INVIAS).
WHEREAS by the Loan Agreement of even date herewith between Republic of Colombia 
(the Borrower) and the Bank, the Bank has agreed to make available to the Borrower an 
amount equal to one hundred thirty seven million one hundred thousand dollars 
($137,100,000), on the terms and conditions set forth in the Loan Agreement, but only 
on condition that INVIAS agree to undertake such obligations toward the Bank as are 
set forth in this Agreement;
WHEREAS INVIAS, in consideration of the Bank’s entering into the Loan Agreement 
with the Borrower, has agreed to undertake the obligations set forth in this 
Agreement;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
Definitions
Section 1.01. Unless the context otherwise requires, the several terms defined 
in the Loan Agreement, the Preambles thereto and to this Project Agreement, and the 
General Conditions (as so defined) have the respective meanings therein set forth.
ARTICLE II
Page  2
Execution of the Project
Section 2.01. (a) INVIAS declares its commitment to the objectives of the 
Project as set forth in Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement, and, to this end, shall take 
all such action on its part as shall be required to carry out the Project and to cause 
the Concessionaire to carry out the Project, all with due diligence and efficiency and 
in conformity with appropriate administrative, financial, engineering and 
environmental practices, and shall provide, or cause to be provided, promptly as 
needed, the funds, facilities, services and other resources required for the Project, 
as stipulated in the Contract.
(b)
INVIAS: (i) shall comply with all its obligations under the Environmental 
Management Plan, the Resettlement Plan and the Social Assistance Program in respect of 
the Project; and (ii) shall exercise all of its rights under the Contract, including 
the joint and several rights conferred upon INVIAS by virtue of the stipulations of 
Section 2.1.1 (iii) of the Contract and the corresponding stipulations in each of the 
Technical Agreements entered into between the Concessionaire and each of the Technical 
Assistants and referred to in clause (D) of the Preamble to the Loan Agreement, to 
cause the Concessionaire and each of the Technical Assistants to comply with their 
respective obligations under the Contract, the applicable Technical Agreement and 
under the aforesaid Plans and Program.
(c)
Except as otherwise agreed with the Bank: (i) INVIAS shall not assign, 
amend, revoke, abrogate or fail to enforce the Contract or any of the aforesaid Plans 
and Program or any provision thereof; and (ii) shall cause the Concessionaire not to 
assign, amend, revoke, abrogate or permit the assignment or fail to enforce any 
Technical Agreement or any provision thereof.
(d)
Without limitation upon the provisions of paragraph (a) of this Section 
and except as the Bank and INVIAS shall otherwise agree, INVIAS shall:
(i)
maintain policies and procedures adequate to enable it to monitor 
and evaluate 
on an ongoing basis, in accordance with the indicators set 
forth in the letter 
from INVIAS to the Bank of even date herewith, 
the carrying out of the Project 
and the achievement of the objectives 
thereof;
(ii)
prepare, under terms of reference satisfactory to the Bank, and 
furnish to the 
Bank, on or about August 31 in each year, a report 
integrating the results of 
the monitoring and evaluation activities 
performed pursuant to subparagraph 
(i) above, on the progress 
achieved in the carrying out of the Project during 
the twelve-month 
period preceding the date of said report and setting out the 
measures recommended to ensure the efficient carrying out of the Project and 
the achievement of the objectives thereof during the period following such 
date; and
(iii)
review with the Bank, by September 30, or such later date as the 
Bank shall 
request, the corresponding report referred to in 
subparagraph (ii) above, and, 
thereafter, take all measures required 
to ensure the efficient completion of 
the Project and the achievement 
of the objectives thereof, based on the 
conclusions and 
recommendations of the said report and the Bank’s views on the 
matter.
Section 2.02. Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree, INVIAS shall ensure that 
procurement by the Concessionaire (or a future concessionaire for the Project, if any) 
of goods, works and services required for the Project shall be governed by the 
provisions of Section 3.03 of the Loan Agreement.
Section 2.03. (a) INVIAS shall carry out the obligations set forth in Sections 
9.06, 9.07, and 9.09 of the General Conditions, (relating to plans and schedules, 
records and reports and land acquisition, respectively) in respect of the Project.
(b)
INVIAS shall:
Page  3
(i)
prepare on the basis of guidelines acceptable to the Bank, and 
furnish to the 
Bank not later than a date six (6) months after the 
date on which the Project 
is expected to be completed according to 
Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement or 
the date on which the minutes of 
commencement of the operation stage are 
signed in accordance 
with the provisions of Clause 2.2 of the Contract, 
whichever is 
earlier, or on any other date as may be agreed for this purpose 
between the Bank and INVIAS, a plan for the future operation of the Project; 
and
(ii)
afford the Bank a reasonable opportunity to exchange views with 
INVIAS on said 
plan.
Section 2.04. If during the life of the Contract, an unforeseen population 
resettlement is required from any site where Project works will be carried out (other 
than the resettlement(s) specifically foreseen under, and provided for in, the 
Resettlement Plan): (a) INVIAS shall submit to the Bank an ad-hoc resettlement plan 
for the population in question, such plan to be prepared following the same principles 
and guidelines satisfactory to the Bank contained in the documents titled “Política 
Reasentamiento INVIAS” (INVIAS Resettlement Policy) and “Metodología para el avalúo de 
predios afectados por la Construcción de Vías” (Methodology for the Assessment of 
Properties Affected by Road Construction); and (b) after receiving Bank’s confirmation 
that the ad-hoc plan submitted is consistent with such principles and guidelines, 
INVIAS shall execute, and cause the Concessionaire and each of the applicable 
Technical Assistants to execute, the resettlement in question in accordance with the 
corresponding ad-hoc plan, and shall cause the Concessionaire and each such Technical 
Assistant not to commence any work unless INVIAS has given the corresponding 
authorization after being satisfied, and having satisfied the Bank, that all the 
corresponding steps under the ad-hoc plan in question have been taken.
Section 2.05. (a) INVIAS shall, at the request of the Bank, exchange views with 
the Bank with regard to progress of the Project, the performance of its obligations 
under this Agreement and under the contracts and other matters relating to the 
purposes of the Loan, including, the performance of the Concessionaire’s obligations 
under the Contract and of each Technical Assistant’s obligations under the applicable 
Technical Agreement.
(b)
INVIAS shall promptly inform the Bank of any condition which interferes 
or threatens to interfere with the progress of the Project, the accomplishment of the 
purposes of the Loan, or the performance by INVIAS of its obligations under this 
Agreement, the performance by INVIAS and the Concessionaire of their respective 
obligations under the Contract, the Environmental Management Plan, the Resettlement 
Plan, the Social Assistance Program and any ad-hoc resettlement plan to be executed 
under Section 2.04 of this Agreement, and the performance by the Technical Assistants 
of their respective obligations, if any, under the applicable Technical Agreement and 
the aforesaid Plans, Program and ad-hoc resettlement plan(s), if any.
ARTICLE III
Financial Covenants
Section 3.01. (a) INVIAS shall: (i) maintain and cause the Concessionaire to 
maintain records and, exclusively in the case of the Concessionaire, separate accounts 
adequate to reflect in accordance with sound accounting practices their respective 
operations and financial condition in respect of the Project; and (ii) cause the 
Trustee to maintain similar records and separate accounts adequate to reflect in 
accordance with such practices the transactions and other entries in each Trust 
Account.
(b)
INVIAS shall:
(i)
have its records in respect of the Project, for each fiscal year 
audited, in 
accordance with appropriate auditing principles consistently 
applied, by 
independent auditors acceptable to the Bank;
(ii)
furnish to the Bank as soon as available, but in any case not 
later than four 
months after the end of each such year: (A) certified 
Page  4
copies of its records in 
respect of the Project for such year as so 
audited; and (B) the report of such 
audit by said auditors of such 
scope and in such detail as the Bank shall have 
reasonably 
requested; and
(iii)
furnish to the Bank such other information concerning said 
records, as well as 
the audit thereof, as the Bank shall from time 
to time reasonably request.
(c)
Without limitation upon the provisions of paragraphs (a) and (b) above, 
INVIAS shall:
(i)
exercise its rights under the Contract to ensure that the 
Concessionaire and 
the Trustee have the Concessionaire’s accounts and 
each Trust Account, 
respectively, audited for each fiscal 
year in accordance with appropriate 
auditing principles 
consistently applied, by independent auditors; 
(ii)
furnish to the Bank, as soon as available, certified copies of all 
information 
on accounting matters to be furnished to INVIAS under 
Clauses 5.9 and 5.22 of 
the Contract; and
(iii)
take all such action as shall be necessary or convenient to ensure 
that the 
services of the Interventor shall be available until the 
termination of the 
Contract.
Section 3.02 (a) Without limitation or restriction upon its obligations under 
Section 2.01 (e) of this Agreement, INVIAS shall exercise its rights under Clauses 
2.1.1. (v) and 6.2 of the Contract, or under the laws of the Borrower in respect of: 
(i) the Concessionaire; or (ii) the performance by a Trustee, both in regard to each 
Trust Account and especially INVIAS’ ability to recover any amount unduly paid by the 
Trustee to the Concessionaire.
(b)
If any amount is recovered pursuant to the preceding provisions, INVIAS 
shall promptly inform the Bank thereon, so as to enable the Bank to determine whether 
the amount in question shall be refunded to the Bank and, in such case, whether said 
amount shall be credited to the Loan Account for subsequent withdrawal or for 
cancellation in accordance with the relevant provisions of this Loan Agreement, 
including the General Conditions.
ARTICLE IV
Effective Date; Termination;
Cancellation and Suspension
Section 4.01. This Agreement shall come into force and effect on the date upon 
which the Loan Agreement becomes effective.
Section 4.02. This Agreement and all obligations of the Bank and of INVIAS 
thereunder shall terminate on the date on which the Loan Agreement shall terminate in 
accordance with its terms, and the Bank shall promptly notify INVIAS thereof.
Section 4.03. All the provisions of this Agreement shall continue in full force 
and effect notwithstanding any cancellation or suspension under the General 
Conditions.
