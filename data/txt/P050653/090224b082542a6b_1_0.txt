                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project will assist Government of Karnataka (GOK) in: (i) increasing rural communities' access to improved and sustainable drinking water and sanitation services;
                         and (ii) institutionalizing decentralization of Rural Water Supply and Sanitation (RWSS) service delivery to Gram Panchayats (village governments) and user groups.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         1. The project continues to make good progress towards achieving targets on provision of access to improved water sources (1.47 million people against a target of 1.78 million)
                         and improved sanitation facilities (1.26 million people, against an end target of 1.4 million people).
                         2. A little more than 50% of the households have private water connections, up several times relative to the baseline figure of 8%.
                         3. Innovative activities such as solar pumping, solar water purifying kiosks, mobile potable water carriers, etc. aimed at supplementing and complementing the service delivery of
