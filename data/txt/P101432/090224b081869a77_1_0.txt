                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation continues to advance satisfactorily. The Project reached the PDO indicators and 9 of the 22 intermediate indicators. The Project is fully disbursed. From now
                         until the Project's close on March 31, 2013, OSE will focus on expanding the application of output based disbursement for sanitation connections, improving NRW reduction activities,
                         and strengthening environmental management among other activities.
