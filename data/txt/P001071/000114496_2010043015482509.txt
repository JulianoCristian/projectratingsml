     Re:  Credit No. 1995-GUI
          (Socio-Economic Development Support Project)
          Amendment
Excellency,
     Please refer to the development Credit Agreement (the
Agreement), dated April 19, 1989 between the Republic of Guinea (the
Borrower) and the International Development Association (the
Association).
     Pursuant to your letter of December 1, 1992 (No.
0020/MPF/CAB/CSE/92), we are pleased to inform you that the
Association agrees to amend the Agreement as follows:
     - The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is revised
     to read in its entirety as set out in Attachment 1 hereto;
     - Part D of Schedule 2 is deleted;
     - Schedule 5, paragraph 1 (c), the words "an amount of
     $100,000" are replaced with the words "an amount of $150,000".
     Please confirm your agreement with this amendment of the
Agreement by signing, dating and returning the form of confirmation
on the enclosed copy of this letter.  This amendment shall become
effective upon receipt by the Association of the duly signed copy.
Sincerely yours,
Olivier Lafourcade
