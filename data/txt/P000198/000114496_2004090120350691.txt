Re:  BURUNDI:  Credit No. 1881 BU
(Education Sector Development Project)
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
Monsieur le Ministre,
I refer to the agreement (hereinafter referred to as the Development Credit 
Agreement) dated March 28, 1988 between the Republic of Burundi (the Borrower) and the 
International Development
Association (the Association) and to your letter dated December 27, 1989.
I am pleased to inform you that the Association has agreed to your request to 
increase the authorized allocation for the Special Account to $1,000,000.
Consequently, the Association proposes to hereby amend the Development Credit 
Agreement by the deletion of the amount of $500,000 in paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 5 
thereto and the substitution therefor of the amount $1,000,000.
If you agree to the proposed amendment, please signify such agreement by signing 
and dating the form of confirmation below on one of the duplicate originals sent to 
you and returning it to us
for our records.  The amendment shall come into force on the date of countersignature 
by you.
Yours sincerely,
By /s/ Paul Isenman
Director
South-Central and Indian Ocean Department
Africa Region
