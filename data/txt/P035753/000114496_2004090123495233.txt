Credit 3084-NI (Health Sector Modernization Project)
Amendment to the Credit Agreement
Dear Mr. Duque Estrada:
With reference to our discussions during the past months in respect of changes 
required to adjust the Development Credit Agreement between the Republic of Nicaragua 
(the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association) for the 
above Project, dated July 8, 1998 (such Agreement hereinafter called the Credit 
Agreement), to the circumstances now prevailing and to lessons learned in the carrying 
out of the Project described in Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement, we are pleased to 
propose the following amendments to the Credit Agreement:
1.
Paragraph (c) of  Section 1.02 of the Credit Agreement is hereby deleted, 
paragraphs (a) and (b) of the said Section are hereby re-lettered as paragraphs (b) 
and (c) thereof, and a new paragraph (a) for said Section is hereby inserted to read 
as follows:
"(a)
BHSP Subproject" means the provision of a given basic health 
services package to a given community through (i) community-based arrangements 
which will involve the contracting by MOH (as defined below), for the benefit 
of the 
community in question, of one or more non public and non profit service 
providers 
with experience in the provision of health services; and (ii) which 
package will 
be eligible for financing out of the proceeds of the Credit in 
accordance 
with the provisions of this Agreement and of the Operational Manual 
(as defined 
below);"
2. 
Paragraph (d) of Section 1.02 of the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to read 
as follows:
"(d)
"Indicator" and "Action Plan Benchmark" mean, respectively, a 
performance and impact indicator to be used to measure performance in and 
impact of the carrying out of activities at the end of the Project,  and an 
indicator to guide and measure implementation progress while the Project is 
under execution, all as set forth in the supplemental letter of even date 
herewith which supersedes the supplemental letter No. 1 signed by the Borrower 
and the Association and dated July 8, 1998;"
3. 
Paragraph (g) of Section 1.02 of the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to read 
as follows:
      "INSS Subsidiary Agreement means the agreement entered into between the
Borrower and INSS pursuant to Section 3.01 (b) of this Agreement, as amended 
as of April 15, 2000, and as the same may be amended from time to time in 
accordance with the provisions of said paragraph (b);"
4. 
Paragraph (m) of Section 1.02 of the Credit Agreement is hereby amended to read 
as follows:
"Operational Manual" means a manual, satisfactory to the Association, 
setting forth procedures for implementation of all parts of the Project and 
the criteria for preparing and selecting BHSP Subprojects and Hospital 
Rehabilitation Subprojects, as such manual may be amended from time to time by 
agreement between the Borrower and the Association;"
5. 
The first line of Paragraph (n) of Section 1.02 of the Credit Agreement is 
hereby amended to read as follows:

      The following changes are made  in the Description of the Project set

in  Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement:
(I) -  Subparagraph (a) of Part A.1 of the Project (Health Care Primary 
 Health are hereby amended to read as follows:
"(a)

provision of micronutrients to women and children for food fortification.
 (b) Carrying out of: (i) an information, education and communication 
campaign on nutrition and breastfeeding; and (ii) epidemiological surveillance 
of nutritional deficiencies."

 is hereby amended by reducing the number of Women Centers to be

 hereby amended to read as follows:
"1

strengthening of hospital management in selected hospitals; and (b) technical 
assistance for the monitoring and evaluation of the activities in Part B.1, 
through 3 (a) above."

to read as follows:
"8. Provision of technical assistance for the monitoring and evaluation of 
the activities in Part C.1 through C.7 above."
(VI) - Part D of the Project  (INSS), is hereby expanded adding thereto new 
 paragraphs 5 and 6 to read as follows:
"5. Provision of technical assistance and training for the development of

activities in Part D.1 through D.5 above."

2.
For the purposes of this Schedule:
(a)
the term "foreign expenditures" means expenditures in the currency of any 
country other than that of the Borrower for goods or services supplied from the 
territory of any country other than that of the Borrower;
(b)
the term "local expenditures" means expenditures in the currency of the 
Borrower or for goods or services supplied from the territory of the Borrower;
(c)
the term "Incremental operating costs" means the incremental expenses 
incurred by MOH on account of Project implementation, management and monitoring, 
including office supplies, vehicle operation, travel and supervision costs but 
excluding salaries of officials of the Borrower;
(d)
the term "Printing and IEC Services" means the cost of printing and 
distribution of materials for all Parts of the Project, and implementation of 
workshops and seminars, media advertising and other costs related to the information 
and communication campaigns to be carried out under Parts A.1 (a) (ii), A.2 (b) (i), 
C.7, and D.2 (b) of the Project; and
(e)
the term "Training" means the cost of tuition fees, travel and per-diem 
of trainers and participants, training materials, rental of facilities and equipment 
and other costs related to training to be carried out under the Project.
