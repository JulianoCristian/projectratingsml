Re: Credit 3084-NI (Health Sector Modernization Project)
    Amendment of Development Credit Agreement: Indicators and 
    Action Plan Benchmark. Sections 1.02 (d) and  3.06.
We refer to the letter of even date herewith entered into between the Republic 
of Nicaragua (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the 
Association), amending the Development Credit Agreement for the above Project dated 
July 8, 1998. 
 With reference to said Credit Agreement, in particular the Borrower’s 
obligations under section 3.06 thereof, and the amendments to said Agreement, in 
particular those in paragraph 2 of the aforesaid letter, we wish to advise you that 
the end of Project Indicators and Action Plan Benchmarks referred to in said paragraph 
2 for purposes, inter alia, of the aforesaid Section 3.06, are those set forth in 
Annexes I and II to this letter. It is our understanding that said Indicators and 
Benchmarks are satisfactory to the Association.
