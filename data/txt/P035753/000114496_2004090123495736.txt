Re: 
Credit 3084-NI (Health Sector Modernization Project) 
Fifth Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency: 
 
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement between the Republic of Nicaragua 
(the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association) dated July 8, 
1998 as amended, with regard to the above-captioned Project. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that, following the request made by the Ministry of Health, 
in a letter dated April 23, 2003 in relation to the aggregate amounts to procure goods under 
international and national shopping procedures, the Association hereby proposes to amend 
Section I, Parts C. 3 and C.4 of Schedule 3 to the Development Credit Agreement to read as 
follows: 
 
“3. International 
Shopping 
 
Vehicles estimated to cost less than $25,000 equivalent per contract and other 
goods estimated to cost les than $150,000 equivalent per contract, up to an aggregate amount not 
to exceed $580,000 equivalent each for vehicles and other goods, may be procured under 
contracts awarded on the basis of international shopping procedures in accordance with the 
provisions of paragraphs 3.5 and 3.6 of the Guidelines. 
 
4. National 
Shopping 
 
Goods (other than vehicles) and printing services estimated to cost less than 
$25,000 equivalent per contract, up to an aggregate amount not to exceed $340,000 equivalent, 
may be procured under contracts awarded on the basis of national shopping procedures in 
accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 3.5 and 3.6 of the Guidelines.”
