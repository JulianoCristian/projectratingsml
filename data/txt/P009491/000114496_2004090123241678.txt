Re: BANGLADESH - Development Credit No. 1940-BD
(Rural Road and Markets Improvements and Maintenance Project)
Amendment of the Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement between the People's 
Republic of Bangladesh (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association) dated July 29, 1988, as amended. We refer 
also to your Nlinistry's letter of April 6, 1995 requesting the 
Association for an extension of the Closing Date of the Credit, and a 
reallocation of Credit proceeds, in order to facilitate the financing of 
auditing services and additional road maintenance and building works 
required under the project.
The Association is pleased to inform you that, after due 
consideration, it has agreed to amend the Development Credit Agreement as 
follows:
