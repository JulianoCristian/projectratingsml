Dhaka, Bangladesh
Re:
Credit No. 1940-BD
(Rural Road and Markets Improvements and Maintenance Project)
Amendment of the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Sir:
We would like to refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) dated 
July 29, 1988 between People's Republic of Bangladesh and the International 
Development Association (the Association). We would also like to refer to your letter 
of November 9, 1992 in which you requested the Association to amend the Agreement. We 
are pleased to inform you that the Association has agreed to your request and the 
Agreement will be amended as follows:
1.
Category 3 of paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement will read as follows:
 
"(3)  Feeder Road Maintenance
900,000
70% in FY 92
and FY 93;
56% in FY 94 and
42% in FY 95."
2.
Category 5 of paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement will read as follows:
 
"5)(a) Consultant's Services and Training for Part A of the Project 
(except those under Part A.9 of the Project)
(b) Consultant's Services under Part A.9 of the Project 140,000 75%"
3.
Category 6 of paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement will read as follows:
 
"6 Unallocated
5,260,000"
 
4.
Paragraph 9 of Part A of Schedule 2 to the Agreement will read as follows:
 
"9. Providing architectural, engineering design and advisory services 
and the construction of a new headquarters building for the Local Government 
Engineering Bureau."
5.
Paragraph 10 of Part A of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is hereby deleted and 
replaced by the following:
"10. Carrying out a study on disaster assessment and emergency 
maintenance planning."
6.
A new paragraph 11 will be added to Part A of Schedule 2 to the Agreement as 
follows:
"11. Preparation of a follow-up Project."
If you agree with the above proposals, please sign, date and return to us the 
attached copy of this letter. The amendments shall become effective on the date of 
your confirmation as specified hereunder.
Sincerely,
Ann O. Hamilton
Director
