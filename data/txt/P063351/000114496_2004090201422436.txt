LOAN AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated March 9, 1999, between FEDERATIVE REPUBLIC OF BRAZIL (the 
Borrower) and INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (the Bank):
WHEREAS (A) the Bank has received a letter dated December 9, 1998 from the 
Borrower: (i) describing its macroeconomic framework and a program of actions, 
objectives and policies designed to achieve structural adjustment of the Borrower’s 
social protection system through the adoption, development and funding of a set of 
selected social protection programs (hereinafter called the Program), which Program 
consists of actions and policies that have already been taken and adopted by the 
Borrower as described in Schedule 3 to this Agreement (hereinafter called the First 
Phase of the Program) as well as actions and policies that the Borrower intends to 
take and adopt in the near future in order to increase the equity, efficiency and 
counter-cyclical response capacity of the Borrower’s social protection system; (ii) 
declaring the Borrower’s commitment to the objectives of the Program; and (iii) 
requesting assistance from the Bank in support of the Program during the execution 
thereof; and
(B)
on the basis, inter alia, of the foregoing, the Bank has decided to 
provide assistance to the Borrower in support of the First Phase of the Program by 
making the loan provided for in Article II of this Agreement (the Loan) as hereinafter 
provided;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:

ARTICLE II 
The Loan 
Section 2.01. The Bank agrees to lend to the Borrower, on the terms and 
conditions set forth or referred to in this Agreement, an amount equal to two hundred 
fifty-two million five hundred twenty thousand Dollars ($252,520,000). 
Section 2.02. (a) Subject to the provisions of paragraphs (b) and (c) of this 
Section, the Borrower shall be entitled to withdraw the amount of two hundred fifty 
million Dollars ($250,000,000) from the Loan Account in support of the First Phase of 
the Program. 
(b)
The Borrower shall open, prior to furnishing to the Bank the first 
request for withdrawal from the Loan Account, and thereafter maintain in the 
Borrower’s Central Bank a deposit account in Dollars on terms and conditions 
satisfactory to the Bank. Except as provided in Section 2.04 of this Agreement, all 
withdrawals from the Loan Account shall be deposited by the Bank into the Deposit 
Account. 
(c)
The Borrower undertakes that the proceeds of the Loan shall not be used 
to finance expenditures excluded pursuant to the provisions of Schedule 1 to this 
Agreement.  If the Bank shall have determined at any time that any proceeds of the 
Loan shall have been used to make a payment for an expenditure so excluded, the 
Borrower shall, promptly upon notice from the Bank; (i) deposit into the Deposit 
Account an amount equal to the amount of said payment; or (ii) if the Bank shall so 
request, refund such amount to the Bank.  Amounts refunded to the Bank upon such 
request shall be credited to the Loan Account for cancellation.

Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be June 30, 1999 or such later date as the 
Bank shall establish.  The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower of such later date. 
Section 2.04. The Borrower shall pay to the Bank a fee in the amount of two 
million five hundred twenty thousand Dollars ($2,520,000).  On or promptly after the 
Effective Date, the Bank shall, on behalf of the Borrower, withdraw from the Loan 
Account and pay to itself the amount of said fee. 
Section 2.05. The Borrower shall pay to the Bank a commitment charge at the rate 
of three-fourths of one percent (3/4 of 1%) per annum on the principal amount of the 
Loan not withdrawn from time to time.  
Section 2.06. (a) The Borrower shall pay interest on the principal amount of the 
Loan withdrawn and outstanding from time to time, at a rate for each Interest Period 
equal to LIBOR Base Rate plus 4 (four) percentage points.
(b)
For the purposes of this Section: 
(i)
“Interest Period” means the initial period from and including the 
date of this 
Agreement to, but excluding, the first Interest Payment Date 
occurring 
thereafter, and after the initial period, each period 
from and including an 
Interest Payment Date to, but excluding the 
next following Interest Payment 
Date. 
(ii)
“Interest Payment Date” means any date specified in Section 2.07 
of this 
Agreement. 
(iii)
“LIBOR Base Rate” means, for each Interest Period, the London 
interbank 
offered rate for six-month deposits in Dollars for 
value the first day of such 
Interest Period (or, in the case of the 
initial Interest Period, for value the 
Interest Payment Date occurring 
on or next preceding the first day of such 
Interest Period), as 
reasonably determined by the Bank and expressed as a 
percentage 
per annum. 
(c)
The Bank shall notify the Borrower of the LIBOR Base Rate for each 
Interest Period, promptly upon the determination thereof. 
(d)
Whenever, in light of changes in market practice affecting the 
determination of the interest rates referred to in this Section 2.06, the Bank 
determines that it is in the interest of its borrowers as a whole and of the Bank to 
apply a basis for determining the interest rates applicable to the Loan other than as 
provided in said Section, the Bank may modify the basis for determining the interest 
rates applicable to the Loan upon not less than six (6) months’ notice to the Borrower 
of the new basis.  Such new basis shall become effective on the expiry of the notice 
period unless the Borrower notifies the Bank during said period of its objection 
thereto, in which case said modification shall not apply to the Loan.
Section 2.07. Interest and other charges shall be payable semiannually in 
arrears on February 15 and August 15 in each year.
Section 2.08. The Borrower shall repay the principal amount of the Loan in 
accordance with the amortization schedule set forth in Schedule 2 to this Agreement.

SCHEDULE 3
First Phase of the Program
1.
Adoption or expansion of social protection programs through, inter alia, the 
following measures:  
a.
Education
(i)
Adoption of a program to provide financial support for non-salary 
expenditures 
directly to schools, known as “Dinheiro na Escola” (Money to 
Schools).
(ii)
Increase of about 40% in the coverage of the Borrower’s school 
meal program 
(Alimentação Escolar) by reducing barriers to the 
participation of poorer 
municipalities.
b.
Health 
(i)
Adoption of a policy (Piso de Atenção Básica) to transfer 
resources to the 
municipalities for use on ambulatory care, 
according to a per-capita formula. 
(ii)
Adoption and expansion of a program to improve the Borrower’s 
Family Health 
Program (Programa de Saúde da Família). 
c.
Social Assistance
(i)
Implementation on a pilot basis of the Borrower’s program to 
provide financial 
support to poor families that keep their children in 
primary school (Bolsa 
Escola).
(ii)
Extension of the Borrower’s program for the eradication of child 
labor to new 
industries and geographical areas in the Borrower’s 
territory.
d.
Labor
(i)
Introduction, under certain conditions, of a supplement to 
unemployment 
benefits to formal sector workers who have been 
unemployed for a period of 
more than 12 months, consisting 
of up to three additional payments to be made 
until the 18th month of 
unemployment.
(ii)
Legislation allowing temporary suspension of labor contracts with 
the 
provision of opportunities for retraining of workers.
2.
Submission of a budget proposal to the Borrower’s Congress which includes the 
preservation of appropriate budgetary allocation for 1999 for funding of the social 
protection programs set forth in the Annex to this Schedule 3.
