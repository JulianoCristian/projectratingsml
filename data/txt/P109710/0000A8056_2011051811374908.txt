                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project is to contribute to the GoI's initiatives to assist MSMEs affected by the earthquake to revitalize their businesses and to re-integrate affected low
                         income communities into economic life.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is making progress towards achievement of the development objectives and the outcomes are satisfactory. Despite the eruption of Mr. Merapi (volcano) in October 2010,
                         total cumulative expenditures reached US$6.3 million as at March 1, 2011, reaching 11,157 MSME beneficiaries, with zero non-performing loans (NPL).
