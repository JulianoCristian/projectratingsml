                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the proposed Health System Improvement Project is to put in place the first phase of steps towards reform of the health system in the Republic of
                         Montenegro, giving priority to increasing capacity for policy, planning and regulation; stabilizing health financing and improving primary health care service delivery.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project continues to implement well and is likely to achieve its Project Development Objective. All project activities are likely to be completed prior to the Loan closing date of
                         December 31, 2012. The Additional Financing portion of the loan has disbursed 44%, with an additional 22% committed. The most complex ICB tenders—for the three information
                         systems—have now been committed. Two ICB tenders for medical equipment and hardware have been delayed (17% of the Loan), but are still expected to be implemented before
                         the end of the project. Since the last visit, it is worth noting that international technical assistance for developing the basic benefit package (BBP) and to support the hospital
