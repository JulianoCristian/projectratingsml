                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The proposed APL project, Phase A, would increase The City of Buenos Aires resilience to flooding through protection of its critical infrastructure and the
                         introduction of a risk management approach to the government investment program. The project would focus on the issue of risk identification and reduction
                         through prevention, mitigation, education and training.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project has a good implementation pace, 63% of the tunneling works have been completed and are expected to be finalized in mid 2012. The "Centro de Interpretación
                         Hidráulico" (Hydraulic Interpretation Center), inaugurated in October 2009, is open to the public and offers information on the Project and guided visits to students and visitors in
                         general.
