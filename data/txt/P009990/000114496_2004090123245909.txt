                                                          January 29, 1991
The Secretary to the Government of India
Ministry of Finance
Department of Economic Affairs
New Delhi, India
Dear Sir:
      INDIA - Vocational Training Project - Amendment
              Credit 2008-IN
            Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated June
16, 1989 (the "Development Credit Agreement") between India and the
International Development Association (the "Association").  In response
to the request from Mr. Hardeepak Singh, Director, Department of Economic
Affairs vide his telex dated 10 December 1990, we are pleased to inform
you that the Association agrees to amend the Development Credit Agreement
for the purpose of increasing the limit under prudent local shopping.
            Accordingly, the Association agrees to amend the Development
Credit Agreement as follows:
1.          Part C of Schedule 3 to the Development Credit Agreement is
amended as follows:
      (a)   The amount "$118,500,000" in paragraph (2) is deleted and the
            amount "$106,000,000" is substituted therefor.
      (b)   The amount "$24,800,000" in paragraph (3) is deleted and the
            amount "$37,300,000 is substituted therefor.
            Please confirm your agreement on behalf of India to the
foregoing amendment by signing and dating this letter in the space
provided below.  This amendment may be executed in two counterparts each
of which shall be an original.
            Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed
original to the Association.  This amendment will be effective as of the
date of this letter upon receipt by the Association of one fully executed
original of this amendment.
                                         Very truly yours,
                                      Heinz Vergin, Director
                                   Country Department IV, India
                                            Asia Region
CONFIRMED:
INDIA
By:   /s/ Hardeepak Singh
