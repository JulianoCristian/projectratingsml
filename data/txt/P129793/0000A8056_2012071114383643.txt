                         Program Development Objectives
                         Program Development Objective (from Program Document)

                         GPRC-2 provides support to help overcome policy constraints and institutional bottlenecks to growth by: (i) improving the business environment; (ii) enhancing road

                         The financing agreement was signed on February 24, 2012, one day after Board approval. The credit was declared effective on May 21, 2012 and subsequently disbursed. The
