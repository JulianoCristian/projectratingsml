                         Implementation Status Overview
                          The overall progress and quality of the project is good. The project has achieved 93.5 percent physical progress and about 87 percent financial progress (relative to the overall
                         project revised cost of US$207.3 million; US$100 million from the MDF grant and US$ 107.3 million as contribution from the government of Indonesia. In August 2010 the project
                         received a suplemental additional financing of US$36.7 million to rehabilitate and reconstruct arount 50 km along Aceh West Coast national road from Calang to Meulaboh including
                         Kuala Bubun Bridge. Fifty sub-projects out of total fifty six have been completed, involving reconstruction of infrastructure and restoration of services related to water supply, roads,
                         ports, and drainage and flood control. The assets of completed sub-projects have been successfully transferred to the authorities responsible for their ongoing operation and
