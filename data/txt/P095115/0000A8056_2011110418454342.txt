                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Post Chernobyl Recovery Project (PCRP) has made good progress towards achieving its development objectives. About 140,000 patients, students, teachers, hospital staff and
                         families have benefited from the energy efficiency improvements. About 3,244 individual homes have been connected to gas. An estimated 360,000 megawatt hours/year of heat
                         energy have been saved. An estimated 6,000 megawatt hours/year of electricity have been saved. Carbon dioxide emissions have been reduced by 90,000 tons.
