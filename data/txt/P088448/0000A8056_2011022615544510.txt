                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall progress on project implementation remains moderately satisfactory. The Project Development Objectives are being successfully met by ECTEL as policy and regulatory
                         reforms in the sector continue to progress well, and the ICT pilot projects component is almost complete. However, the universal service component is experiencing delays, but is
                         expected to be completed by end 2011.
