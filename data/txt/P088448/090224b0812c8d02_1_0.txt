                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall progress on project implementation has been assessed as satisfactory. The Project Development Objectives are being successfully attained by ECTEL. Policy and regulatory
                         reforms in the sector with the support of the project have been assessed as satisfactory. In particular, as of October 1, 2011, consumers benefited from an 18 percent fall in the rate
                         of fixed to mobile calls, as per the terms of the new Price Cap Plan, supported under the Project.
