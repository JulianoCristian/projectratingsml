Restructuring Paper 
 
1. 
Proceeds for the eight loans and credits under the OECS Telecommunications & 
ICT Development Project (P088448), IBRD-47750, IBRD-47760, IBRD-47770, IBRD-
47780, IDA-40560, IDA-40570, IDA-40580, IDA-40590, will be reallocated as per Table 
1
in the following page. 
2. 
 The category definitions and percentages of financing remain the same as in the 
original loan and credit agreements as follows: 
 
3. 
The Project Development Objectives are being successfully met by ECTEL as 
policy and regulatory reforms in the sector continue to progress well, and the sector is 
experiencing substantial growth. The ICT pilots component is also progressing 
satisfactorily and is expected to be completed on schedule. However, there continue to be 
implementation delays on the Universal Service component, due to challenges in defining 
the projects for each of the ECTEL member states.  Thus progress on the project 
implementation remains moderately satisfactory. 
4. 
The proposed reallocation is necessary to correct a mistake that was made by the 
World Bank in processing an earlier reallocation request. The reallocation is also 
necessary in order to meet urgent payments that have become due under category 1, as 
well as to adjust the funding under category 3, for credits 4056-GRD, 4057-SLU, and 
4058-SV, which had been mistakenly overdrafted.
