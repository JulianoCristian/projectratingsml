First Amendment
Dear Mr. Director,
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the República Oriental del 
Uruguay (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank) dated October 6, 1998 (the “Loan Agreement”).
Pursuant to your request dated September 1, 2000, we are pleased to inform 
you that the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement so as to modify the 
percentage of the expenditures financed under Categories 1 (a) and 1 (b) of 
Schedule 1 of the Loan Agreement as set forth in the attachment to this 
amendment letter.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the 
two originals of this amendment letter and returning one fully signed original 
to us. The amendment letter set forth in this letter will be effective as of the 
date of its countersignature.
