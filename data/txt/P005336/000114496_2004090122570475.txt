Beirut, Lebanon
     Re:  Loan 3562-LE (Emergency Reconstruction and Rehabilitation
          Project) Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
     We refer to the Loan Agreement between the Lebanese Republic (the
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development
(the Bank) dated March 8, 1993, as amended (the Loan Agreement). We
also refer to your request that the Loan Agreement be amended to allow
for the financing of training activities of persons in several trades
through the use of amounts originally allocated for Part D (2) of the
Project.
     We are pleased to inform you that the Bank accedes to your request
and that the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
1.   In Section 1.02 (n) letters "MOTEVT" shall be deleted and replaced
with the letters "MVTE". The words "Technical Education and Vocational
Training" shall be deleted and replaced with the words "Vocational and
Technical Education".
2.   The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is amended to read as set
forth in the Attachment to this Letter.
3.   In Schedule 1, paragraph 2 (c) is amended so that a colon and the
letter (i) are added after the word "for" on the first line, a semi-
colon and the letters (ii) are added after the end of the paragraph
with the following additional words: "fees for staff trainers, and
stipends for trainees involved in the training activities under Part
D (2) of the Project".
4.   Schedule 2 of the Project is amended so that paragraph D (2) is
deleted, D (3) is relettered as D (2), and the new D (2) is amended
as follows:
     (a)  the word "MOTEVT" is deleted and replaced with "MVTE"; and
     (b)  a coma and the following additional words are added at the
end of the paragraph: "and the training of persons in key trades."
     Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing,
dating and returning to us the form of confirmation in the enclosed
copy of this letter.
