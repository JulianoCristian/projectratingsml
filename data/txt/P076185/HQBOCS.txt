Amendment to the Administration Arrangement between the Netherlands
Minister for Development Cooperation and the International Development
Association concerning the Multi-Donor Trust Fund for the Basic Education
Development Project in the Republic of Yemen
(TF No. TF053 719, Activity Number 9709)
Excellency,
1.    We refer to the Administration Arrangement between the International
Development Association ("IDA"), and the Netherlands Minister for Development
Cooperation (the "Donor"), regarding the Multi Donor Trust Fund concerning the Basic
Education Development Project in the Republic of Yemen (the "Recipient") (TF053719)
dated November 30, 2004 (the "Administration Agreement"), as amended.
2.    Pursuant to recent discussions between IDA and the Donor, IDA proposes to
amend Section 8.1 of Annex II to the Administration Agreement, to extend the End
Disbursement Date to December 31, 2013, to read as follows:
"8.1.  It is expected that the Contribution funds will be fully disbursed by IDA
in accordance with the provisions of this Administration Agreement by December
31, 2013, IDA shall only disburse Contribution funds for the purposes of this
Administration Agreement after such date with the written approval of the
Donors."
3.    All other terms of the Administration Agreement shall remain the same.
4.    IDA will disclose this Amendment Letter and related information on this Trust
Fund in accordance with the World Bank Policy on Access to Information. By entering
into this Amendment Letter, the Donor consents to disclosure of this Amendment Letter
and related information on this Trust Fund.
5.    We propose that this Letter shall constitute an amendment to the Administration
Agreement. Please confirm your acceptance to the foregoing, on behalf of the Donor, by
signing and dating the two originals, retaining one original for your records and returning
