                         Global Environmental Objectives
                         Global Environmental Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project development objective is "to support community-based integrated ecosystem management practices in targeted conservancies". The project global
                         objective is "to restore, secure and enhance key ecosystem processes in targeted conservancies with biodiversity and land conservation and sustainable use

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This GEF project is a key instrument assisting the Government of Namibia in addressing rural development in the communal lands of Namibia. A review of the project's outcome
                         targets indicates that four out of five Key Performance Indicators (KPI) have been either almost achieved or reached end-of-project targets. Assessment of KPI 5 (Biological integrity
