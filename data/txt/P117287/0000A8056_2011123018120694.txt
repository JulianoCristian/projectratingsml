                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The PRSC-6 was passed by the National Assembly on December 2, 2010 and ratified by the President on December 10, 2010. Funds were disbursed on December 29, 2010. A
                         Supplemental Financing to PRSC-6 (P125114) was prepared in the amount of US$22 million in response to the floods that hit Benin late in 2010. This Supplemental Financing was
                         approved by the Board on April 21, 2011, the Financial Agreement was signed April 27, 2011 and the additional funds have been disbursed.
