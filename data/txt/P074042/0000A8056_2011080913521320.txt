                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objectives of the Project are to:(i) develop and strengthen the capacity of the Baalbeck Hermel Water and Irrigation Authority (BHWIA) and the Zahle and
                         Chamsine Water Authorities; (ii) Improve access of customers of the BHWIA to satisfactory water supply and wastewater services; (iii) Involve the private sector
                         in the operation and maintenance of the water and wastewater facilities; and (iv) Rationalize the use of water through the introduction of water meters.
