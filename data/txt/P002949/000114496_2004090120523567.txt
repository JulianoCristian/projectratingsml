      Re:   Economic Recovery Credit H (Cr. 2087-UG)
            Amendment of Schedule to Arrangements for Administration
            Certain Funds to Be Made Available by the Minister for
            Development Cooperation of The Netherlands
Dear Sir:
      The International Development Association (the Association)
hereby agrees to the request of the Netherlands Authorities, dated
July 19, 1991, to further amend the Schedule to the above-named
Arrangements, dated December 11, 1986, between the Minister for
Development Cooperation of the Netherlands and the Association, to
add the contribution of 10 million Dutch Guilders (Dfl 10 million)
for the Economic Recovery Credit II (ERC-11) to the list of Projects
covered by the said Arrangements.
      We also confirm that the Netherlands contribution to ERC-II
will be associated with the first tranche as part of the initial
deposit.
