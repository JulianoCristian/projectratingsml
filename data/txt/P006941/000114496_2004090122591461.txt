Re:
Loan No. 3625-CR (Second Water Supply and Sewerage Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Minister Mrs. Ross:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank) and Instituto Costarricense de Acueductos y Alcantarillados 
(the Borrower) dated July 28, 1993, (the Loan Agreement), entered into with respect to 
the above referenced Project.  We are pleased to announce that pursuant to your 
request the Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows: 
Section 3.06. (b)
This Section is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety: "in respect 
to the annual review to be held in 1997 (mid term review), the Borrower shall review 
with the Bank, in addition to the matters referred to in (a) above: (i) progress in 
the achievement of the objectives of the Policy Statement including the implementation 
of the regulatory framework and policies related to the water sector; and (ii) 
progress in the implementation of the plan referred to under Section 7.01 (b) (ii) of 
this Agreement;"
Section 5.03. (a)
This Section is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety: "Except as 
the Bank shall otherwise agree, the Borrower shall maintains for each of its fiscal 
years after its fiscal year ending on December 31, 1992, a ratio of total operating 
expenses to total operating revenues not higher than 0.70 for fiscal years 1993 and 
1994, 0.66 for fiscal year 1995, 0.64 for fiscal year 1996, 0.62 for fiscal year 1997, 
0.60 for fiscal year 1998, 0.57 for fiscal year 1999, and 0.54 for
fiscal year 2000 and thereafter."
Section 5.04. (a)
This Section is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety: "Except as 
the Bank shall otherwise agree, the Borrower shall maintain, for each of its fiscal 
years after its fiscal year ending on December 31, 1992, a ratio of total personnel 
expenses to total operating expenses not higher than 0.58 for fiscal years 1993 
through 1995, 0.52 for fiscal year 1996 through 1999 and 0.51 for fiscal year 2000 and 
thereafter."
Schedule 6
This schedule is hereby amended to read in its entirety as set forth in the 
Attachment hereto.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter may be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation please 
return one fully executed original to us.  The provisions of this amendment letter 
will become effective upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this 
amendment letter.

Attachment
Schedule 6 - Implementation Program
This Schedule is hereby amended to read as follows in its entirety:
I.   Part A of the Project
A.   Part A.1 of the Project
1.   The Borrower, jointly with the Guarantor, shall: (a) carry out a study, not later 
than September 30, 1996, and under terms of reference satisfactory to the Bank, to 
evaluate the investment and cost recovery policies for the water supply and sewerage 
sector; (b) afford the Bank
a reasonable opportunity to review the results and recommendations of such study and 
exchange views with the Borrower and the Guarantor; (c) prepare and furnish to the 
Bank not later than December 31, 1996, an action plan, satisfactory to the Bank, based 
on the results and recommendations of such study and the Bank's comments thereon; and 
(d) implement such action plan in a manner and under a timetable satisfactory to the 
Bank.
2.
The Borrower, jointly with the Guarantor, shall: (a) carry out, not later than 
August 15, 1996, and under terms of reference satisfactory to the Bank, a study to 
evaluate the institutional framework in respect of the provision of water supply and 
sewerage services in the GAM; (b) afford the Bank a reasonable opportunity to review 
the results and recommendations of such study and exchange views thereon with the 
Borrower and the Guarantor; (c) not later than November 15, 1996, prepare and furnish 
to the Bank an action plan, satisfactory to the Bank, based on the results and 
recommendations of such study and the Bank's comments thereon; and (d) implement such 
action plan in a manner, and under a timetable, satisfactory to the Bank. 
3.
The Borrower, jointly with the Guarantor, shall: (a) carry out a study, not 
later than September 30, 1996, and under terms of reference satisfactory to the Bank, 
to evaluate the institutional framework in respect of the provision of water supply 
and sewerage services in Costa
Rica (except for the GAM); and (b) afford the Bank a reasonable opportunity to review 
the results and recommendations of such study and exchange views thereon with the 
Borrower and the Guarantor.
Part A.3 of the Project

The Borrower shall: (a) not later than March 31, 1998, complete the design of an 
integrated financial, budgetary and cost-accounting system; (b) not later than June 
30, 1998, prepare and furnish to the Bank, a plan, satisfactory to the Bank, for 
implementing the above system; and (c) implement such action plan in a manner, and 
under a timetable, satisfactory to the Bank.
C.
Part A.4 of the Project
 
The Borrower shall: (a) not later than July 31, 1996, complete the design of a 
model, acceptable to the Bank, to decentralize selected activities related to the 
provision of water supply and sewerage services including areas and functions to be 
contracted out with the private sector; (b) not later than September 30, 1996, prepare 
and furnish to the Bank an action plan, satisfactory to the Bank, for implementing the 
above model, such plan to include the contracting  out of all areas and functions 
identified in the above model; and (c) implement such action plan in a manner, and 
under a timetable, satisfactory to the Bank.
II.
Part B of the Project
 
The Borrower shall: (a) not later than March 31, 1998, complete the design of a 
comprehensive commercial system; (b) not later than June 30, 1998, prepare and furnish 
to the Bank, a plan, satisfactory to the Bank, for implementing the above system; and 
(c) implement such action plan in a manner, and under a timetable, satisfactory to the 
Bank.
III.
Part E of the Project
 
The Borrower shall: (a) carry out the study referred to under Part E.2 (a) of 
the Project not later than December 31, 1995; (b) afford the Bank a reasonable 
opportunity to review the results and recommendations of such study and exchange views 
thereon with the Borrower; (a) based
on the results and recommendations of the above study and the Bank's comments thereon, 
prepare and present to the Bank, not later than June 30, 1996, a plan, satisfactory to 
the Bank, for purposes of protecting the Orosi Pipeline, such plan to include the 
details of the organizational and engineering measures required therefor; and (c) 
implement the above action plan in a manner satisfactory to the Bank.
