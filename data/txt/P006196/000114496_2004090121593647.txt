September 17, 2001
His Excellency
Mr. Jacques Trigo
Minister of Finance
Palacio de Comunicaciones, Piso 19
La Paz, Bolivia
Re:
Credit No. 2531-BO (Integrated Child Development Project)
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Excellency,
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the DCA) between the Republic 
of Bolivia (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the 
Association) dated February 2, 1994, as amended.
We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to Mr. Bernardo Requena’s request, 
dated September 3, 2001, the Association hereby agrees to amend the table in paragraph 
1 of Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement as follows:
1.
The percentage of Disbursement of Category (8) is increased to 85% and that of 
Subcategory (9) (a) is decreased to 20%.
2.
The amounts allocated in each category have been modified as per the attachment 
to this Amendment Letter. 
This amendment letter shall be executed in two counterparts, each of which shall 
be an original, and shall be effective as of the date of this Amendment Letter, 
provided that the Association receives one fully executed original of such Amendment 
Letter.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
the two counterparts of this amendment letter.  Upon your confirmation, please return 
one fully executed original to us.
Very truly yours,
By /s/ Isabel M. Guerrero
Director
Bolivia, Ecuador and Peru
Latin American and the Caribbean Region
