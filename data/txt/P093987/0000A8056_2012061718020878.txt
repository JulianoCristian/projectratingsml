                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Additional Financing which was approved by the World Bank Board of Executive Directors in June 2011was declared effective in February 2012 after some delays due to the
                         country's approval process. Field activities have now been launched for both Nutrition and HIV/AIDS.

                         HIV/AIDS: For the past six months, due to the lack of funds (WB project was not effective and Global fund's money can't still be disbursed) the country experienced drug stock -out for
                         opportunistic disease drugs, some types of ARV and reagent for testing (this will impact on the results of testing for pregnant women and general population, new inclusion of patients
