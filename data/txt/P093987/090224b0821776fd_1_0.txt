                         Implementation Status Overview
                         - The project's field activities for both components (Component A: nutrition and Component B: AIDS) have been launched in February 2012. Implementation is progressing well:

                         Component A: the project funded activities for community- based nutrition (NGOs), completed the purchase of child food and measurement materials and funded the plan of actions
                         of the health districts and regions across the country.

                         supported one behavioral surveillance studies and mid-term evaluation of the national AIDS plan (2011-2015).
