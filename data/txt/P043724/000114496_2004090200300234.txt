Amendment to Loan Agreement
Your Excellency Abdelkrim Harchaoui:
We refer to the Loan Agreement dated April 17, 1997 as amended ("Agreement") 
entered into between the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the 
"Bank") and the Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria ("Algeria"), in respect of 
the Rural Employment Project ("Project"). We also refer to the project restructuring 
aide memoire, dated October 28, 1998, that was agreed upon at the occasion of the 
mid-term review of the Project, as well as to the Government’s related letter 
requesting the Agreement be amended to reflect the restructuring of the Project.
Therefore, by the present, the Agreement is hereby amended specifically as 
follows:
(i)
Section 2.03 of the Loan Agreement is revised to read as follows:
"The Closing Date shall be March 31, 2003, or such later date as the 
Bank shall establish. The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower of such 
later date."
(ii)
The ultimate sentence appearing at the bottom of Schedule 2 to the 
Agreement is revised to read as follows:
"The Project is expected to be completed by September 30, 2002."
(iii)
The table set forth in Paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement 
is modified to read as follows:

(iv)
Schedule 2 to the Agreement, describing the Project, is modified 
by the inclusion of an additional Part (G) to read as follows:
"Part G:
Flood Control
Carrying out a flood control civil works program and related services 
within the Ras El Ma area of Sidi Bel Abbes Province."
Please confirm your official agreement to the foregoing, as the authorized 
representative of Algeria, by duly signing and dating the corresponding form of 
confirmation below and returning this amendment letter to us at your earliest 
convenience. A signed duplicate original of this amendment letter is enclosed herewith 
for Algeria’s official records.  This amendment letter shall become effective as of 
the date it has been fully executed by each party thereto.
