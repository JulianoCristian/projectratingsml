                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to contribute to improving judicial efficiency and effectiveness, as well as the business climate by: (i) enhancing ministerial and
                         judicial capacity to systematically implement the Judicial Reform Strategy and laws affecting the judiciary; and (ii) improving judicial infrastructure.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The LJIIS Project remains fully on track to attain the objective of improved judicial infrastructure. The rehabilitation of 10 courthouses has been completed. Rehabilitation of the final
                         court in Bitola will be finalized shortly. The largest civil works activity under the LJIIS Project, the construction and renovation of the Skopje 1 Criminal Court is making good progress
                         and after some initial delays, is on schedule. Project support for IT infrastructure is also on track.
