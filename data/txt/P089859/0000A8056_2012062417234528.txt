                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the LJIIS Project is to contribute to improving judicial efficiency and effectiveness and the business climate in FYR Macedonia by: (i) enhancing
                         ministerial and judicial capacity to systematically implement the Government's Judicial Reform Strategy and key laws; and (ii) improving judicial infrastructure.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The LJIIS Project remains on track to achieve its project development objective. Project support for the new case management system (ACCMIS) is now in use in all Macedonian
                         courts. The bankruptcy procedures have been advanced through assistance provided to the Chamber of Trustees. The Administrative Inspectorate has been modernized and its work
                         processes made more efficient. The project provided the court system with an analysis of and recommendations about appointment of judges, their career development, along with


                                                                                                                                                                                                           Page 1 of 5
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                       Report No: ISR6365



                         disciplinary and promotion measures.The rehabilitation of 10 courthouses has been completed. The largest civil works activity under the LJIIS Project, the construction and renovation
                         of the Skopje 1 Criminal Court is ongoing, but it continues experiencing delays. The closing date for the Project was extended by 3 months to June 30, 2012 to allow for full
                         disbursement of Project funds and supervision during completion of Project activities under Government funding.
