                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the LJIIS Project is to contribute to improving judicial efficiency and effectiveness and the business climate in FYR Macedonia by: (i) enhancing
                         ministerial and judicial capaciy to systematically implement the Government's Judicial Reform Strategy and key laws; and (ii) improving judicial infrastructure.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The LJIIS Project remains fully on track to achieve its project development objective. The rehabilitation of 10 courthouses has been completed. The largest civil works activity under
                         the LJIIS Project, the construction and renovation of the Skopje 1 Criminal Court is ongoing and will be completed before the project closing date. Project support for the new case
