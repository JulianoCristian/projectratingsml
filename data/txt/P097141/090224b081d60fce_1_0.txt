                         Implementation Status Overview
                          The total number of trainees currently enrolled in training increased to 1,005 (against a revised end of Project target of 945). The number of youth certified more than doubled from
                         176 in December 2012 to 379 (against a revised end of project target of 407) by end of March 2013. This includes 43 international certifications. Seven out of the eight intermediate
                         outcome indicator targets have either been achieved or surpassed: 93 standards have been vetted (against a revised end of Project target of 87), the number of pre-qualified training
                         providers is 117 (against a revised end of Project target of 117), percentage of enrolled youth in training in sectors other than hospitality is 42 (against an end of Project target of 38);
