Re: Saint Lucia: OECS Catastrophe Insurance Project (Credit No. 4271 - SLU) 
Amendment to the Financing Agreement 
 
Dear Prime Minister,  
 
We refer to the Financing Agreement between Saint Lucia (the Recipient) and the 
Development Association (the Association) dated March 8, 2007 (the Agreement). We also refer 
to your letter dated August 20, 2010, requesting for amendment to the Agreement.  
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association concurs with your request and hereby 
amends the Agreement as follows: 
 
1. 
Schedule 1: Part B of the Agreement is amended to read as follows: 
 
“B. 
“provision of financing for the payment of 100 percent of the Recipient’s 
annual catastrophe risk insurance premiums for the first two years of its 
participation in the CCRIF, 50 percent of the Recipient’s said premium for the 
third year, and 50 percent of the Recipient’s said premium for the fourth year of 
its participation in the CCRIF.”” 
 
2. 
Schedule 2: Section I.A.2 is amended to read as follows: 
 
“A.2. “
Counterpart Financing
:
For the purposes of carrying out the Project, the 
Recipient shall contribute 50 percent of its third and fourth annual catastrophe 
insurance premiums to the CCRIF.”” 
 
 
3. 
The table set forth in Section IV.A.2 of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is deleted and 
replaced with the table set forth in the Attachment to this Letter.  
 
All other provisions of the Agreement, as amended herein, shall remain in full force and effect.
