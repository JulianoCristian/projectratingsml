                         Implementation Status Overview
                         -The project is scheduled to close on 30 June, 2011. At the request of GOB, the Bank has extended the project till June 30, 2012 to allow for completion of the 64 on-going contracts.
                         -To complete implementation of the Institutional Strengthening Action Plan, LGED needs to select rapidly consultants for the assessment and rolling out of the Unified Financial
                         Management System and for the limited impact assessment of the Local Government Improvement Program (LGIP).
