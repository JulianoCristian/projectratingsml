Amendment:  Development Credit Agreement No. 2184-CD
Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Petroleum and Power Engineering 
Project), dated November 29, 1990, between the Republic of Chad (the Borrower) and the 
Inernaitonal Development Association (the Association).  Further to the discussions 
held with the representatives of the Borrowerm, we are pleased to inform you that the 
Association hereby agrees to the following amendments to the above-referenced 
Development Credit Agreement:
1.
The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is amended as follows:
 
(a)
Category (1)(b) is amended by replacing the figure "1,600,000" in the 
second column with the figure "1,700,000";
(b)
Category (2) is amended by deleting the words "of foreign expenditures" 
from the third column;
(c)
Category (3) is amended by deleting the words "of foreign expenditures" 
from the third column; and
(d)
Category (6) is amended by replacing the figure "910,000" in the second 
column with the figure "810,000".
2.
Paragraph 1(c) of Schedule 3 is amended by replacing the figure "200,000" with 
the figure "300,000".
3.
The amendment provided for: (i) in paragraph 1(b) above shall apply to any 
expenditure made on or after July 1, 1991; and (ii) in papragraph 1(c) above shall 
apply to any expenditure made on or after July 1, 1992.
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing by countersigning and dating 
the form of confirmation on the two original copies of this letter provided and 
returning one original, as countersigned, to us.  This amendment shall become 
effective as of the date of receipt by the Association of this letter duly 
countersigned by you.
