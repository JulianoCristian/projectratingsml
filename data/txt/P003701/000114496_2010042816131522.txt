Dear Mr. Minister:
Re:
Montreal Protocol Ozone Depleting Substances Phase Out Investment 
Project Amendments to the Grant Agreement OTF Grant No. TF 021982
We refer to the Ozone Projects Trust Fund Grant Agreement (Montreal Protocol 
Ozone Depleting Substances Phase Out Investment Project) between the Republic of 
Indonesia (the Recipient) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), acting as Trustee of grant funds provided by Parties to the 
Montreal Protocol on Substances that Deplete the Ozone Layer, dated November 10, 1994. 
We also refer to the Ministry of Finance's letter dated May 18, 1998, requesting 
amendments to the Grant Agreement.
The Bank is pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, it has agreed to your 
request to amend the Grant Agreement.
1.
Section 2.01 of the Grant Agreement is amended to read:
"Section 2.01. The Trustee agrees to make available to the Recipient, on 
the terms and conditions set forth or referred to in this Agreement, the OTF 
Grant in an amount in various currencies not to exceed the equivalent of 
twenty 
seven million dollars ($27,000,000).
2.
Section 2.03 of the Grant Agreement is amended to read:
"Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be December 31, 1999, or such 
later date as the Trustee shall establish. The Trustee shall promptly notify 
the Recipient of such later date."
3.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Grant Agreement is amended to
