Re:  BRAZIL: Bahia Rural Poverty Alleviation Project - Loan 3917-BR
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement for Loan 3917-BR, dated July 16, 1995 between the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the State of Bahia (the 
Borrower).
In response to your April 22, 1997 fax requesting the inclusion in the Loan Agreement of 
studies, training and consultant services for the purposes of the state reform program and to 
undertake competitiveness studies, we are pleased to inform you that the Bank hereby proposes 
to amend the Loan Agreement to add an Item 5 to Part B of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement, 
as follows:
“5.  Provision of technical assistance to support the reform of the Borrower’s 
administrative, institutional and financial sectors, and to undertake competitiveness studies for 
the Borrower and the Guarantor’s northeast region.”
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall be an 
original, and shall become effective on the date of this Amendment Letter once the Bank has 
received one fully executed original of this Amendment Letter.  Please confirm your agreement 
to the foregoing amendment by signing the two counterparts of this Amendment Letter.  Upon 
your agreement, please return one fully executed original to us.
Sincerely,
