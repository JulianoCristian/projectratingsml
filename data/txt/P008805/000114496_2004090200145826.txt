Moscow, Russia
Re:
RUSSIA:  Loan No. 3768-RU (Second Oil Rehabilitation Project)
Amendment to Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Kasyanov:
We refer to the letter of Mr. Viugin (reference # 27-00-09/1603) dated September 
6, 1999, and to the Loan Agreement between the International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (IBRD) and Russian Federation, dated September 27, 1994,  for the 
Second Oil Rehabilitation Project (Loan No. 3768-RU), and to our discussions regarding 
the delegation of authority for withdrawals under the Loan Agreement.  We are pleased 
to inform you that the above-referenced Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
1.
Section 2.08, wherein it is provided that Yuganskneftegas is designated as 
representative of the Borrower in respect of Part C of the Project, is hereby deleted.
2.
Section 2.09, wherein it is provided that Tomskneft is designated as 
representative of the Borrower in respect of Part B of the Project, is hereby deleted.
3.
Section 2.10, wherein it is provided that Megionneftegas is designated as 
representative of the Borrower in respect of Part A of the Project, is hereby deleted.
4.
In Section 6.01, the words “except as provided in sections 2.08, 2.09, and 2.10 
of this Agreement” are deleted. 
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing, on behalf of the Russian 
Federation, by signing, dating and returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter. 
This amendment will become effective  \0cas of the date hereof, upon your 
countersignature.  A signed duplicate original of the letter is enclosed for your 
records.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR 
   RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT
