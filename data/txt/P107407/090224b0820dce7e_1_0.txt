                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project continues to make good progress towards building capacity of the key institutions established to support the Education System Transformation Program. The National
                         Education Inspectorate (NEI), National Education Trust (NET) and Jamaica Teaching Council (JTC) are in place and continue to operate with specific budget allocations. The
                         functional profile for the Central Ministry (CM) and Department of School Services (DSS) has been approved by the Minister and a human resources transition policy has been
                         completed. A capacity building plan is in place and activities are already being implemented. Leadership training has been undertaken for the first and second tiers of leadership in
                         the Ministry, and training has been provided to improve the level of efficiency - Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt Certification. The number of DSS/CM staff trained is 134 (compared to
                         end-of-Project target of 80); all schools are implementing school improvement plans; 216 school leaders have been trained (compared to end-of-project target of 15); 72 percent of
