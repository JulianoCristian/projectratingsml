Amendment to the Project Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Project Agreement relating to the Sana’a Emergency Power Project, between the 
International Development Association (the Association) and the Public Electricity Corporation (PEC), 
dated October 5, 1998 (the Project Agreement).  We also refer to the proposed amendments discussed in 
the Aide-Memoire dated May 31 - June 27, 2001, following the mid-term review and as confirmed by our 
letter dated August 9, 2001.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association, after due consideration, has acceded to the 
proposed amendments.  Accordingly, the Project Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
1.
Section 4.02 (a) of the Project Agreement is amended to read as follows :
“Section 4.02. (a) The Borrower shall, except as may otherwise be agreed with the 
Association: (i) for fiscal years 1999, 2000, 2001 and 2002, produce total revenues equivalent to 
not less than the sum of its (x) total operating expenses and (y) interest expenses and other 
charges on debt which are normally chargeable to operations; (ii) produce funds from internal 
sources equivalent to not less than 5% for fiscal year 2003; and, 10% for fiscal year 2004, and 
each of the following fiscal years, of the annual average of the Borrower's capital expenditures 
incurred (or expected to be incurred) during such year, the preceding fiscal year and the 
following fiscal year; and (iii) not incur a debt, unless a reasonable forecast of its estimated funds 
from internal sources during the term of the debt to be incurred shall be at least 1.1 times PEC’s 
estimated debt service requirement for fiscal year 2003, 1.3 times PEC’s estimated debt service 
requirements for fiscal year 2004, and 1.5 times PEC’s estimated debt service requirements for 
fiscal year 2005 and each year thereafter, on all its debt in each relevant year including the debt 
to be incurred.”
H.E. Yahya Al-Abyad 
- 2 -
   December 7, 2001
2.
Paragraph 3 of Schedule 1 to the Project Agreement is amended to read as follows:
Page  2
“3. (a) PEC shall submit to the Association for its review by March 1, 2002, a proposed 
power and gas master plan, and complete by March 31, 2002 said power and gas master plan, 
taking the comments of the Association into account; and
(b) based upon said power and gas master plan, by September 30, 2002, the Borrower 
shall determine future investment in the in the next large power plant, together with domestic 
natural gas production and distribution facilities to supply that power plant.”
3.
All the terms and conditions of the Project Agreement, as amended hereby, shall remain in full 
force and effect.
