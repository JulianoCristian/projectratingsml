PROJECT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated      JA   4-  2-5    , 1982, between STATE
OF MARANHAO (hereinafter called the State) and INTERNATIONAL BANK
FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (hereinafter called the Bank).
WHEREAS (A) by the Loan Agreement between Federal Republic
of Brazil (hereinafter called the Borrower) and the Bank of even
date herewith (hereinafter called the Loan Agreement), the Bank
has agreed to make available to the Borrower an amount in various
currencies equivalent to forty-two million seven hundred thousand
dollars ($42,700,000), on the terms and conditions set forth in
the Loan Agreement, but only on condition that the State agree to
undertake such obligations toward the Bank as are hereinafter set
forth;
(B) the State warrants and represents that it is authorized
to undertake the obligations in this Agreement contained under
the laws of the State and the Borrower; and
WHEREAS the State, in consideration of the Bank's entering
into the Loan Agreement with the Borrower, has agreed to under-
take the obligations hereinafter set forth;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
Definitions
Section 1.01. Wherever used in th.s Agreement, unless the
context shall otherwise require, the several terms defined in the
Loan Agreement, the Preamble to this Agreement and the General
Conditions (as so defined) have the respective meanings therein
set forth.

(iii) by November 30, 1982, furnish to the Bank a plan

Section 2.02. The State shall: (i) enter into and thereafter

Bank, with each of the entities listed in the Schedule to this

(ii) cause the entities listed in the Schedule to the COLONE
Project Agreement to enter into the agreements with COLONE set
forth in Section 2.02 of such Project Agreement; (iii) unless the
Bank shall otherwise agree, not amend, and cause the entities
referred to in (ii) above not to amend, nor fail to enforce, and
cause the entities referred to in (ii) above to enforce, their
respective rights and obligations under the agreements referred
to in (i) and (ii) above, as therein set forth; (iv) perform all
of its obligations under the Land Tenure Regularization Agree-
ment, and unless the Bank shall otherwise agree, not amend or
fail to enforce any provision therein set forth.

procurement of the goods and civil works required for the Project
and to be financed out of the proceeds of the Loan shall be
governed by the provisions of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement.
Section 2.05. (a) The State undertakes to insure, or make
adequate provision for the insurance of, the imported goods to be
financed out of the proceeds of the Loan made available to it by
the Borrower against hazards incident to the acquisition, trans-
portation and delivery thereof to the place of use or installa-
tion, and for such insurance any indemnity shall be payable in a
currency freely usable by the State to replace or repair such

(b)  The State shall cause all goods and services financed
out of the proceeds of the Loan made available to it by the
Borrower to be used exclusively for the purposes of the Project.

tract documents and work and procurement schedules for the State
Sub-project, and any material modifications thereof or additions
thereto, in such detail as the Bank shall reasonably request.
(b) The State: (i) shall maintain or cause to be maintained
records and procedures adequate to record and monitor the prog-
ress of the State Sub-project (including its cost and the bene-

cluded in the State Sub-project and to examine the goods financed
out of the proceeds of the Loan and any relevant records and

cerning the Project, its cost and, where appropriate, the bene-
fits to be derived from it, the expenditure of the proceeds of
the Loan and the goods and services financed out of such pro-
ceeds; (iv) without limitation to sub-paragraph (iii) above,
shall furnish to the Bank quarterly reports on the progress made
in the carrying out of the Project, in a format satisfactory to
the Bank, such quarterly reports to be furnished promptly upon

above in terms satisfactory to the Bank.
(c) Upon the award by the State of any contract for goods,

Section 2.07. The State shall: (i) not later than April 1,
1983, establish, and thereafter maintain, a system of statistics
and monitoring of the credit provided pursuant to Section 4.01 of
the Loan Agreement in terms satisfactory to the Bank; (ii) not
later than June 30 in each year furnish to the Bank, for its
review and exchange of views with the State, a report on the
execution of the Project related credit activities component
during the previous POLONORDESTE Fiscal Year.
Section 2.08. The State shall not later than October 1 in
each year, furnish to the Bank and SUDENE the proposed plan of
operation for Project activities for the following POLONORDESTE
Fiscal Year in terms satisfactory to the Bank.
Section 2.09. (a) The State shall include in its annual
program for road construction and improvement, such priority
secondary or feeder roads within the Project Area which are not
being constructed or rehabilitated under the Project; and shall
cause the works on such roads to be made in accordance with
appropriate standards.
(b) The State shall: (i) cause DERMA to provide, promptly
as needed, the mechanized assistance, including additional equip-
ment and personnel, to municipalities included in the Project
Area, in accordance with appropriate engineering equipment to be
financed out of the proceeds of the Loan to be used exclusively
for purposes of executing road maintenance activities in the
roads included in the Project Area.

Section 2.10. (a) The State shall maintain and cause to be
maintained records adequate to reflect in accordance with con-
sistently maintained appropriate accounting practices on the
operations, resources and expenditures in respect of the State
Sub-project, including, without limitation to the foregoing,
separate accounts reflecting all expenditures on account of which
withdrawals are requested from the Loan Account on the basis of
statements of expenditure.
(b) The State shall cause to be retained, until one year
after the Closing Date, all records (contracts, orders, invoices,
bills, receipts and other documents) evidencing the expenditures
on account of which withdrawals are requested from the Loan
Account on the basis of statements of expenditure, and shall
enable the Bank's representatives to examine such records.
Section 2.11. The State shall:
(a) cause the accounts referred to in Section 2.10 (a) of
this Agreement to be audited annually, in accordance with appro-
priate auditing principles consistently applied, by independent
auditors acceptable to the Bank;
(b) furnish to the Bank as soon as available, but in any
case not later than six months after the end of each POLONORDESTE
Fiscal Year: (i) certified copies of the accounts referred to in
Section 2.10 (a) of this Agreement for such year as so audited;
and (ii) the report of such audit by said auditors of such scope
and in such detail as the Bank shall have reasonably requested,
including, without limitation to the foregoing, separate opinions
by said auditors: (A) on the separate accounts referred to in
Section 2.10 (a) of this Agreement; and (B) in respect of the
expenditures and records referred to in Section 2.10 (b) of this
Agreement, as to whether the proceeds of the Loan made available
to it and withdrawn from the Loan Account on the basis of state-
ments of expenditure have been used for the purpose for which
they were provided; and
(c) furnish to the Bank such other information concerning
said accounts, records and expenditures, as well as the audit
thereof, as the Bank shall from time to time reasonably request.

Section 2.13. The State shall take out and maintain, or

(b) The State shall promptly inform the Bank of any condi-

of the Project, the accomplishment of the purposes of the Loan,

Section 2.15. The State shall not take or permit to be taken

ARTICLE III
Effective Date; Termination;
Cancellation and Suspension
Section 3.01. This Agreement shall come into force and
effect on the date upon which the Loan Agreement becomes effec-
tive.
Section 3.02. This Agreement and all obligations of the Bank
and of the State thereunder shall terminate on the date on which
the Loan Agreement shall terminate in accordance with its terms,
and the Bank shall promptly so notify the State thereof.
Section 3.03. All the provisions of this Agreement shall
continue in full force and effect notwithstanding any cancella-
tion or suspension under the General Conditions.
