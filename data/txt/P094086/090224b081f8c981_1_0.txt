                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The PDO indicators of the project remain on track with above target enrollment and satisfactory student and teacher attendance at community schools. The project continues to
                         support 633 functional schools across the Balochistan province with an enrollment of 26,000 children (43% Girls). The last phase of construction of community schools is underway
                         now with an end target of 225 community schools expected to be completed at the conclusion of the project. Of this target, 124 schools are in advanced stages of construction, and
                         23 schools are ready to be handed over to the Parent Education Committees (PEC).
