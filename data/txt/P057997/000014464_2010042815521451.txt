AGREEMENT AMENDING PROJECT AGREEMENT 
 
AGREEMENT, dated July 5, 2005 between INTERNATIONAL 
DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (the Association) and GAMBIAN AGENCY FOR 
THE MANAGEMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS (GAMWORKS). 
 
WHEREAS (A) the REPUBLIC OF THE GAMBIA (the Borrower) and the 
Association have entered into a Development Credit Agreement (Poverty Alleviation and 
Capacity Building Project - Credit Number 3176 GM), dated April 1, 1999 (the 
Development Credit Agreement), for the purpose of providing support during the 
execution of the Poverty Alleviation and Capacity Building Project, as described in the 
Preamble of the Development Credit Agreement (the Project); 
 
(B) 
the Borrower has agreed to make part of the proceeds of the Credit 
available to GAMWORKS and entered into a framework agreement (the Framework 
Agreement) with GAMWORKS for that purpose; 
 
(C) 
the Association and GAMWORKS have entered into a Project 
Agreement, dated April 1, 1999 (the Project Agreement), for the purpose of the carrying 
out of the Project; 
 
(D) 
the Borrower has requested the Association to provide further additional 
assistance in the support of the Project by increasing the amount made available under the 
Development Credit Agreement by an amount in various currencies equivalent to two 
million seven hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR2,700,000); and 
 
WHEREAS the Association has agreed, on the basis, 
inter alia
,
of the foregoing, 
to provide such additional assistance to the Borrower provided that an agreement 
amending the Project Agreement (the Agreement Amending the Project Agreement) upon 
the terms and conditions set forth hereafter be executed; 
 
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:

ARTICLE I 
 Section 1.01.  
Recital (A) of the Project Agreement is amended to read as 
follows: 
“
(A) 
by the Development Credit Agreement, the Association has 
agreed to make available to the Borrower an amount in various currencies 
equivalent to thirteen million four hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights 
(SDR13,400,000) (the Credit), which includes: (a) an original in various 
currencies equivalent to ten million seven hundred thousand Special 
Drawing Rights (SDR 10,700,000) (the Initial Financing); and (b) a 
supplemental amount in various currencies equivalent to two million seven 
hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 2,700,000) (the Additional 
Financing), on the terms and conditions set forth in the Development Credit 
Agreement, but only on condition that GAMWORKS agree to undertake 
such obligations toward the Association as are set forth in this Agreement;” 
Section 1.02.   Part B.1 of the Project is added to the parts of the Project to be 
implemented by GAMWORKS, and to this end, Section 2.01, 2.02, 2.05, 4.01, 
paragraphs 1, 2 (a), (b) and (d) are amended, accordingly. 
Section 1.03.   Paragraph 2 of Part D of Section I of Schedule 1 to the Project 
Agreement is amended to read as follows: 
“
With respect to each contract for works estimated to cost the equivalent 
of $250,000 or more, and goods estimated to cost the equivalent of $150,000 
or more, the procedures set forth in paragraphs 2 and 3 of Appendix 1 to the 
Guidelines shall apply.” 
Section 1.04.   Paragraph 2 (a) of Schedule 2 to the Project Agreement is amended 
to read as follows: 
“
maintain policies and procedures adequate to monitor and evaluate on an 
ongoing basis, in accordance with indicators satisfactory to the Association, 
set forth in Schedule 6 of the Development Credit Agreement, the carrying 
out of Parts A, B.1, B.2 and B.3 of the Project and the achievement of the 
objectives thereof;”                                            
 
 
 
ARTICLE II 
Effective Date; Termination 
 
Section 2.01.  This Agreement Amending the Project Agreement shall come into 
force and effect on the date upon which the Amending Agreement becomes effective.
