                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is nearing completion. An action plan for tendering/contracting of the remaining 14 civil works/school furniture contracts has been successfully implemented, based on
                         which the extension of the project closing date from March 31, 2011 to September 30, 2011 has been granted in order to allow for the full loan disbursement. Construction/renovation
                         of 30 schools has been completed and is ongoing in another 8 schools. Some of the key reform activities supported by the project have been successfully implemented in 2010 after
