     Re:  Credit No. 1949 MOZ (Urban Rehabilitation Project)
          Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Sir:
     We refer to the above-mentioned Development Credit Agreement
(the Agreement) dated October 18, 1988, between the Republic of
Mozambique (the Borrower) and the International Development
Association (the Association). Further to discussions held between
representative of the Borrower and the Association, during the
Project implementation review, and your letter No. 182/MF/GN/92
dated May 20, 1992, we are pleased to inform you that the
Association hereby agrees to amend the Agreement, as follows.
     Categories (1) and (3) of paragraph 1 of Schedule to the
Agreement are amended and replaced by the following:
