Credit No. 2472 BUR (Private Sector Assistance Project) Amendment 
to the 
Development Credit Agreement
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the “Agreement”) dated July 
15, 1993, between Burkina Faso (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association) for the above referenced Credit and to your letter to 
the Association, dated December 19, 1997, requesting the Association for additional 
support in the areas of implementation of telecommunications reform and OHADA’s 
regional business law reform.
We are pleased to confirm, therefore, our consent to amend the Agreement as 
follows:
1. 
The following paragraphs are added at the end of Section 1.02: 
“(q)
“OHADA” means the Organisation pour l’Harmonisation du Droit des 
Affaires en 
Afrique; and”
“(r)
“MCC” means Ministère de la Communication et de la Culture.”
2.
Section 3.01 (a) is deleted and replaced by the following:
“Section 3.01. (a) The Borrower declares its commitment to the objectives 
of the 
Project as set forth in Schedule 2 to this Agreement and, to this 
end, shall carry out Parts 
A and B.1 through B.4 of the Project through MICM, 
Part B.5 through MJ, Part C.1 through C.4 
thereof through MFP, and Part D through 
MCC, with due diligence and efficiency and in 
conformity with appropriate 
administrative and financial practices, and shall provide, 
promptly as needed, 
the funds, facilities, services and other resources required for the 
Project.”
3.
The table in Schedule 1 to the Agreement is amended to read as set forth in the 
Attachment to this letter.
4.
Part B.5 of Schedule 2 is deleted and replaced by the following language:
“5.
Support MJ for the implementation of new business laws promulgated by 
OHADA, 
including (i) the public dissemination of such laws; (ii) training 
of magistrates, lawyers 
and affected members of the private sector; and (iii) where 
necessary, revision of Burkinabe  laws in order to harmonize them with the OHADA laws.”
5.
Paragraph C.5 of Schedule 2 is deleted.
6.
Schedule 2 is further amended by adding a new Part D, as follows:
“Part D:
Telecommunications Sector
1.
Capacity building in MCC, through the provision of technical advisory 
services and  the acquisition of computer equipment.
2.
Strengthening the public dissemination of the Borrower’s 
telecommunications sector  strategy through the organization of a national workshop 
attended by all key stakeholders. 
3.
Design of a legal and regulatory framework to encourage competition in 
the 
telecommunications sector, through the provision of technical 
Page  2
advisory services.
4.
Provision of assistance to MCC to (i) establish a regulatory 
telecommunications 
agency; (ii) formalize a telecommunications sector policy 
and develop a letter of sector 
policy; and (iv) facilitate the granting by 
the Borrower of a wireless telecommunications  license to a private sector operator,  
through the provision of technical advisory services  and the acquisition of 
equipment.”
If you agree with the foregoing amendments, please sign and date this letter in 
the spaces provided below. This amendment letter shall be executed in two 
counterparts, each of which shall be an original. Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us.
The provisions of this amendment letter will become effective as of the date the 
Association receives one fully executed original of this amendment letter. Sincerely,
