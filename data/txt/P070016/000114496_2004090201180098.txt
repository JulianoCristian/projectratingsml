Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency: 
 
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (DCA) for the above-referenced 
Project between the Republic of Nicaragua (the Recipient) and the International Development 
Association (the Association), dated January 24, 2001. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to your requests dated July 10, 2003 and 
September 12, 2003, respectively, the Association agrees to reallocate the amounts as reflected in 
the Annex to this Letter of Amendment and amend the DCA as set forth below: 
 
1. 
Schedule 1
The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 (Withdrawals of the Proceeds of the 
Loan) to the DCA has, therefore, been amended to read as set forth in the Annex to this 
Amendment Letter. 
 
2. 
Schedule 3
Paragraph 2, Part C, Section I of the Schedule 3 to this DCA has been amended to read as 
follows: 
 
“Section I
Part C
:
Other Procurement Procedures
2. National 
Shopping

Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating each
