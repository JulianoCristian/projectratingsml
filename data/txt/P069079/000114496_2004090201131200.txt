Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Your Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement between Albania and the 
International Development Association for the above-captioned Project dated 
June 
8, 
2000, (the Credit Agreement).  We have received your telex dated 
February 5, 2003,  requesting a reallocation of proceeds under the above Credit.   
 
We are pleased to concur with your request.  Therefore, the Association hereby 
reallocates the proceeds of the Credit as set out in the table in Paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 
to the Credit Agreement as follows: 
 
SDR 223,000 from Category 2 (Consultants’ Services) to Category 1 (Goods) 
SDR 18,000 from Category 3 (Incremental Operating Costs) to Category 1 
(Goods) 
SDR 236,000 from Category 4 (Unallocated) to Category 1 (Goods)
