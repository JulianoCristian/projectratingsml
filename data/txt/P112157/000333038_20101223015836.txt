7a.  Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
The objectives of the project are: (i) to increase domestic food production and reduce the loss of livestock 
to help at least 55,000 poorest households; (ii) to improve the ability of poor households to deal with seed 
shortages due to poor crops; and (iii) to promote private commercial farming in order to increase food 
production and diversification.
