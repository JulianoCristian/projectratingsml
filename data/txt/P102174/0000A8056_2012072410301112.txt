                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The long-term overall goal of the proposed project is to support the government in the implementation of the Strategy for the Development of Pre-University Education in
                         Kosovo and the Strategy for the Development of Higher Education. The specific objective of the proposed project is to strengthen systems, institutions and
                         management capacities needed for education quality improvements.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The overall project implementation has improved and significant progress is made in the last year with several activities close to successful completion. Available data on key
                         outcome indicators are showing that the Project is on track to meet its development objective: out of the five indicators, three have been met, and two are on track to be met by the
                         end of the year. In addition, out of the thirteen intermediate indicators eight were already met; one partially met; one is on track; and two not met. In particular, progress is noted in (i)

                         (ii) scaling up of the school financial autonomy to a national level and gradual increase of school control over their resources; (iii) implementation of the 1st and 2nd rounds of School
                         Development Grants (SDGs), as well as rolling out the 3rd round of SDGs; and (iv) completion of the teacher performance assessment pilot phase and the reports on teacher
                         professional development. With the successful award of the civil works contract in June 2012, the Project is well positioned to be completed successfully.
