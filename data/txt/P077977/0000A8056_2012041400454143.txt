                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has been successfully implemented and closed. All the four participating states of the project are now also participating in the PMGSY Rural Roads Project. The
                         institutional development initiatives started under this project will continue under the PMGSY Rural Roads Project.
                         About 150 road works will be completed by end June 2012. These works are mostly in Uttar Pradesh and Himachal Pradesh. MORD will provide necessary funds to complete these
