                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation is satisfactory. The project is scheduled to close on March 31, 2012 - only about $ 11 m remains undisbursed out of a total Bank funding of $ 400m. All the
                         four participating states of the project are now also participating in the recently started PMGSY Rural Roads Project. The institutional development initiatives started under this project
                         will continue through the Bank support available under the PMGSY Rural Roads Project.
