                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation is satisfactory: the project is scheduled for closure on March 31, 2012. Most of the project activities are already over. All the four participating states of the
                         project are now also participating in the recently started PMGSY Rural Roads Project. The institutional development initiatives started under this project will continue through the Bank
                         support available under the PMGSY Rural Roads Project.
