                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to support the Recipient's efforts towards the: (i) improvement of access to, and sustainability of, the water supply and sanitation
                         services for consumers in Lusaka; and (ii) development of a comprehensive institutional structure supporting a coordinated approach to water supply and
                         sanitation investments.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The implementation of the Zambia Water Sector Performance Improvement Project (WSPIP) is proceeding in a satisfactory manner. The civil works under Component 1 (Support to
                         the Lusaka Water and Sewerage Company [LWSC]) are nearing completion, although all have experienced issues in implementation and/or operation, particularly the drilling of new
                         boreholes, construction of water kiosks and the installation of pumps at the Iolanda intake. There has been slow progress in the implementation of Component 2 (Support to the
                         Ministry of Local Government and Housing). Goods to support MLGH's WSS teams have been purchased and delivered and a consultancy to assist design a financing framework for
