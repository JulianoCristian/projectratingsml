Re:   VENEZUELA - Agricultural Extension Project (3862-VE) -
Fifth Amendment to the Loan Agreement 
Dear Sirs: 
 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement (the Loan Agreement) between the Bolivarian 
Republic of Venezuela (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), dated October 12, 1995, as amended. 
 
Pursuant to your letter of request dated June 28, 2002, the Bank hereby agrees to 
amend the Loan Agreement so as to:  (i) extend the Closing Date; (ii) increase the Bank’s 
financing percentage; and (iii) reallocate the loan proceeds among Categories. 
 
Consequently the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:  
 
1. Section 
2.03
is hereby amended and replaced to read in its entirety as follows: 
“the Bank has established December 31, 2003, as the later date for purposes of 
Section 2.03 of the Loan Agreement”. 
 
2. Schedule 1
–
The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended 
and to read as follows: 
 
(a) an increase in the Bank financing percentage for Category 4 from 46% to 
60% is reflected in the new table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 as set forth 
in the Annex to this Amendment Letter.  
 
(b) the new Loan proceed amounts allocated to each Category is reflected in 
the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 as set forth in the Annex to this 
Amendment Letter.
