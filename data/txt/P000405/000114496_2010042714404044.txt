July 21, 1992
His Excellency Tchouta Moussa
Minister of Planing and Regional Development
Ministry of Planing and Regional Development
Yaounde
Republic of Cameroon
Re:
Loan No. 3206 CM
(Social Dimensions of Adjustment Project)
Amendment to Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the above-mentioned Loan Agreement between Republic of Cameroon (the 
Borrower) and International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), dated 
August 3, 1990, your letter No. 1137/MINPAT/CAB/IG2 dated July 8, 1992 requesting an 
increase in the disbursement percentage to be financed for small projects under the 
Programme de Developpement Communautaire
(PRODEC), and discussions held with your representatives during the recent Bank 
supervision mission.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank hereby agrees to your request and 
that, effective as of the date of confirmation of your agreement as indicated 
hereafter, the percentage of expenditures to be financed under Category (12) of the 
table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is increased from 
50% to 100%.  Said Category therefore reads:
     "(12) Goods and services                 2,500,000
     100%
     for Part F.1 of the Project"
Please indicate your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
the form of confirmation on the enclosed copy of this letter and returning it to us.
Very truly yours,
By /s/ Michael Gillette
Director
Occidental and Central
Africa Department
Africa Region
CONFIRMED:
REPUBLIC OF CAMEROON
By /s/ 
Authorized Representative
Date:
August 5, 1992
