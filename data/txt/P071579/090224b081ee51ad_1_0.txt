                         Global Environmental Objectives
                         Global Environmental Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Global Environment Objective of the project is "to Promote participatory conservation and sustainable use of biological diversity of coastal wetlands and marine

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This ISR covers the mid-term review of the project held in Benin from April 8 to 16, 2013. The MTR noted that overall implementation progress of the project is on track. The main
                         activities that were lagging behind, i.e. the development and implementation of income generating activities (IGAs) for communities adjacent to the three community-based marine
                         protected areas created by the project, are now on track. The mission visited project beneficiaries in: (i) Bamezoun in the commune of the Aguegues and Dangbo, (ii) Vodounto in

                         the commune of Ouidah; and (iii) Togbin-Adounko in the commune of Abomey-Calavi and noted that the project signed the first set of financing agreements for IGAs which will benefit
                         1600 beneficiaries. A second and third sets of IGAs financing agreements will be signed before October 2013 and their implementation will be launched the following month.
