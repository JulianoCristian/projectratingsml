(hereinafter referred to as "Selected Agreements")
We also refer to your letter dated March 14, 2001, requesting the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (hereinafter the 
"Bank"), the International Development Association (hereinafter the 
"Association") in its own capacity and the Association in its capacity as 
Administrator of Interim Trust Funds (hereinafter the "Administrator") to 
reallocate funds from the Selected Agreements to assist in financing 
emergency earthquake reconstruction activities in the State of Gujarat 
(hereinafter "Gujarat").  Pursuant to the discussions between the 
respective representatives of India (hereinafter the "Borrower"), the Bank, 
the Association, the Administrator and Gujarat, we are pleased to inform 
you that we have agreed to your request.
Accordingly, the Selected Agreements are amended as follows:
1.
For the purpose of this Amendment: (a) the term "World Bank" shall mean 
the Bank, the Association or the Administrator that is a party to the 
respective Selected Agreement; (b) the term "Financing" shall mean the 
Loan, the Credit or the Interim Fund Credit that is provided under the 
respective Selected Agreement; and (c) the term "Financing Account" means 
the Loan Account, Credit Account or the Interim Fund Credit Account 
corresponding to such Account referred to in the respective Selected 
Agreement.
2.
In the Preamble to each Selected Agreement, two new clauses (y) and (z) 
are added as the last enumerated recitals to read as follows:
"(y)
The Borrower has requested the World Bank to reallocate 
funds from the Selected Agreements (as hereinafter defined) in order 
to assist in financing Part (Z) of the Project;
(z) Notwithstanding any other provisions herein, Part (Z) of the 
Project will be carried out by the State of Gujarat (Gujarat) with 
the  Borrower’s assistance, and as part of such assistance, the 
Borrower will make available to Gujarat a portion of the proceeds of 
the Financing as provided in this Agreement and in other Selected 
Agreements."
3.
The reference to "terms and conditions" in the final "WHEREAS" paragraph 
of the Preamble to each Selected Agreement shall be deemed to include a 
reference to the terms and conditions set forth in the Gujarat Agreement 
(as hereinafter defined) in addition to the Agreement(s) referred to 
therein.
4.

5.
The following new section is added at the end of Article III (Execution 
of the Project) of each Selected Agreement:
"Section 30.01 (a) The Borrower declares its commitment to the 
objectives of the Project, and to this end, without any limitation or 
restriction upon any of its other obligations under this agreement, 
shall cause Gujarat to carry out Part Z of the Project with due 
diligence and efficiency and in conformity with appropriate 
administrative, technical and financial practices, and shall provide 
promptly as needed the funds, facilities, services and resources for 
such Part of the Project.
(b) Notwithstanding any other provisions of this Agreement, the 
Borrower shall make the proceeds of the Financing allocated for Part Z of 
the Project available to Gujarat in accordance with standards arrangements 
for developmental assistance to the States of India.
(c) The provisions of this Agreement relating to procurement of the 
goods, works and services required for the Project and to be financed out 
of the proceeds of the Financing shall apply to all Parts of the Project 
except for Part Z. Except as the World Bank shall otherwise agree, 
procurement of the goods, works and services required for Part Z of the 
Project and to be financed out of the proceeds of the Financing shall be 
governed by the provisions of Schedule 1 to the Gujarat Agreement.
(d) Notwithstanding any other provisions of this Agreement and without 
any limitation or restriction thereto, the Borrower and the World Bank 
hereby agree that the obligations set forth in sections 9.03, 9.04, 9.05, 
9.06, 9.07 and 9.08 of the General Conditions (relating to insurance, use 
of goods and services, plans and schedules, records and reports, 
maintenance and land acquisition, respectively) shall be carried out by 
Gujarat pursuant to Section 2.03 of the Gujarat Agreement in respect of 
Part Z of the Project".
6.
The following new section is added at the end of Article IV (Financial 
Covenants) of each Selected Agreement:
"Section 40.01: (a) The Borrower shall cause Gujarat to maintain 
records and accounts to reflect in accordance with sound accounting 
practices the operations, resources and expenditures, in respect of 
Part Z of the Project, of the departments or agencies of Gujarat or 
of the Authority responsible for carrying out such Part of the 
Project.
(b) The Borrower shall ensure that the annual audit wherever referred 
to in

The World Bank) of each Selected Agreement:
"Section 50.01 (a) Pursuant to the relevant provisions of the General

additional events are provided:
(i)

(ii)

of the Gujarat Agreement, an extraordinary situation 
shall have arisen which shall make it improbable that 
Gujarat will be able to perform its obligations under the

(iii)
Gujarat shall have taken any action for the dissolution, 
disestablishment or suspension of operation of GSDMA, or 
GSDMA shall have amended or repealed its memorandum or 
rules and regulations so as to materially and adversely 
affect the ability of GSDMA to carry out its functions 
under this Agreement.
(b) Pursuant to the relevant provision of the General Conditions 
relating to other events of acceleration, the following additional event is 
specified, namely, that the event specified in Section 50.01 (a) (i) of 
this Agreement shall occur and shall continue for a period of sixty days 
after notice thereof shall have been given by the World Bank to the 
Borrower".

Borrower."

ATTACHMENT  2
1.
In each Selected Agreement, the following new paragraph is added after 
the first paragraph of Schedule 2 (Description of  the Project) setting out 
an additional objective of the Project:
"A further objective of the Project is to assist Gujarat in carrying 
out a program of rehabilitation and reconstruction in the areas 
affected by the Gujarat earthquake of February 26, 2001, including 
restoration of housing and public buildings, restoration of basic 
infrastructure in the education, health, roads and irrigation 
sectors, and development of an institutional framework to allow 
better disaster mitigation and risk management for future natural 
disasters."
2.
In each Selected Agreement, the following new Part is added at the 
end of Schedule 2 (Description of the Project):
"Part Z:
Earthquake Reconstruction and Rehabilitation Program
1.
Housing Component
(a) Construction of temporary shelters for people whose homes have 
been damaged by the earthquake in the Affected Area.
(b) Removal of debris and rubble resulting from earthquake damages in 
the Affected Area..
(c) Reconstruction of fully collapsed houses and repair of partially 
damaged houses in the Affected Area.
(d) Training of masons, artisans, and communities in appropriate 
seismic and cyclone resistant building technology.
(e) Provision of technical assistance for retrofitting of private 
houses to make them seismic and cyclone resistant.
2.
Health Component
Page  23
(a) Reconstruction and repair of health and nutrition infrastructure 
facilities damaged by the earthquake in the Affected Area, including 
provision of training, equipment, furniture and supplies and improving 
health care delivery and waste management systems at these facilities.
(b) Establishment and operation of a Rehabilitation Center in Bhuj for 
physical rehabilitation of  affected patients and management of post 
traumatic stress, including provision of training to doctors and 
para-medical personnel at such center.
(c) Upgrading Regional Depots of Central Medical Stores  organization.
(d) Carrying out a program of information, education and communication 
to improve health care seeking behavior of the population in the Affected 
Area.
3.
Education Component
(a) Reconstruction and repair in the Affected Area of government 
primary schools, secondary schools, colleges, technical institutions and 
teacher training colleges damaged by the earthquake and replacement of 
instructional materials and furniture.
(b) Establishment of counseling centers in the Affected Area and 
implementation of a counseling program for psychologically affected 
students and teachers, including provision of training to counselors.
4.
Dam Safety and Irrigation Component
(a) Reconstruction, strengthening and repair of damaged earthen dams 
for medium and minor irrigation and water supply  schemes in the Affected 
Area, including embankments, appurtenant works, and distribution networks, 
and provision of technical assistance for design, construction monitoring, 
quality control, supervision and implementation of the works related to 
such schemes, as well as strengthening the capacity of the Water Resources 
Department of Gujarat for quality control and supervision monitoring.
(b) Carrying out of studies on seismic design standards and hydrology 
of each scheme referred to in (a) above.
(c) Establishment and operation of: (i) five Seismological 
Observatories for monitoring of seismicity in Gujarat; and (ii) two Dam 
Safety Panels.
5.
Public Buildings Component
(a) Demolition of damaged public buildings and removal of debris.
(b) Repairs and strengthening of damaged public buildings, 
construction of temporary offices, and provision of furniture, equipment 
and technical assistance.
(c) Reconstruction of administrative buildings, police stations, 
outposts, and residential quarters, restoration works and retrofitting of 
buildings, preservation of historical monuments and provision of furniture, 
equipment and technical assistance.
6.
Roads Component
The rehabilitation of damaged State roads in the Affected Area, 
including reconstruction of damaged bridges and structures, and provision 
of technical assistance for survey, design and technical audit.
7.
Capacity Building and Community Organizations
Page  24
Improving the capacity of government agencies, communities and NGOs in 
the Affected Area to participate in activities related to the 
implementation of Part Z of the Project through, inter alia, mobilization, 
communication, and provision of training.
8.
Disaster Management Component
(a)
Establishment and operation of the Gujarat State Disaster 
Management  Authority to manage this Part of the Project and to prepare and 
implement  strategies and programs for dealing with future natural 
disasters.
(b)
Strengthening the capacity of the Authority to carry out its 
functions through, inter alia, provision of technical assistance and 
equipment."
