Extension of Closing Date and Amendment to the
Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the above-mentioned Development Credit Agreement, between Rwandese 
Republic (the Borrower) and International Development Association (the Association), 
dated August 14, 1987 and to your letter of June 19, 1995.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association has agreed to extend the 
Closing Date of the Agreement from December 31, 1994 to December 31, 1996.
follows:
The Association has also agreed to amend the Agreement to increase the 
percentages of expenditures for all activities to be carried out and to be financed 
under Categories 3 and 4 of
the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 of the Agreement.
Consequently, and taking into account actual and foreseen expenditures incurred 
for items to be financed under each category, the Association proposed to amend the 
table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement to read as follows:
