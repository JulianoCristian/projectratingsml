Re: 
Interim Fund Credit No. 008-EGT 
 
(Education Enhancement Program) 
 
Amendment Letter                         
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Interim Fund Development Credit Agreement (Education Enhancement 
Program) dated May 9, 1997 between the Arab Republic of Egypt (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association as Administrator of the interim trust funds contributed by 
certain members of the International Development Association pursuant to Resolution No. IDA 184 
of the Board of Governors of the International Development Association (the Association).  We 
also refer to the request to the Association contained in a letter from Ms. Souraya Abo El-Saoud 
dated September 8, 2003 to amend the Interim Fund Development Credit Agreement. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association, after due consideration, has agreed to 
the said request.  Accordingly, the Interim Fund Development Credit Agreement is hereby 
amended as follows: 
 
1. 
In Schedule 3 to the Interim Fund Development Credit Agreement, the second and third 
paragraph under Part C (Other Procurement Procedures), are modified to read as follows 
 
“International Shopping
Goods estimated to cost less than $250,000 equivalent per contract, up to an 
aggregate amount not to exceed $2,200,000 equivalent, may be procured under contracts 
awarded on the basis of international shopping procedures in accordance with the 
provisions of paragraphs 3.5 and 3.6 of the Guidelines. 
 National 
Shopping
Goods estimated to cost less than $100,000 equivalent per contract, up to an 
aggregate amount not to exceed $3,500,000 equivalent, may be procured under contracts 
awarded on the basis of national shopping procedures in accordance with the provisions 
of paragraphs 3.5 and 3.6 of the Guidelines.” 
 
2. 
All the terms and conditions of the Interim Fund Development Credit Agreement, as 
amended hereby, shall remain in force full and effect.
