Nigeria: Science and Technology Education in Post Basic Project  
 
(Credit 4304-UNI) Amendment to the Financing Agreement 
 
We refer to the Financing Agreement (“Agreement”) dated August 28, 2007 for the above-
referenced Project between the Federal Republic of Nigeria (“Recipient”) and the International 
Development Association (“Association”); to the Aide-Memoire of the Mid Term Review of the 
Project held from February 15-25, 2010 recommending restructuring of the Project; and to your 
letter dated August 31, 2009 requesting reallocation of funds.  
 
Pursuant to the Aide Memoire and to the above mentioned request, we are pleased to 
inform you that the Association hereby proposes to amend the Agreement to read as follows: 
 
1. 
Section II.A of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is amended to read as follows:  
“The Recipient shall monitor and evaluate the progress of the Project and prepare Project 
Reports in accordance with the provisions of Section 4.08 of the General Conditions and 
on the basis of indicators, acceptable to the Association, set forth in the Project 
Implementation Manual”  
2. 
The table in Section IV.A.2 of Schedule 2 to the Agreement shall be amended as set forth 
in the attachment to this letter.  
3. 
The Closing Date specified in Section IV.B.2 of Schedule 2 to the Agreement shall be 
amended to state June 30, 2013. 
 
Page  2
All provisions of the Agreement, as amended herein, shall remain in full force and effect.  
 
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendments to the Agreement on 
behalf of the Recipient by countersigning and dating the two original copies of this letter and 
returning one countersigned original to us. The amendments to the Agreement shall become 
effective as of the date of countersignature of this letter.
