                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project objective is to contribute to the reduction of the negative impact of food price volatility on the poor and vulnerable in selected areas, and support the
                         protection and building of community assets in poor communities.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project, which is funded through European Union Food Crisis Rapid Response Facility under the Global Food Crisis Response Program, became effective on April 27, 2010 and
                         has been under implementation for about 7 months. To date, EUR4.9 million of the total EUR17.3 million has been disbursed, and a withdrawal application in the amount of EUR4.6
                         million is under process. The project's current closing date is June 30, 2011.
