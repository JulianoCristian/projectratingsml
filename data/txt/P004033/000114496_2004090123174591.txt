Dear Sir:
We refer to the Interim Fund Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) of 
even date herewith between the Kingdom of Cambodia (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association (the Association) as Administrator (the 
Administrator) of the interim trust fund (the Interim Fund) established with funds 
contributed by certain members of the Association pursuant to Resolution No. IDA 184 
of the Board of Governors of the Association and, more particularly, to the definition 
of "Participating Countries" to be found in Section 1.01 (c) thereof.
We have to inform you that, for purposes of the Agreement, the following 
countries are not "Participating Countries" and therefore, not eligible to supply 
goods and services under the Credit: Bahamas, Bahrain, Barbados, Brunei Darussalam, 
Cyprus, Israel, Oman, Qatar, Singapore, United Arab Emirates and United States.  This 
list of ineligible countries is in addition to the list of ineligible countries under 
paragraphs 1.6 and 1.8 of the "Guidelines for Procurement under IBRD Loans and IDA 
Credits" published by the Bank in January 1995 and revised in January and August 1996 
(the Guidelines).
In accordance with the operational policies of the Administrator, the term 
"ineligible countries" applies both to the nationality of bidders, and to the source 
of goods and services provided.\0c 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing the enclosed copy of 
this letter and returning it to us for our records.
Sincerely yours,
