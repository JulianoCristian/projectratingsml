                         Global Environmental Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Global Objective of the proposed project is to enhance the productivity of fadama areas and the livelihood systems they support through sustainable land

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The overall implementation of the project has been very significant. To date, the project has been able to disburse about 88% of the grant. This comprise of goods (76%),
                         Consultancy Services (94%), Trainings and Workshop (90%), Sub-projects (96%), Incremental Operating Costs (59%). This current disbursement status includes the actual draw-
                         down, pending SOEs and the firm commitments on sub-projects, incremental operating costs and training. The beneficiary assessment / impact evaluation has been conducted while

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The project has received a 100% positive response in the six participating states during the just concluded beneficiary assessment. A key finding was the existence of watershed
                         management coordination capacity; portraying that the project has exceeded the minimum key performance indicator of having sustainable watershed management coordination
                         capacity established in at least 60 percent of participating states. The project has also exceeded 35 percent cut off point that sustainable land and water management practices must
                         have been mainstreamed into LDP of participating states. The project is yet to achieve the third key performance indicator of having area under sustainable land and water
                         management practice by the end of project at three pilot sites increased by at least 80%.
