Re: Loan 3105-AL (Third Ports Project)
    Amendment to the Loan Agreement
We refer to the Loan Agreement for the Third Ports Project (Loan No. 3105-AL) 
between the Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria (the  Borrower) and the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), dated Septozaber 17, 
1990, as amended (the Loan Agreement).  We also  refer to the Borrower's request to 
amend the Loan Amendment by letter to the Bank dated March 8, 1996.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank, after due consideration, has acceded 
to the above request.  Accordingly, the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
          In Schedule 1, the percentage of expenditures to be financed under 
Categories l(a), l(b), l(c) and l(d) shall be 80%.
All the terms and conditions of the Loan Agreement, not amended hereby shall 
remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the 
confirmation on the enclosed copy of this Letter Amendment and returning it to us.
