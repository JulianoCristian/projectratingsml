Madame:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Youth Training Project) dated October 29, 1997, 
between the Republic of Hungary (the Borrower) and the International Bank of 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), and to your letter dated March 19, 1998, 
requesting certain amendments to the Loan Agreement. 
We hereby confirm the Bank’s agreement to the following amendments to the Loan 
Agreement:
1.
Section 2.02 (b) is amended to replace “Dollars” with “Deutsche Marks.”
2.
The amounts in paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 6 are amended as follows:  
(i) 
the amount of  “$4,000,000” is replaced with “DEM6,900,000”; 
(ii)
the amount of  “$2,000,000” is replaced with “DEM3,400,000”; and 
(iii)
the amount of  “$4,000,000” is replaced with “DEM6,900,000.”
 
Please confirm your agreement to these amendments by signing, dating and 
returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter.
This amendment shall become effective upon receipt of the countersigned copy of 
this letter by us.
