                         Decentralized Rural Electrification Project (P074288)
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)
                         The project objective is to test institutional, financial and delivery mechanisms to promote better access to electricity in rural and peri-urban areas.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Further to Bank re-engagement in Guinea and payment of country's arrears, closing date for both IDA and GEF financings was retroactively extended from December 31, 2010 to
                         June 30, 2013. The Project is on track to meet its development objective by new closing date. Out of a target of 30 villages, the Project has already electrified 24 villages.
