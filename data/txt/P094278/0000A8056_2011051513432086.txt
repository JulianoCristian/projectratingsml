                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The HNSP overall objective is to strengthen the health system and its capacity to improve the health and nutrition status of the population, notably of women,
                         children, and the poor, as it will support the implementation of the Government Program for the health and nutrition sectors during the period 2006-2008.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                            Yes            No
