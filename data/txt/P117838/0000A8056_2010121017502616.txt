                         Implementation Status Overview
Public Disclosure Copy




                         The First Education Development Policy Loan became effective during the period under review, on the basis of a series of completed policy actions that form part of the
                         Government's education reform program 2009-2012. Government has made progress towards carrying out a number of the policy actions identified for the Second Education
                         Development Policy Loan, in the areas of demand-side incentives for primary school attendance, the roll-out of a computerized budget accounting system at the regional level, and
                         the on-going decentralization of decision making (e.g. on asset management) to regional education offices.
