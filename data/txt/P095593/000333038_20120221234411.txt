Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
The objectives are to stabilize the power services in Dili, by restoring or improving operational efficiency,
reliability, safety and availability of the power supply, and to promote long-term sustainability of the power
sector. These would, in turn, contribute to the social stability and economic development of the country.
