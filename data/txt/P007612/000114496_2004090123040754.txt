Re:
MEXICO - Second Solid Waste Management Project (Loan 3752-ME):
Amendment                                                                                                  
Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement (Second Solid Waste Management Project) 
between International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and Banco 
Nacional de Obras y Servicios Públicos, S.N.C. (the Borrower) dated October 2, 1994, 
as amended (the Loan Agreement) and the Guarantee Agreement for the same Project 
between the Bank and United Mexican States (the Guarantor) of even date therewith, as 
amended. Whenever used in this letter, the several terms defined in the Loan Agreement 
have the respective meanings therein set forth.
Pursuant to discussions between staff of the Borrower, the Guarantor and the 
Bank, the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement and the Guarantee Agreement 
as follows:
Loan Agreement
1.
In Section 1.02 (n), the definition of “Participating Municipality” is deleted 
and replaced by the following: “Participating Municipalities” means the Guarantor’s 
municipalities of Aguascalientes, Tampico, Guanajuato, Manzanillo, Mexicali, San Luis 
Rio Colorado, and Durango. This list can be modified prior agreement between the Bank 
and the Borrower.
2.
In Section 2.03, the date “December 31, 1999” is replaced by “June 30, 2000”.
3.
In Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement, the table in paragraph 1 is deleted and 
replaced by the table set forth in Annex 1 to this letter.
4.
In Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement:
(a)
In the first paragraph, subparagraph (a) is deleted and replaced by the 
following: 
“(a) to carry out a pilot program for the development and execution of 
integrated  
solid waste management plans by the Participating Municipalities;”
(b)
Part C is deleted.
Guarantee Agreement
All references to Part C of the Project are deleted.
