          Re:  Credit No. 1904 KE (Third Population Project)
               Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Sir:
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the above-
mentioned Project dated October 24, 1988, between the Republic of
Kenya and the International Development Association and to Mr. A.H.
Ali's letter dated July 17, 1991, requesting an amendment to the
Project to provide for temporary office accommodation for the
National Council for Population and Development.
     This letter is to inform you of our agreement to your request
and to propose the following amendments to the Development Credit
Agreement.
     1.   The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is amended to
read as appears in the Attachment to this letter.
     2.   Part A of Schedule 2 is amended by the addition of a new
Part as follows:
     "6.  Rental of office accommodation for NCPD".
     All other provisions of the Development Credit Agreement
remain in full force and effect.
     Your letter of July 17, 1991, and this letter shall constitute
an agreement between the Borrower and the Association and the
amendment shall become effective as of the date of this letter.
                              Yours sincerely,
                              Callisto Madavo, Director
                              Eastern Africa Department
