            Re:   Loan No. 3091-VE
                  (Structural Adjustment Loan)
                  Amendment of Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
            Please refer to the Loan Agreement (Structural
Adjustment Loan), dated October 16, 1989 (the Loan Agreement) ,
between Republic of Venezuela (the Borrower) and International Bank
for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), and to the letter,
dated February 9, 1990, sent to the Bank by Ms. Maria Ocando
Troconis, Directora General Sectorial de Finanzas Publicas.
            We hereby propose to amend the Loan Agreement as
follows:
(a)   The following subparagraph should be inserted after
subparagraph (v) of Section 3.03:
      "(vi) auditing services to assist the Borrower in discharging
      its obligations under Section 3.04 of this Agreement; and";
(b)   Paragraph 2 (b) of Schedule 3 should be modified to read:
      "(b) Contracts for computer and office equipment to be
      acquired as part of the technical assistance program referred
      to in Section 3.03 of this Agreement may be awarded on the
      basis of comparison of price quotations solicited from a list
      of at least three suppliers, in accordance with procedures
      satisfactory to the Bank.  The amount of each such contract
      shall not exceed the equivalent of $50,000.".
      Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments to
the Loan Agreement by signing and dating the enclosed confirmation
copy of this letter and returning it to us.  Upon your signature and
the receipt by us of the said copy, this amendment will become
effective, retroactively, as of January 1, 1990.
                            Sincerely yours,
