                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project's Development Objectives are to assist Vietnam's banking sector to (i)meet the demands of the economy for fast, reliable and safe payment
                         services, (ii)broaden access to finance to facilitate the achievement of the government's povertyreduction targets, and (iii) improve the operations of the
                         participating banks through theexpansion of the IBPS and the CBS.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Significant progress has been made towards achieving the project development objectives (PDO). The system connectivity target has been met. The Inter-Bank Payment System
                         (IBPS), and the core banking systems of three participating banks have been performing well. The participating banks have improved customer service levels, and are able to reach
                         out to more customers through the new delivery channels. All this will facilitate the broadening of the access to finance. Furthermore, the operational efficiency of the participating
