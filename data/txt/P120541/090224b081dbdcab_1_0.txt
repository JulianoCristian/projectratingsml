                         Program Development Objectives
                         Program Development Objective (from Program Document)

                         To strengthen the schools sector’s institutional arrangements in the short term, in ways that in the medium term will: (i) increase access to school education, especially
                         for rural girls and boys at the lower secondary level; (ii) improve the quality of teaching and learning in primary and lower secondary education; and (iii) enhance
                         efficiency in decentralized governance of the schools sector.
