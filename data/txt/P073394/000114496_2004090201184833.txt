Minister of  Finance
Ministry of Economy and Finance
Phnom Penh

Re:  Credit No.3472 KH
(Flood Emergency Rehabilitation Project)
Participating Countries
Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the Flood Emergency 
Rehabilitation Project of even date herewith (Development Credit Agreement) between 
the Kingdom of Cambodia (the Borrower) and International Development Association 
(IDA).
Pursuant to Resolution No. IDA 194 of the Board of Governors of IDA, we wish to 
inform you that, for purposes of the Project, the following members of the Association 
are not Participating Countries within the meaning of Section 2.01(12) of the General 
Conditions referred to in Section 1.01 (a) of the Development Credit Agreement:
Bahamas, Bahrain, Barbados, Brunei Darussalam, Cyprus, Oman, Qatar, Singapore, 
and United Arab Emirates.
Sincerely yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
By /s/ Ian C. Porter
Authorized Representative
