                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         To support markets for land and real estate and the more intensive and effective use of land and real estate. This will be done through the further development
                         of a reliable and well-functioning system for registration of rights in immovable property.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The restructured project design has been formally agreed, with revised results targets, time frame, cost estimates and descriptions of project activities to take into account
                         implementation experience, and updates in the focus of project activities. The Government signed the amendment which extended the closing date by one year, to May 31, 2013.
                         Noteworthy progress includes:
                         (a) A charter and a Government resolution have been drafted to reform the land and real estate registry and cadastral system into a more sustainable organizational structure by
                         rationalizing and consolidating the headquarters and local registry offices into a single public self-financing enterprise;


                                                                                                                                                                                                            Page 1 of 9
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                       Report No: ISR3056



                         (b) The average transaction time for sales and mortgages has been reduced to 1.3 days;
                         (c) Information technology achievements include the use of open-source geographic information system (GIS) software and the introduction of a variety of e-services for the public;

                         (d) There have been re-invigorated activities to upgrade the financial management capacity and establish a transparent and modern automated financial accounting and management
                         system; and
                         (e) The implementing agency, the Department of Cadastre and Registration, has improved technical capacity in cadastral surveying and mapping.
