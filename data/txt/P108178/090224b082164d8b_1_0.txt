                         Implementation Status Overview
                         As the Project reaches its Closing Date of December 31, 2013, it has fully achieved its objective. All five PDO outcome targets have been achieved. The time to register transaction
                         has decreased from 1 day in 2007 to 0.5 days. The number of sales has increased by 69.8 %, and mortgages by 60.4% compared to 2007. Within the Doing Business Survey, the
                         Kyrgyz property registration system was ranked 9th in the world on the property registration indicator by June 2013.
