                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project demonstrates notable implementation progress since the last mission in March 2011. The Department of Cadastre and Registration of Rights to Immovable Property
                         (DCR) and the Local Registry Offices (LROs) have made steady progress in implementing the project. The project is maintaining the pace established after restructuring and is
                         expected to meet its development objectives by the closing date of May 31, 2013. The key achievements to date include: (i) reduced average transaction time to 1.1 days; (ii)
                         success in the fully operational Kyrgyz Land Information System (KLIS) taking into account the automated registry system (ARS) data from all 49 local registry offices (LROs) with
