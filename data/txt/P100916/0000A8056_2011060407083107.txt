                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The development objective of the proposed RFIII would be to increase economic benefits to rural private enterprises and households by increasing their access
                         to finance. The expected outcomes would include: (i) improved access to financial services for rural entrepreneurs; (ii) increased capital investment made by
                         the rural entrepreneurs as well as increased employment; (iii) increased lending, particularly term lending to the rural private sector for capital investment by all
                         participating financial institutions (PFIs) on market-based terms.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall, the implementation of the project has progressed well and is on the right track to meet its development objective. The implementation of the credit funds has progressed very
                         well and all targets concerning the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) have been met or exceeded. The cumulative investments made by the rural private sector as a result of the
                         project amounted to USD 118 million, well above the target of USD 77 million. According to the Project monitoring system, these investments have created 32,142 new jobs
