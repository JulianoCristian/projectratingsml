                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The proposed Investment Guarantee Facility is intended to assist Afghanistan in its reconstruction efforts by stimulating foreign direct investment through a
                         program of political risk insurance . in an environment where the perception of political risk by foreign investors is very high. The project is designed to
                         stimulate private business activity and attract foreign private investment in a variety of sectors, including but not limited to manufacturing, agribusiness,
                         banking, and smaller scale infrastructure projects. It will stimulate the local economy, generate employment, create tax revenues, and assist in the transfer of
                         modern technologies and business practices. from abroad to Afghanistan
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?
