                         Implementation Status Overview
                         After project restructuring progress has beefed up with improvements in the provision of infrastructure through the construction of water supply facilities, classrooms, health centers,
                         roads and drainage facilities. This is not withstanding, disbursement has remained below expectation, after six years of implementation only 40% has been disbursed and about 40%
                         commitment with the balance not committed. There are still challenges related to delays in finalizing procurement issues, weak coordination between some government agencies and
