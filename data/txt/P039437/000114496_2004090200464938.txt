Re: 
Loan 7067-EC (Poverty Reduction and Local Rural Development Project) 
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency: 
 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement for the above-referenced Project between the Government of 
Ecuador (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) dated 
January 3, 2002 (The Loan Agreement). 
 
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to the request in your letter dated March 12, 2003, we 
agree to amend the Loan Agreement as follows: 
 
Schedule 1, Part A, Paragraph 2 (c) is hereby amended and replaced to read in its entirely as follows: 
“(c) 
the term “Operating Costs” means the cost of:  (i) operation and maintenance of vehicles; (ii) 
office equipment and computer maintenance; (iii) rental and refurbishment of office space, office 
utilities and office supplies of PCU; and (iv) travel and per diem of personnel of PCU; and”. 
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing this letter in the space 
provided and returning it to us.  The provisions set forth in this amendment letter shall become effective as of 
May 13, 2002 upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this amendment letter.
