7a. Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
To assist the Government of Serbia in reducing cost and improving safety for road users through: (a)
improving the condition of selected road sections of the main and regional road network; (b) improving the
effectiveness of road maintenance through the introduction of performance-based contracting in two pilot
regions; and (c) introducing safe road design principles, road safety audits and targeted improvement
measures.
