           Kenya:     National Agricultural Research Project (Cr.1849-KE)
                      Amendments
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the above-mentioned
Project, dated May 13, 1988 between the Republic of Kenya (the Borrower) and
the International Development Association (the Association) as amended to
date, and to your letter of February 10, 1994 requesting an extension of the
project closing date, a revision of disbursement percentages and a
reallocation of funds.
     We are pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to your
request and proposes the following amendments to the Development Credit
Agreement:
     (a)   The date in Section 2.03 is amended to read "June 30, 1995".
     (b)   The table set out in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the
           Development Credit Agreement is amended to read as shown in the
           attachment to this letter.
     All provisions of the Development Credit Agreement except as amended
herein shall remain in full force and effect.
     Kindly countersign, date and return to us the enclosed copy of this
letter.  As requested, this amendment shall become effective retroactively
from January 1, 1994.
                                      Sincerely yours,
                                      /s/ Robert Hindle
