      Kenya:  National Agricultural Research Project (Cr. 1849-KE)
      Amendment
      We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the above-mentioned
Project, dated May 13, 1988 between the Republic of Kenya (the Borrower) and
the International Development Association (the Association) as amended to
date, and to your letter of June 10, 1993 requesting an extension of the
project closing date and a reallocation of funds.
      We are pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to your
request and proposes the following amendment to the Development Credit
Agreement as follows:
      (a)   "The date in Section 2.03 is amended to read "June 30, 1994."
      (b)   The table set out in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the
Development Credit Agreement is amended to read as shown in the attachment
to this letter.
      All provisions of the Development Credit Agreement as amended herein
shall remain in full force and effect.
      Kindly counter-sign, date and return to us the enclosed copy of this
letter.  This amendment shall become effective as of date of counter-
signature.
                                    Sincerely yours,
                                    Francis X. Colaco
                                    Director
