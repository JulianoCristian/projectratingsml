Re:
BELIZE - Loan 2959-BE (Agricultural Credit and
Export Development Project)
- Second Amendment
Dear Mr.  Arnold:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between Belize (the Borrower) and the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) for an 
Agricultural Credit and Export Development Project dated July 19, 1988, as 
amended.  We are pleased to inform you that the Bank, in view of the transfer of 
responsibilities from BCB, as defined in the Loan Agreement, to the Banana 
Growers Association, also as defined in the Loan Agreement, and pursuant to your 
request agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
Section 1.02 (o): the words "or BGA" are added between the word 
"Association" and the word "means", before the quotes.
2.
Section 3.01 (a) (iii) and (d): The abbreviation 'IBCB" as it 
appears in both paragraphs is replaced by "BGA".
3.
Section 3.14 is replaced by the following: "The Borrower shall 
cause BGA to maintain a project manager with qualifications and 
experience satisfactory to the Bank."
4.
Section 3.15 is deleted and Section 3.16 is redesignated 3.15.
5.
Schedule 2. Part Cl: The abbreviation "BCB" as it appears in the 
heading and in paragraph 1 is replaced by "BGA".
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing the form of 
confirmation attached, returning it to us duly dated.
Sincerely yours,
Krishna Challa
Acting Director
Country Department III
Latin America and the Caribbean Region
CONFIRMED
BELIZE
Keith Arnold
Authorized Representative
