                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Ministry of Peace and Reconstruction (MOPR) is currently delivering Employment and Self Employment Services (ESES) to around 3,000 conflict affected persons (CAPs) in 12
                         districts, and is preparing a plan to scale up the delivery of these services nationally to CAPs. Interim cash benefits have been provided to 13,877 families of the deceased and 4,279
                         widows. MOPR has conducted a third monitoring exercise which shows that 87% beneficiary were satisfied with the cash benefits delivery procedures; 82% expressed that the
                         government officials were friendly while processing their applications after the submission of proper documents; about 74% of the recipient of the cash benefits are spouses and

                         expenses, repair home and buy capital ( land/cattle), repay loans, and self and children education.
