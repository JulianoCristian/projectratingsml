                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The road rehabilitation and maintenance works are fully completed. While the sustainability of the overall rehabilitation program is still at risk due to relatively low national budget for
                         this component of the General Directorate for National Roads and Motorways (GDDKiA) program for 2011 and onwards, the new national roads program and recently proposed set of
                         institutional support projects for GDDKiA and Ministry of Infrastructure is expected to reduce such risk. In addition the ongoing policy dialog with the government stimulated by the
