                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The road rehabilitation and maintenance works are fully completed. While the sustainability of the overall rehabilitation program is still at risk due to relatively low national budget for
                         this component of the General Directorate for National Roads and Motorways(GDDKiA) work program for 2010 and onwards, the expected new roads program and recently proposed
                         set of institutional support projects for GDDKiA and Ministry of Infrastructure is expected to reduce such risk. In addition the ongoing policy dialog with the government stimulated by
