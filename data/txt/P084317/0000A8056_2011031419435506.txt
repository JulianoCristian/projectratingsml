                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The revised project development objective is to improve efficiency, quality and equity in the provision of basic education in theterritory of the Recipient with a
                         view to achieving universal quality primary education by 2015.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation is progressing, at a slower pace than expected. One major positive development is the finalization of the Education Sector Strategy that has been adopted
                         recently by the Government and will provide the framework for further development of the sector. Delays in the production of statistics do not allow a comprehensive assessment of
