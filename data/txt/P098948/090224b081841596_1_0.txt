                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project will complete on 31 December 2012 as per its original schedule. The Project will deliver on its development outcome to improve water supply services, wastewater
                         services, and flood protection measures in municipalities selected from the inland parts of Croatia. Most of the project results indicators will reach, and in some case exceed, their
                         targets by the end of 2012. The technical assistance components to improve the financial capacities of the local utilities, for TAs, studies, and for the preparation of projects for EU
                         financing are completed or nearing completion.
