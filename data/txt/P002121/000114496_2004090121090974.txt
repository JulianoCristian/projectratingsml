     NIGERIA:  Credit No. 2353 UNI (Environmental Management Project)
               Amendment of Schedules 1 and 4 to the
               Development Credit Agreement
          We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Environmental
Management Project) dated May 11, 1992 between the Federal Republic of
Nigeria (the Borrower) and the International Development Association
(the Association) and to the Federal Ministry of Finance's letters
dated December 5, 1995 and February 1, 1996 requesting an amendment to
the Development Credit Agreement. The Association agrees to your request
and accordingly proposes that the said Development Credit Agreement be
amended as follows:
          (a)  in Schedule 1 thereto by:
               (i)  the deletion of the provisions of  "100% of foreign
                    expenditures" occurring in the third column of
                    Category (5)  of the table in paragraph 1 and the
                    substitution therefor of the following new provisions,
                    namely, "100% of local expenditures;"
               (ii) the addition in Category (5) of the table in para-
                    graph 1 next after subcategory (d): "FMANR (Land
                    Resources Department)" of the following new sub-
                    category (e):
                    "(e) FMWR 40,000";
              (iii) the deletion of the amount of $1,600,000 allocated to
                    "Unallocated" and the substitution therefor of the
                    amount of $1,560,000"; and
               (iv) the deletion in paragraph 2 of (A) the word "and" at
                    the end of the definition of the term "foreign
                    expenditures" in subparagraph (a) of paragraph 2,
                    and (B) the period at the end of the definition of
                    the term "local expenditures" in subparagraph (b) of
                    the said paragraph; and the substitution for the
                    said period of a semi-colon and the word "and";
               (v)  the addition next after the definition of "local
                    expenditures" in said subparagraph (b) of the
                    following new definition: `(c) the term "incremental
                    operating costs" means Project related costs (other
                    than civil service salaries, maintenance costs for
                    vehicles, equipment and premises) and includes office
                    administration and communication costs;' and
          (b)  in Schedule 4 by:
               (i)  the deletion in subparagraph (a) of paragraph 1 of the
                    definition of the term "eligible Categories" and the
                    substitution  therefor of the following new definition:
               `(a) the term "eligible Categories" means Categories (1)

               (a), (b), (e), (f), (g) and (h); (3) (a) and (c); (4) (a),
               (b), (d), (e), (f) and (h); and (5) (a), (c), (d) and (e)
               set forth in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to
               this Agreement.'; and
               (ii) the deletion of the amount of $100,000 in the
                    definition of "Authorized Allocation" in subparagraph
                    (b) of the said paragraph and the substitution
                    therefor of the amount of $300,000.
          Kindly indicate your agreement with the amendments proposed
above by signing and dating the form of confirmation on the enclosed
copy of this letter and return the signed copy to us. The amendments
shall come into force on the date of your signature.
