                                          May 24,  1990
His Excellency Manuel Santos
Minister of State for Economy and Finance
Ministry of Economy and Finance
Republic of Guinea-Bissau
Re:  Credit No. 2019 GUB (Second Structural Adjustment Credit)
     Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (referred to as
the "Agreement"), dated June 29, 1989, between the Republic of
Guinea-Bissau (the "Borrower") and the International Development
Association (the "Association").
     We are pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to
your request to reduce the minimum amount for contracts eligible for
financing under the Agreement from $10,000 to $5,000.  Consequently,
paragraph 2(d) of Schedule 1 of the Agreement is hereby amended by
substituting in line 2 the number "$10,000" with "$5,000."
     Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing and
dating the form of confirmation on the two originals of this letter
and returning one signed original to us. The amendment will take
effect from the date of your confirmation.
                        Sincerely yours,
                 INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT  ASSOCIATION
                        Caio K. Koch-Weser
                 Director, Western Africa Department
                          Africa Region
