The Credit
Section 2.01. The Association agrees to lend to the Bor-

Special Drawing Rights (SDR 8,700,000).
Section 2.02. (a) The amount of the Credit may be withdrawn
from the Credit Account in accordance with the provisions of
Schedule 1 to this Agreement, as such Schedule may be amended
from time to time by agreement between the Borrower and the Asso-
ciation, for amounts paid (or, if the Association shall so agree,

Project and to be financed out of the proceeds of the Credit;
provided, however, that: (A) no withdrawal shall be made in
respect of a Sub-loan unless (1) the Sub-loan has been approved

loan an for which the Association has authorized withdrawals
from the Credit Account; and (B) except as the Association shall
otherwise agree, no Sub-loan made to any one Investment Enter-
prise shall exceed the sum of forty-four thousand Special Draw-
ing Rights (SDR 44,000) equivalent or the aggregate amount of
Sub-loans made to any one Investment Enterprise shall exceed
the sum of one hundred thirty thousand Special Drawing Rights
(SDR 130,000) equivalent; provided, further that each Sub-
loan made to either Emporium, Nepal Wool Trading Company or
Nepal Metal Trading Company shall not exceed each the sum
of one hundred seventy-four thousand Special Drawing Rights
(SDR 174,000) equivalent, or the aggregate amount of Sub-loans
made to either Emporium, Nepal Wool Trading Company or Nepal
Metal Trading Company shall not exceed for each of them the sum
of one hundred seventy-four thousand Special Drawing Rights
(SDR 174,000) equivalent.
(b) A free-limit Sub-loan shall be a Sub-loan for a Sub-
project in an amount to be financed out of the proceeds of the
Credit which shall not exceed the sum of twenty-two thousand

SCHEDULE 2
Description of the Project
The objectives of the Project are to assist the Borrower in
implementing its policies on efficient industrial development and
export growth as well as increasing output, exports, earnings and
income in cottage and small industries in Nepal.
The Project consists of the following parts, subject to such
modifications thereof as the Borrower and the Association may
agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives:
Part A:
(i)  Implementation of a program by NRB to refinance
Sub-loans granted or made by Participating Credit
Institutions to Investment Enterprises for Sub-
projects.
(ii)  Implementation of a program to improve and upgrade
the overall operation of the Nepal Bank Limited
and the Rastriya Banijya Bank, and to strengthen
the Participating Credit Institutions, and the CSI
Refinance Unit, including provision of appropriate
staff training and technical assistance therefor.
Part B:
Implementation of an export development program, including:
(i) improvement in product design, production techniques, cost
reduction and quality control on exportable Nepalese goods;
(ii) strengthening the operations of the manufacturer/exporter
associations in Nepal; (iii) overseas marketing development; and
(iv) provision of technical assistance therefor.
Part C:
Implementation of skill training and entrepreneurial devel-
opment programs, including provision of raw material supply,
product marketing and technical assistance therefor.
Part D:
Implementation of a program to improve procurement and
inventory management, including provision of technical assistance
therefor.


﻿- 25 -
Part E:
Implementation of a program to assist the Borrower in
improving   its  industrial   export  policies   and   procedures,
including provision of technical assistance therefor.
Part F:
Implementation of a program to provide management support
and to monitor and evaluate the various activities under the
Project, including provision of technical assistance therefor.
The Project is expected to be completed by December 31,
1993.
