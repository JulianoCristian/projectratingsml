                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project development objective is to provide the public training system with improved mechanisms for responsiveness to employment needs, and to
                         enhance the capacity and effectiveness of the Skills Development Fund in articulating and financing the training needs of enterprises.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Driven by the rising social and political unrest, the Bank's staff evacuation to Amman, and the rising difficulty in fulfilling the Bank's fiduciary responsibility in providing supervision
                         support to the project, suspension of disbursement has been declared effective as of July 28, 2011. The situation will be reassessed by October 26, 2011.
