                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project's ultimate development objective is to stimulate higher economic growth by increasing effectiveness in the use of the federal road infrastructure. The
                         objective is to be achieved through (a) support to the first phase of the Federal Road Maintenance and Rehabilitation Program, and (b) a comprehensive program of
                         institutional strengthening activities to improve efficiency and sustainability of road transport investment programs.
