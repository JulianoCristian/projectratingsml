LOAN NUMBER 4594 IN
PROJECT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated February 27, 2001, among INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION 
AND DEVELOPMENT (the Bank) and RAJASTHAN RAJYA VIDYUT UTPADAN NIGAM LIMITED (RVUN), 
RAJASTHAN RAJYA VIDYUT PRASARAN NIGAM LIMITED (RVPN), Jaipur Vidyut VITRAN Nigam 
Limited (JAIPUR DISCOM), Ajmer Vidyut VITRAN Nigam Limited (AJMER DISCOM), and Jodhpur 
Vidyut VITRAN Nigam Limited (JODHPUR DISCOM).
WHEREAS (A) by the Loan Agreement of even date herewith between India, acting by 
its President (the Borrower) and the Bank, the Bank has agreed to lend to the Borrower 
an amount of one hundred eighty million dollars ($180,000,000), on the terms and 
conditions set forth in the Loan Agreement, but only on condition that RVUN, RVPN, 
JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM agree to undertake such obligations 
toward the Bank as are set forth in this Agreement; 
(B) 
by the Rajasthan Project Agreement of even date herewith between the Bank 
and the State of Rajasthan (Rajasthan), Rajasthan has undertaken certain obligations 
towards the Bank as set forth in the Rajasthan Project Agreement;
(C) 
by the Subsidiary Loan Agreements to be entered into by Rajasthan with 
Page  2
RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM the proceeds of the loan 
provided for under the Loan Agreement and made available to Rajasthan will be further 
onlent to  , RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM as the case 
may be, on the terms and conditions set forth in such Subsidiary Loan Agreements;
(D) 
the Bank has received a letter dated September 25, 2000 from Rajasthan 
enclosing a policy statement designed to restructure, reform and develop the power 
sector in Rajasthan (hereinafter the Reform Program or Program) and declaring 
Rajasthan’s commitment to the execution of such Program; and
WHEREAS RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM in 
consideration of the Bank’s entering into the Loan Agreement with the Borrower, has 
agreed to undertake the obligations set forth in this Agreement.
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows: 
ARTICLE I
Definitions
Section 1.01. Unless the context otherwise requires, the several terms defined 
in the Loan Agreement and in the General Conditions (as so defined) have the 
respective meanings therein set forth.
ARTICLE II
Execution of the Project
Section 2.01. (a) RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM 
declare their commitment to the objectives of the Project as set forth in Schedule 2 
to the Loan Agreement, and, to this end, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR 
DISCOM shall carry out relevant activities under Parts A, B, C, D.2, and E of the 
Project and RVUN shall carry out the relevant activities under Part D.2 of the 
Project, all with due diligence and efficiency and in conformity with appropriate 
administrative, financial, engineering, and technical practices, and shall provide, or 
cause to be provided, promptly as needed, the funds, facilities, services and other 
resources required for their respective Parts of the Project.
(b)
In carrying out Parts A, B, C and D.2 of the Project, RVPN, JAIPUR 
DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM shall at all times act in accordance with the 
objectives, policies and procedures set out in the Social Policies and Procedures and 
in the Resettlement Action Plan.
(c)
Without limitation upon the provisions of paragraph (a) of this Section, 
and except as the Bank shall otherwise agree, activities to be carried out by RVUN, 
RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM under Parts A, B, C and D.2 of 
the Project respectively shall meet the criteria set forth or referred to in the 
Schedule to this Agreement and RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR 
DISCOM shall submit to the Bank for its approval, the activities proposed to be 
financed out of the proceeds of the Loan including all details in respect of the 
criteria to be met and such other information as the Bank shall reasonably request.
(d)
RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM shall carry 
out the activities approved for financing pursuant to the provision of paragraph (c) 
of this Section 2.01 under the following arrangements:
(i)
for activities under Parts A, B, C and D.2 of the Project, in 
accordance with technical specifications and 
standards satisfactory to 
the Bank; and
(ii) 
for activities under Parts A and B of the Project, in accordance 
with the objectives, policies, principles 
and time schedule of the 
environmental management plan, the resettlement and rehabilitation plan formulated 
pursuant to the provisions of paragraph (3) of the Schedule to 
this Agreement and in accordance with the

Social Policies and Procedures and the Resettlement Action Plan as the

duly perform all the obligations under their respective Subsidiary Loan Agreement. 
Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree, RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and 
JODHPUR DISCOM shall not take or concur in any action which would have the effect of 
assigning, amending, abrogating or waiving the respective Subsidiary Loan Agreement or 
any provision thereof.
Section 2.05. RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM, with 
the participation of Rajasthan, shall by December 31, 2001, submit to the Bank: 
(a) 
a report detailing the outcome of negotiations between RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR

their payables, debt from lenders, and debt service; and
(b)
an updated Financial Restructuring Plan taking account of such 
negotiations and other relevant factors, including the finalization of the Transfer 
Scheme and the privatization strategy for the Distribution Companies.
Section 2.06. (a) RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM 
shall, at the request of the Bank, exchange views with the Bank with regard to the 
progress of the Project and the Program, the performance of their obligations under 
this Agreement and under each Subsidiary Loan Agreement, and other matters relating to 
the purposes of the Loan.
(b)
RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM shall promptly 
inform the Bank and the Borrower of any condition which interferes or threatens to 
interfere with the progress of the Project and the Program, the accomplishment of the 
purposes of the Loan, or the performance by RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM 
and JODHPUR DISCOM of their obligations under this Agreement and under the respective 
Subsidiary Loan Agreement.

ARTICLE III
Management and Operations of
RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM
AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM
Section 3.01. RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM shall:
(a) 
carry on their operations and conduct their affairs in accordance with 
sound administrative, financial and public utility practices under the supervision of 
qualified and experienced management assisted by competent staff in adequate numbers;
(b) 
at all times operate and maintain their plant, machinery, equipment and 
other property, and from time to time, promptly as needed, make all necessary repairs 
and renewals thereof, all in accordance with sound engineering, financial and public 
utility practices; and
(c) 
take out and maintain with responsible insurers, or make other provision 
satisfactory to the Bank for, insurance against such risks and in such amounts as 
shall be consistent with appropriate practice. 
Section 3.02. JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM and JODHPUR DISCOM shall take all such 
measures as may be necessary or required on their part to enable Rajasthan to fulfill 
its obligations under Section 2.07 of the Rajasthan Project Agreement.

Eligibility Criteria
The following eligibility criteria shall apply for activities to be carried out 
under Parts A, B, C and D.2 of the Project by RVUN, RVPN, JAIPUR DISCOM, AJMER DISCOM 
and JODHPUR DISCOM referred to in Section 2.01 (c) of this Agreement.
1. 
The justification (in the context of the overall Rajasthan Power Sector 
Investment Plan) and technical feasibility shall have been established to the 
satisfaction of the Bank.
2. 
All clearances/approvals including environmental clearances shall have been 
obtained from the relevant ministries, departments, agencies or other authorities of 
the Borrower and Rajasthan and documented in the project report.
3. 
An environmental assessment shall have been carried out, and an Environmental 
Management Plan, if needed, based on the environment assessment report and the 
Environment Framework and Safeguards for Transmission Projects, and a plan for the 
handling of materials to be replaced in the rehabilitation program, satisfactory to 
the Bank, shall have been prepared.
4. 
Resettlement Action Plan in accordance with the Social Policy and Procedures, 
satisfactory to the Bank, shall have been prepared.
5. 
Procurement plan shall have been prepared in accordance with the overall 
procurement strategy, namely, bulk procurement of main items, turnkey construction by 
prequalified contractors (except for these owner-supplied main items).
