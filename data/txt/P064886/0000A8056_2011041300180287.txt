                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Progress toward the Project Development Objective meets expectations. Additional priority natural production forests are being brought under Participatory Sustainable Forest
                         Management in 5 new provinces. The area of independently certified sustainable forest management has increased and areas previously certified have been re-certified for another 5
                         years. Villagers' support for the project remains strong and livelihood improvements linked to Village Development Grants are expected in the coming months. Project activities in
