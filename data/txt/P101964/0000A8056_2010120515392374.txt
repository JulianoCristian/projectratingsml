                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The first priority in the new Commissioner's tax administration reform plan is to carry out a gap analysis of the agency's standing with regard to good international practices. The
                         exercise will also support the development of a strategy to narrow that gap through attendant changes in BIR's business processes and the attendant information technology and
                         staffing. Because this is a demanding task, the mission would like to encourage the government to set tax administration reform as a top-priority lest it be derailedby an excessive

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         Key steps ahead include: a) producing the benchmark report on the high-level KPIs for 2009; b) approving the regulation for the automatic enlisting of taxpayers into the LTS; c)
                         finalizing the review of the VAT refund system and proposing recommendations for reform; d) carrying out the gap analysis exercise. All these steps could usefully be completed
                         before the end of the calendar year. Oncethe Government has carried out the gap analysis exercise, they will put forth their proposal for realignment of NPSTAR support.
