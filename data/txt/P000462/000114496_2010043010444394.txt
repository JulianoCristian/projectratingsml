Re:  Credit No. 2162 CA
(Third Structural Adjustment Credit)
Amendment to the Development Credit Azreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the above-mentioned Development Credit Agreement between Central 
African Republic (the Borrower) and International Development Association (the 
Association), dated July
2, 1990 (the Development Credit Agreement).
Pursuant to your request by a telex dated August 9, 1990 regarding an increase 
in the amount of the retroactive financing and in the maximum amount authorized for 
petroleum products and
foodstuffs, we are pleased to inform you that the Association accedes to said request 
and agrees to amend the Development Credit Agreement between us as follows:
1.
The amount referred to in paragraph 2 (c) of Schedule I to the Development 
Credit Agreement is increased from SDR 3,500,000 to SDR 10,000,000.
2.
The amount referred to in paragraph 2 (g) of said Schedule is increased from SDR 
12,075,000 to SDR 17,250,000.
Please indicate your agreement with these amendments by signing and dating the 
enclosed duplicate original of this letter in the space provided therefore and 
returning it to us.  This amendment shall become effective upon receipt by the 
Association of the duplicate original of this letter duly confirmed.
Sincerely yours,
