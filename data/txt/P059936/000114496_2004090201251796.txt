Re: Danish Grant for the Preparation of the proposed Northern Mountains
  Poverty Reduction Project
TF021338: Amendment to Administration Agreement
We refer to the Administration Agreement dated December 17, 1998, between the 
Government of the Kingdom of Denmark (Denmark), and the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (IBRD) and the International Development Association 
(IDA) (collectively the Bank), and to the letter dated January 11, 2000, from you 
(Deputy Head of Mission, Royal Danish Embassy, Hanoi), authorizing the extension of 
closing date of the proposed Northern Mountains Poverty Reduction Project.
Accordingly, we are pleased to inform you that the Bank has amended paragraph 4 
of the Administrative Agreement to read as follows:
"It is expected that the funds will be fully utilized in accordance with 
the provisions of this Administration Agreement by December 31, 2000.  The 
Bank shall only disburse the funds for the purposes of this Administration
Agreement after such date upon the approval of Denmark."
Except as specifically amended by this amendment letter, all provisions of the 
Administration Agreement remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement to the amendment set out above by signing this 
amendment letter as set forth below.  Please return to us one fully executed original 
of the amendment letter at your earliest convenience, and retain one original of the 
amendment letter for your records.
