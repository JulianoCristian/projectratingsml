                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The overall project development objective is to increase access to, and use of, interventions for malaria prevention and treatment by the target population.

                         Board Approved Revised Project Development Objective (If project is formally restructured)

                         To increase coverage of interventions for malaria prevention and treatment and other key maternal and child health interventions.
