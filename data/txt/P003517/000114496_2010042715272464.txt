Dear Minister Wang:
Re:
Loan No. 2943-0-CHA
(Pharmaceuticals Project)
Extension of Financing of Interest and Other Charges
We refer to the Loan Agreement between the People's Republic of China (the 
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), 
dated December 22, 1988, as amended, for a Pharmaceuticals Project (the Loan 
Agreement), and to the request from the World Bank Department of the Ministry of 
Finance of the Borrower for an extension to August 14, 1992 of the latest date for 
financing of interest and other charges on the Loan.
We agree to your request. Consequently, the Bank hereby agrees to amend the date 
specified in Category (3) of the table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the 
Loan Agreement to read "August
14, 1992".
Please confirm the agreement on behalf of the Borrower to the foregoing 
amendment by signing and dating the enclosed confirmation copy of this letter and 
returning it to us for our files.
Sincerely yours,
