August 7, 1989
Minister Wang Bing-qian
Minister of Finance
Ministry of Finance
Sanlihe
Beijing, China
Dear Minister Wang:
Re:
Loan Number 2943 (CHA)
(Pharmaceutical Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
1.
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Pharmaceutical Project) between the People's 
Republic of China (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), dated December 22, 1988; to paragraph 7 of the Aide Memoire, 
dated June 2, 1989, of the Bank's supervision mission; and to the telex from the World 
Bank Department, Ministry of Finance, dated July 14, 1989, requesting amendment of 
Schedules 1 and 3 to the Loan Agreement, to revise the amount of the Loan to be used 
by the Xin Hua and Shanghai Factories.
2.
As discussed by the last supervision mission, the amount of the Loan to be 
utilized by the Xin Hua Factory should be $5,000,000 and the amount to be utilized by 
the Shanghai Factory should be
$4,500,000 . Therefore, the Bank hereby agrees to amend Schedule 1 to the Loan 
Agreement so that the amounts allocated to Categories (5), (6), (7), (8), (9) and (10) 
shall read as follows:
                                                Amount of the
                                                Loan Allocated
                                                (Expressed in
           Category                             Dollar Equivalent)
Part C.1 of the Project
(5)    Goods                                           3,900,000
(6)    Consultants' services
       and training                                      300,000
(7)    Unallocated                                       800,000
Part C.2 of the Project:
(8)    Goods                                           3,600,000
(9)    Consultants' Services
       and training                                      500,000
(10)   Unallocated                                       400,000
The "% of Expenditures Financed" for each Category shall remain unchanged.
3.
The resulting revisions to Schedule 3 (Amortization Schedule) will be reflected 
in the billing statement for the payment date of February 15, 1990. A revised 
amortization schedule will be sent to
you shortly.
4.
Please confirm the agreement on behalf of the Borrower to the amendments 
described in paragraph 2 of this letter, by signing the form of confirmation on the 
enclosed copy of this letter and returning it to us for our files.
Very truly yours,
By /s/ Zafer Ecevit
