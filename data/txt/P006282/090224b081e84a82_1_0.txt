SUPPLEMENTAL GUARANTEE AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated       t>IO) /1              , 1979, between
FEDERATIVE REPUBLIC OF BRAZIL (hereinafter called the Guarantor)
and INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (herein-
after called the Bank).
WHEREAS by an agreement (hereinafter called the Share-
holder Assumption Agreement) of even date herewith between the
Bank and Fertilizantes Fosfatados S.A. - FOSFERTIL (hereinafter
called FOSFERTIL) and Petrobras Fertilizantes S.A. - PETROFERTIL
(hereinafter called PETROFERTIL) and Insumos Basicos S.A. Finan-
ciamento. e Participaq5es - FIBASE (hereinafter called FIBASE)
and Petroleo Brasileiro S.A. - PETROBRAS (hereinafter called
PETROBRAS) and Banco Nacional do Densenvolvimento Economico
(hereinafter called BNDE), the Bank recognizes the succession of
FOSFERTIL to the rights and obligations of Companhia Vale do Rio
Doce (hereinafter called CVRD) under the Shareholder Agreement,
-dated April 29, 1977, between the Bank and CVRD, on the conditions
set forth in the Shareholder Assumption Agreement, but only on
condition that the Guarantor agrees to confirm, maintain and
supplement its guarantee undertaken in the Guarantee Agreement,
dated April 29, 1977, between the Guarantor and the Bank (herein-
after called the Guarantee Agreement);
WHEREAS the Guarantor, in consideration of the Bank's enter-
ing into the Shareholder Assumption Agreement, has agreed to
confirm, maintain and supplement such guarantee;
NOW THEREFORE it is hereby agreed as follows:
Section 1. For the purposes of the Guarantee Agreement,
the Guarantor hereby approves the Shareholder Assumption Agreement
and agrees and confirms that the Guarantee Agreement, as supple-
mented herein, shall remain in full force and effect.
Section 2. The Guarantor shall cause FOSFERTIL, PETROFERTIL,
FIBASE, PETROBRAS and BNDE to perform, punctually, all of their
respective obligations under the Shareholder Assumption Agreement.
Section 3. The Supplemental Guarantee Agreement shall come
into force and effect on the date on which the Shareholder Assump-
tion Agreement becomes effective.
