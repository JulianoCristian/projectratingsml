﻿LOAN NUMBER 1411 BR
SECOND SHAREHOLDER ASSUMPTION AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated March 25, 1981, between INTERNATIONAL BANK
FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (hereinafter called the Bank)
and, on the other side, PETROBRAS FERTILIZANTES S.A. (herein-
after called PETROFERTIL) and INSUMOS BASICOS S.A. FINANCIAMENTO E
PARTICIPAg0ES - FIBASE (hereinafter called FIBASE) and
COMPANHIA VALE DO RIO DOCE S.A. (hereinafter called CVRD) and
PETROLEO BRASILEIRO S.A. (hereinafter called PETROBRAS) and BANCO
NACIONAL DO DESENVOLVIMENTO ECONOMICO (hereinafter called BNDE)
(hereinafter sometimes individually called "Shareholder" and
collectively called "the Shareholders").
WHEREAS by a loan agreement (hereinafter called the Loan
Agreement), dated April 29, 1977, between the Bank and FERTILI-
ZANTES VALE DO RIO GRANDE S.A. - VALEFERTIL (hereinafter called
VALEFERTIL), the Bank has made a loan (hereinafter called the
Loan) to VALEFERTIL in an amount in various currencies equivalent
to eighty-two million dollars (US$82,000,000), on the terms and
conditions set forth in the Loan Agreement, and the Loan Agreement
has been amended to reflect, inter alia, the sale of the shares of
VALEFERTIL by CVRD to FERTILIZANTES FOSFATADOS S.A. - FOSFERTIL
(hereinafter called FOSFERTIL);
WHEREAS by a guarantee agreement (hereinafter called the
Guarantee Agreement), dated April 29, 1977, between the Federative
Republic of Brazil (hereinafter called the Guarantor) and the
Bank, the Guarantor guaranteed the obligations of VALEFERTIL
in the Loan Agreement contained, on the terms and conditions set
forth in the Guarantee Agreement, and by agreement between the
Guarantor and the Bank (hereinafter called the Supplemental Gua-
rantee Agreement) dated August 17, 1979, the Guarantor confirmed
its obligations under the Guarantee Agreement and undertook
certain additional obligations to the Bank;
WHEREAS by a shareholder agreement (hereinafter called
the Shareholder Agreement), dated April 29, 1977, between the
Bank and CVRD, CVRD undertook the obligations in the Shareholder
Agreement contained, and by a shareholder assumption agreement
(hereinafter called the Shareholder Assumption Agreement) dated
August 17, 1979, between the Bank and PETROFERTIL, FOSFERTIL,
FIBASE, PETROBRAS and BNDE (hereinafter sometimes collectively
called "the Former Shareholders"), the Former Shareholders
assumed, as shareholders of VALEFERTIL, the obligations of CVRD
under the Shareholder Agreement with respect to VALEFERTIL;

WHEREAS CVRD, PETROFERTIL and FIBASE own approximately 99%
of the shares in the capital of FOSFERTIL;
WHEREAS VALEFERTIL and MINERAgAO VALE DO PARANAIBA S.A.
- VALEP (hereinafter called VALEP) have merged into FOSFERTIL,
and FOSFERTIL, PETROFERTIL, FIBASE, CVRD, PETROBRAS, BNDE and the
Guarantor have requested the Bank to approve such merger and to
agree to the undertaking by FOSFERTIL of all of VALEFERTIL's
convenants, agreements and obligations in the Loan Agreement, as
amended, contained;
WHEREAS the Bank has agreed to such request on condition
that:
(a) PETROFERTIL, FIBASE, PETROBRAS and BNDE agree to con-
firm their obligations in the Shareholder Assumption Agreement
contained;
(b) CVRD agree to undertake certain obligations to the
Bank;
(c) the Guarantor agree to confirm, maintain and supplement
its guarantee undertaken in the Guarantee Agreement and in the
Supplemental Guarantee Agreement;
WHEREAS the Guarantor has agreed to confirm its obligations
in the Guarantee Agreement and Supplemental Guarantee Agreement
contained and to undertake certain additional obligations to the
Bank under a Second Supplemental Guarantee Agreement of even date
herewith between the Guarantor and the Bank;
WHEREAS upon the basis, inter alia, of the foregoing the Bank
and the Shareholders have agreed to enter into this Agreement upon
the terms and conditions hereinafter set forth;
NOW THEREFORE, it is hereby agreed as follows:
Article I
Section 1.01. Wherever used in this Agreement, unless the
context requires otherwise, the several terms defined in the
General Conditions Applicable to Loan and Guarantee Agreements of
the Bank, dated October 27, 1980 and in the Loan Agreement have
the respective meanings therein set forth.


﻿ARTICLE II
Section 2.01. The Shareholders hereby approve the Loan
Assumption Agreement of even date herewith between the Bank and
FOSFERTIL (hereinafter called the Loan Assumption Agreement) and
assume, and agree to carry out and fulfill, all of the covenants,
agreements and obligations of the Former Shareholders in the
Shareholder Assumption Agreement contained, and the Bank hereby
recognizes the succession of the Shareholders to the rights of the
Former Shareholders under the Shareholder Assumption Agreement,
all with the same effect as though the Shareholders were substi-
tuted for th, Former Shareholders as a party to the Shareholder
Assumption Agreement.
Section 2.02. The Shareholders acknowledge that all action
heretofore taken under the Shareholder Assumption Agreement by the
Former Shareholders shall be valid and binding on the Shareholders
as though the Shareholders were the Former Shareholders and had
taken such action and that, expressly as provided in this Agree-
ment, the coming into effect of this Agreement shall not affect
any right acquired or obligation incurred or the consequences of
action taken or omitted to be taken by the Bank or the Former
Shareholders under the Shareholder Assumption Agreement.
Section 2.03. The obligations of each Shareholder under this
Article shall be independent of each other and no Shareholder
shall be entitled to deny or delay performance of its obligations
on the grounds of non-performance by any or all of the other
Shareholders.
ARTICLE III
Section 3.01. (a) PETROFERTIL, FIBASE and CVRD shall exercise
every power, right and recourse available to them to cause
FOSFERTIL to perform all of the obligations assumed by FOSFERTIL
under the Loan Assumption Agreement, and shall not take, and shall
use its best efforts to prevent others from taking, any action
which would prevent or interfere with such performance.
(b) PETROBRAS and BNDG shall cause PETROFERTIL and FIBASE,
respectively, to perform all of their obligations under paragraph
(a) of this Section.
(c) The Bank and PETROBRAS, BNDE, PETROFERTIL, FIBASE and
CVRD shall cooperate fully to assure that the purposes of the Loan

will be accomplished. To that end, the Bank, on one side, and
PETROBRAS, BNDE, PETROFERTIL, FIBASE and CVRD, on the other side,
shall: (i) from time to time, at the request of any of them,
furnish to the other party all such information as such party
shall reasonably request; and (ii) inform each other of any
condition which interferes with, or threatens to interfere with,
the progress of the Project, the accomplishment of the purposes
of the Loan, or the performance of the obligations referred to in
paragraph (a) thereof.
(d) The obligations of PETROBRAS, PETROFERTIL, FIBASE, BNDE
and CVRD under this Section shall be independent of each other and
neither PETROBRAS nor 4NDE nor CVRD nor PETROFERTIL nor FIBASE
shall be entitled to deny or delay performance of its obligations
on the grounds of non-performance by the other.
ARTICLE IV
Effectiveness
Section 4.01. This Agreement shall come into force and effect
on the date upon which the Loan Assumption Agreement becomes
effective.
