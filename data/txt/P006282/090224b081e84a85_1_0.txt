LOAN ASSUMPTION AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated March 25, 1981, between INTERNATIONAL BANK
FOR RECONSTRUe;TION AND DEVELOPMENT (hereinafter called the Bank)
and FERTILIZANTES FOSFATADOS S.A. - FOSFERTIL (hereinafter called
FOSFERTIL).
WHEREAS by a loan agreement (hereinafter called the Loan
Agreement), dated April 29, 1977, between the Bank and
FERTILIZANTES VALE DO RIO GRANDE S.A. - VALEFERTIL (hereinafter
called VALEFERTIL), as amended, the Bank made a loan (hereinafter
called the Loan) to VALEFERTIL in an amount in various currencies
equivalent to eighty-two million dollars (US$82,000,000) on the
terms and conditions set forth in the Loan Agreement, and the Loan
Agreement has been amended by agreement dated August 17, 1979
between the Bank and VALEFERTIL in order to reflect, inter alia,
the sale of shares of VALEFERTIL by COMPANHIA VALE DO RIO DOCE
S.A. (hereinafter called CVRD) to FOSFERTIL;
WHEREAS by a guarantee agreement (hereinafter called the
Guarantee Agreement), dated April 29, 1977, between the Federative
Republic of Brazil (hereinafter called the Guarantor) and the
Bank, the Guarantor guaranteed the obligations of VALEFERTIL
in the Loan Agreement contained, on the terms and conditions set
forth in the Guarantee Agreement, and by an agreement between the
Guarantor and the Bank, dated August 17, 1979 (hereinafter called
the Supplemental Guarantee Agreement), the Guarantor confirmed its
obligations under the Guarantee Agreement and undertook certain
additional obligations to the Bank;
WHEREAS by a shareholder agreement (hereinafter called the
Shareholder Agreement), dated April 29, 1977, between the Bank and
CVRD, CVRD undertook the obligations in the Shareholder Agreement
contained, and by a shareholder assumption agreement (hereinafter
called the Shareholder Assumption Agreement), dated August 17,
1979, between the Bank and FOSFERTIL and PETROBRAS FERTILIZANTES
S.A. (hereinafter called PETROFERTIL) and INSUMOS BASICOS S.A.
FINANCIAMENTO E PARTICIPAg0ES - FIBASE (hereinafter called
FIBASE) and PETROLEO BRASILERO S.A. (hereinafter called PETROBRAS)
and BANCO NACIONAL DE DESENVOLVIMENTO ECONOMICO (hereinafter
called BNDE), the latter assumed, as shareholders of VALEFERTIL,
the obligations of CVRD to the Batik under the Shareholder Agree-
ment with respect to VALEFERTIL;

WHEREAS VALEFERTIL and MINERAgAO VALE DO PARAMAOBA S.A. -
VALEP (hereinafter called VALEP) have merged into FOSFERTIL, and
FOSFERTIL, PETROFERTIL, FIBASE, CVRD, PETROBAS, BNDE and the
Guarantor have requested the Bank to approve such merger and to
agree to the undertaking by FOSFERTIL of all of VALEFERTIL's
covenants, agreements and obligations in the Loan Agreement
contained and, for that purpose, to enter into this Agreement and
into the agreements described below with PETROFERTIL, FIBASE,
CVRD, PETROBRAS, BNDE, and the Guarantor;
WHEREAS CVRD, PETROFERTIL and FIBASE own approximately 99% of
the shares in the capital of FOSFERTIL;
WHEREAS PETROFERTIL and FIBASE, PETROBRAS and BNDE have
agreed to confirm, mutatis mutandi, their obligations in the
Shareholder Assumption Agreement contained and to undertake
certain additional obligations to the Bank in respect of FOSFERTIL
in a new Shareholder Assumption Agreement (hereinafter called the
Second Shareholder Assumption Agreement) of even date herewith;
WHEREAS CVRD has agreed to undertake certain obligations to
the Bank in the Second Shareholder Assumption Agreement;
WHEREAS the Guarantor has agreed to confirm its obligations
in the Guarantee Agreement and in the Supplemental Guarantee
Agreement and to undertake certain additional obligations to the
Bank in a new supplemental guarantee agreement (hereinafter called
the Second Supplemental Guarantee Agreement) of even date
herewith;
WHEREAS upon the basis, inter alia, of the foregoing the Bank
and FOSFERTIL have agreed to enter into this Agreement (herein-
after called the Loan Assumption Agreement) upon the terms and
condition3 hereinafter set forth;
NOW THEREFORE, it is hereby agreed as follows:
ARTICLE I
Definitions
Section 1.01. Except as otherwise provided in Section 3.01 of
this Agreement, where used in this Agreement, unless the context
requires otherwise, the several terms defined in the General
Conditions Applicable to Loan and Guarantee Agreements of the

Bank, dated October 27, 1980, and in the Loan Agreement have the
respective meanings therein set forth.
ARTICLE II
Particular Covenants of FOSFERTIL
Section 2.01. FOSFERTIL hereby assumes, and agrees to
carry out and fulfill, all of the covenants, agreements and
obligations of VALEFERTIL in the Loan Agreement, as amended,
contained, and the Bank hereby recognizes the succession of
FOSFERTIL to the rights of VALEFERTIL uader the Loan Agreement,
all with the same effect as though FOSFERTIL were substituted for
VALEFERTIL as a party to and Borrower under the Loan Agreement.
Section 2.02. All action taken under the Loan Agreement by
VALEFERTIL shall be valid and binding on FOSFERTIL as though
FOSFERTIL were VALEFERTIL and the coming into effect of this
Agreement shall not affect any right acquired or obligation
incurred or the consequences of action taken or omitted to be
taken by the Bank or VALEFERTIL under the Loan Agreement,

ARTICLE IV
Effective Date
Section 4.01. (a) The Loan Assumption Agreement and the
amendments to the Loan Agreement provided in Article III thereof
shall become effective on the date upon which the Bank dispatches
to PETROFERTIL, FIBASE, CVRD, PETROBRAS, BNDE and the Guarantor
notice of its acceptance of evidence that: (i) the Loan Assumption
Agreement has been registered with Banco Central do Brasil; and
(ii) the execution and delivery of the Loan Assumption Agreement,
the Second Shareholder Assumption Agreement and the Second Supple-
mental Guarantee Agreement on behalf of the parties thereto have
been duly authorized or ratified by all necessary governmental and
corporate action.
(b) As part of such evidence, there shall be furnished to
the Bank an opinion or opinions satisfactory to the Bank of
counsel acceptable to the Bank showing: (i) on behalf of
FOSFERTIL, that the Loan Assumption Agreement has been duly
authorized or ratified, and executed and delivered on behalf of,
FOSFERTIL and is legally binding upon FOSFERTIL in accordance with
its terms; (ii) on behalf of FIBASE, CVRD, BNDE, PETROBRAS and
PETROFER'IL, that the Second Shareholder Assumption Agreement has
been duly authorized or ratified by, and executed and delivered on
behalf of, and is legally binding upon, FIBASE, CVRD, BNDE,
PETROBRAS and PETROFERTIL, respectively, in accordance with its
terms; and (iii) on behalf of the Guarantor, that the Second
Supplemental Guarantee Agreement has been duly authorized or
ratified by and delivered on behalf of, the Guarantor and is
legally binding upon the Guarantor in accordance with its
terms.
