                  Credit No. 2278 GH (Community Secondary Schools

                  Amendment of Development Credit Agreement
Dear Sir,
      We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Community
Secondary Schools Construction Project), dated July 8, 1991, between
the Republic of Ghana (the Borrower) and the International
Development Association (the Association).  Further to the
discussions held with the representatives of the Borrower, we are
pleased to inform you that the Association hereby agrees to the
following amendments to the above-referenced Development Credit
Agreement:
      a.    The date of December 31, 1994 is hereby established for
purposes of Section 2.03.
      b.    Section 3.04(b)(ii) is amended by replacing the
reference to "at least 33 percent" with "at least 10 percent".
      c.    Category (1) of the table set out in paragraph 1 of
Schedule I is amended by replacing the figure of "67%" in the third
column with "90%".
      Please indicate the agreement of the Borrower with the
foregoing by countersigning and dating the form of confirmation on
the two original copies of this letter and returning one original,
as countersigned, to us.  This amendment shall become effective as
of the date of receipt by the Association of this letter duly
countersigned on behalf of the Borrower.
                         Sincerely yours,
                           Joanne Salop
                          Acting Director
                     Western Africa Department
