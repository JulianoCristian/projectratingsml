AGREEMENT AMENDING PROJECT AGREEMENT 
 
AGREEMENT, dated July 21, 2004 between INTERNATIONAL 
DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (the Association) and 
FONDS D’INTERVENTION 
POUR LE DEVELOPPEMENT (FID). 
 
WHEREAS (A) the REPUBLIC OF MADAGASCAR (the Borrower) and the 
Association have entered into a Development Credit Agreement (Community 
Development Project - Credit Number 3498 MAG), dated May 22, 2001 (the 
Development Credit Agreement), for the purpose of providing support during the 
execution of the Community Development Project, as described in Recitals of the 
Development Credit Agreement (the Project); 
 
(B) 
the Borrower has agreed to make the proceeds of the Credit available to 
FID and entered into a Convention (the Convention) with FID for that purpose; 
 
(C) 
the Association and FID have entered into a Project Agreement, dated 
May 22, 2001 (the Project Agreement), for the purpose of the carrying out the Project; 
 
(D) 
the Development Credit Agreement has been amended on November 9, 
2002, and on April 30, 2004; 
 
(E) 
the Borrower has requested the Association to provide further additional 
assistance in the support of the Project by increasing the amount made available under the 
Development Credit Agreement by an amount in various currencies equivalent to thirty-
four million two hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 34,200,000); and 
 
WHEREAS the Association has agreed, on the basis, inter alia, of the foregoing, 
to provide such additional assistance to the Borrower provided that an agreement 
amending the Project Agreement (the Agreement Amending the Project Agreement) upon 
the terms and conditions set forth hereafter be executed; 
 
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:  
ARTICLE I 
Amendments to the Project Agreement 
  Section 1.01. Recital (A) of the Project Agreement is amended to read as follows:

(A)

agreed to make available to the Borrower an amount in various currencies

(d)

ARTICLE II 
Effective Date; Termination 
 
Section 2.01. This Amending Agreement shall come into force and effect on the 
date upon which the Agreement Amending the Development Credit Agreement becomes 
effective.
