Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to your request dated June 8, 1999, to amend the Loan Agreement so as 
to increase the disbursement percentage for Civil Works under Category 1 of Schedule 1 
of said Loan Agreement in order to allow for the timely completion of the Project 
objectives under Part A (1) of the Project.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank has acceded to the above request and, 
therefore, proposes the following amendments:

2.
In Schedule 2 of the Loan Agreement (Description of the Project), Part A (1) is 
amended to read as follows: 
“Part A:     Primary Health Care
Construction, equipping, furnishing and staffing of 623 RHCs,
including 28 with maternity facilities, in the Project Area in the following sequence:
(a)
construction of RHCs in the regions of South Khorasan; Khuzestan; Sistan 
Balouchistan; Hormozgan; Fars and Kerman;
(b) 
construction of RHCs in the regions of Zanjan, Ghazwin, West Azarbaidjan, 
East Azerbaidjan, Ardabil, Hamadan, and Kordestan; and
(c) 
construction of RHCs in the regions of North Khorasan, Tehran, Lorestan, 
Golestan and Mezadaran.”
 All the terms and conditions of the Loan Agreement, as amended hereby, shall 
remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm your official agreement to the foregoing, as the Borrower’s 
authorized representative under the Agreement, by duly signing and dating the 
corresponding form of confirmation below and returning this amendment letter to us at 
your earliest convenience. A signed duplicate original of this amendment letter is 
enclosed herewith for the Islamic Republic of Iran’s official records.
