                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project was approved by the RVP on January 31, 2011. The First Grand Funding Request was approved on April 18, 2011, and the Grant Agreement became effective on June
                         14, 2011. Project implementation has taken off since then and it is likely that all planned activities will be completed by end of project closing, and that PDO will achieved. Component
                         I (13% of the grant proceeds) has significantly progressed. Contracts for animal restocking and construction of villages lean banks are under execution and will be totally completed
                         by July 2012.
