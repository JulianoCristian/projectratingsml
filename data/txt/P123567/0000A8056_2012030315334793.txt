                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project was approved by the RVP on January 31, 2011. The First Grand Funding Request was approved on April 18, 2011, and the Grant Agreement became effective on June
                         14, 2011. Project implementation has taken off since then and it is likely that all planned activities will be completed by end of project closing, and that PDO will achieved. Component
                         I (13% of the grant proceeds) is fully committed and contracts for animal restocking and construction of villages lean banks are planned to be executed and completed by July 2012.
                         The Procurement process and environmental and social impact assessment reports for the rehabilitation of irrigations schemes (Component 2- 74% of the grants proceeds) have
