                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This ISR represents findings of the final supervision mission before the project closing on January 31, 2014. All targets for various project activities were either achieved or
                         surpassed. The project would be closing by the designated date. Preparation of the follow up project, the Sindh Irrigated Agriculture Productivity Enhancement Project is under
                         advance stage.
