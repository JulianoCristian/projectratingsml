Re: Guatemala: Loan No. 7374-GU (Rural Economic Development Project)
Third Amendment to Loan Agreement
Dear Sir,
We refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development (the "Bank") and the Republic of Guatemala (the "Borrower") dated October 22,
2007, restated on April 14, 2009, and amended on June 30, 2011, with respect to the above-
captioned Project ("Loan Agreement"). Please note that capitalized terms used in this letter
("Amendment Letter") and not defined herein have the meaning ascribed to them in the Loan
Agreement.
Pursuant to your letter dated August 20, 2012, wherein a request is made to amend the
Loan Agreement, we are pleased to inform you that the Bank proposes to amend the Loan
Agreement as follows:
(1)    Part 1.3 of Schedule I to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as follows:
"the carrying out by FONAPAZ of public rural infrastructure investments"
(2)    Section 1.1.(a)(iv)(iii) of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as
follows:
"(iii) the Investment Subprojects under Part 1.1;"
(3)    Section I.4.(b) of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as
follows:
"MINECO enter into agreements with the Beneficiaries wherein the modalities for the
implementation of the Investment Subprojects shall be specified, all in accordance with
the provisions set forth in the OM; and"
(4)    Section I.5.(a) of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as
follows:


﻿"(a) ensure that the Investment Subprojects carried out under MINECO are identified,
appraised, approved, implemented, managed and evaluated in accordance with the
procedures set forth or referred to in the OM;"

﻿(6)    Section IV.B.2. of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as
follows:
"2. The Closing Date is July 31 2014."
(7)    The following definitions on Section I of the Appendix to the Loan Agreement are hereby
amended to read in their entirety as follows:
(a) ""Beneficiary" means either: (i) a municipality, for Investment Subprojects under Part
1.1 of the project exclusively; or (ii) a cooperative, producer organization, trade
intermediary, association or private firm, which all meet the selection criteria, set
forth in the OM."
(b) ""FONAPAZ" means Fondo Nacional para la Paz, the Borrower's Acuerdo
Gubernativo No. 408-91 dated June 28, 1991, and Acuerdo Gubernativo No. 244-92
dated April 13, 1992, as such accords have been amended as of the date to this
Agreement, or any successor thereto, acceptable to the Bank."
(c) ""Investment Subproject" means:
(i) either a specific investment in collective or local public productive
infrastructure; and/or
(ii) any technical assistance in relation to a Subproject, including the costs
associated with legal incorporation of the Beneficiary, and prepared pursuant to
Part 1.1 of the Project."
(d) "Operating Costs" means expenditures which would not have been incurred absent
the Project (other than for Consultant Services) which, subject to the no-objection of
the Bank, are incurred by SEGEPLAN and the Co-Executing Agencies in connection
with the carrying out of their functions and responsibilities under the Project,
including: transportation costs, utilities, office supplies and rent, travel and per diem,
communications, public announcements, management and technical meeting costs
and attendance fees, small maintenance works and vehicle insurance. Operating
costs under Part 1.1 also include costs of works' design, and supervision, monitoring,
operation and management of Investment Subprojects under Part 1.1 of the Project.
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing by countersigning and dating the form
of confirmation on the two original copies of this letter provided to you and returning one
countersigned original to us. Thereafter, all the provisions of the Loan Agreement, except as
amended through this Amendment Letter, shall remain in full force and effect.
The provisions of this Amendment Letter will become effective as of the date of this
letter upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this Amendment Letter (Third
Amendment Effective Date).
Please also note that the restructuring paper dated March 29, 2013 will be disclosed on
the Bank's external website.
