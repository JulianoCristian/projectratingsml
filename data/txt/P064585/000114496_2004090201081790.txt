Re:
Credit No. 3259 KG (Second Rural Finance Project)
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
Your Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement between the Kyrgyz Republic and the 
International Development Association for the above-mentioned Project dated October 1, 
1999,  (the Credit Agreement), and to the letter of the Deputy Minister of Finance of 
the Kyrgyz Republic Mr. Moldokulov dated August 30, 2001, requesting amendment to the 
Credit Agreement.
We hereby agree to amend the Credit Agreement as follows:
In the table in paragraph 1 of Part A of Schedule 1 the percentage of expenditures to 
be financed under Category (2) Sub-loans is amended to read: “100% of amounts 
disbursed by PFIs on or before June 30, 2003, and 80% of amounts thereafter”.
Please confirm your agreement on behalf of the Kyrgyz Republic with the above 
amendment by signing, dating and returning to us one of the original copies of this 
Amendment Letter. The amendment will become effective as of the date of your 
countersignature, upon the receipt of your countersigned letter.
Sincerely,
