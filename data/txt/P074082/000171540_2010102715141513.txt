Amendment to the Administration Agreement between the Minister of 
Foreign Affairs of Ireland, as represented by Irish Aid, and the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development and the 
International Development Association concerning the Trust Fund for 
Financing Improvements in Education in Africa (TF028942) 
 
Dear Mr. O’Brien: 
 
We refer to the above-referenced Administration Agreement between Irish Aid and the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development and the International Development 
Association (collectively, the “Bank”), dated November 7, 2001, as amended to date (the 
“Agreement”).  We also refer to an email from Liz Higgins of Irish Aid, dated July 23, 2010, 
agreeing to extend the Closing Date of the above mentioned trust fund.  
 
Accordingly, we propose to amend paragraph 6 of the Agreement as follows: 
 
“6. 
It is expected that the Contribution will be fully utilized in accordance 
with the provisions of this Agreement by December 31, 2011. 
The Bank shall only 
disburse Contribution funds for the purposes of this Agreement after such date with the 
written approval of the Donor.” 
 
All other provisions of the Agreement, as amended shall remain unchanged.
