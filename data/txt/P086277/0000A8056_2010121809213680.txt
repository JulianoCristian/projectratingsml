                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The revised Project Development Objectives are to: (i) upgrade and rehabilitate the secondary and local roads network; and (ii) increase RDMRDI's and local
                         governments' capacity to manage the road network in a cost effective and sustainable manner.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The implementation progress of the project has been downgraded from Satisfactory to Moderately Satisfactory to reflect the issues with land take, ongoing works for one of the
                         sections which is in the process of tendering, and moderately satisfactory overall safeguard compliance. The project disbursement remains on track and there is overall improvement
                         of quality of road works and design quality. The ongoing contracts are progressing as scheduled and they should be completed within the contractual time period
