                           SUMMARY OF DISCUSSION*

                          Meeting of the Executive Directors
                                 December 9, 2010


The Executive Directors approved the Fourth Economic Reform Support Grant (R2010-
0346/1, November 18, 2010) to the Republic of Burundi in the amount of SDR 16.1
million (US$25 million equivalent) on the payment terms and conditions set out in the
President's Memorandum.

Directors expressed support for the core areas of the ERSG ­ public finance management
through improvements in budget credibility, planning and controls; private sector
development through promotion of private investment, facilitation of public enterprises
reform and modernization of export sectors ­ critical for achieving the objectives of the
PRSP, and commended the Government's commitment and efforts to achieving them.
Directors underscored the critical need to improve good governance, peace and security
in order to sustain reform implementation and flagged continued concerns on corruption.
Directors also expressed the need for enhanced coordination and harmonization among
development partners in assisting the authorities in implementing their reform program.
In this regard, they noted Norway's intention to support this operation.
