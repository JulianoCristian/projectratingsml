            Re:  Credit No.2093-MAU
                 (Agricultural Sector Adjustment and Investment
                 Project)
                 Amendments to the Development Credit Agreement
                 and Reallocation of Funds
Excellency,
      We refer to the above-mentioned Credit Agreement between the
Islamic Republic of Mauritania (the Borrower) and the International
Development Association (the Association) dated February 26, 1990
and your letter No. 00551 dated August 23, 1992, requesting the
Association's assistance for the extension of the Project area, and
requesting the Association's assistance in the reallocation of
funds.
      We are pleased to confirm the Association's willingness to
provide this assistance and, therefore, agree to amend the
Development Credit Agreement as follows:
1.    Section 1.02 (c) of the Development Credit Agreement is
amended to read:
      "Project Area" means the Foum Gleita irrigated area, the flood
recession cropping areas around the Foum Gleita reservoir, and the
public smallholder irrigated schemes in the Senegal River Valley,
administered by SONADER and situated to the east of the District
("Wilaya") of Trarza."
2.    The provision of funds and the percentage of expenditures to
be financed according to Schedule I of the Development Credit
Agreement shall be changed by:
1)    a transfer of SDR 100,000 from Category (4), Technical
Assistance, Studies and Training (a) for MDR to Category (5),
Incremental Operating Costs, (a) for MDR; and
2)    the percentage of disbursement: Category (4) for (a) MDR, (b)
SONADER, (c) UBD shall be augmented from 50% to 100%.
      Therefore, Schedule 1 of the Development Credit Agreement
shall read:
