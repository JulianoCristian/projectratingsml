La Paz - Bolivia
Re:
Economic Management Strengthening Operation Project
Credit No. 1977-BO
Dear Mr. Minister:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated January 4, 1989 
(hereinafter the "Agreement") executed by and between the International Development 
Association and the Republic of Bolivia for the above-referenced project.
The purpose of this letter is to amend certain provisions of the Agreement, as 
follows:
1.
The typographical error in the fourth line of Section 2.01 of the Agreement is 
hereby corrected and said line is amended to read "million seven hundred thousand 
dollars ($9,700,000)".
2.
The dollar sign ($) appearing before the total amount of the Credit at the 
bottom of the table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement is deleted 
and said amount is amended to read "7,200,000".
3.
Paragraph 4 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement is hereby amended to read as follows:
"4. Notwithstanding the allocation of an amount of the Credit and of the 
Swiss Contribution, or the disbursement percentages set forth in the table in 
paragraph 1 above, the disbursement percentage for Category (4) shall be 
reduced to: (a) 90% once the aggregate amount of disbursements made under such 
Category shall have reached the amount of SDR 1,000,000; and (b) 70% once the 
aggregate amount of disbursements made under such Category shall have reached 
the 
amount of SDR 1,300,000."
4.
The footnote appearing below Section 6.05 of the Agreement, which footnote reads 
"90 days after the date of this Agreement", is hereby deleted.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing and duly dating the 
enclosed duplicate of this amendment letter in the space below and returning that 
duplicate to us.
This amendment letter will become effective on the date the Agreement becomes 
effective.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT.ASSOCIATION
By /s/ Gerald F. Flood, Jr.
Acting Director
Country Department III
Latin America and the Caribbean
