Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Balqar:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement, dated June 28, 1994, between the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the “Bank”) and Jordan 
Telecomunications Corporation (the "Loan Agreement").  Pursuant to the incorporation 
of the Jordan Telecommunications Company, and its assumption of the obligations of 
Jordan Telecommunications Corporation, we hereby agree to amend the Loan Agreement as 
follows:
1.
The first clause is changed to read as follows: 
“AGREEMENT, dated June 28, 1994, between INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR 
RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (the Bank) and JORDAN TELECOMMUNICATIONS 
COMPANY (the 
Borrower).
2.
Paragraph (a) of Section 1.02 is amended to read as follows:
“(a)
‘Articles of Association’ means the Articles of Association 
of the Borrower pursuant to The Companies Law No. 1 of 1989, dated 
October 8, 
1996.”
3.
Paragraph (b) of Section 1.02 is amended to read as follows:
“‘By-laws’ means the Wireless Telegraphy By-law of 1966; By-law 
Regulating Telex Services No. 16 of 1975; By-law Regulating the 
Relationship 
between Ministries and Public Bodies No. 16 of 1988; Civil 
Service By-law of 1988; 
By-law Regulating Telephone Services No. 61 of 
1992, By-laws Concerning 
Employees, Supply and Works and Finances of 
1997, as amended to the date of this 
Agreement;”
4.
In Section 8.02, the address for the Borrower is changed to read: "Director 
General Jordan Telecommunications Company Post Office Box 1689 Amman The Hashemite 
Kingdom of Jordan”
5.
The signature line on page 16 is changed to read: “JORDAN TELECOMMUNICATIONS 
COMPANY”.
6.
Sub-paragraph 1(c) of Part A of Schedule 2 is amended to read as follows:
“(c) register the Jordan Telecommunications Corporation under the 
Company Law of the Guarantor; and”
7.
This amendment will enter into effect on the date that the Bank issues its 
written notice to Bankers Trust under Clause 14(c) of the Trust Deed.
Please confirm your agreement to the amendment by signing and dating the form of 
confirmation on the enclosed copy of this letter and returning the same to us.
Very truly yours,
