               Re:  Loan No. 3738 JO
                    (Telecommunications Project)
                    Amendment to the Loan and Guarantee Agreements
Excellency:
   Please refer to the Loan Agreement, dated June 28, 1994,
between the International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development (the "Bank") and the Jordan Telecommunications
Corporation (the "Loan Agreement"), and the Guarantee
Agreement, dated June 28, 1994, between the Bank and the
Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan (the "Guarantee Agreement").
Pursuant to your letter dated August 19, 1995, we hereby agree
to amend the Loan Agreement and Guarantee Agreement as follows:
Loan Agreement:
1.      In Section 1.02, a new paragraph (g) is added to read:
   "(g) 'EXIMBANK Loan' means the loan facility made available
   by the Export-Import Bank of Japan to the Guarantor.
2.      Section 6.01(d)(i)(A) is amended to read as follows:
   "the right of the Borrower or the Guarantor to withdraw the
   proceeds of any loan (including, without limitation, the
   EXIMBANK Loan) or grant made to the Borrower or the Guarantor
   for the financing of the Project shall have been suspended,
   canceled or terminated in whole or in part, pursuant to the
   terms thereof, or"
3.      The date in the covenant contained in Paragraph 2 of
Schedule 5 of the Loan Agreement for review with the Bank of
the findings and recommendations of the tariff study has been
changed to August 31, 1995, and the date for commencement of
the program to restructure and rebalance telecommunications
tariffs has been changed to November 30, 1995.
Guarantee Agreement:
1.      The date in the covenant contained in Section 3.02 of
the Guarantee Agreement for provision to the Bank of proposed
regulations for the telecommunications sector is changed to
February 29, 1996.
2.      The date in the covenant contained in Section 3.03 of
the Guarantee Agreement for registration of the Borrower under
Article 8 of the Company Law is changed to February 29, 1996.
   Please confirm your agreement to the amendment by signing and
dating the form of confirmation on the enclosed copy of this
letter and returning the same to us.
