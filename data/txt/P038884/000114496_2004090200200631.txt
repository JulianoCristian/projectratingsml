Re: BRAZIL: Ceará Rural Poverty Alleviation Project - Loan 3918-BR 
Amendement to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement for Loan 3918-BR, dated January 27, 1996 
between the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the 
State of Ceará (the Borrower).
In repsonse to your April 10, 1997 fax requesting the inclusion in the Loan 
Agremeent of studies, training and consultant services for the purposes of the state 
reform program and to undertake competitiveness studies, we are pleased to inform you 
that the Bank hereby proposes to amend the Loan Agreement to add and Itwem 5 to Part B 
of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agrement, as follows:
“5. Provision of technical assistance to support the reform of the 
States’s 
administrative, institutional and financial sectors, and to 
undertake competitiveness 
studies for the Borrower and the Guarantor’s 
northeast region.”
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall 
be an original, and shall become effective on the date of this Amendment Letter once 
the Bank has received one fully executed original of this Amendment Letter.  Please 
confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing the two counterparts of 
this Amendment Letter.  Upon your agreement, please return one fully executed original 
to us.
Sincerely,
By /s/ Paul Meo
Acting Director
