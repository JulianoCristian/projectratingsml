Please refer to the Loan Agreement for Loan 3918-BR, dated February 27, 1996 
between the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the 
State of Ceará (the Borrower).
We have received your letter dated May 14, 1998, requesting  an increase in the 
Bank’s participation in project financing from 60% to 75%.  We are pleased to concur 
with the request.  Therefore the attached table substitutes Table 1 in paragraph 1 of 
Schedule 1 of the Loan Agreement, as follows: 
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts, each of which shall 
be an original, and shall become effective on the date of this Amendment Letter once 
the Bank has received one fully executed original of this Amendment Letter.  Please 
confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing the two counterparts of 
the Amendment Letter.  Upon your agreement, please return one fully executed original 
to us.
