                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The overall development objective of the Project is to contribute to Government's National Plan which aims to minimize the threat posed to humans and the
                         poultry sectors by highly pathogenic avian influenza (HPAI) infection and other zoonoses in Lao PDR, and to prepare for, control, and respond to influenza
                         pandemics and other infectious disease emergencies in humans.
