    El Salvador's Disaster Risk Management Development Policy Loan with a Catastrophe
                           Deferred Drawdown Option (CAT DDO)

                                  SUMMARY OF DISCUSSION*

                             Meeting of the Executive Directors

                                           February 1, 2011



The Executive Directors approved a Disaster Risk Management Development Policy Loan with a
Catastrophe Deferred Drawdown Option (CAT DDO) to the Government of El Salvador in the
amount of US$50 million on the payment terms and conditions set out in the President's
Memorandum.

Speakers expressed support for the innovative Disaster Risk Management DPL with a CAT
DDO, which was consistent with the priority areas identified in the Country Partnership Strategy.
Speakers noted that this loan would strengthen the Government's capacity to mobilize resources
for disaster risk management and prevention. Directors were pleased with a comprehensive and
integrated disaster risk management program implemented by the Government. Executive
Directors encouraged the Bank to continue its cooperation in this important area with the
Government of El Salvador and with other donors.
