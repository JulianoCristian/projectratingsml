7a.  Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
The objective of the Project are: (a) to strengthen the capacity of the Recipient to promote and improve 
coordinated and sustainable land and water management in the Basin; (b) to strengthen institutional 
mechanisms in Member Countries for management of transboundary land and water issues; (c) to develop 
a
Strategic Action Plan (SAP) to improve the conservation and management of land and water resources in 
the Basin; and (d) to assist the recipient in coordinating donor support for implementing the Strategic 
Action Plan and an effective transboundary management.
