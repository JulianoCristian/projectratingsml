                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to improve the health and nutritional status of the people of Afghanistan, with a greater focus on women and children by
                         increasing accessibility and quality of the BPHS in the provinces of Balkh and Samangan and urban Kabul.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall project performance is satisfactory with progress in continuing and expanding access to BPHS services in Balkh and Samangan provinces and development of urban BPHS
                         proposal which is making progress despite a fragile security situation and human resource challenges. Despite satisfactory implementation progress, results according to HMIS data
                         are either stagnating or declining in 2010 as compared to the previous years on key performance indicators both for NGO and Ministry of Public Health managed provinces. The
                         trends seems to similar in all provinces including remaining provinces not financed by the Bank. The performance trend needs close follow up by MOPH management to analyze
