                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project is to contribute to the GOI's initiatives to support the recovery of MSEs in Yogyakarta and Central Java and enable them to reach at least their pre-
                         earthquake capacity through enhanced access to finance and targeted assistance.
