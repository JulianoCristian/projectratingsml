Amendment to Loan Agreement                     
Dear Mr. Karaoz,
This letter refers to the Loan Agreement between the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (IBRD) and the Republic of Turkey, dated September 11, 
1998, as amended, for the Emergency Flood and Earthquake Recovery Project and your 
request to amend the Loan Agreement to response to earthquake emergency in the Marmara 
Region.
We are pleased to inform you that the above referenced Loan Agreement is hereby 
amended as follows:
1.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended as set 
forth in Annex to this letter.
2.
The first paragraph of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read as 
follows:
“The objectives of the Project are to (a) restore basic infrastructure in 
municipalities and rural areas affected by the 1998 flood; (b) 
restore housing in the areas 
affected by the 1998 earthquake in the province of 
Adana, as well as by 1999 earthquake in 
the Marmara Region; and (c) reduce 
vulnerability to future natural disasters”.
3.
Part B of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows:
“Carrying out of investments identified under the Eligibility Criteria to 
upgrade 
hydrotechnical infrastructure, modernize flood management systems, 
improve flood forecasting  and introduce measures to improve construction standards and 
reduce potential losses from 
future natural disasters including the establishment 
of a Turkish catastrophic insurance 
scheme”.
4.
Part C of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows:
“Carrying out of investments identified under the Eligibility Criteria to 
reconstruct 
household units in affected municipalities and rural areas in the 
province of Adana and in 
the Marmara Region”.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing, on behalf of Republic of 
Turkey, by signing, dating and returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter. This 
amendment will become effective on the date of countersignature.  A signed duplicate 
original of the letter is enclosed for your records.
Very truly yours,
