                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         1. Improve the provision of adequate water and wastewater services in Gaza
                          2. Functionally establish the Coastal Municipalities Water Utility (CMWU) as an autonomous well-run modern utility.
                          3. Strengthen PWA's capacity to assume sector regulatory functions.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The original project has closed on January 31st, 2010. Both the first additional financing (US$ 5 million) and the second additional financing (US$ 3 million) are scheduled to close on
                         June 30, 2011. On April 12, 2011, a third additional financing was approved by the board (US$ 3 million) and expected to close on January 31, 2012. Despite the difficult political and
                         security conditions in Gaza, he project has been implemented satisfactorily and progress has been achieved on both fronts the service provision and institutional development of the
                         utility.
