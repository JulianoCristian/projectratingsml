Re: 
Second Municipal Infrastructure Development Project - TF 023961
Amendment to the Trust Fund Credit Agreement
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Trust Fund Credit Agreement for the Second Municipal 
Infrastructure Development Project (TF023961) between the Palestine Liberation 
Organization (for the benefit of the Palestinian Authority) (the “Borrower”) and the 
International Development Association, acting as administrator of the Trust Fund for Gaza 
and West Bank (the “Administrator”), dated September 2, 2000, as amended (the “Credit 
Agreement”). We also refer to the letter from H.E. Mr. Mohammed Nashashibi, the 
former Minister of Finance of the Palestinian Authority and authorized representative of 
the Borrower, dated June 1, 2002, requesting the Administrator to reallocate the proceeds 
of the Credit to assist in financing emergency damage repair and services support 
activities in the West Bank and Gaza. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Administrator has agreed to the above 
request. Accordingly, the Credit Agreement is hereby amended as follows: 
 
1. 
Section 1.02 (a) shall read as follows: ““Eligible Categories” means categories 
(1) (a), (1) (b), (1) (c), (2) (a), 2 (b), (2) (c), (3) (a), (3) (b), (3) (c), (4), (5) (a), (5) (b) and  
(5) (c) set forth in the table in Part A.1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement;”.

5. 
In Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement, a new Part Z shall be added and shall read as 
follows:  
 
“Part Z
:
Damage Repair and Emergency Service Support Activities
Carrying out a program, consisting of the provision of goods and the execution of 
works, with a view to rehabilitating damaged physical and institutional infrastructure, and 
mitigating the decline in essential public service provision, including electricity, water, 
wastewater, solid waste and social services.” 
 
6. 
In paragraph 2 of Schedule 4 to the Credit Agreement, the word “and”, appearing in 
the first line, shall be deleted and shall be replaced by a comma, and the words “and Z” 
shall be inserted between the words “C.1” and “of”. 
 
7. 
All the terms and conditions of the Credit Agreement, not amended hereby, shall 
remain in full force and effect.
