Re:
Loan No. 3654-CR (Health Sector Reform Project)
Amendment to the Loan and Project Agreements
Dear Sirs:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank) and the Republic of Costa Rica (the Borrower) dated 
December 6, 1993, (the Loan Agreement), and the Project Agreement between the Bank and 
the Caja Costarricense de Seguro Social (CCSS) dated December 6, 1993.  We are pleased 
to announce that pursuant to your request the Bank agrees to amend the Loan and 
Project Agreements as follows:
A.
Loan Agreement
 
1.
Section 3.07. (c)
 
This Section is hereby amended by deleting "October 1, 1995" and 
substituting 
"December 31, 1996" therefor.
 
2.
Section 5.01. (f)
 
This Section is hereby amended by deleting "July 31, 1996" and 
substituting "December 
31, 1997" therefor.
 
3.
Schedule 1
 
(a)
The Category (6) of the table in paragraph 1 of this Schedule is 
hereby 
amended by changing the percentage of expenditures to be financed 
under the third column to  read as follows: "100% of expenditures made until the 
aggregate of withdrawals under this 
category reaches $100,000 equivalent; 
thereafter 75% of expenditures until the aggregate of 
withdrawals under this 
category reaches $200,000 equivalent; thereafter 50% until the 
aggregate of 
withdrawals under this category reaches $300,000; and thereafter 25%.
(b)
Paragraph 3 (c) (i) is hereby amended to read as follows: "(i) 
there is 
progress, satisfactory to the Bank, in the transfer of Health 
Centers and Health Posts in 
the respective Health Area from MH to CCSS together 
with the respective personnel in 
accordance with the Programa de Integracion;"
 
4.
Schedule 4
 
Paragraph 2 of Section II of this schedule is hereby amended in its 
entirety to read 
as follows:
 
"2.  Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph 1 of this Section, the 
provisions of 
the Consultant Guidelines requiring prior review or approval of 
budgets, short lists, 
selection procedures, letters of invitation, 
proposals, evaluation reports and contracts 
shall not apply to: (a) contracts
