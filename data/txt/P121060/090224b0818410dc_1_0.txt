                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Health Commodity Security Project (HSCP) has been under implementation for almost two years. The Project has achieved its goal as a source of predictable financing of key
                         public health commodities during a constrained budgetary environment. In 22 months, it has disbursed over 80percent of the funds. Through the Project support, the supply system
                         has been able to maintain 85 - 100 percent order fulfillment rates above the target set in the Project in the results framework.
