                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Since the project will close in May 31, 2013, the following critical activities can be carried out by that time: (i) recurrent and basic training in air transport safety and security oversight
                         and operations for the technical staff at the five beneficiary agencies (NCAA, NCAT, FAAN, NAMA, and NIMET); (ii) continuation of NCAA’s airlines’ Air Operator Certificate (AOC)
                         recertification process; recruitment of a legal expert to support the NCAA; and (iii) validation of updated results of the latest ICAO safety and security audits by external individual
