H.E. Dogo Negje Bhe
Minister of Economy, Plan
  and Environment Cooperation
Bangui, 
Central African Republic
Re:   Credit  No. 2126 CA (Transport Sector Project)
Amendment of Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement), dated May 21, 
1990, concluded between the Central African Republic (the Borrower) and the 
International Development Association (the Association), as amended to date.
Further to your Government's request regarding an increase in the amount of the 
authorized allocation for the Special Account, dated October 30, 1995, we are pleased 
to inform you that the Association accedes to your request and hereby agrees to amend 
the Agreement betwen us by modifying the amount established in paragraph 1 (c) of 
Schedule 5 to the Agreement to read $850,000 instead of $500,000.
Please kindly confirm your agreement with this amendment by signing and dating 
the two original copies of this letter in the space provided therefore and by 
returning to us one duplicate original duly signed and confirmed.
This amendment shall become effective upon receipt by the Association of one 
duplicate original of this letter duly signed and confirmed.
Sincerely yours,
