Dear Mr. Peprah:
                 Amendment:  Development Credit Agreement
        (National Agricultural Extension Project, Credit No. 2346-GH)
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (National Agricultural
Extension Project), signed on April 22, 1992, between the International
Development Association (the Association) and the Republic of Ghana (the
Borrower) and the amendment to the Development Credit Agreement effective
December 30, 1994.  Further to the discussions held with the representa-
tives of the Borrower during the mid-term review in July 1995 and to
your letter of May 15, 1996 requesting IDA to maintain the financing
ratios for Operating Costs at 80 percent throughout the remaining life of
the project, we are pleased to inform you that the Association hereby
agrees that the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Credit Agree-
ment shall be amended and revised as indicated in the attached Withdrawal
of the Proceeds of the Credit.
     Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing by countersigning
and dating the form of confirmation of this letter and returning one
countersigned copy to us. This amendment shall become effective as of the
date of receipt by the Association of this letter duly countersigned.
Sincerely,
Franz Kaps
Acting Country Director for Ghana
