                         Project Development Objectives
                         Agricultural Acquis Cohesion Project (P091715)
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)
                         The objective of the project is to develop sustainable systems and capacities within the MAFWM to ensure timely compliance with EU acquis conditions in the
                         rural sector.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Program?

                            Yes           No

                         Agricultural Pollution Control Project (P100639)
                         Global Environmental Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)
                         The development objective of the project is to significantly increase the use of environmentally friendly agricultural practices by farmers in Croatia's Pannonian
                         plain in order to reduce nutrient discharge from agricultural sources to surface and ground water bodies.
                         Has the Global Environmental Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Program?

                            Yes           No

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project restructuring was successful. Implementation speed has picked up, and all critical contracts are now under implementation. Croatia'S negotiations of its EU accession
                         agricultural chapter are now technically complete. Thus it is anticipated that the project will achieve its development objective. Bank's focus during the coming year will be on
                         providing agricultural acquis related support to the Ministry.
