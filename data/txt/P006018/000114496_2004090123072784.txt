Re:
Loan No. 3877-AR (Second Provincial Development Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank) and the Argentine Republic (the Borrower) dated December 5, 
1995 (the Loan Agreement), as amended, with regard to the above-captioned Loan.  We 
are pleased to inform you that, purusant to your request and our recent discussions on 
the subject, the Bank agrees to amend paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 5 to the Loan 
Agreement to read as follows in its entirety: "the term "Authorized Allocation" means 
an amount equivalent to $12,000,000 to be withdrawn from the Loan Account and 
deposited in the Special Account pursuant to paragraph 3 (a) of this Schedule.".
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signging and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter may be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us.  The provisions of this amendment letter 
will become effective as of the date first above written upon receipt by the Bank of 
one fully executed original of this amendment letter.
Very truly yours,
