   SYNOPSIS
  Facing the need for a sustainable growth strategy, Moldova has been working with the International
  Development Association (IDA) to address its main development challenges through increased eco-
  nomic competitiveness, promotion of human development and social inclusion, and increased effec-
  tiveness of the public sector.
