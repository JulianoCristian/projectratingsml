     Re: Credit No.2320-MOG (Economic Rehabilitation Project)
         Amendment of the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Mr. Ambassador:
      We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Economic
Rehabilitation Project) between Mongolia (the Borrower) and the
International Development Association (the Association) dated
December 27, 1991.  We refer also to the, telex dated May 14, 1992,
from the Minister of Finance of the Borrower requesting the
Association to increase the amount of Authorized Allocation of the
Special Account from $3.0 million to $6.0 million and authorizing
you to countersign an amendment letter in this regard.
      The Association is pleased to inform you that, after due
consideration, it agrees to the above request.  Therefore the amount
of $3,000,000" indicated in paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 4 to the
Development Credit Agreement is substituted by $6,000,000".
      Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by
signing and dating the enclosed form of confirmation on behalf of
the Borrower and by returning it to us for our record.  This
amendment will become effective as of the date hereof upon receipt
of your confirmation in this regard.
      A copy of this letter is being sent to Mr. Bazzarkhuu,
Minister of Finance, for his information.
                                    Sincerely,
                                   David Pearce
                                 Acting Director
                               China and Mongolia Department
                              East Asia and Pacific Region
