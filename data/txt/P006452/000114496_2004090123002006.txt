Third Northeast Basic Education Project (Loan 3663 BR) - Amendment
 
Dear Minister,
We refer to the Loan Agreement between the Federative Republic of Brazil (the 
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) 
dated February 10, 1994. We are pleased to inform you of our agreement to amend the 
Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
Category 3 of the table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan 
Agreement is amended to read as follows:
"(3)
Books, including 
51,500,000
the following
percentages are
applicable to
services related
expenditures made
on or before
June 30, 1998
to their distri-
bution with respect
to (i) foreign
expenditures and
to local
expenditures
(ex-factory cost) for
books; and (ii)
total expenditures
for services
related to the
distribution of
books: 100% up
to an aggregate
amount equivalent
to $33,500,000;
75% up to an
aggregate amount
equivalent to
$45,000,000;
50% up to an
aggregate amount
equivalent to
$49,500,000;
25% with respect
to the remaining
amount."
2.
A new Subparagraph (c) is included in Part C.1 of Section I of Schedule 4 to the 
Loan Agreement to read as follows:
"(c)
services related to books distribution; provided that the 
distribution of 
books for the school year of 1995 may be contracted directly 
with Empresa Brasileira de
Correios e Telegrafos, under terms and conditions acceptable to the Bank."
