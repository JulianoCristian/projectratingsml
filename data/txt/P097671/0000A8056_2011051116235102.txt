                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project's development objective is to improve the institutional capacity of MHMS in the areas of public expenditure management and sector performance
                         monitoring. Such capacity development would be critical to the overall success of the Program in improving health services delivery and outcomes.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The HSSP-TA grant is supporting the Ministry of Health and Medical Services to strengthen its public expenditure management, and broader sector planning and performance
                         monitoring. Grant implementation is generally progressing well. With support from the grant, the Ministry of Health and Medical Services is finalising its National Health Strategic Plan
                         2011-2015 and the associated monitoring and evaluation framework, and medium term expenditure framework.
