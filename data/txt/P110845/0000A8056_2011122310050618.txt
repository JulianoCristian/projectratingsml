                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Albania Disaster Risk Mitigation and Adapation Project (AL-DRMAP) is to strengthen institutional capacities (a) to reduce Albania's
                         vulnerability to natural and man-made hazards; and (b) to limit human, economic, and financial losses due to these disasters.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project implementation shows a continued progress towards achievement of the project development objectives. The General Directorate for Civil Emergencies has increased its
                         emergency management capacity through the acquisition of emergency response equipment and establishment of a National Emergency Command and Control Center. The
