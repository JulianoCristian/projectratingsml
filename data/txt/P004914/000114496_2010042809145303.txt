               Re:  Loan No. 2977 AL (Vocational Training Project)
                    Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
     We refer to the Loan Agreement in respect of the above-referenced Project, 
entered into by and between the Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria (the 
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development, dated April 
7, 1989.
     The purpose of this letter is to amend Sections 3.03, 3.04 and 3.05 of the Loan 
Agreement as set forth hereinbelow.
     Section 3.03 of the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as follows:
     "The Borrower shall, not later than January 31, 1991, employ all consultants to 
assist the Borrower in carrying out Part C of the Project."
     Section 3.04 of the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as follows:
     "The Borrower shall take all steps necessary to ensure that the number of CFPA 
instructors receiving in-service training every year increases to reach one-third of 
all such instructors by 1993.
     Section 3.05(a) of the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as follows:
     "Not later than December 31, 1991, take all necessary steps to complete and 
furnish to the Bank for its review, the studies referred to under Parts B.5 and C.5 of 
the Project; and"
     Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and duly 
dating the attached duplicate original of this amendment letter, and returning it to 
us at your earliest
convenience.  A signed duplicate original of this amendment letter is enclosed 
herewith for your records.
