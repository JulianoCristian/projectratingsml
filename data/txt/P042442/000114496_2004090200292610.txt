PERU - Sierra - Natural Resources Management and Poverty Alleviation 
Project 
(Loan No. 4130-PE)  Amendment to Loan Agreement
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated April 11, 1997 for the above captioned 
Project, between the Republic of Peru and the International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development, as amended. In this letter, all the terms defined in the Loan 
Agreement have the same meaning as therein set forth.
I am pleased to advise you that pursuant to the request presented by the 
Director General of Public Credit dated March 26, 2001 the Bank hereby agrees to amend 
the Loan Agreement by increasing the percentage of expenditures to be financed under 
category (5) (Training) of the table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the 
Loan Agreement from 50% to 85%.
Consequently, the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is 
revised as set forth in the Attachment to this letter.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing and dating this letter 
in the space provided below and returning the same to the undersigned. This amendment 
letter shall be executed in two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.
Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed original to us. The 
provisions of this amendment letter will become effective, as of the date of this 
letter, once the Bank has received one fully executed original of this amendment 
letter.
Very truly yours,
By /s/ Evangeline Javier
Acting Country Director
Bolivia, Ecuador and Peru
