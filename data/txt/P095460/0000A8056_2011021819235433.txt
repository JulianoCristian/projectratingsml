                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project's ultimate development objective is to increase effective use of the state road infrastructure, with an aim at stimulating higher economic growth.

                         Project implementation is satisfactory with 3/4 of all the resources from the loan devoted to the works component already disbursed -- approx. 860 km rehabilitated to date -- and
