                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project implementation is satisfactory with 97% of all the resources from the loan already disbursed. The 1,100 km of State highways covered by PREMAR currently benefit from
                         performance-based routine maintenance. All technical assistance activities are now on-going, about half of them having been completed.
