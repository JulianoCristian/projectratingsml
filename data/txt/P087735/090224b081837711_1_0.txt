                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the proposed Emergency Road Rehabilitation Project is to improve the condition of road assets by rehabilitating highly damaged segments of the
                         country's highway and rural road networks, reestablishing critical river crossings, and restoring the capacity to manage and maintain road assets.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is progressing towards achieving its original objectives. This includes the improvement of the condition of selected roads and replacement of temporary river crossings
                         with more permanent ones. Moreover, institutional capacities are gradually improving whether through technical assistance, structured training sessions or through on the ground
                         capacity improvement while implementing the sub projects.
