7a.  Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
The proposed Project aims at supporting the Government of Argentina to consolidate, strengthen, and 
increase the coverage of a lifelong learning and competency-based training system for disadvantaged adults 
18
years or older with the objectives for those who participate to: (i) enhance employability (for 
unemployed); and (ii) improve career opportunities (for employed).
