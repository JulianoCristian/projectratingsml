Excellency,
    We refer to the Loan Agreement for the above-mentioned Project,
dated June 5, 1992, entered into by and between the International
Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the
Municipality of Tehran (the Borrower), and the Guarantee Agreement,
dated June 5, 1992 entered by and between the Islamic Republic of
Iran (the Guarantor) and the Bank.
    I am pleased to inform you that, pursuant to the letter received
from the Director of the Office for International Organizations and
Institutions, Ministry of Economic Affairs and Finance, dated
October 19, 1993, the Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement to
provide for the financing of part of local expenditures.
    Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is therefore amended to read as
set forth in Attachment A.
    Please confirm the Borrower's agreement with the foregoing
amendment by signing and dating the attached duplicate original of
this amendment letter, and returning it to us at your earliest
convenience.

2. For the purposes of this Schedule:
   (a) the term "foreign expenditures" means expenditures in the currency of
any country other than that of the Guarantor for goods or services supplied 
from the territory of any country other than that of the Guarantor; and
   (b) the term "local expenditures" means expenditures in the currency of
the Guarantor or for goods or services supplied from the territory of the
Guarantor.
3. Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph 1 above, no withdrawals shall
be made in respect of payments made for expenditures prior to the date of this 
Agreement.
