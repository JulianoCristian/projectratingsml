                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project's Development Objective is to support national Government policy to: (i) improve the coverage, efficiency, and quality of the Argentine education system,
                         and (ii) to improve the governance of the Argentine education system through strengthening ofthe normative, planning, information, monitoring, and evaluation capacity
                         at the national and provincial levels.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         With about 18 months left to closing, the Project is entering its final phase, and preparing for the Implementation Completion Report, and the possibility of a new follow-on operations.
                         The Government plans to update progress toward targets for the 2011 school year in September 2012. The updated data will be reflected in the next ISR. The highlights for coverage
                         and efficiency are as follows (data from 2010): with regards to coverage, the trend of 6 year olds in 1st grade living in rural areas and who did not attend preschool continues to
                         decline, reaching a low of 6,265 children in 2010. This compares very favorably to figures for 2009 (8,154) and since the baseline of 2004 (15,861), and even surpasses the end of
