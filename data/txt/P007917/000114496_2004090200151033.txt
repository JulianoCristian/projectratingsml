PARAGUAY (Private Sector Development Project)
Loan No. 3774 PA - Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated July 20,1994 entered into between the Republic 
of Paraguay (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the 
Bank), as amended.
In response to your request and our recent discussions on the subject, we are pleased to 
advise you that the Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows, so as to strengthen the 
UTEP:
1.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended to read as follows:

2.
A new Paragraph 4 is added to Schedule 1 to read as follows:
“4.
For the purposes of this Schedule the term ‘Technical Assistance for UTEP’ 
means the expenditures incurred in connection with the provision of consultants’ services 
and training to the UTEP.”.
3.
The description of the Project in paragraph 2 of Schedule 2 is amended to read as follows:
“The Project consists of: (a) the financing of Investment Projects and SC-Investment 
Projects; and (b) the strengthening of the UTEP through the provision of consultants’ 
services and training, subject to such modifications thereof as the Bank and the Borrower 
may agree upon from time to time to achieve such objectives.”.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by signing and dating this letter in the 
space provided below and returning the same to the undersigned. This Amendment Letter shall 
be executed in two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.
Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed original to us. The provisions of 
this amendment will become effective once the Bank has received one executed original of this 
Amendment Letter.
