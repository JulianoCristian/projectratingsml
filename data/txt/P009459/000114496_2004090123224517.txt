Re:
Credit 1827-BD (Road Rehabilitation and Maintenance
Project) - Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Sir:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Road Rehabilitation and 
Maintenance Project) between the People's Republic of Bangladesh (the Borrower) and 
the International Development Association (the Association) dated September 2, 1987. 
The Association hereby proposes to reduce the percentage of expenditures to be 
financed by the Association under Category 1 (a) (Phase 1 of Rehabilitation Works) 
from 70% to 57% in order to make allowance for additional funding for that component 
provided by the OPEC Fund under the Project.
Accordingly, the Association proposes to amend the Table in Schedule 1 to the 
Development Credit Agreement to read as follows:
