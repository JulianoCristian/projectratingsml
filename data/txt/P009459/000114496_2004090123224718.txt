(Road Rehabilitation and Maintenance Project)
Amendment of the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Mr. Chaudhury:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated September 2, 1987 between the 
People's Republic of Bangladesh (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association) for the Road Rehabilitation and Maintenance Project. We 
also refer to the letters dated March 31, 1992, and May 17, 1992 from the Economic 
Relations Division requesting that the Development Credit Agreement be amended as set 
out in said letters.
We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to the above request, the Association 
has agreed to amend the Development Credit Agreement as set out below:
(i)
Paragraph 3 (a), Schedule 4: the following words and figures are 
added to paragraph 3(a) of Schedule 4 immediately following the 
words "satisfactory to the Association":
"By December 31, 1992";
(ii)
Paragraph 4 (c), Schedule 4: the following words and figures are 
added to paragraph 4(c) of Schedule 4 immediately following the 
words "taking into account the Association's comments thereon":
"by December 31, 1992";
(iii)
Paragraph 6 (b), Schedule 4: the following words and figures are 
added to paragraph 6(b) of Schedule 4 immediately following the 
words "in a manner satisfactory to the Association":
"by December 31, 1992";
(iv)
Paragraph 2 (b), Schedule 1: delete quote 3(a), 4(c), 6(b) 
unquote.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the 
enclosed form of confirmation and returning it to us. Subject to your confirmation as 
indicated above, this amendment will be effective as of the date of this letter.
Yours sincerely,
Jochen Kraske
Director
Country Department 1
South Asia Region
