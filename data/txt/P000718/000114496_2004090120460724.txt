                    Re:  Credit No. 2002-ET (Transport Project)
                    Amendments
Dear Sir:
1.   We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the Transport
Project dated May 30, 1992, between Ethiopia (the Borrower) and the
lnternational Development Association (the Association) for the
Transport Project.  We also refer to discussions between the
Borrower and the Association on reallocating funds under the Project
to finance emergency requirements in the transport sector, including
the railways.
2.      We are pleased to inform you that the Association has agreed
to amend the Development Credit Agreement as follows:
(a)  The table in Schedule I to the Development Credit Agreement
     shall be amended as in the attachment to this letter.
(b)  A new Part F shall be added to Schedule 2 to the Development
     Credit  Agreement which shall read as follows:
"Part F: Railways
     Strengthening the capacity of Chemin de Fer Djibouti-Ethiopie
to carry out operational management, locomotive maintenance, civil
works and the  provision of equipment and spare parts for the
railways."
     If you agree with the proposed amendments, please sign, date
and return to us the enclosed copy of this letter.  The amendments
will become effective on the date of your signature.
                      Sincerely,
                   Francis X. Colaco
                       Director
               Eastern Africa Department
