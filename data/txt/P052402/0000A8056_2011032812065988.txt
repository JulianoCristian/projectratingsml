                         Global Environmental Objectives
                         Global Environmental Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to assist the Recipient in implementing environmental protection and biodiversity conservation activities in the Sahelian priority
                         area of Gourma, through the improved sustainable management of natural resource use, improved returns for the local population.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has achieved tangible results in the field as measured by: (i) an increase in biodiversity knowledge among the Gourma population as a result of strong public awareness
                         activities (through local radios and community meetings) and the carrying out of 84 biodiversity conservation initiatives out of the 50 initially targeted by project closure; (ii) the
