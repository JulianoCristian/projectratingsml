                         Implementation Status Overview
                          Most project activities have been fully implemented. The original loan for the project has been extended to accommodate the preparation of an Additional Financing (AF).
                         The AF started implementation in late January 2010. Of the 16 indicators related to the project development objectives, two have been completed under the original loan, and only
                         one is off-track. Project implementation has experienced delays from the original implementation plan mostly due to the volume and the complexity of IT procurement. It has also
