7a. Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
The overall project development objective is to streamline the compulsory standards requirements for
business, develop systems to enhance product quality and safety, and increase enterprise competitiveness in
pilot sectors. The key project outcomes will be a decrease in the cost of regulatory compliance for business
and, in the longer term, an increase in the share of non-gold trade with WTO partners.
