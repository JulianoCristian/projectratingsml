                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is nearing completion. It has showed good progress towards reaching its development objectives. The last project monitoring and evaluation report covering June 2011 -
                         May 2012 period showed that the targets for the majority of PDO indicators have been met or exceeded. The final project monitoring and evaluation report covering June 2012 – May

                         2013 period will be submitted by end-May and its findings will be reflected in the Implementation Completion Report.

                         The Government approved the Decree on the Approval of the Operational-Structural Model of Comprehensive System of Integrated Social Protection Service Provision in July 2012
                         and has been piloting the new model in several Integrated Social Protection Delivery Centers (ISPDCs) since February 2013. The project saw a major progress in the implementation
