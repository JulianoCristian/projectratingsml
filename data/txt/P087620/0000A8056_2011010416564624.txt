                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project shows significant progress towards its development objective " Improve Borrower's capacity to provide a more efficient, effective and high quality services to the
                         population by the public employment, pension and social assistance agencies." Several activities have been initiated in all components of the project and the Bank team expressed

                         delay in the passage of the laws underpinning Armenia's new Pension Reform Program. The National Parliament expected to adopt the laws by the end of the year and implementing

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The Government is committed to speed up the work under Component 3. Ministry of Labor and Social Issues, the implementing agency, and the State Revenue Committee, the main
                         beneficiary of the IT system for personified reporting and record-keeping of the unified income tax, have signed a memorandum of understanding to expeditiously and jointly
                         implement the development of the IT system as soon as the pension reform program package is adopted by the National Parliament.
