We refer to the Grant Agreement ("Agreement") for a Multi-donor Trust Fund
("MDTF") for the Co-financing of the General Education Quality Improvement Project in
Ethiopia (TF094224) entered into between the International Development Association
("World Bank"), acting as administrator of grant funds provided by various donors under the
Multi-donor Trust Fund ("MDTF") and the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia
("Recipient"), dated July 21, 2009, and countersigned on July 24, 2009, and as amended to the
date of this letter.
We are pleased to inform you that the World Bank has received some additional
contributions pursuant to commitments of funds from the participating donors to the MDTF.
Consequently, we propose to amend the Agreement in respect of the provisions below:
1.    First paragraph of the Agreement is amended to read as follows:
"In response to the request for financial assistance made on behalf of the Federal Democratic
Republic of Ethiopia ("Recipient"), I am pleased to inform you that the International
Development Association ("World Bank"), acting as administrator of grant funds provided by
various donors under the Multi-donor Trust Fund for Co-financing of the General Education
Quality Improvement Project in Ethiopia, proposes to extend to the Recipient, a grant in an
amount not to exceed two hundred eighteen million United States Dollars (US$218,000,000)
("Grant") on the terms and conditions set forth or referred to in this letter agreement
("Agreement"), which includes the attached Annex, to assist in the financing of the project
described in the Annex ("Project"). The Project is being co-financed by the World Bank
through a: (a) Financing Agreement ("Credit No. 45350-ET"), entered into between the
Recipient and the World Bank on December 29, 2008 for an amount equivalent to Special
Drawing Rights 33,500,000 ("Financing Agreement"); (b) Letter Agreement between the
Recipient and the World Bank, dated November 12, 2008, and countersigned on November
21, 2008, for a grant of US$70,000,000 provided under the associated Education for All - Fast
Track Initiative Catalytic Fund Grant Agreement ("Grant No. TF093227"); and (c) Letter
Agreement between the Recipient and the World Bank, dated July 26, 2010 for a grant of
US$98,000,000 provided under the associated Education for All - Fast Track Initiative
Catalytic Fund Grant Agreement ("Grant No. TF097263"). This Grant is funded out of the
