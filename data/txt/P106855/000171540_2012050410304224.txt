Ethiopia: Multi-donor Trust Fund for the Co-financing of the General Education
Quality Improvement Project in Ethiopia (TF094224)
Sixth Amendment to the Grant Agreement
We refer to the Grant Agreement ("Agreement") for a Multi-donor Trust Fund
("MDTF") for the Co-financing of the General Education Quality Improvement
Project in Ethiopia (TF094224) entered into between the International Development
Association ("World Bank"), acting as administrator of grant funds provided by various
donors under the Multi-donor Trust Fund ("MDTF") and the Federal Democratic
Republic of Ethiopia ("Recipient"), dated July 21, 2009, and countersigned on July 24,
2009, and as amended to the date of this letter. .
We are pleased to inform you that the World Bank has received some
additional contributions pursuant to commitments of funds from the participating
donors to the MDTF. Consequently, we propose to amend the Agreement in respect
of the provisions below:
1.    First paragraph of the Agreement is amended to read as follows:
"In response to the request for financial assistance made on behalf of the Federal
Democratic Republic of Ethiopia ("Recipient"), I am pleased to inform you that the
International Development Association ("World Bank"), acting as administrator of
grant funds provided by various donors under the Multi-donor Trust Fund for Co-
financing of the General Education Quality Improvement Project in Ethiopia, proposes
to extend to the Recipient, a grant in an amount not to exceed one hundred sixty-four
million seven hundred thirty thousand United States Dollars (US$164,730,000)
("Grant") on the terms and conditions set forth or referred to in this letter agreement
("Agreement"), which includes the attached Annex, to assist in the financing of the
project described in the Annex ("Project"). The Project is being co-financed by the
World Bank through a: (a) Financing Agreement ("Credit No. 45350-ET"), entered into
