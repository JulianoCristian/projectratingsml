TURKEY:  Loan No. 3541-TU 
(Employment and Training Project)
Amendment to Loan Agreement    
Dear Mr. Karaoz,
This letter refers to the Loan Agreement between the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (IBRD) and the Republic of Turkey, dated February 1, 
1993, as amended, for the Employment and Training Project and your request to amend 
the Loan Agreement to include a new temporary community employment training program 
and reallocate the proceeds of the Loan.  We are pleased to inform you that the above 
referenced Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows.
1.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended as set 
forth in Annex to this letter.
2.
Paragraph 1 of Part A in Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is modified to read as 
follows:
“Design and carrying out of a job insertion training, self-employment 
training and  temporary community employment training programs to facilitate the 
absorption of unemployed 
workers in productive employment.”
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing, on behalf of Republic of 
Turkey, by signing, dating and returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter. This 
amendment will become effective as of August 24, 1999, upon your countersignature.  A 
signed duplicate original of the letter is enclosed for your records.
Very truly yours,
