Re:   
Cr. 3135-AM (Title Registration Project) 
 
Amendment to Credit Agreement  
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Title Registration Project), dated 
December 23, 1998, between the Republic of Armenia (the Borrower) and the International 
Development Association (the Association). We also refer to your letter of August 2, 2002 to the 
Association requesting extension of the closing date. We are pleased to inform you, that the 
Association agrees to the following amendments: 
 
1.  
Section 2.02(a) of the Development Credit Agreement is revised by adding the word 
“works” after the words “of goods”. 
 
2. 
The Association has established December 31, 2003 as the later date for purposes of 
Section 2.03 of the Development Credit Agreement. 
 
3. 
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement is revised 
to read as set forth in the Attachment to this Letter.  
 
4. 
Paragraph 3 of Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement is amended by deleting 
the word “and” before the numerical “v” and adding a new sub-category (vi) after the words “(v) 
training” which reads as follows: 
 
“vi. works”. 
 
5. 
Schedule 2, Part A of the Development Credit Agreement is revised by adding a new 
paragraph 5 which reads as follows:   
 
“5. Upgrading of the office design and layout of about 40 IRCs.”  
 
6.  
Part A1 of Section 1 of Schedule 3 to the Development Credit Agreement is revised by 
adding the words “and works” after the word “Goods”.

7. 
Part C of Section 1 of Schedule 3 of the Development Credit Agreement is revised by 
adding a new paragraph 5 which reads as follows:  
 
“5. 
Procurement of Small Works  
Works estimated to cost less than $5,000 equivalent per contract, up to an aggregate 
amount not to exceed $120,000 equivalent, may be procured under lump-sum, fixed-price 
contracts awarded on the basis of quotations obtained from three (3) qualified domestic 
contractors in response to a written invitation. The invitation shall include a detailed 
description of the works, including basic specifications, the required completion date, a 
basic form of agreement acceptable to the Association, and relevant drawings, where 
applicable. The award shall be made to the contractor who offers the lowest price 
quotation for the required work, and who has the experience and resources to complete 
the contract successfully.” 
8.  
Part D 2(b) of Section 1 of Schedule 3 to the Development Credit Agreement is revised 
by deleting the word “and” before the numerical “(ii)” and adding a new subparagraph (iii) which 
reads as follows: 
 
“and (iii) the first two contracts awarded in accordance with the provisions of Part C.5 of 
this Section, “ 
9.  
Part D.2 (b) (i) is revised adding the words “or contractor” after the words “any supplier”. 
10.  
Paragraphs 1 (a) and (b) of Schedule 5 to the Development Credit Agreement  are revised 
to read as follows:  
“(a) 
the term “eligible Categories” means Categories (1) through (6) set forth in the 
 
table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement;   
 
(b)   
the term “eligible expenditures” means expenditures in respect of the reasonable 
  cost of goods, works, services required for the Project and to be financed out of the 
  proceeds of the Credit allocated from time to time to the eligible Categories in 
 
accordance with the provisions of Schedule 1 to this Agreement;” 
 
All the terms and conditions of the DCA, as amended hereby, shall remain in full force 
and effect.
