Your Excellency: 
 
Re: 
Ukraine Loan No. 4324-UA 
 
(Kiev District Heating Improvement Project) 
Amendment to Loan Agreement
 
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Kiev District Heating Improvement Project) between 
Ukraine (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the 
Bank) dated October 14, 1998, and the related Project Agreement of even date therewith between 
the Bank and Kievenergo (Kievenergo) for the above-referenced Loan.  We also refer to the 
request contained in the letter dated January 30, 2003, from Mr. A. A. Maksiuta, State Secretary, 
Ministry of Finance, to amend the legal documents for the Project so as to adequately introduce 
changes required as a consequence of the merger of the Kiev Municipal District Heating 
Company (KMDHC) with Kievenergo. 
 
I
am pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, the Bank has acceded to the 
aforementioned request.  Therefore, the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:  
 
1.
 
The phrase “Parts A, B, C.2 and C.3 of the Project” substitutes the phrase “Parts A, C.2 
and C.4 of the Project” in Recitals (A) and (B) of the Preamble, Section 2.02 (a) and (b), Section 
3.01 (b), Section 3.02, Section 4.01 (a), and paragraphs 1 (b) and 5 (d) of Schedule 4
 
(
Special 
Account
).

In Recital (B) of the Preamble, the “; and” is substituted for “;” at the end of the said 
Recital. 
 
3. 
Recital (C) of the Preamble is deleted in its entirety. 
 
4. 
Section 1.02 is modified as follows: 
 
(i) 
paragraph (a) is modified to read:  
 
“(a) 
“Kiev District Heating System” means the physically integrated 
system of heat production, transmission and distribution for the City of Kiev 
operated by Kievenergo;”.

9. Schedule 
2

Description of the Project
)
is modified as follows: 
 
(i) 
In the first paragraph, phrase (iii) is revised to read:  
 
“(iii)

(ii) 
Part A.3 is revised to read: 
 
“3. 
Rehabilitation and expansion of boiler capacity at TETS-5 and TETS-6

(iii)

“7.

(iv)

“Part B:
Kievenergo District Heating Distribution System Improvement

(v) 
In Part C, paragraphs 3 and 5 are deleted in their entirety and paragraph 4 is 
renumbered as paragraph 3 and modified to read: 
 
“3. 
Provision of: (i) technical assistance to Kievenergo required for the

disbursement activities; and (ii) funding for the carrying out of audits required

All the terms and conditions of the Loan Agreement, as amended hereby, shall remain in 
full force and effect.  Due to its interest in this matter, a copy of this Letter is being forwarded to 
Kievenergo.  
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating the 
confirmation on the enclosed copy of this Amendment Letter and returning it to us. This 
amendment will come into force and effect as of the date of your countersignature and upon 
receipt by the Bank of the said duly countersigned original Amendment Letter and the correlated 
countersigned original Amendment Letter between the Bank and Kievenergo.
