Amendment to Project Agreement                  
Dear Mr. Plachkov: 
 
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Kiev District Heating Improvement Project) between 
Ukraine (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the 
Bank) dated October 14, 1998, and the related Project Agreement of even date therewith between 
the Bank and Kievenergo (Kievenergo) for the above-referenced Loan.  We also refer to the 
request contained in the letter dated January 30, 2003, from Mr. A. A. Maksiuta, State Secretary, 
Ministry of Finance of the Borrower, to amend the legal documents for the Project so as to 
adequately introduce changes required as a consequence of the merger of the Kiev Municipal 
District Heating Company (KMDHC) with Kievenergo. 
 
I
am pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, the Bank has acceded to the 
aforementioned request.  Therefore, the Project Agreement is hereby amended as follows:  
 
1. 
The phrase “Parts A, B, C.2 and C.3 of the Project” substitutes the phrase “Parts A, C.2 
and C.4 of the Project” in Recital (B) of the Preamble, Section 2.01, Section 2.03, Section 2.04 
(b) (i), Section 2.05 (a), Section 2.06 (a), (b), (c) and (d), and Section 4.01 (a). 
 
2. 
Section 4.06 is deleted in its entirety. 
 
3. 
Section 4.07 is re-numbered as Section 4.06. 
 
4. 
The Schedule (
Procurement and Consultants’ Services
)
is modified as follows: 
 
(i) 
In Part C, paragraphs 1. and 2. are renumbered, respectively, as paragraphs 2. and 
3., and a new paragraph 1 is added to read as follows: 
 
“National Competitive Bidding
(i) 
The first two (2) contracts for works, including installation of 
equipment, under Parts A and B of the Project estimated to cost less than 
$2,000,000 equivalent each; and (ii) thereafter, subject to: (A) the satisfactory 
outcome of the competitive process for the aforementioned first two (2) contracts 
for works; and (B) the Bank’s prior review and approval, each of the following 
contract for works, including installation of equipment, under Parts A and B of
