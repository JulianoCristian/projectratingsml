Dear Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (the "Loan Agreement") entered into between the State
of Rio Grande do Norte (the "Borrower") and the International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development (the "Bank"), and the Guarantee Agreement entered into between the Federative
Republic of Brazil (the "Guarantor") and the Bank, dated June 20, 2008, all for the above-
captioned Project (the "Project"). Please note that capitalized terms used in this letter (the
"Amendment Letter") not defined herein have the meaning ascribed to them in the Loan
Agreement.
We also refer to your letter of request dated October 21, 2013, and endorsement letters
from: (i) Commission for External Financing (COFIEX) letter of Recommendation No. 01/0243
dated November 13, 2013, and (ii) National Treasury concurrence letter dated December 11,
2013, and rectified on January 16, 2014, wherein a request is made to amend the above-
captioned Loan Agreement as follows: (i) extend Project Closing Date for 6 months; (ii)
reallocate Loan proceeds among its disbursement Categories, and (iii) increase the percentage of
finance by the Bank among 2 Categories of disbursement.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank acknowledges your requests and agrees to
amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.     Section 2.03 of the Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read in its entirety as follows:
"Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be September 30, 2014, or such later date as the
Bank shall establish, after having received evidence that the Guarantor has authorized the
extension of the Closing Date. The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower and the
Guarantor of such later date, which shall not be a date beyond the date authorized by the
Guarantor."
