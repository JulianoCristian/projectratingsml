                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project continues to make solid progress toward meeting its objectives. Project results include: (a) electricity distribution companies have completed grid extension projects to
                         serve 92,000 rural households representing about 400,000 people, (b) ten electricity distribution companies are participating in the Project, co-financing projects based on grid
                         extension and PV systems; and (c) three contracts under the productive uses promotion component have assisted more than 3000 rural families to improve their productivity and
                         incomes through electricity using equipment in Cuzco, Junin and Lima Provinces, while an additional 11 contracts are underway in these and other areas.
