                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project continues to make solid progress toward meeting its objectives. Project results include: (a) electricity distribution companies have completed grid extension projects to
                         serve 72,500 rural households representing more than 310,000 people, (b) the Project has received additional proposals for the installation of more than 8,000 solar home systems
                         (SHS) which are expected to be installed and operated under the supervision of electricity distribution companies by the close of the Project, and (c) Three contracts under the
                         productive uses promotion component supported more than 3,000 rural families to improve their productivity and incomes through the adoption of electricity.
