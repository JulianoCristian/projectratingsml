             Re: Loan 2932-IND (First Jabotabek Urban Development Project)
                 Amendment of the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
      We refer to the Loan Agreement (First Jabotabek Urban
Development Project) between the Republic of Indonesia (the
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development (the Bank) dated April 20, 1988.  We also refer to your
Ministry's letter dated November 22, 1991, requesting the Bank for
a modification of the percentage of expenditures to be disbursed
under Category 1, and for a reallocation of proceeds under the above
Loan.
      The Bank is pleased to inform you that, after due
consideration, it has agreed to the above request.  Therefore, the
table set forth in Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is hereby
amended as follows:
