                         Implementation Status Overview
                         A Bank mission visited the Project from May 23 to 30, 2014, to supervise the project implementation progress. Component B1 (Environmental Management) has progressed as
                         planned and good progress has also been made with the studies implemented under Component C (Policy Development). With respect to Component A (Productive Investments), at
                         this stage, it is expected that approximately 32 sub-projects could be completed by the Project closing date. With regards to Subcomponent B2 (Land Administration), activities are
