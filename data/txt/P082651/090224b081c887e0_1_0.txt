                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project development objective is to build conditions in which the rural poor of Para can increase their incomes in a sustainable and secure environment.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         An Action Plan was developed by the Government and the Bank to improve the implementation of the Project by March 2013. Progress was reviewed in March 2013, and was found
                         to be satisfactory. In particular, the management of the Project has been strengthened and implementation is improving significantly. The Project is being restructured and the State
                         Government has requested an extension of the closing date of the Project from June 30, 2013, to December 31, 2014.
