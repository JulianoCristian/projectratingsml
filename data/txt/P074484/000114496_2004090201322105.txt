CREDIT NUMBER 3693 YF 
PROJECT AGREEMENT 
 
AGREEMENT, dated October 31, 2002, between INTERNATIONAL 
DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (the Association) and SERBIA AND 
MONTENEGRO EXPORT CREDIT AGENCY (SMECA). 
 
WHEREAS  (A) by the Development Credit Agreement of even date herewith 
between Federal Republic of Yugoslavia (the Borrower) and the Association, the 
Association has agreed to make available to the Borrower an amount in various currencies 
equivalent to Nine Million One Hundred Thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 
9,100,000), on the terms and conditions set forth in the Development Credit Agreement, but 
only on condition that SMECA agrees to undertake such obligations toward the Association 
as are set forth in this Agreement;  
 
(B) 
by a Grant Agreement to be entered into between the Borrower and 
SMECA (the Grant Agreement), the proceeds of the credit provided for under the 
Development Credit Agreement will be made available to SMECA on the terms and 
conditions set forth in said Grant Agreement; and 
 
WHEREAS SMECA, in considerat
ion of the Association’s entering into the 
Development Credit Agreement with the Borrower, has agreed to undertake the obligations 
set forth in this Agreement; 
 
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows: 
ARTICLE I 
Definitions 
 
Section 1.01. Unless the context otherwise requires, the several terms defined in 
the Development Credit Agreement and the General Conditions (as so defined) have the 
respective meanings therein set forth and the following terms have the following meanings: 
 
(a) 
“SITC” means the Standard International Trade Classification, Revision 3 
(SITC, Rev. 3), published by the United Nations in Statistical Papers, Series M, No. 
34/Rev. 3 (1986); and 
(b) 
“Financial Monitoring Report” and “FMR” means each report prepared in 
accordance with Section 4.02 of this Agreement.

ARTICLE II 
Execution of the Project 
 
Section 2.01. (a) SMECA declares its commitment to the objectives of the Project 
as set forth in Schedule 2 of the Development Credit Agreement, and, to this end, shall 
carry out the Project with due diligence and efficiency and in conformity with appropriate 
administrative, financial and insurance practices, with qualified and experienced 
management and personnel, and shall provide, or cause to be provided, promptly as needed, 
the funds, facilities, services and other resources required for the Project. 
(b) 
SMECA shall, for the purposes of Parts A, C and D
 
of the Project:  
(i)  
open and maintain in Euro one or more Facility Escrow Accounts, 
as the Project needs may require, on terms and conditions 
satisfactory to the Association, including appropriate protection 
against set-off, seizure or attachment, wherein it shall deposit the 
amounts required to support and back up the obligations 
assumed under the Political Risk Insurance Facility, the Exporter 
Performance Facility, and the Credit Insurance Facility. 
 
Deposits into and payments out of the Facility Escrow 
Account(s) shall be made exclusively for the purposes of the 
Project and on terms and conditions specified in the Operations 
Manual, including (except as the Association shall otherwise 
agree) those set forth or referred to in Schedules 1, 3 and 4 to this 
Agreement for the relevant Insurance Facility, and in accordance 
with the provisions of Schedule 4 to the Development Credit 
Agreement;  
(ii) 
enter into the Insurance Facility Agreements, the Recourse 
Agreements, and the Security Trust Agreements, all such 
agreements to contain terms and conditions satisfactory to the 
Association; 
(iii) 
enter into, and cause the Insurers other than SMECA to enter into, 
Insurance Contracts containing terms and conditions substantially 
similar to those specified in the Standard Form of Insurance 
Contract; and 
(iv) 
duly and punctually comply with all its obligations under the 
Project Documents to which it is a party or by which it is bound. 
(c) 
For the purposes of carrying out Parts B (b), C and D of the Project, 
SMECA shall:

Association a report, of such scope and detail as the Association shall reasonably request,

Development Credit Agreement, the Project Agreement and the other Project Documents,

acquisition, respectively) in respect of the Project Agreement. 
Section 2.10. Except as the Association shall otherwise agree, all
 
funds at the

purposes authorized by the Development Credit Agreement for the Project. 
Section 2.11. Within 30 days following the end of the calendar quarter

principal amount, and term (if any) thereof, as well as the interest rate applicable thereto, 
and the principal amount of any proceeds of disposition, as well as the costs and expenses 
related to such disposition, if any, as the case may be.

ARTICLE III 
Management and Operations of SMECA 
 
Section 3.01. (a) SMECA shall carry on its operations and conduct its affairs in 
accordance with sound administrative, financial, and insurance practices, and the provisions 
of the Operations Manual, under the supervision of qualified and experienced management 
assisted by competent staff in adequate numbers. 
 
(b) 
SMECA shall take out and maintain with responsible insurers, or make 
any other provision satisfactory to the Association for, insurance against such risks and in 
such amounts as shall be consistent with appropriate practice. 
Page  8
-
7
-
(c) 
Except as the Association shall otherwise agree, and any provision to the 
contrary contained in the Charter notwithstanding, SMECA shall: 
(i) 
conduct only the business and activities, and shall only make the 
investments, contemplated by or referred to in the Development 
Credit Agreement and the Italian Grant Agreement;  
(ii) 
carry out the environmental reviews and the environmental 
impact assessments referred to and described in Section 3.07 of 
the Development Credit Agreement: 
(iii) 
not declare or pay any dividends or otherwise distribute any 
profits; 
(iv) 
not amend, suspend, abrogate, terminate or waive any provision 
of the Charter without the prior written consent of the 
Association; 
(v) 
not amend any material or substantial provision of the Operations 
Manual without the prior written consent of the Association; 
(vi) 
not amend any provision of the Standard Form of Insurance 
Contract without the prior written consent of the Association; and 
(vii) 
not amend, assign, suspend, abrogate, terminate or waive any 
provision of any of the Insurance Contracts or of any of the 
Participation Agreements without the prior written consent of the 
Association.

 (b)

ARTICLE V 
Effective Date; Termination; 
Cancellation and Suspension

SCHEDULE 2 
Working Capital Loans/Guarantee Facility  
Implementation Program 
 
A. 
Selection of Participating Financial Institutions
1. 
SMECA shall select financial institutions and enterprises, in consultation with 
the Association, for participation under Part B (a) of the Project, based on criteria 
specified in the Operations Manual.  
B. 
Participation Agreements and Participation Loans
1. 
SMECA shall make available a portion of the proceeds of the Credit to 
Participating Financial Institutions, for them to make Subloans to Exporting Enterprises 
for Export Projects, under separate Participation Agreements to be entered into between 
SMECA and each of the Participating Financial Institutions under terms and conditions 
which shall have received the prior written approval of the Association, including the 
following:  
 
(a) 
the term of the Participation Loans shall be up to one year;  
 
(b) 
the Participating Financial Institutions and Exporting Enterprises, 
respectively, shall be required to pay interest not less than one-half of one percent above 
LIBOR for the period corresponding to the term of their respective Participation Loan;  
 
(c) 
the Participating Financial Institutions and the Exporting Enterprises 
shall each make payment of interest and other charges and repayment of principal in 
foreign currency; and
