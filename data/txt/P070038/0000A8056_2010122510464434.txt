                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The development objective of the project is to increase the productivity of theHonduran private sector, thereby improving Honduras' competitiveness. The
                         immediate objective of the project is to improve Honduras investment climate and its local firms' capacity to export in order to prepare Honduras to take
                         advantage of trade opportunities under existing and new Trade Promotion Agreements.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is closing on December 31, 2010 and nearly all planned activities have been completed as of November 30, 2010. All components had demonstrable positive impacts and
                         many exceeded their planned targets. There is some uncertainty regarding the sustainability of a few activities after the project closes, but the Government has expressed its
                         willingness to actively address these issues.
