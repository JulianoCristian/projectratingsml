AGREEMENT AMENDING PROJECT AGREEMENT 
AGREEMENT, dated May 26, 1999, between INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION 
(the Association) and KYRGYZ NATIONAL ENERGY HOLDING COMPANY (KNEHC). 
WHEREAS (A) the KYRGYZ REPUBLIC (the Borrower) and the Association have entered 
into a Development Credit Agreement (Power and District Heating Rehabilitation 
Project) dated July 3, 1996 (the Development Credit Agreement) for the purpose of 
assisting in the financing of the project described in Schedule 2 to the Development 
Credit Agreement; 
(B)
in consideration of the Association’s entering into the Development 
Credit Agreement with the Borrower, KNEHC has agreed to undertake the obligations set 
forth in a Project Agreement between the Association and KNEHC, dated July 3, 1996;  
(C)
by the Amending Agreement of even date herewith between the Borrower and 
the Association, the Association has agreed to make available to the Borrower an 
amount in various currencies equivalent to eleven million two hundred thousand Special 
Drawing Rights (SDR 11,200,000), on the terms and conditions set forth in the Amending 
Agreement, but only on condition that KNEHC agree to undertake such obligations toward 
the Association as are set forth in this Agreement; 
(D)
by an amendment to the Subsidiary Loan Agreement to be entered into 
between the Borrower and KNEHC, the proceeds of the supplemental amount provided for 
under the Amending Agreement will be made available to KNEHC on the terms and 
conditions set forth in said amendment to the Subsidiary Loan Agreement; and 
Page  2
WHEREAS KNEHC, in consideration of the Association’s entering into the Amending 
Agreement with the Borrower, has agreed to undertake the obligations set forth in this 
Amending Project Agreement;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I 
Amendments of the Project Agreement 
Section 1.01. In Subsection 4.04(b) of the Project Agreement, the date is 
amended to read June 30, 2000. 
Section 1.02. In Subsection 4.04(c) of the Project Agreement, the date is 
amended to read September 30, 2000.
Section 1.03. Section 4.08 of the Project Agreement is amended to read as 
follows:
“Section 4.08. KNEHC shall take all actions required on its part to 
ensure that its 
accounts receivable, both for power and heat, shall not exceed the 
equivalent of: (i) 120 
days of its average annual billing by December 31, 1998; 
(ii) 90 days of its average annual 
billing by December 31, 1999; and (iii) 60 
days of its average annual billing by December 
31, 2000 and thereafter.” 
ARTICLE II 
Effective Date; Termination 
Section 2.01. This Amending Project Agreement shall come into force and effect 
on the date upon which the Amending Agreement becomes effective. 
Section 2.02. If the Amending Agreement shall not have come into force and 
effect by a date ninety (90) days after the date of the Amending Agreement, this 
Amending Project Agreement and all obligations of the parties hereunder shall 
terminate, unless the Association establishes a later date for the purposes of Section 
2.04 of the Amending Agreement. If this Amending Project Agreement shall terminate 
under the provisions of this Section, the Project Agreement shall continue in full 
force and effect, as if this Amending Project Agreement had not been executed.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto, acting through their duly authorized 
representatives, have caused this Amending Project Agreement to be signed in their 
respective names in the District of Columbia, United States of America, as of the day 
and year first above written.
