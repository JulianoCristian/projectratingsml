                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of this project is to increase access to safe water and sanitation facilities in all states throughout Southern Sudan and to build capacity for
                         sustainable management, regulation and expansion of the rural water and sanitation sector.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The team rated the progress towards achieving the project development objective as satisfactory due to the progress made in the following areas. With regard to water, drilling of 616
                         new boreholes, rehabilitation of 528 boreholes, construction of 12 small distribution networks and construction of 3 hafirs have been completed. With regard to Hygiene and

                         Sanitation, a total of 103 institutional latrines, 6 public latrines and 1011 Household latrines have been constructed and are under use. A total of 608,000 people are expected to
                         benefit from these improved systems. Moreover, 518 water committees have been trained; hygiene and sanitation awareness training has been conducted.
Public Disclosure Copy




                         Out of the 30 million USD allocated for the project, a total of 28,439,618 USD has been committed and the project's procurement plan has been revised and posted on October 15,
                         2010 and lists activities planned to utilize the remaining funds. The project closing date has been changed to June 30, 2011. This will give the much needed time to successfully close
                         the remaining contracts. Preparation for the completion report has started both from the client as well as from the Bank side.
