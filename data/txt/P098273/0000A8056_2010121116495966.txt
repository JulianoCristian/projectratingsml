                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         Based on the results to date of LGSP, an agreement was reached between IDA and the Government of Bangladesh (GOB) to commence the preparation of LGSP-II immediately. As
                         a starting point, a public consultation launch for LGSP II was held on Oct 30 at Dhaka. The event was attended by the Hon. Minister and State Minister for LGRD, the Secretary, lGD
                         and the Country Director World Bank, as well as over 150 dignitaries, including a large number of Union Parishad representatives.
