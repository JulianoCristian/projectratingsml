                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project is continuing to make steady progress toward meeting the Project Development Objectives (PDO), the financial and institutional capacity of HFIC has significantly
                         improved through implementation of HDP. The Project has been able to maintain good leverage of private sector funding, with private/HFIC investment ratio of 2 to 1 under the
                         context of HDP. The investment portfolio has been maintained balanced between hard technical infrastructure and soft social infrastructure, also for risk diversification purpose. All
                         subprojects are being implementing in accordance with the Project Manual in term of financial management, procurement and safeguards. However, the IDA disbursements in 2011
