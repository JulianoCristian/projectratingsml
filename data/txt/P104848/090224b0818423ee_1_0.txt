                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Project has made significant progress towards meeting its Project Development Objective (PDO). The financial and institutional capacity of Hochiminh City Finance and
                         Investment Company (HFIC) has significantly improved through implementation of Hochiminh CityFinance and Investment Company Development Project (HDP). HFIC became a
                         model Local Investment and Development Fund (LDIF) which recognized by the Ministry of Finance and other LDIFs. The Project continues with good leverage of private sector
                         funding, accounting to approximately 52% of total investment in sub-projects using HDP proceeds. All sub-projects are being implemented in accordance with the Project Manual in
