                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Implementation is progressing well. Recruitment into the SES program was open and transparently undertaken. SES professionals are in 27 institutions providing valuable services
                         (management, advisory, mentoring and coaching, policy formulation, coordination) to their host institutions.
