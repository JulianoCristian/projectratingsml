Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Financing Agreement as restated pursuant to the Agreement providing 
for the Amendment and Restatement of the Development Credit Agreement (the “Agreement”) 
for the above-referenced Project, dated September 20, 2010, between the Republic of 
Madagascar (the “Recipient”) and the International Development Association (the 
“Association”).  We have to advise you that both for accounting reasons and to ensure 
consistency with the original agreement, we have to amend the table in Section IV. A. 1 of 
Schedule 2 to the Agreement to read as shown in the annex to this letter.   
 
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing, on behalf of the Borrower, by 
countersigning and dating the form of confirmation on the two original copies of this letter 
provided and returning one original, as countersigned, to us.
