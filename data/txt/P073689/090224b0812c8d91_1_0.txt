                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Further to the project restructuring in September 2010, the contract for the construction and rehabilitation of 3 bridges on the RN13 are being implemented in accordance with the
                         agreed planning.
                         The contract for the reconstruction of seven bridges on the RN44, the rural national road connecting the main rice growing area of Madagascar to the capital city, Antananarivo, and
                         the main port, Toamasina, has been awarded on January 7, 2012. Regarding the construction of jetties (in 5 lots), to provide for safe and smooth decking/mooring of the APL3-
