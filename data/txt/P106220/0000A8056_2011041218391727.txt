                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The overall objective of the Project is to promote youth empowerment and inclusion in development by expanding the capacities of, and opportunities for, youth
                         groups to initiate and participate in community and local development initiatives.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?
