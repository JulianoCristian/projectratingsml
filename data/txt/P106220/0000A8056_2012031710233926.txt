                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The overall objectives of the YDP are to promote youth empowerment and inclusion in development by expanding the capacities of, and opportunities for, youth groups
                         to initiate and participate in community and local development initiatives.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         To date, the project has provided grants for 280 small subprojects, identified and implemented by community youth groups, to finance livelihood activities, rehabilitate youth centers,
                         carry out social events, construct small-scale infrastructure, and provide life skills training. In addition, youth have benefited from the operationalization of four youth centers
                         managed by the Secretariat of State for Youth and Sport (SSYS) and supported with equipment and training provided by the project.
