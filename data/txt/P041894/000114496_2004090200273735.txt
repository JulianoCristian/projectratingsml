Re: Ln. 4095-IND (Sumatra Junior Secondary Education Project) Amendment 
of the Loan 
Agreement
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Sumatra Junior Secondary Education Project) 
dated November 7, 1996  between the Republic of Indonesia and the International Bank 
for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), as amended.  We also refer to the 
Ministry of Finance’s letter dated September 6, 2000, requesting an amendment to the 
Loan Agreement.  We are pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, the Bank 
has agreed to amend paragraph of 1(c) of Schedule 7 to the Loan Agreement to read as 
follows:
“(c) the term “Authorized Allocation” means an amount equivalent to US$ 
14,000,000  
to be withdrawn from the Loan Account and deposited into the Special 
Account pursuant to 
paragraph 3 (a) of this schedule.”
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the 
enclosed copy of this letter and returning it to us.  This amendment will be effective 
as of the date of your confirmation.
With best regards, 
Sincerely,
