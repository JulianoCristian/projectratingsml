Re: 
Gaza Industrial Estate Project - TF 026048
Amendment to the Trust Fund Credit Agreement
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Trust Fund Credit Agreement for the Gaza Industrial Estate Project 
(TF026048) between the Palestine Liberation Organization (for the benefit of the 
Palestinian Authority) (the “Borrower”) and the International Development Association, 
acting as administrator of the Trust Fund for Gaza and West Bank (the “Administrator”), 
dated March 24, 1998, as amended (the “Credit Agreement”). We also refer to the letter 
from H.E. Mr. Mohammed Nashashibi, the former Minister of Finance of the Palestinian 
Authority and authorized representative of the Borrower, dated June 1, 2002, requesting 
the Administrator to reallocate the proceeds of the Credit to assist in financing emergency 
damage repair and services support activities in the West Bank and Gaza. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Administrator has agreed to the above 
request. Accordingly, the Credit Agreement is hereby amended as follows: 
 
1. 
In Section 3.01 (a), the word “and” in the fourth line shall be deleted and shall be 
replaced by a comma, and the words “and Z” shall be inserted between the words “C” 
and “of”. 
 
2. 
All references to Parts A and C of the Project shall be deemed to refer to Parts A, 
C
and Z of the Project. 
 
3. 
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Credit Agreement shall read as 
follows:

In Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement, a new Part Z shall be added and shall read as 
follows:  
 
“Part Z
:
Damage Repair and Emergency Service Support Activities
Carrying out a program, consisting of the provision of goods and the execution of

mitigating the decline in essential public service provision, including electricity, water, 
wastewater, solid waste and social services.”
