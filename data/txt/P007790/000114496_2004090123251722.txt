          Re:  TOGO - Credit No. 2018 TO
               (Pre-Investment Project)
               Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
     Excellency:
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement)
dated  June  29, 1989, between the Republic of Togo and the
International Development Association (the Association).
     Pursuant to the Borrower's request dated January 14, 1992, we
propose to amend the Development Credit Agreement as follows:
1.   The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule I to the
Agreement shall be amended to read as follows:
                         Amount of the
                         Credit Allocated    % of
                         (Expressed in       Expenditures
 Category                SDR Equivalent)     to be Financed
(1)Consultants' services:
   (a) for Parts A and B12,290,000           85%
       of the Project
   (b) for Part B2 of the1,000,000           100%
       Project
(2)Training                210,000           100%
(3)Unallocated             400,000
   TOTAL                 3,900,000
2.   Part B of Schedule 2 to the Agreement shall be amended to read
as  follows:
     "Part B - Sectoral Studies
     1.  Execution of Sectoral Studies to evaluate the results of
         investments already implemented and identify future
         investments needed in specific sectors of the Borrower's
         economy.
     2.  Execution of studies to identify and prepare priority
         projects addressing key environmental and poverty
         alleviation issues."
     The amendment shall come into force retroactively as from 14
January 1992. Please indicate your agreement to the foregoing by
signing and dating the form of confirmation below on one of the
duplicate originals and returning it to us for our records.
