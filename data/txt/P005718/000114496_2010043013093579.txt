                    Re:  Loan No. 2962 TUN
                         (Structural Adjustment Loan)
                         Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
         We refer to the Loan Agreement between the Republic of
Tunisia (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction
and Development (the Bank), dated September 26, 1988, for the
above-mentioned Loan and to your request for an amendment expressed
during the Bank supervision mission of March 1989.
      The Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
I.    A new Section 3.04. is added to read as follows:
     "Section 3.04.  The Borrower shall not later than July 31,
     1989, submit to its Assembly of Representatives a draft law on
     direct taxation satisfactory to the Bank.
II.  Previous paragraph (c) in Section 5.01 is deleted and
     paragraph (d) becomes paragraph (c).
     Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing
and dating the form of confirmation below on the enclosed copy of
this letter, and returning it to us.
