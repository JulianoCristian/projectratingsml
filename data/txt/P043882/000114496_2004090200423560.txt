Dear Mr. Minister: 
 
Romania: Loan No. 4533-RO - Agricultural Support 
Services Project 
 Loan Agreement  Reallocation of Loan Proceeds
Pursuant to your letter dated July 14, 2003, requesting  the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) to reallocate the  proceeds of the loan   set 
forth in the Loan Agreement between the Bank and Romania dated March 27, 2000 (the 
Loan Agreement), we are pleased to concur with your request.  Therefore, the Bank 
hereby reallocates the proceeds of the loan  set  forth in the table in paragraph 1 of 
Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement as follows:  US$100,000 from Category 1 to Category 
2. 
 
Accordingly, the amounts now allocated to the  Categories are as follows:  
Category 1, US$ 7,900,000;  Category 2, US$ 200,000;  Category 3, US$ 1,280,000;  
Category 4, US$ 80,000;  Category 5, US$ 380,000;  Category 6, US$ 1,160,000;  The 
revised table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is attached as annex to 
this letter.  
Sincerely, 
 
/s/  Andrew N.Vorkink
