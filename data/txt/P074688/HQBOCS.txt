Excellency:
We refer to the Financing Agreement (Amending and Restating Development Credit
Agreement) ("Agreement") dated July 9, 2009 between the Socialist Republic of Vietnam
("Recipient") and the International Development Association ("Association"), for the Second Rural
Energy Project ("Project"). We further refer to: (a) a letter dated November 1, 2013 from Mr. Le
Minh Hung, Deputy Governor of State Bank of Vietnam, on behalf of the Recipient, requesting
certain modifications to the Agreement and disbursement arrangements, namely the use of Project
proceeds to support the rehabilitation and expansion of the low voltage grid networks under Part B
of the Project which is being implemented by Northern Power Corporation (NPC) under the
Vietnam Electricity, and the increase of the ceiling of the Designated Account of the NPC; and (b)
a letter dated January 14, 2014 from Mr. Do Viet Hung, Deputy Director General, International
Cooperation Department of State Bank of Vietnam, requesting the retroactive application of the
modifications to the Agreement as of March 1, 2012. We also refer to relevant discussions between
the Association and the Recipient's Ministry of Industry and Trade regarding the necessity of
increasing said Designated Account ceiling, and the revised Disbursement Letter dated November
21, 2013 facilitating the concurrence reached at these discussions.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to amend Part B of Schedule 1 to
the Agreement as follows:
"PART B:      Medium Voltage and Low Voltage Systems in the Northern Region
Rehabilitate and expand the MV and LV electric power distribution grid in Project
communes in the Northern Region, through the provision of equipment for and
construction of MV and LV lines and substations."
Except as specifically amended herein, all other provisions of the Agreement remain in full
force and effect.
