﻿AMENDING DEVELOPMENT CREDIT AGREEMENT
DATED OCTOBER 9, 1970 AS AMENDED
BETWEEN UNITED REPUBLIC OF TANZANIA
AND INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
AGREEMENT, dated January 10, 1973, between UNITED REPUBLIC
of TANZANIA (hereinafter called the Borrower) and INTERNATIONAL
DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (hereinafter called the Association).
(A) WHEREAS by an agreement dated October 9, 1970, between the
Borrower and the Association as amended by an agreement between
the Borrower and the Association dated April 28, 1971 (hereinafter
called the Development Credit Agreement), the Association agreed to
assist in the financing of a flue-cured tobacco development project of
the Borrower by making available to the Borrower a development
credit (hereinafter called the Credit) in an amount in various cur-
rencies equivalent to nine million dollars ($9,000,000);
(B) WHEREAS by an agreement (hereinafter called the Project
Agreement) dated October 9, 1970 between the Association, Tanzania
Rural Development Bank (hereinafter called TRDB) (as successor to
the National Development Credit Agency), the Tanganyika Tobacco
Board (hereinafter called TTB), and Tanzania Tobacco Processing
Company Limited (hereinafter called TTPC), TRDB, TTB and TTPC,
in consideration of the Association's entering into the Development
Credit Agreement, undertook certain obligations to the Association in
respect of the carrying out of the above-mentioned Project;
(C) WHEREAS in consequence of the reorganization of certain public
corporations of the Borrower the Tobacco Authority of Tanzania
(hereinafter called TAT) lias been established by the Tobacco Indus-
try Act 1972 for the purpose inter alia of promoting the development
of the tobacco industry in Tanzania;
(D) WHEREAs by the same Act the Borrower has made provision
for the transfer of the rights and liabilities of TTB and TTPC under
the Project Agreement to TAT;


﻿4
(E) WHEREAS the Association concurs in the foregoing arrange-
ments; and
(F) WHEREAS the Borrower and the Association have, in view of the
foregoing, agreed to amend the provisions of the Development Credit
Agreement as hereinafter provided;
Now THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
Section 1. Wherever used in this Agreement, unless the context
shall otherwise require and subject to the amendments made herein,
the several terms used in the Development Credit Agreement shall
have the respective meanings therein set forth.
Section 2. The definition of the term  4Project Agreement" set
forth in Section 1.02 of the Development Credit Agreement is amended
to read as follows:
"The term "Project Agreement" means the agreement dated
October 9, 1970 between the Association, the Tanzania Rural Devel-
opment Bank (hereinafter called TRDB) (as successor to NDCA
by virtue of Public Corporations (Transfer of Assets and Liabili-
ties) (Tanzania Rural Development Bank) Order, 1971), the
Tobacco Authority of Tanzania (hereinafter called TAT) (as
successor to TTB by virtue of the Tobacco Industry Act 1972 and
to TTPC by virtue of Companies (Transfer of Assets and Liabil-
ities) (Tanzania Tobacco Processing Company Limited) Order,
1972) as the same may be amended or supplemented from time
to time by agreement between the Borrower, the Association,
TRDB and TAT."
Section 3. Sections 4.01, 4.10, 4.11 and 5.02 of the Development
Credit Agreement are amended by deleting the term    "TTB" and
substituting the term "TAT" for the term "TTPO".
Section 4. Schedule 2 to the Development Credit Agreement is
amended by
(a) deleting from Part A(7) the clause "within the Borrower's Min-
istry of Agriculture, Food and Cooperatives", and


﻿5
(b) by substituting in Part B(3) the term "TAT" for the term
"TTB ".
Section 5. Section 5.02(c) of the Development Credit Agreement
is amended by
(a) substituting the term "Tobacco Industry Act, 1972" for the
terms "the Agricultural Products (Control and Marketing) Act 1962,
the Agricultural Products (Tanganyika Tobacco Board) (Amendment)
Order 1965", and
(b) deleting the clause "or the Memorandum and Articles of Asso-
ciation of Tanzania Tobacco Processing Company Limited of October
19, 1966, ".
Section 6. This Agreement shall come into force and effect on the
date on which Parts III, IV, V and VI of the Tobacco Industry Act,
1972 shall come into operation.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto acting through their repre-
sentatives duly authorized, have caused this Agreement to be signed
in their respective names and delivered in the District of Columbia,
United States of America, as of the day and year first above written.
