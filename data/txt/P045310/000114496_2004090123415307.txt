TF024088 RELATED TO CREDIT NUMBER 2904 BOS
SWISS GRANT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated September 29,1997, between BOSNIA AND HERZEGOVINA (the 
Recipient) and INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (the Association) acting as 
Administrator (the Administrator) of grant funds provided by the Government of the 
Swiss Confederation (Switzerland).
WHEREAS (A) pursuant to the Development Credit Agreement between the Recipient 
and the Association dated July 31, 1996 (the IDA Credit Agreement) the Association 
made a credit to the Recipient in an amount in various currencies equivalent to seven 
million Special Drawing Rights (SDR 7,000,000) to assist in the financing of the 
Emergency Public Works and Employment Project described in Schedule 2 to the IDA 
Credit Agreement (the Project);
(B)
pursuant to a letter of agreement dated June 30, 1997, between 
Switzerland and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) 
and the Association, Switzerland has requested the Bank and the Administrator to 
administer a trust fund to be made available by Switzerland in the form of a grant for 
the benefit of the Recipient in the amount of four hundred ninety thousand US dollars 
($490,000) for supplemental financing of the Project; and
(C)
the Recipient has requested of the Administrator, and the Administrator 
has agreed, that the Administrator provide to the Recipient, out of the proceeds of 
such grant, funds additional to those provided under the IDA Credit Agreement, for the 
carrying out of the Project (the Supplemental Grant), under the terms and conditions 
Page  2
set forth in this Agreement;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:

ARTICLE II
The Supplemental Grant
Section 2.01. The Administrator agrees to make available to the Recipient, on 
the terms and conditions set forth or referred to in this Agreement, the Supplemental 
Grant in an amount of four hundred ninety thousand US dollars ($490,000) to be made 
available to the Federation, Republika Srpska, FETF and RETF for the purposes of 
carrying out the Project.
Section 2.02. (a) The amount of the Supplemental Grant may be withdrawn from the 
Supplemental Grant Account in accordance with the provisions of Schedule 1 to the IDA 
Credit Agreement, as modified by the Schedule to this Agreement and as such Schedule 
may be amended from time to time by agreement between the Recipient and the 
Administrator, for expenditures made (or, if the Administrator shall so agree, to be 
made) in respect of the reasonable cost of goods, works and services required for the 
Project and to be financed out of the proceeds of the Supplemental Grant.
(b)
The Recipient may, for the purposes of the Project, open and maintain in 
Dollars two separate special deposit accounts (Special Accounts E and F) in a 
commercial bank on the terms and conditions set forth in paragraph (b) of Section 2.02 
of the IDA Credit Agreement and in the Schedules referred to in such Section, except 
that for the purposes of this Agreement, "Eligible Categories" shall mean, Categories 
(A) and (B) set forth in the Schedule to this Agreement and "Authorized Allocation" 
shall mean amounts equivalent to 150,000 and 50,000 respectively.
Section 2.03. Except as the Administrator shall otherwise agree, good, works and 
services to be financed under this Agreement shall be procured in accordance with the 
provisions of Schedule 3 to the IDA Credit Agreement.
Section 2.04. The Closing Date shall be December 31, 1997 or such later date as 
Page  4
the Administrator shall establish.  The Administrator shall promptly notify the 
Recipient of such later date.
ARTICLE III
Execution of the Project
Section 3.01. Except as otherwise expressly provided in this Agreement or where 
the context otherwise requires, Articles III, IV and V of the IDA Credit Agreement, 
Schedule 1 thereto, as modified by the Schedule to this Agreement, and Schedules 2 
through 5 to the IDA Credit Agreement are hereby incorporated into this Agreement with 
the same force and effect as if they were fully set forth herein, it being understood 
that:
(a)
all references in said Articles to the "Association" shall be deemed to 
be references to the Administrator under this Agreement;
(b)
all references to the "Borrower" shall be deemed to be references to the 
Recipient; and 
(c)
all references to the "Credit" and the "Credit Account" shall be deemed 
to be references to the Supplemental Grant and the Grant Account, respectively.
ARTICLE IV
Effectiveness; Termination
Section 4.01. The following events are specified as additional conditions to the 
effectiveness of this Agreement: 
(a)
the Supplemental Subsidiary Financing Agreements, pursuant to which the 
proceeds of the Supplemental Grant shall be made available to FETF through the 
Federation, and RETF through Republika Srpska, respectively, shall have been executed 
with terms and conditions satisfactory to the Administrator; and
(b)
 the Administrator shall have received a legal opinion, satisfactory to 
the Administrator, that this Agreement has been duly authorized by, and executed and 
delivered on behalf of, the Recipient and is legally binding upon the Recipient on 
accordance with its terms.
Section 4.02. This Agreement shall continue in effect until the Supplemental 
Grant has been fully disbursed and the parties to this Agreement have fulfilled all 
their obligations hereunder.
