Mr. Guillermo Perry Rubio
Minister of Finance and Public Credit
Ministry of Finance and Public Credit
Bogota, Colombia
Re:
Loan 3871-CO (Agricultural Technology
Development Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
In connection with the Loan Agreement dated June 16, 1995 between Republic of 
Colombia (the Borrower) and the Bank for the above Project (the Loan Agreement), we 
are pleased to propose, on the basis of your request, the following changes thereto:
(a)
Paragraph (b) of Section 1.02 of the Loan Agreement is hereby proposed to 
be amended so as to read as follows:
"(b) 'Coordinating Unit' means the unit or subcommittee established 
within the 
Ministry (as defined below) for purposes of Part B.1 (a) of the 
Project pursuant to the 
provisions of: (i) the Ministry's Resolution 476 of 
1994 dated August 4, 1994; and (ii) the 
Ministry's Resolution 812, dated 
December 1994, as amended by the Ministry's Resolution 156, 
dated May 11, 
1995;".
(b)
Paragraph (d) of Section 1.02 of the Loan Agreement is hereby proposed to 
be amended by adding thereto before the phrase 'and a professional expert' (in the 
ninth line of said paragraph), the following phrase: "the Director of Sectoral Policy 
of the Ministry's Vice Ministry of Policy Coordination,".
(c)
Paragraph (h) of Section 1.02 of the Loan Agreement is hereby proposed to 
be amended so as to read as follows:
"(h)  'Panel' means the consultative group of experts established for 
purposes of 
Part B.1 (b) of the Project pursuant to: (i) the provisions of the 
Ministry's Resolution No.  632, dated August 11, 1993, as amended by the Ministry's 
Resolution No. 214 dated June 22, 
1995; and (ii) the terms of the letter to the 
Bank of the Borrower's Vice Minister of 
Agriculture dated November 4, 
1993;".
(d)
the beginning of paragraph (k) of Section 1.02 of the Loan Agreement is 
hereby proposed to be amended so as to read as follows: "(k) 'Regional Panel' means a 
panel to be established in accordance with the provisions of the Resolutions referred 
to in paragraph (h) of this Section and pursuant to the provisions of Section 
3.04...etc. etc.".
(e)
Paragraph (e) of Section 3.01 of the Loan Agreement is hereby proposed to 
be amended so as to read as follows:
"(e)   For purposes of the carrying out of Approved Research Subprojects 
under Part A  of the Project, and such other activities under Parts B. 2 (a), B.4, B.5 
and B.6 of the 
Project as the Borrower (through the Minister or such person 
or persons as the Minister 
shall appoint) and the Bank shall from time to 
time agree, the Borrower may seek the 
assistance of one or more companies 
acceptable to the Bank (including fiduciary companies 
and services management 
companies, or both), which shall provide their services to the 
Borrower 
under one or more contracts satisfactory to the Bank in connection with: (A) the 
management of funds (other than Loan proceeds except when paid to the company in 
question as 
a down payment to initiate provision of  their services) for the Approved 
Research 
Subproject or Subprojects in question and for funding services of 
the applicable Regional 
Unit under the other Parts of the Project referred to above; 
and (B) the payments to be made 
by the Participating Entity or Entities in question 
for the acquisition of goods or services  
(including works) under such Subproject 
Page  2
or Subprojects or for the provision of goods or 
services (including works) 
to the Regional Unit in question. To that end, the Borrower 
through the 
Ministry, shall ensure that any such company, after having been accepted by the 
Bank pursuant to the preceding provisions of this paragraph shall, if warranted,  
become a 
party also (or alternatively, as the case may be) to the contractual 
arrangements referred 
to in paragraph (d) (ii) of this Section. In any event, it 
is hereby further agreed that the 
Borrower shall not take or concur in any 
action which would have the effect of amending, 
abrogating, assigning or 
waiving any of such contractual arrangements or contracts, as the  case may be, or any 
provision thereof.".
If you are in agreement with the amendment proposals stated in paragraphs (a) 
through (e) above, please signify so on behalf of the Borrower by dating and 
countersigning the attached copy of this letter, and by returning said copy duly 
signed for our records. Upon receipt by us of said copy, the preceding amendments 
shall become effective retroactively to November 17, 1995.
