Dear Sirs
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Credit Agreement), 
for the above-referenced project, entered into between the Republic of Bolivia 
(the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association), 
dated July 31, 1987, and to the Project Agreement entered into between Empress 
Nacional de Electricidad S.A. (ENDE) and the Association, of even date therewith.
I am pleased to advise you that the Association is in agreement with your 
request, pursuant to the letter from ENDE to the Association dated September 27, 
1988, to amend the Credit Agreement as follows:
(i)
the table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the 
Credit Agreement is deleted and replaced in its entirety to 
read as per Attachment 1 hereto.
(ii)
Part b of Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement is amended by 
deleting paragraphs 3, 4 and 5 and adding the following new 
paragraphs 3, 4 and 5 instead:
3.
Modification and addition of transformers in Parque 
Industrial, Urbana Norte and Arocagua substations in order to 
increase transformer capacity and addition of switching equipment 
in the Cochabamba substation.
4.
Addition of one transformer in the Warnes (Santa Cruz) 
substation in order to increase transmission capacity.
5.
Construction of transmission line 1 @ s between Huaracachi, 
Urbana Sur and Urbana Oeste substations, in order to close the 
Santa Cruz subtransmission loop.
(iii)
Part C in Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement is amended by 
deleting paragraph 2 (c) and adding the following new 
paragraph 3 instead:
3.
Acquisition of software and hardware required by ENDE for 
future review of the Power Expansion Master Plan and for partial 
implementation of the integrated Management Information System.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing the 
attached duplicate original of this letter and returning each to us duly dated.  
A signed original of this letter is enclosed for each of your records.
Sincerely yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
