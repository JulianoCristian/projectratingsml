Amendment of the Develooment Credit Agreement
Dear Mr. Majid:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated February 19, 1988 between the 
People's Republic of Bangladesh (the Borrower) and the International Development 
Association (the Association) for the Second Small Scale Flood Control, Drainage and 
Irrigation Project. We also refer to the earlier discussions with the Economic 
Relations Division concerning the proposal that proceeds of the captioned Credit be 
utilized to finance certain urgently needed rehabilitation and reconstruction of 
certain coastal embankments and related structures damaged during the cyclone that hit 
Bangladesh in April, 1991 and for technical assistance services for Part A of the 
Project, and to the letter of July 31, 1994 from Assistant Secretary Mr. Md. 
Nasiruddin requesting that IDA proceed with the reallocation and amendments for this 
purpose.
We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to the above request, the Association 
has agreed to amend the Development Credit Agreement as set out below:
A.
Schedule 2 to the Development Credit Agreement
(1)
At the end of the first paragraph of the Preamble, the following words 
are inserted "and the April 1991 cyclone".
(2)
At the end of Part D, para 1 the following words are inserted "damaged 
during the 1987 and 1988 floods."
(3)
The following is added as a new Part E to the said Schedule 2:
Part E:
Rehabilitation and reconstruction of coastal embankments and related structures 
damaged by the cyclone of April, 1991, as well as aforestation of foreshore areas in 
front of such embankments, in and around Sandwip, Island and the coastal areas of 
Chittagong and Cox's Bazar Districts.
B.

B.
Preference for Domestic Contractors
In the procurement of works in accordance with the procedures described 
in Part A(l) hereof, the Borrower may grant a margin of preference to domestic 
contractors in accordance with, and subject to, the provisions of paragraphs 
2.55 and 2.56 of the Guidelines and paragraph 5 of Appendix 2 thereto.

amended by inserting the following words immediately before the words "Civil Works" in 
the first line of said paragraph: "Except for works under Part E of the Project".

hereinabove, of said Schedule 3:
4. Works for aforestation of coastal embankments or foreshore areas in 
front of coastal embankments under Part E of the Project, up to an aggregate 
amount not exceeding SDR 500,000 equivalent, may be carried out through force 
account.

(1)

1994, to assist the Borrower in carrying out Parts A and C2 of the Project" are 
inserted.
H.
Schedule 4 to the Development Credit Agreemen
(1)
The following heading is inserted before para. I "A. Applicable to Parts 
A. B. C and D of the Project".
(2)
The following is added following paragraph 7:
"B. Applicable to Part E of the Project
1. Works to be undertaken under Part E of the Project shall be selected 
in accordance with criteria and procedures agreed between the Borrower 
and the Association.
2. Notwithstanding any other provision of this Agreement, Part E of the 
Project shall be carried out by BWDB in accordance with administrative 
arrangements and procedures satisfactory to the Association.

in the table set out in paragraph I of Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement

Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the 
enclosed form of confirmation and returning it to us. Subject to your confirmation as 
indicated above, this amendment win be effective as of the date of this letter.
