BANGLADESH - Second Small Scale Flood Control,

   (Credit 1870-BD) Amendment to the     
 Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Second Small Scale Flood 
Control, Drainage and Irrigation Project), dated February 19, 1988, between 
the People's Republic of Bangladesh (the Borrower) and the International 
Development Association (the Association), as amended. We also refer to our 
Ministry's letter of October 11, 1989, requesting the Association for an 
amendment of the Development Credit Agreement in order to provide financial 
assiAtance to the completion of the River Research Institute at Faridpur 
(RRI), so local funds no longer needed for the Institute could be made 
available to the irrigation projects financed by the Association.
The Association is pleased to inform you that it agrees to finance the 
remaining civil works for the Institute. Therefore, the Development Credit 
Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
1.
A new paragraph (d) is added to Section 3.01 of the Development
Credit Agreement to read as follows:
"(d) Without limitation upon the provisions of paragraph (a) 
of this Section, the Borrower shall reallocate to the flood control, 
drainage and irrigation projects financed by the Association: (i) the local 
funds allocated in its Annual Development Program for FY 1990 to RRI, and 
(ii) an equal amount of local funds, in real terms, 
for FY 1991."
2.
A new paragraph 5 is added to Part D of Schedule 2 to the Development
Credit Agreement to read as follows:
"Completion of the River Research Institute at Faridpur."
All the provisions of the Development Credit Agreement, as amended 
hereby, shall remain in full force and effect. This amendment will come into 
force and effect upon signature by both parties and upon receipt by the 
Amendment of Development Credit Agreement dated February 19, 1988 for the 
Second Small Scale Flood Control, Drainaize and Irrization. Project (Credit 
1870-BD)
