Dear Mr. Davaasambuu:
             Re:  Credit No. 2321-MOG (Technical Assistance Project)
                  Amendment of the Development Credit Agreement
         We  refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Technical
Assistance Project) between Mongolia (the Borrower) and the
International Development Association (the Association) dated
December 27, 1991.  We refer also to the Aide Memoire dated April
10, 1993 between the IDA/UNDP mission and the Minister of Finance of
the Borrower,  and subsequent communications,  requesting the
Association to increase the amount of Authorized Allocation of the
Special Account from $10,000 to $30000 and authorizing you to
countersign a letter in this regard.
         The Association is pleased to inform you that, after due
consideration, it agrees to the above request.  Therefore, the
amount of "$10,000" indicated in paragraph 1(c) of Schedule 4 to
the Development Credit Agreement is substituted by "$30,000".
         Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment
by signing and dating the enclosod form of confirmation on behalf of
tho Borrower and by returning it to us for our rocord.  This
amendment will become effective as of the date hereof upon receipt
of your confirmation in this regard.
                                Sincerely yours,
                                David Pearce
                                Chief

                                Country Department 2
