Secondary Education Improvement Project (Loan 3941-PY)
Third Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the Republic of Paraguay (the Borrower), 
dated September 18, 1995 (the Loan Agreement), as amended.  Pursuant to your letters 
of request dated April 13, 2000 and April 14, 2000, we are pleased to inform you that 
the Bank hereby agrees to amend the table set forth in paragraph 1 Schedule 1 to the 
Agreement to read in its entirety as per the Annex to this amendment letter.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter may be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us.  The provisions of this amendment letter 
will become effective upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this 
amendment letter; and shall be applied retroactively as of December 31, 1999.
Very truly yours,
