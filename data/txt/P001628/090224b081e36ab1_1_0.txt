﻿AGREEMENT AMENDING
AFRICAN FACILITY CREDIT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated                        /3, 1987, between
REPUBLIC OF KALAWI (the Borrower) and INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT
ASSOCIATION as ADMINISTRATOR of the SPECIAL FACILITY FOR
SUB-SAHARAN AFRICA (the Administrator).
WHEREAS: (A) the Borrower and the Administrator have entered
into an African Facility Credit Agreement (Third Structural
Adjustment Program), dated December 20, 1985 (the African
Facility Credit Agreement), in an amount of thirty-seven million
four hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 37,400,000)
for the purpose of assisting in the financing of the program
(hereinafter called the Program) described in a letter, dated
November 1, 1.985, from the Borrower to the Administrator and
referred to in the Preamble to the African Facility Credit
Agreement;
(B) the Administrator has received a letter, dated
December 24, 1986, from the Borrower, whereby the Borrower has
requested supplemental assistance in support of the Program;
(C) the Borrower intends to receive additional financing
for the Program from Japan, under the Special Joint Financing
Agreement for the Special African Facility, in the form of a
grant in an amount of five hundred million yen (Yen 500,000,000)
and a loan in an amount of two billion two hundred million
yen (Yen 2,200,000,000);
(D) the Borrower intends to contract from Kreditanstalt
flr Wiederaufbau (hereinafter called KFW) a financial contri-
bution from the Federal Republic of Germany, under the Special
Joint Financing Agreement for the Special African Facility (the
KFW Supplemental Financial Contribution), in an amount equivalent
to ten million deutsche mark (DM 10,000,000) in support of the
Program on the terms and conditions set forth in an agreement
(the Supplemental KFW Agreement) to be entered into between the
Borrower and KFW;


﻿-2-
(E) tho Borrower intends to contract from the Overseas
Development Administration (hercinafter called ODA) a finan-
cial contribution from the United Kingdom, under the Special
Joint Financing Agreement for the Special African Facility (the
ODA Financial Contribution), in an amount equivalent to five
million pounds sterling (E5,000,000) in support of the Program on
the terms and conditions set forth in an agreement (the ODA
Agreement) to be entered into between the Borrower and ODA; and
WHEREAS the Administrator has agreed on the basis, inter
alia, of the foregoing, to provide such additional assistance to
the Borrower upon the terms and conditions set forth in this
Agreement;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
Amendments to the African Facility
Credit Agreement
Section 1.01.   Section 1.02 of the African Facility Credit
Agreement is amended by adding a new Subsection (g) and a new
Subsection (h) as follows:
"(g) "Amending Agreement" means the Agreement amending the
African Facility Credit Agreement (Third Structural Adjustment
Program) between the Borrower and the Administrator, dated
1987; and
(h) "Program" means the program of actions, objectives and
policies  designed   to  achieve  structural  adjustment of   the
Borrower's economy described in the Borrower's letter to the
Administrator, dated November 1, 1985, and includes the supple-
mental actions, objectives and policies designed to achieve
agricultural marketing   and  pricing  reform  described  in  the
Borrower's letter to the Administrator, dated December 24, 1986."
Section 1.02.   Section 2.01 of the African Facility Credit
Agreement is amended to read as follows:


﻿-3-
"Section 2.01.   The Administrator agrees to lend to the
Borrower, on the terms and conditions set forth or referred to
in the African Facility Credit Agreement, an amount in various
currencies equivalent to forty-five million seven hundred
thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 45,700,000) in two tranches,
the first tranche being an amount in various currencies
equivalent to thirty-seven million four hundred thousand Special
Drawing Rights (SDR 37,400,000) (the First Tranche), and the
second tranche being an amount in various currencies equivalent
to eight million three hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights
(SDR 8,300,000) (the Second Tranche)."
Section 1.03.   Section 2.02 of the African Facility Credit
Agreement is amended by adding a new paragraph (d) as follows:
"(d) Except as the Borrower and the Administrator shall
otherwise agree, all amounts withdrawn from the African Facility
Credit Account or made subject to a special commitment pursuant
to Section 5.02 of the General Conditions shall initially be
charged against the First Tranche until that Tranche has been
exhausted, and shall thereafter be charged against the Second
Tranche."
Section 1.04.  A proviso is added at the end of paragraph
(a) of Section 2.04 reading as follows:
"; provided, however, that commitment charges in the Second
Tranche shall accrue from a date sixty days after the date of the
Amending Agreement."
ARTICLE II
Effective Date; Termination
Section 2.01.    This Amending Agreement shall not become
effective until evidence satisfactory to the Administrator shall
have been furnished to the Administrator that the execution and
delivery of this Amending Agreement on behalf of the Borrower
have  been   duly  authorized  or   ratified  by  all  necessary
governmental action.


﻿Section 2.02.    As part of the evidence to be furnished
pursuant to Section 2.01 of this Amending Agreement, there shall
be furnished to the Administrator an opinion or opinions ,
satisfactory to the Administrator, of counsel acceptable to the
Administrator showing, on behalf of the Borrower, that this
Amending Agreement has been duly authorized or ratified by, and
executed and delivered on behalf of, the Borrower and is legally
binding upon the Borrower in accordance with its terms.
Section 2.03. This Amending Agreement shall come into force
and effect on the date upon which the Administrator dispatches to
the Borrower notice of its acceptance of the evidence required by
Section 2.01 above.
Section 2.04.   If this Amending Agreement shall not have
come into force and effect by a date ninety (90) days after the
date of this Amending Agreement, this Amending Agreement and all
obligations of the parties hereunder shall terminate, unless the
Administrator establishes a later date for the purposes of this
Section. If this Amending Agreement shall terminate under the
provisions of this Section, the African Facility Credit Agreement
shall continue in full force and effect, as if this Amending
Agreement had not been executed.
