GUATEMALA:
Private Participation in Infrastructure Technical 
Assistance Loan (Loan No. 4149-GU) 
Amendment to Loan Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated March 3, 1998 entered into between the 
Republic of Guatemala (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank).
In response to your March 2,1998 request to make some minor amendment to the 
Loan Agreement, the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
Paragraph (k) of Section 1.02 is hereby amended to read as follows:
“(k)
‘Sectoral Ministries’ means the Borrower’s Ministry of 
Energy 
and Mines (Ministerio de Energía y Minas), the Ministry of 
Communications, 
Transport, Public Works and Housing (Ministerio de 
Comunicaciones, Transportes, 
Obras Públicas y Vivienda) or any other 
ministry acceptable to the Bank;”.
2.
The year “1997” referred to in Section 3.05 (a) is hereby replaced with 
the year “1998”.
3.
The date “January 1998” referred to in Section 3.06 is hereby replaced 
with the date “July 1998”.
4.
The date “February 1998” referred to in Section 3.08 is hereby replaced 
with the date “February 1999”.
5.
The date “January 13,1997 ” referred to in paragraph 3(a) of Schedule 1 
is hereby replaced with the date “March 4, 1997”.
This Amendment Letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall 
be an original.  Upon receipt by the Bank of one fully executed original of this 
Amendment Letter, these amendments will be effective as of the date first above 
written. Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and 
dating the two counterparts of this Amendment Letter.
Sincerely,
