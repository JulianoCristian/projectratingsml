Credit No. 3320 KH & GEF Trust Fund Grant No.23524 KH 
(Biodiversity and Protected Areas Project) Representations on 
Project Counterpart Funding     
Dear Sirs:
We are pleased to refer to the Development Credit Agreement of even date 
herewith between Kingdom of Cambodia and International Development Association (the 
Association), and the GEF Trust Fund Grant Agreement of even date herewith between 
Kingdom of Cambodia and International Bank for Reconstruction and Development acting 
as Implementing Agency of the Global Environment Facility (the Bank) for the 
above-mentioned Project.
Without any restriction or limitation upon Section 3.01 of the Development 
Credit Agreement, Section 3.01 of the GEF Trust Fund Grant Agreement, or any other 
provision of the Development Credit or GEF Trust Fund Grant Agreement, Kingdom of 
Cambodia hereby undertakes and warrants to the Association and the Bank that, in 
respect of each of the fiscal years included in the implementation period of the 
Project, it shall make available, from its own national budgetary resources, 
counterpart funds as follows, or in such other amounts as may be agreed from time to 
time with the Association and the Bank: 
(a) 
Fiscal Year 2000:
US$ 60,000 equivalent;
(b) 
Fiscal Year 2001:
US$ 60,000 equivalent;
(c) 
Fiscal Year 2002:
US$ 65,000 equivalent;
(d) 
Fiscal Year 2003:
US$ 65,000 equivalent;
It is our understanding that, in making the Credit and the Grant to Kingdom of 
Cambodia, and entering into the Development Credit and GEF Trust Fund Grant 
Agreements, the Association and the Bank may rely on the representations contained 
herein.
Very truly yours,
KINGDOM OF CAMBODIA
By /s/ Roland Eng
Authorized Representative
