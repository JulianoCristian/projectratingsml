                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This is the first supervision mission that allows us to substantially track progress since the project was restructured in November2009. Some good progress has been made as


                                                                                                                                                                                                            Page 1 of 5
                             The World Bank                                                                                                                                         Report No: ISR1276



                         follows:
                         (i) Quantitative pay and grade targets have been exceeded with 80% of positions regraded to date. In recent months the quality of re-grading has also improved;
Public Disclosure Copy




                         (ii) HR foundation training in approved HR policies is being provided systematically across government;
                         (iii) HR departments have been established and are well staffed in all 5 ministries. Ministries of Agriculture, Rural Development and Rehabilitation and Finance are implementing new
                         HR policies more effectively;
                         (iv) Improved coordination has been established with the USAID funded ACSS project to ensure complimentarity;
                         (v) Senior management and technical capacity of counterparts, particularly in the CSMD, has increased significantly.

                         Outstanding issues include: strengthening impementation arrangements, improving senior appointments process and IARCSC's monitoring ministry compliance with HR policies.
