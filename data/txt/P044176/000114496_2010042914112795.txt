Re:   Biodiversity Conservation Management Project.
GEF Grant Number TF022499 - Amendment to Grant Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
We refer to the Grant-Agreement (the Grant-Agreement) between the International 
Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and Romania (the Recipient) for the 
above-mentioned Grant, dated June 17, 1999, and your letter received February 5, 2001, 
where you propose amendments to the same.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank agrees to make the requested amendments.  
Accordingly, the Grant-Agreement is proposed to be amended as set forth below:
1.
In Section 1.02, subparagraph (a) is amended to read as follows:
"(a)
'DNBC' means the Directorate for Nature and Biodiversity 
Conservation, Protected Areas and Natural Monuments within MWFEP;"
2.
In Section 1.02, a new subparagraph (e) is added to read as follows:
"(e)
'MAFF' means the Recipient’s Ministry of Agriculture, Food and 
Forestry, or any successor thereof;"
3.
In Section 1.02, existing subparagraphs (e) to (n) are re-lettered as (f) to (o) 
and 'MWFEP' is replaced with ", MAFF" in the re-lettered subparagraph (i).
4.
In Section 3.01.(a) , ", MAFF" is added after "through MWFEP".
5.
In paragraph 1 of Schedule 4 to the Grant Agreement, "Secretary of State of 
MWFEP" is replaced with "Secretary of State of Forestry of MAFF" and ", MAFF" is added 
after "and experts from MOF, MWFEP".
6.
In paragraph 2 of Schedule 4 to the Grant Agreement, "MWFEP" is replaced with 
"MAFF".
7.
In subparagraphs (a), (b) and (c) of paragraph 3 of Schedule 4 to the Grant 
Agreement, ", MAFF" is added after "through MWFEP". 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments on behalf of the 
Recipient, by signing, dating, and returning to us the enclosed copy of this Letter.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION
