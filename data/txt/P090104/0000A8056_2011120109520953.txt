                         Project Development Objectives
                         Nigeria National Energy Development Project (P090104)
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)
                         I.       To continue support for the Borrower#s energy sector reform and key regulatory and policy institutions; II. To increase efficiency in the power sector
                         and to improve the supply of electricity and service delivery; III. To demonstrate possible models to scale-up electricity access; and IV. To continue support
                         for the Borrower#s gas sector reform to improve gas supply for power generation.

                         NEDP overall implementation progress continues and the Project is progressing to be concluded on schedule by June 2012. Procurement is concluded for all but one contract. All
                         activities under the contract should be completed before the Project closing date.

                         Concluded activities include best-practice distribution investments such as metering and roll-out of HV distribution systems (completed in Abuja Suburbs Kubwa and Karu) leading to
Public Disclosure Copy




                         improved revenue recovery (meters), enhanced quality of supply, reduced electricity theft and lower losses. The project is also funding key TA support to BPE, notably the TCN
                         management contract bidding documents and Environmental audits of GENCO's up for privatization.
