                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The implementation of the INESTA Project has commenced, with the Government of Iraq (Prime Minister's Advisory Commission - PMAC) has now signed a contract with an
                         international strategy/energy Consultant that will support the Government of Iraq in the development of its Integrated National Energy Strategy.

                         Although the project has only been under implementation since January 2010, the Prime Minister's Advisory Commission has already demonstrated strong management of the
                         project. The PMAC quickly established a primary Project Management Team (PMT), and Steering Committee for the Project, and has worked effectively and efficiently with the Bank

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The Bank has agreed to support the Prime Minister's Advisory Commission in close monitoring of the Consultant's work to ensure that the quality of this work meets the requirements
                         and expectations of the assignment. This monitoring will involve monthly review of progress reports via VC, visits to Baghdad by the TTL and possibly other technical specialists to
                         monitor the Consultant's work, and ongoing daily communications with the PMT.
