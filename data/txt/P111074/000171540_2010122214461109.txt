Iraq: Additional Financing for 
Integrated National Energy Strategy Technical Assistance Project 
Amendment to the Trust Fund Grant Agreement 
–
TF095802
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Trust Fund Grant Agreement between the Government of the Republic of 
Iraq
 (the “Recipient”) and International Development Association, acting as administrator (the 
“Administrator”) of the World Bank Iraq Trust Fund for the Integrated National Energy Strategy 
Technical Assistance Project (the “Project) dated January 11, 2010 (the “Trust Fund Grant 
Agreement”). 
 
We also refer to you letter, dated March 15, 2010, requesting an additional financing in 
the amount of one million nine hundred thousand Dollars ($1,900,000) for the Project (Additional 
Financing). 
 
The Administrator hereby accedes to your request and amends the Trust Fund Grant 
Agreement as follows: 
 
1. 
Section 3.01 is amended to read: 
 
“3.01.  The World Bank agrees to extend to the Recipient, on terms and conditions set 
forth or referred to in this Agreement, a grant in an amount equal to six million 
nine hundred thousand Dollars ($6,900,000) (“Grant”) to assist in financing the 
Project.” 
 
2. 
The table in paragraph 2 of Part A of Section IV of Schedule 2, is amended to 
read:
