                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The INESTA Project is now close to producing a final report containing critical strategic recommendations for the development of Iraq's energy sector.

                         The Consultant (Booz and Co.) submitted the INESTA Draft Final Report on 9 December, 2011.

                         The Iraq Project Steering Committee (PSC), supported by the Bank team, met with the Consultant on December 14-16, 2011 to review the Draft Final Report. The Draft Final Report

                         was also distributed to all stakeholder Ministries, and independent comments received from each of these Ministries.

                         The Consultant is now addressing all comments received in a revised Draft Final Report, which is due for completion at the end of February, 2012.
