                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project is approaching its closing date (February 28, 2011) with all activities expected to be completed by then; disbursement of funds under the project is near 100%.
                         Despite some disbursement delays at the project start up, especially under the component implemented by the State Social Protection Fund, the project has made significant
                         progress towards achieving its development objective: "to significantly improve the effectiveness and transparency in the provision of public pensions and social assistance to eligible
                         beneficiaries". Efforts to transform the State Social Protection Fund (SSPF) into a modern, efficient and effective E-pension administration gave its fruits, Similarly, by introducing
