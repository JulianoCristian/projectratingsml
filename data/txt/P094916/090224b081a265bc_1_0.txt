                         Project Development Objectives
                         WAPP APL 2 - OMVS Felou Hydroelectric Project (P094916)
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)
                         The project development objectives are to (i) augment the supply of low cost hydroelectricity from the OMVS Power System to the national utilities of Mali (EDM),
                         Mauritania (SOMELEC) and Senegal (SENELEC); and (ii) develop a nucleus of a well-functioning, cooperative, power pooling mechanism for the West Africa Power Pool
                         (WAPP) Zone B Organisation Pour la Mise en Valeur du Fleuve Sénégal (OMVS) Countries of West Africa.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Program?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         A review of project implementation progress was conducted by the WB and EIB on December 5, 2012. Construction of the Felou hydropower plant is progressing well with full
                         commissioning expected for August 2013. SOGEM and its Owner's Engineer are closely supervising works execution, as well implementation of safeguards measures.
