       Kenya Forestry Development Project (Cr. 2198-KE) - Amendment to
                       Development Credit Agreement
     We refer to the above-mentioned Development Credit Agreement (the
Agreement), dated February 21, 1991, between the Republic of Kenya (the
Borrower) and International Development Association (the Association),
and to the letter from Mr. Shakaba, dated October 26, 1995, requesting
an amendment to the Credit Agreement to include local expenditures
under Categories 1 and 2 of the Project.
     This letter is to inform you of our agreement and to propose the
following amendment to the Agreement:
     Schedule 1 to the Agreement should be amended to read as follows
for Categories 1 and 2:
"

                     Credit Allocated         Expenditures
                       (Expressed in         to be Financed
(1)  Vehicles            2,230,000           100% of foreign

                                             of local expenditures

    equipment                                expenditures and 90%
                                             of local expenditures
     This amendment shall have effect in respect of all disbursement
applications submitted as of the date of this letter. All provisions
of the Agreement, except as amended herein, shall remain in full
force and effect.  Please confirm your agreement to the amendments by
countersigning, dating and returning the enclosed copy of this letter.
