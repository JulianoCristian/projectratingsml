     Re:  GHANA - Credit No. 1854 GH

                  Amendments to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Dr. Botchwey:
     We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Cocoa
Rehabilitation Project) dated February 18, 1988, between the
Republic of Ghana and the International Development Association (the
Association).
     In order to take into account the outcome of the donors,
meeting hold in Accra in October 1989 during which a revised
financing plan for the above-referenced Project was agreed upon
between the donors and the authorities in Ghana, the Association
agrees to your request in your letter of May 15, 1990, to amend the
Development Development Credit Agreement.
     Therefore, the table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to
the Development Credit Agreement shall be amended to read as set
forth in the Annex to this letter.
     Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing
the form of confirmation below on the enclosed copy and returning it
to us.  This amendment shall become effective upon receipt of the
signed copy by the Association.
                   Sincerely yours,
                     Edwin R. Lim
                       Director
               Western Africa Department
