August 21, 1989
His Excellency
Minister of Planning and Cooperation
Ministry of Planning and Cooperation
B.P. 7008
Ouagadougou, Burkina Faso
Re:
Credit No. 1896 BUR (Agricultural Research Project)
Amendment to the Credit Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the "Agreement") for the 
above-referenced Project between Burkina Faso (the "Borrower") and the International 
Development Association (the
"Association") dated May 23, 1988.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to amend the 
description of the Project contained in Schedule 2 in order to better cover the civil 
works program. Consequently, Part B of Schedule 2 of the Agreement is hereby amended 
by inserting after paragraph 4 the following:
"5.  Construction in Di, Farako-BA, Katchari, Kouare and Saria of nine 
laboratories, 
and of various buildings, offices, greenhouses and drying areas.
 
6.
Construction of water distribution systems in Di, Katchari, 
Kouard, Saria and 
of an extension reaching to Farako-BA.
 
7.
Rehabilitation of a laboratory, a store, a conference room and 
construction of 
three offices, a stable and a drying area in Niangoloko."
 
Please confirm your agreement by signing and dating the form of confirmation on 
the enclosed copy of this letter so that the amendment may become effective, and 
returning it to us.
Sincerely yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
By /s/ Jean-Francois Bauer
Acting Director, Sahelian Department
Africa Region

BURKINA FASO
