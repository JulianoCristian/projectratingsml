                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall, project performance is now downgraded to moderately satisfactory as service delivery performance of implementing partner NGOs and SM provinces has declined in terms
                         of quality of care and coverage of services. The causes include the unstable security situation and the lack of health professional resources which constrain the provision of and
                         access to services. The Ministry of Public Health (MOPH) and Implementing partners have analyzed these negative trends of service delivery performance and are taking steps in
