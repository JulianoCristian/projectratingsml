                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall project performance has been satisfactory with significant progress in expanding access to BPHS services, undertaking development of EPHS and urban BPHS proposals for
                         implementation and strengthening MOPH capacity to deliver health care and monitor progress, keeping in mind a fragile security situation and human resource challenges. Three
                         remaining contracts for BPHS services have been signed. Despite satisfactory implementation progress, results according to HMIS data are either stagnating or declining in 2010 as
                         compared to the previous years on key performance indicators both for NGO and Ministry of Public Health managed provinces. The trends seems to similar in remaining provinces
