                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This final implementation status report assesses the Project as Highly Satisfactory. 302 Community micro projects have been completed and project results have been
                         comprehensively documented and disseminated. The Project has established a robust reputation in Azerbaijan and outside in delivering results on the ground and for its significant
                         impact achieved in community mobilization, rural infrastructure upgrading and design stage planning and provisioning for infrastructure investment maintenance and sustainability.
                         Total disbursements stand at $14.69 million out of total Project funds of $15 million. The sectoral breakdown of the completed Community Projects comprises: roads – 64%; potable
                         water – 13%; electricity – 6%; irrigation – 5%; drainage – 5%; schools – 3%; health – 2% and community renovation - 2%.
