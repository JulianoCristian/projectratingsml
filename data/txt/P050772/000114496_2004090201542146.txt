Brazil:  Land-based Poverty Alleviation Project (Loan 7037-
BR) 
Amendment to the Loan Agreement 
 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement, dated July 16, 2001 between the Federative 
Republic of Brazil (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), and the Minister of Agrarian Development’s request of 
December 3, 2002, (Oficio/MDA/SE/No.241). 
 
We are pleased to concur with that request to amend the Loan Agreement, 
Schedules 1 and 2, as follows: 
 
(i) 
Schedule 1, Category (2) of the Disbursement Table
:
substitute “Consultants’ 
Services and Publicity and Training expenditures under Parts C and D of the Project” 
with “Consultants’ services, publicity and costs of training and strengthening of the 
STU’s capacity under Parts C and D of the Project”. 
 
(ii) 
Schedule 1, Category (3) of the Disbursement Table
:
substitute “Consultants’ 
services under Part E of the Project” with “Consultants’ services, data processing services 
and equipment, and costs of training and coordination activities under Part E of the 
Project”. 
 
(iii) 
Schedule 2, Part E of the Project
:
substitute “Project Evaluation and 
Dissemination at the Borrower’s Level” with “Project Evaluation, Dissemination, 
Training and Coordination at the Borrower’s Level”.

(iv) 
Schedule 2, Part E.3 of the Project
:
substitute “Support for the activities to be 
carried out under the Project by the NTU through the provision of consultants’ services 
and data processing services” with “Support for the activities to be carried out under the 
Project by the NTU through the provision of consultants’ services, data processing  
 
services and equipment, and training and financing of coordination and operational 
expenditures”. 
 
This amendment letter shall be executed in two counterparts, each of which shall 
be an original, and shall become effective on the date of this letter once the Bank has 
received one fully executed original of it.  Please confirm your agreement to the 
foregoing amendment by signing the two counterparts of this amendment letter.  Upon 
your agreement, please return one fully executed copy to us.
