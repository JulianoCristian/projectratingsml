                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The overall physical progress on July 31, 2011 was 97%, compared with 56% on October 31, 2010 (last mission). All road sections are substantially completed, lighting and minor
                         pedestrian lane works are remaining to be completed in August and September. Similarly the cumulative disbursement by end July reached 56% compared to 45% in the last
                         mission. The task team considers this to be a reasonable progress.
