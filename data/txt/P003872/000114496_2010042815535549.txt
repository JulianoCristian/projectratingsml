Re:   Loan 2944-IND (Higher Education Development Project)
      Amendment of the Loan Agreement
Dear Minister Sumarlin:
      We refer to the Loan Agreement (Higher Education Development
Project) between the Republic of Indonesia (the Borrower) and the
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank)
dated June 10, 1988.  We also refer to your Ministry's telex of July
27, 1989 requesting the Bank for an increase in the authorized
allocation of the Special Account under the above project.
      The Bank is pleased to inform you that it agrees to the above
request.  Therefore, the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as
follows:
      1.    The amount stated in paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 6 to
the Loan Agreement is amended to read "$25,000,000".
      Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by
signing and dating the enclosed form of confirmation, and returning
it to us.
                           Sincerely yours,
                           /s/ Amar Bhattacharya
                           Acting Director
                           Country Department V
                           Asia Region
