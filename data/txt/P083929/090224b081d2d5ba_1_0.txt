                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the project is to improve the viability and effectiveness of urban services provided by the participating TMAs, and to make such improvements
                         sustainable and replicable in other TMAs through the creation of a performance-based management framework at both TMA and provincial levels.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         PMSIP has continued to make satisfactory progress towards achievement of its development objective. Institutional impact assessments provide evidence of improvements inTMAs
                         capacity for providing demand-driven and sustainable urban services. Moreover, an integrated web-based Performance Management System is operational, providing data on TMA
                         service delivery indicators across Punjab. Under the Infrastructure Development component, 35 of the 39 infrastructure sub-projects undertaken are complete, while 4 are at various
                         stages of completion. Under the Cultural Heritage component, the Walled City of Lahore Authority (WCLA) has started operations following promulgation of the Walled City of Lahore
