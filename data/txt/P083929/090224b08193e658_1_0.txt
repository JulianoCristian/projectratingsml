                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the project is to improve the viability and effectiveness of urban services provided by the participating TMAs, and to make such improvements
                         sustainable and replicable in other TMAs through the creation of a performance-based management framework at both TMA and provincial levels.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         PMSIP has continued to make satisfactory progress towards implementation of its various project components over the current review period. The project has been recently
                         extended for another year up to November 2013, primarily to help deepen and provide continuity of support to the systems and business processes introduced in the beneficiary
                         TMAs, as well as to allow some of the ongoing activities to be completed in an orderly fashion.

                         The Institutional Development (ID) Component of the project is steadily meeting its progress targets, in terms of both deepening of the reform activities introduced in the original 37
