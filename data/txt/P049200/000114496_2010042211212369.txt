Re: ROMANIA:  Social Development Fund Project (Loan Number 
4434 RO) 
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (the Loan Agreement) between  the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and Romania 
(the Borrower) for the above-mentioned project, dated January 20, 1999, and your 
letter dated September 22, 2000, where you propose amendments to the same.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank agrees to make the requested 
amendments.  Accordingly, the Loan Agreement is proposed to be amended as set 
forth below:
1.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement, is 
amended to read as shown in Attachment 1 to this letter.
2.
The date “September 15, 2000” in paragraph 2 in Part D of Schedule 
5 to the Loan Agreement is replaced with the date “November 15, 2000”.
3.
The date “November 15, 2000” in paragraph 3 in Part D of Schedule 5 
to the Loan Agreement is replaced with the date “December 15, 2000”.
This amendment will become effective upon receipt of a countersigned copy 
of this letter.  
Page  2
Please indicate your agreement to the above amendment to the Loan 
Agreement by signing and returning to us the attached copy of this letter. Upon 
your signature, this amendment will enter into force as of the date indicated 
above.
