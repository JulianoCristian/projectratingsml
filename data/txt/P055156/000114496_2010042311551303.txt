Dear Mr. Minister:
We refer to the Loan Agreement for the above-referenced Project, dated December 
4, 1998, between the Republic of Bulgaria (the Borrower) and the International Bank 
for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank).  We also refer to your letter dated 
October 18, 1999 requesting the Bank to amend the said Loan Agreement.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank has agreed to your request.  
Accordingly the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as set forth in the Annex to this 
letter.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by countersigning and dating 
the enclosed copy of this letter and returning it to us.  This amendment will become 
effective on the date hereof upon receipt of your countersigned copy by the Bank.  A 
signed duplicate copy of the letter is enclosed for your records.
Sincerely yours,

II.
Paragraph 2 (c) of Schedule 1 is amended to read as follows: 
(c)
the term “PMU’s operating costs” means expenditures incurred by PMU on 
account of management of Project implementation including expenditures for consultants 
to be employed in the PMU (including the cost of daily subsistence allowances for 
local travel); project audit fees; travel and subsistence related to Microproject 
appraisal, implementation and evaluation; minor office refurbishment; vehicle 
operation and maintenance; communications; and other miscellaneous office expenditures 
such as utilities and stationery.” 
III.
Paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 6 is amended to increase the amount of the 
Authorized Allocation from “DEM 750,000” to “DEM 1,000,000”.
