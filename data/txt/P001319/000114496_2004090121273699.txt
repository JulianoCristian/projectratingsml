KENYA:  Urban Transport Infrastructure Project (Credit 2811-KE)
        Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the above-mentioned Agreement, dated February 13, 1996 (the 
Agreement), between the Republic of Kenya (the Borrower) and the International 
Development Association (the Association), and to your letter, dated June 12, 1997, 
requesting the Association to amend the Agreement in order to add a new disbursement 
category to allow for the financing of micro-projects in a number of eligible local 
authorities as part of the preparation of the proposed municipal infrastructure 
investment program.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association accedes to your request and 
agrees, accordingly, to amend the Agreement as follows:
(a)
in Section 1.02 thereof, by inserting in their proper alphabetical order 
the new paragraphs (a), (d), (e), (f) and (p) as set forth in Attachment 1 to this 
letter, the existing paragraphs (a) through (l) being renumbered accordingly;
(b)
by amending Section 2.02 (a) thereof to read as shown in Attachment 2 to 
this letter;
(c)
in Schedule 1 to the Agreement,
(i)
in the table in paragraph 1 thereof, by adding therein a new 
Category (8), and amending the existing Category (1) thereof, 
to read as 
shown in Part A of Attachment 3 to this letter;
(ii)
by amending paragraph 3 thereof to read as shown in Part B of 
Attachment 3 to this letter;
(d)
in Schedule 2 to the Agreement, by amending Part E thereof to read as 
follows:
"Preparation of a municipal infrastructure investment program and 
pilot 
demonstration projects, including provision of grants to local 
authorities to 
finance micro-projects."
(e)
in Schedule 4 to the Agreement, by adding two new paragraphs 8 and 9 as 
set forth in Attachment 4 to this letter; and
(f)
in Schedule 5 thereto, by amending paragraph 1 (b) thereof to read as 
shown in Attachment 5 to this letter.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment, on behalf of the 
Borrower, by signing and dating the confirmation form on the enclosed copy of this 
letter and returning it to us for our records.

Attachment 1 
Section 1.02
(a)
“Eligible Local Authorities” means the town or municipal councils of all 
or any of the following: Ahero, Bomet, Embu, Kakamega, Kisumu, Naivasha, Nakuru, 
Nyeri, Mavoko, Muragua and Thika;
(d)
“Micro-Project” means a micro-project under Part E of the Project, which 
satisfies the eligibility criteria specified in the Micro-Projects Grant Funds 
Operations Manual, and in respect of which a Micro-Project Grant is provided, or 
proposed to be provided, for the benefit of an Eligible Local Authority;
(e)
"Micro-Project Grant" means a grant made or proposed to be made out of 
the proceeds of the Credit for the benefit of an Eligible Local Authority to finance a 
Micro-Project; 
(f)
“Micro-Projects Grant Funds Operations Manual” means the Micro-Projects 
Grant Funds Operations Manual referred to in paragraph 8 (a) of Schedule 4 to this 
Agreement, as same may be amended from time to time, and such term includes any 
schedules to the Micro-Projects Grant Funds Operations Manual;
(p)
“Works Contract” means a contract referred to in paragraph 9 (d) of

Attachment 4 
SCHEDULE 4
8.
For purposes of Part E of the Project, the Borrower shall: 
(a)
prepare and transmit to the Association a Micro-Projects Grant Funds 
Operations Manual, acceptable to the Association, giving details of all procedural 
arrangements for the preparation, approval and implementation of Micro-Projects, 
including procedures for identification of Micro-Projects and submission of requests 
for financing, criteria for the determination of Eligible Local Authorities and 
approval of Micro-Projects, monitoring and evaluation processes, applicable 
Page  4
procurement, disbursement and other implementation guidelines, and model forms of 
Works Contracts; and
(b)
carry out the Project in accordance with procedures set out in the 
Micro-Projects Grant Funds Operations Manual, and, except as the Association shall 
otherwise agree, shall not amend or waive any provision thereof, if such amendment or 
waiver may, in the opinion of the Association, materially or adversely affect the 
implementation of the Project.
9. 
For the purposes of Part E of the Project, the Borrower shall allocate an amount 
of the Credit not exceeding such amount as may be allocated from time to time to 
Category (8) of the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement, to be used 
to provide Micro-Projects Grants to Eligible Local Authorities to finance 
Micro-Projects, on such terms and conditions as shall be acceptable to the 
Association, including, but without being limited to, the following terms and 
conditions:
(a)
the amount so provided shall be used exclusively to provide 
Micro-Projects Grants to finance Micro-Projects, in accordance with criteria, and 
terms and conditions, set forth in the Micro-Projects Grant Funds Operations Manual;
(b)
Micro-Projects Grants shall be provided for the sole benefit of Eligible 
Local Authorities, and according to a cost-sharing formula, which shall be acceptable 
to the Association and specified in the Micro-Projects Grant Funds Operations Manual; 
provided, however, that: 
(i)
the proceeds of a Micro-Project Grant shall not be used to 
finance taxes or duties levied on or in respect of eligible 
expenditures 
relating to a Micro-Project, or any part thereof; 
(ii)
the amount of each Micro-Project Grant shall not exceed 90 
percent of the cost of the Micro-Project or Kenya Shillings 
6,000,000, 
whichever is less;
(c)
goods, services and civil works required for the purposes of financing 
Micro-Projects shall be procured in accordance with procedures acceptable to the 
Association, and set forth in the Micro-Projects Grant Funds Operations Manual;
(d)
for purposes of each Grant, the Borrower shall conclude a Works Contract 
with a contractor in the form of the model form provided in the Micro-Projects Grant 
Funds Operations Manual (hereinafter called Works Contract), setting forth the 
respective obligations of the parties thereunder, including details of the 
Micro-Project to be implemented, the contract price and works schedules, details of 
disbursement and procurement schedules and procedures, including, but without being 
limited to, the right of the Borrower to:
(i)
require the contractor to carry out the Micro-Project with due 
diligence and efficiency and in accordance with sound 
administrative, financial, engineering, environmental, and 
technical 
practices, and to maintain adequate records; and
(ii)
obtain all such information as the Borrower, Association or the 
Eligible Local Authority shall reasonably request relating to 
the 
implementation of the Micro-Project and the performance of 
the contractor 
thereunder.
