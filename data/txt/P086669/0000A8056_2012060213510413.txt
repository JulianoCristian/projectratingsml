                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This ISR is to report the progress achieved since the Mid Term review visit conducted in March 2012. In the last two months the project has continued showing progress toward the
                         Project Development Objectives. The number of County Institute of Emergency Medical Services (CIEMS) reporting data has increased from 10 to 13, including Zagreb. Additional
                         contracts for medical equipment for the EMS Centers have been signed while the Geographic Information System (GIS) for the Public Health institute is in the final phase of
                         evaluation. Additional technical assistance and training for dispatchers as well as pre-hospital activities are being conducted. Several other procurement processes have been
