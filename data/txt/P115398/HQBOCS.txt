                                           Amendment to the Grant Agreement

            Excellency:

                    We refer to the Grant Agreement dated November 26, 2009 ("Agreement"), between the
            Republic of C6te d'lvoire ("Recipient") and the International Development Association
            ("Association"), as amended on November 9, 2011.

                     We also refer to your letter dated September 16, 2013, requesting specific amendments to
            the Agreement. Please note that capitalized terms used in this letter ("Amendment Letter") and
            not defined herein have the meaning ascribed to them in the Agreement.

                    We are pleased to inform you that the Association accedes to your request and proposes,
            through this Amendment Letter, to amend the Agreement as follows:

            1.       The table set forth under Section III.A.2 of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is deleted in its
            entirety and replaced with the table shown in the Attachment to this Amendment Letter.

            2.      Section Ill.B.2 of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is amended to read as follows:

                    "2.       The Closing Date is October 31, 2014."

            3.      Paragraph 18 of the Appendix to the Agreement is amended to read as follows:

                    "18.      "Participating Institution" means any one (1) of the institutions targeted under the
                              Project, namely, the Small and Medium Enterprise Credit and Financing Mutual
                              Fund, the Investment Promotion Agency, the Ministry of Industry and Mines, the
                              Ministry of Commerce, Handicrafts and Small and Medium Enterprise
                              Promotion, the Ministry of Interior and the Ministry of Justice and Human
                              Rights, and "Participating Institutions" means two (2) or more of said
                              institutions."
