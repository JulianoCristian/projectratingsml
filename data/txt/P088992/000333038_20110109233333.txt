7a.  Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
The Project is to assist Mongolian in its efforts to promote private and financial sector development and 
reinforcing the institutional capacity of the banking sector by (i) increasing the availability of commercial 
bank term lending to investment enterprises, (ii) strengthening the financial intermediation function and 
resource allocation capabilities of the participating financial institutions; (iii) strengthening Bank of 
Mongolia#s financial supervision functions; and (iv) underpinning the Ministry of Finance#s role in 
fostering private sector growth and a sound Mongolian financial sector.
