Re.: Credit 2344-BEN (IVatural Resources'Management Project)
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) between the 
Republic of Benin (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the 
Association) dated April 9, 1992, as well as to your letter of September 6, 1995, 
requesting an amendment to the Agreement.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association hereby agrees to amend the 
Agreement as follows:
The amount in paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 4 to the Agreement shall be amended to 
read: "CFAF 400,000,000
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing by countersigning and dating 
the form of confirmation on the two original copies of this letter and returning one 
countersigned original to us for our records.
