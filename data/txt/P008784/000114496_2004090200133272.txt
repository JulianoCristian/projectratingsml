Re:
ROMANIA:  Education Reform Project (Ln. 3724-RO)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (the Loan Agreement) between  the International 
Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and Romania (the Borrower) for the 
above-mentioned project, dated May 23, 1994, and as amended on September 22, 2000, and 
your letter dated March 6, 2001, where you propose amendments to the same.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank agrees to make the requested 
amendments.  Accordingly, the Loan Agreement is proposed to be amended as set forth 
below:
1.
In Schedule 2, Part A, subparagraph (8)(b), the word "seven" is replaced with 
the word "eight".
2.
To Schedule 4, Section 1, Part C, new paragraphs 5 and 6 are added to read as 
follows:
"5.
Furniture under Part A(8) estimated to cost less $300,000 
equivalent per contract, up to an aggregate amount not to exceed $2,000,000 
equivalent, may be procured under contracts awarded in accordance with the 
provisions of paragraphs 3.3 and 3.4 of the Revised Guidelines.
6.
Booklets under Part A(8)(b) which are of a proprietary nature and 
costing $500,000 or less in the aggregate, may with the Bank’s prior 
agreement, be procured in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 3.7(c) 
of the Revised Guidelines."
3.
In Schedule 4, Section I, Part D, paragraph 1(a), the first sentence is revised 
to read as follows:
"(a)  With respect to: (i) the first contract for goods awarded in 
accordance with Section I, C.5 of this Schedule; (ii) each contract 
for goods estimated to cost the equivalent of $250,000 or more awarded 
in accordance with Section I, C.4 and C.5 of this Schedule; and (iii) 
each contract for goods awarded in accordance with Section I, C.6, the 
procedures set forth in paragraphs 2 and 4 of Appendix 1 to the 
Revised Guidelines shall apply."
Please indicate your agreement to the above amendments to the Loan Agreement by 
signing and returning to us the attached copy of this letter.  This amendment will 
become effective upon receipt of a countersigned copy of this letter.
