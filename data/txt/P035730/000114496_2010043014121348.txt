Third Amendment to the Loan and Credit Agreements
Dear Mr. Primer Minister: 
 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between the International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank) and Commonwealth of Dominica (the Borrower) (the Loan 
Agreement), and the Credit Agreement between the International Development Association (the 
Association) and Commonwealth of Dominica (the Borrower) (the Credit Agreement) both dated 
June 8, 1998, for the above-captioned Project, as amended.   
 
Pursuant to our recent discussions on the subject, we are pleased to inform you that the 
Bank and the Association hereby agree to amend the Loan Agreement and the Credit Agreement 
as follows:  
 
1. 
Section 2.03 of the Loan and Credit Agreements is hereby amended and replaced to read 
in its entirety as follows: 
 
“Section 2.03 The Closing Date shall be September 30, 2003 or such later date as the 
Bank shall establish. The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower of such later date.” 
 
2. 
Schedule 1 of  the Loan and Credit Agreements is hereby amended by replacing in its 
entirety the table in paragraph 1, by the new table detailed in the Attachment to this letter. 
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating both 
originals  of this letter in the spaces provided below. This amendment letter will be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.
