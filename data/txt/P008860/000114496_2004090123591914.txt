Tajikistan:  Second Poverty Alleviation Project 
Project Agreement (Credit No. 3642-TJ)
 
Mr. Zardov: 
 
In your communication dated November 18, 2002, the Association is requested to amend 
the Project Agreement dated July 3, 2002, (Credit No. 3642 
–
TJ) (hereinafter, “the Project 
Agreement”) in order to implement the Project more effectively. 
 
We are pleased to concur with this request, and agree to make the following amendments to 
the Development Credit Agreement: 
1. 
The paragraph 1 (a) to Schedule 4 to the Project Agreement is replaced with the 
following: “legally registered MFI or other types of financial institutions;”; and 
2. 
The paragraph 1 (b) to Schedule 4 to the Project Agreement is replaced with the 
following: “have computerized accounting systems in compliance with accounting standards which 
are capable to produce timely, reliable and accurate financial reporting;”. 
 
Please confirm your agreement on behalf of the National Investment Fund of Tajikistan 
with the above amendments by signing, dating and returning to us one of the original copies of this 
Amendment Letter.  The amendment will become effective as of the date of your countersignature. 
 
Very truly yours,
