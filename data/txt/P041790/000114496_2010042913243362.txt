Third Amendment to the Trust Fund Grant Agreement
Excellency: 
 
Please refer to the GEF Trust Fund Grant Agreement (the Grant Agreement) for the 
above-referenced Project between the Republic of Nicaragua (the Recipient) and the International 
Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) acting as an implementing agency of the 
Global Environment Facility (GEF) dated August 25, 1997, as amended on January 15, 1999 and 
February 11, 2003. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to the request of the Minister of the 
Environment and Natural Resources dated June 20, 2003 to: (a) waive the condition of 
disbursement regarding the setting up of the Recipient’s national environmental fund (NEF), 
remove all references to NEF under the Project and replace them by MARENA (the Recipient’s 
Ministry of the Environment and Natural Resources); and (b) to reallocate amounts among 
categories by increasing the amount allocated to Category 6 “Beneficiary Grants”; the Bank 
agrees to reallocate the amounts as reflected in the Annex to this Letter of Amendment and to 
amend the Grant Agreement as set forth below: 
 
Paragraph (E) of the Preamble to the Grant Agreement will read as follows: 
 
“Part A of the Project will be carried out by the Recipient’s Ministry of the Environment 
and Natural Resources (MARENA) with the Recipient’s assistance and, as part of such 
assistance, the Recipient will make available to MARENA the proceeds of the GEF Trust Fund 
Grant as provided in this Agreement”.

Sub-sections (r) and (u) are deleted and sub-sections (s) through (aa) are re-lettered.
 
All references to “NEF” in sub-sections (d), (e), (q), (r), (s), (t) and (v) will be replaced 
by “MARENA”.
