Second Amendment to the Trust Fund Grant Agreement
Excellency: 
 
Please refer to the Trust Fund Grant Agreement (the Grant Agreement) for the above-
referenced Project between the Republic of Nicaragua (the Recipient) and the International Bank 
for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) acting as an implementing agency of the Global 
Environment Facility (GEF) dated August 25, 1997, as amended on March 11, 1999. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to the request of the Vice Minister of 
Environment and Natural Resources dated November 13, 2002 (amended by a request from the 
same Minister dated January 7, 2003 to revise the amounts in Schedule 1 to the Grant Agreement) 
to: (a) extend the Closing Date from March 31, 2003 to October 31, 2004; (b) reallocate amounts 
amongst categories by reducing the amount allocated to Beneficiary Grants; (c) waive the 
condition of disbursement regarding the approval by the National Assembly of the Indigenous 
Law; (d) eliminate the sub-component referring to demarcation activities and all references to  
“Demarcation” under the Project; and (e) ensure that audit costs are eligible expenses under the 
Grant, the Bank agrees to extend the Closing Date to October 31, 2004, reallocate the amounts as 
reflected in the Annex to this Letter of Amendment and amend the Grant Agreement as set forth 
below: 
 
Section 1.02
Sub-sections (g), (h), (i) and (j) are deleted and sub-sections (k) through (gg) are re-
numbered. 
 
Section 3.01
Sub-section (a) (ii) is amended to read as follows: 
 
“The Recipient shall carry out Part D of the Project, through MARENA”.
