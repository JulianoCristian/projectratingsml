                         Implementation Status Overview
                         This ISR is submitted following the retroactive extension of the project's closing date from December 2009 til June 30 2013 in order to allow completion of activities that are critical to
                         the attainment of PDOs. As agreed with the Guinean authorities during the January 2011 re-engagement mission, the project will require further restructuring to (i) revise the scope of
                         project components in light of budget overruns, and in order to optimize the project#s impact without changing the project development objectives (PDOs), (ii) revise and fine-tune the
                         project#s results framework, and (iv) amend the development credit agreement (DCA) to cancel outdated clauses and covenants, and to merge the categories of expenditures of the
