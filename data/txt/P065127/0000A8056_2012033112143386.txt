                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Implementation of restructured project activities has gained significant momentum. Disbursements have significantly improved from 32% in July 2011 to 49% as of end-January 2012.
                         Furthermore, 75% of project funds are committed to date thanks to recent launch of works of the 3 rural bridges.
