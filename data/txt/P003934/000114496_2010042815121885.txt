            Re:   Loan 3031-IND (Agricultural Research Management Project)
                  Amendment of the Loan Agreement
Dear Minister Sumarlin:
      We refer to the Loan Agreement (Agricultural Research
Management Project) between the Republic of Indonesia (the Borrower)
and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the
Bank) dated May 1, 1989, as amended.  We also refer to your
Ministry's telex dated February 27, 1993 requesting the Bank for an
increase in the authorized allocation of the Special Account under
the above project.
      The Bank is pleased to inform you that, after due
consideration, it has agree to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
      1.    The amount stated in paragraph 1 (c) of Schedule 6 to
the Loan Agreement is amended to read "$3,500,000".
      Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by
signing and dating the enclosed form of confirmation, and returning
it to us.
                                Sincerely yours,
                                /s/ Marianne Haug
                                Director
                                Country Department III
                                Asia Region
