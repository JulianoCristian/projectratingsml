Re: Venezuela - Loan 3494-VE (Student Loan Reform Project)
    Amendment to the Loan Agreement and Project Agreement
Dear Sirs:
     We refer to the Loan Agreement between the Republic of Venezuela
(the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development (the Bank), dated April 5, 1994 and the Project Agreement
between the Bank and FUNDAYACUCHO dated April 5, 1994. We are pleased
to inform you of our agreement to amend the Loan Agreement and Project
Agreement as follows:
Loan Agreement
1.   Section 3.04 (b) (ii) is hereby amended by deleting "June 30, 1996,
December 31, 1996, and March 31, 1997," and substituting "March 31, 1998,
December 31, 1997, and June 30, 1998" therefor.
2.   Paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is hereby deleted in its entirety and
substituted for Attachment 1 to this letter.
3.   Part A of Schedule 2 is amended to read as follows:
     Part A:   Student Loan Program
     1.   A program to make loans to selected personnel, under institu-
tional sponsorship, in the public and private sectors and to eligible
individuals for approximately 2000 student-years of overseas graduate
studies, 2500 student-years of local graduate studies, and 900 student-
years of local undergraduate studies in selected areas agreed between
the Borrower and the Bank.
     2.   A program to make loans to selected personnel in the public
and private sectors, under institutional sponsorship, for purposes of
financing approximately 300 student-years of eligible overseas and
local short courses in selected areas agreed between the Borrower and
the Bank.
     3.   Part B.1 of Schedule 2 is amended by adding a new subpara-
graph (e) to read as follows:
          "(e) the carrying out of a public awareness campaign to
          inform the public with respect to the Project and the
          Borrower's policy on financing of higher education."
Project Agreement
     1.   Section 2.09 (a) is hereby amended by deleting "September 30,
1995" in paragraph (i), "December 31, 1993" in paragraph (ii) and
"December 31, 1995" in paragraph (iii) and substituting "March 31,
1997", "December 31, 1997" and  "September 30, 1997" therefor
Page  2
respectively.
     2.   Section 2.10 is hereby amended by deleting "December 31, 1995"
in paragraph (i), "June 30, 1996" in paragraph (ii) and "December 31,
1996" in paragraph (iii) and substituting "December 30, 1997", "August
31, 1997" and "June 30, 1998" therefor respectively.
     If you agree with the proposed amendments, please sign the form of
confirmation below. The amendments set forth in this letter shall enter
into effect upon receipt by the Bank of a duly executed counterpart of
this letter.
