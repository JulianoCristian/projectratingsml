                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project development objective is to improve urban living conditions in regard to environmental health, sanitation, drainage, vehicular access, and solid waste
                         management in a sustainable fashion, with special emphasis on the poor.
