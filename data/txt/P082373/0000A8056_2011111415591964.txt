                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project activities for the Storm Drainage, Sanitation and Community Infrastructure Upgrading components have been completed or over 90% completed in Accra, Kumasi, Sekondi-
                         Takoradi and Tamale. Storm Drain works in Tema are significantly behind schedule as well as the Tema Sewerage Rehabilitation Works and the Tema Septage Treatment Plant
