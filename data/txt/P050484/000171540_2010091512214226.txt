Re: 
Secondary Education Enhancement Project (Credit Number 3194-EGT) 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement 
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated April 19, 1999 between the Arab 
Republic of Egypt (the Borrower) and International Development Association (the Association) 
for the above-referenced Project (the Development Credit Agreement).  We further refer to the 
letters dated December 1, 2009, and June 22, 2010 from your Excellency on behalf of the 
Borrower, requesting an extension of the Closing Date to enable the Project to fully meet its 
development objectives. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, the Association concurs with 
your request and establishes June 30, 2012 as the later date for purposes of Section 2.03 of the 
Development Credit Agreement. 
 
In addition, to enhance efficiency in Project implementation, we propose to amend the 
Development Credit Agreement as follows: 
 
1. 
Part A, Section I of Schedule 3 to the Development Credit Agreement shall be amended 
to read as follows: 
 
“Part A:  General 
 
Goods and works required for the Project and to be financed out of the proceeds of the 
Credit shall be procured in accordance with the requirements set forth or referred to in 
Section I of the “Guidelines: Procurement under IBRD Loans and IDA Credits” 
published by the Bank in May 2004 and revised in October, 2006 and May 2010, and 
with the provisions of Section I of this Schedule.” 
 
2. 
Part A, Section II of Schedule 3 to the Development Credit Agreement is amended to 
read as follows: 
“Part A:  General 
Consultants’ services required for the Project and to be financed out of the proceeds of 
the Credit shall be procured in accordance with the requirements set forth or referred to in 
Page  2
2
Sections I and IV of the “Guidelines: Selection and Employment of Consultants by 
World Bank Borrowers” published by the Bank in May 2004 and revised in October 2006 
and May 2010, and with the provisions of Section II of this Schedule.” 
Except as specifically amended in accordance with the provisions of paragraphs 1 and 2 
of this letter of amendment, all other provisions of the Development Credit Agreement shall 
remain in full force and effect. 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing on behalf of the Borrower by 
countersigning and dating the corresponding form of confirmation set forth below, and returning 
one fully signed original of this letter to us.  This letter of amendment shall become effective as 
of the date hereof upon receipt by the Association of a duly countersigned copy of this letter of 
amendment. 
 
Sincerely yours, 
 
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
