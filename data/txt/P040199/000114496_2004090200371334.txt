2.
Section 2.03 is hereby amended to read as follows:
"Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be December 31, 2001 or such later date 
as the Bank shall establish.  The Bank shall promptly notify the Borrower and 
the Guarantor of such later date."
3.
Part A of Schedule 2 is hereby amended to read as follows:
"Part A: 
Quality Improvements in Initial, Pre-School and Basic Education
1.
Construction and furnishing of new classrooms within existing Project 
Public Schools, and rehabilitation and furnishing of existing facilities 
within, and supervision offices and warehouses serving, such schools, to meet 
expanding enrollment at the pre-school and basic education (i.e., grades 1 
through 9) levels."
4.
Schedule 2 in fine is hereby amended to read as follows:
"The Project is expected to be completed by June 30, 2001."
5.
Part C.1 of Schedule 4 is hereby amended to read as follows:
"Part C:
Other Procurement Procedures
1.
National Competitive Bidding
Textbooks, library books and other didactic materials for children and 
the printing thereof, estimated to cost $500,000 equivalent or less per 
contract or per bid package, up to an aggregate amount not to exceed 
$17,200,000 equivalent, may be procured under contracts awarded in accordance 
with the provisions of paragraphs 3.3 and 3.4 of the Guidelines.  In the 
procurement of textbooks, library books and other didactic materials for 
children and the printing thereof in accordance with this Part C.1, standard 
bidding documents acceptable to the Bank shall be used."
The new version of the Loan Agreement integrating the amendments set forth in 
the above paragraph is attached the Annex to this Amendment Letter.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
the three originals of this letter in the space provided below, and returning one to 
us.  The terms of this letter shall enter into effect retroactively as of January 1, 
2001, upon receipt by the Bank of one original of this letter signed by you.
