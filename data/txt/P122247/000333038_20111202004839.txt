                                                                      65847
             Republic of Rwanda: Eighth Poverty Reduction Support Financing

                                 SUMMARY OF DISCUSSION

                               Meeting of the Executive Directors

                                           November 29, 2011




        Executive Directors approved the Financing in the amount US$125.0 million, consisting
of a grant in the amount of US$60.0 million and a credit in the amount of US$65.0 million to the
Republic of Rwanda on the payment terms and conditions set out in the President’s
Memorandum (IDA/R2011-0299).

        Executive Directors expressed support for the Eighth Poverty Reduction Support
Financing and commended it for the close alignment with Rwanda’s national development
strategy, the Country Assistance Strategy, the Africa Strategy and the programs of other
development partners. Directors encouraged continued attention to fiscal consolidation, the
development and implementation of a comprehensive private sector development strategy,
further public financial management improvements and continued strengthening of public sector
capacity.
