      Re:   Credit No. 2068-GUI (Forestry and Fisheries Management Project)
            Amendment
Excellency,
      Please refer to the development Credit Agreement (the Agreement)
dated November 9, 1989 between the Republic of Guinea (the Borrower) and the
international Development Association (the Association).
      With reference to your letter of February 9, 1993, we are pleased to
inform you that the Association agrees to amend the Agreement as follows:
      - The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is revised to read
      in its entirety as set out in Attachment 1 hereto;
      - Schedule 4, paragraph 1 (c), the words "an amount of $150,000 for
      the Forestry Special Account" are replaced with the words "an amount
      of $300,000 for the Forestry Special Account".
      Please confirm your agreement with this amendment of the Agreement by
signing, dating and returning the form of confirmation on the enclosed copy
of this letter.  This amendment shall become effective upon receipt by the
Association of the duly signed copy.
Sincerely yours,
Olivier Lafourcade
