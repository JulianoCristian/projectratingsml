AGREEMENT, dated       1) t   V  0 2-i        , 1983, between
REPUBLIC OF THE IVORY COAST (hereinafter called the Ivory Coast)
and INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT (here-
inafter called the Bank).
WHEREAS (A) by a Guarantee Agreement (CIMAO Regional Clinker
Project) dated June 28, 1976 between the Ivory Coast and the Bank
(hereinafter called the Ivory Coast Guarantee Agreement), the
Ivory Coast has agreed to undertake such obligations toward the
Bank as therein set forth;
(B) by a Development Credit Agreement of even date here-
with between the Republic of Togo and the Association, the
Association has agreed to make available to the Republic of Togo
an amount in various currencies equivalent to five million three
hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 5,300,000), on the
terms and conditions set forth in such Development Credit
Agreement; and (ii) by a Development Credit Agreement of even
date herewith between the Republic of Ghana and the Association,
the Association has agreed to make available to the Republic of
Ghana an amount in various currencies equivalent to eight million
six hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 8,600,000), on
terms and conditions set forth in such Development Credit
Agreement, all said amounts to assist Ciments de l'Afrique de
l'Ouest (CIMAO) (hereinafter called CIMAO or the Borrower, as the
context may require) in carrying out the Project described in
Schedule 2 to each said Development Credit Agreement (hereinafter
called the CIMAO Restructuring Project); and
(C) the Association has agreed to make said amounts avail-
able to the Republic of Togo and the Republic of Ghana only on
condition that: (i) the Ivory Coast agree to certain amendments
to the Ivory Coast Guarantee Agreement as such amendments are
hereinafter set forth; and (ii) CIMAO agree to undertake such
obligations toward the Association as are set forth in a Project
Agreement of even date herewith between the Association and CIMiAO
(hereinafter called the Project Agreement);
WHEREAS the Ivory Coast, in consideration of the Association
entering into the agreements referred to in Recital (B) above,


﻿- 2 -
has agreed to the amendments to the Ivory Coast Guarantee Agree-
ment which are hereinafter set forth;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
1.   Paragraph (a) of Section 2.02 of the Ivory Coast Guarantee
Agreement is hereby amended by adding the words "and the Project
Agreement" after the words "the Loan Agreement" whenever the
latter words appear in said paragraph.
2.   Paragraph (b) of Section 2.02 of the Ivory Coast Guarantee
Agreement is hereby amended by adding the woris "and the CIMAO
Restructuring Project" after the words "the Project" whenever the
latter words appear in said paragraph.
3.   Paragraph (a) of Section 2.03 of the Ivory Coast Guarantee
Agreement is hereby amended by replacing "30.74%" by the words
"one-third (1/3)" and by replacing the words "Section 5.09 of the
Loan Agreement" by the words "Section 4.07 of the Project
Agreement".
4.   Paragraph (b) of Section 2.03 of the Ivory Coast Guarantee
Agreement is hereby amended by replacing the words "Section 5.08
(a) of the Loan Agreement" by the words "the Project Agreement".
5.   Paragraph (a) of Section 3.02 of the Ivory Coast Guarantee
Agreement is hereby amended by adding thereto the words "and at
least at the level set forth in Section 4.08 of the Project
Agreement".
6.   A Section 3.03 is hereby added to Article III of the Ivory
Coast Guarantee Agreement to read as follows:
"Section 3.03. (a) The Ivory Coast shall, at the
request of the Bank, exchange views with the Bank with
regard to the progress of the CIMAO Restructuring Project.
(b) The Ivory Coast shall promptly inform the Bank of
any condition which interferes or threatens to interfere
with the progress of the CIMAO Restructuring Project."
7.   The above amendments shall come into force and effect on the
date upon which the agreements referred to in paragraphs (B) and
(C) (ii) of the Preamble to this Agreement shall have become
effective.
