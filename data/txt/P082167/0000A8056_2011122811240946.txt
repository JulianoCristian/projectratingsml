                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project Development Objective is to strengthen the National Agricultural Science and Technology and Sanitary and Phytosanitary Systems by supporting
                         the joint participation of both the public and private sectors, through the mechanism of production chains, thereby contributing to the competitiveness of
                         Colombian agriculture and improving the accessibility of export-potential products to international markets.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project impact evaluation has shown the benefits produced by the investments in agriculture innovation and animal health. The final works for obtaining the certification of the
                         biosafety laboratory (LNSB3A) are under way, financed by own Government resources. The timeline of activities for LNSB3A has been updated and the work plan for achieving
                         certification has been reviewed. The additional works and activities in concluding the LNSB3A are expected to be concluded by June 2012 and the project is still programmed to be
                         closing on December 30, 2011.
