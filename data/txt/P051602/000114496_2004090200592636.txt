Re: Credit 3038 ALB (Private Industry Recovery Project) 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement 
 
Dear Mr. Islami: 
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the above-referenced Project, dated 
February 25, 1998, as amended, between Albania and the International Development Association 
(the Association). 
 
We also refer to your letter dated February 4, 2003, requesting the Development Credit 
Agreement be amended. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association hereby agrees to your request.   
 
Accordingly, the Development Credit Agreement is hereby amended as follows: 
 
1. 
Article IV, Section 4.02 is deleted. 
 
2. 
Part B of the Project is deleted in the Schedule 2. 
 
3. 
Paragraph 2 of Part A of Schedule 4 is revised to read as follows: 
 
“The Committed GF Amounts may be made available to the Borrower after the 
First GF Closing Date as they become Available Amounts.” 
 
4. 
Paragraph 1 (c)(ii) of Part B is deleted, and following paragraphs 1 (c) (iii) and (iv) 
are renumbered as paragraphs 1 (c)(ii) and (iii). 
 
5. 
Part C of Schedule 4 is deleted and Part D of Schedule 4 is amended to read as Part 
C.
