                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         DO1. To strengthen the capacity for strategic planning and sustainable development, and management of the surface and groundwater resources in Rajasthan.
                         DO2. To increase the productivity of irrigated agriculture through improved surface ir rigation systems performance, and strengthened agricultural support

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The overall project implementation progress of RWSRP is on track. Of the 87 irrigation schemes now covered under the project, canal rehabilitation works in 79 schemes (about 91%)
                         have been completed. In the remaining 8 schemes, canal rehabilitation works consist of total 29 packages; of which 20 packages have also been completed and the balance 9
                         packages are at various stages of implementation and are expected to be completed within the remaining project period. The activities under other components are also either
                         completed or are nearing completion. Memorandum of Understandings (MoU) between the Water User Associations(WUA) and Water Resources Department (WRD) for transfer of
