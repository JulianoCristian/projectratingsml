                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The overall project implementation progress of original RWSRP is on track. Of the 87 irrigation schemes covered under the original project, canal rehabilitation works in 80 projects
                         have been completed, and the works in the remaining 7 schemes also are nearing completion and are expected to be completed by project closure (March 31, 2013). Memorandum
                         of Understandings (MOU) between the 523 Water User Associations (WUAs) and WRD for transfer of operations and maintenance (O&M) and irrigation management responsibilities
                         to WUAs have already been signed, and only 7 MOUs are left to be signed (due to on-going works in case of 2 WUAs in Bhakara system, some pending elections of 5 WUAs due to
