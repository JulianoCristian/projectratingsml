                         Implementation Status Overview
                         In the context of the additional financing of SDR 12.4 million (USD 19.0 million equivalent) to the Rajasthan Water Sector Restructuring Project (RWSRP) approved by the Bank in
                         March 2010, the project closing date of the ongoing RWSRP, which was earlier scheduled to close on March 31, 2010, has been extended for three more years till March 31, 2013.
                         Though, the original project has already met most of the project outcome indicators, the extended project period is planned to be used by the Government of Rajasthan (GoR) to
                         utilize the available balance Credit amount of about USD28.1 million to complete a few leftover/ongoing works and scale up certain activities with a view to further consolidate the

                         The overall project implementation progress of RWSRP is good. Of the 87 irrigation schemes now covered under the project, canal rehabilitation works in 79 schemes (about 91%)
                         have been completed. In the remaining 8 schemes, canal rehabilitation works are in advance stages of implementation and are scheduled to be completed within the extended
                         project period. The activities under other components are also either completed are nearing completion. Memorandum of Understandings (MoU) between the Water User
                         Associations(WUA) and Water Resources Department (WRD) for transfer of operation & maintenance (O&M) and irrigation management responsibilities to 503 WUAs in 79 irrigation
                         schemes have been signed and 14 MoUs in the remaining 8 schemes are planned to be signed by June 30, 2011.
