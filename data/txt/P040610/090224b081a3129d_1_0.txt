                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         DO1. To strengthen the capacity for strategic planning and sustainable development, and management of the surface and groundwater resources in Rajasthan. DO2.
                         To increase the productivity of irrigated agriculture through improved surface ir rigation systems performance, and strengthened agricultural support services, involving
                         greater participation of users and the private sector in service delivery.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Most of the activities presently covered under the project, including Additional Financing, have been completed and a few balance on-going activities are nearing completion and are
                         also expected to be completed by project closure (March 31, 2013). Of the 87 irrigation schemes covered under the original project, canal rehabilitation works in 80 projects have
                         been completed, and the works in the remaining 7 schemes also are nearing completion and are expected to be completed by project closure. The activities under other components
                         are also either completed or nearing completion.

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The project is closing on March 31, 2013, and most of the activities under the project have been completed. Few on-going activities are also at advanced stages of implementation,
                         and it was agreed that the Government of Rajasthan will put maximum effort in completing these on-going activities by project closure as well.

                         Restructuring History
                         There has been no restructuring to date.
