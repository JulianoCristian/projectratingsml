7a.  Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
The development objective of the project is to improve the welfare of the population in the Nura River 
Basin by cleaning up serious mercury pollution in area adjacent to the Nura River, providing a safe, secure 
and cost effective alternative source of water supply to meet growing needs of local water users, and 
restoring flow control in the river for flood management and ecological purposes.
