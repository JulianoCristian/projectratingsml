                              PROJECT AGREEMENT
      AGREEMENT, dated as of July 13, 1995, between the
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (hereinafter called the
Association) and DISTRIBUIDORA DE COMBUSTIVEIS E LUBRIFICANTES
(hereinafter referred to as DICOL).
      WHEREAS: (A) By Development Credit Agreement dated August
21, 1991 between the Republic of Guinea-Bissau and the
International Development Association, the Association has
agreed to make available to the Borrower an amount in various
currencies equivalent to eleven million three hundred thousand
Special Drawing Rights, on the terms and conditions set forth
in the Development Credit Agreement, but only on condition that
DICOL agrees to undertake certain obligations towards the
Association;
      (B)   By Project Agreement of August 21, 1991, as amended
on May 25, 1992 between the Association and DICOL, DICOL agreed
to carry out certain parts of the Project and to undertake
certain obligations to the Association; and
      (C)   The Development Credit Agreement has been amended by
the Agreement Amending Development Credit Agreement
(hereinafter called Amending Agreement) as of July 13, 1993,
Page  2
with the effect, inter alia, that DICOL is no longer
responsible for carrying out any part of the Project, and hence
its obligations under the Project Agreement become obsolete;
      NOW THEREFORE the parties to this Agreement hereby agree
to the following:
      (1)   The Project Agreement for the Energy Project, between
the International Development Association and DICOL dated
August 21, 1991 is hereby terminated.
      (2)   This Agreement shall come into force and effect on
the date upon which the Amending Agreement becomes effective.
      IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto, acting through
their representatives thereunto duly authorized, have caused
this Agreement to be signed in their respective name as of the
day and year first above written.
