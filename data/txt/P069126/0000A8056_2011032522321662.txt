                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         (a) Enhance the policy and institutional environment for efficient sector development and operation.
                          (b) Improve the availability and reliability of electricity supply in the area covered by the national power utility, SONABEL.
                          (c) Promote rational and efficient use of energy in public administration buildings through demand-side management initiatives.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Construction works on the Bobo-Dioulasso-Ouagadougou 225kV lines as well as the reinforcement work on the Kompiengra-Bagre-Tenkodogo-Ouaga lines are now completed. The
                         construction work for additional thermal capacity (of the 14MW and 18 MW power plant) have also been completed. The commissioning of the 14 MW power plant is pending as
                         some verifications on the level of noise are to be undertaken. Progress is also noted in the energy efficiency component with energy saving devices installed in 15 administrative
