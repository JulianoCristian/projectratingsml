                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The TDF aims to support the establishment of the National Integrated Framework Governance Structure (NIFGS) and implement NIFGS's initial activities to address the
                         key institutional and operational bottlenecks and capacity gaps that hinder cross-border trade.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Trade Development Facility is now an increasingly mature operation and entering the latter stages of implementation. Good progress has been achieved across components and
                         the capacity of the Ministry of Industry and Commerce to manage a large multisectoral program has grown significantly. Promising results already achieved include the approval of

                         electronic Trade Portal; the establishment of an inter-agency Sanitary and Phytosanitary risk evaluation group; the completion of a Technical Barriers to Trade diagnostic
                         assessment; the launch of fee-based productivity training services in the Garments Skills Development Centre; support to the operations of the Lao Handicrafts Fair; substantial
                         progress in trade negotiations to accede to the World Trade Organization; and measured growth in domestic trade policy research capacity, including the design of a technical trade
