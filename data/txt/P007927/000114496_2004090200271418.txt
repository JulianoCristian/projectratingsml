Re:
Loans No. 4086/4087-PA (Maternal Health and Child

Amendment to the Loan Agreements
Dear Minister:
Please refer to the above-captioned Loan Agreements between the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the Republic of Paraguay (the Borrower), as amended 
on February 19, 1997 (Loan 4087-PA), both dated September 29, 1996 (the Loan Agreements) with 
regard to the Maternal Health and Child Development Project (the Project).
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to your request and our recent discussions, the Bank 
agrees to amend the Loan Agreements so as to: (a) reallocate funds amount Categories and (b) introduce 
changes to Schedule 4 to update Bank Procurement and Consultant’s Guidelines and standard bidding 
documents.
Consequently, the Loan Agreements are amended as set forth in Attachment I and II to this 
Amendment Letter.
In all respects other than as set forth above, the provisions of the Loan Agreements shall continue 
to apply with full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating the two 
originals of this amendment letter and returning one fully signed original to us. The amendment letter set 
forth in this letter will be effective as of the date of its countersignature.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION
AND DEVELOPMENT
