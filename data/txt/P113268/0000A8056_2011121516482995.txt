                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The disbursement freeze on the Guinea portfolio was lifted in January 2011 and project implementation has been proceeding satisfactorily. During the current cropping season (April-
                         December 2011), 1600 tons of improved seed, 1000 tons of fertilizer and 15,290 liters of pesticides were distributed to smallholders.

                         Data on the actual number of beneficiary farmers in 2011, and the number of hectares cultivated, have not yet been received. Estimates of yield increases resulting from project

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The project closing date was extended to June 30, 2012. This will allow the project to disburse the remaining USD 3 million and reach the original development objectives. A USD 20
                         million follow-up project (funded by the multi-donor Global Food Crisis Response Fund) is under preparation to consolidate and scale-up the results of the current GFRP project.
                         Approval is expected by the end of January 2012.

                         There has been no restructuring to date.
