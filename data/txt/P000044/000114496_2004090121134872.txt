August 26, 1998 
His Excellency Emmanuel Moreira Carneiro 
Minister of Planning 
Ministry of Planning 
Luanda Angola
Re:
Credit No. 2421-ANG (Financial Institutions Modernization Project)
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) dated September 15, 
1992 between Republic of Angola (the Borrower) and International Development 
Association (the Association), and to your letter dated, June 25, 1998, requesting a 
number of amendments to the Agreement.
This is to inform you of our agreement to your request and to propose the 
following amendments to the Agreement.
Schedule 1
1.
A new Category  for "Operating Costs" is created and the table in paragraph 1 of 
Schedule 1 to the Agreement.
2.
Paragraph 2 of  Schedule 1 to the Agreement is amended to read as follows"
"For the purposes of this Schedule" (a) the term "training" means the costs of 
fellowships, workshops, seminars, study tours, including travel and per-diem and other 
costs strictly related to training activities carried out by the Borrower under any of 
the training programs financed under the Project; and (b) the term "Operating Costs" 
means the costs incurred on account of the maintenance of housing for Project 
consultants." 
Schedule 3
3.
The amount of US$400,000 in Part D.2 of Schedule 3 to the Agreement is amended 
to read "US$800,000".
All other provisions of the Agreement, except as amended herein, shall remain in full 
force an effect.
Please confirm your agreement to the amendments by countersigning, dating and 
returning to us the enclosed copy of this letter.  This amendment shall become 
effective as of the date of countersignature.
Sincerely,
Barbara Kafka 
Country Director, Angola 
Africa Region
