         Re: Reform of Secondary Education Project (Ln3580-JM)
      Proposed Amendments to Loan Agreement   
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated May 7, 1993, between Jamaica (the 
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), as 
amended.
In response to the Planning Institute of Jamaica’s (PIOJ’s) May 30, 1997 request 
(and subsequent letter of clarification dated June 19, 1997) to: (a) reallocate the 
equivalent of $2,600,000 from Category (5) Unallocated to Category (3) Civil Works for 
Part A (5) of the Project; (b) reduce the number of New Schools constructed under the 
project from three to one; (c) raise the threshold for International Competitive 
Bidding from the equivalent of $500,000 to $1,500,000; and (d) increase the aggregate 
limit for Local Shopping from the equivalent of $250,000 to $750,000, we are pleased 
inform you that the Bank hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:   
1.
The table in paragraph 1. of Schedule 1 is hereby amended to read as set forth 
in the annex to this amendment letter.
2.
Paragraph 5. (a) of Schedule 2, Part A., Secondary Reform Support, is hereby 
amended as follows:
"(a) construction of one junior high school"
3.
Paragraph 2. of  Schedule 4, Section I, Part A: International Competitive 
Bidding, is hereby amended as follows:
"To the extent practicable, contracts for works or goods shall be 
grouped into bid packages estimated to cost the equivalent of more than 
$1,500,000 
for works or the equivalent of more than $100,000 for goods.";
and paragraph 1. of  Schedule 4, Section I, Part C: Other Procurement 
Procedures, is hereby amended as follows:
"Works or groups of works estimated to cost the equivalent of $1,500,000 or less 
per contract shall be procured under contracts awarded on the basis of competitive 
bidding, advertised locally, in accordance with procedures acceptable to the Bank."
4.
Paragraph 6. of Schedule 4, Section I, Part C: Other Procurement 
Procedures, is hereby amended as follows:
"Goods estimated to cost the equivalent of less than $20,000 per contract, up to 
an aggregate amount equivalent to $750,000, shall be procured under contracts awarded 
on the basis of comparison of price quotations obtained from at least three suppliers 
eligible under the 
Guidelines, in accordance with procedures acceptable to the Bank."
This amendment letter shall be executed in two counterparts each of which shall 
be an original, and shall become effective on the date that the Bank has received one 
fully executed original of such amendment letter.  Please confirm your agreement to 
the foregoing amendment by signing and dating the two counterparts of this amendment 
letter.  Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed original to us.
Sincerely,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR
