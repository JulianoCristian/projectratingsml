                         Program Development Objectives
                         Program Development Objective (from Program Document)

                         The proposed operation has three main development objectives: (i) supporting the institutionalization of the stewardship of the social inclusion agenda under MIDIS; (ii)
                         laying the foundations for improving the inclusiveness and effectiveness of key social programs implemented by MIDIS; and (iii) developing adequate systems and
                         strategies for targeting, monitoring and evaluation, and overall coordination.
                         Has the Program Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Program?

                            Yes           No
