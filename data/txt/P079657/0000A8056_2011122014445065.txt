                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         To assist the borrower to establish a consistent and equitable mechanism that will empower schools and communities to improve the quality of schooling at the
                         primary and secondary school levels, and to produce an upward trend in educational outcomes.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The IDA Credit was fully disbursed.

                         The closing dates for the NZAP Trust Fund and the IDA Credit in support of TESP were extended to June 30, 2011 in order to allow schools to spend grant funding at the school
                         level. All expenditures have now been completed.

                         About $470,000 undisbursed in the NZAP Trust Fund was returned to New Zealand.
