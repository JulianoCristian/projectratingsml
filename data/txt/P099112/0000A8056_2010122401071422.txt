                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project aims to increase the effective use of the road infrastructure in the Anhui Province to support its social and economic development, as evidenced by
                         the increase in the flows of passenger and freight traffic along key corridors across the province at lower costs and with improved safety.
