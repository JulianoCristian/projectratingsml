                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project aims to increase the effective use of the road infrastructure in the Anhui Province to support its social and economic development, as evidenced by
                         the increase in the flows of passenger and freight traffic along key corridors across the province at lower costs and with improved safety.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has substantially completed all activities 6 months prior to the closing date. The overall progress of the project implementation has been highly satisfactory with the pace
                         exceeding the original schedule. The project indicators show a satisfactory trend in achieving the PDO. The intermediate indicators for institutional development program
                         demonstrates satisfactory achievement of the intended results, and the rehabilitated and improved roads indicate greater traffic increase than expected.
