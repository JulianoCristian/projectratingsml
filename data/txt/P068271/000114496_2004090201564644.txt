Re:   REPUBLIC OF CHILE: Loan 7106-CH 
(Lifelong Learning and Training Project) 
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Minister, 
 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement for the above-captioned Project between the 
Republic of Chile (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank), dated June 20, 2002, (the Loan Agreement), and your letter 
dated November 14, 2003 requesting the amendment to the Loan Agreement to comply 
with the recently enacted Ministry of Education Decree Number 131, published in the 
Borrower’s Official Gazette on August 14, 2003.  
 
The Bank agrees with your request, and proposes to amend the Loan Agreement 
as follows: 
 
1) 
Article 3.01 (d) (ii) (A) is amended to read as follows:  
 
“(A) a 35% advance at the time the Adult Education Agreement is 
signed;” 
 
2)   
Schedule 4, Section I, Part C 2 (b) is amended to read as follows: 
 
“(b) 
for Adult Education Services and Education Assessment Services 
which are estimated to cost less than $35,000 equivalent per contract, and 
not exceeding in the aggregate $360,000 equivalent per Adult Education 
Institution or Education Assessment Institution,” 
 
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and 
dating the two originals of this Amendment Letter. Upon receipt by the Bank of one fully
