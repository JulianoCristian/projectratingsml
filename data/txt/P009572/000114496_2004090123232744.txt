BHUTAN:
Primary Education Project (Credit 1899-BHU)
Amendment to the Develoloment Credit Acrreement
We refer to the above-mentioned Development Credit Agreement between the Kingdom 
of Bhutan (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the 
Association) dated May 9, 1988. We also refer to your letter dated 7/8/93 Reference 
Number NEA/ERD/IDA/1899/2134, requesting the Association to amend the Development 
Credit Agreement to allow several of the new schools being constructed under the PEP 
to operate as primary schools with some extension into classes VII and VIII along 
similar lines to existing Junior High Schools.
The Association is pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, it has 
agreed to your Ministry's request. Therefore, Schedule 2, part A of the DCA is amended 
as follows:
"Increase the number and quality of primary and junior high schools."
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing the 
enclosed copy of this letter and returning it to us.
