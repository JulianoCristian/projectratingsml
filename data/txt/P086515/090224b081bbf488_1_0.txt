                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Based upon the information gained during the site visits, the project implementation is being carried out in satisfactory manner. The activities financed by the Bank are being
                         completed: (i) 200.86 km of the line constructed; (ii) 158 km of the existing track were abandoned; (iii) the sections of the line funded by the Bank (200.86 km) were certified for train
                         operation at 160 km/h maximum and both tracks of the main line are opened to traffic since December 2012; and (iv) 99% of the loan was disbursed. The construction work at
                         Liupanshui hub funded by the local funds is ongoing and is expected to be completed by end December 2013. There are no pending safeguard issues and the living standard of the
