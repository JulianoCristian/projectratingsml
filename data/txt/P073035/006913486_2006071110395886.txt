Re:  Credit No. 3435-HO 
 
(Access to Land Project - PACTA) 
 
First Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement 
 
and Extension of Closing Date. 
 
Dear Sir: 
 
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Credit Agreement) between the 
Republic of Honduras (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the 
Association), dated January 29, 2001, with respect to the above-captioned Project. 
 
Pursuant to your letter of January 7, 2004 requesting the International Development 
Association (the Association) to extend the closing date of the credit and to amend the Credit 
Agreement, we are pleased to inform you that: 
 
(a) 
the Association has established December 30, 2006 as the later date for purposes of 
Section 2.03 of the Credit Agreement; and 
 
(b) 
the Association hereby proposes to amend the Credit Agreement as follows: 
 
1. 
A new paragraph (v) is added to Section 1.02. of the Credit Agreement, to read as 
follows: 
 
“(v) “TA Subgrant” means a grant made or proposed to be made by INA, through 
the Council, to a Beneficiary out of the proceeds of the Credit for purposes of financing 
technical assistance in support of a Subproject under Part C of the Project. 
 
2. 
Paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Credit Agreement (including without limitation the 
disbursement table therein) is hereby amended in its entirety to read as per the Annex to this 
Amendment Letter. 
 
Page  2
Cr-3435-HO 
3. 
A new subparagraph (d) is added in paragraph 2 of Schedule 1 to the Credit Agreement, to 
read as follows: 
 
“(d) the term “incremental operational costs” means reasonable expenditures which 
would not have been incurred absent the Project, for rental of facilities, operation and 
maintenance of vehicles and equipment, office supplies, utilities, travel, transportation and 
accommodations.”  
 
4. 
Part C of the Project in Schedule 2 to the Credit Agreement is hereby amended in its entirety 
to read as follows: 
 
“Part C:  Subgrants 
 
1. 
Provision of Subgrants to Beneficiaries to finance Subprojects. 
 
2. 
Provision of TA Subgrants to Beneficiaries in support of Subprojects.” 
 
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing, dating and 
returning to us the enclosed copy of this Amendment Letter.  This Amendment Letter will be 
executed in two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  The provisions of this Amendment 
Letter will become effective as of the date of countersignature upon receipt by the Association.
