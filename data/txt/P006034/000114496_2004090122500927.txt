Dear Mr. Minister
Re:
Loan 3460-AR (Second Tax Administration Technical
Assistance Project): Amendment
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated September 19, 1992 between the 
Argentine Republic (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank) for the above-referenced Project (the Loan Agreement). Whenever 
used in this letter, the terms defined in the Loan Agreement have the respective 
meanings therein set forth.
Pursuant to the request of the Secretaria de Ingresos Publicos dated April 20, 
1995 and further discussions between staff of the Borrower and the Bank, the Bank 
hereby agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read 
as set forth in Annex 1 to this letter.
2.
Paragraph 3 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended by replacing the 
amount "1,200,000" by "3,950,000".
3.
Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is amended as follows: 
 
(a)
Section I, Part B, paragraph 1 (i) is amended to read as follows: "(i) 
for pedagogical and information technology equipment up to aggregate amounts of 
$100,00 and $400,000, respectively;" and
(b)
Section I, Part B, paragraph 2 is amended by adding the expression 
"information technology equipment," immediately after "pedagogical equipment" and by 
adding the amount "$250,000" immediately after the amount "100,000".
4.
Schedule 5 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read as set forth in Annex 2 to 
this letter.
Please confirm your agreement to these amendments by signing the enclosed copy 
of this letter in the space provided and returning it to us.  Upon receipt of said 
countersigned copy, the amendments set forth above will be effective with retroactive 
effect as of April 20, 1995.
Sincerely,

Latin America and the Caribbean Region
