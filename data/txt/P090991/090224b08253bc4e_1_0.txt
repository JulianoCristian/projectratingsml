                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Continued progress has been made in improving and expanding water supply services in the three targeted districts, with remaining investments expected to be completed by
                         mid-2014.
                         The project has already increased water production capacity by almost 25 percent, provided new connections to more than 52,500 households, and improved the reliability of water
