                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project aims to improve the efficiency, effectiveness and sustainability of urban services under the control of Da Nang City through a package of priority
                         investments including urban upgrading, environmental improvement and strategic road access, as well as measures for institutional strengthening for management o f
                         urban services.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project continues to keep the good momentum since the mid-term review mission in March, 2012 and is progressing well towards achieving its development objectives. All PDO
                         indicators for 2012 have already exceeded the targets. Up to date, the project has brought benefit to about 350,000 citizens (of which females account for 54%). Especially, project
                         provided better living condition for the citizens in Low Income Areas (LIAs). About 63% percent of households in LIAs are satisfied with the quality of urban services provided under

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The project will be closed on time by the original closing date of June 30, 2013 without further extension. It was agreed between the Bank and the GoV that the Financing gap (US$
                         30 million of IDA ) and time extension required to complete the works under PIIPs wll be transferred to the Da Nang Sustainable Development Project (SCDP) which is planned to be
                         delivered in FY13.
