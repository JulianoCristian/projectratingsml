Amendments to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Java Irrigation Improvement and Water 
Resources Management Project) between the Republic of Indonesia (the Borrower) 
and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), dated 
July 25, 1994, as amended.  We also refer to the Ministry of Finance’s letter 
dated September 14, 2000, requesting amendments to the Loan Agreement and 
requesting the cancellation of $ 7,174,033 from the Loan.
1.
The Bank is pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, it has 
agreed to your request to amend the Loan Agreement as set forth below. 
Furthermore, in accordance with your request and pursuant to Section 6.03 of the 
General Conditions, the Bank hereby cancels, as of September 14, 2000, the 
amount of US dollars 7,174,033 from the Loan, and the commitment charges on such 
amount cease to accrue from September 14, 2000.  
(a)
The date in Section 2.03 of the Loan Agreement is amended to read 
“December 31, 2001.”

(c)
Part F of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read as 
follows:
“Part F: Irrigation Service Fees
Carrying out of a pilot program for the establishment of 
institutions for cost recovery of operation and maintenance from 
irrigation beneficiaries organized into water user associations, in 
the Project Area and in provinces in the Outer Islands as agreed 
with the Bank, and provision of related technical assistance.”
(d)
Part D.1 of Section I of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is 
amended by replacing the words “paragraph 2 “ by the words “paragraphs 2 and 5”.
(e)
A new paragraph 5 is added to Part D of Section I of Schedule 4 to 
the Loan Agreement as follows:
“5.
Community Participation
Works required for Part D.1 of the Project and to be carried 
out by water user associations shall be procured in accordance with 
the “Guidelines for Procurement under IBRD Loans and IDA Credits” 
(the Guidelines) published by the Bank in January 1995 and revised 
in January and August 1996, September 1997 and January 1999, and 
may be procured in accordance with procedures acceptable to the 
Bank.”
(f)
Paragraph 7 of Schedule 5 to the Loan Agreement  is amended to 
read:
Page  3
“7.
In carrying out Part F of the Project, the Borrower shall 
cause the Directorate General for Regional Development in its 
Ministry of Home Affair to include the areas to be selected for the 
pilot program in each annual work program to be prepared in 
accordance with paragraph 10 of this Schedule.”
2.
For purposes of paragraph 5 of Part D of Section I of Schedule 4 to the 
Loan Agreement as set forth in paragraph 1 (e) above, the Community 
Participation procedures acceptable to the Bank shall be the procedures set 
forth in the Petunjuk Teknis Pelaksanaan Pembangunan/Rehabilitasi Irigasi Oleh 
Perkumpulan Petani Pemakai Air (P3A)/Gabungan Perkumpulan Petani Pemakai Air 
(GP3A) issued on July 20, 2000.
3.
The Borrower will take the steps necessary to complete the procurement of 
equipment and materials under Category (2) of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement 
by September 30, 2001, and will thereafter request cancellation of any funds 
remaining uncommitted in such category.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and 
dating the enclosed duplicate of this letter in the space provided, and by 
returning it to us.  The above amendments will become effective as of the date 
of confirmation.
