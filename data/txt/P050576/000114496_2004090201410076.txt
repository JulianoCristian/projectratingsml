Re:
Colombia – Youth Development Project (Loan 4383-CO)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement, dated August 12, 1998, between the Republic 
of Colombia and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) 
for the above referenced Project, and to the request from the Project Director, 
through the letter dated July 21, 2000, for certain amendments to the Loan Agreement.
The Bank agrees with such request, and, therefore, proposes to amend the Loan 
Agreement as follows:
1.  Section 1.02 (p) is amended to read as follows in its entirety:
"(p)  'Colombia Joven' means the Program known as 'Colombia Joven', 
within the 
Administrative Department of the Borrower's Presidency;"
2.  The expression "Vice Ministry" is replaced by "Colombia Joven" wherever it appears 
in the Loan Agreement.
3.  Section 3.01 (d) is amended to read as follows in its entirety:
"(d)  Without limitation or restriction upon the provisions of paragraph 
(a) of this 
Section, the Borrower shall maintain, until the Closing Date, a unit 
within Colombia Joven 
with functions and responsibilities satisfactory to the Bank 
for purposes of coordinating 
and managing the Project, and shall provide such 
unit, promptly as needed, with the funds, 
facilities, services and other resources 
required by such unit to discharge such functions 
and responsibilities, including 
at all times during Project execution the services of a 
Project coordinator, 
a financial management specialist, and an expert on impact evaluation 
and another 
on youth development, all with experience and qualifications acceptable to the 
Bank."
4.  In Section 7.02 the address of the Vice Minister of Youth is replaced by the 
following:
"Colombia Joven Presidencia de la República 
Calle 10 No. 1-15 
Santa Fe de Bogotá 
Colombia
  Facsimile: (57-1) 5623571"
