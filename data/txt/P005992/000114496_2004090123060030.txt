Re:
Loan 3794-AR (Decentralization and Improvement of Secondary 
Education Project) Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Dr. Fernández:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between Argentine Republic (the Borrower) and 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) dated March 24, 1995 
(the Loan Agreement), as amended on December 21, 1995, and to the request from the 
Secretary of Education, Planning and Evaluation of the Ministry of Culture and 
Education, dated June 19, 1998. 
Pursuant to discussions between the Borrower and the Bank, the Bank hereby 
agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended to 
provide for: (a) reallocation of funds among disbursement Categories; and (b) 
amendment of Category (5) to be called Project Coordination Units, to read as set 
forth in the Annex to this letter. It includes the NPCU and PPCUs costs. 
2.
Paragraph 2 (b) of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is deleted and replaced by 
the following new paragraph (b):
"(b) the term ‘Project Coordination Units’ means expenditures for the 
employment of consultants’ services retained by the NPCU and PPCUs, and for 
expenditures incurred by such units for goods, office materials, rents, 
utilities, publications, advertising and bidding procedures." 
3.
Paragraph 2 (d) of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended to  read as 
follows: 
"(d) the term "Project implementation costs" means expenditures for 
travel and per diems related to training activities and Project coordination. 
4.
Paragraph 4 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended to increase the 
contract amounts eligible for disbursement under statements of expenditure to read as 
follows:
(a)
"$50,000" is replaced by "100,000", under paragraph (d); and
(b)
"$20,000" is replaced by "$50,000", under paragraph (e).
5.
Paragraph 2 of Section II of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is amended to 
increase the contract amounts for contracts requiring Bank prior review as follows:
(a)
"$50,000" is replaced by "$100,000"; for the employment of 
consulting firms under paragraphs (a) and (d); and
(b)
"$20,000" is replaced by "$50,000" for the employment of 
individual consultants, under paragraphs (b) and (e).
6.
Paragraph 3 of Schedule 6 to the Loan Agreement is hereby deleted.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
the Amendment Letter in the spaces provided below. This Amendment Letter may be 
executed in two counterparts, each of which shall be an original. Upon your 
confirmation, please return one fully executed original to us. The provisions of this 
Amendment Letter will become effective as of the date first above written upon receipt 
by the Bank of one fully executed original of this Amendment Letter.
