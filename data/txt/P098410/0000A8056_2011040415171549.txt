                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The main objective of the project is to modernize the NSS and improve its efficiency and effectiveness to collect, process and disseminate accurate, timely,
                         coherent and reliable statistical data for evidence-based policy making in support of Government's poverty reduction strategies and the process of economic
                         development.
