A. Summary of Proposed Changes
The categories of expenditures have been reallocated to match with the actual work plan and progress without affecting
the total cost of project. The reallocation includes mainly the increase in civil works and goods and equipment while
simultaneous decrease in other categories.

B. Project Status
Project is under implementation and scheduled to close by March 28, 2014. Project has been extended for 23 months
and has completed: (i) majority of 277 dam safety works and 234 canal rehabilitation/modernization works; (ii) major
irrigation sector reforms actions of irrigation management transfer and implementation of water entitlements to the
WUOs in 234 completed schemes.
