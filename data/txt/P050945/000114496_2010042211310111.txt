Amendment to the Japanese Grant Agreement  
Your Excellency:
We refer to the Japanese Grant Agreement (Technical Assistance for the 
Preparation of the Social Safety Net Project) dated April 27, 1993, as amended, 
between the Republic of Tunisia (the Recipient) and the International Bank for 
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) acting as Administrator (the Administrator) 
of Grant Funds provided by Japan.  We also refer to your request dated April 7, 1997, 
to amend the Japanese Grant Agreement to reflect and focus better on the activities 
required to prepare the proposed Education Sector Project.
We are pleased to inform you that the Administrator, after due consideration, 
has agreed to the above.  Accordingly, the Japanese Grant Agreement is hereby amended 
as follows:
1.
In Section 2.03, the date of "May 31, 1997" is changed to "September 30, 1998".
2.
In Schedule 1 (Withdrawal of the Grant), the table set forth in paragraph 1 is 
modified to read as set forth in the Attachment to this Letter.
3.
In Schedule 2 (Description of the Technical Assistance), Parts B and C are 
deleted in their entirety and the following is substituted therefor:
"Part B:
"Carrying out of a program to explore, identify, propose and assess 
recommendations and proposals to support the Recipient’s ongoing education reforms, 
properly emphasizing the considerations of equity and access, quality, and efficiency 
in the basic and secondary education systems.
Part C:
Provision of training, including overseas fellowships and study tours, to the 
staff of the Recipient involved in activities required to implement the basic and 
secondary education reform."
All the terms and conditions of the Japanese Grant Agreement, as amended hereby, 
shall remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendments by signing and 
dating the confirmation on the enclosed copy of this Amendment Letter and returning it 
to us.
Sincerely,
