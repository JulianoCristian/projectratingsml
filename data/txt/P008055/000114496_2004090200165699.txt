Excellency:
Perú:  Primary Education Quality Project (Loan No. 3826-PE)  
Amendment to Loan Agreement 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement dated December 7, 1995, between the Republic 
of Perú (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development 
(the Bank), for the above-referenced Project, as amended (the Loan Agreement).  
Whenever used in this letter, all terms defined in the Loan Agreement have the 
meanings therein set forth.
We are pleased to inform you that pursuant to the requests of the Ministry of 
Economy and Finance dated 15 July 1999 and 12 August 1999, the Bank hereby agrees to 
amend the Loan Agreement as follows: 1. 
1.
Section 3.04
(a)
 Section 3.04 (a) is amended by replacing the date “December 31” with the 
date “March 31”;
(b) Section 3.04 (b) is amended by replacing the date “December 31” with the 
date “March 31”; and
(c) Section 3.04 (c) is amended by replacing the date “December 31” with the 
date “January 31”.
2.
Section 3.05 (a) is amended by replacing “January 31” with the date “April 30”.
3.
A new subparagraph (d) is added to Paragraph 3 of Schedule 1 to the Loan 
Agreement, to read as follows: 
“(d)
 payments made for expenditures under Categories (1) and (2) for 
Part B.5 of the 
Project unless such expenditures do not exceed, in an 
aggregate amount, $2,800,000.”
4.
A new Paragraph 5 is added to Part B of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement, to 
read as follows: 
“(5)
(a) Construction and equipping of a documentation center within 
MOE; and (b) 
upgrading and refurbishing of MOE’s electrical infrastructure.”
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
this letter in the space provided below.  This amendment letter shall be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon receipt by the Bank of one 
such fully signed original, the amendments set forth in this amendment letter will 
become effective as of the date of this letter.
