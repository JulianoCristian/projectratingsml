            Re: Loan No. 3707-EC (Third Social Development

Dear Sir:
        Please refer to the Loan Agreement between
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the
Bank) and the Republic of Ecuador (the Borrower) dated May 12,
1994 (the Loan Agreement).  We are pleased to inform you that,
as a result of your request and our recent discussions on the
subject, the Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as
follows:
Schedule 4
1.  Section I, Part B, paragraph 3 (a) of this Schedule is
hereby amended by replacing the figure þ$11,500,000þ with the
figure þ$5,100,000þ.
2.  Section I, Part B, paragraph 4 of this Schedule is hereby
amended by replacing the figure þ$25,000þ with the figure
þ$35,000þ and by replacing the figure þ$3,600,000þ with the
figure þ$10,000,000þ.
Schedule 5
3.  Paragraph 1 (c) of this Schedule is hereby amended by
replacing the figure þ$2,000,000þ with the figure þ$3,000,000þ.
        Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing
amendments by signing and dating this letter in the spaces
provided below.  This amendment letter may be executed in two
counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your
confirmation, please return one fully executed original to us.
