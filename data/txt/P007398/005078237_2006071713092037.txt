Re: HONDURAS (Rural Land Management Project)
Credit No. 2940-HO
Amendment to Agreement Amending Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated June 10, 1997 entered into between the 
Republic of Honduras (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association), 
as amended, and the Agreement Amending Development Credit Agreement dated November 22, 2001 
entered into between the Borrower and the Association.
In response to the letter of July 10, 2002 from Henry Merriam, National Coordinator of the 
Project, to James Smyle, Senior Natural Resources Specialist of the Association, requesting to modify the 
condition of effectiveness set forth in Section 2.01 (c) of the Agreement Amending Development Credit 
Agreement, so as to accurately reflect AFE-COHDEFOR’s commitment under the Subsidiary Agreement 
to a specific program to promote and facilitate access to the financing of Prioritized Subprojects with the 
proceeds of the Upland Fund, the Association hereby agrees to amend Section 2.01 (c) of the Agreement 
Amending Development Credit Agreement, as follows:
“(c) the Subsidiary Agreement has been amended, in form and substance satisfactory to the 
Association, so as to provide for a commitment from AFE-COHDEFOR to a specific program to promote 
and facilitate access to the financing of Prioritized Subprojects with proceeds of the Upland Fund 
through AFE-COHDEFOR’s Social Forestry Department and the latter’s Regional Offices and their 
respective Forest Management Unit Offices;”.
This Amendment Letter will be executed in two counterparts each of which shall be an original.  
Upon your confirmation, please return one fully executed original to us.  The provisions of this 
Amendment Letter will become effective as of the date first above written upon receipt by the 
Association of one fully executed original of this Amendment Letter.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing amendments by signing and dating this 
Amendment Letter in the spaces provided below.
