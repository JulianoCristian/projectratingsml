                         Implementation Status Overview
                          The performance of the HIV/AIDS project has improved since late 2009, when the Government and the Bank agreed on a series of actions to address key constraints. These agreed
                         actions have now been implemented and are beginning to bear fruit. 1) The South Sudan AIDS Commission (SSAC) at federal level is now fully operational and is doing reasonably
                         well at coordinating the national HIV/AIDS response; 2) Carefully selected priority lines ministries are now implementing their activities and1st quarterly progress report have been
