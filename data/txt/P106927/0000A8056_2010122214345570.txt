                         Implementation Status Overview
                          Since November 2009, when a series of actions were identified to improve the performance of the project, significant progress has been achieved. 1) The capacities of South Sudan
                         AIDS Committee (SSAC) at federal level have been strengthen through recruitment of core staff such as procurement and financial specialists; 2) Lines ministries work plans have
                         been approved and implementation of activities started; 3) Lead agencies (NGOs/firms) have been recruited, whose tasks will be to support the state SSACs to : (i) improve the
