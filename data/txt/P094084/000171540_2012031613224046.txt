The Ist Series of Projects under the First Phase of the
West Africa Agricultural Productivity Program (WAAPP-1A) (IDA Credit 4286-GH)
Re: Restructuring Notice
We refer to the Financing Agreement (the Agreement) dated June 21, 2007 between the
International Development Association (the Association) and the Republic of Ghana (the Borrower)
for the above mentioned Program, and to your letters dated November 30, 2010 and October 21,
2011, requesting the Association to: (i) extend the Closing Date of the Program from June 30, 2012 to
December 31, 2012; and (ii) amend Schedule 2; Section IV (A2) and (B2) of the Agreement to
reallocate proceeds to different categories.
We are pleased to inform you that the Association agrees to your request and has, therefore,
established December 31, 2012, as the later date for the purposes of Section 2.03 of the Agreement
and has revised the table in Schedule 2, Section IV of the Agreement as shown in the Annex attached
to this letter.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing, on behalf of the Borrower, by signing, dating,
and returning to us the enclosed copy of this Amendment Letter. Upon receipt by the Association of
the copy of this Amendment Letter countersigned by you, this Amendment Letter will become
effective as of the date of your countersignature.
