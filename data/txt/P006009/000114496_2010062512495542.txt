(A) pursuant to a letter agreement dated June 3, 1987 between 
Japan and the Bank, Japan has requested the Bank, and the Bank has agreed, to
administer grant funds to be made available by Japan for the financing of certain

ARTICLE II
The Grant
Section
2.01.
The Administrator agrees to extend to the Recipient, on the

2.02. The amount of the Grant may be withdrawn from the Grant

Execution of the Technical Assistance
Section
3.01.
(a) The Recipient shall carry out the Technical Assistance

the Recipient shall carry out the Technical Assistance in accordance with the

3
to this Agreement.

3.02.

Section
3.03.
(a) The Recipient shall maintain or cause to be maintained

SCHEDULE
2
Description of Technical Assistance
A
technical assistance program (comprising local and foreign 
consultancies, training and studies) to support the preparation of the various 
components of an Agricultural Services and Institutional Development Project 
which consists of: 
(a)
Research by INTA into the management of forest plantations and 
other non-traditional agricultural export products (i.e. excluding grains and beef) 
to permit greater diversification of agricultural exports;
(b)
Maritime and fresh water fisheries research;
(c)
Institutional strengthening of INIDP and administrative units in 
DNPyA; and
(d)
Studies and research by DNPA into the management of forestry 
and other natural resources.
