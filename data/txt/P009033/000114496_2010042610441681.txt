          Re:  Loan No. 2901-0 TU
               Industrial Export Development Project
               Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Erzik:
          We refer to the Loan Agreement between the International
Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and Turkiye Sinai
Kalkinma Bankasi (the Borrower), dated January 25, 1988, for the
Industrial Export Development Project and to your letter of March 5,
1992 requesting that the commitment period under the Project be
extended to December 31, 1992.
          This is to advise you that the Bank hereby agrees to
amend Section 5(e) of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement by changing
the date of "December 31, 1990" to "December 31, 1992".  All other
terms and conditions of the Loan Agreement shall remain unchanged.
          Please indicate your agreement to the foregoing by
signing and dating the confirmation on the enclosed copy of this
letter and returning it to the Bank.
