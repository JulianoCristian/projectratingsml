AGREEMENT dated   >     1 a  315, 1980, among UNITED REPUBLIC OF
TANZANIA (hereinafter ca led the Borrower), the KINGDOM OF SWEDEN
(hereinafter called Sweden), INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION
AND DEVELOPMENT (hereinafter called the Bank) and TANZANIA ELEC-
TRIC SUPPLY COMPANY LIMITED, a company organized and existing
under the laws of Tanzania (hereinafter called TANESCO).
WHEREAS by a development co-operation agreement with the
Borrower dated August 12, 1976 (hereinafter called the Swedish
Agreement), Sweden agreed to grant to the Borrower eighty million
Swedish Kronor (SKr8O,000,000) (hereinafter called the Swedish
Grant), to be made available by the Borrower to TANESCO, for the
purpose of assisting in the financing of the Project described
in Schedule 1 to the Joint Financing Agreement as hereinafter
defined;
WHEREAS by an agreement with the Borrower dated August 12,
1976 (hereinafter called the Bank Agreement), the Bank agreed
to make to the Borrower a loan in a principal amount in various
currencies equivalent to thirty million dollars ($30,000,000)
(hereinafter called the Bank Loan) for the same purpose;
WHEREAS by a Joint Financing Agreement dated August 12,
1976 (hereinafter called the Joint Financing Agreement) the
Borrower, Sweden, the Bank and TANESCO agreed, inter alia, on the
allocation, withdrawal and use of the proceeds of the Swedish
Grant and the Bank Loan;
WHEREAS by a special action credit agreement of even date
herewith (hereinafter called the Special Action Credit Agreement),
the International Development Association (hereinafter called the
Administrator), acting as Administrator of the Special Action
Account established by the Administrator with funds contributed by
the Member States of the European Economic Community, has agreed
to extend to the Borrower an amount in various currencies equiva-
lent to seven million dollars ($7,000,000) (hereinafter called the
Special Action Credit), to be made available by the Borrower to
TANESCO, for the purpose of assisting in the financing of the
civil works component of the Project; and

WHEREAS, the parties hereto have agreed to suspend as of
the Effective Date of the Special Action Credit Agreement, with-
drawals from the Swedish Grant Account and the Bank Loan Account
in respect of Category (2) of Schedule 2 to the Joint Financing
Agreement, until the Special Action Credit shall have been fully
disbursed;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
Section 1.01. Wherever used in this Agreement, unless the
context otherwise requires and subject to the provisions of
this Agreement, the several terms defined in the Joint Financing
Agreement shall have the respective meanings therein set forth.
ARTICLE II
Section 2.01. As of the Effective Date of the Special Action
Credit Agreement, withdrawals from the Swedish Grant Account and
the Bank Loan Account pursuant to Article II of the Joint Financ-
ing Agreement in respect of Category (2) of Schedule 2 to said
Joint Financing Agreement shall be suspended until the Special
Action Credit shall have been fully disbursed.
Section 2.02. The Bank shall notify TANESCO and the Borrower
when the full amount of the Special Action Credit has been with-
drawn.
ARTICLE III
Section 3.01. Schedule 1 of the Joint Financing Agreement
is hereby amended by deleting the date "July 31, 1980" at the end
thereof and substituting therefor the date "June 30, 1981".
ARTICLE IV
Section 4.01. As provided in Section 7.07 of the Joint
Financing Agreement, the execution of this Agreement by the
representative of the Bank, constitutes agreement to the foregoing
by the Bank and Sweden.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto, acting through
their representatives thereunto duly authorized, have caused this
Agreement to be signed in their respective names, and to be
