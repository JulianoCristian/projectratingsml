                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project development objective is to strengthen the capacity of the Judiciary and the Ministry of Interior and Justice (MIJ) to deliver timely, efficient, effective and
                         quality dispute resolution services to citizens.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         As of November 30, 2013, the Project has continued the successful implementation of activities scheduled under the AOP for 2013. The Project has recently completed an ICB
                         process worth USD $2,003,603 for the purchase of audio-video equipment and ICT tools to support oral procedures in civil, family and labor courts in the cities of Barranquilla,
                         Bogota, Bucaramanga, Cali, Cartagena and Medellin. The Procurement and Annual Operating Plans for CY14 have been presented to the magistrates of the Judicial Branch
                         following the extension of Project closing date to June 30, 2014.
