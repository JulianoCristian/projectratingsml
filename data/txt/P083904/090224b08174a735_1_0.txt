                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         As of October 1, 2012, the Project has continued the successful implementation of the activities scheduled for CY12, as well as the targets agreed upon during the supervision
                         mission held during the month of March. The Project has successfully completed the ICB for the purchase of computer equipment for audio and video systems to support the
                         implementation of the management model for oral processes in labor, civil and family law. Given the amount of the ICB, USD 5,903,494, the Project team has requested the Bank
                         and DNP to increase the cap of the designated account from USD 1m to 5m in order to allow for the payments of SOEs related to the ICB as well as scheduled payments for the
