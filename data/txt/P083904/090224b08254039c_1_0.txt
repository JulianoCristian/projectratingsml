                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project development objective is to strengthen the capacity of the Judiciary and the Ministry of Interior and Justice (MIJ) to deliver timely, efficient, effective and
                         quality dispute resolution services to citizens.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Project objectives continue to be fully achievable as the Project has been performing satisfactorily over the past year. The Project has disbursed to date USD 17,638,050.52,
                         representing 88% of the total loan amount. After some initial issues with the implementation, the project got up to speed and has been consistently rated satisfactory over the last
                         year and a half.
