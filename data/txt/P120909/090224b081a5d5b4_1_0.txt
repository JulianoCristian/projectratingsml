                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project Development Objective is to: (a) contribute to improved food security of rural households in selected poor villages in upland areas in the Project Provinces
                         by demonstrating and implementing improved rice-based farming systems, and (b) address recovery needs of cyclone-affected households in the Xekong, Saravane and
                         Attapeu provinces.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project continues to make good progress in the implementation of its activities. Both Ketsana recovery components have been completed, and it is expected that sub-projects
                         under Rural Infrastructure for Improved Agricultural Livelihoods Component 3a, which is implemented by the Poverty Reduction Fund (PRF), will be completed by June 2013. The
                         planned investments in the rehabilitation of the seed centers have been also completed and seed multiplication activities are progressing well.
