Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
The specific objectives of this project are: (i) to upgrade MOH and HIF capacity to formulate and 
effectively implement health policies, health insurance, financial management and contracting of providers; 
and (ii) to develop and implement an efficient scheme of restructuring of hospital services with emphasis 
on developing day-care services and shifting to primary care.
