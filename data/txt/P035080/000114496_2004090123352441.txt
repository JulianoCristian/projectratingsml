Re:
Nicaragua:  Credit No. 2690-NI (Institutional Development Project)
Second Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Institutional Development 
Project), dated March 17, 1995 between the Republic of Nicaragua (the Borrower) and 
the International Development Association (the Association) as amended on October 13, 
1995 and July 23, 1997.
Pursuant to your request, the Association agrees to amend Schedule 4. Paragraph 
1 (a) of  the Development Credit Agreement by deleting “and (4)” and replacing it by 
“(4) and (5)”.
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing this 
letter in the space provided below and returning it to us.  This amendment shall be 
executed in two counterparts, each of which shall constitute an original.  The 
amendment shall become effective upon receipt by the Association of one fully executed 
original of this letter and shall be applied retroactively as of July 23, 1997.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
