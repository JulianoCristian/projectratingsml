Re:
Nicaragua: Credit No. 2690-NI (Institutional Development Project)
Fourth Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Institutional Development 
Project), dated March 17, 1995 between the Republic of Nicaragua (the Borrower) and 
the International Development Association (the Association) as amended on October 13, 
1995, July 23, 1997 and June 19, 1998.
Pursuant to your request, the Association agrees to amend the Development Credit 
Agreement as follows:
1.
Schedule 1:
(a)
The table in paragraph 1 of this Schedule is amended as per the 
Attachment to this letter.
(b)
Paragraph 2 is amended by: (i) deleting the word “and” at the end of 
paragraph (b); (ii) replacing the colon by “; and” at the end of paragraph (b); and 
(iii) adding a new paragraph (c) to read as follows:
“(c)
The term “Integrity Program” means the cost of consultants’ services, 
goods, supplies 
and other operating costs related to the integrity program to be 
carried out under Part C of 
the Project.”
2.
Schedule 2:  Part C.4 is amended by adding, after the words “and anti-corruption 
functions”; the following words:  including implementation of workshops, surveys and 
focus group meetings under the Borrower’s integrity program.”
3.
Schedule 3:
(a)
Section I, paragraph 2.  The amount “$500,000” is deleted and replaced by 
“$600,000”.
(b)
Section II, paragraph 3 is amended by adding, after the words “Part F”, 
the words “and C.4 in respect of the integrity program”.
4.
Schedule 4:  Paragraph 1 (a) is amended by deleting “and (5)” and replacing it 
by “(5) and (6)”.
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing this 
letter in the space provided below and returning it to us.  This amendment shall be 
executed in two counterparts, each of which shall constitute an original.  The 
amendment shall become effective upon receipt by the Association of one fully executed 
original of this letter and shall be applied retroactively as of June 1, 1998.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
