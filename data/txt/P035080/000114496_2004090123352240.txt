Re:
Nicaragua: Credit No. 2690-NI (Institutional Development
Project) Second Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Institutional Development 
Project), dated march 17, 1995 between the Republic of Nicaragua (the Borrower) and 
the International Development Association (the Association) as amended on October 13, 
1995.
Pursuant to your request, the Association agrees to amend the Development Credit 
Agreement so as to finance certain costs of the privatization of the Borrower’s 
Empresa Nicaragüense de Telecomunicaciones, its telecommunications company.  
Therefore, the Development Credit Agreement is amended as follows:
1.
Section 1.02.
(a)
Subparagraph (k): the word “and” is deleted.
(b)
Subparagraph (l): the colon is replaced by a semicolon.
(c)
New subparagraphs (m) and (n) are added to read as follows:
“(m)
“ENITEL” means Empresa Nicaragüense de 
Telecomunicaciones, the Borrower’s telecommunications company; 
and
“(n)
“Law 210” means the Borrower’s law for the incorporation 
of private operators and the expansion of telecommunication 
services 
published in the Gazette Number 231 on December 7, 
1995.
2.
Section 3.01, paragraph (a) is amended to read as follows:
“Section 3.01. (a) The Borrower declares its commitment to the objectives of the 
Project as set forth in Schedule 2 to this Agreement, and, to this end, shall carry 
out:
(i)
Parts A through E of the Project through CERAP and the 
Ministry of Finance; and 
(ii)
Part F of the Project with the assistance of: (A) under 
contractual arrangements satisfactory to IDA, ENITEL and (B) 
UCRESEP for 
the handling of the procurement, auditing and 
disbursements matters 
related thereto, all with due diligence 
and efficiency and in conformity 
with appropriate 
administrative, financial and public 
management practices, and 
the IR Arrangements in respect of Parts A through E 
of the 
Project, and shall provide, promptly as needed, the funds, 
facilities, services and other resources required for the 
Project.”
3.
Section 5.01.  A new subparagraph (c) is added to read as follows:
“(c)
Law 210 or any modifications thereof enacted in accordance with the 
provisions of paragraph 3 (c) of Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement”.
4.
Section 5.02. Subparagraph (a): the word “paragraph (a)” is replaced by 
“paragraphs (a) or (c)”.

5.
Schedule 1:
(a)
the table in paragraph 1 is amended as per the attachment to this letter.
(b)
paragraph 3 is amended by:
(i)
deleting the word “and” at the end of paragraph (a); and
(ii)
adding a new paragraph (c) to read as follows:
“(c)
payments made for expenditures under subcategory

ENITEL”.
6.
Schedule 2:
(a)
Part C, paragraph 4:
(i)
The word “and” is deleted in the second line and 
replaced by a colon; and
(ii)
The words “and anticorruption” are added before the word 
“functions”.
(b)
A new Part F is added to read as follows:
“Part F:  Privatization of ENITEL
Technical assistance for purposes of preparing for the 
privatization of, including preparation of the amendments to Law 210 
and if such 
amendments are passed by the Assembly privatizing, the 
provision of 
telecommunication services which are currently offered 
by ENITEL, including the 
hiring of the investment bank required 
thereto and the carrying out of a 
promotional campaign.”
7.
Schedule 3:
(a)
Section II.
(i)
Paragraph 1:  In the first line, after the word “
carrying out” the words “Parts A through E of” are 
added.
(ii)
New paragraphs 3, 4, 5 and 6 are added to read as 
follows:
“3.  Consultants’ services for Part F of the Project 
shall be procured in accordance with the provisions of 
the 
Introduction and Section IV of the “Guidelines:  
Selection and 
Employment of Consultants by World Bank 
Borrowers” published by 
the Bank in January 1997 (the 
1997 Consultant Guidelines) and the 
following provisions 
of Section II, paragraphs 3 through 6 of this 
Schedule.
4.
Quality- and Cost-based Selection
The investment bank, and financial and/or legal 
consulting firms hired for the privatization of ENITEL 
shall 
be procured under contracts awarded in accordance 
with the provisions 
of section II of the 1997 Consultant 
Guidelines, paragraph 3 of 
Appendix 1 thereto, Appendix 
2 thereto, and the provisions of 
paragraphs 3.13 through 
3.18 thereof applicable to quality- and 
cost-based 
selection of consultants.
5.
Individual Consultants

Services for the preparation of the modifications 
of Law 210 and for the dissemination and promotion 
campaign and which meet the requirements set forth in 
paragraph 
5.01 of the Consultant Guidelines shall be 
procured under 
contracts awarded to individual 
consultants in accordance with 
the provisions of 
paragraphs 5.1 through 5.3 of the 1997 
Consultant Guidelines.
6.
Review by the Bank of Procurement Decisions
(a)
Prior Review
(i)
With respect to each contract for the 
employment of consulting firms estimated 
to cost the equivalent of $200,000 or 
more, 
the procedures set forth in 
paragraphs 1, 2 and 
(other than the third 
subparagraph of paragraph 2 (a))

shall apply.
(ii)
With respect to each contract for the 
employment of consulting firms estimated 
to cost the equivalent of $100,000 or 
more, 
but less than the equivalent of 
$200,000, the

paragraphs 1, 2 (other 
than the second 
subparagraph of paragraph 2 (a)) 
and 5 of 
Appendix 1 to the Consultant Guidelines 
shall apply.
(iii)
With respect to each contract for the 
employment of individual consultants 
estimated to cost the equivalent of 
$50,000 or more, the qualifications, 
experience, 
terms of reference and terms 
of employment of the 
consultants shall be 
furnished to the Bank for its 
prior review 
and approval.  The contract shall be 
awarded only after the said approval shall 
have been given.
 (b)
Post Review
With respect to each contract not governed by 
subparagraph (a) of this Part, the procedures set forth in 
paragraph 4 
of appendix 1 to the 1997 Consultant Guidelines 
shall apply.”
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing this 
letter in the space provided below and returning it to us.  This amendment shall be 
executed in two counterparts, each of which shall constitute an original.  The 
amendment shall become effective upon receipt by the Association of one fully executed 
original of this letter.
