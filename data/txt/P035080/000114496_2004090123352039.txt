      RE:  NICARAGUA- Credit No. 2690-NI (Institutional Development Project)
           Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
      Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement
(Institutional Development Project), dated March 17, 1995
between the Republic of Nicaragua (the Borrower) and the
International Development Association  (the Association).
      Pursuant to your request in letter dated  September 25,
1995, the Association agrees to amend the Development Credit
Agreement so as to finance certain operating cost of the
Project implementation unit.  Therefore,  the Development
Credit Agreement is amended as follows:
1.    Schedule 1:
      a)   the table in paragraph 1 is amended as per the
attachment to this letter.
      b)   paragraph 2 is amended by:
           (i) deleting the word "and" at the end of paragraph (b);
           (ii) deleting the colon at the end of paragraph (a) and
           replacing it by "; and"; and
           (iii) adding a new paragraph (d) to read as follows:
                 "(d) the term "UCRESEP's operating cost" means the
                 cost of office rental, utilities and other
                 operational costs (excluding salaries)"
      c)   paragraph 4 is amended by:
           (i) deleting the word þandþ at the end of the paragraph (a); and
           (ii) adding the following words after the word Schedule in the
           seventh line:
                   "and under categories (3) and (4)."
2.    Schedule 4:
      Paragraph 1 (a) is amended by deleting "and (3)" and replacing it
      by ", (3) and (4)".
      Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by
signing this letter in the space provided below and returning it to us.
This amendment shall be executed in two counterparts, each of which
shall constitute an original. The amendment shall become effective upon
receipt by the Association of one fully executed original of this letter.
                                    Very truly yours,
