                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Significant progress has been made since the January 2014 mission. The civil works being conducted by MRD are substantially completed and the Management Consultant (MC) is
                         preparing the project completion report. The supplier and the consultant in NCDM are handing over the emergency management information and early warning system to the client
                         for simulation and trial operation. The project is expected to be completed within budget and on the original closing date of July 31, 2014.
