﻿AGREEMENT AMENDING
DEVELOPMENT CREDIT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated May 27, 1977 between KINGDOM OF NEPAL
(hereinafter called the Borrower) and INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT
ASSOCIATION (hereinafter called the Association).
WHEREAS (A) By a development credit agreement (Water Supply
and Sewerage Project) dated May 8, 1974, between the Borrower and
the Association (hereinafter called the 1974 Agreement), the
Association made a development credit to the Borrower in an amount
in various currencies equivalent to seven million eight hundred
thousand dollars ($7,800,000) on the terms and conditions set
forth in the 1974 Agreement;
(B) Supplemental financing is now necessary to cover the
estimated increases in the cost of carrying out the Project
described in Schedule 2 to the 1974 Agreement; and
(C) The Association has agreed to amend the 1974 Agreement
by adding the equivalent of four million dollars ($4,000,000)
to the amount of the said Credit, upon the terms and conditions
hereinafter set forth;
NOW THEREFORE, it is hereby agreed as follows:


﻿- 2 -
ARTICLE 1
Section 1.01. Section 2.01 of the 1974 Agreement is hereby
amended to read as follows:
"The Association agrees to lend to the Borrower, on
the terms and conditions in the Development Credit Agreement
set forth or referred to, an amount in various currencies
equivalent to eleven million eight hundred thousand dollars
($11,800,000)."
