Re:  Credit No. 3410 KG (Urban Transport Project)
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
Your Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement between the Kyrgyz Republic 
and the International Development Association for the above-mentioned Project 
dated September 18, 2000,  (the Credit Agreement), and to the letter of the 
Ministry of Finance of the Kyrgyz Republic dated November 21, 2000 requesting 
amendment to the Credit Agreement.
We are pleased to concur with this request and hereby agree to make the 
following amendment to the Credit Agreement:
Paragraph 2 of Part B of Schedule 3 is amended to read as follows:
"2.
The following provisions shall apply to goods and works to be 
procured under contracts awarded in accordance with the provisions of 
paragraph 1 of this Part B.
Preference for domestically manufactured goods and domestic contractors
The provisions of paragraphs 2.54 and 2.55 of the Guidelines and Appendix 2 
thereto shall apply to goods manufactured in the territory of the Borrower and 
works to be carried out by domestic contractors."
Please confirm your agreement on behalf of the Kyrgyz Republic with the 
above amendment by signing, dating and returning to us one of the original 
copies of this Amendment Letter. The amendment will become effective as of the 
date of your countersignature, upon the receipt of your countersigned letter.
Sincerely,
