Eighth Amendment to Letter Agreement
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Letter Agreement (the Agreement), dated October 26, 2006, as amended, 
between the International Development Association (IDA), acting as administrator of grant funds 
provided by the Multi Donor Trust Fund for the Ethiopia Protection of Basic Services Project and 
the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia (the Recipient).   
 
We are pleased to inform you that IDA proposes to amend the Agreement in respect of 
the following provisions:  
 
1. 
Annex I of the Agreement is deleted in its entirety. 
 
2. 
 Paragraph 1 of the Agreement is amended to read as follows: 
 
“I am writing on behalf of the International Development Association (IDA) 
to indicate IDA's agreement, as administrator of grant funds for Ethiopia 
Protection of Basic Services Project-Subprogram B - Health Millennium 
Development Goals Performance Facility provided from time to time by 
various donors (the Donors), to make available to the Federal Democratic 
Republic of Ethiopia (the Recipient) an amount of US$92,613,194 (Ninety 
two million six hundred and thirteen thousand one hundred and ninety four 
United States Dollars) referred to as “the Grant”.”
 
3. 
Paragraph 2 of the Agreement is amended to read as follows: 
 
“The administrator will promptly inform the Recipient when Donors make 
further contributions to the Grant funds, and will from time to time amend 
the Agreement to reflect these changes. The Trust Fund Account will be 
maintained in United States Dollars.”
