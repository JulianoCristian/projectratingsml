7a.  Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes 
The development objective of the Project is to contribute to rural development and poverty reduction 
through supporting provision of priority public goods and services at the commune level, as well as to 
promote good local governance through support of decentralized and deconcentrated participatory local 
governance systems at the commune and provincial levels.
