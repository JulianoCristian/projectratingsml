                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has been extended from September 31st, 2011 to December 30th, 2011. The restructuring is under process and is expected to be completed by December 30th 2011.
                         The aim of the restructuring is to: (i) revise the PDOs; (ii) modify the institutional arrangements; (iii) revise disbursement categories; and (iv) extend the closing date to December
                         30th, 2013.
