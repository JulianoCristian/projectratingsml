Amendment to the Loan Agreement
 
Dear Mr. Ozen: 
 
This refers to the Loan Agreement between Cesme-Alacati Union (the Borrower) and the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) dated May 8, 1998, as 
amended, for Cesme-Alacati Water Supply and Sewerage Project and to your letter dated  June 
24, 2002, and November 7, 2002, requesting to amend the Loan Agreement.  I am pleased to 
inform you that the Bank received the signed contract with the private operator and hereby agrees 
with your request to amend the Loan Agreement as follows: 
 
1. 
The percentage to be financed under Category 1(b) for civil works under Part C of the 
Project (except Part C(4)) in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement  has 
been amended to read “85%”. 
 
2. 
The amounts allocated to different Categories in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan 
Agreement has been reallocated and the revised Schedule 1 is attached as Annex to this Letter of 
Amendment. 
 
Except the amendment set forth in paragraph 1 of this letter of amendment, this 
amendment will become effective as of the date of your countersignature, upon receipt by the 
Bank of the copy of this letter duly countersigned by you.  The amendment set forth in paragraph 
1
of
this letter of amendment will become effective as of March 31, 2002.
