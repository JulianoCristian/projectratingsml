Re: Loan 3639-BR ( Minas Gerais Municipal Management
                 and Environmental Infrastructure Project) - Amendment
Dear Mr. Vice-Governor:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement, dated October 19, 1993, between the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the State of 
Minas Gerais for the above referenced Project, and to the Aide Memoire of the May 1997 
mission, at which time your request for some amendments to Schedule 1, Schedule 2 and 
Schedule 4, was discussed
We have reviewed your request and propose to amend the Loan Agreement as 
follows:
(a) Schedule 1: revise table (see attached)   
(b) Schedule 2:  modify Part B to read “Making of Sub-Loan to finance Infrastructure 
Subprojects and Private Sector Pilot Sub-projects; delete Part  C; in Part E, add at 
the end: “and studies on pre-privatization of selected municipal water and sanitation 
companies”; 
(c) Schedule 4:  Section 1 Part C Para 1: (i) change “equivalent of  $250,000” to 
“equivalent of $350,000”; (ii) change aggregate limit for goods from  "$30,000,000” to 
“$70,000,000”; (iii) change aggregate limit for works from "$155,000,000” to 
“192,000,000”.  Part C Para 2:  change “equivalent of $25,000 or less and $100,000 or 
less” to “equivalent of $100,000 or less and $350,000 or less”.   
If you agree with the foregoing amendment, please sign and date the two 
originals of this letter in the space provided below and return one to us. Upon 
receipt by the Bank of one copy of this letter countersigned by you, this amendment 
will become effective as of the date of countersignature.
Sincerely,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR 
  RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT
