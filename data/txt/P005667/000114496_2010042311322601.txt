                                 Amendment of the Loan Agreement

Dear Sir:

     We refer to the Loan Agreement (Second Small and Medium Scale
Industry Development Project) between the Republic of Tunisia (the
Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and
Development (the Bank), dated April 13, 1988. We also refer to the
telefax to the Bank, dated January 22, 1992, from the Director,
Agence de promotion de l’Industrie, requesting a reallocation of the
proceeds of the Loan from Category (2) (b) to Category (2) (c).

     We are pleased to inform you that the Bank, after due
consideration, has agreed to the above request. Accordingly, the
Loan Agreement is hereby amended to read as follows:

     The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is amended to read as
follows:
