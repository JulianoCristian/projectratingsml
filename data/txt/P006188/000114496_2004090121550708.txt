Please refer to the Development Credit 
Agreement, dated April 16, 1991, between The 
Republic of Bolivia (the Borrower) and

I would like to inform you that the 
Association agrees to amend the Development Credit 
Agreement, as follows:
(a)
Section 3.03 is amended by 
deleting, in the fourth line thereof, 
the 
words "and December 31, 1991, respectively,".
(b)
Section 3.05
(i)
Paragraph (a),

the third line thereof, the

"July 31, 
1992", and
(ii)
paragraph (b) is amended 
by replacing, in the second line 
thereof, the date "November 30" with 
"May 31", and in the 
third line 
thereof, the date "November 30, 
1991" with "May 
31, 1992".
(c)
Section 3.06 Paragraph (a) is 
amended by replacing, in the third 
line 
thereof, the date "March 31" with "September 30", 
in the fourth line the 
date "March 31, 1992" 
with "September 30, 1992", and in the fifth and 
sixth 
line thereof the dares "January 1 - 
December 31", with September 1 - August 
31".
Please confirm your agreement to the 
foregoing amendments by signing and dating this 
letter in the spaces provided below.  This 
amendment letter shall be executed in two 
counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  
Upon your confirmation, please return one fully 
executed original to us.  The provisions of this 
amendment letter shall become effective as of the 
date of this letter upon receipt by the Association 
of such fully executed original.
