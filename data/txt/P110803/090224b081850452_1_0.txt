                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project was declared effective on 5 November 2009. The original Project closing date was July 31, 2012. However, based on project progress and an assessment of time
                         required for completion of project activities the project closing date was extended by 24 months and revised to July 31, 2014.

                         The project activities suffered major delays at the start due to: (i) heavy procurement aspect of the project; (ii) lack of preparedness, and (iii) lack of familiarity with Bank procedures
                         and constrained capacity within the MOES procurement unit.. The Ministry was provided technical support for preparation and planning of civil works and the project was closely
