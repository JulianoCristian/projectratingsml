7a. Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
The main objective of the project is to develop a modern integrated national payments system that is
accepted and used by the commercial banks and their clients. This will provide basic infrastructure that is
essential for the development of a modern banking system that will increase the efficiency of payment
services and facilitate the growth of financial intermediation.
