                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project Development Objectives are to: a) Create effective capacity within the Government (Ministry of Power, Energy and Mineral Resources) to put in place
                         power sector policies, industry structures, and a gas supply strategy needed for balanced development of Bangladesh's power sector; b) Create capacity within
                         the Energy Regulatory Commission to regulate the sector effectively; and c)Prepare and secure financing for, at least two well-structured power sector
                         investment projects that are then efficiently implemented.

                         The project remains satisfactory. GOB has addressed the longstanding implementation issues of the project. Project development impact continued to be substantial.

                         Notable project supports are -i) transaction advice to IPPs and EPC power plant procurement (Siddhirganj 335 MW CCPP) resulting in selection of investors/EPC; ii) feasibility study
                         on energy efficiency improvements (performance improvement of existing power plants including Ghorasal repowering) and power evacuation debottlenecking (NG3 & NG4 with 400
Public Disclosure Copy




                         kV transmission); iii) environmental/social impact assessment for utility projects and utility operational improvements (MIS/ERM); iv) installation of interface meters providing accurate
                         inter-utility energy flow readings in utility invoices and implementation of power sector financial restructuring/recovery plan reducing inter-utility disputes; v) periodic review and
                         workshops that increased cooperation/coordination within power/gas utilities and ministries on implementation of reforms, utilities performance improvements, energy pricing,
                         procurement, trainings, and investment project preparation and implementation; vi) multiple trainings on power/fuel shortages mitigations, and vii) Operationalisation of Bangladesh
                         Energy Regulatory Commission (BERC) that issued several tariff orders on electricity (bulk and retail) and CNG.
