     Re:  Loan No. 3946-ES
          (Competitiveness Enhancement Technical Assistance Project) -
          Amendment
Excellency:
     Please refer to the Loan Agreement (Competitiveness Enhancement
Technical Assistance Project) dated January 19, 1996 between the
Republic of El Salvador (the Borrower) and the International Bank for
Reconstruction and Development (the Bank).
     1.   Pursuant to your Ministry's request in letter dated June 28,
1996, the Bank agrees to amend:
     (a)  Section 6.01, so that the representative of the Borrower
designated therein shall be the "Minister of Finance of the Borrower".
     (b)  Section 6.02, so that the address specified for the Borrower
for purposes of Section 11.01 of the General Conditions shall be:
          "For the Borrower:
          Ministerio de Hacienda
          Edificio Tres Torres
          Av. Alvarado, diagonal Centro Am\02rica
          Urbanizacion Buenos Aires
          San Salvador, El Salvador
          Telex:  30120245"
     2.   Pursuant to the request in letter dated February 13, 1997
from Ms. Yolanda M. de Gavidia, the Bank agrees to amend Schedule 1
to the Loan Agreement as follows:
     (a)  The Table in paragraph 1 is amended by adding a new Category
to read as follows:
          "(3)  Operating Costs    250,000   100%"
     (b)  The amount of the Loan allocated to Category (2) of the table
in paragraph 1 was reduced to $13,080,000.
     (c)  Paragraph 2 is amended by:
          (i)  deleting the word "and" at the end of subparagraph (b);
         (ii)  adding the word "and" at the end of subparagraph (c);
               and
        (iii)  adding a new paragraph (d) to read as follows:
               "(d) the term "Operating Costs" means the cost of
               maintenance and utilities, transportation, office
               materials and supplies, and other costs related to
               the operation of the Project coordination Unit,
               excluding all salaries."
     Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by
signing this letter in the space provided below and returning it to
