                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to preserve health and education services following severe flooding and other natural disasters that took place in Guatemala between May
                         and September 2010.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Loan implementation has been satisfactory, with cumulative disbursements of US$89.5 million through November 30, 2012. The government has requested a restructuring of the
                         project to accelerate the disbursement of the remaining balance and to facilitate the monitoring of the outcome indicators. There is no change in the project's development objective.
