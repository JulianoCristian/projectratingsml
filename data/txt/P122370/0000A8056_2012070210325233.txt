                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of the Project is to preserve health and education services following severe flooding and other natural disasters that took place in Guatemala between May
                         and September 2010.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The loan was approved by the Guatemalan Congress on December 7, 2011, and became effective on December 22, 2011. Retroactive disbursement of 40 percent of the loan
                         amount (US$40 million) took place on December 29, 2011, covering teacher and health worker salaries for January-November 2011. The government is currently preparing the
                         request for a second disbursement.
