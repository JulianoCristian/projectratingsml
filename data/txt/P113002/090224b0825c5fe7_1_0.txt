                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall implementation performance since the last mission in January 2014 has improved. Activities under both sub components 1b (Community Challenge Fund) and 1c
                         (Strengthening Safety Nets Systems) are now being rolled out across all the selected districts. Under sub component 1b, the cash transfers to (COs) Community Organizations have
                         been completed in all eligible COs. Under sub component 1c, the Management Information System (MIS) for the cash transfers has been established, with data of over 70,000 cash
                         transfer beneficiaries now entered in it. Branchless banking enrollment and first payments of the transfers has already been initiated in all three districts through the Payment Service
