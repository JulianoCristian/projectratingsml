                    REPUBLIC OF GUINEA
July 2, 1996
International Development Association
1818 H Street, N.W.
Washington, D.C. 20433
United States of America
          Re:  Credit No. 2874 GUI (Mining Sector Investment
               Promotion Project) ANAIM Study under Paragraph 5
               (b) of Schedule 4 of the Development Credit
               Agreement
Dear Sirs:
     With reference to the ANAIM study captioned above, we are pleased
to confirm hereby that the Republic of Guinea (the Borrower) shall carry
out by June 30, 1997 a study, under terms of reference and conditions
satisfactory to the Association, aimed at assessing the mission and the
efficiency of the Agence Nationale d'Amenagement des Infrastructures
Minieres (ANAIM) with a view to enhancing the Borrower's attractiveness
for new investments in the mining sector.
     The Borrower shall further: (a) submit the results and recommen-
dations of the study referred to above to the Association, as soon as
they become available, for its review and comments; (b) take into
account the comments and recommendations made thereupon by the Asso-
ciation; and (c) implement in accordance with the provisions of para-
graph 7 (c) of Schedule 4 to the Development Credit Agreement any
recommendations stemming from the said study as agreed upon between
the Borrower and the Association.
                                   Very truly yours,
                                   REPUBLIC OF GUINEA
                                   By:
                                          Authorized Representative
