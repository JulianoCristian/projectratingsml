                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is due to close in December 31, 2010 and has been Unsatisfactory in terms of reaching its objectives. After 65 months of project implementation, counted from the date of
                         Loan effectiveness, only the following 3 out of 13 major sub-components included in the appraised project design achieved or surpassed their agreed targets: the training of master
                         trainers and the first and continued participation of Ukraine in international comparative studies (TIMSS 2007, TIMSS 2011 and PIRLS 2011). Other activities such as in-service
                         training for teachers, leadership training for school directors, improvement of Regional Institutes of Teacher Training (RITTs) and the provision of teaching and Learning tools, have
