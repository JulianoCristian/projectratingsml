His Excellency
Captain Abdourahamane Diallo
The Minister of Agriculture
and Animal Resources
Ministry of Agriculture and

Conakry, Republic of Guinea
     Re:  Credit No. 1864-GUI (National Seeds Project)
          Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Excellency:
     Please refer to the above-mentioned Development Credit
Agreement between the Republic of Guinea (the Borrower) and the
International Development Association (the Association) dated
February 1, 1988.
     Pursuant to an exchange of telexes between representatives of
the Borrower and the Association dated December 30, 1988, March 4
and March 17, 1989 respectively, the Association hereby agrees to
amend Category 5 of the Table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule
1 to the Development Credit Agreement as follows:
     "(5) Operating   330,000      100% of foreign expenditures
                                   costs in FY 1989 through
                                   FY 1992, and 70% of
                                   local expenditures in
                                   FY 1989, 50% of local
                                   expenditures
                                   in FY 1990, 30% of local
                                   expenditures in FY 1991,
                                   and finally 15% of local
                                   expenditures in FY 1992."
     Please confirm your agreement to the above amendment by
signing and dating the form of confirmation below of the enclosed
copy of this letter and returning it to us.
     This amendment shall take effect as of March 1, 1989.
Sincerely yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
By:  Ismail Serageldin
     Director
     Occidental and Central
     Africa Department
CONFIRMED:
REPUBLIC OF GUINEA
