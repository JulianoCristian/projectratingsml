Excellency,
     Please refer to the development Credit Agreement (the
Agreement) dated February 1, 1988 between the Republic of Guinea
(the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the
Association), as amended by letters of May 23, 1988, July 7, 1989
and August 2, 1989.
     Pursuant to your request we are pleased to inform you that the
Association agrees to amend the Agreement as follows:
     The table set forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is revised to
read in its entirety as set out in Attachment 1 hereto;
     Please confirm your agreement with this amendment of the
Agreement by signing, dating and returning the form of confirmation
on the enclosed copy of this letter, This amendment shall become
effective upon receipt by the Association of the duly signed copy.
