Re:  Health II Project 
Credit No. 3979-UZ and Grant H124-UZ 
Amendment to the Development Financing Agreement
Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Development Financing Agreement for the Health II Project between the 
Republic of Uzbekistan (the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the 
Association), dated October 3, 2004, as amended (the Development Financing Agreement).  We 
also refer to your letters dated June 15 and December 15, 2009 and further discussions requesting 
the Bank to amend the Development Financing Agreement. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association, after due consideration, has acceded 
to the above request.  Accordingly, the Development Financing Agreement is hereby amended as 
follows 
 
1. 
Section 2.03 of the Development Financing Agreement is amended to read as follows: 
 
“Section 2.03. The Closing Date shall be June 30, 2011 or such later date as the 
Association and the Borrower shall agree. The Association shall promptly notify the 
Borrower of such later date.” 
 
2. 
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Development Financing Agreement is 
modified to reflect the reallocation of proceeds of the Credit between the Categories of items to 
be financed under the Project and is set forth in Attachment 1 to this letter. 
 
3. 
Parts A.6 and A.7 under Part A of the Project in Schedule 2 to the Development 
Financing Agreement are added to read as follows: 
 
“6. 
Providing dental equipment for dental cabinets of SVPs and of rayon and oblast 
polyclinics, through the provision of goods. 
 
Page  2
7. 
Development of an equipment maintenance system and repairs, through the 
provision of consultants
’
services” 
 
4. 
Part D of the Project in Schedule 2 to the Development Financing Agreement is amended 
to read as follows: 
 
“Part D
:
Project Management, Monitoring and Evaluation
1. 
Provision of training, consultants’ services (including auditing services), and 
office equipment to strengthen the Project implementation, and monitoring and 
evaluation capacity of CPIB and PIBs. 
2. 
Provision of consultants’ services for the preparation of a follow up operation.” 
 
5. 
The last sentence in Schedule 2 to the Development Financing Agreement is amended to 
read as follows: 
 
“The Project is expected to be completed by December 31, 2010.” 
 
6. 
For the purpose of Section 4 (a) of Schedule 4 to the Development Financing Agreement, 
the Supplemental Letter No.2 reflecting the performance Monitoring Indicators shall be revised 
as set forth in Attachment 2 to this letter. 
 
7. 
All the terms and conditions of the Development Financing Agreement that have not 
been amended hereby shall remain unchanged and in full force and effect. 
 
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the confirmation 
on the enclosed copy of this Letter Amendment and returning it to the Association.  Upon receipt 
by the Association of the copy of this letter countersigned by you, this amendment will become 
effective as of the date of the countersignature. 
 
Sincerely, 
 
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION 
 
/s/ Mehrnaz Teymourian
