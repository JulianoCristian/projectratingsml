                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project targets to rehabilitate about 3,800 tanks with a total command area of 164,000 ha. The tanks may be categorized into three groups with respect to source of funding:
                         Group 1: 1,829 tanks financed under original credit, Group 2: 842 tanks financed from the remaining funds under the original credit, and Group 3: 1,062 tanks financed from the
                         Additional Financing (AF). A total of 1,276 Group 1 tanks have been handed over to Tank User Groups (TUG), with almost all remaining tanks expected to be handed over by June
                         2011. During past two years, the project team has worked very hard to complete the process for implementation of works, starting from the formation of TUGs, preparation of
