Re:
Loan No. 3956 TR (Basic Education Project) Amendment to the 
Loan Agreement
Dear Sir:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank) and the Republic of Trinidad and Tobago (the Borrower) 
dated March 7, 1996 (the Loan Agreement) with regard to the above-captioned Project.  
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to the letter dated September 6, 1999 sent 
by Dr. Adesh Nanan (Hon. Minister of Education) and our recent discussions on the 
subject, the Bank agrees to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
Section I, Part C.(6)(b) of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is amended by 
replacing the 
figure of $7,000 equivalent per contract, with the figure of 
$15,000 equivalent per 
contract.
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
this letter in the spaces provided below.  This amendment letter may be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us. Upon receipt by the Bank of one fully 
executed original of this amendment letter, the provisions of this amendment letter 
will be deemed effective as of the date hereof.
Because of their interest in the implementation of the Project, we are sending a 
copy of this amendment letter to the Honorable Minister of Education and the Honorable 
Minister of Planning
Very truly yours,
