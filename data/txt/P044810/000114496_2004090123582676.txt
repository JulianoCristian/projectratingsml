Dear Sir:
Re: (Legal and Judicial Capacity Building Project): Cr.3485 BD
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement dated April 30, 2001 between the International 
Development Association (the Association) and the People’s Republic of Bangladesh (the Borrower) for 
the above referred Project.  We also refer to your letters dated December 03, 2001, March 23, 2002 and 
June 06, 2002 requesting the Association to amend the Development Credit Agreement.
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to said requests, the Association has agreed to 
amend the Development Credit Agreement as follows:
1.
Paragraph (j) of Section 1.02 is amended to read as follows:
‘
“Eligible Categories” means in respect of the Special Account for Parts A.4 (b) (ii) and 
A.6 of the Project, Category (1) set forth in the table in Part A.1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement; 
and in respect of the Special Account for the other Parts of the Project, Categories (2) through (4) 
set forth in the table in Part A.1 of Schedule 1 to this Agreement;’.
2.
Paragraph (k) of Section 1.02 is amended to read as follows:
‘
“Eligible Expenditures” means in respect of the Special Account for Parts A.4 (b) (ii) 
and A.6 of the Project, the expenditures for goods and services referred to in Section 2.02 of this 
Agreement and to be financed out of the proceeds of the Credit allocated from time to time to 
said Special Account's respective Eligible Categories; and, in respect of the Special Account for 
the other Parts of the Project, the expenditures for goods and services referred to in Section 2.02 
of this Agreement and to be financed out of the proceeds of the Credit allocated from time to 
time to said Special Account's respective Eligible Categories;’.
Page  2
3.
Paragraph (ff) of Section 1.02 is amended to read as follows:
‘
“Stage II Districts” means collectively the Borrower’s Districts of Bagerhat, Barisal, 
Bhola, Bogra, Brahman Baria, Chittagong, Cox’s Bazar, Feni, Gopalgonj, Madaripur, 
Munshiganj, Moulavibazar, Mymemsingh, Noakhali, Pabna, Rajshai, Sylhet and Thakurgaon, or 
such other Districts as may be substituted therefor with the agreement of the Association; and 
“Stage II District” means each and any of those Districts individually;’.
4.
The date in the first paragraph of Section 4.02 (a) is changed from “June 30, 2002,” to 
“December 31, 2002,”.
5.
Paragraph (c) of Part A.2 of Schedule 1 is amended to read as follows:
‘the term “incremental staff and operating costs” means: (i) in respect of Category (4) 
(a), the costs of legal aid and legal services provided or financed by NLAO; and (ii) in respect of 
Category (4) (b): (aa) the salaries and allowances of staff appointed to positions created in MOL, 
PWD, the Supreme Court and the District Courts on or after July 1, 2000 and engaged on a 
full-time basis for purposes of implementation of the Project; (bb) the operating costs of 
equipment and vehicles procured under the Project; (cc) the costs of operating and maintaining 
office buildings acquired for purposes of the Project, including the provision of utilities and 
supplies; and (dd) the in-country travel and other out-of-pocket expenses incurred by members of 
SCPIC in connection with the Project’.
6.
The table in Part A.1 of Schedule 1 is amended as set forth in the Attachment to this Amendment 
Letter.
Please confirm your agreement to the above amendment by signing the form of confirmation set 
forth below, returning to us one fully executed original of this amendment letter, and retaining one 
original for your records. 
Except as specifically amended by this Amendment Letter, all provisions of the Development 
Credit Agreement remain in full force and effect. 
This amendment shall be deemed to have become effective from the date on which the 
Development Credit Agreement became effective.
Sincerely
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
/s/ Frederick Thomas Temple
Country Director for Bangladesh
