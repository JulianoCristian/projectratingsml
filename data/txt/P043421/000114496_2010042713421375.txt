RE: Loan 4291-BR (Rio de Janeiro Mass Transit Project – PET) 
Third Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Ariston, 
 
Please refer to the Loan Agreement, dated June 1, 1999, between the International Bank 
for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the State of Rio de Janeiro (the Borrower) 
for the above referenced Project, as amended (the Loan Agreement), and to the letter from 
SEAIN, dated February 23, 2003, informing the Bank of the Federal Government’s agreement 
with a revision of the Loan disbursement percentages for the several categories of items financed 
by the above Loan and a reallocation of Loan proceeds, as requested by the Borrower. 
The Bank agrees to your request and propose to amend the table in Schedule 1 to the 
Loan Agreement as provided in the Annex to this letter.  
 
Please confirm your agreement to this amendment letter by signing and dating both 
originals of this amendment letter in the spaces provided below and returning one to us. Upon 
receipt by the Bank of one original of this amendment letter signed by you, this amendment will 
become effective as of January 1, 2003. 
 
Sincerely,
