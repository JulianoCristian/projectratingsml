Re: 
Credit No. 4038-AM 
(Yerevan Water and Wastewater Project) 
 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Mr. Gabrielyan: 
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) for the above-
captioned Project dated April 15, 2005, as amended, between the Republic of Armenia 
(the Borrower) and the International Development Association (the Association). We also 
refer to your letter dated August 24, 2010, requesting the project restructuring and 
extension of the Agreement until December 31, 2011.  
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Association accedes to your request and 
hereby amend the Agreement as follows: 
 
1. 
The objective of the Project in Schedule 2 to the Agreement is amended to read 
as follows: 
 
“The objective of the Project is to provide Yerevan with safe and 
constant water supply while reducing environmental pollution.” 
 
2. 
The Closing Date set forth in Section 2.03 of the Agreement is extended to 
“December 31, 2011”. 
 
3. 
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement is hereby amended. A 
new table is attached as Annex 1 to this amendment letter. 
 
4. 
The Project performance monitoring indicators set forth in an attachment to the 
Supplemental Letter No. 2 are revised as per attached Annex 2 to this amendment letter. 
 
5. 
All other provisions of the Agreement shall remain unchanged and in full force 
and effect. 
 
Page  2
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by countersigning both copies of 
this amendment letter and the Supplemental Letter No. 2 attached this amendment letter. 
Upon receipt by the Association of the copy of this amendment letter and the 
Supplemental Letter No. 2 attached this amendment letter countersigned by you, this 
amendment letter, will become effective as of the date of your countersignature. We will 
notify you of the receipt of your countersigned letter. 
 
Sincerely, 
 
By /s/Asad Alam 
Regional Director 
South Caucasus Country Department 
Europe and Central Asia Region
