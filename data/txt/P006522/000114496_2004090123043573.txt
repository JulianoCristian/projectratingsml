Amendment to the Loan and Project Agreements
Dear Sirs:
We refer to the Loan Agreement between the International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank) and the State of Espírito Santo (the Borrower), as amended, 
and to the Project Agreement between the Bank and Companhia Espírito Santense de 
Saneamento (CESAN), both dated October 28, 1994.  We are pleased to inform you that 
the Bank agrees with your request to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:
1.
Section 3.02 of the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows:
"Section 3.02. Except as the Bank shall otherwise agree, procurement of 
the goods, works and consultants’ services required for the Project shall be 
governed by the provisions of Schedule 4 to this Agreement."
2.

3.
Part B.2 (c) of the Project is amended to read as follows:
"a program to reorganize CESAN and to institutionalize its operational 
and financial planning;"
4.
A third and fourth paragraphs are added to Part C of Schedule 4 to the Loan 
Agreement to read as follows:
"3.
Goods estimated to cost the equivalent of $100,000 or less per 
contract, up to an aggregate amount of $2,000,000 equivalent, may be procured 
under contracts awarded on the basis of a comparison of price quotations 
solicited from at least three suppliers eligible under the Guidelines.
4.    For the procurement of those contracts for goods and works not financed 
out of the proceeds of the Loan, the Borrower shall adopt procedures 
satisfactory to the Bank, such procedures to ensure, inter alia, that the 
Project shall be carried our diligently and efficiently, and that the goods 
and works to be procured are of satisfactory quality, will be delivered or 
completed in a timely fashion and are priced so as not to affect  adversely 
the economic and financial viability of the Project."
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
both originals of this letter in the space provided below, and returning one to us.  
This amendment shall enter into effect on the date of receipt by the Bank of one 
original of this letter signed by you.  For your information, a copy of this letter is 
being sent to the Minister of Finance as representative of the Guarantor, the 
Federative Republic of Brazil.
