      Re:   ARGENTINA - Municipal Development Project (Loan 2920-AR)
                        Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Cavallo:

forwarded to the Bank by Mr. Alberto E. Biagosch on May 29, 1992, the Bank has agreed 
to amend the Loan Agreement as follows:

Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating
