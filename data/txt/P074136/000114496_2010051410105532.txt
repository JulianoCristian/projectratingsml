TRUST FUND NUMBER TF029870
TRUST FUND GUARANTEE AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT, dated as of August 23, 2001 among FEDERAL REPUBLIC OF YUGOSLAVIA, 
REPUBLIC OF SERBIA (jointly the Guarantors), and INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION 
AND DEVELOPMENT (the Bank) AND INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION (the Association) 
(jointly, the Administrator).
WHEREAS (A) the Board of Governors of the Bank, acting on the recommendation of 
its Board of Executive Directors, decided on April 30, 2001, pursuant to Resolution 
No. 539, to transfer from Bank surplus, by way of grant, $30,000,000 to a trust fund 
for the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia (TFFRY), established by Resolution of the 
Executive Directors of the Bank (IBRD Resolution No. 2001-0005) and Resolution of the 
Executive Directors of the Association (IDA Resolution No. 2001-0003), and to be 
administered by the Administrator, to be used for financing an emergency economic 
recovery and transition program in the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia;
(B)
the Guarantors and Recipient, having satisfied themselves as to the 
feasibility and priority of the Project, have requested the Administrator to assist, 
from the TFFRY, in the financing of the Project; 
(C)
by the Trust Fund Grant Agreement dated August 15, 2001 between the 
Administrator and the Recipient, the Administrator has agreed to extend to the 
Recipient a grant equivalent to six million Dollars ($6,000,000), on the terms and 
conditions set forth in the Trust Fund Grant Agreement, but only on condition that the 
Guarantors agree to guarantee the obligations of the Recipient in respect of such 
Grant and to undertake such other obligations as provided in this Agreement; and
WHEREAS the Guarantors, in consideration of the Administrator’s entering into 
the Trust Fund Grant Agreement with the Recipient, have agreed so to guarantee such 
Page  2
obligations of the Recipient;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:
ARTICLE I
General Conditions; Definitions
Section 1.01. The General Conditions set forth in Schedule 5 to the Trust Fund 
Grant Agreement constitute an integral part of this Agreement.
Section 1.02. Unless the context otherwise requires, the several terms defined 
in the General Conditions, in the Preamble to this Agreement, and in the Preamble to 
and Section 1.02 of the Trust Fund Grant Agreement have the respective meanings 
therein set forth.
ARTICLE II
Guarantee; Implementation of Part C.1 of the Project
Section 2.01. The Guarantors declare their commitment to the objectives of the 
Project as set forth in Schedule 2 to the Trust Fund Grant Agreement.
Section 2.02.  Without limitation or restriction upon any of its other 
obligations under the Guarantee Agreement, the Republic of Serbia hereby 
unconditionally guarantees the punctual performance of all the obligations of the 
Recipient set forth in the Trust Fund Grant Agreement, and the due and punctual 
payment of any funds due to the Administrator from the Recipient under the Trust Fund 
Grant Agreement.
Section 2.03. Without limitation or restriction upon the provisions of Section 
2.01 of this Agreement, the Republic of Serbia shall ensure that Part C.1 of the 
Project shall be implemented by the MME, with the assistance in the areas of 
procurement, contract management and accounting to be provided by the PIU.
ARTICLE III
Representatives of the Guarantors; Addresses
Section 3.01. The Deputy Prime Minister of the government of the Federal 
Republic of Yugoslavia and the Minister of the Ministry of Mining and Energy of the 
Republic of Serbia are designated as representatives of the Guarantors for the 
purposes of Section 31 of the General Conditions.
