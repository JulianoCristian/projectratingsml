Re: 
ROMANIA:  Employment and Social Protection Project 
(Loan Number 3849 RO)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (the Loan Agreement) between  the International 
Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and Romania (the Borrower) for the 
above-mentioned project, dated August 23, 1995 and amended on September 18, 1997 and 
August 13, 1998, and your letter dated March 26, 2001, where you propose amendments to 
the same.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank agrees to make the requested 
amendments.  Accordingly, the Loan Agreement is amended as set forth below:
1.
In Section 1.02, a new paragraph (g) is added to read as follows:
"(g)
'Operational Manual' means the manual setting for the the 
operational and administrative procedures for carrying out labor redeployment 
services under Part D of the Project, as such manual may be amended from time 
to 
time upon the agreement with the Bank".
2.
The table in paragraph 1 of  Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read 
as shown in the Attachment to this letter.
3.
Part D of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read as follows:
"Part D:
      Carrying out of programs to improve services, including the provision of labor 
redeployment services, in accordance with principles and criteria acceptable to the 
Bank, to employees affected by large scale layoffs during enterprise restructuring and 
to the personnel discharged from military service."
4.
Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is amended as follows:
(a)
In Section I, Part C, paragraph 2, the amount "$3,000,000" is replaced 
with "$11,500,000".
(b)
A new paragraph 3 is added to Section II to read as follows:
"3.
Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph 1 of this Section, labor 
redeployment services under Part D of the Project shall be procured: (a) in 
accordance with the procedures set forth in the Operational Manual; or (b) in 
accordance with the procedures whereby the Borrower shall prepare the terms of 
reference, request expressions of interest and information on the consultants’ 
experience and competence relevant to the assignment, establish a short list, 
and 
select the firm with the most appropriate qualifications and references. 
The 
selected firm shall be asked to submit a combined technical-financial 
proposal and then be invited to negotiate the contract."
5.
In Schedule 5 to the Loan Agreement, paragraph 2(a)(iv) is amended to read as 
follows:
"(iv) a group of specialists, acceptable to the Bank, to be responsible 
for the development and implementation of the labor redeployment 
services under Part D of the Project,"
Please indicate your agreement to the above amendments to the Loan Agreement by

become effective upon receipt of a countersigned copy of this letter.
