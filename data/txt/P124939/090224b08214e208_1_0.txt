                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project was declared effective on May 4, 2011, and will close on December 31, 2013. The project consists of 7 physical works sub-projects (30 meters of road re-alignment/river
                         defense/slope stabilization sub-project, 3 school rehabilitation sub-projects, and 3 community center rehabilitation sub-projects), and 2 technical assistance activities for a total of US
                         $5 million (100% IDA financed).

                         Final Review: The Final Review of the Hurricane Tomas Emergency Recovery Project (HTERP-P124939) was completed on November 15, 2013. The final review concluded that
