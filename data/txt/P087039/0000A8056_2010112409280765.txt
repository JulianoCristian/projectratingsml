                         Global Environmental Objectives
                         Global Environmental Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The Project Development Objective is to strengthen institutional and community capacity for anticipating and managing land degradation in Bhutan. The
                         Project Global Objective is to contribute to more effective protection of trans-boundary watersheds in a manner that preserves the integrity of ecosystems in
                         Bhutan.
