﻿PROJECT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT,        dated      September     28,      1972,    between
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT
(hereinafter called the Bank), INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
(hereinafter  called  the   Association)  and    SOCIETE     NATIONALE
D'EXPLOITATION ET DE DISTRIBUTION DES EAUX (hereinafter called
SONEDE).
WHEREAS by the Loan Agreement of even date herewith between Republic
of Tunisia (hereinafter called the Borrower) and the Bank, the Bank has agreed
to make available to the Borrower an amount in various currencies equivalent to
fourteen million dollars ($14,000,000), on the terms and conditions set forth in
the Loan Agreement, but only on condition that SONEDE agree to undertake
such obligations toward the Bank as hereinafter set forth;
WHEREAS by the Development Credit Agreement of even date herewith
between Republic of Tunisia (hereinafter called the Borrower) and the Association,
the Association has also agreed to make available to the Borrower an amount in
various currencies equivalent to ten million dollars ($10,000,000), on the terms
and conditions set forth in the Development Credit Agreement, but only on
condition that SONEDE agree to undertake such obligations toward the Association
as hereinafter set forth; and
WHEREAS SONEDE, in consideration inter alia of the foregoing, has agreed
to undertake the obligations hereinafter set forth;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows;
ARTICLE I
Definitions
Section 1.01. Wherever used in this Agreement, unless the context shall
otherwise require, the several terms defined in the Loan Agreement and in the
General Conditions (as so defined) have the respective meanings therein set forth
and the term "Project" means the Project described in Schedule 1 to the Loan
Agreement.


﻿4
ARTICLE II
Water Rates and Sewerage Charges
Section 2.01. Without any limitation and restriction upon the provisions of
Article IV of the Joint Financing Agreement between the Borrower, the Kingdom
of Sweden, the Association and SONEDE dated June 30, 1970, SONEDE shall,
upon completion of the hydrogeological and water consumption studies that
SONEDE is presently undertaking, establish for the Project Zones water rate
schedules acceptable to the Bank and the Association. Such water rate schedules
shall be determined so as to charge large consumers with progressive seasonal rates
reasonably related to the costs of the services and facilities provided.
Section 2.02. Unless the Bank and the Association shall otherwise agree,
SONEDE shall, before commencing to operate the sewerage and water pollution
abatement facilities in the Project Zones, set and, thereafter, maintain, in such
zones, sewerage charges which shall provide revenues sufficient to cover all the
operating costs of such facilities, including adequate maintenance of such facilities
and annual provision for depreciation or renewal of the fixed assets put in service
for the benefit of such zones; such annual provision shall be (i) based on the
lifespan of the sewerage and pollution control works, facilities and equipment
operated by SONEDE and (ii) computed at the rates specified in Schedule 1 to
this Agreement.
ARTICLE III
Consultation, Information and Inspection
Section 3.01. The Bank, the Association and SONEDE shall cooperate fully
to assure that the purposes of this Agreement will be accomplished. To that end,
the Bank, the Association and SONEDE shall from time to time, at the request
of either party, exchange views through their representatives with regard to the
performance of their respective obligations under this Agreement.
Section 3.02. The Bank, the Association and SONEDE shall promptly inform
each other of any condition which interferes with, or threatens to interfere with
the performance by either of them of its obligations under this Agreement.
ARTICLE IV
Effective Date; Termination;
Cancellation and Suspension
Section 4.01. This Agreement shall come into force and effect on the date
upon which the Loan Agreement becomes effective.

Section 4.02. This Agreement and all -obligations of the Bank, the Association
and SONEDE thereunder shall terminate on the later of the following two dates:
(a)   the date on which the Loan Agreement shall terminate in accordance
with its terms; or
(b)   the date on which the obligations of the Borrower under Section 6.03
of the Development Credit Agreement shall terminate in accordance with the terms
of such Section.
Section 4.03. All the provisions of this Agreement shall continue in full force
and effect notwithstanding any cancellation or suspension under the Loan
Agreement or the Development Credit Agreement.
