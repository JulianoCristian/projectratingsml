Re:
CHILE- Loan 4466-CH (Millennium Science Initiative Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Excellency:
Please refer to the Loan Agreement, dated June 22, 1999, between the Republic of 
Chile (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development 
(the Bank) for the above referred Project, and to the request in your letter dated 
March 15, 2001 for an amendment to the Loan Agreement that will enable disbursements 
of Loan proceeds for grants to finance networking and promotion activities under Part 
B of the Project.
The Bank agrees with your request, and proposes to amend the Loan Agreement as 
follows:
1. Section 1.02 (b) is amended to read as follows in its entirety:
"(b)  ‘Competitive Fund’ means the fund referred to in Parts A and B of 
the Project;"
2. Section 3.01 (a) and (b) is amended to read as follows in its entirety:
"(a)  The Borrower declares its commitment to the objectives of the 
Project, and, to this end, shall carry out the Project through MIDEPLAN and with 
the assistance of the Beneficiaries in respect of Part A and B of the Project, 
with due diligence and efficiency and in conformity with appropriate 
administrative, educational, financial and environmental practices, and shall 
provide, promptly as needed, the funds, facilities, services and other resources 
required for the Project.
(b)  The Borrower shall, in the carrying out of Parts A and B of the 
Project, through MIDEPLAN, enter into agreements with each Beneficiary, under 
terms and conditions substantially similar to those of the model form contained 
in the Operational Manual (the Grant Agreements), such agreements to provide 
for: (i) the transfer by the Borrower to such Beneficiary, on a grant basis, of 
the funds required by such Beneficiary to carry out a Research Subproject and/or 
any of the activities referred to in Part B of the Project; and (ii) the 
obligation of such Beneficiary to carry out such Research Subproject and/or 
activities as provided in the Operational Manual."
3.  Section 3.05 (c) is amended to read as follows in its entirety:
"(c)
requirements for implementation of Research Subprojects and the 
activities referred to in Part B of the Project, including standards, 
satisfactory to the Bank, for environmentally sound laboratory practices and 
disposal of hazardous materials; and"
4.  The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is amended to read as set forth in 
the Annex to this letter.
5.  In Schedule 2, Part B of the Project is amended to read as follows in its 
entirety:
"Operation of the Competitive Fund through the provision of grants to 
Beneficiaries for the carrying out of networking for scientific collaboration, 
and outreach activities to disseminate the benefits of quality research to 
potential collaborators and benefactors such as students, fellow researchers 
and/or partners from business and industries, including: (a) visits to 
international science and technology institutions; (b) design and delivery of 
Page  2
post graduate courses; (c) exchange programs for researchers and graduate and 
post-doctoral students; and (d) dissemination workshops."
If you agree with the foregoing amendments, please sign and date the two 
originals of this Amendment Letter in the space provided below and return one to us.  
Upon receipt by the Bank of one original of this Amendment Letter signed by you, the 
amendments will become effective as of the date of this Amendment Letter.
