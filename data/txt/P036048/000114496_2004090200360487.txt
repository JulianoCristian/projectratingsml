Amendment to the Grant Agreement
We refer to the Loan Agreement and the GEF Trust Fund Grant Agreement between 
the Republic of Indonesia (the Borrower) and the International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank), both dated May 1, 1998, for the above referenced Project. 
Further reference is made to the letter from Dr. Mas Widjaja, Director of External 
Funds, Director-ate General of Budget, Ministry of Finance, Republic of Indonesia, 
dated May 10, 2000, requesting the Bank to make amendments to both the Loan Agreement 
and the Grant Agreement.
The Bank is pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, it has agreed 
to your request to amend the following provisions of the Grant Agreement:
1.
Paragraph 2(e) of Schedule 1 is amended to read as follows:
"the term operation and maintenance for surveillance' means operation and 
maintenance of equipment procured under Part C.1(b) and (c) of the Grant 
Agreement, including all operation and maintenance expenses."
2.
Section 1, Part C.l of Schedule 3 is amended by adding the following words `MCS 
Equipment', now to read as follows:
"National Shopping 
Spare parts, replacement of surveillance equipment, MCS Equipment and 
fuel estimated to cost less than US$50,000 equivalent per contract, up to an 
aggregate amount not to exceed US$300,000 equivalent, may be procured under 
contracts awarded on the basis of national shopping procedures in accordance 
with the provisions of paragraph 3.5 and 3.6 of the Guidelines."
3.
Section II, Part C of Schedule 3 is amended to add the following paragraph, 
below C.3:
"4.
Selection of Individual Consultants
Services referred to in Part A.2(a)(iii) of Schedule 2 to the Loan 
Agreement, and under Part D of the said Schedule, may be procured in 
accordance with provisions of paragraph 5.1 through paragraph
5.3 of the Consultants Guidelines."
Further, the Bank is pleased to inform you that it has agreed to your request 
that the amounts of the proceeds allocation under the Grant Agreement, appearing in 
Categories 1 and 8 of Schedule 1, be re-allocated as shown in the Attachment. to this 
Amendment Letter. In Furtherance thereof, the reallocation is approved along the 
following lines:
(i)
in Category 1 of Schedule 1, the allocated amount of the Grant 
proceeds is increased from SDR350,000 
equivalent to SDR453,000 equivalent; and
(ii)
in Category 8 of Schedule 1, the unallocated amount is reduced 
from SDR220,000 equivalent to SDR117,000 equivalent.
Except as specifically amended by this amendment letter, all provisions of the

Grant Agreement remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement to the amendment set out above by having the
