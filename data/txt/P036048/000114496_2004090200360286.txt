Amendments to the Loan and GEF Trust Fund Grant Agreements
We refer to the Loan Agreement (Coral Reef Rehabilitation and Management 
Project) between the Republic of Indonesia (the Borrower) and the International Bank 
for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), dated May 1, 1998, as amended, and to 
the Global Environment Facility Trust Fund Grant Agreement between the Republic of 
Indonesia (the Recipient) and the International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank) acting as an Implementing Agency of the Global Environment 
Facility, dated May 1, 1998, as amended.
We also refer to the Ministry of Finance’s letter dated March 15, 2001, 
requesting amendments to the Loan and GEF Grant Agreements. Furthermore, we have 
received a letter from  Dr. Anugerah Nontji, Director, PMO-COREMAP, dated April 20, 
2001, requesting that activities in the Province of Maluku be deleted from the 
Project.  
The Bank is pleased to inform you that, after due consideration, it has agreed 
to your request to amend the Loan and GEF Grant Agreements, and has also agreed to the 
deletion of Maluku, as follows.
A.
