                         Program Development Objectives
                         Program Development Objective (from Program Document)

                         The Program Development Objective is to support economic policies to mitigate the impact of the global crisis and strengthen the medium-term framework for
                         sustainable economic recovery and growth. The program will contribute to achieving this objective by furtherdeveloping the regulatory, monitoring and financial
                         framework for fiscal and financial sustainability, labor market efficiency, andtrade liberalization.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The loan was approved on November 24, 2009 and fully disbursed one month later. The loan was closed on December 31, 2010 allowing for at least one year in which the policies
                         could show their impact as measured by the selected outcome indicators. An ICR will be prepared and discussed with the authorities between April and June, 2011.
