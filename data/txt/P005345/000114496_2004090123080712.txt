Re:
Loan No. 3899-LE (Solid Waste/Environmental
Management Project) Amendment                                                          
We refer to the Loan Agreement for the above-referenced Project (the Loan 
Agreement) between the Lebanese Republic and the International Bank for Reconstruction 
and Development (the Bank), dated, September 6, 1995, and the Project Agreement for 
the same between the Bank and the Council for Development and Reconstruction (CDR) 
(the Project Agreement), dated September 6, 1995. We also refer to the letter dated 
December 5, 1996, from Mr. Nabil El-Jisr, President of CDR, requesting amendments to 
Section 3.04, paragraph (e) of Section 5.01 of the Loan Agreement, and paragraph 3 of 
Schedule 2 to the Project Agreement. 
We are pleased to inform you that we accede to your request and that therefore:
A.
Loan Agreement
(a)
the words “December 31, 1995” in the first and second lines of Section 
3.04 are deleted and replaced with the words “October 1, 1997”; and
(b)
the words “March 31, 1997” in the second line of paragraph (e) of Section 
5.01 are deleted and replaced with the words “March 31, 1998”.
B.
Project Agreement
The words “September 30, 1996” in the first line of paragraph 3 of Schedule 2 
are deleted and replaced with the words “September 30, 1997”.
Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing, dating and 
returning to us the form of confirmation in the enclosed copy of this letter.
