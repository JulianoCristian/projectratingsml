                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project will help to increase the capacity of institutional actors and Beneficiaries in order to improve access to and the efficiency, equity and quality of local delivery

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project closing date is December 31, 2013. The three ministries have prioritized the activities which are considered essential for maximizing the project development impact and
                         which are feasible to be completed before closing date. Most of the Project objectives have been achieved and the remaining one is on track to be achieved before December 31,
                         2013. Progress towards achievement of PDO has been upgraded to satisfactory, as the steps agreed with the three line ministries to enhance overall implementation progress have
                         given satisfactory results. The Bank and the government ministries have reached an agreement to accelerate implementation in the remaining months before the project closing by
