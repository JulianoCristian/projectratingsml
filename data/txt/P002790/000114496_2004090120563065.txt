Amendment to the Norwegian Grant Agreement
      We refer to the above mentioned Development Grant
Agreement (the Agreement), dated June 16, 1992, between the
United Republic of Tanzania (the Recipient) and the
International Development Association as Administrator of a
grant provided by the Kingdom of Norway (the Administrator)
and to your letter dated July 28, 1994, requesting amendments
to the Agreement to allow the Grant funds to be used to cover
the þoperating costsþ of the National Education Trust Fund
(NETF).
      This letter is to inform you of our agreement and to
propose the following amendments to the Agreement:
1.    The table in Paragraph 1 of the Schedule to the
      Agreement is amended to read as appears in the
      Attachment hereto.
2.    A new Paragraph 2 is added to the Schedule to the
      Agreement to read as follows:
           "2. The term þOperating Costsþ means expenditures
           for the rental of office space, project management,
           including salaries and travel, and operating and
           maintenance costs for vehicles and equipment as
           approved in the annual budget of NETF."
      All provisions of the Agreement, except as amended
herein, shall remain in full force and effect.
      Please confirm your agreement to the amendments by
countersigning, dating and returning to us the enclosed copy
of this letter. This Amendment shall become effective as of
the date of countersignature.
Sincerely,
Sushma Ganguly
Acting Director
Eastern Africa Department
