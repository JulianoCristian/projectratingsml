Brazil: Paraná Basic Education (Loan 3766-BR) First Amendment to the Loan 
Agreement
Dear Madam:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (the Agreement) between the International Bank 
for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and the State of Paraná, dated October 
26, 1994.  Pursuant to your request, we are pleased to inform you that: 
1.
The date set forth in Section 2.03 of the Agreement (the Closing Date) is 
amended to read “December 31, 2000”. 
2.
The table set forth in paragraph of 1 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement is amended 
in its entirety to read as shown in the Annex to this letter.
3.
Paragraph 2 (a) of Part C of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is amended to 
read:
“Goods estimated to cost the equivalent or less than $50,000 per 
contract, up to an 
aggregate amount equivalent to $1,000,000 for goods other than 
instructional materials,           
$30,000,000 for instructional materials other 
than books, and $9,000,000 for reading and 
reference books.”
4.
Paragraph 3 of Part C of Schedule 4 to the Loan Agreement is amended to read:
“Reading and reference books estimated to cost the equivalent of $5,000 
or less per 
contract, up to an amount equivalent to $5,000 for Eligible School and up 
to an aggregate 
amount equivalent to $9,000,000 procured by the relevant Eligible 
School directly from the 
publishers in book fairs, may be procured by direct 
contracting, in accordance with 
procedures acceptable to the Bank.”
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
both originals of this letter in the space provided below, and returning one to us.  
The provisions set forth in this letter of amendment shall enter into effect on the 
date of this letter, upon receipt by the Bank of one signed original.
Sincerely,
