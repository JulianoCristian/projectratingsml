                         Implementation Status Overview
                         GEO/PDO has been upgraded to Satisfactory, with Implementation Progress remaining at Moderately Satisfactory. The IEM JO project has made positive progress in the carrying
                         out of tasks and project activities that lead towards the significant progress with respect to Global and Project Development Objectives. Firstly, the project has delivered 3 Protected

                         Areas (PAs) and 10 Special Conservation Areas (targets are 4 PAs and 7 SCAs), which contributes to the primary Project Development Objective. Secondly, the PMU has received
                         all the final baseline surveys as well as the management plans of the protected areas, as prepared by the Research and Survey Section and the Nature Conservation Section
                         respectively. Thirdly, the project is able to specify the baseline for the outcome indicators in the Arrangements for Results Monitoring (see p.33, PAD) and quantitatively measure
                         progress based on this indicators. With respect to the IP, the project's disbursement percentage has increased from 22% to 44% to 55% to 77%, according to each quarter along the

                         key government agencies which have led to several breakthrough discussions with the Ministry of Environment, Jordan Valley Authority, and Ministry of Agriculture, among other
