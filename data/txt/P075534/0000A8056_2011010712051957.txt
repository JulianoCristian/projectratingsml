                         Implementation Status Overview
                         1. The project had had a challenging year - particularly in terms of leadership. The position of the Project Director which had been vacant since Jan. 2010 was only filled from Nov. 1.
                         the new director is very competent and has already produced an updated action plan to ensure the project accelerates its activities on the ground and accordingly the disbursement.
