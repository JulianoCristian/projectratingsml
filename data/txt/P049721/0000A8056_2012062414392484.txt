                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The projects development objective is to increase the competitiveness of the agricultural sector in Kazakhstan. To achieve this objective, the project would facilitate
                         access to markets by supporting measures to improve the quality and safety of agricultural products, enhance access to information, and harmonize standards. It will
                         also help to increase the quality, quantity, and relevance of applied agricultural research and facilitate transfer of knowledge to farmers.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Overall, the project has partially achieved good results moving towards improving the competitiveness of the agricultural sector. In particular, the performance of the Competitive
                         Grant Scheme should be mentioned, bringing tangible benefits on the ground. Almost 600 competitive grants are implemented under the project, allowing companies to improve their
                         competitiveness in the following aspects: development of agricultural marketing infrastructure (slaughter houses, storage facilities, etc.), modernization of private laboratories, image

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The World Bank Loan will close on June 30, 2012. All project activities financed by the Loan are completed or close to completion. Completion of some ongoing project activities
                         (design of the National Reference Laboratory, revision of the use of the laboratory equipment purchased under the project and consultancy and service contracts to support extension
                         services and completion of a number of subprojects under the Competitive Grant Scheme) will occur after the Loan Closing Date, with financing from the Republican Budget.
