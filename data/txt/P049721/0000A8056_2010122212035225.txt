                         Implementation Status Overview
                           To date, the project's most important achievement is the creation of the Competitive Grant Scheme (CGS). The CGS is progressing well with a total of 636 grants approved in
                         four rounds. 41 grants have been completed and 476 grants are under implementation from the four rounds. 83 more contracts from the fourth round are about to be signed. The
                         program disbursed 11.8 mln USD in total. The amount allocated for grants by the project is fully committed. The Project's other achivements include supporting capacity building in
