Amendment to Schedule 1 of the Loan Agreement
We refer to the Loan Agreement between the Federative Republic of Brazil and the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) dated October 14, 
1993, as amended, for the above referenced Project.  
During the last supervision of the Project by the Bank, it was agreed that there 
is a need to increase the percentage of expenditures to be financed by the Loan for 
civil works and consultants' services under the Project, as well as to reallocate 
proceeds of the Loan among the disbursement categories, in order to enable a 
successful completion of the Project.  Therefore, we propose to amend the table set 
forth in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement as follows:
