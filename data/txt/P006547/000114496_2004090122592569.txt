2.
Part B of the Project is amended to read as follows:
 
"Part B:  Physical Rehabilitation
The set of programs to rehabilitate the infrastructure and equipment 
included in 
CBTU-RJ System:
 
1.
A track rehabilitation program consisting of:  (a) infrastructure 
works, 
including the improvement of drainage systems on about 12 line-km, 
the rehabilitation of 8 
small bridges and 175 m of culverts, the construction of 13 
pedestrian overpasses, the 
construction of about 3,100 m2 of retaining 
walls, about 8,438 m of fences along the 
right-of-way and about 16,000 m3 
of earthworks for improving the road-bed; (b) 
superstructure works on 
specific sections, including the replacement of sleepers, 
fastenings, 
ballast and rail switches, on a total of about 243 track-km, and the laying of 
about 
4,5 km of double track; (c) rehabilitation of track maintenance equipment and 
provision of related spare parts for maintenance; and (d) substitution of one viaduct.
2.
A passenger station improvement program consisting of the 
modernization/rehabilitation of about 22 stations, including installation 
of fences.
3.
A rehabilitation of the rolling stock program including: (a) the 
rehabilitation of about 73 wagons; (b) the rehabilitation/modernization 
of about 34 EMUs; 
(c) the rehabilitation of about 21 EMUs, Series 500; and (d) the 
rehabilitation of nine 
diesel locomotives.
 
4.
A workshop program including: (a) the acquisition and utilization 
of plant 
equipment for workshops and depots; and (b) the rehabilitation and 
modernization of three 
workshops.
 
5.
A telecommunications rehabilitation program consisting of: (a) the 
installation of train loudspeaker systems in about 216 trains; and 
(b) the installation of 
about 22 station clocks. 
 
6.
An electrification program consisting of: (a) the installation of 
an overhead 
catenary on about 5.4 km of track; and (b) the reform/duplication of 
circuit 4.4 kV."
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments by signing and dating 
both originals of this letter in the space provided below, and returning one to us.  
This amendment shall enter into effect on the date of receipt by the Bank of one 
original of this letter signed by you and countersigned by the representatives of the 
State of Rio de Janeiro and of Companhia Brasileira de Trens Urbanos (CBTU).
