                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is ready to begun distribution of farmer subsidies in the South and South East Departments in October and November 2012 for the planting season. The farmer registry is
                         in place with more than 3000 farmers registered and the number is growing. Synergies with RESEPAG II project are beginning in these areas with the agriculture extension activities.
                         The ITC system is being implemented as the contract with the firm providing the hardware and training is being signed. A mid term review is planned for 2012.
