Re:
Japanese Grant for Forest Development Program
Grant Number TF026361 – Amendment to Letter Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister:
We refer to the Letter-Agreement (the Letter-Agreement) between the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) and Romania (the 
Recipient) for the above-mentioned Grant, dated August 2, 2000, and your letter 
received February 5, 2001, where you propose amendments to the same.
We are pleased to inform you that the Bank agrees to make the requested amendments.  
Accordingly, the Letter-Agreement is proposed to be amended as set forth below:
The words “Ministry of Water, Forests and Environmental Protection” in 
subparagraph 1.1.(d)(iii) and in paragraph 4.4. of the Annex to the Letter-Agreement 
shall be replaced with “Ministry of Agriculture, Food and Forestry”.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendments on behalf of the 
Recipient, by signing, dating, and returning to us the enclosed copy of this Letter.
Very truly yours,
