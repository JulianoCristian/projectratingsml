Romania:  Loan No. 4258 RO (General Cadastre and Land Registration Project) 
 
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister: 
 
I
am referring to the Loan Agreement for the above-referenced Project between Romania 
and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank) dated January 23, 
1998 and amended on September 26, 2001 and August 13, 2002 (the Agreement) and your letters 
dated September 25, 2002 and January 27, 2003 requesting certain amendments to the 
Agreement. We hereby accede to the request and hereby amend the Agreement as follows (the 
modifications are shown 
in italics
): 
1. 
Section 1.02 (b)
is amended to read as follows: 
 
“Section 1.02. Unless the context otherwise requires, the several terms defined in the 
General Conditions and in the Preamble to this Agreement have the respective meanings therein 
set forth and the following additional terms have the following meanings: 
 
(a) 
“Cadastre Law” means the Borrower’s Law on General Cadastre and  Real Estate 
Publicity, No. 7, dated March 13, 1996; 
 
(b) 
“Decision No. 1038” means the Government Decision No. 1038, dated October 
28, 1996, and amended by the Government Decision No. 590, dated June 21, 2001, which 
established ONCGC under subordination of the MAP (as hereinafter defined); 
 
(c) 
“MoJ” means the Borrower’s Ministry of Justice, and includes any successor 
thereto; 
 
(d) 
“MOP” means the Borrower’s Ministry of Public Administration, and includes 
any successor thereto
;
(e) 
“ONCGC” means the Borrower’s National Office for Cadastre, Geodesy and 
Cartography, and includes any successor thereto;  
 
(f) 
“PCG” means the Project Coordination Group comprising a Director and an 
Economic Director of each PMU (as hereinafter defined);
