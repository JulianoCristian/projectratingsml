                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project development objective is to support the Government of Peru's efforts to enhance the environmental and socio-economic sustainability of historical,
                         cultural and ecological assets in the Vilcanota Valley including the Historic Sanctuary of Machu Picchu (HSMP) and the Sacred Valley of the Incas.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?
