We refer to the above-mentioned Development Credit Agreement (the Agreement) 
between Burkina Faso (the Borrower) and the International Development (the 
Association), dated May 15, 1991, and discussions held with your Ministry during the 
mid-term review of the Project in 1995, which resulted in your requesting certain 
changes to the Agreement.
We are pleased to confirm the Association's approval of the request and 
therefore agree to amend the Agreement as follows:
1.
Schedule 1 to the Agreement:
The percentage of financing of expenditures under Category 5, Incremental 
Operating Costs, 
has been increasedfrom 60% to 80%.
The table in paragraph (1) is deleted and replaced by the table setforth in the 
Annex hereto;
2.
Schedule 2 to the Agreement:
In Part C the words: "in 18 selected provinces" are deleted.
3.
Schedule 5 to the Agreement:
In paragraph (1) (c) the amount US$700,000 is replaced by the amount US$815,000.
4.
Schedule 6 to the Agreement:
The word "and" is deleted at the end of paragraph (3) and added to the end of 
paragraph (2); 
paragraph (4) is deleted.
All other provisions of the Development Credit Agreement as amended herein shall 
remain in force.
Please indicate your agreement with the foregoing amendment by signing and 
dating the confirmation form on the attached copy of this letter and returning it to 
us, while keeping the original for your records.
This amendment will become effective as of the date of confirmation of your 
agreement as indicated hereafter.
