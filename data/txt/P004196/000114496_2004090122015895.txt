Your Excellency,
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (“DCA”) dated June 29, 1994,  
between the Lao People’s Democratic Republic (the “Borrower”) and the International  
Development Association (the “Association”), as amended by amendment letters dated 
October 18, 1996, August 11, 1997 and August 4, 1998 respectively, and to the letter 
dated April 20, 2000 from you requesting the Association to reallocate the proceeds of 
the Credit.
I am pleased to inform you that the Association, after due consideration, has 
accepted your request for reallocation and establishment of a new disbursement 
category of Training.  The Association hereby reallocates the proceeds of the Credit 
as set forth in the Attachment to this amendment letter.
Except as specifically amended by this amendment letter, all provisions of the 
DCA remain in full force and effect.
Please confirm your agreement to the amendment set out above by having an 
authorized representative of the Borrower sign the form of confirmation set forth 
below, and returning one fully executed original of this amendment letter to us at 
your earliest convenience and retaining one original of the letter for your records.
This amendment letter shall become effective as of the date of countersignature.
Sincerely yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
