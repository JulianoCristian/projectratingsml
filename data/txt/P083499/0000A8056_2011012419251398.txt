                         Implementation Status Overview
                         While progress with implementation continues to be good, taking a conservative approach, ratings for DO and IP are downgraded due delays in the delivery of wagons and
                         continuing downturn in global economic activities on the traffic carried by the railways. Extension of the loan closing date to April 30, 2011 has been granted to allow completion of on-
                         going contracts and disbursement of funds. Government is implementing the agreed action for deepening the reform of the sector with the railway companies (Macedonia Railways
