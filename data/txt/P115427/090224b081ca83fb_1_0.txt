                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The overall project development objective is to improve conditions for teaching and learning in basic education including early childhood development. This objective is
                         aligned with the ongoing IDA project and consistent with the overall objectives of the Government's Education Sector Strategy Plan and the Medium-Term Plan for
                         2009-2011.

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The Education for All Fast-Track Initiative (EFA-FTI) Catalytic Fund (CF) Project (Project) now called the Global Partnership for Education (GPE) for the Republic of The Gambia was
                         approved on July 31, 2009. The Grant Letter Agreement (TF094961) was signed on August 6, 2009 and the project became simultaneously effective. The Project's performance has
                         been rated Satisfactory with regard to PDO achievement and Implementation Progress. This is the last ISR as the project closes on June 30, 2013.
