                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The overall project development objective is to improve conditions for teaching and learning in basic education including early childhood development. This objective is
                         aligned with the ongoing IDA project and consistent with the overall objectives of the Government's Education Sector Strategy Plan and the Medium-Term Plan for
                         2009-2011.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Of a total of US$28 million, 89.1% (US$24,967,681) has been disbursed. The project is expected to meet its objectives and expected results by June 2013.
                         Most of the activities are being implemented satisfactorily. With the extension of the closing date of 6 months from December 31, 2012 to June 30, 2013, all the activities including
                         construction are expected to be fully implemented by project end.

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         The Education for All Fast-Track Initiative (EFA-FTI) Catalytic Fund (CF) Project (Project) for the Republic of The Gambia (The Gambia or Recipient) was approved on July 31, 2009.
                         The Grant Letter Agreement (TF094961) was signed on August 6, 2009 and simultaneously became effective. Of a total of US$28 million, 89.1 percent of the funds or US
                         $24,967,681 has been disbursed. The Project’s performance has been rated Satisfactory with regard to PDO achievement and Implementation Performance.

                         The project is being extended from December 31, 2012 to June 30, 2013.
