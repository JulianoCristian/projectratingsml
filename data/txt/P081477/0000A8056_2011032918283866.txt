                         Implementation Status Overview
                           Project implementation remains satisfactory. The Ministry of Social Affairs (MOSA) has fulfilled its commitment to include all remaining 32,000 poor households in the West Bank in
                         its database by mid February 2011. The actual number of poor households that was certified and entered into the database is 32,401. This has been a negotiation condition for the
                         new Cash Transfer Project which is now scheduled for negotiations on March 22, 2011. On the Gaza side, there is considerable progress as the recertification process of poor
                         households is well underway. The MOSA is committed to also reaching out to the remaining poor households (40,000) in Gaza by the end of 2011. The transition year for the
