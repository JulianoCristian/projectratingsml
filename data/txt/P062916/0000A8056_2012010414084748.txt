                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The pace of implementation has picked up and all three major ITpackages: ERP; Banking application;and Data Warehouse have gone Live. Moreover, all Bangladesh Bank
                         branches are now connected to each other and also to Bangladesh Bank's Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Banking systems. Moreover, the Credit Information System and
                         the Clearing House have been automated. To achieve the project objectives fully, the full implementation of the National Payment Switch has to be accomplished which is expected

                         to be complete by December 31, 2012. The likelihood of achieving the PDO is good.

                         Key Decisions Regarding Implementation
                         To fully complete all of the ongoing activities, and to allow time for the implementation of the Office Layout and Modernization and the National Payment Switch, the Project is being
                         considered for an extension of its Closing Date by twelve months to December 31, 2012. Moreover, to recognize the improvement in Project and Financial Management, and in view
                         of the rise in disbursement levels, the rating of Implementation Progress is being upgraded to Moderately Satisfactory.
