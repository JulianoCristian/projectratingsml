Re:
Loan 3751-ME (Second Water and Sanitation Sector Project)
Amendment to the Loan Agreement
Dear Sirs:
We refer to the Loan Agreement between International Bank for Reconstruction and 
Development (the Bank) and Banco Nacional de Obras y Servicios Públicos, S.N.C., (the 
Borrower) dated June 10, 1994 (the Loan Agreement), as amended, entered into with 
respect to the above-captioned Project.  We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant  
to your request dated June 30, 1998, and our recent discussions on the subject, the 
Bank agrees to amend said Loan Agreement as follows so as to permit the creation of a 
new component for the rehabilitation or construction of public water and sanitation 
infrastructure in urban areas as a result of natural disasters, as indicated below.
1.
Section 1.02 (x) is hereby amended by deleting the word “and” at the end 
thereof.
2.
Section 1.02 (y) is hereby amended by replacing the period at the end thereof 
with a “;”.
3.
New Section 1.02 (z) is hereby inserted immediately after Section 1.02 (y), to 
read as follows in its entirety:
“(z)
“FONDEN’ means Fondo de Desastres Naturales, the trust fund 
(Fideicomiso) 
established and operating pursuant to the provisions 
of the Guarantor’s Budget Law for 1996 and 
the Operational Guidelines issued by the 
Guarantor’s Finance and Public Credit Secretariat (SHCP) 
(Acuerdo) on March 31, 
1999;”.
4.
 New Section 1.02 (aa) is hereby inserted immediately after Section 1.02 (z), to 
read as follows in its entirety:
“(aa)
“Emergency Subproject” means a specific project for the 
rehabilitation or 
construction of water supply or sanitation systems 
damaged by natural disasters, as detailed in 
FONDEN’s Operational Guidelines;”.
5.
New Section 1.02 (bb) is hereby inserted immediately after Section 1.02 (aa), to 
read as follows in its entirety:
“(bb)
“Grant” means a non-reimbursable contribution made by the 
Borrower, out of the 
proceeds of the Loan, to an Eligible Beneficiary for 
an Emergency Subproject; and”.
6.
New Section 1.02 (cc) is hereby inserted immediately after Section 1.02 (bb), to 
read as follows in its entirety:
“(cc)
Eligible Beneficiary” means any of the Guarantor’s agencies 
selected by FONDEN to 
carry out an Emergency Subproject.”.

Schedule 1 to this Agreement is hereby amended by inserting a new paragraph “2” 
immediately after the new table attached as Annex to this Letter, to read as follows 
in its entirety:
“2.
The term “Emergency Works” means civil works and consultants’ 
services required to 
carry out an Emergency Subproject which has met the 
following eligibility criteria:
(a)
studies and civil works shall be directly related to the 
rehabilitation or 
construction of water supply and sanitation systems;
(b) 
expenditures financed with the proceeds of the Loan shall be for 
civil works 
contracted: (i) on or after October 9, 1997, in the case of 
Hurricane Paulina; and (ii) on or 
after September 1, 1998, in the case of 
flooding in the State of Chiapas, and other natural 
disasters included under 
FONDEN;
(c) 
civil works shall be under the supervision of an agency appointed 
by the Guarantor; 
and
(d)
civil works and consultants' services contracts shall have been 
procured in 
accordance with the provisions of Schedule 4 to this Agreement and 
the corresponding contracts 
have been approved by the Bank.”
