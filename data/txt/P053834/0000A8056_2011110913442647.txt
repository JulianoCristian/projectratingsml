                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The Grant, funded by USAID, provides a total of US$10 million in funding for technical assistance for the privatization and economic reform program of the Government of Jordan.
                         The Grant supported Jordan's major privatization transactions and assisted the Executive Private Commission, the implementing agency, in developing a pipeline of potential Public-
                         Private Participation projects in infrastructure. Implementation is proceeding well with disbursement standing at 99%.
