                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project has now completed reconstruction of nearly 17,500 houses and restoration of community infrastructure in 291 villages affected by the 2006 earthquake and/or the 2010
                         Merapi Volcano eruption. Earthquake-related reconstruction had nearly been completed at the time of the eruption, with strong satisfaction expressed by beneficiaries. The eruption-
                         related reconstruction is now also mostly completed, including resettlement of many affected families from damaged or destroyed homes in high-risk zones to new housing (2,316

                         units) in safer locations. The project has also provided assistance on environmental management and livelihood support to improve the sustainability of the new settlements.
                         Communities have also been supported in ecosystem restoration, sewerage and solid waste management, and mapping of hazard areas in sub-villages.
