Re:  Kazakhstan: Electricity Transmission Rehabilitation Project (Loan No. 4526 KZ)
We refer to the Guarantee Agreement, dated December 21, 1999, for the above-
referenced project between the Republic of Kazakhstan (the Guarantor) and the 
International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the Bank), and to your letter, 
dated December 24, 2002, regarding the request for extension of  the date for 
establishment of the Electricity Spot Market under Section 3.01(d)(ii) of the Guarantee 
Agreement.  
 
We hereby confirm the Bank’s agreement to amend the Guarantee Agreement  by 
changing the date from December 31, 2002 to December 31, 2003.  
Please confirm your agreement, on behalf of the Guarantor, to the above 
amendment by signing and dating below.   Please return an original signed copy to us.
