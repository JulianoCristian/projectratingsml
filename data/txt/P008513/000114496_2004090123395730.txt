Re:
Credit No. 2859 KG (Sheep Development Project)
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement
Your Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement between the Kyrgyz Republic and the 
International Development Association for the above-mentioned Project dated June 13, 
1996,  (the Agreement), and to the letter of the Ministry of Finance of the Kyrgyz 
Republic dated May 3, 2000, requesting amendments to the Agreement.
We hereby agree to amend the Agreement as follows:
1.
At the end of Subsection 1.02 (a) words "or any successor thereto" are added.
2.
Subsection 1.02 (c) is amended to read as follows:
“(c)
"PIU" means the Project implementation Unit established by the Borrower 
for Project monitoring and implementation;".
3.
In Section 2.03 the date "June 30, 2001" is changed for the date "December 31, 
2002".
4.
The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Agreement is amended to reflect 
the cancellation of SDR 2,000,000 equivalent and reallocation of the proceeds of the 
Credit and is set forth in the Annex to this Amendment Letter.
5.
Paragraph 3 of Part A of Schedule 2 to the Agreement is amended by deleting 
words "and setting up of a national wool testing laboratory at a location, with 
facilities and equipment, acceptable to the Association".
6.
Paragraph 6 of Schedule 4 to the Agreement is deleted.
7.
Paragraph 9 of Schedule 4 to the Agreement is amended by deleting words "the 
breeding stock or".
Please confirm your agreement on behalf of the Kyrgyz Republic with the above 
amendments by signing, dating and returning to us one of the original copies of this 
Amendment 
Letter. The amendment will become effective as of the date of your countersignature, 
upon the receipt of your countersigned letter.
Sincerely,
By /s/ Fred King 
Acting Country Director

Europe and Central Asia Region
