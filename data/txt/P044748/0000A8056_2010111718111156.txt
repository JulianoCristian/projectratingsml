                         Implementation Status Overview
                         All contracts are under implementation and are expected to be completed by the loan closing date of December 31, 2010. The target values of most of the monitoring indicators are
                         expected to be met or exceeded. It is expected that the project development objectives will be achieved. About 700 buildings, located in all parts of the country, have benefitted from
                         the project, with almost 200,000 beneficiaries, including pupils and teachers at schools, and patients,nurses and doctors at clinics and hospitals.
