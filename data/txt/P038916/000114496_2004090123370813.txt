Re:
Credit No. 2767-NI (Second Social Investment Fund Project)
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Minister:
Please refer to the Development Credit Agreement between the Republic of 
Nicaragua and the International Development Association (the Association), dated July 
28, 1995.
We are pleased to inform you that, pursuant to your request, the Association 
agrees to amend said Development Credit Agreement by deleting “June 30, 1997” in all 
places where it appears in the table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1, and substituting 
therefor “December 31, 1997 or a later date as the Association may establish”, 
respectively.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing amendment by signing and dating 
this letter in the space provided below.  This amendment letter shall be executed in 
two counterparts, each of which shall be an original.  Upon your confirmation, please 
return one fully executed original to us.  This amendment letter will become effective 
upon receipt by the Association of a duly executed original of this letter.
Very truly yours,
INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ASSOCIATION
/s/ Ian Bannon 
    Acting Director 
    Central America Department 
    Latin America and the Caribbean Region
AGREED:
REPUBLIC OF NICARAGUA
