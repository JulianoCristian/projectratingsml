                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project's development objective is for Ugandan universities and research institutes to produce more and better qualified science and engineering graduates, and
                         higher quality and more relevant research, and for firms to utilize these outputs to improve productivity for the sake of enhancing S&T-led growth.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Implementation of MSI was reviewed in September-October 2011 and the project is progressing smoothly. Disbursement has improved ($27 million) Delivery of equipment for Round
                         2 grantees is almost complete. Procurement for Round 3grantees is ongoing. Activities are proceeding smoothly. Funds are largely allocated. No new activities ae being undertaken.
                         The projects main focus is now on completion of activities, completing the remaining procurements, and taking stock of results from the research completed, proper asset
                         management. The project was due to close in December 2011 but given the delays in procurement, additional time is needed to allow for research activities to be completed. The
