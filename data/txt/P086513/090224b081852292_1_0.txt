                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The project's development objective is for Ugandan universities and research institutes to produce more and better qualified science and engineering graduates, and
                         higher quality and more relevant research, and for firms to utilize these outputs to improve productivity for the sake of enhancing S&T-led growth.

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Implementation of MSI was reviewed in September 2012. Project progress is assessed to be satisfactory. To date, the project has disbursed US $30.16 million out of the current
                         credit value of US $31.8 million (historical credit of $30 million) representing 93.59 percent utilization. The closing date for the project has been extended by 6 months from December
                         31, 2012 to June 30, 2013 to allow for completion of project activities.
