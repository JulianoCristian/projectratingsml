                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The principal objective of the project is to assist in restoring basic water supply and sanitation services for the capital city of Baghdad through (a) the
                         reconstruction and rehabilitation of existing priority networks and treatment facilities and (b) providing capacity building support through training and technical
                         assistance.
