          Re: Agricultural Research and Pilot Extension Project
(Loan No. 3216 AL) - Amendment to Loan Agreement
Excellency:
          We refer to the loan agreement relating to the Agricultural Research and 
Pilot Extension Project between the Democratic and Popular Republic of Algeria (the 
"Borrower") and the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (the 
"Bank"), dated September 26, 1990 (the "Loan Agreement"). We also refer to the 
Borrower's request to utilize part of the Loan proceeds to finance the strengthening 
of the research capabilities of the National Institute of Agronomy of Algiers 
(Institut National Agronomique d'Alger).
          We are pleased to inform you that the Bank has acceded to the Borrower's 
request. Accordingly, the Loan Agreement is hereby amended as follows:
1.        In Section 1.01, the word "and" at the end of paragraph (o) is deleted, the 
fullstop at the end of paragraph (p) is replaced by a semi-colon, the word "and" is 
added after the semi-colon at the end of paragraph (p) and a new paragraph (q) is 
inserted to read as follows:
""INA" means Institut National Agronomique d'Alger, Algiers' National 
Institute of  Agronomy of Algiers, an institute established and operating pursuant to 
the laws of the 
Borrower."
 
2.        In Sections 3.01 (b) and 3.05, the word "and" preceding the word "ITEBO" 
shall be deleted and the words "and INA" shall be added after the word "ITEBO".
3.        In Section 6.01 (a), a comma and the words "and INA" shall be inserted after 
the word "ITEBO".
4.        The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 to the Loan Agreement shall read as 
set out in the Annex to this Letter Amendment. 
5.        In Part A.2 of Schedule 2 to the Loan Agreement, the word "and" preceding 
the word "INPV" shall be deleted and the words "and INA" shall be added after the word 
"INPV".
6.        All the terms and conditions of the Loan Agreement that have not been 
amended hereby shall remain in full force and effect.
           Please confirm your agreement with the foregoing by signing and dating the 
confirmation on the enclosed copy of this Letter Amendment and returning it to the 
Bank.
Sincerely,
INTERNATIONAL BANK FOR
  RECONSTRUCTION AND DEVELOPMENT
