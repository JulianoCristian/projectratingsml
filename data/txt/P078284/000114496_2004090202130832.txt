Re: AFGHANISTAN: Emergency Transport Rehabilitation 
Project 
Amendment to Development Credit Agreement (Credit No. 3735-AF)
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement relating to the Emergency 
Transport Rehabilitation Project (the Project) between Transitional Islamic State of 
Afghanistan (the "Borrower") and the International Development Association (the 
"Association"), dated March 17, 2003 (the "Development Credit Agreement").  We also 
refer to Deputy-
Minister of Finance’s request to modify the Project description to include 
the provision of communication equipment, generator sets and other equipment at 15 
regional airports, and your request to include clearance of land mines at Kabul 
international airport under the scope of the project as a separately funded activity.  
Accordingly, the Development Credit Agreement is hereby amended as follows: 
 
1.
sub-paragraph 1 of Part B under Schedule 2 shall read as follows: 
“Rehabilitation of the runway at Kabul's International Airport, and provision of 
consultants’ services and goods, including, without limitation, communication, radio, 
fire-fighting vehicles, meteorological, ground-handling and de-icing equipment, and a 
power generation and distribution system, to upgrade the capabilities of said airport along 
with about fifteen (15) other local airports.” 
 
2.
sub-paragraph 2 of Part E under Schedule 2 shall read as follows: “Areas 
to be rehabilitated under Part B.1 of the Project, together with selected areas where 
construction activities shall take place within the boundaries of Kabul's International 
Airport, as well as areas within an outer 5-meter corridor around Kabul's International 
Airport” 
 
All the terms and conditions of the Development Credit Agreement that have not 
been amended hereby shall remain unchanged and in full force and effect.
