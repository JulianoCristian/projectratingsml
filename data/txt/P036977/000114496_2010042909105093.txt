Re:  Credit Number 3579-KG (Rural Water Supply and Sanitation Project) 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Your Excellency: 
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement between Kyrgyz Republic (the Borrower) and 
the International Development Association (the Association) for the above-mentioned Project dated 
February 12, 2002, and to the letter of the acting Minister of Finance of the Kyrgyz Republic dated 
August 13, 2003, requesting amendment to the Credit Agreement. 
 
We hereby agree to amend the Credit Agreement as follows: 
 
1. The amount in sub-paragraph 4 (b) of Part A of Schedule 1 is changed from $200,000 to 
$300,000. 
2. The amount in Part C.1 of Section I of Schedule 3 is changed from $200,000 to $300,000. 
 
3. The amount in Part C.4 of Section I of Schedule 3 is changed from $25,000 to 50,000.   
 
Please confirm your agreement on behalf of the Borrower with the above amendments by 
signing, dating and returning to us one of the original copies of this Amendment Letter.  The 
amendments will become effective as of the date of your countersignature, upon the receipt of your 
countersigned letter. 
 
Sincerely,
