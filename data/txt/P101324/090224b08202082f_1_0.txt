                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         (i) To help the State of Minas Gerais improve the efficiency and effectiveness of public resource use and allocation for economic and social development; (ii) to support
                         the adoption of innovations in public management by the State; and (iii) to support the State in strengthening its results-based management system of monitoring and
