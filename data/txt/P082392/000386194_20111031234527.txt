7a. Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
The objective of this project is to assist the process of modernizing Grenada's public sector with a focus on
improving public sector efficiency in service delivery and management, enhancing private sector
collaboration in support of developing small businesses, and promoting regionalization initiatives.
