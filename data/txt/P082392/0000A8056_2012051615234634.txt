                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of this project is to assist the process of modernizing Grenada's public sector with a focus on improving public sector efficiency in service delivery and
                         management, enhancing private sector collaboration in support of developing small businesses, and promoting regionalization initiatives.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?
