                         Project Development Objectives
                         Project Development Objective (from Project Appraisal Document)

                         The objective of this project is to assist the process of modernizing Grenada's public sector with a focus on improving public sector efficiency in service
                         delivery and management, enhancing private sector collaboration in support of developing small businesses, and promoting regionalization initiatives.
                         Has the Project Development Objective been changed since Board Approval of the Project?

                         Implementation Status Overview
                         After the project restructuring in 2010, implementation performance has improved. Prospects for achieving the revised project development objectives by closing date remain positive
                         given continued Government commitment as demonstrated by the recent Cabinet approval of the Framework Document to guide the implementation of the Grenada Lands Agency.
