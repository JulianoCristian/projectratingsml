WHEREAS (A) the Borrower has requested the Association to
assist in the financing of the Project described in Schedule 2 to
this Agreement by extending the Credit as hereinafter provided;
(B) the Project will be carried out by the National
Cooperative Development Corporation (hereinafter called NCDC)
with the Borrower's assistance and, as part of such assistance,

WHEREAS the Association has agreed, on the basis, inter

Agreement of even date herewith between the Association and NCDC;
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:

The Credit

Section 2.02. The amount of the Credit may be withdrawn from

1 to this Agreement, as such Schedule may be amended from time to
time by agreement between the Borrower and the Association, for
expenditures made (or, if the Association shall so agree, to be

a date sixty days after the date of the Development Credit Agree-
ment to the respective dates on which amounts shall be withdrawn
by the Borrower from the Credit Account or shall be cancelled.
(b) The commitment charge shall be paid: (i) at such places
as the Association shall reasonably request; (ii) without re-
strictions of any kind imposed by, or in the territory of, the
Borrower; and (iii) in the currency specified in this Agreement
for the purposes of Section 4.02 (,,f the General Conditions or in
suc.- other eligible currency or currencies as may from time to
time be designated or selected pursuant to the provisions of that
Section.
Section 2.06. The Borrower shall pay to the Association a
service charge at the rate of three-fourths of one per cent (3/4
of 1%) per annum on the principal amount of the Credit withdrawn
and outstanding from time to time.

SCHEDULE 2
Description of the Project
The objectives of the Project are: (i) to meet pre-harvest
and  post-harvest   service  and   agro-processing  requirements,
through the construction of rural storage, soybean processing and
cotton processing facilities; (ii) to promote the production of
soybean, through the establishment of Cooperative Development
Services; and (iii) to improve the operations and management of
the cooperative institutions involved in the Project by manpower
planning and training programs.
The Project consists of the following Parts:
Part A:   Provision of NCDC Loans to Participating Banks for the
establishment of:
(i)  about 7,800 rural godowns for the storage of agricul-
tural inputs and outputs with capacities ranging from
about 50 tons to 10,000 tons, to be located in Karna-
taka, Madhya Pradesh, Orissa, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh,
and West Bengal;
(ii) processing facilities to be located as follows: (1) one
soybean seed processing plant in Rajasthan of a capa-
city of about 2.5 tons throughput per hour, (2) two
soybean seed processing plants in Madhya Pradesh of a
capacity of about 5 tons throughput per hour, (3) one
solvent oil extraction plant for soybean in Rajasthan
of a capacity of about 200 tons throughput per day, (4)
three solvent oil extraction plants for soybean in
Madhya Pradesh of a capacity of about 200 tons through-
put per day, (5) one solvent oil extraction plant for
cottonseed in Haryana of a capacity of about 100 tons
throughput per day, (6) one solvent oil extraction
plant for cottonseed in Karnataka of a capacity of
about 100 tons throughput per day, (7) one solvent oil
extraction plant for cottonseed in Maharashtra of a
capacity of about 100 tons throughput per day, (8) one
solvent oil extraction plant for cottonseed in Rajas-
than of a capacity of about 100 tons throughput per
day, (9) four ginneries equipped with sawgins, two
each in Haryana and Rajasthan, each with a capacity of
about 5,440 tons (lint) per annum and together with


﻿- 14 -
a baling press, (10) two ginneries equipped with saw-
gins or double roller gins, as the case may be, in
Maharashtra, each with a capacity of about 3,570 tons
(lint) per annum and together with a baling press, (11)
five ginneries equipped with sawgins or double roller
gins, as the case may be, two in Maharashtra, two in
Karnataka and one in Andhra Pradesh, each with a capa-
city of about 5,440 tons (lint) per annum and together
with a baling press, (12) three ring-frame spinning
mills, one in Andhra Pradesh, one in Karnataka and one
in Maharashtra, each with a capacity of about 25,000
spindles, and (13) two spinning mills, open-end (unless
otherwise agreed with the Association), one in Haryana
and one in Rajasthan, each with a capacity of about
25,000 spindles equivalent.
Part B:
(i) Establishment of new, or upgrading, as appropriate, of
training institutes for the agricultural cooperative
staff, one each in Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Orissa,
Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal;
(ii) Provision of office and teaching equipment, and vehi-
cles for NCDC's Center for Cooperative Trainers' Train-
ing and Materials Production;
(iii) Provision of teaching equipment for the Participating
States' Cooperative Training Centers and Mobile Train-
ing Units; and
(iv) Training and fellowships for the staff of NCDC and the
Participating Cooperatives.
Part C:
Establishment of the cooperative development services
for soybean production to cover five administrative
districts in Rajasthan and 15 administrative districts
in Madhya Pradesh.
The Project is expected to be completed by December 31,
1988.
