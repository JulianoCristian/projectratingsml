7a. Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
Original/Current Project Development Objectives/Outcomes
The Argentina Project follows the higher level objectives of the Regional Program, and have a common
focus in assisting participant cities in: (i) towards reducing GHG emissions by increasing use of less energy
intensive transport modes in cities; and (ii) inducing policy changes in favor of sustainable transport
projects.
