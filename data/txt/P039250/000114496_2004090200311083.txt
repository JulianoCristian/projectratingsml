Your Excellency:
We refer to the Loan Agreement (the Agreement) between us for the 
above-mentioned Project dated  July 1, 1997, and to our discussions concerning a 
proposed amendment to the same.
We hereby confirm the Bank’s agreement that incremental operating costs (i.e., 
auditing expenses) be financed under the Loan. The Attachment hereto reproduces the 
provisions of the Agreement, as modified, affected by the foregoing change.
Please confirm your agreement, on behalf of Romania, with the foregoing by 
signing and dating the form of confirmation on the attached copy of this letter and 
returning it to us. Very truly yours,

SCHEDULE 2
Description of the Project
