      Credit No. 2349 GH (Literacy and Functional Skills Project)
      Amendment of Development Credit Agreement
Dear Sir,
      We refer to the Development Credit Agreement (Literacy and
Functional Skills Project), dated April 22, 1992, between the
Republic of Ghana (the Borrower) and the International Development
Association (the Association).  Further to the discussions held with
the representatives of the Borrower, we are pleased to inform you
that the Association hereby agrees to the following amendment to the
above-referenced Development Credit Agreement:
      The table in paragraph 1 of Schedule 1 is hereby amended and
restated in its entirety to read as set out in the Annex to this
Amendment.  In paragraph 2 of Schedule I the word "and" is deleted
after sub-paragraph (a), and"; and" replaces the full stop after
sub-paragraph (b), after which a new subparagraph (c) is inserted:
      "(c) the term "Operational Costs" means the incremental
      operational costs arising under the Project on account of per
      diem and allowances of local staff, maintenance of vehicles
      and fuel, supplies and utilities incurred by NFED in the
      management and supervision of literacy classes under the
      Project."
      Please indicate the agreement of the Borrower with the
foregoing by countersigning and dating the form of confirmation on
the two original copies of this letter and returning one original,
as countersigned, to us.  This amendment shall become effective as
of the date of receipt by the Association of this letter duly
countersigned on behalf of the Borrower.
                          Sincerely yours,
                           Joanne Salop
                          Acting Director
