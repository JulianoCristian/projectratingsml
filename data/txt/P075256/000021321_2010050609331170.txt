AGREEMENT AMENDING PROJECT AGREEMENT
 
AGREEMENT, dated November 26, 2008, between PAMIR ENERGY 
COMPANY (the PamirEnergy) and INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT 
ASSOCIATION (the Association). 
 
WHEREAS (A) the Republic of Tajikistan (the Borrower) and the Association 
have entered into a Development Credit Agreement (Pamir Private Power Project),  
dated July 3, 2002 (the Development Credit Agreement), for the purpose of providing a 
Credit in support for the Project, as described in the Preamble of the Development Credit 
Agreement (the Project), but only on condition that PamirEnergy agree to undertake such 
obligations towards the Association as set forth in the Project Agreement between 
PamirEnergy and the Association, dated July 3, 2002; 
 
(B) 
the Borrower has requested, and the Association has agreed, to provide 
further additional assistance in the support of the Project by increasing the amount made 
available under the Development Credit Agreement by an amount in various currencies 
equivalent to one million six hundred thousand Special Drawing Rights (SDR 1,600,000) 
pursuant to the Agreement Amending the Development Credit Agreement, dated of even 
date herewith; and 
 
WHEREAS (C) PamirEn
ergy, in consideration of the Association’s entering in 
into the Agreement Amending the Development Credit Agreement with the Borrower, 
has agreed to undertake the obligations set forth in this Agreement; 
 
NOW THEREFORE the parties hereto hereby agree as follows:  
 
ARTICLE I 
 
Amendments to the Project Agreement 
 
Section 1.01. Any reference in the Project Agreement to “Credit” shall be 
deemed to be a reference to “Credit and Grant”. 
 
Section 1.02. The Table at Section B2 of Schedule 1 of the Project Agreement is 
amended as follows:

Section 2.01. This Amending Agreement shall come into force and effect on the

effective.
