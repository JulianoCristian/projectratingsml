Re: Credit 3067 ALB (Health System Recovery and Development Project)
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Your Excellency:
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the above-referenced Project, dated June 9, 
1998, between Albania and the International Development Association (the Association) and your letter 
dated December 3, 2002.
We are pleased to inform you that the Development Credit Agreement is hereby amended as 
follows:
1.
The Closing Date set forth in Section 2.03 of the Development Credit Agreement is extended to 
“January 31, 2004” and the Project Completion Date set forth in Schedule 2 to the Development Credit 
Agreement is extended to “January 31, 2003”.
2.
The table in Schedule 1 to the Development Credit Agreement is revised as per Annex 
attached to this amendment letter.
3.
The aggregate amount for Section I, Part C.1 of Schedule 3 to the Development Credit 
Agreement (National Competitive Bidding) is changed to $2,200,000.  The aggregate amount for 
Section I, Part C.2 of Schedule 3 to the Development Credit Agreement (International Shopping) 
is changed to $620,000.  The aggregate amount for Section I, Part C.3 of Schedule 3 to the 
Development Credit Agreement (National Shopping) is changed to $90,000.
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by countersigning both copies of this 
amendment letter and returning one of the countersigned copies to us.  Upon receipt by the
