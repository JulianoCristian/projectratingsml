Re: Credit 3067 ALB (Health System Recovery and Development Project) 
Amendment to the Development Credit Agreement
Dear Mr. Minister: 
 
We refer to the Development Credit Agreement for the above-referenced Project, 
dated June 9, 1998, between Albania and the International Development Association (the 
Association) and your letter dated January 19, 2004. 
 
We are pleased to inform you that the Development Credit Agreement is hereby 
amended as follows: 
 
The aggregate amount for Section I, Part C.2 of Schedule 3 to the Development 
Credit Agreement (International Shopping) shall be deleted.  
 
Please confirm your agreement to the foregoing by countersigning both copies of 
this amendment letter and returning one of the countersigned copies to us.  Upon receipt 
by the Association of the copy of this amendment letter countersigned by you, this 
amendment letter will become effective as of the date of your countersignature.  We will 
notify you of the receipt of your countersigned letter.
