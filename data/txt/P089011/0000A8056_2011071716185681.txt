                         Implementation Status Overview
                         The project is evolving well and is on track to reach the development objectives. The team is in place and well trained. The Municipal commitment to this project is very high. A
                         waste water treatment plant is operating and works for a new one has just started. 15km (out of 26km) of sewerage collectors in the urban center of the city has been concluded. A
                         water retention reservoir is operating, works for microdrainage have started and the bidding process for macrodrainage works in the municipal central area is under way. Bidding
