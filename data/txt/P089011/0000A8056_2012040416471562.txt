                         Implementation Status Overview
                         Implementation progress is satisfactory and project is on track to reach its development objectives. The Municipal commitment to this project is very high. A waste water treatment
                         plant is operating and works for a new one is 20 percent concluded. 19km (out of 26km) of sewerage collectors in the urban center of the city have been concluded. A water retention
                         reservoir is operating, works for micro and macro drainage have started in the municipal central area. Replacement of 74 percent of 91 thousand micrometers is ongoing. Capacity
                         building activities and studies are being conducted as planned.
